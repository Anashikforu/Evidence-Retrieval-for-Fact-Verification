

'''Robert Renfroe "Bob" Riley''' (born October 3, 1944) is an Americans|American politician. He is a member of the United States Republican Party|Republican party. He was the 52nd Governor of Alabama from 2003 until 2011. He was first election|elected to office in 2002 and re-elected in 2006. Riley was born in Ashland, Alabama|Ashland.

Personal life
He has a wife, four children (one is dead), and seven grandchildren.
