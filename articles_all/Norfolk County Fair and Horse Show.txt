The '''Norfolk County Fair and Horse Show''' is an annual showcase of rides, country music, carnival food, expensive contraptions, and horses that takes place in Simcoe, Ontario|Simcoe, Ontario, Canada.  The fair has been held in Norfolk County, Ontario|Norfolk County since the 19th Century. Simcoe Composite School people enjoy the competitions and Cheerleader|cheerleading that goes on in the grandstands.
