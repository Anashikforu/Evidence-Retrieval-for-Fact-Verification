

'''Rob Zombie''' (born '''Robert Bartleh Cummings''' on July 10, 1965 in Haverhill, Massachusetts) is an Americans|American heavy metal music|heavy metal singer, director, producer and screenwriter. He formed the band White Zombie.

He has also directed the films ''House of 1000 Corpses'', ''The Devil's Rejects'', the 2007 remake of Halloween (2007 movie)|Halloween and the sequel to that Halloween II (2009 movie)|Halloween II.
