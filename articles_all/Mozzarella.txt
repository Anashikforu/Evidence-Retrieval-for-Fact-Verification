


'''Mozzarella''' is a kind of  fresh cheese. Originally, it was Italy|Italian but now it can be found all over the world. Its made of the milk of cows or water buffalos. A cheese produced in a similar way, but with sheep's milk is common in Sardinia, Abruzzo and Lazio. It is sometimes called mozzapecora. Mozzarella made with Domestic goat|goats milk can also be found. Its development is more recent, though. The main reason it is produced is that goats' milk is easier to Digestion|digest than that of cows or buffalos.
Mozzarella is common in Italian cuisine. It can be used on pizza.

Mozzarella can be kept in the Refrigerator|fridge for a few days, if its covered in brine. 

Mozzarella can come in different moistures.
