
'''Asnières-sur-Saône''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department  in the east of France. In 2009, 73 people lived in the commune. The inhabitants are the ''Asniérois''. Famous people from Asnières-sur-Saône include José Mingret, a French painter in the 20th century who was inhumation|inhumated there. The commune has been twinned with Bad Waldsee in Germany since 1991.
