

'''''Violent by Design''''' is the second studio album by United States|American hip hop music|hip hop group, Jedi Mind Tricks. It was released on October 3, 2000. The producers of the album were Stoupe the Enemy of Mankind|Stoupe and Mr. Len. The album was re-released on May 25, 2004 by Jedi Mind Tricks current label, Babygrande Records.
