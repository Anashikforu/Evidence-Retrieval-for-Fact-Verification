'''Rots''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Basse-Normandie in the Calvados (department)|Calvados Departments of France|department in the northwest of France. On 1 January 2016, the former communes of Lasson, Calvados|Lasson and Secqueville-en-Bessin were merged into Rots.
