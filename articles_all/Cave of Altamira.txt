
'''Altamira''' (Spanish language|Spanish for 'high view') is a cave in Spain famous for its Paleolithic|Upper Paleolithic cave paintings featuring drawings and coloured paintings of wild mammals and human hands. It is located near the town of ''Santillana del Mar'' in Cantabria, Spain, 30&nbsp;km west of the city of ''Santander''. The cave with its paintings has been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO.

The cave is approximately 300 meters long
