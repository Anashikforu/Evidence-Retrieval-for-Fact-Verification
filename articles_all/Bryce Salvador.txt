
'''Bryce Chad Salvador''' (born February 11, 1976 in Brandon, Manitoba) is a former Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Defenceman (ice hockey)|defenceman that played for the New Jersey Devils of the National Hockey League|NHL. He also played for the St. Louis Blues. Salvador was drafted in the sixth round, 138th overall by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft.
