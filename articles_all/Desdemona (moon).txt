'''Desdemona''' is a closer moon to Uranus (planet)|Uranus. It was found from the images taken by ''Voyager 2'' on 1986-01-13, and was given the designation '''S/1986&nbsp;U&nbsp;6'''.

Desdemona belongs to Portia Group of moons, which also includes Bianca (moon)|Bianca, Cressida (moon)|Cressida, Juliet (moon)|Juliet, portia (moon)|Portia, Rosalind (moon)|Rosalind, Cupid (moon)|Cupid, Belinda (moon)|Belinda and Perdita (moon)|Perdita. These moons have similar orbits and photometric properties. Unfortunately, other than its orbit, radius of  34&nbsp;km and geometric albedo of 0.08 almost nothing is known about it.
