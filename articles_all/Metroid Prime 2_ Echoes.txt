

'''''Metroid Prime 2: Echoes''''' is a first-person shooter|first-person action-adventure game|adventure video game made by Retro Studios and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo GameCube game console. It is a sequel to ''Metroid Prime'', and it has a sequel called ''Metroid Prime 3: Corruption''.
