


The '''University of California, San Diego''' ('''UCSD''' or '''UC San Diego''') is a public university|public research university. It is in the La Jolla neighborhood of San Diego, California, United States.
There were 22,048 undergraduate and 5,073 graduate students in Fall 2007. The university awarded 6,802 degrees in 2005/06.

In 2012, UCSD was ranked the 15th best university in the world by the Academic Ranking of World Universities.
