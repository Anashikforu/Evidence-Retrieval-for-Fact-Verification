
'''Cabbio''' was a municipality of the district Mendrisio (district)|Mendrisio in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 25 October 2009 the former municipalities of Bruzella, Cabbio, Caneggio, Morbio Superiore, Muggio and Sagno merged into the new municipality of Breggia, Switzerland|Breggia
