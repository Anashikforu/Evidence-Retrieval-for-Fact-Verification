'''Zeh''' ('''З''', '''з''') is the eighth letter in the Cyrillic alphabet. It sounds like z. It looks like a ''three|3'' and can be mixed up with E (Cyrillic)|Э.

It came from the Greek alphabet|Greek letter Zeta (letter)|zeta and corresponds to the Roman alphabet|Roman letter Z.
