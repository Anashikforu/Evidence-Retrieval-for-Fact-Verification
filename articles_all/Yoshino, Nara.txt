

'''Yoshino''' (吉野町; -cho) is a town in Yoshino District, Nara|Yoshino District, Nara Prefecture, Japan.

As of 2003, the town had an about 10,550 people living there and a population density|density of 110.61 people per square kilometer|km². The total area was 95.65 km². 

Mt. Yoshino is famous for its 500,000 sakura trees.  They are planted in three gardens at different :wikt:altitude|altitude so that they bloom at different times of the spring.
