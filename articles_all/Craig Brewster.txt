

'''Craig James Brewster''', (born in Dundee on 13 December 1966), is a Scottish people|Scottish Coach (sport)|manager and former professional football (soccer)|footballer . He was most recently manager of Inverness Caledonian Thistle F.C.|Inverness Caledonian Thistle for the second time, but had previously made his name in a long and successful career playing as a prolific centre forward for many clubs in Scotland, but most notably for Ionikos in Greece.
