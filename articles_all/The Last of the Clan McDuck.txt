'''The Last of the Clan McDuck''' is a comic about Scrooge McDuck. It is the first Chapter (books)|chapter of ''The Life and Times of Scrooge McDuck''. The story is set from 1877 to 1880 when Scrooge is a child. At his 10th birthday, Scrooge goes with his father, Fergus, to visit the McDuck family castle. Fergus tells him the history of Clan McDuck.
