
'''Sport''' is commonly defined as an athletic activity that involves a degree of competition, such as netball or basketball.  Some games and many kinds of racing are called sports. A professional at a sport is called an athlete. Many people play sports with their friends.  Sportsperson|They need Coach (sport)|coaches to teach or train teams or individuals how to do better.  Sports can be played indoors or Position|outdoors and by individuals or team.

Sport. Different types of sports help our body in different ways. For children, sports play an extremely important part in their lives by providing all round development of the child, physically, mentally and emotionally.

Some people like to watch other people play sports. Those who watch others playing sports are called ''fanatic|fans''. While some fans watch sports on television, others actually go to stadiums or other places where people pay to watch them in person. These fans are called ''spectators''.
