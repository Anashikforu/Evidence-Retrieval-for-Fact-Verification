'''Chastity''' is the human sexuality|sexual behavior of a man or woman which is right and proper according to the morality|moral rules of a culture or religion. It has recently begun to mean sexual abstinence (not having sexual intercourse|sex at all), especially before marriage. However, it can also be used for all kinds of people (even married people) with more meaning than simply not having sex. The word chastity came from the Latin language|Latin word ''castitatem'', meaning "purity".
