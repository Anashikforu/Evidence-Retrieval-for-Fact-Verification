
'''Backlash (2005)''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view List of WWE pay-per-view events|event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It was held on May 1, 2005 at the Verizon Wireless Arena in Manchester, New Hampshire. It was the seventh annual Backlash PPV.
