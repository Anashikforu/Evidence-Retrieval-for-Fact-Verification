


The '''''Shahnameh''''' or '''''Shāhnāma''''' (''Book of Kings'',  977 to 1010 Anno Domini|AD. The ''Shahnameh'' is about the mythology|mythical and Persian Empire|historical past of Iran|Persia/Iran. It is the national epic of Persia/Iran and one of the greatest literary works of the Persian language.

''Shahnameh'' has been composed of 3 main parts: 1. mythical, 2. heroic, 3. historical.
