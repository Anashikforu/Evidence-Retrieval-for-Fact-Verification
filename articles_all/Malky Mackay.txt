
'''Malcolm George "Malky" Mackay''' (born 19 February 1972, in Bellshill) is a Scotland|Scottish manager (football)|football manager and former player.  He is the manager of Cardiff City F.C.|Cardiff City.  Mackay began his playing career in Scottish football, with Queen's Park F.C.|Queen's Park and Celtic F.C.|Celtic.  He joined English side Norwich City F.C.|Norwich City in 1998. He also played in the English Premier League|Premier League with Norwich, West Ham United F.C.|West Ham and finally Watford F.C.|Watford. He retired as a player in 2008.
