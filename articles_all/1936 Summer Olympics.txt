


The '''1936 Summer Olympics''', officially known as the '''Games of the XI Olympiad''', were held in Berlin, Germany.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics), 
The games were thought to be successful. The costs of the games was mostly paid for by the German government.Zarnowski, C. Frank. 
 Participating nations 
A total of 49 nations sent athletes to compete at the Berlin games.Taking part in the games for the first time were Afghanistan, Bermuda, Bolivia, Costa Rica, Liechtenstein and Peru.
