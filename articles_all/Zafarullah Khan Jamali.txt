
'''Mir Zafarullah Khan Jamali''' (Urdu/Baloch: میر ظفراللہ خان جمالی) (January 1, 1944 &ndash; December 2, 2020) was a Pakistani politician and businessman. He was the 15th Prime Minister of Pakistan from 2002 to 2004. He was the chairman of the Pakistan Hockey Federation. Jamali was born in present-day Balochistan, Pakistan|Balochistan, Pakistan.

Jamali died of a heart attack on December 2, 2020 in Rawalpindi, Pakistan at the age of 76.
