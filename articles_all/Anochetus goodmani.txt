


'''''Anochetus goodmani''''' is a species of ant. It has been found and described by Fisher, B. L. & Smith, M. A. in 2008.Fisher, B. L. &amp; Smith, M. A., 2008, A revision of Malagasy species of Anochetus Mayr and Odontomachus Latreille (Hymenoptera: Formicidae)., PlosOne (3), pp. 1-23: 6-8
