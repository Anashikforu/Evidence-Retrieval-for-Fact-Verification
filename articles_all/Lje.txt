The Cyrillic alphabet|Cyrillic letter '''lyeh''' (Љ, љ) is a combination of El (Cyrillic)|Л and Soft sign|Ь and is used in Serbian language|Serbian and Macedonian language|Macedonian. It is used to denote the palatal lateral approximant  in Serbian language and Croatian language. It is romanized using the letter L with caron (Ľ ľ).
