'''Agent Orange''' is a very powerful herbicide that was used in the Vietnam War. It is a defoliant that wikt:destroy|destroys forests. It was used by the United States military so their enemy|enemies had no place to hide.

Problems
The problem with Agent Orange is that when it is released, some dioxins are also generated. Dioxins stay a long time in the environment. They are very toxic, and cause genetics|genetic modifications and cancer.
