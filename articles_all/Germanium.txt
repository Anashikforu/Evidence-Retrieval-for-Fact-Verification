'''Germanium''' is a chemical element. Its chemical symbol is ''Ge''. Its atomic number is 32. It was discovered by Clemens Winkler. It is a shiny, hard, silver-white metalloid. The chemistry of Germanium is quite like tin. Germanium forms many Organometallic compound|organometallic chemical compound|compounds. It is an important semiconductor material used in transistors.
