

The '''Pandyan kingdom''' பாண்டியர் was an ancient Tamil people|Tamil state in South India of unknown antiquity. Pandyas were one of the three ancient Tamil kingdoms (Chola dynasty|Chola and Chera dynasty|Chera being the other two) who ruled the Tamil country from pre-historic times until end of the 15th century. They ruled initially from Korkai, a sea port on the southern most tip of the Indian peninsula, and in later times moved to Madurai.
