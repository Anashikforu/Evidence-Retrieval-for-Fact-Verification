
'''Brett DiBiase''' (born March 16, 1988) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler.

Personal life
DiBiase's stepgrandfather Michael DiBiase, his grandmother Helen Hild, and his father Ted DiBiase were professional wrestlers. His older Sibling|half brother Mike DiBiase II and his older Sibling|brother Ted DiBiase, Jr. are also professional wrestlers.

On June 9, 2009, DiBiase married his high school sweetheart Leah Nicole May.
