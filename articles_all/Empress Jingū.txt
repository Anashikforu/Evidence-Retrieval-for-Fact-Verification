 Traditional history

Jingū was the principle wife of Emperor Chūai  Her son would become known as Emperor Ōjin.Aston (1998), pp. 217-223. The ''Gukanshō'' mentions her, but she is not included on the list of persons born within the line of Imperial descent.Brown, Delmer ''et al.'' (1979) 
 Events of Jingū's life
