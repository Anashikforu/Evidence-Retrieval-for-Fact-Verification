'''Kharan''' () is a Districts of Pakistan|district in the north-west of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province of Pakistan.

Administration
The district is administratively broken up into the following tehsils:

 Kharan Tehsil
 Sar Kharan Tehsil
 Toumulk Tehsil
 Besima Tehsil
 Mashkel Tehsil
 Nag Tehsil
 Wasuk Tehsil
