

'''''Will Wright''''' (born on January 20, 1960 in Atlanta, Georgia) is an United States|American video game game designer|developer and is the co-founder of Maxis, now a part of Electronic Arts. He's most famous for ''SimCity'', ''The Sims'' and Spore (video game)|Spore.  He is currently working on a new project, and has set up the "Stupid Fan Club".
