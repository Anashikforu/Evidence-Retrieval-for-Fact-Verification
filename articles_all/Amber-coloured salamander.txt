The '''amber-coloured salamander''' ('''''Hynobius stejnegeri''''') is a kind of salamander in the Hynobiidae family of animals.  It can only be found in Japan.  It likes to live in forests in rivers that are warm.  Because of habitat loss, it is losing its place to live.
