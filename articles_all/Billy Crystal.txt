


'''Billy Crystal''' (born March 14, 1948) is a Golden Globe Award-nominated and Emmy Award-winning United States|American actor, writer, movie producer|producer, comedian, singer, movie director and television host. He became famous in the 1970s for playing Jodie Dallas on the American Broadcasting Company|ABC Comedy-drama, ''Soap (TV series)|Soap''.  Crystal was a castmember of Saturday Night Live.  He became a Hollywood movie star during the late 1980s and the 1990s, appearing in the box office successes ''When Harry Met Sally...'' and ''City Slickers''.
