
 
'''''Up''''' is a 2009 American animated movie produced by Pixar and distributed by Walt Disney Pictures|Disney. It was first shown at the 2009 Cannes Film Festival. It was a successful movie and was one of the top movies for 2009. The main characters are boy named Russell, an old man named Carl Fredricksen, and Kevin the rare bird. They take an adventure to Venezuela on a flying house. The movie won two Academy Awards, including for Best Animated Feature and Best Original Score.
