
'''Schizanthus''' ('''butterfly flower''') is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family (biology)|family ''Solanaceae''. The genus is made up of twelve species: ''Schizanthus alpestris'', ''Schizanthus candidus'', ''Schizanthus grahamii'', ''Schizanthus hookerii'', ''Schizanthus integrifolius'', ''Schizanthus lacteus'', ''Schizanthus laetus'', ''Schizanthus litoralis'', ''Schizanthus parvulus'', ''Schizanthus pinnatus'', ''Schizanthus porrigens'' and ''Schizanthus tricolor''.
