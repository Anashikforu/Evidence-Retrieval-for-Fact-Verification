

'''Red Bull Racing''' is a Formula One racing team owned by Austrian beverage company Red Bull. Red Bull also own another F1 team: Scuderia Toro Rosso|Scuderia AlphaTauri. The Red Bull team is based in Milton Keynes in the United Kingdom|UK but holds an Austrian licence. The team is managed by Christian Horner who is also the boss of the Arden International, which participates to the GP2 Series and to the Formula Renault 3.5 Series.
