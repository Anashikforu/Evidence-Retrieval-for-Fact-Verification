

'''Sierre''' is the Capital (city)|capital of the Districts of Switzerland|district of Sierre (district)|Sierre in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Valais in Switzerland.

Image:Sierre from Crans Montana.JPG|thumb|left|Sierre seen from Crans-Montana.

It has a population of 14,355. It is situated at the language border of the German language|German-speaking upper part of the "''Wallis''" and the French language|French-speaking "''Valais''". This city counts as one of the three official bilingual towns in Switzerland. Sierre is starting point for the tourist sites of the Val d'Anniviers including the villages of Vercorin, Chandolin, St-Luc Vissoie, St-Jean Chandolin, Grimentz, Zinal and Ayer. Sierre is connected with Crans-Montana by a aerial tramway|cable car.

At the beginning of the 20th century, Sierre became economically important as very early aluminium smelting is enabled by its access to hydroelectricity.
