

'''Lorenzo "Jerry" Music''' was an Americans|American actor, voice actor, writer, producer and musician. He was born '''Gerald David Music''' on May 2, 1937, in Duluth, Minnesota.  He died in 2001.

Music was most famous as the voice of Carlton the Doorman in the television programme|television series ''Rhoda'', and as the cartoon voice of Garfield the Cat.
