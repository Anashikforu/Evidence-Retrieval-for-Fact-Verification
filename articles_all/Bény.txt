'''Bény''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department in the east of France. In 2009, 734 people lived in the commune. The inhabitants are the ''Bénéens''. The local economy is mostly based around agriculture. The most important monument in the commune is the Manoir de Marmont|Marmont manor house (''manoir''), listed as a ''monument historique'' since 1992.
