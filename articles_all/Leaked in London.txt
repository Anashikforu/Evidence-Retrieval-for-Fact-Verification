'''''Leaked in London''''' is a live Extended play|EP that was recorded by Fall Out Boy in 2007. The EP was made in response to their fourth album, ''Infinity on High'', being leaked onto the internet three weeks before its actual release date. Those who bought ''Infinity On High'' could download ''Leaked In London'' from Fall Out Boy's website by using the CDPass software and inserting the ''Infinity On High'' CD into their computer.
