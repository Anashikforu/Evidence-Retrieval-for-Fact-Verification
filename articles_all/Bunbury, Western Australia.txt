'''Bunbury''' is the third largest city in Western Australia, behind Perth, Western Australia|Perth and Mandurah, Western Australia|Mandurah, with a population of 54,967. It is 175 kilometres south of the Perth central business district|CBD. It was founded in 1836. Bunbury is connected to Perth via transport by the Transwa Australind passenger train.
