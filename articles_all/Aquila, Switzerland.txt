
'''Aquila''' is a former municipality of the district of Blenio (district)|Blenio in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 22 October 2006 the former municipalities of Aquila, Campo (Blenio), Ghirone, Olivone and Torre, Switzerland|Torre merged into the new municipality of Blenio.
