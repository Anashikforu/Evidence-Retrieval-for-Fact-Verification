
'''Sean James Pronger''' (born November 30, 1972 in Thunder Bay, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired ice hockey player who grew up in Dryden, Ontario|Dryden, Ontario, and has played in the National Hockey League for the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim, Pittsburgh Penguins, New York Rangers, Los Angeles Kings, Boston Bruins, Columbus Blue Jackets and the Vancouver Canucks.  He was wikt:drafted|drafted 51st overall by Vancouver and played in 260 regular season games, scoring 23 goals and 36 assists for 59 points, picking up 159 penalty minutes. He is also the brother of defenceman Chris Pronger.
