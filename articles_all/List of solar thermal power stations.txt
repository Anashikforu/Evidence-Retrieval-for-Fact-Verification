This is a '''list of solar thermal energy|solar thermal power stations'''. These include the 354&nbsp;megawatt (MW) Solar Energy Generating Systems power plant in the USA, Solnova solar power station (Spain, 150&nbsp;MW), Andasol solar power station (Spain, 100&nbsp;MW), Nevada Solar One (USA, 64&nbsp;MW), PS20 solar power tower (Spain, 20&nbsp;MW), and the PS10 solar power tower (Spain, 11&nbsp;MW). Many other plants are under construction or planned, mainly in Spain and the USA.REN21 (2008).
