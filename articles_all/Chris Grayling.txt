
'''Christopher Stephen Grayling''' (born 1 April 1962) is an England|English politician and former Secretary of State for Transportation. He is the Member of Parliament for the Epsom and Ewell (UK Parliament constituency)|Epsom and Ewell constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 2001|2001 general election.

He is a member of the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.

Privy Council
He was appointed as a Privy Council of the United Kingdom|Privy Counsellor on 9 June 2010.
