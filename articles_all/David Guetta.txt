
'''Pierre David Guetta''' (; born 7 November 1967) is a French people|French music producer, singer and DJ of Jewish descent. He originally was a DJ at nightclubs during the 1980s and 1990s. He later co-founded ''Gum Productions'' and published his first album, ''Just a Little More Love'', in 2001. Later, he published ''Guetta Blaster'' (2004) and ''Pop Life (David Guetta album)|Pop Life'' (2007). His 2009 dance-pop album ''One Love (David Guetta album)|One Love'' included the hit singles "When Love Takes Over" (wikt:featuring|featuring Kelly Rowland), "Gettin' Over You" (featuring Chris Willis, Fergie (singer)|Fergie & LMFAO (group)|LMFAO) and "Sexy Bitch" (featuring Akon). The latter became a top five hit in the US and all three reached number 1 in the UK. He has worked with many pop and hip-hop artists including Akon, Chris Willis, Kelly Rowland, Lil' Wayne, Kelis, Madonna (entertainer)|Madonna, Kid Cudi, Estelle (musician)|Estelle, Fergie, Amy Palmer, K'naan, will.i.am, LMFAO, Dane Bowers, Flo Rida, Shakira, Julie Mcknight and apl.de.ap.
