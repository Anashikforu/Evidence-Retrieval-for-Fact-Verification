

'''''Shadow of the Hegemon''''' (2001) is the second book in the ''Ender's Game series#Shadow series|Ender's Shadow'' series (also called the ''Bean Quartet'') by Orson Scott Card.  It is also the sixth book in the Ender's Game series#Ender series|Ender's Game series. It is told mostly from the point of view of Bean (Ender's Game)|Bean, a mostly minor character in the first novel ''Ender's Game''.
