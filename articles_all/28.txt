



'''28''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a leap year starting on Thursday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Silanus and Nerva'''.

 Events 
 King Daru of Baekje succeeded the throne of Baekje in the Korean peninsula.
 The Frisians make a treaty with the Roman Empire|Romans to avoid conquest.

 Births 
 Emperor Ming of Han (d. 75)

 Deaths 
King Onjo of Baekje
