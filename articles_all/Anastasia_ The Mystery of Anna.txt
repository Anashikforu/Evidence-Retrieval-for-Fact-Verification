
'''''Anastasia: The Mystery of Anna''''' (Sometimes called '''''Anastasia: The Story of Anna''''' It was Christian Bale's first movie and was originally broadcast in two parts.

 Plot 
The movie is about Anna Anderson, who believes that she is Grand Duchess Anastasia Nikolaevna of Russia|Anastasia, daughter of Nicholas II of Russia. Anna first tells her story in the 1920s when she is at a Berlin asylum. Her story of escape from the Bolsheviks who killed the rest of her family in 1918 seems so vivid that many Russians believe her. She slowly gains more wikt:trust|trust, but the Romanov family does not believe her. The movie never reveals if Anna really is Anastasia.
