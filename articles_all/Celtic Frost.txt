
'''Celtic Frost''' was a thrash metal|thrash/black metal band from Zurich, Switzerland. The band was formed in 1984 and broke up in 1993. The reunited in 2001 and broke up again in 2008.

 Discography 
Celtic Frost released 6 studio albums:
