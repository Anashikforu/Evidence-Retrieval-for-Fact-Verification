The '''Pentaport Rock Festival''' is a rock music|rock festival in South Korea. It is held each year in Incheon for three days in the last week of July.  Bands from many genres of music perform.

The name of this festival comes from five philosophy|philosophical themes of the festival: music, wikt:passion|passion, the environment, DIY (Do it yourself), and friendship.  The festival started in 2006
