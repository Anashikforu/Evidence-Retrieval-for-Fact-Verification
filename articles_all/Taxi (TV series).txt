
'''''Taxi''''' is an United States|American Situation comedy|sitcom. It originally aired from 1978 to 1982 on American Broadcasting Company|ABC, and from 1982 to 1983 on NBC. The show is about the employees of a taxi company in Manhattan, New York City.
