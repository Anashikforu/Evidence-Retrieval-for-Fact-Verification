
'''Kamer Genç''' (February 23, 1940 &ndash; January 22, 2016) was a Turkey|Turkish politician.He was a member of parliament as a member of the Republican People's Party (Turkey)|Republican People's Party in the 18th, 19th, and 23rd election cycles, and in the 20th and 21st as a member of the True Path Party. He was born in Nazımiye, Tunceli Province.

Genç died of cancer in Istanbul, Turkey on January 22, 2016 at the age of 75.
