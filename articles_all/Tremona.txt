
'''Tremona''' was a municipality of the district of Mendrisio (district)|Mendrisio in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 5 April 2009 the former municipalities of Arzo, Capolago, Genestrerio, Mendrisio, Rancate and Tremona merged into the municipality of Mendrisio.
