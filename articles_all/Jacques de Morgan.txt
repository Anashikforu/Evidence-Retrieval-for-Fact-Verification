'''Jacques de Morgan''' (1857-1924) was a France|French civil engineer, Geology|geologist and Archaeology|archaeologist.  He was the head of Antiquities in Egypt in the 19th century. He excavated in Memphis, Egypt|Memphis and Dashur, providing many drawings of many Egyptian pyramids.
