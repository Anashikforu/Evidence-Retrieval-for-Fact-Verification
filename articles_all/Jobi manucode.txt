

The '''Jobi manucode''' (''Manucodia jobiensis'') is a medium-sized Birds of Paradise|bird of paradise, that grows up to 34cm long. It looks greenish blue, black and purple glossed crow-like with red Iris (anatomy)|iris, lightly crinkled bluish short upper breast and neck feathers. They eat fruits and arthropods.

They can be found in lowland forests of Yapen|Jobi Island and northern New Guinea and is the most common bird of paradise.
