
'''William McChord Hurt''' (March 20, 1950 &ndash; March 13, 2022) was an Americans|American actor. He won an Academy Awards|Academy Award in 1985 for his work in the movie movie ''Kiss of the Spider Woman (movie)|Kiss of the Spider Woman''. He played Thaddeus Ross (Marvel Cinematic Universe)|Thaddeus Ross in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

In May 2018, Hurt was diagnosed with terminal illness|terminal prostate cancer. Hurt later died from the disease on March 13, 2022 at his home in Portland, Oregon, only one week before his 72nd birthday.
