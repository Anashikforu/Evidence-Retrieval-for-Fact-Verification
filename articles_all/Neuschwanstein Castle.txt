




'''Neuschwanstein Castle''' (, Southern Bavarian: ''Schloss Neischwanstoa''), which translates as New Swan Stone Castle, is a Romanesque architecture|Romanesque palace that was made in the 19th century by King Ludwig II of Bavaria. The palace is in Bavaria in southern Germany. It is one of the most famous sites among tourists.
