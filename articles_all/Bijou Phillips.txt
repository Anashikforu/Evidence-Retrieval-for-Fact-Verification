

'''Bijou Lilly Phillips''' (born April 1, 1980) is an Americans|American actress, fashion model (person)|model, and singer. Some of her larger movie roles include ''Bully'', ''Havoc'', ''Hostel: Part II'', and ''Tart''.

She is the daughter of singer John Phillips, of the 1970s group The Mamas & the Papas. Her sisters are actress Mackenzie Phillips and singer Chyna Phillips. She marriage|married actor Danny Masterson, of ''That '70s Show'' in 2011. She was previously engaged to singer Sean Lennon.

Phillips released an album ''I'd Rather Eat Glass'' in 2002. The title was based on her experience modeling in her teenager|teens.
