'''Steven Joshua Sondheim''' (March 22, 1930 &ndash; November 26, 2021) was an United States|American composer and Lyrics|lyricist.

Sondheim died on November 26, 2021 from heart disease in Roxbury, Connecticut at the age of 91.

 Career 
Sondheim wrote the words for songs in ''Gypsy: A Musical Fable|Gypsy'' and ''West Side Story''. Of American composers still living, he had won the most awards. He won:
Multiple Grammy Awards
9 Tony Awards (more than any other composer)
A Lifetime Achievement Tony
An Academy Award
A Pulitzer Prize.

Sondheim wrote both the music and the words to his musicals.
 
Many critics like his work, but some people do not like it. ''Sweeney Todd'' is very different from the music of Rodgers and Hammerstein.
