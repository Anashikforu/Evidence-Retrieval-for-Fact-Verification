
'''Eduardo Alonso Nájera Pérez''' (born July 11, 1976) is a Mexico|Mexican basketball player that currently plays for the Charlotte Bobcats of the National Basketball Association|NBA. He also played for the Dallas Mavericks for five seasons, the Golden State Warriors for one season, the Denver Nuggets for 4 seasons and the New Jersey Nets for two seasons. He went to University of Oklahoma for college.
