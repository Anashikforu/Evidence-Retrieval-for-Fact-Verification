
'''Ralph Macchio''' (born November 4, 1961 in Huntington, New York) is an Americans|American actor. He is best known for his roles in ''My Cousin Vinny, The Karate Kid'', ''The Karate Kid II'', and ''The Karate Kid III''. He was also in the movie ''The Outsiders'' as Johnny.
