

'''Appleby-in-Westmorland''' is a  market town and civil parishes in England|civil parish. It is in Eden, Cumbria, in North West England. It has a population of 3,048. It is in the Historic counties of England|historic county of Westmorland, of which it is the traditional county town. 

The town's name was simply Appleby, until the local government changes of 1974. The council changed the town's name, to preserve the historic county name.
