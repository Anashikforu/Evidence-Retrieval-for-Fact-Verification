The '''Springfield Falcons''' are an American professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1994. They currently play in the American Hockey League and are the farm team of the Columbus Blue Jackets of the National Hockey League|NHL. They play their home games in Springfield, Massachusetts at the MassMutual Center. Their Central Hockey League|CHL affiliate team is the Fort Wayne Komets.
