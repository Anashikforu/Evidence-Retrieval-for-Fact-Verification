

'''Rascal Flatts''' is a country music|country rock band from Columbus, Ohio. They became a band in 1999 and released their debut album ''Rascal Flatts'' in 2000. They are signed to Lyric Street records and have won many CMT and ACM awards.

Their newest album is ''Unstoppable'', which came to stores in 2009.
