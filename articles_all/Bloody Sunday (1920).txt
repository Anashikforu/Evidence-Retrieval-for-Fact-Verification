''' Bloody Sunday''' was a day of violence in Dublin, Ireland, United Kingdom, on November 21, 1920. It happened during the Irish War of Independence. In total, 31 people were killed. This included fourteen British people|British people, fourteen Irish people|Irish civilians and three Irish republicanism|republican prisoners.
