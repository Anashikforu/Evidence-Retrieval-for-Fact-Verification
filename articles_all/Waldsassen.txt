
'''Waldsassen''' is a town in Tirschenreuth (district)|Tirschenreuth in the Upper Palatinate in Bavaria.

Geography
Waldsassen is the northernmost municipality of the Upper Palatinate. In the northeast, it borders the town of Cheb (''Eger'') in the Czech Republic. The historic tripoint of noble Altbayern, the Franconian lands of Principality of Bayreuth|Bayreuth, and the Bohemian Egerland is near the village of Pechtnersreuth.


Twin towns and cities
