
'''''Need For Speed: Carbon''''' is an racing game by Electronic Arts that belongs to the Need for Speed|''Need for Speed'' series. It is the 12th game in the ''Need For Speed'' series and is available on many video game console|gaming systems such as the GameCube, Wii, Game Boy Advance, Nintendo DS, PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, Xbox, Xbox 360, and PC. It is the last in the series to be released for the GameCube and GBA.

Gameplay
''Need for Speed: Carbon'' is like ''Need for Speed: Most Wanted'', but the biggest change is that it is night time.
