
'''Andrea Bocelli'''  (born 22 September 1958) is an Italians|Italian tenor and crossover artist. He was blinded by a Association football|Football accident at the age of 12, and later graduated from law school, before beginning a career as a singer.

Since 1994, he has recorded both pop music|pop and Classical music|Classical albums, and has sold over 85 million copies worldwide. He is generally considered as both the most popular Italy|Italian and Classical music|Classical singer in the world, today.

On March 2, 2010, he was honoured with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
