'''Vanessa Anne Hudgens''' (born December 14, 1988) is an Americans|American actress and singer. She is most well known for starring in ''High School Musical'' and ''High School Musical 2'' on Disney Channel. She has won many awards and been nominated since ''High School Musical''. She has been on many TV shows and movies. She began acting and singing at an early age. She is now engaged with her long time boyfriend TITO, who is a professional guitarist|musician, performer & actor.

Early life
Hudgens was born in Salinas, California|Salinas, California, and has lived with her parents along the West Coast, from Oregon to Southern California. Her mother, Gina (née Guangco), held a succession of office jobs, and her father, Gregory Hudgens, was a firefighter. She has a younger sister, Stella Hudgens, who is also an actress. All of her grandparents were musicians.
