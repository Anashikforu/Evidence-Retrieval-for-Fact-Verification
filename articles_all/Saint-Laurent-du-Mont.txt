
'''Saint-Laurent-du-Mont''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Basse-Normandie in the Calvados (department)|Calvados Departments of France|department in the northwest of France. In 2016, 189 people lived there. On 1 January 2019, it became a part of the commune Cambremer.
