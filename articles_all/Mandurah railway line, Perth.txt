The '''Mandurah railway line''' is a suburban railway line in Perth, Western Australia|Perth, Australia. It was opened around Christmas in 2007. The line is a major transport route in Perth, connecting with the four other main Transperth railway lines: Joondalup railway line, Perth|Joondalup, Midland railway line, Perth|Midland, Fremantle railway line, Perth|Fremantle and Armadale railway line, Perth|Armadale.
