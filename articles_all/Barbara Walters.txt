'''Barbara Walters''' (born September 25, 1929) is an author and Broadcasting|broadcast journalist. 

Walters was the News presenter|news anchor at NBC's ''Today''. For 25 years Walters also Presenter|hosted the American Broadcasting Company|ABC show ''20/20''. She co-hosted the morning television show ''The View'' from 1997 to 2014. 

Walter's has interviewed every U.S. President since Richard Nixon.

Legacy
Walters has received 3 Daytime Emmy Awards. She has received Lifetime Achievement Awards for News and Documentary and daytime programs. She was inducted into the Television Hall of Fame in 1989. On June 15, 2007, Walters received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. 

In 1992 Walters received a Courage in Journalism Award for journalist|reporting in dangerous areas.
