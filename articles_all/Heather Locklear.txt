
'''Heather Locklear''' (born September 25, 1961) is an Americans|American actress. She has been in many movies and television series. Her most notable performance was starring as Amanda in ''Melrose Place''. She also played Sammy in ''Dynasty (TV series)|Dynasty'' and Caitlin in the sitcom ''Spin City''. Locklear was born in Los Angeles.

On June 24, 2018, after being released on bail for accounts of battery, Locklear overdosed on drugs and was hospitalized.
