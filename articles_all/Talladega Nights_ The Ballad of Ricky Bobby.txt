

'''''Talledega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby''''' is a 2006 United States|American sports movie|sports comedy movie that was produced by Adam McKay, Will Ferrell, Judd Apatow and Jimmy Miller and directed by Adam McKay. ''Talledega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby'' received positive reviews with a 72% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 66 out of 100 rating on Metacritic.
