

'''Shirley MacLaine''' (born April 24, 1934) is an Americans|American movie and theatre actress. She is famous for her acting as well as her strong belief in reincarnation. She is the writer of a many autobiography|autobiographical works, some about her New Age beliefs, as well as her Hollywood career. She is the older sister of Warren Beatty. She won the Academy Award for "Best Actress" in 1983, and has also received many other awards, including the Emmy, Golden Globe Award|Golden Globe and BAFTA Awards.
