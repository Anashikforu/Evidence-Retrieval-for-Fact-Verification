

The '''2005–06 NHL season''' was the 88th season of the National Hockey League. The 2006 Stanley Cup Playoffs|2006 Stanley Cup playoffs began on April 21 2006 and ended on June 19. The Carolina Hurricanes defeated the Edmonton Oilers. This was the first Stanley Cup for the Hurricanes.

The 2004-05 NHL season|2004–05 season was cancelled due to a 2004-05 NHL lockout|labour dispute with the NHL Players Association. The dispute was over the NHL Collective Bargaining Agreement|Collective Bargaining Agreement (CBA) between the league and its players.

 Tiebreaking procedures 

If two or more clubs are tied in points during the regular season, the order of the clubs is determined in the following order: 

# The fewer number of games played (i.e., superior points percentage).
# The greater number of games won.
# The greater number of points earned in games between the tied clubs.
# The greater differential between goals for and against.
