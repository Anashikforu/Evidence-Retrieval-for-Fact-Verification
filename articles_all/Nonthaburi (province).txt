'''Nonthaburi''' is a province in Thailand. Its central town is Nonthaburi (city)|Nonthaburi,  about 25&nbsp;km north-west of Bangkok. There are many government buildings in Nonthaburi.
Tourists and locals head to Wat Poramai-yikawat  -- a mon styled temple, and Wat Sanam Nua temple for worship.
