'''Coutinho''' (11 June 1943 &ndash; 11 March 2019) was a former Brazilian Football (soccer)|football player. He mostly played for Santos F.C. and the Brazil national football team|Brazil national team.

Coutinho died on March 11, 2019, in Santos, São Paulo from a heart attack due to diabetes and hypertension, aged 75.
