'''Mount Agung''' () is a volcano on Bali. Mount Agung last Volcanic eruption|erupted in 1963-1964. The lava just missed the Mother Temple of Besakih, which is located on high on the slopes of this Sacred site|sacred mountain. Over 1,000 people were killed and a number of villages were destroyed in this eruption.

Gunung Agung is an active volcano, with a large and deep crater that occasionally emits smoke and ash. The last major eruptions 2017 eruption of Mount Agung|occurred in November 2017.
