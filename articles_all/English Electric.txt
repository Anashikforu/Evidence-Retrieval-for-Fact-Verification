 
'''English Electric''' (EE) was a United Kingdom|British industrial manufacturer. Founded in 1918, it initially specialised in industrial electric motors and transformers. Its activities would expand to include railway locomotives and Railway electric traction|traction equipment, steam turbines, consumer electronics, guided missiles, aircraft and computers. 

English Electric Diesel Engines
'''English Electric Diesel Engines''' was the diesel engine division of English Electric.  It built diesel engines under the names '''English Electric''', '''Napier''' and '''Dorman'''.
