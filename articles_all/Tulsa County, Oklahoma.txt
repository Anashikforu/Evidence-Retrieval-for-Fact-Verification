
'''Tulsa County''' is a county in the U.S. state of Oklahoma.  As of 2000, 563,299 people lived there. The county seat is Tulsa, Oklahoma|Tulsa. It has more people than any other county in Oklahoma.

 Geography 
The county has a total area of 587 square miles.
