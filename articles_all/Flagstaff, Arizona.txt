
'''Flagstaff''' is a city in the northern part of Arizona in the United States. It is the county seat of Coconino County, Arizona|Coconino County.  About 58,200 people were living in Flagstaff in 2006. Flagstaff has an area of 63.6 square miles. It has a humid continental climate (''Dfb'' in the Koeppen climate classification).
