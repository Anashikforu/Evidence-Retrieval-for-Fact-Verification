'''Johann Andreas Wagner''' (21 March 1797 &ndash; 17 December 1861) was a Germany|German palaeontologist, zoologist and archaeologist.

Wagner was a professor at the University of Munich. He was also the  of the Zoologische Staatssammlung (the German State Zoology Collection). He was the author of ''Die Geographische Verbreitung der Säugethiere Dargestellt'' (1844-46).
