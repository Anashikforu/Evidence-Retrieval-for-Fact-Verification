
'''Stavropoleos Monastery''' or the '''Stavropoleos Church''' (''), is an Eastern Orthodox monastery for nuns in central Bucharest|Bucharest, Romania that was built in the year 1724. The monastery was named after the Romanian version of a Greek language|Greek word, ''Stauropolis'', which means "The city of the Cross".
