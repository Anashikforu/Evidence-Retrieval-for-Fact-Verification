
'''''Footloose''''' is a 1984 United States|American musical movie|musical drama movie. It stars Kevin Bacon and was movie director|directed by Herbert Ross. Lori Singer, Dianne Wiest and John Lithgow are co-stars.

It was made into a Musical theater|musical on Broadway in 1999. A movie remake Footloose (2011 movie)|came out in 2011.
