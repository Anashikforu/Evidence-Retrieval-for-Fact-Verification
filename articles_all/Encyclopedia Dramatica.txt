
'''Encyclopædia Dramatica''' is a website that stored mostly articles that make fun of people or things. The pages satire|satirize current events, and many events on the internet.--> Encyclopædia Dramatica has been described as a "wikt:snarky|snarky (sarcastic and nasty) Wikipedia anti-fansite".

The site is a wiki that runs on MediaWiki software. Many articles are written in a Satire|satirical way to upset those who take the content seriously.
