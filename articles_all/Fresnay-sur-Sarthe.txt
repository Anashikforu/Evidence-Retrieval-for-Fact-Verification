

'''Fresnay-sur-Sarthe''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Pays de la Loire in the Sarthe Departments of France|department in the west of France. On 1 January 2019, the former communes of Coulombiers, Sarthe|Coulombiers and Saint-Germain-sur-Sarthe were merged into Fresnay-sur-Sarthe.
