The '''1910–11 NHA season''' was the second Season (sport)|season of the former National Hockey Association. Five teams played 16 games each, starting on December 31, 1910 and ending on March 10, 1911. The Ottawa Senators (original)|Ottawa Hockey Club won the league championship and took over the Stanley Cup from the Montreal Wanderers.
