'''Limousin''' is a former administrative region of France. It is now part of the administrative region of Nouvelle-Aquitaine. Its capital city|capital was Limoges. With 42 people per square kilometer, this was one of the least populated regions. About 710,000 people lived on roughly .

The departments in the region were Corrèze, Creuse, and Haute-Vienne.

The local economy had been recentered around services.
