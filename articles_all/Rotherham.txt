


'''Rotherham''' is a town in South Yorkshire, England. It lies on the River Don, South Yorkshire|River Don, close to its confluence with the  River Rother, South Yorkshire|River Rother, between Sheffield and Doncaster. Its football club, Rotherham United F.C., was founded in 1870.
