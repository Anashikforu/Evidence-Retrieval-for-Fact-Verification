



'''Twelve''' is a number. It comes between eleven and thirteen, and it is an even number. It is divisible by 1 (number)|1, Two|2, Three|3, Four|4, Six|6, and 12. Its ordinal is 12th (twelfth). In Roman numerals, it is written as '''XII'''. It is the base (mathematics)|base of duodecimal numbers. It is often called '''dozen'''.

12 is also referred to as, The Twelve Imams or the Twelve Apostles of Christ 




Mathematics
 Twelve is the first abundant number.
