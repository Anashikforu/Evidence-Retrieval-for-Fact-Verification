
'''James Bradley McLeod''' (April 8, 1937 – May 18, 2019) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender. McLeod started his career with the St. Louis Blues (ice hockey)|St. Louis Blues in the National Hockey League during the 1971–72 NHL season|1972. Later in his career he played in the World Hockey Association for the Chicago Cougars, Los Angeles Sharks, Michigan Stags, and New York Golden Blades.
