
The '''Nhật Lệ River''' is a river in Đồng Hới City, Quảng Bình Province, Vietnam. 

 Appearance 
The Nhat Le River is 152&nbsp;km long and splits up into other rivers such as the Kien Giang River, which is 58&nbsp;km in length, and the Long Dai River, which it is 77&nbsp;km long.
