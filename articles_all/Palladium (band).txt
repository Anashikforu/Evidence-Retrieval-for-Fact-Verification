They were soon signed to Virgin Records. The deal being signed whilst they were on tour in Japan.
Through their live shows Palladium have gained many fans, including Amy Winehouse, Mystery Jets and Get Cape, Wear Cape, Fly.
Their debut single, "Happy Hour", was released on 23 July 2007.
They have recorded their debut album, ''The Way It's Not'', with Tony Hoffer (The Kooks, Beck Hansen|Beck, The Fratellis). However, according to HMV and Amazon.co.uk it is now expected to be released on 17 March 2008.
They were announced as main support for Mika (singer)|Mika's late 2007 UK tour.

Musical Style

Palladium get ideas from a wide range of music. Pink Floyd, The Police, Toto (band)|Toto, Fleetwood Mac, Stevie Wonder and Hall and Oates have all been mentioned by the band. They have also played at Godolphin and Latymer School in Hammersmith.
