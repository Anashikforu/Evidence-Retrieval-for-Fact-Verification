A '''graduated cylinder''' (also called '''measuring cylinder''') is a piece of laboratory glassware used to measurement|measure the volume of liquids. It is used to wikt:accuracy|accurately measure the volume of chemicals for use in Chemical reaction|reactions. 

Often, the biggest graduated cylinders are made of polyethylene or other stiff plastic, making them lighter and less fragile than glass, but more difficult to sterilization (microbiology)|sterilize.
