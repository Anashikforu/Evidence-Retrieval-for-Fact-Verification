

'''Forecasting''' is studying and saying what is likely to happen in the future. It is similar to prediction|predicting, but usually forecasting is done with science|scientific Scientific method|methods. Forecasting can be done for many different things, like weather forecasting (predicting the weather) or economy forecasting.  Science cannot know the future for sure, so forecasters try to identify the most probable events, and sometimes they are wrong.
