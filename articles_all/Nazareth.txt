

'''Nazareth''' ('') is the capital (city)|capital city of the north district of Israel. Most of the people in this city are Arabs (who are either Muslim or Christianity|Christian). There are also some Jewish people. The city is very old and is also famous for being the city where it is believed Jesus grew up in the care of Mary (mother of Jesus)|Mary and Saint Joseph|Joseph.
