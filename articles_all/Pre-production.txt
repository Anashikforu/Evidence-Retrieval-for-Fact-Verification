'''Pre-production''' is the time before making a movie, Play (theatre)|play, or other performance when all the things that will be needed to make it are prepared.

In the movie industry, pre-production usually only starts when the screenplay|script is ready, the studio has agreed to make it and money has been made available.  By this time, a project will almost always have a director (movie)|movie, a movie producer|producer, cast members and a production team.
