
'''Group 16''' is the third last group in the periodic table. This group is called the '''oxygen family''' or the '''chalcogens'''. The elements in this group are oxygen, sulfur, selenium, tellurium, polonium and livermorium. The first two elements are nonmetal|nonmetals, elements are in between are metalloids and  the last two are semimetal|metals  Polonium and livermorium are radioactive. Oxygen (as O2) is a colorless gas.
