


'''Jill Scott''' (born April 4, 1972) is an Americans|American poet, actress, soul music|soul and R&B singer and songwriter. She is from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Scott has recorded three albums. She has also acted in some movies and television series. These include ''Hounddog'', ''Why Did I Get Married?'' and ''The No. 1 Ladies' Detective Agency''.
