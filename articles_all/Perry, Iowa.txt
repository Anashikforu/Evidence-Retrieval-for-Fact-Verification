
'''Perry''' is a city in Iowa in the United States.  It is in Dallas County, Iowa|Dallas County.  It is by the Raccoon River|North Raccoon River. There were 7,702 people living in Perry at the 2010 census.

Tyson Foods operates a pork plant in Perry.
