
'''"Three Men and a Comic Book"''' is the twenty-first episode of the second season of ''The Simpsons''. It first started on the Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox network in the United States on May 9, 1991. In this episode, Bart catches a quick look of the wikt:original|original issue of "Radioactive Man" for sale at a comic book wikt:convention|convention. So Bart, Martin, and Milhouse wikt:combine|combine their money to buy the wikt:valuable|valuable comic book, only to lose it because to their selfishness and being unable to share.
