

'''''Guitar Hero: World Tour''''' is a music game that is part of the Guitar Hero (series)|''Guitar Hero'' franchise. The game was released in North America on October 27 2008. This is the first Guitar Hero game that allows players to play drums and sing vocals. There are 85 songs that come on the disc.  It will also allow players to make their own characters and songs. On the PS3, Xbox 360, and Wii versions, you can download songs online.
