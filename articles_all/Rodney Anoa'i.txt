He is best known for wrestling for the WWE|World Wrestling Federation under the ring name '''Yokozuna'''. He is a member of the famous Anoa'i family, Dwayne Johnson|The Rock, Rikishi (wrestler)|Rikishi, Samu, Rosey, Manu, and Eddie Fatu|Umaga were his cousins. He was a two time WWE Championship|WWF Champion and two-time WWE Tag Team Championship|Tag Team Champion and also won the 1993 Royal Rumble.

On March 31, 2012, he was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame by The Usos|Jimmy and The Usos|Jey Uso and Solofa Fatu|Rikishi.

Death
On October 23, 2000, Anoa'i died at the age of 34 from pulmonary edema in his room at the Moat House Hotel in Liverpool, England while touring for an independent wrestling show.
