'''Aubenas''' is a city and commune in France|commune in the southern part of the Ardèche ''département in France|département''.  The Ardèche River flows through the commune.

Twin towns
Aubenas is twinned with:
  Cesenatico, Italy
  Delfzijl, Netherlands
  Schwarzenbeck, Germany
  Sierre, Switzerland
  Zelzate, Belgium
  Palamos, Spain (friendship agreement)
