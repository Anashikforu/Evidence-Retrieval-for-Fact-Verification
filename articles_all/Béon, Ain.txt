
'''Béon''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department in the east of France. In 2009, 352 people lived in the commune. The inhabitants are the ''Béonais''. The most important monuments in the commune are the ''Château de Béon'', a 19th-century castle, and the ruins of the ''Château de Luyrieu'', a feudal castle. The commune is twinned with Béon, Yonne|Béon in the Yonne département.
