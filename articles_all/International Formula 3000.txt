'''Formula 3000''' was a type of formula racing|formula racing, below Formula One and above Formula Three. It was named because the cars were powered by 3000&nbsp;cc engines. The '''International Formula 3000''' was introduced by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA) in 1985 to replace Formula Two, and was itself replaced by the GP2 Series in 2005.
