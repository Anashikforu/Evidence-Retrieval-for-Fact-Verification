A '''valve''' is a device that it is used to decrease or stop the flow of a fluid (like gases, liquids, fluidization|fluidized solids, or slurry|slurries). It can be installed for example in a pipe or a vessel.

There are many types of valves. Some examples are:
 globe valve
 gate valve
 butterfly valve
 safety valve.

They can be operated manually or automatically.
