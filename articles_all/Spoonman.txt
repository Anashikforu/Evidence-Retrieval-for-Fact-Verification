

"'''Spoonman'''" is a song by the United States|American grunge music|grunge band Soundgarden, from their fourth studio album ''Superunknown'', which was released in 1994. The song describes Artie the Spoonman, a real-life musician who makes music with spoons. He was also featured in the song's music video. "Spoonman" won the Grammy Award for Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance|Best Metal Performance at the 37th Annual Grammy Awards|1995 Grammy Awards.
