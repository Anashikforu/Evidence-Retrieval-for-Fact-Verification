The '''Oklahoma Panhandle''' is the far west part of the state of Oklahoma. It includes Cimarron County, Oklahoma|Cimarron County, Texas County, Oklahoma|Texas County, and Beaver County, Oklahoma|Beaver County. Its name comes from the shape that looks like the panhandle|handle of a Frying pan|cooking pan.
