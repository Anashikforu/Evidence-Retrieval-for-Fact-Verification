
'''Ekiga''' (formerly called '''GnomeMeeting''') is a free software|free and open source software|open source VoIP and video conferencing computer application for GNOME and Microsoft Windows|Windows. Ekiga supports both the Session Initiation Protocol|SIP and H.323 (based on OpenH323) protocols and is fully interoperable with any other SIP compliant application and with Microsoft NetMeeting. It supports many high-quality audio and video codecs.
