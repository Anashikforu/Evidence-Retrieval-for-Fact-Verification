'''Wale Victor Folarin''' (born '''Olubowale Victor Akintimehin'''; September 21, 1984 in Washington D.C.) is an Americans|American rapper better known by his stage name, '''Wale'''. 

In 2010 Wale was nominated for Best New Artist from both the Soul Train Awards and BET Awards but did not win.
