
'''Lawrence Taylor''' (February 4, 1959) is a retired Americans|American American football|football player who played for the New York Giants in the National Football League (NFL). Taylor was famous for sack (football)|sacking the other team's quarterbacks. After he retired he got in trouble for doing drugs. He has since stopped doing drugs. He also appeared on Dancing With the Stars in the 2009 season.
