
'''''The Return of Jafar''''' is a 1994 direct-to-video animation|animated movie, released on VHS by Buena Vista Pictures, and the sequel to the 1992 Disney animated movie ''Aladdin (1992 movie)|Aladdin''. The movie is about Jafar attempting to get revenge on Aladdin.
