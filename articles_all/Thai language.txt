

The '''Thai language''' is the national language|national and official language|official language of Thailand. It is the first language of the Thai people, Thailand's main ethnic group. Thai is closely related to the Lao language. In fact, some Dialect|dialects of Thai and Lao are Mutual intelligibility|mutually intelligible, meaning that speakers of one language can talk with and understand speakers of the other language without knowing the other's language.
