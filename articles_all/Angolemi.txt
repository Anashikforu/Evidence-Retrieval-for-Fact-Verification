
'''Angolemi''' (Turkish language|Turkish: ''Taşpınar'' or ''Angolem'' - the old name) is a village in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus. Its population is 212. Its area is 6.727&nbsp;km2. It is a village of Lefka Municipality. It is in the Nicosia District (Republic of Cyprus)|Nicosia District of the Republic of Cyprus and Güzelyurt District of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.
