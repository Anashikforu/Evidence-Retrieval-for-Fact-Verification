'''Shōnen''' is a type of manga or anime meant for young teen boys usually between the ages of about 12 and 18. It often refers to manga. Examples include ''Dragon Ball'', ''Bleach (manga)|Bleach'', ''One Piece'', ''InuYasha'', ''Naruto (manga)|Naruto'', ''YuYu Hakusho'', ''Yu-Gi-Oh!'', and  Fairy Tail

Shounen is usually noticed by its high amounts of fighting. It often has comedy|humorous plots with male protagonist|characters. It often has themes of friendship. sentai|Fighting teams and similar groups are often shown. Physical attractiveness|Attractive  female Fictional character|characters are also common, but not always present. The art design|style of shounen is usually less pretty than shōjo.

Beyond shōnen manga, manga for men (age 18+) is called seinen manga. Very few seinen manga have been published outside of Japan. In Japan, many older men read shounen magazines. Because of this, in Japan, shounen manga magazines are the most popular manga magazines.
