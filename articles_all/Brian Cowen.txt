
'''Brian Cowen''' (born 10 January 1960) was the Taoiseach (prime minister) of the Republic of Ireland.  He became the taoiseach on 7 May 2008, after Bertie Ahern, he resigned after losing a general election on 9 March 2011. He was the leader of a political party called Fianna Fáil. They were in a coalition government with the Green Party (Ireland)|Green Party and the Progressive Democrats. Independent (politician)|Independent Teachta Dála|TDs (Irish members of parliament) also support his government.
