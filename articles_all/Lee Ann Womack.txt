
'''Lee Ann Womack''' (born August 19, 1966)  is an Americans|American country music singer and songwriter. Womack has won a number of music awards for her music and singing.

Her 2000 single, "I Hope You Dance (song)|I Hope You Dance" was a major crossover (music)|crossover music hit.  It reached #1 on the Hot Country Songs|Billboard Country Chart.  It made the Top 15 of the Billboard Hot 100.
