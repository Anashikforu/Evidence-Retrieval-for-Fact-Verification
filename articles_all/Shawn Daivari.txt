

'''Shawn Daivari''' (born April 30, 1984) is an Iranian-United States|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler  who currently works for Ring of Honor under his name, '''Shawn Daivari'''. He has also wrestled for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) as '''Daivari''' and for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) as '''Sheik Abdul Bashir'''.
