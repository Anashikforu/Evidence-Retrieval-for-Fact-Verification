

'''Benno von Arent''' (July 19, 1898 in Görlitz - October 14, 1956 in Bonn) was a member of the Germany|German NSDAP|Nazi Party and SS, responsible for art, theatres, movies etc.

Von Arendt worked as a Theatre|stage designer; he joined the SS in 1931 and the NSDAP in 1932. The same year, he was one of the people who started the "''Bund nationalsozialistischer Bühnen- und Filmkünstler''" ("Trade union|Union of Nazi|national-socialist stage and movie artists"), which was later called "''Kameradschaft deutscher Künstler''" ("fellowship of Germany|German artists") after Adolf Hitler|Hitler began to rule Germany in 1933. Von Arent was made "''Reichsbühnenbildner''" ("Reich stage designer") in 1936 and "''Reichsbeauftragter für die Mode''" ("Reich wikt:agent|agent for fashion") in 1939. In 1944, he was given the Rank (military)|rank of ''SS-Oberführer'' (senior colonel).

Von Arent died in 1956, aged 58.
