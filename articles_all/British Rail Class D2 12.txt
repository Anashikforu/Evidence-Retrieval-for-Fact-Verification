
'''British Rail Class D2/12''' was a locomotive commissioned by British Rail in England. It was a Diesel engine|diesel powered locomotive in the pre-TOPS period built by Hudswell Clarke with a L. Gardner and Sons Ltd.|Gardner engine.  The mechanical transmission, using a scoop control fluid coupling and four-speed ''Power-flow SSS'' (synchro-self-shifting) gearbox, was a Hudswell Clarke speciality.
