
'''Johns Creek''' is a city in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. It was only made a city on December 1, 2006. It is a suburb in the northeast of Atlanta. Johns Creek has about 75,000 people living in it. It is a part of Fulton County, Georgia|Fulton County. Johns Creek is the 10th largest city in Georgia.
