

'''Marc  Staal''' (born January 13, 1987 in Thunder Bay, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Defenceman (ice hockey)|defenceman with the New York Rangers of the National Hockey League. He is the younger brother of Eric Staal of the Minnesota Wild, and older brother of Jordan Staal of the Pittsburgh Penguins and Jared Staal, who was signed by Eric's team, the Hurricanes but is currently playing for the Ontario Hockey League|OHL's Sudbury Wolves.
