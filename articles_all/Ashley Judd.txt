
'''Ashley Judd''' (born '''Ashley Tyler Ciminella''' on April 19, 1968) is an Americans|American Actor|actress. She is famous for her roles in a series of late 1990s and early 2000s Thriller (genre)|thriller movies, including ''Kiss the Girls (film)|Kiss the Girls'', ''Double Jeopardy (film)|Double Jeopardy'' and ''High Crimes''.
