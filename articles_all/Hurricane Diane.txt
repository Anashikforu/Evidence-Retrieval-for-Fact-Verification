'''Hurricane Diane''' was really devastating to the East Coast of the United States|US during 1955. The storm caused severe destruction and an unusually severe death toll. Diane was among several hurricanes which bashed the East Coast during 1955, the others being Connie and Ione. The name '''Diane''' was later retired.
