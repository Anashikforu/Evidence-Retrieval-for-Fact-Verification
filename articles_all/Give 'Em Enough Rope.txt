

'''''Give 'Em Enough Rope''''' is the second album by The Clash. It was released in 1978. In the United States, this was their first official album because it was released before the U.S. version of ''The Clash (album)|The Clash''.  The album was well liked and got to number 2 in the United Kingdom|UK chart, and number 128 in the U.S.

 Track listing 
All songs were written by Joe Strummer and Mick Jones (The Clash)|Mick Jones, except where noted. All songs were Arrangement|arranged by The Clash. All singer|lead vocals were by Strummer, except "Stay Free" by Jones.
