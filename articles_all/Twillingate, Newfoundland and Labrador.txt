'''Twillingate''' is a town that can be found on the Twillingate Islands off the coast of Newfoundland. About 2500 people live in the town. It is known for its scenic landscape and friendly hospitality. Twillingate is about an hour and a half north of Gander, Newfoundland and Labrador|Gander and an hour north of Lewisporte, Newfoundland and Labrador|Lewisporte.

Twillingate's economy is based around the fishery.
