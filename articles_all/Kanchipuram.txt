'''Kanchipuram''' is one of the seven most Holy city|holy cities of Hindus. It is about  southwest of Chennai, Tamil Nadu state, India. Kanchipuram was an important Religion|religious and Culture|cultural place of Ancient India. The city continues to be an important religious place. 


For a long time, from the 6th century till the 8th century, the city was capital (city)|capital city of the Pallava dynasty.

There are many temples in the city.
There is a Math (religious centre) of Shankaracharya in this city.

Kanchipuram silk sarees are famous
