

'''Macedonian''' (македонски јазик, ''makedonski jazik'') is a Slavic language spoken mainly in North Macedonia. Macedonian is a part of the Balkan linguistic union, which also includes Greek language|Greek, Bulgarian language|Bulgarian, Romanian language|Romanian, Albanian language|Albanian and Torlakian dialect of the Serbian language. Macedonian is very similar to Bulgarian and some linguists say it is a dialect of Bulgarian.
