

'''"Genie in a Bottle"''' is a 1999 pop music|pop song by United States|American singer Christina Aguilera, from her first album ''Christina Aguilera (album)|Christina Aguilera''. It was her first hit single and got to number one on the Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100. Aguilera got a Grammy Award nomination for it and a Grammy Award for Best New Artist later in the year.
