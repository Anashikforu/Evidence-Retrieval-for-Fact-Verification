

'''Schwarzenberg''' (German pronunciation: Help:IPA/Standard German|ʃvaɐ̯t͡sənbɛɐ̯k) is a town in the rural district of Erzgebirgskreis in Saxony, Germany. The town was built in the 12th century. It is located about 15&nbsp;km southeast of Aue, and 35&nbsp;km southwest of Chemnitz.
