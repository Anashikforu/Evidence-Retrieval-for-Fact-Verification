

'''Aleknagik''' (; ''Alaqnaqiq'' in Yupik language|Central Yup'ik) is a city in the US State|state of Alaska in the United States. It is in the Dillingham Census Area, Alaska|Dillingham Census Area of the Unorganized Borough, Alaska|Unorganized Borough. In 2010, there were 219 people living there.
