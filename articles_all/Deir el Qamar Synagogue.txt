The '''Deir el Qamar Synagogue''' is a synagogue in Deir el Qamar, Lebanon; it is the oldest synagogue in Mount Lebanon Governorate|Mount Lebanon. The synagogue was built in the seventeenth century to serve the local Jewish Lebanese population. Some of them were under the rule of Emir Fakhreddine II.
