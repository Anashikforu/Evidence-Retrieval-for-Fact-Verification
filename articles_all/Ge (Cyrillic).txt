'''Geh''' ('''Г, г''') is the fourth letter of the Cyrillic alphabet. It is said as g or h.

It comes from the Greek language|Greek Gamma (letter)|gamma. Its old name was ''glagoli''. It stands for the Roman alphabet|Roman G.
