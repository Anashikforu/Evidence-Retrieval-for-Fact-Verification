
'''Jacobabad District''' () is a Districts of Pakistan|district of Sindh, Pakistan. According to the 1998 census, 1,425,572 people lived there, of which 24.10% were urban. The city of Jacobabad is the capital of the District.

Administration
The district is administratively subdivided into the following tehsil|talukas:

 Ghari Khairo Tehsil
 Khairo Tehsil
 Jacobabad Tehsil
 Thull Tehsil
