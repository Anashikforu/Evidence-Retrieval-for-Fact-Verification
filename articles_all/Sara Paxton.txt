
'''Sara Paxton''' (born April 25, 1988, California) is an Americans|American actor|actress and singer. She is best known for her role as Mari Collingwood in ''The Last House on the Left''. She is also known for acting in the movies, ''Aquamarine (movie)|Aquamarine'', ''Halloweentown 3''. She has starred in her own television show called ''Darcy's Wildlife''. Her, mother is a Mexico|Mexican Jew and her father, is an United States|American who converted to Judaism when marrying her mother.
