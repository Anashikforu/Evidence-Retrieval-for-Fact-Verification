
'''Angoulême''' () is a Communes of France|commune in southwestern France in the Charente Departments of France|department, Nouvelle-Aquitaine Regions of France|region, of which it is its Prefectures in France|prefecture.

It was the capital of the old province of '''Angoumois'''.

 Administration 
Angoulême is the Prefectures in France|prefecture of the Charente department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of Arrondissement of Angoulême|Angoulême and the administrative centre () of three cantons:
 Canton of Angoulême-1|Angoulême-1 formed by the ''commune'' Fléac and part of Angoulême, with 18,498 inhabitants (2014).
 Canton of Angoulême-2|Angoulême-2 formed by the ''commune'' L'Isle-d'Espagnac and part of Angoulême, with 18,990 inhabitants (2014).
 Canton of Angoulême-3|Angoulême-3 formed by the ''commune'' Soyaux and part of Angoulême, with 22,883 inhabitants (2014).

It is part of the Communes of France|intercommunality ''Grand Angoulême'' ().
