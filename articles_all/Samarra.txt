
'''Sāmarrā''' () is a town in Iraq. It is on the east bank of the Tigris in the Salah ad Din Governorate, 125&nbsp;km north of Baghdad. In 2003, the town had an estimated population of 348,700 people. 

Samarra is a city of culture and heritage. Samarra is home to the Malwiya Mosque Minerat.
