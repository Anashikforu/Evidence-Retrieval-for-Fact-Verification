

The '''Romanian War of Independence''' was battled in 1877.  It was against the Ottoman Empire. On , Kingdom of Romania|Romania and the Russian Empire wikt:sign|signed a treaty that allowed Russian troops to pass through Romanian land, so long as the Russians wikt:respect|respected the wikt:integrity|integrity of the Romanians.

On , in the Romanian parliament, Mihail Kogălniceanu said that it was the will of the Romanian people to be independent.  In the beginning of this war, Russia did not want to help the Romanians with the war.

War

The Romanian Army defeated the Turks in all the battles. The battles took place in Grivitsa, Rahova, Vidin and Smârdan.
