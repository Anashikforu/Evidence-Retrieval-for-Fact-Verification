
The '''2011 Myanmar earthquake''' was a 6.8 magnitude earthquake on March 24 in Shan State, Myanmar. Its hypocenter was  deep. It had two aftershocks, one of magnitude 4.8, another at magnitude 5.4.

Casualties
''The Bangkok Post'' reported on 27 March 2011, that the death toll was "over 150", which is double the official figure of 75.
