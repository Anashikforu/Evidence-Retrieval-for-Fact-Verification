
'''Elimination Chamber''' is a professional wrestling match held by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). One or two matches have taken place each year at the pay-per-view (PPV) event, also called Elimination Chamber (or '''No Escape''' in Germany), since 2010.
