
'''JC Chasez''' (born '''Joshua Scott Chasez''' on August 8, 1976 in Washington, D.C.), is an Americans|American singer, songwriter, actor, and record producer|producer. He is best known as one of the lead singers in the boy band NSYNC. On February 24 2004 Chasez's solo album ''Schizophrenic'' was released. Three singles were released from the album. Chasez made a girl group called Girl Radical with Jimmy Harry.
