'''Gwen Ren√©e Stefani''' is an Americans|American singer, songwriter, fashion designer, actress and television presenter. She was born on October 3, 1969 in Fullerton, California|Fullerton, California. She was raised in Anaheim, California. She has millions of fans around the world. She is a member of the band No Doubt, and has also released two solo albums. One of her biggest hits is ''Hollaback Girl''.

 Personal life 
On September 14, 2002 Gwen married Gavin Rossdale, the singer of Bush (band)|Bush. They have two sons, Kingston James McGregor Rossdale (born on May 26, 2006) and Zuma Nesta Rock Rossdale (born on August 21, 2008). On September 4, 2013 it was reported that Stefani was pregnant with her third child.  

Stefani says as a child she was diagnosed with dyslexia and attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder. On October 20, 2014, Stefani released the first single from her third album, "Baby Don't Lie".

As of 2017, Stefani is said to be dating her "The Voice (TV series)|The Voice" co-star Blake Shelton.
