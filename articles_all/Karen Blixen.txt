
Baroness '''Karen Christenze von Blixen-Finecke''' (born '''Dinesen'''; 17 April 1885&nbsp;– 7 September 1962) was a Danish author who wrote works in Danish and English. She is also known under her pen names '''Isak Dinesen''', used in English-speaking countries, '''Tania Blixen''', used in German-speaking countries, '''Osceola''', and '''Pierre Andrézel'''.
