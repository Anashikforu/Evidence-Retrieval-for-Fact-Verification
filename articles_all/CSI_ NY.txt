

'''''CSI: NY''''' (also known as '''''Crime Scene Investigation: New York''''') is an United States|American Police procedural|crime drama television series that was created by Anthony E. Zuiker, Carol Mendelsohn and Ann Donahue. ''CSI: NY'' debuted on September 22, 2004 on CBS. It is a second spin-off of the original series, ''CSI: Crime Scene Investigation''. It also debuted on CTV Television Network|CTV in Canada.
