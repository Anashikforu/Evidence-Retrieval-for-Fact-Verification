The '''Nissan Micra''' is a subcompact car produced by Nissan Motor Company since 1982. It entered its second generation in 1992, then its third generation in 2002, and its fourth generation in 2010. It can have three or five doors and three or four cylinder engines. The Micra is called the March in some countries including Japan.
