The '''High Middle Ages''' was the part of history of Europe|European history in the 11th, 12th, and 13th centuries (AD 1000–1299). Before the High Middle Ages came the Early Middle Ages (from the 5th century to approximately AD 1000). After the High Middle Ages came the Middle Ages#Late Middle Ages|Late Middle Ages (about AD 1300–1500).
