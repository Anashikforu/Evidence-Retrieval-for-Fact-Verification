The '''Flaming Gorge Dam''' is a concrete-arch dam on the Green River in Utah, in the United States. Built in 1958-1964, the dam is  long, and covers 42,000 acres (170 square kilometers). The dam was built for water storage and hydroelectric power, and it produces about 152 megawatts of electricity.
