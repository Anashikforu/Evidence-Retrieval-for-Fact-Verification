
The year '''1570''' was a common year starting on Sunday.

Events
 February 8 &ndash; A magnitude 8.3 1570 Concepción earthquake|earthquake occurs in Concepción, Chile.
 February 25 &ndash; Pope Pius V excommunicates Queen Elizabeth I of England with the bull ''Regnans in Excelsis''.
 July 14 &ndash; Pope Pius V issues ''Quo Primum'', promulgating the 1570 edition of the Roman Missal.
