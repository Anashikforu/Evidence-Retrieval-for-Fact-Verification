

'''Greenock Morton Football Club''' are a Association football|football team, who currently play in the Ladbrokes sponsored Scottish Championship. The club was founded as '''Morton Football Club''' in 1874, making it one of the oldest senior Scotland|Scottish clubs.

Greenock Morton are popularly known as "The Pride of the Clyde" - a tradition which pre-dates the club's renaming in 1994 to celebrate the links with its home town of Greenock. The club has a traditional local rivalry with St Mirren F.C.
