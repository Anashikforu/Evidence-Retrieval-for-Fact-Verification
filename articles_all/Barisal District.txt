'''Barisal''' is a district in southern Bangladesh. It is also the headquarters of Barisal Division. 

History
Barisal was a semi-independent area in the Mughal Empire|Mughal period. Later it came under the control of British India. After British rule it was part of Bangladesh#History|East Bengal (later Bangladesh#History|East Pakistan) and finally, Bangladesh.
