Biography
Takabayashi was born in Saitama, Saitama|Saitama on August 2, 1931. After graduating from Rikkyo University, he played for Tanabe Mitsubishi Pharma SC|Tanabe Pharmaceutical. He also played for Osaka SC.

In March 1954, when Takabayashi was a Rikkyo University student, he was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for 1954 FIFA World Cup|1954 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on March 14, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea. In May, he played at Football at the 1954 Asian Games|1954 Asian Games and scored 2 goals. In 1956, he was selected Japan for Football at the 1956 Summer Olympics|1956 Summer Olympics, but he did not play in the match. He also played at Football at the 1958 Asian Games|1958 Asian Games. He played 9 games and scored 2 goals for Japan until 1954.

On December 27, 2009, Takabayashi died of heart failure in Saitama, Saitama|Saitama at the age of 78.
