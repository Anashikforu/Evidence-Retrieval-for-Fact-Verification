'''Sony Ericsson Mobile Communications AB''' is a mobile phone manufacturer.  The company is a joint venture that was established in 2001 by Japanese consumer electronics company Sony and the Sweden|Swedish telecommunications company Ericsson.  The two companies no longer make mobile phones independently.
