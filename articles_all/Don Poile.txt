'''Donald Bruce Poile''' (born June 1, 1932 in Fort William, Ontario|Fort William, Ontario) is a retired professional ice hockey player who played 66 games in the National Hockey League with the Detroit Red Wings. In addition to his time in the NHL, Poile had a successful minor league career with the Edmonton Flyers of the Western Hockey League (minor pro)|Western Hockey League. His brother Bud Poile is in the Hockey Hall of Fame|Hall of Famer.
