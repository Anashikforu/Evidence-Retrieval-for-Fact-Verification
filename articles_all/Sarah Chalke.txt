
'''Sarah Louise Christine Chalke''' (born August 27, 1976) is a Canadians|Canadian-Americans|American actor|actress most famous for her role as "Elliot Reid" on ''Scrubs (TV series)|Scrubs'' and "Becky Conner" on ''Roseanne (TV series)|Roseanne'' and in the CBS comedy ''How I Met Your Mother'', and ''The Powerpuff Girls''.
She is engaged to lawyer Jamie Afifi, with whom she has a son, Charlie Rhodes Afifi, born December 24, 2009.
