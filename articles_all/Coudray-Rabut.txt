
'''Coudray-Rabut''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Basse-Normandie in the Calvados (department)|Calvados Departments of France|department in the northwest of France. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the commune of Pont-l'Évêque, Calvados|Pont-l'Évêque.
