
'''Adam Birch''' (born July 18, 1979) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler. Birch wrestled independent circuit|in the indies with the name '''Joey Matthews''', and is now working with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) as a trainer in WWE's developmental territory Florida Championship Wrestling.
