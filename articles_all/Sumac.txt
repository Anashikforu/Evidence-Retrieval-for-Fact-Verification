'''Sumac''', also spelled '''sumach''', is a flowering plant.

Sumacs are small trees. The leaf|leaves are arranged like spirals. The flowers are in spikes. Each flower is very small, greenish, creamy white or red, with five petals. The fruits form wiktionary:cluster|clusters of reddish drupes called "sumac bobs".
