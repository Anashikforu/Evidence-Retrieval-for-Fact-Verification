

'''Brandon Jennings''' (born September 23, 1989 in Compton, California) is an Americans|American basketball player. He  plays point guard for the Milwaukee Bucks of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played basketball in Italy for Lottomatica Roma of the Italian Lega A.
