

'''Monte Carasso''' is a former municipality of the district Bellinzona (district)|Bellinzona in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

On 2 April 2017 the former municipalities of Camorino, Claro, Ticino|Claro, Giubiasco, Gnosca, Gorduno, Gudo, Moleno, Monte Carasso, Pianezzo, Preonzo, Sant'Antonio and Sementina merged to form the municipality of Bellinzona.

The Swiss Heritage Society chose Monte Carasso for the 1993 Wakker Prize.
