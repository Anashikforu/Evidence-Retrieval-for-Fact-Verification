
'''Brighton & Hove Albion F.C.''' are an England|English Association football|football team based in the city of Brighton and Hove, East Sussex, England. They began playing in the Premier League in the 2017–18 Premier League|2017–18 season.

 History 
Brighton & Hove Albion F.C. were formed in 1901.
