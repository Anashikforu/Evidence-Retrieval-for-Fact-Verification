

The '''asthenosphere''' is part of earth's Mantle (geology)|mantle. It is about 100&nbsp;km to 350&nbsp;km under the surface. It is the lower part of the upper mantle, and behaves as a hot viscosity|viscous liquid.

It consists of hot, weak material that can be deformed like Silly Putty|silly putty. That means it is capable of gradual Flux|flow. It is under the lithosphere.
