'''Cumming''' is a city in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. The city is the county town|county seat of Forsyth County, Georgia|Forsyth County. About 5,800 people live in Cumming. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 15.3 km² (5.9 square mile|mi²).  15.3&nbsp;km² (5.9&nbsp;mi²) of it is land and 0.1&nbsp;km² (0.04&nbsp;mi²) of it (0.34%) is water.
