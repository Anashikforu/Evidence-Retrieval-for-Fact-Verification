The '''Dean of the United States Senate''' is the United States Senator|Senator that has been in office the longest.
 
Unlike the office of President pro tem, the Office of Dean is not an official position.

History
After the death of Daniel Inouye on December 17, 2012, Patrick Leahy became the new Dean of the Senate. Also since the death of Inouye, Leahy became the President pro tempore of the United States Senate|President pro tempore and "dean".Cillizza, Chris.
