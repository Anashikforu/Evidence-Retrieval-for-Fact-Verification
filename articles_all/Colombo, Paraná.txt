'''Colombo''' is the third largest city in Greater Curitiba, state of Paraná (state)|Paraná, found in the Southern Brazil|Southern Region of Brazil. It is located at  25º17'30" S,  49º13'27" W, at an altitude of about 1.027 metres above sea level, some 18 km from state capital (city)|capital, Curitiba. 

It was founded on  5 February 1890. The city is the largest Italy|Italian colony in the state.
