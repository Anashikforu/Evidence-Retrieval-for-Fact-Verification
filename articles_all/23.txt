



'''23''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a common year starting on Friday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Pollio and Vetus'''.

 Events 
= Roman Empire =
 Strabo publishes ''Geography''.  It covers the world known to the Ancient Rome|Romans and History of Greece|Greeks at the time of Emperor Augustus. 
 Aelius Sejanus gains power in the Roman Senate and Tiberius.
 Gaius Asinius Pollio, Gaius Antistius Vetus become consuls

= Asia =
 Emperor Gengshi of Han|Liu Xuan proclaims himself emperor against Wang Mang.
 Battle of Kunyang - troops under Emperor Guangwu of Han|Liu Xiu defeat 450,000 of Wang Mang's troops

 Births 
 Pliny the Elder, Roman scientist and writer

 Deaths 
 September 14 – Drusus Julius Caesar, son of the Emperor Tiberius, poisoned
