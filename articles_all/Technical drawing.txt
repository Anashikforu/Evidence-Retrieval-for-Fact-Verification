
'''Technical drawing''', also known as '''drafting''' or '''draughting''', is the act and Discipline (study)|discipline of making detailed drawings. These drawings Communication|visually communicate how something works or how it is to be made.

 Communicating ideas 
Technical drawing is wikt:essential|essential for communicating ideas in industry and engineering. 

To make  drawings easier to understand, people use familiar symbols. They use standard units of measurement, notation systems, visual styles, and page layouts. These drawing wikt:convention|conventions are internationally accepted standards. Technical drawings are a wikt:universal|universal way of communicating complex wikt:mechanical|mechanical concepts. Technical drawing are very different from the wikt:expression|expressive drawing of the visual arts. Artistic drawings may have many meanings. Technical drawings are understood to have one intended meaning.

Technical drawings are used to make Electronics|electronic parts as well as wikt:mechanical|mechanical parts.Antonio Ramirez; Jana Schmidt; Douglas Smith, ''Technical Drawing 101 with AutoCAD 2015'' (Mission, KS: SDC Publications, 2014), p. 3 They are used in Commerce|commercial and House|residential construction. Roads, bridges, and wastewater systems are just some of the things that need technical drawings. Almost anything that is manufactured requires technical drawings.  

 Technicians 
A '''drafter''', '''draftsperson''', or '''draughtsman''' is a person Training|trained to make technical drawings. A professional drafter who makes technical drawings is sometimes called a '''drafting technician'''. Professional draftspersons bridge the gap between engineers and manufacturers. They contribute experience and technical expertise to the design process.
