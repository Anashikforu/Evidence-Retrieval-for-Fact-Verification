
'''Haltern am See''' (''Haltern at the lake'', before December 2001 only '''Haltern''') is a town and a municipality in the Recklinghausen Rural District|district of Recklinghausen, in North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It is next to the Lippe River and the Wesel-Datteln Canal, approx. 15 km north of Recklinghausen.

  Roost-Warendin, France
  Sankt Veit an der Glan, Austria
