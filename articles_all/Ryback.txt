
'''Ryan Reeves''' (born November 10, 1981 in Las Vegas, Nevada|Las Vegas, Nevada) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He formally wrestled for the WWE under the ring name '''Ryback'''. He was formerly known under the ring name '''Skip Sheffield''' and was a former member of the wrestling stable, The Nexus.
