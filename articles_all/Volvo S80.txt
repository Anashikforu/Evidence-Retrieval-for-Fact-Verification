'''Volvo S80''' is a car produced by Volvo from 1998 to 2016 in two generations. The first generation was made from 1998 to 2006. The second generation was made from 2006 to 2016 and had an estate variant called Volvo V70|V70. In 2016 the car was replaced by the Volvo S90|S90.
