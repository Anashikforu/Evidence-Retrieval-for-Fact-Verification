A '''fluid ounce''' is a unit of volume in both the Imperial unit|imperial system of units and the U.S. customary units system. However, the two measures are not quite the same:
 The Imperial fluid ounce is (1/160) of an imperial gallon or 8 fluidram|fluid drams, about 1.734 cubic inches or exactly 28.413 07425 ml (as specified legally in  The U.S. fluid ounce is (1/128) of a U.S. fluid gallon, about 1.805 cubic inches or exactly 29.573 529 562 5 ml. This volume of water weighs about 1.04 avoirdupois ounces.
 U.S. law 21CFR101.9(b)(5)(viii) also defines a fluid ounce of exactly 30 ml, for use in nutrition labeling.
