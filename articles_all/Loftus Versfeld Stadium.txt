

'''Loftus Versfeld Stadium''' is a sports stadium in Pretoria, South Africa.

It is mostly used for Rugby union|Rugby games.

 FIFA World Cup 
The stadium co-hosted the 2010 FIFA World Cup. It is one of the 10 stadiums where World Cup matches were played.
