



'''''Khamosh Pani''''' () is a 2003 France|French/Germany|German production about a widowed mother and her young son set in a late 1970s village in Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab, Pakistan which is coming under radical influence.

The movie was released in India too. It was shot in a village in Pakistan.
