
The '''1986 Atlantic hurricane season''' officially started on June 1, 1983, and ended on November 30. The season had a total of 6 named storms and 4 hurricanes.

 Storms 


=Tropical Storm Andrew=

Tropical Depression Six formed on November 18 and quickly strengthened into a tropical storm. The storm curved northeast and strengthened further, reaching hurricane strength on November 20. However, a high pressure system to the north caused the storm to fall apart. Frances was later destroyed by an extratropical cyclone|extratropical low on November 21.
