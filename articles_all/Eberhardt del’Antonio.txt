'''Eberhardt del’Antonio''' (April 21, 1926 in Lichtenstein (Saxony)|Lichtenstein – February 22, 1997 in Dresden) was a German writer.

 Life 
Eberhardt Del'Antonio was the son of a locksmith. He learned the job as an technical drawer. He was fighting for the German army during the WWII|Second World War.

 Publications 
 ''Gigantum'', Das neue Berlin, Berlin 1957
 ''Titanus'', Das neue Berlin, Berlin 1959
 ''Projekt Sahara'', Tribüne, Berlin 1962
 ''Heimkehr der Vorfahren'', Das neue Berlin, Berlin 1966
 ''Okeanus'', Greifenverlag, Rudolstadt 1988
 ''Volle Kraft voraus'', Junge Welt, Berlin 1991 (with Jens Prockat)
