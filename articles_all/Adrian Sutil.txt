
'''Adrian Sutil''' (born January 11, 1983 in Starnberg, Germany) is a Germany|German Formula One auto racing driver. He is the son of professional musicians Monika, a German, and Jorge, a Uruguayan. Sutil started Kart racing|karting at 14 before moving up to Switzerland|Swiss Formula Ford 1800 in 2002. He won all ten rounds of the season from pole position|pole and added five wins in the International Formula Master|Formula Masters Austria championship.  Sutil speaks fluent German language|German, English language|English, Spanish language|Spanish and a little Italian language|Italian.
