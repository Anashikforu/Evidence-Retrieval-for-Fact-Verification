

'''''Ghost Rider''''' is a 2007 United States|American supernatural superhero Horror movie|horror fantasy action movie that was movie producer|produced by Avi Arad, Stan Lee, David S. Goyer, Michael DeLuca and Gary Foster and was directed by Mark Steven Johnson. Ghost Rider was released on February 16, 2007 in North America. The movie received negative reviews with a 27% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and Metacritic gave the movie a 35 out of 100.
