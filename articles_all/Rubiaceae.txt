

'''Rubiaceae''' is a family (biology)|family of flowering plants, variously called the madder, bedstraw, or Coffea|coffee family. There are about 600 genera and more than 10,000 species in Rubiaceae. 

There are three subfamilies: ''Rubioideae'', ''Cinchonoideae'' and ''Ixoroideae''.
