'''Angela Aki''' (born September 15, 1977) is a J-pop singer-songwriter. Her former Japanese name is Aki Kiyomi. 

Aki was born in Itano of Tokushima prefecture. Her father is Japanese and her mother is Italy|Italian-United States|American.  She is bilingual in English language|English and Japanese language|Japanese. She was raised in Japan, but she moved to Hawaii when she was fifteen. 

Then she went to university at George Washington University. She released an indie (music)|indie album in the United States in 2000, called "These Words". Now she is signed under Sony Japan.
