
'''''Mean Streets''''' is a 1973 United States|American crime drama movie movie director|directed by Martin Scorsese. It is set in New York City and stars Harvey Keitel and Robert De Niro. This movie was released by Warner Bros. on October 2, 1973. It received mostly positive reviews from movie critics.
