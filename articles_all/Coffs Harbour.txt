
'''Coffs Harbour''' is a city on the north coast of New South Wales, Australia.  It is 540&nbsp;km north of Sydney and 440&nbsp;km south of Brisbane. Coffs Harbour is famous for its bananas  and is also an important diving spot. It also has a campus of the Southern Cross University. It is the home of the Big Banana, a tourist attraction and amusement park.
