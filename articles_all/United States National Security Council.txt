
The White House '''National Security Council''' ('''NSC''') in the United States is the principal forum used by the President of the United States|President for considering national security and Foreign policy of the United States|foreign policy matters with his senior National Security Advisor (United States)|national security advisors and United States Cabinet|Cabinet officials and is part of the President's Executive Office of the President of the United onStates|Executive Office. It began under President Harry S. Truman.
