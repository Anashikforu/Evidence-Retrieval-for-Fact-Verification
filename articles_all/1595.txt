
__NOTOC__
Year '''1595''' ('''Roman numerals|MDXCV''') was a common year starting on Sunday of the Gregorian calendar and a common year starting on Wednesday of the 10-day slower Julian calendar.

 Events 
 January &ndash; Mehmed III succeeds Murad III as Ottoman Emperor|Sultan of the Ottoman Empire.
 May 18 &ndash; The Treaty of Tyavzino brings to an end the Russo-Swedish War (1590–1595).
 May 24 &ndash; The ''Nomenclator'' of Leiden University Library appears, the first printed catalog of an institutional library.
 June 9 &ndash; Battle of Fontaine-Française:  Henry IV of France defeats the Spain|Spanish, but is nearly killed.
 July 21 &ndash; A Spanish expedition led by navigator and exploration|explorer Álvaro de Mendaña de Neira makes the first European landing in Polynesia, on the Marquesas Islands.
 August 13 &ndash; Battle of Calugareni: The Wallachians, led by Michael the Brave, accomplish a great victory against the large army of the Turks, numbering over 150,000 men, led by Sinan Pasha. Wallachian tactical victory.
 October 26 &ndash; Battle of Giurgiu: Michael the Brave again defeats the Turkish army led by Sinan Pasha, pushing them on the right side of the Danube.
 December 9 &ndash; Probable first performance of William Shakespeare's ''Richard II (play)|Richard II''.
