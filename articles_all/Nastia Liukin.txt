
'''Anastasia Valeri'yevna "Nastia" Liukina''' (simply credited as Nastia Liukin, born 1989, Moscow, Russia, USSR) is a Russian born United States|American gymnastics|artistic gymnast. She is daughter of Anna Kotchneva, former rhythmic gymnastics|rhythmic gymnast and Valeri Liukin, a former artistic gymnast. He is also her wikt:coach|coach. The family moved to United States|America when Nastia was a baby. She trains in Texas. Liukin won the all-around Olympic gold medal in the Beijing 2008 Summer Olympics. She is fluent in both Russian language|Russian and English language|American English.
