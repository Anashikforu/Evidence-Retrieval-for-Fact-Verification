 Geography 
The area of the district is 372.99 Square kilometre|square kilometers (144.01 Square mile|sq mi). Of this area, 205.69 km2 (79.42 sq mi) or 55.1% is used for Farming|agricultural purposes, while 126.4 km2 (48.8 sq mi) or 33.9% is Forest|forested.The remaining area, 38.25 km2 (14.77 sq mi) or 10.3% is settled (Building|buildings or Road|roads) and 2.66 km2 (1.03 sq mi) or 0.7% is unproductive land.
