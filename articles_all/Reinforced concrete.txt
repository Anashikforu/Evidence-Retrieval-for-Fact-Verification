'''Reinforced concrete''' is concrete with long bars inside to make it stronger. The reinforcing material has greater tensile strength than concrete has. Usually the bars are steel. Galvanizing saves the steel from rusting and corrosion.  In rich countries, almost all concrete in buildings and roads is reinforced. Reinforced concrete is Strength of materials|stronger, and can be even stronger if the steel is stretched to make prestressed concrete.
