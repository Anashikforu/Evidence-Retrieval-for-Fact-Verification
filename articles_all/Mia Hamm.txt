
'''Mariel Margaret Hamm-Garciaparra''' (born on March 17, 1972, in Selma, Alabama|Selma, Alabama), best known as '''Mia Hamm''', is a retired Americans|American Association football|soccer player. Hamm played as a Striker for the United States women's national soccer team from 1987 to 2004.
