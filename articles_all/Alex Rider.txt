


'''''Alex Rider''''' is a series of spy fiction|spy books. It is written by Great Britain|British author Anthony Horowitz. The series is about a teenage spy named Alex Rider. The series is aimed at young adults. There are ten books in the series, but short stories and other books have been published too. The novels are published by Walker Books in the United Kingdom. The first novel, ''Stormbreaker (novel)|Stormbreaker'', was published in the United Kingdom on 4 September 2000
