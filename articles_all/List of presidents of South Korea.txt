This is a '''list of the presidents of South Korea'''.

Since the creation of the Government of the '''Republic of Korea''' which is commonly known as '''South Korea''' in 1948, twelve people have served nineteen terms as President of South Korea. The president is the country's head of state as well as its head of government. The president is also the commander-in-chief of the Republic of Korea Armed Forces|Armed Forces.
