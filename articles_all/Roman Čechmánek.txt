
'''Roman Čechmánek'''  (born March 2, 1971 in Zlín|Gottwaldov (now called Zlín), Czech Republic|Czechoslovakia) is a Czech professional ice hockey goaltender.

International play
His only Olympic appearance was for the Czech national ice hockey team|Czech Republic in the 1998 Winter Olympics. Backing-up star goaltender Dominik Hašek, Čechmánek's team won the gold medal after defeating Russia in the gold medal deciding match, despite not playing a single game. Czech Republic won in a tight 1–0 game.

His other debuts were in the 2004 and 2007 IHWC also playing for the Czech Republic team.
