

'''Publius Quinctilius Varus''' (46 BC in Cremona, Roman Republic - 9|AD 9 in Germania) was a Ancient Rome|Roman politician and general under Emperor Augustus. He is mainly remembered for having lost three Roman legions and his own life when attacked by Germanic tribes|Germanic leader Arminius in the Battle of the Teutoburg Forest.
