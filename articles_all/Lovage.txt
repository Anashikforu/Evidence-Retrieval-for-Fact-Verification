
'''Lovage''' (''Levisticum officinale'') is a tall perennial plant.

 Uses 

Lovage leaf|leaves can be used in salads or to make soup, and the roots can be eaten as vegetable or wikt:grate|grated for use in salads. Lovage seeds can be used as a spice. In the UK, lovage cordial was traditionally mixed with brandy as a winter drink. 
Category:Apiaceae
