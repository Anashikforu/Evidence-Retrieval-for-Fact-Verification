
Sea captain|Captain '''Hector Barbossa''' is a fictional character and a recurring antagonist of the ''Pirates of the Caribbean'' franchise. He appeared in the first five movies of the series. 

At first he was the main antagonist as an undead pirate in ''Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl|The Curse of the Black Pearl'' (2003). The character dies at the end of the movie, however, he is revealed to have been brought back to life at the end of ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest|Dead Man's Chest''. 

He later became an antihero in supporting roles as a Brethren Court|Pirate Lord in ''Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End|At World's End'' (2007), a privateer with the Royal Navy in ''Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides|On Stranger Tides'' (2011), and finally as the rich pirate in ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales|Dead Men Tell No Tales'' (2017).

He is seen as the evil version of Captain Jack Sparrow.''Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl'' DVD audio commentary with Stuart Beattie, Ted Elliott (screenwriter)|Ted Elliott, and Jay Wolpert
