'''Ovules''' produce the female reproductive Cell (biology)|cells which get fertilization|fertilized during sexual reproduction in seed plants.

The megagametophyte (also called the ''embryo sac'' in flowering plants) is inside the ovule. The megagametophyte produces the egg cell.
