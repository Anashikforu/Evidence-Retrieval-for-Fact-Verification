

'''''Make Way for Ducklings''''' is a children's picture book written and wikt:illustration|illustrated by Robert McCloskey. It was first printed in 1941. The book is about two mallard ducks who decide to raise their family on an island in the lagoon in Boston Public Garden, a park in the middle of Boston, Massachusetts|Boston, Massachusetts.

''Make Way for Ducklings'' won the Caldecott Medal|1942 Caldecott Medal for McCloskey's pictures, drawn in charcoal, then lithography|lithographed on zinc.

The book is the official children's book of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. However, it was wikt:criticism|criticised for having a loose plot (story) and weak characters. The book is very popular around the world.
