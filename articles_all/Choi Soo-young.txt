


'''Choi Soo-young''' (; Japanese: チェ・スヨン also known as '''Sooyoung Choi''' or '''Summer Choi'''; born February 10, 1990) is a South Korean singer-songwriter, actress and Presenter|TV presenter. She is a former member of the Korean-Japanese duo group ''Route θ'' and a current member of the South Korean girl group Girls' Generation. Sooyoung is the only Asian woman in ''Complex (magazine)|Complex's'' Hottest '90s Babies.
