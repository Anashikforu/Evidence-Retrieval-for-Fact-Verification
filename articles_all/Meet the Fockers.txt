

'''''Meet the Fockers''''' is a 2004 United States|American comedy movie. It was movie producer|produced by Robert De Niro, Jon Poll, Jay Roach, Jane Rosenthal and Nancy Tenenbaum and was movie director|directed by Jay Roach. Meet the Fockers was released on December 22, 2004 in North America. The movie received mixed reviews with a 38% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 41 out of 100 from Metacritic. It is the first sequel to the 2000 movie, ''Meet the Parents''. The second sequel which was called, ''Little Fockers'' was released in 2010.
