


The '''Southern Ocean''' is the ocean around Antarctica. It means the waters of the Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic, Pacific Ocean|Pacific, and Indian Oceans around the continent of Antarctica. Since the 1770s geographers have discussed its limits. Nowadays, sixty Degree (angle)|degrees south latitude is often accepted. Some people call this ocean the '''Antarctic Ocean'''.
