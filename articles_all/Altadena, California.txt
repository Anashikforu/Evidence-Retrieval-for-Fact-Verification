

'''Altadena''' is an unincorporated area|unincorporated census-designated place of Los Angeles County, California|Los Angeles County in the U.S. state|state of California, in the United States.  42,610 people were living in Altadena as of 2000.  It is about fourteen miles from downtown Los Angeles.  It was built as a suburb of Pasadena, California|Pasadena in 1887.  However, although it was meant to be a suburb of Pasadena, it has so far refused to become part of Pasadena.
