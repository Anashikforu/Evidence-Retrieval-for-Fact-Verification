The '''Sassuntsi-Davit Tank Regiment''' (; previously the '''119th Separate Rifle Tank (Mixed) Regiment''') was a Soviet Union|Soviet separate tank regiment during the World War II|Second World War. It had a lot of ethnic Armenians in it. Other ethnic groups that were in the regiment included Azeris, but they were largely relegated to logistics support and other non-combatant roles.
