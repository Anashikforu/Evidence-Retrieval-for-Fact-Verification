

'''Nathalia Goyannes Dill Orrico''', better known as '''Nathalia Dill''' (March 24, 1986 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) is a Brazil|Brazilian actress. She played the villain, Débora Rios, in the fifteenth season of the series Malhação. In her last television work, Nathalia was protagonist of the novel "Paraíso", Benedito Ruy Barbosa, where she played the character Maria Rita (Santinha).
