'''Richard Earl "Dick" Locher''' (June 4, 1929 &ndash; August 6, 2017) was a cartoonist. He has made cartoons for lots of things, including the Chicago Tribune. He was born in Dubuque, Iowa|Dubuque, Iowa.

Locher died of Parkinson's disease on August 6, 2017 in Naperville, Illinois, aged 88.
