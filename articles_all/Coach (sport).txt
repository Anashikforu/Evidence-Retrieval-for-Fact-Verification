In sports, a '''coach''' or '''manager''' is a person who Direction|directs and Training|trains a sports team or sportsperson. If there is a difference between coach and manager, it is this:
the coach will train the players
the manager will deal with the business aspects
The borderline areas are selecting the team, and buying new players.
