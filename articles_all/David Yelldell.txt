

'''David Yelldell''' (born October 1, 1981, in Stuttgart) is a Germans|German-Americans|American goalkeeper who is currently playing for Bayer 04 Leverkusen. Yeldell has dual citizenship. His mother is German; his father is African-American.

At March 29, 2011, he had his first match for the United States national team. He is allowed to join the team because his father is American.
