
'''Groundskeeper Willie''' is a fictional character in the long-running cartoon show ''The Simpsons''.

 Role in The Simpsons 
Willie works at Springfield Elementary as the head janitor or groundskeeper. He is portrayed as an angry Scotsman. He shows some :wikt:hate|hate towards Principal Skinner and Bart Simpson. He lives in a shack in the grounds of Springfield Elementary.
