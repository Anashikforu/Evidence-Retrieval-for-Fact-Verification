


'''David Lloyd Johnston''', Order of Canada|CC Order of Military Merit (Canada)|CMM Order of Merit of the Police Forces|COM Canadian Forces Decoration|CD Royal Society of Canada|FRSC(''hon'') (born June 28, 1941) is a Canadian academic and author. He was the Governor General of Canada from October 1, 2010 to October 2, 2017.

Style and titles
 June 28, 1941– October 1, 2010: ''Mister'' David Lloyd Johnston
 October 1, 2010–: ''His Excellency the Right Honourable'' David Lloyd Johnston, Governor General and Commander-in-Chief in and over Canada
