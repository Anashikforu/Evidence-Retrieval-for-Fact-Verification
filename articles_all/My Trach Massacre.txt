The '''My Trach Massacre''' was the murder of many people in My Trach Village, My Thuy Commune, Le Thuy District, Quảng Bình Province, Vietnam. The massacre took place on 29 November 1947 between 2:00 Ante meridiem|a.m. and 12:00 post meridiem|p.m.  Soldiers of the France|French army killed nearly half of the 320 people who lived in the village. Many of the civilians who were killed were women, old people and children.
