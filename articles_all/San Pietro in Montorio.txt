

'''San Pietro in Montorio''' is a church in Rome, which includes in its courtyard ''The Tempietto'' (a small commemorative Tomb|martyrium) built by Donato Bramante.

 The Tempietto 
The ''Tempietto of San Pietro in Montorio'' is a small commemorative Tomb|martyrium built by Donato Bramante, possibly as early as 1502, in the courtyard of ''San Pietro in Montorio''. It is considered a masterpiece of High Renaissance architecture.
