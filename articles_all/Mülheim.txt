
'''Mülheim an der Ruhr''' (Low German language|Low German and Low Franconian: ''Mölm'', Ripuarian language|Ripuarian: ''Müllem'') is a city in the Germany|German state of North Rhine-Westphalia, belonging to the Ruhr area.
Around 170,000 people live there.
