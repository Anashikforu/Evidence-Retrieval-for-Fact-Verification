'''Georgi Ivanov Markov''' (; 1 March 1929 – 11 September 1978) was a Bulgarians|Bulgarian dissident writer. He was born in Sofia and was murdered in London. He was stabbed in the leg with an umbrella which injected ricin, a deadly poison. It is believed that Bulgarian security persuaded the KGB to do the job.
