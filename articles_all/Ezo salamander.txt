The '''Ezo salamander''' or '''Hokkaido salamander''' ('''''Hynobius retardatus''''') is a kind of salamander in the Hynobiidae Family (biology)|family of animals.  It can only be found in Japan.  The places where it likes to live are forests, shrubland, marshes, and swamps.
