
'''Helen Reddy''' (25 October 1941 &ndash; 29 September 2020) was an Australia singer-songwriter and actress. Her hits are "Angie Baby" and "No Way to Treat a Lady". She was born in Australia in 1941.

Reddy died on 29 September 2020 in Los Angeles from dementia-related problems at the age of 78.
