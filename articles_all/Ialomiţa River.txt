 

The '''Ialomiţa River''' (''Râul Ialomiţa'' in Romanian language|Romanian) is a river in the southern part of Romania. It comes up from the Bucegi|Bucegi Mountains of the Carpathian Mountains|Carpathians and flows into the Danube.

The upper part of the river is sometimes called '''Valea Obârşiei River''' or '''Obârşia Ialomiţei River'''.
