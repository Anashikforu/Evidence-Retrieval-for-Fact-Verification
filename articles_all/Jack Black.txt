

'''Thomas Jacob "Jack" Black''' (born August 28, 1969) is an Americans|American actor, voice actor, comedian, musician, producer, singer, and writer. He is an important member of the Frat Pack, a name used by members of the media for a group of comedians active in today's Hollywood movies. Black is also in demand for some of Hollywood largest movies and smallest art films|indie films. He and Kyle Gass make up the comedy/rock and roll|rock duo Tenacious D. Black starred in the movie ''Tenacious D in The Pick of Destiny'', which is based on a true story. Black is also known by his nicknames, Jables or JB. He also played in the 2008 movie ''Tropic Thunder''.

Early and personal life
Black was born in Santa Monica, California to Thomas Black and Judith Cohen. His father is of English, French, German, Irish, Scottish, and Welsh descent and His mother is Jewish (of Russian descent). Since 14 March 2006, he is married with Tanya Haden. They have two sons (2006 and 2009) he is also a relation of Ibiza Resident DJ DAN BLACK UK
