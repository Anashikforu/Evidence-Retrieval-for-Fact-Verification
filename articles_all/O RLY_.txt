'''O RLY?''' is an Internet meme often used in chat rooms or Internet forum|Forums. It is short for ''Oh, really?''. O RLY is used as a response to an above post or comment to show (:wikt:sarcasm|sarcastically) that it is obvious, clear, or apparent. O RLY? is usually followed by ''YA RLY'', then ''NO WAI''. This sarcasm is not to be confused with internet troll|trolling, disrupting a forum, or a chat room. The meme has spread on many forums, across the Internet. It is usually seen with pictures of animals with open mouths.
