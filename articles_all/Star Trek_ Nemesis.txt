


'''''Star Trek: Nemesis''''' is the tenth movie set in the Star Trek fictional universe|universe. It was made in 2002 by Paramount Pictures. The Romulans create a clone of Captain Jean-Luc Picard|Picard, and the clone tries to destroy the United Federation of Planets|Federation.
