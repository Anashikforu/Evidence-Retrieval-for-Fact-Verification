
'''Kyle Thomas Busch''' (born May 2, 1985) is a NASCAR Racing|race car driver. Busch races in the NASCAR#Sprint cup|Monster Energy Cup, Xfinity Series and the Camping World Truck Series. Kyle's brother, Kurt Busch also races in the Sprint Cup series.

Busch won his 100th NASCAR race on July 17, 2011. He is only the third driver in NASCAR history to do this. The other two drivers are Richard Petty with 200 wins, and David Pearson with 106 wins.

As of December 9th 2019 he is the WWE 24/7 Champion after pinning R-Truth.
