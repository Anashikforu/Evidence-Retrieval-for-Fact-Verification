

'''2D''' or '''two-dimensional''' objects have two measurements: width and length, like a Square (geometry)|square. Two-dimensional objects do not have depth, like a cube. It's a mathematics tool, but it's also used in video games, and computer science. 

Throughout maths, it is used in geometry and other Category|categories.

In video games, it is used as a perspective (graphical)|perspective, like in Grand Theft Auto 1 and 2.
