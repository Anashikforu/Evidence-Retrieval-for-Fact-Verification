

'''Bridget Jane Fonda''' (born January 27, 1964) is a retired Americans|American actress. She has been wikt:nominate|nominated for both an Emmy Award and a Golden Globe Award. Fonda is the daughter of Peter Fonda, the granddaughter of Henry Fonda|Henry and the niece of Jane Fonda. She is of Dutch people|Dutch, English, French Canadians|French-Canadian, German, Irish people|Irish, Italian, Norwegian and Scottish descent.

Fonda is known for  her roles in such movies as ''The Godfather Part III'', ''Single White Female'', ''Singles (movie)|Singles'', ''Doc Hollywood'', ''Lake Placid (movie)|Lake Placid'' and ''Jackie Brown''. She voiced Jenna in the 1995 animated movie ''Balto''.

Award nominations
1990: Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress - Motion Picture for ''Scandal''
1997: Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress - Miniseries or a Movie for ''In the Gloaming''
2002: Golden Globe Award for Best Performance by an Actress In A Mini-series or Motion Picture Made for Television for ''After Amy''
