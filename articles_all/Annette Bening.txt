'''Annette Bening''' (born May 29, 1958) is an Americans|American movie actress. She was born in Topeka, Kansas. She has been nominated four times for an Academy Award.

 Achievements 
Bening is a four-time Oscar nominee for her roles in ''The Grifters (film)|The Grifters'', ''American Beauty (film)|American Beauty'', ''Being Julia'', and ''The Kids Are All Right (film)|The Kids Are All Right'', winning Golden Globe Awards for the latter two films. She won a BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role|BAFTA and a Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Leading Role|SAG award for her performance in ''American Beauty (film)|American Beauty''.

In 1995, Bening appeared in ''The American President'', playing Sydney Wade, a PR (public relations) consultant who dates the President, played by Michael Douglas.

 Personal life 
From 1984 to 1991, Annette was married to J. Steven White. On March 3, 1992, she married Warren Beatty. They have four children.
