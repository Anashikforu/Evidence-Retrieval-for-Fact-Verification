

'''''Malcolm in the Middle''''' is an United States|American sitcom. It was shown on Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox. It ran from January 9, 2000 to May 14, 2006. ''Malcolm in the Middle'' won 9 Emmy Awards, 1 Peabody Award and 1 Grammy Award.

 Cast 
Frankie Muniz as Malcolm
Jane Kaczmarek as Lois
Bryan Cranston as Hal
Justin Berfield as Reese
Christopher Masterson as Francis
Erik Per Sullivan as Dewey
