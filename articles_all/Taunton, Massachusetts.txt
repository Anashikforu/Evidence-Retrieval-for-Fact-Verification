

'''Taunton''' is a city in Bristol County, Massachusetts|Bristol County, Massachusetts,  United States. It is the  county seat|seat of Bristol County, Massachusetts|Bristol County and the hub of the Greater Taunton Area. As of the United States Census, 2000|2000 census, Taunton had 55,976 people living in it.  The United States Census Bureau says that the city has an area of 48.0&nbsp;square miles.
