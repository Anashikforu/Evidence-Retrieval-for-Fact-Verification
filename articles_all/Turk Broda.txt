
'''Walter Edward “Turk” Broda''' (May 15, 1914 – October 17, 1972) was a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey goaltender for the Toronto Maple Leafs. He was born in Brandon, Manitoba on May 15, 1914 to a Polish-Canadian family. He joined the Maple Leafs in 1936.

 Awards & Achievements 
Turnbull Cup MJHL Championship (1933)
Memorial Cup Championship (1933)
Vezina Trophy (1941 & 1948)
National Hockey League|NHL First All-Star Team Goalie (1941 & 1948)
Stanley Cup Championships (1942, 1947, 1948, 1949, & 1951)
National Hockey League|NHL Second All-Star Team Goalie (1942)
Inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1978
 In 1998, he was ranked number 60 on ''The Hockey News''' list of the 100 Greatest Hockey Players
“Honoured Member” of the Manitoba Hockey Hall of Fame
Inducted into the Manitoba Sports Hall of Fame and Museum in 1983
