'''Psycholinguistics''' or '''psychology of language''' is the study of the psychology|psychological and neurobiology|neurobiological factors that allows humans to get, use, and understand language. It studies how our minds allow us to understand language and produce language. One part of psycholinguistics, developmental linguistics|developmental psycholinguistics studies how children learn to talk.
