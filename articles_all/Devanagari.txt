

'''Devanāgarī''' (देवनागरी) is an abugida and brahmic script used to write many North Indian languages, including Sanskrit, Hindi, Marathi language|Marathi, Sindhi language|Sindhi, Bihari languages|Bihari, Bhili language|Bhili, Konkani language|Konkani, Bhojpuri language|Bhojpuri, Nepal Bhasa and Nepali language|Nepali from Nepal and sometimes Kashmiri language|Kashmiri. About a hundred smaller languages also use Devanāgarī.
