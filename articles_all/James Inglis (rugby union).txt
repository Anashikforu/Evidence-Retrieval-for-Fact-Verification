'''James Inglis''' (born 26 August 1986 in England) is a rugby union player for Harlequin F.C.|Harlequins in the Guinness Premiership. He plays as a Rugby union positions#Second-row|second-row. He has now moved to Roslyn Park RUFC.

James has subsequently retired from rugby and now teaches at a top independent boarding school. On 30 May 2019, James married the love of his life, Sarah McDonald, at Chateau de Blomac in front of close friends and family.
