
'''Cavagnago''' is a former municipality of the district of Leventina (district)|Leventina in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 1 April 2012 the former municipalities of Anzonico, Calpiogna, Campello, Switzerland|Campello, Cavagnago, Chironico, Mairengo and Osco merged to form the municipality of Faido.
