

'''Vicques''' is a former municipality of the district of Delémont (district)|Delémont in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Vicques, Montsevelier and Vermes, Switzerland|Vermes merged into the new municipality of Val Terbi.
