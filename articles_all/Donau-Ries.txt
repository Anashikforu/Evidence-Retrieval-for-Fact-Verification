

'''Donau-Ries''' (Austro-Bavarian language|Bavarian: ''Landkroas Doana-Rias'', Swabian German|Swabian: ''Landchrais Donau-Ries'', lit.'Danube-Nördlinger Ries|Ries')  is a rural district in Swabia (Government Region)|Swabia in southwest Bavaria, Germany. Neighbor districts are: Ansbach (district), Weißenburg-Gunzenhausen, Eichstätt (district), Neuburg-Schrobenhausen, Aichach-Friedberg, Augsburg (district), Dillingen (district) and in Baden-Württemberg:  Heidenheim (district) and Ostalbkreis); Donauwörth is the capital (city)|capital of the Donau-Ries.
