

'''''The Sandman''''' is a children's book written by Ralph Fletcher with drawings by Richard Cowdrey.  It was first :wikt:published|published in 2008 by Henry Holt and Company.

 Plot summary 
Tor is an man one inch tall who can not fall asleep no matter what he tries.  He finds a :wikt:dragon|dragon :wikt:scale|scale while walking in the woods.  He learns that the powder made by grinding the scale help a person to sleep.  He then travels wikt:sprinkling|sprinkling the sand into the eyes of children who are having trouble sleeping.
