
'''Jodie Louise Marsh''' (born 23 December 1978) is an English people|English glamour model and television personality. She has appeared topless in many tabloid newspapers and has appeared on her own reality TV show, Totally Jodie Marsh.

After being in ''Celebrity Big Brother 2006 (UK)|Celebrity Big Brother'', Marsh was on several of the spin off shows including: ''Big Brother's Little Brother'', ''Big Brother's Big Mouth'' and ''Big Brother's EForum''.
In December 2006, Marsh said she would marry Brentwood DJ David Doyle, after dating for 11 days. Marsh said Doyle had been unfaithful.
In 2007 she started presenting her own reality TV show Get a Life on Living TV, but it was stopped after two episodes because not enough people watched it.
