

'''Gregory van der Wiel''' (born 3 February 1988 in Amsterdam) is a Dutch football player who plays for Paris Saint-Germain F.C.|Paris Saint-Germain. He plays as a defender.

Van der Wiel was playing for Afc ajax|Ajax in 2007-2012. During 2002-2005 he was loaned out to HFC Haarlem.
