'''''Billboard'' charts''' are record chart|charts published by ''Billboard (magazine)|Billboard'' magazine. They have information about the popularity of music songs, bands and singers. The Billboard Hot 100 is the most popular chart. It appears every week in the United States. It uses information from radio play and sales to say the most popular song each week.
