

The rural district '''Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge''' is a district of the Free State of Saxony, Germany. It was founded in August, 2008 and consists of the former rural districts Sächsische Schweiz Rural District|Sächsische Schweiz and Weißeritzkreis Rural District|Weißeritzkreis.
