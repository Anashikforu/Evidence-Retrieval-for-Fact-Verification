

The '''Armenian alphabet''' is an alphabet that has been used to write the Armenian language since the 5th century.

The Armenian word for "alphabet" is այբուբեն (Classical Armenian language|CA, Eastern Armenian language|EA: ), named after the first two letters of the Armenian alphabet.
