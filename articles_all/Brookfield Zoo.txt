The '''Brookfield Zoo''' is a large zoo near Chicago, Illinois. The zoo is about  216 acres (874,124&nbsp;mÂ²), and has around 450 species of animals.

 Exhibits 
'''Australia House''': Crested pigeons, emerald tree boas, Cassowary|double-wattled cassowaries, emus, princess parrots, Fruit bat|Rodrigues flying foxes, Short-beaked Echidna|short-beaked echidnas, Southern Hairy-nosed Wombat|southern hairy-nosed wombats, Western Grey Kangaroo|western grey kangaroos, Parma Wallaby|parma wallabies, prehensile-tailed skinks, Argus monitors, tawny frogmouths, and Kookaburra|laughing kookaburras.
'''Children's Zoo''': Harris hawks, Great Horned Owl|great horned owls, Kestrel|American kestrels, Turkey Vulture|turkey vultures, Red-tailed Hawk|red-tailed hawks, raccoons, ravens, chickens, Domestic goat|goats, groundhogs, Holstein cattle, reindeer, Domestic sheep|sheep.
'''Feather and Scales''': Andean Condor|Andean condors, Poison dart frog|blue poison dart frogs, double-striped thick-knees,  Gambel's quails, greater roadrunners, green aracaries, Boa constrictor|boa constrictors, and sungazers.
'''Perching Bird House''': African pygmy-falcons, Argus monitors, Starling|Bali starlings, bay-headed tanagers, Partridge|crested wood partridges, Macaw|green-winged macaws, Kingfisher|Micronesian kingfishers, paradise tanagers, tawny frogmouths, and turquoise tanagers.
'''Fragile Desert''': African crested porcupines, Amur Leopard|Amur leopards, bat-eared foxes, black-footed cats, caracals, meerkats, Hyrax|rock hyraxes, and Sand Cat|sand cats.
'''Fragile Hunters''': Lion|African lions, Amur tigers, sloth bears, and Snow Leopard|snow leopards.
'''Fragile Rainforest''': Asian small-clawed otters, Binturong|bearcats, Burmese pythons, Clouded Leopard|clouded leopard, fishing cats, giant African millipedes, Naked Mole Rat|naked mole rats, and Treeshrew|northern treeshrews.
'''Great Bear Wilderness''': American Bison|American bison, Bald Eagle|bald eagles, Brown Bear|brown bears, and polar bears.
'''Habitat Africa! The Forest''': African forest buffaloes, giant African millipedes, okapis, red-flanked duikers, red river hogs, and Chameleon|reunion chameleons.
'''Habitat Africa! The Savannah''': Aardvarks, African Wild Dog|African wild dogs, Ostrich|common ostriches, klipspringers, reticulated giraffes, and warthogs.
'''Hamill Family Play Zoo''': African hedgehogs, barred tiger salamanders, mongoose lemurs, ring-tailed lemurs, Blanding's turtles, musk turtles, Boa constrictor|boa constrictors, and Cichlid|African cichlids.
'''Hoofed Animals''': Addaxes, Bactrian Camel|Bactrian camels, Grant's zebras, and Grevy's Zebra|Grevy's zebras.
'''The Living Coast''': Humboldt penguins, Inca terns, Gull|grey gulls, and Moray eel|moray eels.
'''Pachyderm House''': Eastern Black Rhinoceros|Eastern black rhinoceroses, hippopotamuses, and South American tapirs.
'''Pinniped Point''': Sea lion|California sea lions, Harbor Seal|harbor seals.
'''Regenstein Wolf Woods''': Mexican Wolf|Mexican wolves.
'''Salt Creek Wilderness''': Trumpeter swans.
'''Seven Seas''': Bottlenose Dolphin|Bottlenose dolphins.
'''The Swamp''': American Alligator|American alligators, alligator snapping turtles, eastern screech owls, little blue herons, River Otter|North American river otters, and Egret|snowy egrets.
'''Tropic World''': Asian small-clawed otters, Bornean Orangutans, collared mangabeys, cottontop tamarins, eastern black-and-white colobuses, Giant Anteater|giant anteaters, Spider monkey|Geoffroy's spider monkeys, Golden Lion Tamarin|golden lion tamarins, mandrills, northern white-cheeked gibbons, Pygmy Hippopotamus|pygmy hippopotamuses, sooty mangabeys, and western lowland gorillas.
