'''Compulsory sterilization''' is a term used for laws that force certain groups of people to be sterilization|sterilized so that they can no longer make babies. International Criminal Court considers compulsory sterilization for a "crime against humanity". Transgender persons are often obliged to undergo sterilization for legal rocognition of legal status depite the Principle 3 of the Yogyakarta Principles. 

Such laws were mainly introduced because of Eugenics|Eugenic views of the start of the 20th century. Today, most compulsory sterilizations are done to mental illness|mentally ill people who are not able to handle a pregnancy on their own.
