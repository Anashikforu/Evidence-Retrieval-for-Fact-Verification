'''Michael Earl Clark''' (born December 25, 1962) is an American record producer and Disc jockey|DJ. Mike E. Clark has worked with famous musicians and groups like Insane Clown Posse, Kid Rock and George Clinton (musician)|George Clinton. As of 2012 he had produced all twelve of the Insane Clown Posse's albums.
