
'''''La fanciulla del West''''', meaning ''The Girl of the Golden West'', is an opera by Puccini. The opera is three acts long. It was based on the play The Girl of the Golden West (play)|''The Girl of the Golden West'' by David Belasco. The first showing of the opera was at the Metropolitan Opera, New York City|New York, in 1910 in music|1910.
