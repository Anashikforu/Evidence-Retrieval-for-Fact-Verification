The '''Hunza-Nagar District''' is the seventh district of Gilgit Baltistan of Pakistan. The district is made up of Hunza (princely state)|Hunza and Nagar (princely state)|Nagar. Sikandarabad is the administrative center of this district. Before the announcement of the new district, Hunza-Nagar was part of district Gilgit.
