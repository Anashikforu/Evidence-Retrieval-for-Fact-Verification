In the 2000 United States Census, there were 1,840 people living in Aberdeen. They made up 603 households and 435 families. The population density was 1,807.9 people per square mile (696.5 people/kilometer|kmÂ²). The people of Aberdeen were 66.30% white, 0.11% black or African-American, 0.71% Native American, 0.33% Asian, 0.05% Pacific Islander, 28.91% other races, and 3.59% were two or more races. 39.29% of the people said they were Hispanic or Latino.

In 2010, there were 1,994 people living in Aberdeen. These people were 43.0% white, 0.1% black or African-American, 0.7% Native American, 0.1% Asian, 1.6% some other race, 1.5% two or more races, and 54.1% Hispanic or Latino.
