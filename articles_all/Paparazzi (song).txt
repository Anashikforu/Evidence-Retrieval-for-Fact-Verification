"'''Paparazzi'''" is a song by American pop singer Lady Gaga from her first album, ''The Fame'' (2008). It was released digitally in Ireland and United Kingdom on July 6, 2009 and Australia was released on July 10, 2009 as the last single from the album. It is about a stalker who follows someone to become famous. The music video is eight minutes long.

The song managed to reach number one in Germany; the top five in Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and the UK; and the top ten in the US.  It became Gaga's fourth consecutive number-one song on ''Billboard'''s Pop Songs airplay chart.
