
'''Tottenham Hale''' , is a National Rail and London Underground Victoria Line station in Tottenham, North London|north London. It is on Hale Road near the Tottenham Hale one-way system. The station is in Travelcard Zone 3.

The station opened on 15 September 1840 as Tottenham. It was on the Northern and Eastern Railway|Northern & Eastern Railway (N&ER) line from Stratford station|Stratford in east London to Broxbourne railway station|Broxbourne in Hertfordshire.

It was renamed Tottenham Hale on 1 September 1968 when it became an interchange station with London Underground on the opening of the first stage of the Victoria line.
