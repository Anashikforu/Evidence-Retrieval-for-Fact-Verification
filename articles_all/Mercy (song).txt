"'''Mercy'''" is a song by pop rock band OneRepublic, from their debut album, ''Dreaming Out Loud (OneRepublic album)|Dreaming Out Loud''. The song helped gain the band's popularity on MySpace, and was voted as one of the best songs on MySpace. The song was released as a single (music)|single in the autumn of 2008 promoting their tour of the UK.
