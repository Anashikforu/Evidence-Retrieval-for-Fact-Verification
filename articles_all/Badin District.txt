 Administration 
The district is administratively subdivided into the following tehsil|talukas:

 Badin Tehsil
 Matli Tehsil
 Shaheed Fazil Rahu Tehsil
 Talhar Tehsil
 Tando Bago Tehsil

With the introduction of the devolution System the talukas have been sub-divided into the Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils numbering 49, Tapas 109 and Dehs 511.
