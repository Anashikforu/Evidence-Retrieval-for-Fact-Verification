'''Saint Vincent''' is an island and is the largest island in the chain of islands called Saint Vincent and the Grenadines. The largest city on the island is Kingstown (Saint Vincent and the Grenadines)|Kingstown. Kingstown has about 25,418 people.

Saint Vincent is a volcanic island. It was disputed between France and the United Kingdom in the 18th century.
