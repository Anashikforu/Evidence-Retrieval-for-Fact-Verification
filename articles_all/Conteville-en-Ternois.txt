
'''Conteville-en-Ternois''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Nord-Pas-de-Calais in the Pas-de-Calais Departments of France|department in the north of France. The inhabitants of this town are called ''Contevillois''. In 2006, there lived about 78 people in Conteville-en-Ternois.
