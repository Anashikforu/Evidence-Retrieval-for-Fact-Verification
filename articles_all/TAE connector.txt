

'''TAE''' (Telekommunikations-Anschluss-Einheit) is the Germany|German standard for telephone plugs and sockets.

Image:TAE-Stecker-F.jpg|thumb|right|150px|TAE plug

The standard covers two TAE types: ''F''&nbsp;("Fernsprechgerät": for telephones) and ''N''&nbsp;("Nebengerät": for other devices such as answering machines and modems).
