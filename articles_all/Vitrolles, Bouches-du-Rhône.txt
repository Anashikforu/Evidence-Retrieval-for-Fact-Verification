'''Vitrolles''' (also known as '''Vitròla''' or '''Vitrolo''') is a Communes of France|commune of 37,760 people (2002). It is found in the Regions of France|region Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur in the Bouches-du-Rhône Departments of France|department in the south of France.
