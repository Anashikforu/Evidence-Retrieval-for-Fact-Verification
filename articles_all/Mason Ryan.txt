
'''Barri Griffiths''' (born January 13, 1982 in Tremadog, Wales) better known by his ring name '''Mason Ryan''', is a Wales|Welsh professional wrestling|professional wrestler currently signed to World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) and to the WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brand where he used to be part of the stable, The Nexus (professional wrestling)|The Nexus. He also wrestled for Florida Championship Wrestling where was a one time FCW Florida Heavyweight Championship. Griffiths is a former Gladiator where he was known by the name, Goliath. He has also wrestled in several wrestling tournaments throughout his home country Wales before joining the WWE.
