The '''University of Texas at San Antonio''', also known as '''UTSA''' (especially when talking about sports), is a public university in San Antonio, Texas|San Antonio, Texas, United States.  It has about 28,000 students enrolled and it was founded in 1969.

UTSA's sports teams, known as Roadrunners, currently play in Conference USA.
