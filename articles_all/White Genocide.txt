


'''White Genocide''' (Armenian language|Armenian: Ճերմակ or սպիտակ ջարդ, Jermag or Spitag Chart) is the word Western Armenians use to describe Cultural assimilation|assimilation in the West.Encyclopedia of Genocide - IW Charny Armenians were forcibly deported from their homeland in Anatolia during the Armenian Genocide in 1915-1918, and never allowed to return.Nations Abroad: diaspora politics and international relations in the Former Soviet Union - Page 96 by Neil J.
