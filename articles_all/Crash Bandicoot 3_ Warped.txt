
'''''Crash Bandicoot: Warped''''' (known in Europe as '''''Crash Bandicoot 3: Warped''''') is the third video game for the ''Crash Bandicoot (series)|Crash Bandicoot'' series. Time trials were a new feature introduced with this game.
