

'''Foals''' are a dance-punk band from Oxford who play a mixture of Indie (music)|Indie, techno and Math-Rock. The band work with Transgressive Records.

 TV Appearances 
In August 2007, the band appeared in a mini episode of ''Skins (TV Series)|Skins'', which aired on Myspace.

The band appeared on the 16th November 2007 episode of Later With Jools Holland playing their songs 'Balloons' and 'Red Socks Pugie'.

The band have also appeared on T-Mobile's Transmission on Channel 4 where they performed the song Hummer.
