


The '''1920 Summer Olympics''', officially known as the '''Games of the VII Olympiad''', were held at Antwerp in Belgium.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics), 
The games were a financial strain for the host country and the participants. The events were not well attended.Zarnowski, C. Frank. 
 Participating nations 
A total of 22 nations sent athletes to compete at the Antwerp games.Taking part in the games for the first time were Czechoslovakia, Brazil, Estonia, Finland, Monaco, New Zealand and Kingdom of Yugoslavia|Yugoslavia.
