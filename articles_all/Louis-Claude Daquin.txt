'''Louis-Claude Daquin''' (born Paris, 4 July 1694; died Paris 15 June 1772) was a France|French composer.  He was writing in the last part of the Baroque music|Baroque period and the early part of the Classical music period.  He was a virtuoso pipe organ|organist and harpsichordist.  He wrote a lot of excellent music for Organ (music)|organ and for harpsichord. His most popular piece is called ''Le Coucou'' (''The Cuckoo'').
