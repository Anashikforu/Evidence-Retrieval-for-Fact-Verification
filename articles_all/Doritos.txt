
'''Doritos''' ,pronounced  as (), is a brand of flavored corn (maize) tortilla chip snack. They have been made by the United States|American food company Frito-Lay since 1966. They come in different flavors, including Nachos|Nacho Cheese and Cool Ranch. They may be eaten with a dip such as Pico de gallo|salsa. They are made with a few different vegetables and are not fruit products.
