
'''Micheline Calmy-Rey''' (born 8 July 1945) is a Switzerland|Swiss politician. She was Switzerland's foreign minister as head of the Federal Department of Foreign Affairs. She was also a member of the Swiss Federal Council from 2003. She was President of the Confederation (Switzerland)|President of the Confederation in 2007 and again in 2011. She resigned her office as member of the Federal Council on 31 December 2011.
