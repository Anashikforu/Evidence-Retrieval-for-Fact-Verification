Her full name is Angelina Jolie Voight, but she decided to go by Angelina Jolie. She grew up in Los Angeles and attended the Lee Strasberg Theatre Institute. Early life
She was born in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of actors Jon Voight and Marcheline Bertrand. She is the sister of actor James Haven, niece of singer-songwriter Chip Taylor, and goddaughter of actors Jacqueline Bisset and Maximilian Schell. Career
Jolie played her first leading role in the 1993 movie ''Cyborg 2''. In 1999 Jolie played Lisa Rowe in ''Girl, Interrupted (movie)|Girl, Interrupted''.

She was awarded an honorary damehood by the British government in 2014 in recognition of her work in preventing sexual violence. She was presented with the award by Elizabeth II|Queen Elizabeth II. As an American citizen, she cannot be called "Dame Angelina", but she can use the letters "Order of St Michael and St George|DCMG" after her name.

Awards
She won an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her performance in ''Girl, Interrupted''.
