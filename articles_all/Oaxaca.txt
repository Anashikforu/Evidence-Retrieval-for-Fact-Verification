'''Oaxaca''' is a state in Mexico. The capital city|capital is Oaxaca de Juárez. Oaxaca is next to Guerrero (state)|Guerrero, Puebla (state)|Puebla, Veracruz, and Chiapas. About 3.5 million people live there. The Zapotec people|Zapotec and the Mixtec people live in the state.  There are more speakers of indigenous languages in this state than in any other state of Mexico. This Mexican state is known for it's diverse culture and it's native people.
