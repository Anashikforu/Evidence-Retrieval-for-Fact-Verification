
'''Marcia Anne Cross''' (born March 25, 1962 in Marlborough, Massachusetts) is an Emmy Award|Emmy and Golden Globe Award-nominated United States|American actor|actress. She graduated from The Juilliard School|Juilliard and earned a master's degree in psychology at Antioch University in Los Angeles, California|Los Angeles. In 2005, she was nominated for her first Emmy for her work as Bree Hodge, the "perfect" Housewife on ''Desperate Housewives''. In December 2006, Cross earned her third consecutive wikt:nominate|nomination for the Golden Globe for Best Actress in a Comedy Series with the television programme|series.

In June 2019, Cross revealed that she suffered from anal cancer, and was now in remission.
