This is a '''list of episodes''' for the United States|American television programme|television series, ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer''. The series began on March 10, 1997 and aired until May 19, 2003. In that time, '''144''' episodes of the series were made.

Seasons one to five of the series ran on the The WB Television Network|WB. In 2001, it moved to the United Paramount Network (UPN) for its final two seasons. In the United Kingdom, the entire series aired on Sky One and BBC Two.

All the seven seasons of the series are available on DVDs for regions 1 and region 2. Two Buffy the Vampire Slayer DVDs#Collections|all-season collections (''The Chosen Collection'' and ''The Complete DVD Collection'') have been also been released.
