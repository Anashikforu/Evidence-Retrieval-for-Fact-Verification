

"'''Arma-Goddamn-Motherfuckin-Geddon''''" is a industrial rock song by the United States|American rock music|rock band Marilyn Manson (band)|Marilyn Manson and is the first single (music)|single from their seventh studio album ''The High End of Low'', which was released in 2009. There is a remix of the song by the Sweden|Swedish rock band, Teddybears. The song reached number 37 on the U.S. Billboard Mainstream Rock Tracks.
