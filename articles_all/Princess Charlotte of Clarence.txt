
'''Princess Charlotte of Clarence''' Born on 27 March 1819, Hanover. She died the same day as her birth. She was the oldest daughter of William IV of the United Kingdom and his wife Adelaide. She had four younger siblings- :en:Princess Elizabeth of Clarence|Elizabeth of Clarence, two stillborn brothers and a stillborn sibling of unknown sex.
