'''Alfalfa''' (''Medicago sativa'') is a species of flowering plant in the family (biology)|family Fabaceae. It is also known as the '''lucerne'''. It is wikt:native|native to the Near East and central Asia. It has been introduced species|introduced to Europe, central Africa, China, North America, and South America. Alfalfa is a crop. It is used to feed livestock.

 Nutritional value 
Image:Sprouted Alfalfa.jpg|thumb|right|Sprouted alfalfa seeds
Alfalfa is high in protein, calcium and other minerals, Vitamin B|vitamins in the B group, vitamin C, vitamin D, vitamin E, and vitamin K.
