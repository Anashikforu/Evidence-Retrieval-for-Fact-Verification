

'''''Halfway Home''''' is a comedy television programme|television series that premiered on Wednesday, March 14, 2007, at 10:30 p.m. on Comedy Central. 
Characters
'''Carly Barzac''' (played by Jessica Makinson) – Ex-convict in for Illegal drug trade|drug trafficking. She has been shown to have a bad relationship with her parents (she even had to hire fake parents for parents day.) She also came very close to setting a world record for pogo-sticking.
'''Kenny Carlisle''' (played by Kevin Ruf) – The house supervisor
'''Serenity Johnson''' (played by Octavia Spencer) – Ex-convict in for Robbery|armed robbery
'''Eulogio Pla''' (played by Oscar Nunez) – Ex-convict in for Male prostitution|prostitution, described as "using sex to get what he wants."
'''Alan Shepard''' (played by Regan Burns) – Ex-convict in for arson, described as "the house narc who has an Pyromania|uncontrollable obsession with fire."
'''Sebastian "C-Bass" Yates''' (played by Jordan Black (actor)|Jordan Black) – Ex-convict in for internet fraud, described as a "wanna-be terrorism|terrorist who's really just a rich kid from California."
