'''Le Mans Cathedral''' (French language|French: ''Cathédrale St-Julien du Mans'') is a cathedral built in the Gothic architecture|Gothic style in the town of Le Mans, France. It is dedicated to Saint Julien; he was the first bishop of Le Mans; he is also buried in the cathedral. It is somewhat unusual in the West. It is often compared to those of Reims Cathedral|Reims and Chartres Cathedral|Chartres. Its building was influenced a lot by the Bourges Cathedral|cathedral of Bourges. It is also special because its nave is built in Romanesque architecture|Romanesque style, but the choir is built in Gothic architecture|Gothic style.
