'''Dazaifu''' (太宰府市, Dazaifu-shi) is a city in the Japanese prefecture of Fukuoka Prefecture|Fukuoka. It has a population of 67,428. The city was founded on April 1, 1982, but it has been history|historically important for around a thousand years.
