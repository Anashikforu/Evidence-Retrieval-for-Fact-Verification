

'''Calafat''' is a city in Dolj County, Romania.  It is near the Danube|Danube River. This is across from the Bulgarian city of Vidin.  Calafat was started in the 14th century by Genoa|Genoese colonists.  About 21,200 people lived in Calafat, as of 2000.

International relations
=Twin towns — Sister cities=
Calafat is town twinning|twinned with:
  Vidin, Bulgaria
  Zaječar, Serbia
  Duiven, Netherlands
  Biñan, Philippines
