
'''Ligaria cuneifolia''' van Tigh, (syn. ''Psittacanthus cuneifolius'' (Ruiz et Pav.) Blume) is a shrubby, glabrous plant. It's fruit a globe|globular berry. It is commonly known as “liga” (Argentina) or “quintral de Roman Cassie|espino” (Chile).
