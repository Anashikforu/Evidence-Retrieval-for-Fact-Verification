
'''Stephanie Kay Panabaker''' (born May 2, 1990) is an Americans|American actor|actress from Orange, Texas|Orange, Texas. Some of her best known roles are Emily on ''Life is Ruff'' and Debbie on ''Phil of the Future''. Panabaker is the younger sister of actress Danielle Panabaker|Danielle. The two sisters starred together in the movie ''Read it and Weep'' for the Disney Channel. She has had recurring roles on the television program|television series ''CSI:Crime Scene Investigations'' and ''Angel (TV series)|Angel''.
