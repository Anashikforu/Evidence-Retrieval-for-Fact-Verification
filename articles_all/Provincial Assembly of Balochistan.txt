
The '''Provincial Assembly of Balochistan''' is one of the four provincial assemblies of Pakistan. The Balochistan assembly is the unicameral (single chamber) legislature of the province of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan. It was set up by Article 106 of the Constitution of the Pakistan. The Assembly has 51 directly elected Members of the Provincial Assembly, representing Constituency|constituencies from each district, as well as 11 seats reserved for women and 3 for non-Muslims.
