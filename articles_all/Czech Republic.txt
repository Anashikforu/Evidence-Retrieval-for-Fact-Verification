Czechia has one of the least religious populations in the world. According to the 2011 census, 34.2% of the population stated they had no religion, 10.3% were Roman Catholic, 0.8% were Protestantism|Protestant (0.5% Evangelical Church of Czech Brethren|Czech Brethren and 0.4% Hussite Church|Hussite), and 9.4% followed other forms of religion both denominational or not (of which 863 people answered they are Neopaganism|Pagan). 45.2% of the population did not answer the question about religion.
