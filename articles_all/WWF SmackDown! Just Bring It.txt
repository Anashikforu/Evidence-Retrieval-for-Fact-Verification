

'''''WWF SmackDown! Just Bring It''''', named '''''Exciting Pro Wrestling 3''''' in Japan, is a wrestling video game released only for the PlayStation 2. It was published by THQ and developed by Yuke's. Dwayne Johnson|The Rock is on the cover of the game. It is part of the WWE (video game series)|WWF Smackdown! series.
