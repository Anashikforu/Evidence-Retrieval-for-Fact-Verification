
'''Stefano Mei''' (born 3 February 1963 in La Spezia) was an Italians|Italian long-distance track event|long distance runner in several international Athletics (track and field)|athletics (also called ''track and field'') events.

Mei won the silver medal in the 5,000 metre race, and the gold medal in the 10,000 metre race during the 1986 European Championships in Athletics in Stuttgart, Germany. Mei also won a bronze medal in the 10,000 metre race during the 1990 European Championships in Athletics, in Split, Yugoslavia (now Croatia). He competed in the 1984 Summer Olympics|1984 and 1988 Summer Olympics.
