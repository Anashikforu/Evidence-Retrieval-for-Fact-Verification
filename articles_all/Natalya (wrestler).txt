
'''Natalie Katherine Neidhart''' (born May 27|March 27, 1982 in Calgary, Alberta) is a Canadians|Canadian female professional wrestler. She is currently signed to the WWE. She competes under the ring name  '''Natalya'''. She is a former WWE Divas Championship|WWE Divas Champion. She is a third generation superstar, her parents are former professional wrestler Jim Neidhart|Jim "The Anvil" Neidhart and Ellie Hart, the daughter of Stu Hart. In June 2013, Neidhart married long time boyfriend and fellow professional wrestler Tyson Kidd. She currently appears on the reality television show Total Divas where she is a part of the main cast.
