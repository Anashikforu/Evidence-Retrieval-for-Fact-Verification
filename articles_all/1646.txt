
The year '''1646''' was a common year which started on Monday.

Events
 February 16 &ndash; First English Civil War: The Battle of Great Torrington, Devon, the last major battle of the conflict, is fought.
 December 21 &ndash; Temperatures around the world start to decline as part of the Little Ice Age.

Deaths
 December 22 &ndash; Peter Mogila, Orthodox Metropolitan of Kiev and Galicia (b. 1596)
