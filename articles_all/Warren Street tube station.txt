
'''Warren Street tube station''' is a London Underground station.  It is on the Charing Cross tube station|Charing Cross branch of the Northern line, between Goodge Street tube station|Goodge Street and Euston station|Euston, and the Victoria Line between Oxford Circus tube station|Oxford Circus and Euston station|Euston.  It is in Travelcard Zone 1 and is the nearest tube station to University College Hospital, being opposite the newly opened main building. It is also very close to Euston Square tube station on the Circle line (London Underground)|Circle, Hammersmith and City line|Hammersmith and City and Metropolitan lines.
