
'''''For a Few Dollars More''''' (1965) is a Spaghetti Western movie film director|directed by Sergio Leone and starring Clint Eastwood. It is the second movie in the Dollars trilogy and was released in the United States in 1967. The first movie in the trilogy is ''A Fistful of Dollars''; the third is ''The Good, the Bad and the Ugly''.
