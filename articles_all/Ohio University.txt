'''Ohio University''' is a public university in Athens, Ohio|Athens, Ohio. The university has about 25,908 students. It was founded in 1804. The current president of the university is M. Duane Nellis. 

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Bobcats. They have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the Mid-American Conference.
