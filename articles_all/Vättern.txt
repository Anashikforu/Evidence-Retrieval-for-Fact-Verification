

'''Vättern''' is the second biggest lake in Sweden. The lake's total surface area is about .

Vättern is famous for the quality of its transparent water. Many of the people living in the area get their drinking water directly from Vättern. The lake water does not need to have many things done to it before it is used in nearby city|cities and towns. The water can be safely drunk from almost any point in the lake. It has been said that Vättern is the largest body of wikt:potable|potable water in the world.

Vättern is also noted for its fishing. Tourism|Tourist sport fishermen and people on vacation are allowed to fish in the lake as long as they do not use Fishing net|nets.
