
'''Lavizzara''' is a municipality of the district Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

The municipality was created in 2004 by a merger of Broglio, Switzerland|Broglio, Brontallo, Fusio, Menzonio, Peccia and Prato-Sornico.
