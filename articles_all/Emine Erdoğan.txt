

'''Emine Erdoğan''', born as ''Emine Gülbaran'', (born February 16, 1955 in Üsküdar, Istanbul) is the First Lady of Turkey and the wife of current President of Turkey|Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. They were married on July, 4 1978. She is of Arab people|Arab descent.

Her family is originally from Siirt in southeast Turkey.
