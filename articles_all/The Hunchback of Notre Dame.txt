

'''''The Hunchback of Notre Dame''''' (, "Our Lady of Paris") is a 1831 France|French novel written by Victor Hugo. It is set in 1482 Paris. The story is about a Gypsy (Esmeralda), a bell-ringer in the cathedral of Notre Dame de Paris (Quasimodo) and the archdeacon (Claude Frollo).
