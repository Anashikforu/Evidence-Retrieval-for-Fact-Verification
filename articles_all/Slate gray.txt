'''Slate gray''' is a gray color with a little bit of azure blue in it. It is the usual color of the material slate.

The first recorded use of ''slate gray'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1705.Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 204; Color Sample of Slate Gray: Page 51  Plate 14 Color Sample A2
