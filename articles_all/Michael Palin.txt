

'''Sir Michael Edward Palin''' Order of St Michael and St George|KCMG Order of the British Empire|CBE (born 5 May 1943) is an English people|English actor, comedian and author.

Palin was born in Broomhill (ward)|Broomhill, Sheffield, West Riding of Yorkshire. He studied modern history at Brasenose College, Oxford. 

He became famous as a member of Monty Python in the 1970s. Afterwards he had his own television series, ''Ripping Yarns'', and also appeared in many movies, such as ''A Fish Called Wanda.'' Over the last 20 years, he has become very well known as a television travel broadcaster ("Around the World in 80 Days (TV series)|Around The World in 80 Days", etc.).
