


The '''United States Department of War''', also called the '''War Office''', was the department of the United States Federal government of the United States|government's Federal government of the United States#Executive branch|executive branch responsible for the wikt:operation|operation and wikt:maintenance|maintenance of land (and later air) Military of the United States|forces from 1789 until September 18, 1947, when it became part of the '''United States Department of Defense|National Military Establishment''', renamed on August 10, 1949, the United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense. The War Department also had responsibility for the young nation's naval affairs until the establishment of the United States Department of the Navy|Navy Department in 1798.
