

'''Billy Dee Williams''' (born April 6, 1937) is an Americans|American actor, artist, and writer. He is famous for playing Lando Calrissian in ''Star Wars'' and Two-Face|Harvey Dent / Two-Face in ''Batman (1989 movie)|Batman'' (1989) and ''The Lego Batman Movie'' (2017).

In a 2019 ''Esquire (magazine)|Esquire'', Williams spoke about having a non-binary gender|fluid sense of gender and uses masculine and feminine pronouns.
