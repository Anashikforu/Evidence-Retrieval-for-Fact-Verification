
'''Anhalt-Bitterfeld''' is a district in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. Its capital is Köthen.

 History 
This district was established by putting together the former districts of Bitterfeld (district)|Bitterfeld, Köthen Rural District|Köthen and a large part of Anhalt-Zerbst as part of the Saxony-Anhalt border reforms of 2007.
