
'''Kukmang''' is one of the 51 Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils of Abbottabad District in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan.

 Location 
Kukmang is located at 34°16'20N 73°25'30E and has a height of 1208 metres (3966&nbsp;feet).  It is in the northwest of the district, it borders Boi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa|Boi to the north and Pattan Kalan to the east.

Subdivisions
The Union Council of Kukmang is divided into the following areas: Kothiala, Kukmang and Pattan Khurd, as part of the local government reforms of 2000 Pattan Khurd Union Council was merged into Kukmang.
