The '''Pioneer program''' is the name of a few unmanned space missions done by NASA between 1958 and 1978. Problably the best-known of these space probe|probes were Pioneer 10 and Pioneer 11|11, launched in 1972 and 1973 respectively.
Both carried a plaque as shown.
