'''Frieze''' has two major, totally unrelated meanings:
In architecture, the frieze is the wide central section part of an entablature: see Frieze, Parthenon Frieze.

In textiles, frieze ("Frisian woolen cloth") is a napped woollen cloth:  see Frieze (textile).
