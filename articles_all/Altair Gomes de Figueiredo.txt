
'''Altair Gomes de Figueiredo''' (22 January 1938 &ndash; 9 August 2019) was a Brazilian Football (soccer)|football player. He played for Fluminense F.C.|Fluminense and Brazil national football team|Brazil national team.

de Figueiredo died on 9 August 2019 in São Gonçalo, Rio de Janeiro at the age of 81.
