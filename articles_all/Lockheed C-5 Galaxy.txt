
The '''C-5 Galaxy''' is a very big military cargo aircraft made by Lockheed (now Lockheed Martin) used by the United States Air Force. It first flew in 1968 and was introduced in 1970. It is one of the biggest transports in the world. It is 247&nbsp;feet (75&nbsp;meters) long, 65&nbsp;feet (19&nbsp;meters) high, has a wingspan of 222&nbsp;feet (67&nbsp;meters), and can weigh up to840,000&nbsp;pounds (378,000 kilograms).
It is used to fly military equipment on to battlefields, and because of its size, it can even carry tanks and helicopters. Five C-5s have crashed.
