
The '''M40 motorway''' is a motorway between Denham, near London and Solihull near Birmingham in the United Kingdom. It was built in stages between 1967 and 1991 and is  long.

Junctions



Data from driver location signs are used to provide distance and carriageway identifier information.  Where a junction goes across several hundred metres, both start and end locations are given.
