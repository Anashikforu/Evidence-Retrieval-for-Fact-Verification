

'''Serdar Taşçı''' (born 24 April 1987) is a Germany|German footballer. 

 Career 
Serdar Taşçı played for SC Altbach and the Stuttgarter Kickers until he came to the youth teams of VfB Stuttgart in 1999. In 2005 he played his first matches for the second senior team, and on 20 August 2006 he had his first Bundesliga match. He won the German Championship with Stuttgart in the 2006-07 Bundesliga season. 

As a child of a Turkey|Turkish family, he had also the chance to play for the Turkey national football team|Turkish national football team, but went to the Germany national football team|German team instead. On 20 August 2008 he played his first international match against Belgium national football team|Belgium.
