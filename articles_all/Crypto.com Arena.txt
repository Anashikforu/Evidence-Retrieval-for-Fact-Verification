

'''Crypto.com Arena''', originally known as '''Staples Center''', is an indoor multi-purpose Stadium|arena in downtown Los Angeles, California.  It is home to three basketball teams—the Los Angeles Lakers and Los Angeles Clippers of the National Basketball Association|NBA and Los Angeles Sparks of the Women's National Basketball Association|WNBA—plus the Los Angeles Kings of the National Hockey League|NHL in ice hockey. They have the Grammy Awards here every year. The building hosted World Wrestling Entertainment's pay-per-view WrestleMania 21 in 2005.

On December 25, 2021, the arena was renamed Crypto.com Arena, for a Singapore-based cryptocurrency exchange that bought the naming rights.
