
'''George Tawlon Manneh Oppong Ousman Weah''' (born 1 October 1966)  is a Liberian politician and retired Association football|footballer. He is the 25th and current President of Liberia|President of Liberia upon winning the Liberian general election, 2017|2017 general election in December 2017. He has played for Liberia national football team|Liberia national team.

In 1989, 1994 and 1995, he was named the African Footballer of the Year, and in 1996, he was named African Player of the Century.

After his football career with his high popularity, he ran unsuccessfully for president in the Liberian general election, 2005|2005 election, losing to Ellen Johnson Sirleaf in the second round of voting. In the Liberian general election, 2011|2011 election, he ran for vice president on Winston Tubman's ticket. Running as a Congress for Democratic Change candidate, Weah was elected to the Senate of Liberia|Senate in Liberian Senate election, 2014|2014.

Weah won the presidency while a candidate for President of Liberia in the Liberian general election, 2017|2017 general election against Vice President of Liberia|Vice President Joseph Boakai.
