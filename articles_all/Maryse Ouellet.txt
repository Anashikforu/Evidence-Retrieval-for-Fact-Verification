
'''Maryse Ouellet''' (born January 21, 1983 in Montreal, Quebec) is a French Canadian Glamour photography|glamour model, professional wrestling|professional wrestler, Manager (professional wrestling)|professional wrestling valet, and businesswoman. She is perhaps best known for her time with WWE where she worked from 2006 to 2011.

During her time in the WWE, she won the WWE Diva's Championship twice. In August, she underwent surgery for an abdominal hernia and took time off of WWE programming. She was released from her WWE contract on October 28 after two months of inactivity. She announced that had plans to start a clothing and jewelry line called House of Maryse.

In 2003, She won Miss Hawaiian Tropic Canada and she also finished second at the International Finals of Miss Hawaiian Tropic 2004.
