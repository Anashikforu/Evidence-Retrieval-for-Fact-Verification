
'''''Spy Kids 4: All the Time in the World''''' is a 2011 movie. It stars Jessica Alba and Joel McHale.  It is the sequel to Spy Kids 3-D: Game Over|Spy Kids 3. In 2018, a tv show based on the Spy Kids series was made by Netflix and it was called Spy Kids: Mission Critical. A reboot of the first film of the Spy Kids film series is also in development.
