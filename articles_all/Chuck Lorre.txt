
'''Chuck Lorre''' (born '''Charles Michael Levine''' on October 18, 1952) is an Americans|American television writer, television director|director, television producer|producer and composer. He was born in Bethpage, Long Island, New York. He has worked on many American sitcoms, including ''Grace Under Fire'', ''Dharma & Greg'', ''Two and a Half Men'', ''The Big Bang Theory'' and ''Mike & Molly''.
