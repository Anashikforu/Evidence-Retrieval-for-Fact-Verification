'''Staffordshire''' (abbreviated '''Staffs''') is a county in the West Midlands region of England. The county town is Stafford. Part of the National Forest, England|National Forest is there. It is next to the counties of Cheshire, Derbyshire, Leicestershire, Warwickshire, West Midlands (county)|West Midlands, Worcestershire and Shropshire.

The largest city in ceremonial Staffordshire is Stoke-on-Trent.
