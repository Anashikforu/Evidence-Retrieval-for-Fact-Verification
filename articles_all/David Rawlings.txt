
'''David Todd Rawlings''' is a United States guitarist.  He is best known as the musical partner of Bluegrass music|bluegrass singer-songwriter Gillian Welch. Rawlings has also been a record producer, producing albums such as those by Welch and other artists like Old Crow Medicine Show.
