
'''Danubio Futbol Club''' is a Association football|football club from Montevideo, Uruguay.

 Overview 
Danubio was founded by two brothers, Miguel and Juan Lazaroff on 1 March 1932 together with other youths from a school in Montevideo. The club name is a reference to the Danube River|Danube river, a major waterway in Europe.
