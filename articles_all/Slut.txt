

'''Slut''' is a broad word that usually means a woman who has sexual intercourse|sex with a lot of people. It is a bad word and is generally meant to be pejorative|offensive to whomever it is describing. It is an insult, and some people would also call it a swear word., Rebecca Nathanson, ''The Village Voice'', October 2, 2011 Originally it meant "A woman of dirty, slovenly or untidy appearance".Shorter OED vol 2, p1921.
