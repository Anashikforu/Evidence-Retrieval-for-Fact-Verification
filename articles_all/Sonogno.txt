

'''Sonogno''' is a former municipality of the district Locarno (district)|Locarno in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. It can be found in Valle Verzasca.

On 17 October 2020 the former municipalities of Brione (Verzasca), Corippo, Frasco, Sonogno and Vogorno merged to form the new municipality of Verzasca, Switzerland|Verzasca.
