
'''Ronald Jeffrey Hextall''' (born May 3, 1964 in Brandon, Manitoba|Brandon, Manitoba) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender most often associated with the Philadelphia Flyers of the National Hockey League. He is the grandson of Hall-of-Famer Bryan Hextall, the son of former NHL player Bryan Hextall Jr. and nephew of former NHL player Dennis Hextall.
