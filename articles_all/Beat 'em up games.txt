'''Beat 'em up games''' are video games where the player beats up bad guys while walking through levels. Sometimes the player has to beat up certain bad guys to progress through the game. Most of the time, these games have level boss (video game)|bosses which are usually at the end of the level. Examples of "beat-em up" games are arcade games like ''Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (game)|Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles'' and ''The Simpsons (game)|The Simpsons''. ''Super Smash Bros. Brawl'' also has a "beat-em up" part in the game where the player fights bad guys from the Subspace Emissary and fights other characters as well in order to beat the levels.
