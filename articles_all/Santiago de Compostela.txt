The city's Cathedral is the destination of an important pilgrimage route, the Way of St. James (Galician language|Galician: ''Camiño de Santiago'', Spanish language|Spanish: ''Camino de Santiago''). Pilgrims have been following this route since the ninth century.

The cathedral is believed to be where James, son of Zebedee is buried. Saint James was one of the Twelve Apostles|apostles of Jesus Christ.
