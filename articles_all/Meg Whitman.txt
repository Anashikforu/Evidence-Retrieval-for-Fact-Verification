

'''Margaret Cushing Whitman''' (born August 4, 1956) is an American billionaire.  

She was CEO of the online auction site eBay from 1998 to 2008.  She also worked for DreamWorks, Procter & Gamble, and Hasbro. In 2010, she ran for Governor of California as a United States Republican Party|Republican.  Her campaign was paid for mostly by millions of her own money.  Even though she spent over $100 million, she still lost to Jerry Brown.

On December 8, 2021, Joe Biden nominated Whitman to serve as United States Ambassador to Kenya|United States ambassador to Kenya.
