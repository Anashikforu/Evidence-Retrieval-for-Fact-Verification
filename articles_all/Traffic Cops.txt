

'''''Traffic Cops''''' is a United Kingdom|British reality TV show narration|narrated by Jamie Theakston. It is about criminals who are seen breaking the law on the road, often in cars. It has been on TV since 2003 and follows police forces in the UK.  It is on BBC1 and old shows are on Blighty (TV channel)|Blighty and Watch (TV channel)|Watch.
