
'''Kyle Davis Richardson''' (born March 2, 1973 in Farmington, Missouri) is a retired Americans|American American football|football punter who used to play for the Miami Dolphins, Seattle Seahawks, Baltimore Ravens, Minnesota Vikings, Philadelphia Eagles, Cincinnati Bengals, Cleveland Browns of the National Football League|NFL. He was on the Baltimore Ravens championship team in 2000 when they won Super Bowl XXXV in 2000.
