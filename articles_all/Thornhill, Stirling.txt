'''Thornhill''' is a village in the Scotland|Scottish council area of Stirling (district)|Stirling. It lies 14 miles from Stirling itself, south of Callander, east of Aberfoyle, Stirling|Aberfoyle and west of Doune.

The 2001 census population for the area covered by the Thornhill and Blairdrummond Community Council is 1,109.
