'''Coutances Cathedral''' () is the cathedral in the town of Coutances in Normandy. It is built in Gothic architecture|Gothic style. Standing 80m tall it dominates the city and the surrounding landscape. Its use of long straight vertical lines is typical for many churches in Normandy. The cathedral was started in Romanesque architecture|Romanesque style. It was consecrated in 1056.
