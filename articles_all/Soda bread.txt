
'''Soda bread''' is a kind of soft, golden bread made with baking powder|baking soda. In its most basic form it contains only four :wikt:ingredient|ingredients; flour, salt, baking soda, and buttermilk. It can be changed when you add other ingredients. For instance, if you add a little sugar, some raisins, then cut in some butter, it may become a ''Raisin'' soda bread.

 Preparation 
Soda bread does not use yeast to get its rise, instead the rise comes from baking soda wikt:react|reacting with the acid (soured milk or buttermilk). This makes it a 'quick' bread that can be made in under 15 minutes. The important thing to remember is to have mix and :wikt:knead|knead the dough quickly and lightly. This will give you a bread that is wikt:dense|dense, but not doughy, with a tender, slightly :wikt:moist|moist :wikt:interior|interior. Once the dough is formed into a round, a cross is cut on the top of the bread, which divides it into quarters called farls.
