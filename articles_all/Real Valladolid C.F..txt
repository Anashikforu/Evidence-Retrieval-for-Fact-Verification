

'''Real Valladolid Club de Fútbol''' is a Spain|Spanish football team (also known as '''Pucela'''). It was founded on 20 june, 1928 and holds its matches at Nuevo José Zorrilla stadium,

Valladolid plays in the La Liga|LFP (Spanish Football League).

The team has a reserve team in the Third División (4th tier of the Spanish league).

Its the just team from its region in play Europeans competitions: UEFA Cup (1984-85, 1997-98) and UEFA Cup Winners' Cup (1989-90).

At 1983-84 they made its most succesful season after won the former Spanish League Cup|League Cup to Atlético de Madrid 0-3.
