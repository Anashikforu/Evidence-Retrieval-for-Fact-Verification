The '''Arroyo Seco''' (Spanish language|Spanish for "dry creek") is a stream in Los Angeles County, California. It is a major tributary to the Los Angeles River. The stream begins near Mount Wilson in the San Gabriel Mountains, and flows into a reservoir created by the Devil's Gate Dam. After flowing away from the reservoir, the stream flows in a concrete channel through La Cañada Flintridge, California|La Cañada Flintridge on the west and Altadena, California|Altadena on the east; then through Pasadena, California|Pasadena, South Pasadena, California|South Pasadena, and the neighborhoods of Highland Park, Los Angeles, California|Highland Park and Montecito Heights, Los Angeles, California|Montecito Heights in Los Angeles before flowing into the Los Angeles River at Elysian Park, California|Elysian Park. Its watershed covers 46.7 square miles (121 square kilometers).
