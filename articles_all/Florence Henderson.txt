

'''Florence Agnes Henderson''' (February 14, 1934 &ndash; November 24, 2016) was an Americans|American actress and singer. She starred as Carol Brady in ''The Brady Bunch'' between 1969 and 1974. She was wikt:host|hosting her own talk show, ''The Florence Henderson Show'', on television.

Henderson died suddenly of heart failure at a hospital in Los Angeles, California on November 24, 2016, aged 82.
