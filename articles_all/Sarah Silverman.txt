
'''Sarah Kate Silverman''' (born December 1, 1970) is an Americans|American stand-up comedienne, writer, actress, singer and musician. Although usually credited as ''Sarah Silverman,'' she is sometimes credited by her nickname, ''Big S.'' Her satire|satirical comedy addresses social taboos and controversy|controversial topics such as racism, sexism and religion.
