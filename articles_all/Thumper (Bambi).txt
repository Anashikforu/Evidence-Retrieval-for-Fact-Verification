
'''Thumper''' is a fictional rabbit in the The Walt Disney Company|Disney movies ''Bambi'' and ''Bambi II''. He is Bambi (character)|Bambi's sidekick. Thumper has a total of 4 sisters and a girlfriend called blossom or miss bunny in some countries. Thumper and bambi are also both friends with Flower the skunk
