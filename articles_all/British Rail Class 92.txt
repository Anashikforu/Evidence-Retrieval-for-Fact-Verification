


The '''Class 92''' is a dual-voltage Great Britain|British rail transport|railway locomotive which can run on List of current systems for electric rail traction|25 kV alternating current|AC from overhead lines or 750 V direct current|DC from a third rail. It was designed specifically to operate services through the Channel Tunnel between Britain and France. Eurotunnel indicates the Class 92 locomotive as the reference for other locomotives which railway undertakings might want to get certified for usage in the Channel tunnel.
