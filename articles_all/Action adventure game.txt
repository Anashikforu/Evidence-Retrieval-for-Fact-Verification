An '''action-adventure game''' is a video game video game genre|genre that uses gameplay from adventure game|adventure and action game|action games. Popular games in this genre are The Legend of Zelda (series)|''The Legend of Zelda'' series, the ''Castlevania'' series, the ''God of War (series)|God of War'' series and the Metroid (series)|''Metroid'' series. The action-adventure game genre consists of the player, who goes on adventures, that are usually filled with monsters who the character has to fight.
