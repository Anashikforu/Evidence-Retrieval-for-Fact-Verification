
The '''King of Saudi Arabia''' is the head of state and head of government of Saudi Arabia. He serves as the head of the Saudi monarchy &mdash; House of Saud. The King is called the '''Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques''' () in 1986.

Their royal palace is the King’s Palace, Riyadh|King’s Palace in Riyadh.
