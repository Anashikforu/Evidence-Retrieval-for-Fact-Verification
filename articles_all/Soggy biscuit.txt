'''Soggy biscuit''' (also called “'''limp biscuit'''”, “'''landscapers lunch'''”, “'''crispy cookie'''”, “'''ookie cookie'''”, “'''jizzcuit'''” or “'''jogo do pão'''”) is a male masturbation game played mostly by teenage groups in which the participants stand around a biscuit masturbating until Ejaculation|ejaculating onto it; the last person to do so must eat the biscuit.Jonathon Green, ''The Cassell Dictionary of Slang'', 1998, page 1110.

Although the way it is spelled may differ slightly, the notability of the game is shown by the variations of it being referred to in popular culture, examples including Stephen Fry's ''The Liar'', the German movie ''Crazy (film)|Crazy'', the 2006 film Sleeping Dogs Lie, the "Chains" episode of Blackadder II, the "Freaks & Greeks" episode of Drawn Together (2004-2006), the "Sleepover" episode of Big Mouth, Limp Bizkit, and Skinless's song "Scum Cookie".
