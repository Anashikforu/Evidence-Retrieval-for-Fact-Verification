

'''Greeeen''' or '''The Green''' is a Japanese J-pop group. The group released their first album in 2007. Two of their most successful songs are "Ai uta" and "Kiseki".

GReeeeN is a group of four singers:
Hide (born April 3, 1980)
Navi (born April 30, 1980)
Soh (born February 2, 1981)
92 (born March 21, 1982)
