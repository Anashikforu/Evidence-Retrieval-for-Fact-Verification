The Midland Railway (MR) experimented with Railway electrification system|electrification on its Heysham-Morecambe-Lancaster, Lancashire|Lancaster line. Electric trains started to run over this route in 1908. The system used was 6,600 Volt, 25 Utility frequency|Hz alternating current from overhead lines.  Power was generated at the MR's own power station in Heysham.
