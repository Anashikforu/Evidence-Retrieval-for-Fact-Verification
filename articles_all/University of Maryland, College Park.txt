
The '''University of Maryland, College Park''', often called simply the '''University of Maryland''', is a public university in College Park, Maryland. It is the largest university in a group called the "University System of Maryland."

 History 
In 1856, the Maryland agriculture|Agricultural College was wikt:charter|chartered. Sixty years later, the state of Maryland took control of the college and renamed it Maryland State University. In 1920, it joined several professional schools in the area to become the University of Maryland. It joined the Association of American Universities in 1925.
