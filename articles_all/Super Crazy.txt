
'''Francisco Pantoja Rueda''' (born on December 3, 1973 in Tulancingo, Hidalgo (state)|Hidalgo, Mexico) is an Mexico|Mexican professional wrestling|professional wrestler who is best known for the time he spent the World Wrestling Entertainment|WWE under the ring name, '''Super Crazy'''.

Super Crazy has also worked for Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) and it was there where he gained American attention.

Super Crazy is currently wrestling on the Independent circuit under the organization, ''Pro Wrestling Guerrilla''.
