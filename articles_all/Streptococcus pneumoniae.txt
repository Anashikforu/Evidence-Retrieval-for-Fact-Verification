


'''''Streptococcus pneumoniae''''', or '''pneumococcus''', is a gram-positive, Hemolysis (microbiology)|alpha-hemolytic diplococcus aerotolerant anaerobe and a member of the genus ''Streptococcus''. An important human pathogen, ''S. pneumoniae'' was recognized as a major cause of pneumonia in the late 19th century and is the subject of many humoral immunity studies.
