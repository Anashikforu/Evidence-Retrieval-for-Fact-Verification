
'''''Going the Distance''''' is a 2004 in movies|2004 Canada|Canadian movie. It was film director|directed by Mark Griffiths. It is set across Canada. Its tagline was "They came. They saw. They came." The film was released in Canada as ''Going the Distance'', but in the USA, it was called ''National Lampoon's Going the Distance''. The Canadian DVD release has its original release title.
