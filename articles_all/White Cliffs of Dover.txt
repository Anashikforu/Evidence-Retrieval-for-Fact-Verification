
The '''White Cliffs of Dover''' are cliffs in Dover, England. The cliffs are a part of the North Downs cliff formation. The cliffs are white because they are mostly chalk with bits of flint mixed in. These cliffs are a major tourism|tourist destination for thousands of people every year. The cliffs are also the biggest cliffs in Great Britain.
