
'''Wood County''' is a county in the U.S. state|state of Wisconsin in the United States.  75,555 people were living in Wood County as of 2000.  The county seat of Wood County is Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin|Wisconsin Rapids.  

Geography
Wood County has a total area of 809 square miles, says the United States Census Bureau|U.S. Census Bureau.
