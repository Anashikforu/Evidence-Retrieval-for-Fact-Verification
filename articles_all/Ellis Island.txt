
'''Ellis Island''' is an island near New York City. It was used for immigration|immigrants coming from other country|countries to the United States from 1892 until 1954. Some immigrants to other countries, like Canada, also went through the island. It is located in Upper New York harbor, north of Liberty Island (Statue of Liberty) and east of Liberty state park.
