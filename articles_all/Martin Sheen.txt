
'''Martin Sheen''' (born August 3, 1940) is a Spanish Americans|Spanish-Irish people|Irish-Americans|American actor. He is best known for his role as ''Captain Willard'' in the film ''Apocalypse Now'' and, most recently, as President ''Josiah Bartlet'' on the acclaimed and long-running television drama series ''The West Wing (TV series)|The West Wing''. He won both the Emmy Award|Emmy and Golden Globe Awards for his works. His mother was an Irish people|Irish immigrant; his father was a Spain|Spanish immigrant.
