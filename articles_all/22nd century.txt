
The '''22nd century''' is the next century.  It will begin on January 1, 2101 and will end on December 31, 2200.

 Astronomical predictions for the 22nd century 
 There will be 239 lunar eclipses
Solar eclipses:
 June 25, 2150: a total solar eclipse with longer than 7 minutes of totality. This will be the first time this has happened in 177 years.
 July 5, 2168: 7 minutes 26 seconds of totality
 July 16, 2186: 7 minutes 29 seconds (close to the theoretical maximum). This is predicted to be the longest eclipse during the 8,000 year period from 3rd millennium BC|3000 BC to CE 5000 (eclipse predictions by Fred Espenak, NASA).
 August 2113: First time Pluto reaches aphelion since its discovery.
 December 11, 2117: transit of Venus
 2123: Triple Conjunction (astronomy and astrology)|conjunction Mars (planet)|Mars-Jupiter (planet)|Jupiter
2123 June 9: Long lunar eclipse.
 September 14, 2123: At 15:28 Universal Time|UTC, Venus (planet)|Venus will occultation|occult Jupiter
 December 8, 2125: Transit of Venus
 July 29, 2126: At 16:08 UTC, Mercury (planet)|Mercury will occultation|occult Mars
 March 10, 2130: At 07:32 UTC, Sun passes through solar system barycenter
 December 3, 2133: At 14:14 UTC, Mercury will occultation|occult Venus
 2134: Comet Halley will return to the inner solar system
 2148: Triple Conjunction (astronomy and astrology)|conjunction Mars-Saturn (planet)|Saturn
 2170: Triple Conjunction (astronomy and astrology)|conjunction Mars-Jupiter
