



The '''pictorella mannikin''' (''Heteromunia pectoralis'') (also known as the '''pictorella munia''' or '''pictorella finch''') is a species of bird in the family (biology)|family Estrildidae. They live in northern Australia, from the Fitzroy River in Western Australia, across the "Top End" of the Northern Territory, over to the Cape York Peninsula.
