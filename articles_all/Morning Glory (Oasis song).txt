"'''Morning Glory'''" is a song by the rock and roll|rock band Oasis (band)|Oasis.  The song was written by Noel Gallagher, and which was released on the band's second album (What's the Story) Morning Glory? in September 1995.  It was given an Australian-only single release and was also a Promotional single|radio single in the United States.
