

'''''Calibrachoa''''' is a genus of the Solanaceae family. Plants of this genus can be used as potted plants. The plants are a lot like Petunias. Petunias and ''Calibrachoa'' together make up the clade Petunieae.Richard G. Olmstead und Lynn Bohs: A Summary of Molecular Systematic Research in Solanaceae: 1982-2006. In: D.M. Spooner et al. (ed.): Solanaceae VI: Genomics Meets Biodiversity, ISHS Acta Horticulturae 745, June 2007
