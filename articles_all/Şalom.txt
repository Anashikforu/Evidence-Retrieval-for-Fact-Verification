
'''''Şalom''''' is a Jewish weekly newspaper published in Turkey. Its name is the Turkish language|Turkish spelling of the Hebrew language|Hebrew word ''shalom'' meaning "Peace". It was established on 29 October 1947 by the Turkey|Turkish Jewish journalist Avram Leyon. It is printed in Istanbul and is published every Wednesday. Apart from one Ladino language|Ladino page, it is published in Turkish language|Turkish. İvo Molinas is its publisher. Yakup Barokas is its editor-in-chief. Its circulation is about 5,000.
