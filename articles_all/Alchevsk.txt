'''Alchevsk''' () is a city in the Luhansk Oblast of south-eastern Ukraine.

Alchevsk is one of the largest industrial centers in the Donbas, and comprises a quarter of the wikt:oblast|oblast's entire production. The city economy depends on OJSC "Alchevsk Iron & Steel Works" and "Alchevsk Coke (fuel)|Coke-Chemical Plant" companies.

The city was known as ''Voroshilovsk'' () until 1992.
