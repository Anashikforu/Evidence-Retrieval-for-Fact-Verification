An '''oil refinery''' or '''petroleum refinery''' is an industrial Factory|plant where crude oil is made into useful petroleum products by fractional distillation and other processes. Products include gasoline, diesel fuel, asphalt|asphalt base, heating oil, kerosene, and liquefied petroleum gas.
