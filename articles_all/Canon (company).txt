
 is a Japanese company that makes Image|imaging and optics|optical product (consumer goods)|products. These include cameras, photographic film|film, image scanners and computer printers. The company was started in 1937.

 History 
Canon began as the '''Precision Optical Instruments Laboratory'''. In 1934, the first Japanese camera|35mm focal plane-shutter camera prototype was made. It was called the "Kwanon". In the next few years, Canon became very important in creating new camera products and making them better.

Today, Canon creates everything from digital cameras and camcorders to computer printer|laser printers and film.

 Name origins 
The name Canon came from the company's first 35&nbsp;mm camera. It was named "Kwanon" after the Buddhism|Buddhist deity|goddess of wikt:mercy|mercy.

 Locations 
Canon is now an international company. Canon has buildings in the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and the South Seas.
