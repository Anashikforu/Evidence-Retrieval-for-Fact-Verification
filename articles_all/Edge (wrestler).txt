
'''Adam Joseph Copeland''' (born October 30, 1973 in Orangeville, Ontario)Copeland, Adam. ''Adam Copeland On Edge'' (p.16) is a Canadians|Canadian actor and retirement|retired professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is sign to  WWE  smackdown brand where he wrestled under the ring name '''Edge'''.

He was forced into early retirement from professional wrestling due to a injury to his neck.

Since retiring, Copeland has regularly appeared on the Syfy series ''Haven'' where he plays the character Dwight Hendrickson. Edge make a shocking return to WWE January 26th 2020 in the royal rumble

On the January 27 Episode Of Raw, Edge was attacked by his former tag team partner Randy Orton. He returned defeated Orton
