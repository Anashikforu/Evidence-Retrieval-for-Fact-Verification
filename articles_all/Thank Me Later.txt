'''''Thank Me Later''''' is the debut studio album by Canada|Canadian rapper Drake (musician)|Drake. ''Thank Me Later'' was released on June 15, 2010. The album appeared at #1 on the US Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200 chart. It sold 447,000 copies in the United States in its first week. It received mostly positive reviews from music critics.
