


'''''Midnight Madness''''' is a 1980 comedy movie from Walt Disney Pictures|Walt Disney Productions, starring David Naughton (actor)|David Naughton. The movie is about a group of college students who participate in an all night puzzle solving race. It is Michael J. Fox's first film role.

The movie did not do well in theaters and many critics did not like it. However, the movie has gained a cult following, and has been the inspiration for many real-life puzzle and alternate reality games.
