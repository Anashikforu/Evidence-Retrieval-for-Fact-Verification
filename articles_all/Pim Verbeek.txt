
'''Pim Verbeek''' (12 March 1956 &ndash; 28 November 2019) was a Dutch people|Dutch Association football|football player turned coach (sport)|coach. His brother Robert Verbeek is also a football coach.

The Korea Football Association signed him to a coaching contract on 26 June 2000
. He was appointed as coach of Oman national football team|Oman at 2016, after Oman's failure to qualify for the 2018 FIFA World Cup.

On 12 February 2019, Sparta Rotterdam announced that they had reached an agreement with Pim Verbeek about joining the club as a member of the board.
Verbeek died of cancer on 28 November 2019 at the age of 63.
