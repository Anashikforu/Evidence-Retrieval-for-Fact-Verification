
'''Oberallgäu''' is a rural district in Swabia (Government Region)|Swabia in southwest Bavaria, Germany. Neighbor districts are: Unterallgäu, Ostallgäu, and Lindau (district) and in Baden-Württemberg: ''Ravensburg'' (district); The district-free city of Kempten (Allgäu)|Kempten, in the middle of the district, is not part of it. To the south of the district are the Austrian states Tyrol (state)|Tyrol and Vorarlberg. Sonthofen is the capital (city)|capital of the ''Oberallgäu''.
