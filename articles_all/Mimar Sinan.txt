

'''Mimar Sinan''' or '''Koca Mi'mâr Sinân Âğâ''' (Ottoman Turkish language|Ottoman Turkish: ; Turkish language|Modern Turkish: '''Mimar Sinan''') (15 April 1489  - 9 April 1588) was an Ottoman Empire|Ottoman architect and civil engineer. He worked for sultans Suleiman the Magnificent, Selim II, and Murad III. He worked about fifty years for Ottoman Empire. His masterpiece is the Selimiye Mosque (Edirne)|Selimiye Mosque in Edirne. His most famous work is the Suleiman Mosque in Istanbul.
