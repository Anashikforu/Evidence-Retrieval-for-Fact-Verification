'''Mercury(I) bromide''', also known as '''mercurous bromide''', is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is Hg2Br2. It has mercury (element)|mercury and bromide ions in it. The mercury is in its +1 oxidation state.

Properties
Mercury(I) bromide is a white crystalline solid. It is highly toxic. It easily disproportionation|disproportionates to mercury(II) bromide and mercury metal. 

Occurrence
It is found very rarely as kuzminite, a mercury(I) bromide and mercury(I) chloride|chloride.

Preparation
It is made by reacting a bromide like potassium bromide or sodium bromide with mercury(I) nitrate. It can also be made by reacting mercury and bromine.
