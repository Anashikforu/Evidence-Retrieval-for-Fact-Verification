


'''Patrick Chukwuemeka Okogwu''' (born 7 November 1988) is a British people|British Grime Rapping|MC and rapper. He was born in Plumstead, London and is of Nigerian origin. He is better known by his stage name '''Tinie Tempah'''. He also founded the record label Disturbing London in 2006. 

Some of his more famous songs are "Pass Out", "Written in the Stars" and "Earthquake".

Tempah released his first album ''Disc-Overy'' in 2010. It went straight to number one in the UK Albums Chart. Seven singles were released from the album.

On 4 August 2013, the first single from ''Demonstration'', "Trampoline", was released. 2 Chainz rapped in it. It reached number three in the UK Singles Chart.
