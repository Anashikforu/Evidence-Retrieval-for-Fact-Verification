A '''cubic foot''' is a measure of volume. It is the amount of liquid that can be fit into a box 1 foot (length)|foot deep, one foot wide, and one foot in length. A cubic foot can be written ft3. It is used in the United States, and partially in Canada, and the United Kingdom.
