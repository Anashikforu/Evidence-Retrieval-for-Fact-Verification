'''Liz Cohen''' (born 1973) is a performance artist.  Her best known project is called ''Bodywork''. She changed an old German Democratic Republic|East German small car (a Trabant), so that it can transform into a larger American car (a Chevrolet).  She hired a personal trainer and dieting|dieted so she could appear in a bikini to be the model for the car.

She currently teaches at Cranbrook Academy of Art and lives in Detroit, Michigan.
