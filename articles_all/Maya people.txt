
The '''Maya people''' form a diverse range of the Native American peoples of southern Mexico and northern Central America. The Mayan civilization was powerful, lasting for over a thousands of years.

The most populations of Maya are in the Mexican states of Yucatán (state)|Yucatán, Campeche, Quintana Roo, Tabasco (state)|Tabasco, and Chiapas, and in the Central American countries of Belize, Guatemala, and the western portions of Honduras and El Salvador.
