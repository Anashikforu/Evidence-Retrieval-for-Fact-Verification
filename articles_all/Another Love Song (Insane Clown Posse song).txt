

"'''Another Love Song''''" is a 1999 midwest Hip hop music|hip hop song by the United States|American Horrorcore band Insane Clown Posse and is the first single (music)|single from their fifth studio album ''The Amazing Jeckel Brothers'', which was released in 1999. The song was produced by Insane Clown Posse and Mike E. Clark|Mike Clark. The main music for "Another Love Song" comes from the 1996 single "Jack-Ass" from Beck.
