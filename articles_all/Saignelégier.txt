

'''Saignelégier''' is a municipality in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland. It is the seat of the Franches-Montagnes (district)|district of Franches-Montagnes. 

The bog and nature preserve around étang de la Gruère is found in the municipality.

On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Goumois and Les Pommerats merged into the municipality of Saignelégier.
