
'''Peter Doherty''' (born 12 March 1979) is an English people|English musician. He was born in Hexham, Northumberland. He is in the popular Rock music|rock band The Libertines. He is also in a band called Babyshambles.

He receives a lot of media coverage for his drug and law|legal problems and the intimate relationship|relationship he had with Clothing|fashion Model (person)|supermodel Kate Moss. Doherty has been convicted of many crimes.  Some of the crimes are drug offences, car theft,  He has served prison sentences for drug possession and burglary.
