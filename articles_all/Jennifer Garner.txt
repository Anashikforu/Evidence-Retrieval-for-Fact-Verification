

'''Jenny Anne Garner''' (born April 17, 1972) is a Golden Globe Award- and SAG Award-winning and Emmy Award-nominated United States|American movie and television actress, and producer. She first became famous for her role as ''Sydney Bristow'' on ''Alias (TV series)|Alias'', a CIA agent. She was also in Juno (movie)|Juno, Pearl Harbor, and Dude, Where's My Car.

Garner joined the Ban Bossy campaign in 2014.
