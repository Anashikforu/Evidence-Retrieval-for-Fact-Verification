 
A '''head coach''', '''senior coach''' or '''manager''' is a person who wikt:train|trains and wikt:develop|develops men and women who play sports. They usually hold a more public profile and are paid more than other Coach (sport)|coaches on the team. In some sports such as Association football|soccer and baseball, the head coach is called a manager (baseball)|manager.  In other sports, such as Australian rules football, they are called a senior coach.  Some sports that use the phrase "head coach" include American football and basketball.  

These head coaches sometimes have other coaches that report to them.  These wikt:assistant|assistant coaches focus on one part of the team rather than the whole team.  Assistant coaches often work in offense (sports)|offensive positions or defense (sport)|defensive positions. They can sometimes coach just one position on the team such as a coach just for the quarterbacks in American football.
