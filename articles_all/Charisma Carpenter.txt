Carpenter was born in Las Vegas, Nevada. At 15, her family moved to Rosarito B.C., Mexico. Later, they moved to Chula Vista (a suburb of San Diego). After graduation|graduating high school, she was a San Diego Chargers cheerleader in 1991 before beginning her Hollywood career. She had also skydiving|sky-dived. She marriage|married Damian Hardy on October 5, 2002. She gave birth to their son Donavan Charles Hardy on March 24, 2003.
