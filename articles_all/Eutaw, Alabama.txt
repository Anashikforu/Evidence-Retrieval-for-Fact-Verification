
'''Eutaw''' is a city in Greene County, Alabama|Greene County, Alabama, United States. At the 2000 census the population was 1,878. The city is the county seat of Greene County, Alabama|Greene County and was named after the Revolutionary War battle of Eutaw Springs, South Carolina. It is part of the Tuscaloosa, Alabama Tuscaloosa metropolitan area|Metropolitan Statistical Area.
