

'''''Ned Kelly''''' is a 2003 Australian movie directed by Gregor Jordan. The movie shows the life of Ned Kelly who was a famous bushranger in Australia. The movie is based on Robert Drewe's book ''Our Sunshine''.

Most of the filming was done at the Little River Earth Sanctuary, Mount Rothwell, near Geelong, Victoria|Geelong. Street scenes were filmed in Clunes, Victoria|Clunes and Ballarat, Victoria|Ballarat. Other locations include Broadford, Victoria|Broadford, Glenfern, Victoria|Glenfern, Hepburn Springs, Victoria|Hepburn Springs and Melbourne.
