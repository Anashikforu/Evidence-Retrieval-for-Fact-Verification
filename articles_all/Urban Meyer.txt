
'''Urban Meyer III''' (born July 10, 1964) is an Americans|American college football Coach (sport)|coach. He is the current head coach of the Ohio State University|Ohio State Buckeyes team. He has also coached the Bowling Green University|Bowling Green Falcons, the University of Utah|Utah Utes and University of Florida|Florida Gators from 2005 to 2010."
