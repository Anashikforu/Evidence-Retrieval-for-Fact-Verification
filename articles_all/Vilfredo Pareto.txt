
'''Vilfredo Federico Pareto''' (born '''Wilfried Fritz Pareto''', 1848-1923) was an Italy|Italian engineer, economist, and sociology|sociologist. He made several important contributions to economics; he looked at the Income distribution|distribution of income. Pareto efficiency and Pareto distribution are named after him.
