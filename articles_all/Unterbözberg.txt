

'''Unterbözberg''' is a former municipality of the district of Brugg (district)|Brugg in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Aargau in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Unterbözberg, Gallenkirch, Linn, Switzerland|Linn and Oberbözberg merged into the new municipality of Bözberg.
