

'''Ara Darzi''' (born 7 May 1960) is a United Kingdom|British Medical doctor|doctor. He was appointed head of Minister of Healthcare of Great Britain.

Ara Darzi was born in Iraq in a family with Armenians|Armenian descent, which escaped the Armenian Genocide|Genocide of 1915. But most of his life was lived in Ireland.
