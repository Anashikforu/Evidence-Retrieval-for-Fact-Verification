
'''Maria Yuryevna Kirilenko''' (; born 25 January 1987 in Moscow, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union) is a Russians|Russian professional tennis player. She won her first WTA Tour title in 2005, defeating Anna-Lena Grönefeld 6–3, 6–4 in the China Open. Kirilenko reached #19, her career-high singles ranking, on the Women's Tennis Association|WTA tour in June 2008. She is a good friend of Maria Sharapova. She is affectionately called Makiri.
