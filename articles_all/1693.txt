
The year '''1693''' was a common year which started on a Thursday.

Events
 January 11 &ndash; 1693 Sicily earthquake: Mount Etna erupts, causing a devastating earthquake that affects parts of Sicily and Malta.
 February 8 &ndash; The College of William and Mary in Williamsburg, Virginia, is granted a Royal charter from King William III of England|William III and Queen Mary II of England.

Religion
 Some members of the Anabaptist movement in Switzerland splits from the group to start the Amish.

Births
 February 7 – Empress Anna I of Russia (d. 1740)
 March 7 &ndash; Pope Clement XIII (d. 1769)
 March 24 – John Harrison, English clockmaker (d. 1776)
