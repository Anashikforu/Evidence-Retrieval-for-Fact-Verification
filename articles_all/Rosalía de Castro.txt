
'''Rosalía Castro de Murguía''' better known as '''Rosalía de Castro''' (24 February 1837 – 15 July 1885) was a Galicia (Spain)|Galician writer and poet.

A native of Santiago de Compostela in the Galicia (Spain)|Galicia region of northwest Spain, she wrote in both Galician language|Galician  and Spanish language|Castilian.
