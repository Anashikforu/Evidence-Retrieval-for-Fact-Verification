

'''Alyson Lee Hannigan''' is an Americans|American actress. She was born March 24, 1974 in Washington, D.C. She moved to Atlanta, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia when she was 2 years old to live with her mother. She started to act in advertisement|commercials for McDonalds, Six Flags theme park|amusement parks and Oreo cookies when she was small. When she was 11, she went to Los Angeles, California|Los Angeles with her mother to go to school and become celebrity|famous. She started getting noticed with her roles in ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer'' as Willow Rosenberg and in the ''American Pie (movie)|American Pie'' movies as Michelle Flaherty. She is currently on the television series ''How I Met Your Mother''.
