'''Josef Manner & Comp&nbsp;AG''' is an Austrian company that is known for its candy|sweets. It is the biggest Austrian producer of sweets. Today, they are present in over 60 countries. They are best known for their wafers filled with chocolate-hazelnut cream (called ''Manner-schnitten'').

Its headquarters are in Vienna.
