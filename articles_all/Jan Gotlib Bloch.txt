
'''Jan Gotlib''' (Bogumi≈Ç) '''Bloch''' () was a banker from Poland. He was born in Radom and died in Warsaw. He wikt:donate|donated money to help railways, and studied industry|industrial warfare. Bloch published a collection of writings called ''La Guerre Future'', translated to ''Is War Now Impossible?'', in 1898.
