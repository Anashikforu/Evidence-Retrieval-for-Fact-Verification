

'''Stoke Newington railway station''' is on the Seven Sisters railway station|Seven Sisters branch of the Lea Valley Lines. Trains run to Liverpool Street station|Liverpool Street in central London, and to Cheshunt railway station|Cheshunt and Enfield Town railway station|Enfield Town farther north. Trains generally run every fifteen minutes, and almost all of the trains that pass through the station stop there.

Stoke Newington is in Travelcard Zone 2.
