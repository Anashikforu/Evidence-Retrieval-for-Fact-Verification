'''Sydney William Ward''' (5 August 1907 &ndash; 31 December 2010) was an Australian-born New Zealand cricketer. 

Ward was a right-handed batsman who bowled right-arm bowling|medium pace. He was born in Sydney, New South Wales. He is the second longest-lived cricketer, behind England's Jim Hutchinson, who at the time of his death was 103 years and 344 days old.
