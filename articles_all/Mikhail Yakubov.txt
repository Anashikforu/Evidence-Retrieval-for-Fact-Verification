
'''Mikhail Yakubov''' (born February 16, 1982 in Barnaul, Russian SFSR) is a retired Russians|Russian professional ice hockey Centre (ice hockey)|centre. He played for the Severstal Cherepovets of the Kontinental Hockey League. Yakubov also played in the National Hockey League|NHL for a total of 3 seasons; 2 with the Chicago Blackhawks and 1 with the Florida Panthers. He was drafted by the Chicago Blackhawks with the 10th overall pick in the 2000 NHL Draft.
