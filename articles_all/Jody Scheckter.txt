'''Jody David Scheckter''' (born January 29, 1950) is a South African former auto racing driver. He won the Formula One driver's title in 1979 driving for Scuderia Ferrari|Ferrari.

He is only South African ever to win the formula one driver's title. Scheckter is of Jewish descent.
