'''Hyderābād District''' (), is a Districts of Pakistan|district of Sindh, Pakistan. Before, it was an administrative division of the Sindh called the '''Hyderabad Division.''' However, reforms of 2000 Wikt:abolish|abolished the third tier of government. Its capital is the city of Hyderabad, Sindh|Hyderabad. The Kirthar National Park is in the Hyderabad district. According to the 1998 census of Pakistan, it had a population of 4,339,445 of which 50.07% were urban (lived in a city). This makes it the second-most Urbanization|urbanised district of Sindh.
