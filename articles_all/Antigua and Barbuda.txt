

'''Antigua and Barbuda''' is an island nation in the eastern Caribbean Sea on the boundary with the Atlantic Ocean. Antigua and Barbuda are part of the Lesser Antilles archipelago.  In 2011, about 81,800 people lived there.  The capital and largest port and city is St. John's, Antigua and Barbuda|St. John's, on Antigua.  The country used to be a part of the British Empire. The nation still belongs to the British Commonwealth.

Economy
Tourism is the main part of the economy.  It is more than half of the Gross Domestic Product (GDP). Antigua is famous for its many luxury resorts. Investment banking and financial services are also important.

Military
The Royal Antigua and Barbuda Defence Force has 285 members.

Languages
English is the official language, but many of the locals speak Antiguan Creole. The Barbudan accent is slightly different from the Antiguan.
