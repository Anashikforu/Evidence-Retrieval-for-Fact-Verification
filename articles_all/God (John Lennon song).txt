
"'''God'''" is a song from John Lennon's first solo album, ''Plastic Ono Band (John Lennon album)|John Lennon/Plastic Ono Band''.  The album was released on December 11, 1970 in the United States|U.S. and the United Kingdom|UK.  

The song was considered controversial upon release, because it was about religion.
