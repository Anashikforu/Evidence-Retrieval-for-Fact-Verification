A '''commentary''' often means some sort of voice that will explain what is going on while a television programme|program is playing. Commentary is most often used in sports. Some famous commentators are Jim Ross, Larry Merchant, Jesse Ventura, Navjot Singh Sidhu and Gorilla Monsoon.

A commentary can mean comments, spoken or written, which help people understand a book, Play (theatre)|play or work of art.
