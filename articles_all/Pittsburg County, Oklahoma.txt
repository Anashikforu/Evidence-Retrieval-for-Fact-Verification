
'''Pittsburg County''' is a county in the U.S. state of Oklahoma.  As of 2000, 43,953 people lived there.  Its county seat is McAlester, Oklahoma|McAlester. It's the hometown to the Pittsburgh players who won 6 times in the Super Bowl. 

 Geography 
The county has a total area of 906 square miles.
