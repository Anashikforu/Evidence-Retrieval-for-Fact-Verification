

'''Mickie Laree James''' (born August 31, 1979) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler and singer.

She worked for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) on its WWE SmackDown|SmackDown and WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brands. She also worked for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling from 2010 to 2013.

James started wrestling in 1999 as a Manager (professional wrestling)|valet on the independent circuit using the name Alexis Laree. She trained in several places to get better at wrestling before working for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) in June 2002. She first became well known while she was while working at TNA.

James started working with WWE in October 2005. She was a part of a storyline with Trish Stratus. James' List of professional wrestling slang#G|gimmick was that she was Stratus' biggest wikt:fan|fan and later became Stratus' stalker. This storyline lasted almost a year.

Outside of wrestling, James has an associate of arts Degree (school)|degree in business administration.  She is studying for her Bachelor of Arts degree in operations management.

She released her debut album ''Strangers & Angels'' on May 18, 2010 on iTunes. Her second album ''Somebody's Gonna Pay'' was released on May 18, 2010 and reached #15 on the Top Heatseekers|Billboards Heatseekers charts. The album contains the song "Hardcore Country" which James uses as her current theme song in TNA.

Early life
Mickie James was raised on a farm with her sister Latoya in Montpelier, Hanover County, Virginia|Montpelier, Virginia. She is of Native American heritage. As a child, she watched weekend horse shows. James started riding horses when she was eleven years old after her grandmother bought her a horse. James was a fan of professional wrestling as a child. She used to watch wrestling with her father. Her favorite wrestlers were Randy Savage, Ricky Steamboat and Ric Flair.

Career
=Early career=
James started her training at a List of professional wrestling terms#S|professional wrestling school in the Washington, D.C. area. In March, she wrestled in her first match. It was an Professional wrestling tag team match types#Intergender tag team match|intergender tag team match with Jake Damian against American Mike Brown and Candie. James trained to get better at wrestling at many places. These places include the Funking Conservatory run by Dory Funk, Jr. and an Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) dojo. She also started wrestling for Maryland Championship Wrestling. While there, she was trained by Ricky Morton and Bobby Eaton. Because the independent circuit did not pay enough, James worked as a Waiting staff|waitress at an Olive Garden restaurant. Trish Stratus, a former WWE wrestler, was and still is Mickie James' role model

=Total Nonstop Action Wrestling=
James worked in Ring of Honor for a year. During this time, she also started wrestling in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling. As Alexis Laree, she was a part of a lingerie Battle royal (professional wrestling)#Divas Battle Royal|battle royal.

=Ohio Valley Wrestling=
James tried for two years to work for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). In August 2003, she was began to work for the WWE. They sent her to train at Ohio Valley Wrestling (OVW). James still used the name Alexis Laree at this time. She began making wresting on television for OVW on January 29, 2004. She wrestled in several tag team matches during the year.

Laree began a feud with Beth Phoenix on July 20. This feud started after Phoenix interrupted Laree during an interview. A match took place on July 29. Laree lost to Phoenix. The feud continued into September. Shelly Martinez was added to the storyline to side with Phoenix against Laree. On October 12, she appeared in OVW under her real name. She finished the year losing matches against Martinez and Jillian Hall.

=World Wrestling Entertainment=
2005–2006
Image:Jamesmarch07.jpg|right|thumb|upright|James at a ''WWE Raw|Raw'' live event
James was first used on television in WWE on the October 10 2005 episode of ''WWE Raw|Raw''. She used her real name. James used the gimmick of WWE Women's Championship (1956–2010)|WWE Women's Champion Trish Stratus' biggest fan.

On March 6 2006 the storyline had Stratus telling James that they needed time apart from each other.

2007–2010
A new feud began between James and Melina on January 29 2007. Melina was the number one contender for the Women's Title at the time.

At a house show in Paris, France on April 24, James won her third Women's Championship. In a Professional wrestling match types#Basic non-elimination matches|Triple Threat Match with Malina and Victoria, James pinned Victoria. However, she did not pin Melina, Jonathan Coachman said that a rematch would take place immediately. James lost the title back to Melina. This gave her the shortest Women’s Championship reign in WWE history.

On the November 26 edition of ''Raw'', James defeated Melina in a number one contenders match for Beth Phoenix's Women's Championship. Mickie James was released from her WWE contract on April 22, 2010.

Personal life
Image:Mickie james in Belfast for the Raw Wrestlemania Revenge tour.jpg|thumb|right|150px|James in April 2008.
James owns three Morgan horses called Rhapsody, Bunny and Casanova. After she has finished her wrestling career, James plans to own a farm and train horses. James also owns a dog named Butch.

She was at one time marriage|engaged to WWE wrestler Ken Doane, better known by his ring name "Kenny Dykstra".

James posed nudity|nude in the May 2000 issue of  ''Leg Show'' magazine.Leg Show Magazine, May 2000 On April 13, 2008, James, along with Layla El|Layla, Melina Perez|Melina and Kelly Kelly, appeared in an episode of ''Celebrity Fit Club (US TV series)|Celebrity Fit Club Boot Camp''.

The WWE sent James, Mr. Kennedy and Josh Matthews to the 2008 Republican National Convention to try and get fans to register to vote in the United States presidential election, 2008|2008 Presidential election.

James made an appearance on ''Redemption Song (TV series)|Redemption Song''. The program is Presenter|hosted by WWE wrestler Chris Jericho. James appeared on the program along with Candice Michelle, Maryse, and Eve Torres. The episode was first aired on November 12, 2008.
