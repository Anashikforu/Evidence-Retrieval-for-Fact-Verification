

'''''Dragon Warrior''''' (called '''''Dragon Quest''''' in Japan) is a role-playing game|role-playing video game made by Chunsoft and published by Enix|Enix Corporation and Nintendo|Nintendo of America for the Nintendo Entertainment System game console.
