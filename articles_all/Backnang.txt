
'''Backnang''' is a town in Germany, in the Bundesland (Germany)|Bundesland of Baden-Württemberg (formerly the kingdom (politics)|kingdom of Württemberg),  northeast of Stuttgart. Its population has grown over the past century or so, from 7,650 (1900) to 35,761 (2005).
