
The '''Phoenix Suns''' are an American professional basketball team that plays in the National Basketball Association (NBA). They play in the Pacific Division (NBA)|Pacific Division of the Western Conference (NBA)|Western Conference. The Suns play at Footprint Center, originally known as America West Airlines|America West Arena and later as US Airways Center, Talking Stick Resort Arena, briefly as PHX Arena, and Phoenix Suns Arena.
