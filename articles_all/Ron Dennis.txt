
'''Ronald "Ron" Dennis''' Order of the British Empire|CBE (born 1 June 1947) is the executive chairman of McLaren Automotive and McLaren Group.  He was the team principal of the McLaren Formula One team until 2009.

He was placed at number 648 in the Sunday Times Rich List 2006 with a wealth of £90 million. ''The Times'' put his wealth at £200&nbsp;million in 2009.
