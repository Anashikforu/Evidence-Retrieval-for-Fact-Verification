
'''Kyoto Station''' (京都駅, ''Kyōto-eki''Help:Japanese|?) is the most important transportation center in Kyoto, Japan.  It has Japan's second-largest train station building (after Nagoya Station). It is also one of the country's largest buildings, with a mall|shopping mall, hotel, movie theater, Isetan department store, and several local government facilities under one 15-story roof.  It also housed the Kyoto City Air Terminal until August 31, 2002.
