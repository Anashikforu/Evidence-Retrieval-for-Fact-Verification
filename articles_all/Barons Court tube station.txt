'''Barons Court''' is a London Underground station on the District Line|District and Piccadilly Line|Piccadilly lines. It is in West Kensington, Greater London. The station is located on Gliddon Road, a short distance from Talgarth Road (A4 road (Great Britain)|A4) in the London Borough of Hammersmith and Fulham. Barons Court is between West Kensington tube station|West Kensington and Hammersmith tube station (Piccadilly and District lines)|Hammersmith on the District Line, and between Earl's Court tube station|Earl's Court and Hammersmith on the Piccadilly Line. It is in Travelcard Zone 2.
