Modern day Thalwil
Today, the economy of Thalwil is mostly some small and medium-sized company|businesses, and a wide range of shops. Most of the shops are located around Gotthardstrasse- the town centre.

The main source of Thalwil's wealth lies with its 7,000 commuting residents|commuters. These people live in Thalwil, but work in Z端rich City|Zurich city. Today, Thalwil has left most of its farming past behind in exchange for a town of commuters, with its public transportation links, low tax and good quality of life.
