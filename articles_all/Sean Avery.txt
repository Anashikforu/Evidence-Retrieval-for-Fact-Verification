
'''Sean Christopher Avery''' (born April 10, 1980 in Pickering, Ontario|Pickering, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|left winger. He played 12 years in the National Hockey League (NHL). He played with the Detroit Red Wings, Los Angeles Kings, New York Rangers, and Dallas Stars. Avery was known for his Pest (ice hockey)|agitating playing style, as well as his controversial behavior both on and off the ice.

He has also worked as an intern for Vogue (magazine)|Vogue,

Avery has been featured on MTV Cribs, Punk'd and TRL and has also opened a sports bar called, Warren 77 in Manhattan.
