

'''Sing: Chapter 1''' is a studio album from country superstar Wynonna Judd, to be released on February 3, 2009. It is her seventh solo studio album and the follow-up to her successful 2003 album ''What the World Needs Now Is Love'' and her holiday-themed 2006 release ''A Classic Christmas''.

 Album information 
Wynonna teamed up with Brent Maher and Don Potter - the duo behind the many successes that Wynonna had as one half of the mother-daughter duo The Judds with mom Naomi.

''Sing'' has been in production for over a year and a half.
