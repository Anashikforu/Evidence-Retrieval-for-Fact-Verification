The '''Maui Hesperomannia''' ('''''Hesperomannia arbuscula''''') is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.
It is found only in the United States.
Its natural habitats are Sub-tropics|subtropical or Tropics|tropical wet low lying land forests and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland (an area with mostly Shrub|bushes and few trees).
It is in danger due to the loss of its wikt:natural|natural area of habitat.
