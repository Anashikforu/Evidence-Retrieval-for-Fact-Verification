The '''Bellingshausen Sea''' is an area along the west side of the Antarctic Peninsula, between Alexander Island and Thurston Island. It takes its name from Admiral Fabian Gottlieb von Bellingshausen|Thaddeus Bellingshausen, who explored in the area in 1821.
