A '''group 12 element''' is a group (periodic table)|group in the periodic table of chemical elements. It includes zinc, cadmium, mercury (element)|mercury, and copernicium. Copernicium is not stable so its properties are not clear. They all have low melting and boiling points. Mercury is even a liquid. Zinc is the least electronegativity|electronegative and most Reactivity (chemistry)|reactive of the Group 12 elements.
