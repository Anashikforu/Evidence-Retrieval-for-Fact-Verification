An '''antenna''' (plural '''antennae''') is one of a pair of feelers (appendages) connected to the front morphogenesis|segments of crustaceans and insects.Concise Oxford Dictionary. They are found on the first two segments of the head. The smaller pair are known as antennules.  

Antennae are jointed and extend forward from the head. They are Sensory system|sensory organs. The organ may sense touch, air motion, heat, vibration (sound), and especially olfaction|smell or taste|gustation (taste).
