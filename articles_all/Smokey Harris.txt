
'''Thomas Wilfred "Smokey, Fred" Harris''' (born October 11, 1890 in Port Arthur, Ontario|Port Arthur, Ontario, Canada – June 4, 1974) was a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey forward (ice hockey)|forward.

Harris began playing professional ice hockey with the Vancouver Millionaires in 1911–12 PCHA season|1911.  After spending some time with the Millionaires he moved on to play for the Seattle Metropolitans and Portland Rosebuds. Eventually he would find his way to the other side of the country to play for the Boston Bruins in the National Hockey League where he scored the first goal in the teams history.
