This is the title history of the WWE Raw Tag Team Championship. Before, it was called the WWE Tag Team Championship and was a WWE SmackDown|SmackDown championship. That ended when it was made into one with the World Tag Team Championship (WWE)|World Tag Team Championship of WWE Raw|Raw in 2009 and the WWE Brand Extension ended in 2011. The brand extension was brought back in July 2016. Then, the title became a Raw championship and was renamed the Raw Tag Team Championship.
