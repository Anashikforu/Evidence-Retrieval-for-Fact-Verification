
'''''Stormbreaker''''' is a 2005 United Kingdom|British movie based on the Anthony Horowitz novel ''Stormbreaker (novel)|Stormbreaker'', part of the Alex Rider series of books.

 Plot 
The plot is the same as the novel.

 Changes 
There are some changes from the novel. For example, the main bad guy, Sayle, is changed from Lebanon|Lebanese to Americans|American, since the actor picked did not have a Lebanese accent.
