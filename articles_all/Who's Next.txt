

'''''Who's Next''''' is the fifth album by the English people|English Rock music|rock band The Who. It was released on July 31, 1971 by Decca Records|Decca and MCA Records|MCA in the United States and August 25, 1971 in the United Kingdom through Track Records|Track and Polydor Records|Polydor. ''Who's Next'' was a huge success when it was released, and has been certified 3x platinum record|platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA.
