

"'''Can't Stop This Thing We Started'''" is a song by Bryan Adams. The song was the second single (music)|single from Adams' 1991 album ''Waking Up the Neighbours'', a successor of the single "(Everything I Do) I Do It for You".

The song
The song is a rock music|rock song unlike "(Everything I Do) I Do It for You". It reached #2 on the Billboard Hot 100, behind Prince (musician)|Prince's "Cream (song)|Cream". This track (music)|track received two wikt:nominate|nominations at the Grammy Awards of 1992 for Grammy Award for Best Rock Song|Best Rock Song and Grammy Award for Best Rock Vocal Performance, Solo|Best Rock Performance, Solo. It did not win either award.
