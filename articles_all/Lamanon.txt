


'''Lamanon''' is a Communes of France|commune of 1,713 people (1999). It is found in the Regions of France|region Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur in the Bouches-du-Rhône Departments of France|department in the south of France. It's known because of the archaeological site of Grottes de Calès, a cave dwelling from prehistoric to medieval times.
