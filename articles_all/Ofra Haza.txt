

'''Ofra Haza''' (Hebrew language|Hebrew: עפרה חזה , full name: ''Bat-Sheva Ofra Haza-Ashkenazi'', 19 November 1957 – 23 February 2000) was an Israel|Israeli (pop) singer. She was born in Tel Aviv and died in Ramat Gan. She died of AIDS and was buried in the Yarkon Cemetery in Petah Tikva.
