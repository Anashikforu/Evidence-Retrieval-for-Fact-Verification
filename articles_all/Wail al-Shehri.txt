
'''Wail al-Shehri''' (وائل الشهري, also transliteration|transliterated '''Alshehri''') was named by the Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI as one of the hijackers of American Airlines Flight 11 from Boston to Los Angeles, California|Los Angeles that crashed into the World Trade Center (1973–2001)|World Trade Center as part of the September 11, 2001 attacks. His brother, Waleed al-Shehri, is also a suspected 9/11 hijacker.
