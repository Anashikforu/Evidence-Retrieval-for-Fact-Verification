

'''''Picross DS''''' is a puzzle video game|puzzle video game made by Jupiter (company)|Jupiter and publishing|published by Nintendo for the Nintendo DS video game console|game console.

Reception

''Picross DS'' got many great reviews. IGN gave it a 9.0/10 and an Editor's Choice Award. The magazine said the game was one of the most addiction|addictive games for the Nintendo DS. They liked it for its downloadable puzzles, create-a-puzzle mode, and the number of puzzles to play. They said that the game's biggest mistake was that it penalty|penalized the player for missing by adding time, but doesn't count how many times the player misses.
