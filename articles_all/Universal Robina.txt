


'''Universal Robina Corporation''' is one of the largest food companies in the Phillipines. URC is a listed as a subsidiary of JG Summit Holdings. This is founded in 1950's by John Gokongwei and it now ventured into packaging, animal food, snacks and beverages sold in more than 50 countries.
The company is famous for its Nips candy, which looks and tastes like M&Ms. Nips are remembered by children from the Philippines.
