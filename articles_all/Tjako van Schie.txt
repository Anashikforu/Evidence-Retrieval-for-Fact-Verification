

'''Tjako van Schie''' (born 17 April 1961 in Coevorden) is a pianist and composer from the Netherlands. At the Amsterdam Conservatory he is professor. Even in China, Van Schie has played piano but he teaches in Holland and Portugal. Van Schie was educated in Zwolle, Overijssel|Zwolle by pianist Ben Smits and a student of New York City|New York pianist Jacob Lateiner. He now lives in the Dutch province Overijssel.
