'''Ardooie''' is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of West Flanders. The municipality includes the towns of Ardooie and Koolskamp. In 2006, Ardooie had a total population of 9,147. The local people are called Ardooienaren.

It is at 50° 58 North, 03° 11 East.
