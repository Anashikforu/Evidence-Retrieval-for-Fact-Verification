

'''Montfaucon''' is a municipality in the district of Franches-Montagnes (district)|Franches-Montagnes in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2009 the former municipality of Montfavergier merged into the municipality of Montfaucon.
