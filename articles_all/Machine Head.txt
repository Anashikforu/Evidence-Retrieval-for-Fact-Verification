
'''Machine Head''' is a Heavy metal music|metal band from Oakland in California. They play a mixture between Thrash metal and Groove metal. The band members right now are: Robb Flynn (guitars, vocals), Jared MacEachern (Bass guitar|bass), Wacław Kiełtyka (lead guitar) and Matt Alston (drums).
