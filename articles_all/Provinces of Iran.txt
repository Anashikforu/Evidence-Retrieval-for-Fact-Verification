'''Iran''' is divided into 31 provinces (Persian Language|Persian: استان ''ostān'', plural استان‌ها ''ostānhā'').  Each is ruled from a local center.  These local centers can usually be found in the biggest local city, called the Capital (city)|capital (Persian Language|Persian: مرکز ''markaz'') of that province.
