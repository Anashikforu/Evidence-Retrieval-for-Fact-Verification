'''Interstate 94''' is an Interstate Highway in the United States. It goes from Billings, Montana east to Port Huron, Michigan at the Canada border. The route is  long. It is the eighth longest Interstate Highway in the United States and the longest Interstate Highway that does not end in a 0 or a 5.
