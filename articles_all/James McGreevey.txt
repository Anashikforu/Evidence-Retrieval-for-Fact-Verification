Image:Jim McGreevey by David Shankbone.jpg|thumb|Jim McGreevey in 2007'''James Edward "'Jim"' McGreevey''' (born August 6, 1957) was the 52nd Governor of New Jersey. In 2004, he said that he was gay. He resigned as governor after he said he had an affair with a male employee. He was the first openly gay governor of any state in the history of the United States.
