
'''Mandello del Lario''' is an Italy|Italian town and ''comune'' in the province of Lecco, in Lombardy. It is on Lake Como. 

Since 1921, Mandello del Lario has been home to the Italy|Italian motorcycle maker Moto Guzzi. Guzzi is owned by  Piaggio|Piaggio & Co. SpA.  Each year since 2001, the commune has hosted GMG (aka Giornata Mondiale Guzzi or Worldwide Guzzi Days).

The Grigna mountain|massif is in Mandello's territory.
