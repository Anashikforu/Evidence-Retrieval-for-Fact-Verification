
'''Bonnières, Pas-de-Calais''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Nord-Pas-de-Calais in the Pas-de-Calais Departments of France|department in the north of France. On 1 January 2019, the former commune Canteleux was merged into Bonnières.
