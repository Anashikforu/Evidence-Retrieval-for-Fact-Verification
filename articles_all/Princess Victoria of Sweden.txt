

'''Victoria, Crown Princess of Sweden''', Duchess of Västergötland (full name in Swedish: '''Victoria Ingrid Alice Désirée''', born 14 July 1977) is the heir apparent|heiress apparent to the throne of Sweden.

 Early life 
Victoria was born on 14 July 1977 at Karolinska University Hospital in Solna. She is eldest child and elder daughter of Carl XVI Gustaf of Sweden|Carl XVI Gustaf and Queen Silvia of Sweden. Victoria has two younger siblings, Prince Carl Philip of Sweden|Prince Carl Philip and Princess Madeleine of Sweden|Princess Madeleine.

 Marriage and children 
Victoria married her former personal trainer, Prince Daniel, Duke of Västergötland|Daniel Westling, on 19 June 2010. They have two children:

 Princess Estelle, Duchess of Östergötland (born 23 February 2012)
 Prince Oscar, Duke of Skåne (born 2 March 2016)
