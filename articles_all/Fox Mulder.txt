
'''Fox Mulder''' is one of the main characters in the science-fiction show The X-Files.  He is an FBI agent working in the section called the X-Files.  He works together with his partner Dana Scully to figure out answers to questions in the X-Files.  The questions he looks at are usually about Extraterrestrial life|aliens or the paranormal.  Later, Mulder begins to date Scully in season 7 of the show.Episode Millennium, season 7 of the X-Files.

Mulder has a sister named Samantha Mulder. He spends a lot of time looking for her. Later in the show, Mulder finds out that his sister was kidnapped by Extraterrestrial life|aliens and the government.
