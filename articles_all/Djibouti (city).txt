

The city of '''Djibouti''' is the capital (city)|capital and largest city of the Djibouti|Republic of Djibouti. It lies on a peninsula between the Gulf of Aden and the Gulf of Tadjoura. Djibouti is home to about  population|400,000 people.

Features of Djibouti City include beaches, a large Market|Central Market, the national stadium Stade du Ville, the Presidential Palace and Hamouli Mosque. The Imperial Railway Company of Ethiopia|Djibouti-Addis Ababa Railway runs from the city to Addis Ababa. Djubouti is also home to the Djibouti-Ambouli International Airport.

 History 
Djibouti was wikt:simple:founder|founded as a Harbor|seaport in 1888. The city became the capital of French Somaliland in 1891. It replaced Tadjourah as the capital.

The peninsula was chosen because it is a natural port. It was attacked during WW2 by the Italians and later by the British.
