The '''Class 376 Suburban Electrostar''' is a model of electric multiple unit|electric multiple-unit passenger train manufactured by Bombardier Transportation. It is part of the Electrostar family, which are the most common EMUs introduced since the privatisation of British Rail. The units were ordered by Connex South Eastern, introduced by South Eastern Trains, and are now operated by Southeastern (train operating company)|Southeastern.
