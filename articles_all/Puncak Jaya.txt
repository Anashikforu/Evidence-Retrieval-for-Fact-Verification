
'''Puncak Jaya''' is a  mountain in New Guinea. It is also called the '''Carstensz Pyramid'''.

The mountain was first named the Carstensz Pyramid, after Jan Carstenszoon. He was a Dutch people|Dutch explorer who saw the glaciers on it in 1623.

Puncak Jaya is thought of by some as the hardest to climb of the Seven Summits.
