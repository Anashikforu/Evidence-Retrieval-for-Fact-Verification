

The '''1999 Formula One''' season was the 50th edition of the championship. The champion was Mika Häkkinen. Scuderia Ferrari|Ferrari won the constructor's championship. Michael Schumacher was injured on the first lap of the British Grand Prix at Silverstone Circuit|Silverstone. His brakes failed, and he broke his leg. He missed 6 races and returned at the Malaysian Grand Prix.
