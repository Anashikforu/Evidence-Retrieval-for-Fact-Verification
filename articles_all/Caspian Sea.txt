The '''Caspian Sea''' is the largest lake on Earth by both area and volume. It has a surface area of . The Caspian has for 40 to 44% of the total lake waters of the world.

The Caspian sea is an Endorheic basin|endorheic body of water. It is bordered by Russia, Azerbaijan, Iran, Turkmenistan and Kazakhstan. It has a maximum depth of about . It is called a sea because when the Ancient Rome|Romans first arrived there, they tasted the water and found it to be salty. It has a salinity of approximately 1.2%, about a third the salinity of sea water.
