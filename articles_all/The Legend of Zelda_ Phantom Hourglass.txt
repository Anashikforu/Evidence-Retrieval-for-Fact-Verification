

 is a part of ''The Legend of Zelda|Legend of Zelda'' series of video games. It is for the Nintendo DS. The game is played only using the touch screen on the Nintendo DS. It is a sequel to the 2002/2003 GameCube game ''The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker''. The game was released in Japan on June 23, 2007, in the United States on October 1, 2007, in Australia on October 11, 2007, and in Europe on October 19, 2007. It was followed by a sequel in 2009, ''The Legend of Zelda: Spirit Tracks'', also released for the DS.

The games story happens after ''The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker'''s story. The player plays as the main fictional character|character of the Zelda games, Link (Legend of Zelda)|Link. The game will let the player sail around a Ocean using a boat. The game also has an online mode.
