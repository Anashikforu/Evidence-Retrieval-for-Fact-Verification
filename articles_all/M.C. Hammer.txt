'''MC Hammer''' (born '''Stanley Kirk Burrell''' on March 30, 1962) is an United States|American rap music|rapper, singer, songwriter, dancer, and actor. He was popular in the late 1980s and early 1990s. Hammer is known for becoming very wikt:rich|rich and well known in a short period of time. His fame and money did not last long though. He lost it all in a very short period. Hammer was also known for the pants he wore. "Hammer Pants" became a fashion trend while he was popular . He became a preacher in the 1990s. Hammer now works as a television host and CEO.

Hammer was a contestant in the first season of ''The Surreal Life''.

Hammer is a Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat and attended the 2012 Democratic National Convention in support of Barack Obama|President Obama's re-election.
