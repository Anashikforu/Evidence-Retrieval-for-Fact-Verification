'''Jafarabad''' or '''Jaffarabad''' () Districts of Pakistan|district is in the southeast of the Pakistani province of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan.

Administration
The district of Jaffarabad is administratively subdivided into the following tehsils:

 Jhat Pat Tehsil
 Usta Mohammad Tehsil
 Gandakha Tehsil
 Suhbat Nagar Tehsil
