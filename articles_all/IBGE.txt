The '''Brazilian Institute of Geography and Statistics''' or '''IBGE''' (), is the agency responsible for statistical, geography|geographic, cartographic, geodetic and Natural environment|environmental information in Brazil. The IBGE performs a national census every ten years, and the questionnaires account for information such as age, household income, literacy, education, occupation and hygiene levels.
The IBGE's headquarters is located in Rio de Janeiro.
