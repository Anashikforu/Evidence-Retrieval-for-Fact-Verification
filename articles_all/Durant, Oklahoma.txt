
'''Durant''' is a city in Bryan County, Oklahoma|Bryan County, Oklahoma in the United States. It is the county seat of Bryan County, Oklahoma|Bryan County. The population was 15,877 at the United States Census, 2010|2010 census.

Durant is home to Southeastern Oklahoma State University and the headquarters of the Choctaw Nation of Oklahoma. The city is officially known as the Magnolia tree|Magnolia Capital of Oklahoma.
