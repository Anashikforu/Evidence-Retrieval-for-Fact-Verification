'''Thymine''', or 5-methyluracil, is a pyrimidine Base (chemistry)|base found in the nucleic acid DNA.

In RNA thymine is swapped for uracil to represent the same genetic information.  In DNA, thymine (T) connects to adenine (A) by two hydrogen Chemical bond|bonds. These pair bonds hold the two strands of the DNA double helix structure together.
