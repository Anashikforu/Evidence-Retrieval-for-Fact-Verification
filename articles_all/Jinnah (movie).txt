
'''''Jinnah''''' is a biographical movie about the :wikt:founder|founder of Pakistan, Muhammad Ali Jinnah. It was movie director|directed by Jamil Dehlavi and written by Akbar S. Ahmed and Jamil Dehlavi. The movie was released in 1998 in London, the United Kingdom and Pakistan.
