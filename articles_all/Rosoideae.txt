

'''Rosoideae''' is a subfamily of plants in the rose family (biology)| family Rosaceae. These plants may be small trees, shrubs, or plants without wood. Many useful plants are in Rosoideae. Strawberry|Strawberries and Raspberry|Raspberries are two common fruits from Rosoideae. Roses are common flowers in Rosoideae.
