
'''Richard Erwin Rood''' (October 8, 1980-April 20, 1999), was an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is best known by his ring name '''"Ravishing" Rick Rude''' and wrestled for the professional wrestling promotions World Championship Wrestling (WCW), Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) and World Wrestling Federation (WWF) from 1987 to 1998. In his wrestling career he was a one-time WWE Intercontinental Championship|WWF Intercontinental Champion and won numerous championships in WCW and ECW.
