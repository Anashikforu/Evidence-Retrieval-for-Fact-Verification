The '''Armenian Air Defense''' is the anti-aircraft group of the Armed Forces of Armenia. It was a part of the military reform program of Ter-Grigoriants. 

Armenian Anti-aircraft warfare|anti-aircraft defense has an anti-aircraft missile brigade and two regiments armed with 100 missile launchers of various models and fixes. These missile launchers include the M79 Osa, Lyulev 9M8 Krug|Krug, S-75, S-125, and S-300. There are also 24 Scud ballistic missiles with eight launchers.
