
The '''2009 US Open''' was a tennis event played on Tennis court|outdoor hard courts, held between August 31 and September 14, 2009, at the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center in Flushing Meadows-Corona Park|Flushing Meadows, New York City, New York (state)|New York, United States.

The event was originally scheduled to be a 14-day event, but due to rain at the 2009 US Open – Men's Singles|men's singles final it was extended by one day.
