

'''Bush''' were a United Kingdom|British post-grunge band, formed in London in 1992. Their debut album was the self-released ''Sixteen Stone'' in 1994 in music|1994. They have sold well over 10 million records in the United States. The band, while hugely successful in the United States, was less well known in their native UK and enjoyed only marginal success there.

Members
 Gavin Rossdale – lead vocals, guitar
 Nigel Pulsford – lead guitar, string arrangements, background vocals (1992-2002)
 Chris Traynor – lead guitar (2002)
 Dave Parsons – bass
 Robin Goodridge – drums, percussion
 Sacha Puttnam – keyboards, background vocals
