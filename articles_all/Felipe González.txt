

 
'''Felipe González Márquez''' (born 5 March 1942) is a Spain|Spanish Socialism|socialist politician. He was the General Secretary of the Spanish Socialist Workers' Party (PSOE) from 1974 to 1997. To date, he remains the longest-serving Prime Minister of Spain, after having served four successive mandates from 1982 to 1996. He is married and has three children.
