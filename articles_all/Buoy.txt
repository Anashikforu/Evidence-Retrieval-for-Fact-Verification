A '''buoy''' is a Buoyancy|floating device that can have many uses. It is usually anchored in a certain place. The word is most commonly said (.


Types

=Navigational aids=
Buoys are used by ships to navigate or sail safely from place to place. They are used to mark a safe channel on a river, lake or harbour. They are used to mark dangerous rocks and other items which ships have to keep clear of.


=Other uses=
Buoys are used as wikt:marker|marker for fishing, moorings for boats, yacht or dinghy racing, diving and may be marked on charts as "Superbuoys".
