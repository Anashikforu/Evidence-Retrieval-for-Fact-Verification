
'''Michael Elliot "Mike" Epps''' (born November 18, 1970) is an Americans|American actor. His movies include ''Next Friday'', ''Friday After Next'', and ''Open Season 2''. Epps' trademark is a voice similar to Martin Lawrence in the original ''Open Season''. He replaced Lawrence as the voice of Boog in ''Open Season 2'', but was replaced by Matthew J. Munn in ''Open Season 3''.
