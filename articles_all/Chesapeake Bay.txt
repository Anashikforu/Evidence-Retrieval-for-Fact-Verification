
The '''Chesapeake Bay''' ( in the District of Columbia and parts of six states: New York, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, and West Virginia. More than 150 rivers and streams drain into it.

The Chesapeake Bay is about 200 miles (300&nbsp;km) long, from the Susquehanna River in the north to the Atlantic Ocean. At its narrowest point between Kent County, Maryland|Kent County's Plum Point (near Newtown) and the Harford County, Maryland|Harford County shore near Romney Creek, the bay isabout 2.8 miles (4.5&nbsp;km) wide. At the widest location, it is 30 miles (50&nbsp;km) wide. The bay is  deep on average.

The bay has two bridges over it. The Chesapeake Bay Bridge crosses the bay in Maryland from Sandy Point (near Annapolis) to Kent Island, Maryland|Kent Island. The Chesapeake Bay Bridge-Tunnel in Virginia connects Virginia Beach, Virginia|Virginia Beach to Cape Charles, Virginia|Cape Charles.
