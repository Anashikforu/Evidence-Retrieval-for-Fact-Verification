'''Infertile''' means not able to have children or other offspring. Humans can be infertile, but animals and plants can too. When a human couple is infertile, they sometimes have fertility treatment to have children. This can be insemination or in vitro fertilization|IVF. They also sometimes adoption|adopt a child.
