'''Faisalabad Division''' () was an administrative Divisions of Pakistan|division of the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province of Pakistan, until the reforms of 2000 abolished the third tier of government. At abolition it contained the following districts: Faisalabad District|Faisalabad, Toba Tek Singh District|Toba Tek Singh and Jhang District|Jhang.
