
'''Attalla''' is a city in Etowah County, Alabama|Etowah County, Alabama, United States. It is part of the Gadsden Metropolitan Statistical Area. According to the 2005 U.S. Census estimates, the city had a population of 6,474.

 Notable Past Residents
