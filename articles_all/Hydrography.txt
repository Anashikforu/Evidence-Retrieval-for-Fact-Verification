Massive hydrographical studies are usually undertaken by national or international organizations that sponsor Information|data collection through surveying|surveys and publication of charts and descriptive materials for navigational purposes.
Hydrography's origin lies in the making of chart like drawings and notations made by individual Marine (military)|mariners. It was usually a private property, even closely held secrets, used for commercial or military advantage.
