
'''John Burke Krasinski''' (born October 20, 1979) is an Americans|American actor, movie director and writer. He is best known for playing Jim Halpert on NBC's ''The Office (U.S. TV series)|The Office'' and for directing and starring as Lee Abbott in the 2018 horror movie ''A Quiet Place (movie)|A Quiet Place''.

Krasinski marriage|married Emily Blunt in Como, Italy on July 10, 2010. The couple's daughter was born in 2014.
