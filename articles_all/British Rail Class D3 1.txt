
'''British Rail Class D3/1''' was a locomotive commissioned by British Rail in England. It was a Diesel engine|diesel powered locomotive in the pre-TOPS period built by the North British Locomotive Company.  The NBL/MAN engines were built by the North British Locomotive Company in Scotland under licence from the German company MAN AG|MAN.
