
The '''pineapplefish''', ''Cleidopus gloriamaris'', is a species of fish. It is also known as the '''knightfish''' or the '''coat-of-mail fish''', due to the scales on its body. Some people call it the port-and-starboard light fish, as it has a pair of luminosity|bright spots that are like a ship's lights.

 Distribution and habitat 
The pineapplefish is found near Queensland, New South Wales, and Western Australia. It lives at a depth of 6-200 meters (20-660 ft). -->
