
'''Paul Peter Murphy''', Sacred Military Constantinian Order of Saint George|KCMCO, Order of Saint Gregory the Great|KSG  (born November 25, 1948) is a Wales|Welsh politician and held several Cabinet of the United Kingdom|cabinet posts, including Secretary of State for Wales. He is the Member of Parliament for the Torfaen (UK Parliament constituency)|Torfaen constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1987|1987 general election. 

He is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
