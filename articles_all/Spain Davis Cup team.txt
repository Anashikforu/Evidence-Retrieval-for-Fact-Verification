The '''Spain Davis Cup team''' represents Spain in the Davis Cup tennis competition.
Spain has won the Davis Cup four times (2000 Davis Cup|2000, 2004 Davis Cup|2004, 2008 Davis Cup|2008 and 2009 Davis Cup|2009), and finished at second position three times (1965 Davis Cup|1965, 1967 Davis Cup|1967, 2003 Davis Cup|2003).
