
'''Shad Gaspard''' (January 13, 1981 &ndash; May 17, 2020) was an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler and actor. He was best known for wrestling for WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He teamed with Jayson Paul|JTG and they formed the tag team, Cryme Tyme. Gaspard later began an acting career which he stated in an interview for Right After Wrestling. He starred in ''Get Hard''.

Gaspard was born in New York City.

Gaspard married his longtime girlfriend Siliana Gaspard in 2009, together the couple had a son, in April 2010.

On March 5, 2011, Gaspard was arrested by police in Columbus, Ohio for having allegedly refused the commands of a police officer and then resisting arrest for jaywalking.
On May 17, 2020, Gaspard was caught in a strong rip current at Marina del Rey beach in Venice Beach, California. He was 39 years old.
