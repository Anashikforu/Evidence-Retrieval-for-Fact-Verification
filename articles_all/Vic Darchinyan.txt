
'''Vic Darchinyan''' (, born January 7, 1976, in Vanadzor, Armenia) is the former International Boxing Federation|IBF flyweight champion. He is a left-handed boxer who currently trains under Billy Hussein, brother of boxers Nedal and Hussein Hussein. He was previously trained by former three-division world champion Jeff Fenech in Sydney, Australia.
