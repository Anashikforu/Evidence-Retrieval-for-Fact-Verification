Biography
Yoshikawa was born in Mie Prefecture on December 13, 1961. After graduating from Yokkaichi Chuo Technical High School, he joined Kashiwa Reysol|Hitachi in 1980. In 1992, he moved to Kyoto Sanga FC|Kyoto Shiko Club (later ''Kyoto Purple Sanga''). In 1995, he moved to Ococias Kyoto AC|Kyoiku Kenkyusya (later ''FC Kyoken''). He retired in 2000.

On February 12, 1983, Yoshikawa debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Syria national football team|Syria.
