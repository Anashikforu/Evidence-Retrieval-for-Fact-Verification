

'''Poznań Fortress''' (in Polish language|Polish: ''Twierdza Poznań'', in German language|German: ''Festung Posen'') was a set of fortifications in the city of Poznań in western Poland, built under Kingdom of Prussia|Prussian rule in the 19th and early 20th centuries. It was one of largest systems of its kind in Europe.
