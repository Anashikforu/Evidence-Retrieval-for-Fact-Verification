The word ''histogram'' is derived from ''histos'' and ''gramma'' in Greek language|Greek. ''Histos'' means ''web'' or ''mast''. ''Gramma'' means ''drawing'', ''record'' or ''writing''. A histogram of something is therefore, etymology|etymologically speaking, a drawing of the web of this something.
