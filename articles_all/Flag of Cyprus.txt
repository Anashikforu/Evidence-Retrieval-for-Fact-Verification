
The '''flag of Cyprus''' is the flag of the European country Cyprus. It features a map of the whole island, with two Olive tree|olive branches below the map (a symbol of peace) on a white background. The map on the flag is a copper-yellow color to show Cyprus has large amounts of copper. The '''Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus''' is a partially recognised state and  uses a different flag similar to the flag of Turkey.
