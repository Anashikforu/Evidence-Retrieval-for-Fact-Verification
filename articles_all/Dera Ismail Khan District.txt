

'''Dera Ismail Khan District''' is a district in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. The city of Dera Ismail Khan is the capital. It is the most southern district of KPK and has an area of  and a population of 852,995 as of the 1998 Census. The main language of the district is a dialect of Saraiki.
