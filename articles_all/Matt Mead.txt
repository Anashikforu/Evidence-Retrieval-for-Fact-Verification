

'''Matthew Hansen "Matt" Mead''' (born March 11, 1962) is an Americans|American politician. He was the 32nd Governor of Wyoming from 2011 to 2019. He easily won against former chairman of the Wyoming Democratic Party, Leslie Petersen in the election and became the governor.

Mead was born in Teton County, Wyoming|Teton County, Wyoming. Mead is married to Carol L. Mintzer and they have 2 children together.
