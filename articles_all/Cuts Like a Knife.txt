'''''Cuts Like a Knife''''' is the third album by the Canadian Rock music|rock artist Bryan Adams. Released on January, 1983 through A&M Records, the album became a big hit in Canada when it was released. Outside Canada and the United States the album did not sell as well. After the release of Reckless (album)|Reckless the album would chart on the United Kingdom|British album chart and would later be a Silver Record by the British Phonographic Industry|BPI. It was made at Little Mountain Sound, in Vancouver, Canada.

Three Single (music)|singles were released from the album: "Straight from the Heart (song)|Straight from the Heart", "Cuts Like a Knife (song)|Cuts Like a Knife" and "This Time (song)|This Time", the three made Adams very popular.
