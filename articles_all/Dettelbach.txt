

'''Dettelbach''' is a Town#Germany|town in Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen in the Regierungsbezirk Unterfranken in Bavaria, Germany. It is on the right edge of the Main (river)|Main, 20&nbsp;km east of Würzburg, and 8&nbsp;km north of Kitzingen. It includes surrounding villages as administrative subdivisions, namely Bibergau, Brück, Dettelbach-Bahnhof, Effeldorf, Euerfeld, Mainsondheim, Neuses am Berg, Neusetz, Schernau und Schnepfenbach.
