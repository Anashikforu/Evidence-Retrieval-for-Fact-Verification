
'''Brian Boucher''' (born January 2, 1977 in Woonsocket, Rhode Island|Woonsocket, Rhode Island) is a professional Americans|American ice hockey goaltender currently playing for the Philadelphia Flyers of the National Hockey League|NHL. Boucher was drafted into the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers with the twenty-second overall pick in the 1995 NHL Entry Draft.

Boucher has also played for many other teams in the NHL: Phoenix Coyotes for 3 seasons, Calgary Flames for 1 season, Chicago Blackhawks for 1 season, Columbus Blue Jackets for 1 season and the San Jose Sharks for 2 seasons.
