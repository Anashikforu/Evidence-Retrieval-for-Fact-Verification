
'''''Haseena Atom Bomb''''' is a 1990 cult movie|cult Pakistani movie. It was movie director|directed by Saeed Ali Khan. The movie was first recorded in Pashto language|Pashto on a low budget. It was later dubbed in Urdu language|Urdu, the national language of Pakistan. The Urdu version became a national blockbuster.
