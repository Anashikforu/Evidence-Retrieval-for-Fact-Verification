'''Marc Dutroux''' (born 6 November 1956 in Ixelles|Ixelles / Elsene) is a Belgium|Belgian criminal. He has been conviction|convicted because he kidnapping|kidnapped and tortured six girls, between eight and 19 years old. He murdered four of them, two eight year old girls starvation|starved and died while he was in prison. He was arrested in 1989 for abducting and rape|raping five young girls. For this he was imprisoned; he was released in 1992. He was imprisoned again, from December 1995 until March 1996. He was arrested one more time in August 1996 and has remained imprisoned since then. The court convicted him in June 2004 and sentenced him to serve life imprisonment|life in prison for the three murders (girls of 17 and 18 years old, and of his accomplice who was a 43 years old man). His wife was convicted and sentenced to serve 30 years, for starving the 8 year olds.

Dutroux has committed many crimes, including theft, robbery and drug dealing. The media showed him mostly as a pedophile.
