
'''Live – Friday the 13th''' is a live album released on September|Sept. 20th, 2005. The album was released as a live Compact disc|CD/DVD album featuring all 12 original songs from pop (music)|Pop band Maroon 5's hit debut called ''Songs About Jane''. This album has been released with the ''Copy Control'' protection system in some regions. The CD has the length of 77:58. 

Performance
Maroon 5 performed in Santa Barbara, California on Friday, May|May 13th of 2005. 

Songs
The songs from the hit debut album by Maroon 5 called ''Songs About Jane'' were all featured performed live on this album. Two songs are performed called ''Hello'' by rock band|band ''Oasis (band)|Oasis'' (cover), and ''Ain't No Sunshine'' (cover).
