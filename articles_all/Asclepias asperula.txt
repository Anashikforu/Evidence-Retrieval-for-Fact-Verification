


'''''Asclepias asperula''''' is a species of milkweed plant. It is commonly known as '''antelope horns''', '''green-flowered milkweed''', '''spider antelope horns''' and '''spider antelopehorns'''.  It wikt:belong|belongs to the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.  It blooms from April to June.  This species likes to grow in moist sandy or Rock (geology)|rocky soil.  Antelope horns grows from 0.3 to 0.6 meters (1-2 feet).
