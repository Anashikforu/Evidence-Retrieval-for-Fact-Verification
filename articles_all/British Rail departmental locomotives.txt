Before the introduction of the TOPS|Total Operations Processing System, British Rail Class 97|Class 97 was issued to self-propelled locomotives in departmental (non-revenue earning) use, British Rail had such locomotives numbered in a variety of series, together with locomotives that were no longer self-propelled.
