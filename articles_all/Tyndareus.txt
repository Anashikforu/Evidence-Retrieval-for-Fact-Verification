'''Tyndareos''' (or Latin language|Latin '''Tyndareus''') was a character in Greek mythology. He was the king of Sparta, and husband of Leda.

He was the father of Klytaimnestra and Castor | Kastor. His wife Leda also had Helen and Pollux (mythology)|Polydeukes by the god Zeus.

Menelaos, husband of Helen, became the next king of Sparta after Tyndareos.
