


'''Matthew David McConaughey''' (born November 4, 1969 in Uvalde, Texas) is an American actor. McConaughey made his big screen debut in the 1993 movie My Boyfriend's Back (movie)|My Boyfriend's Back. He received great claim for his role as Mud in the 2012 movie ''Mud (movie)|Mud''.
