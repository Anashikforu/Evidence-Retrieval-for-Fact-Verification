'''Sela Ann Ward''' (born July 11, 1956) is an United States|American Emmy Award|Emmy and Golden Globe Award winning movie and television actress. She was in the NBC television series ''Sisters''. She acted in the CBS television series ''CSI: NY''. 

Ward was born in Meridian, Mississippi
