'''Szczecin-Goleniów "Solidarność" Airport''' (Polish language|Polish: ''Port Lotniczy Szczecin-Goleniów im. NSZZ "Solidarność"'') is an airport located near Goleniów, Szczecin, Police, Poland|Police and Świnoujście in Poland. The airport has good train connections to reach Szczecin and Kołobrzeg.

Airlines and destinations

operated by EuroLOT (Warsaw)

Charter flights operates to Egypt (Hurghada and Sharm el-Sheikh), Turkey and Tunesia.
