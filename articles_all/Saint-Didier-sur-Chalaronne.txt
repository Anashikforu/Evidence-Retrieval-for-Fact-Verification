

'''Saint-Didier-sur-Chalaronne''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department  in east France.

Geography
The Chalaronne (a long river) flows west through the southern part of the commune. It then flows into the Saône, which is part of the commune's south-western border.
