
'''Robert Urich''' (December 19, 1946 – April 16, 2002) was an Emmy Award|Emmy-winning actor. He was born in Toronto, Ohio. 

Urich is best known for playing private investigators on the television program|television series ''Spenser: For Hire'' (1985 in television|1985–1988 in television|1988) and ''Vegas (1978 TV series)|Vega$'' (1978 in television|1978–1981 in television|1981).  He also acted in many other television series over the years including: ''S.W.A.T. (TV series)|S.W.A.T.'' (1975), ''Soap (TV series)|Soap'' (1977) and The Lazarus Man (TV series)|The Lazarus Man (1996). He was also known for staring in the movie A long Way Home which was released in 2003.

From 1975 until his death, Urich was married to actress Heather Menzies.

Urich died of synovial sarcoma in Thousand Oaks, California at the age of 55.
