
'''''Empty Nest''''' was an American situation comedy|sitcom that aired Saturdays on NBC beginning in the late 1980s and ending in the mid 1990s. Starring in the sitcom were Park Overall and Richard Mulligan. The show was distributed under Touchstone Pictures|Touchstone Pictures and Television.
