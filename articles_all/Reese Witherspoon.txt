'''Reese Witherspoon''' (also known as '''Laura Jeanne Reese Witherspoon''', born March 22, 1976) is an United States|American actress and film producer.

She lived in Wiesbaden, Germany for four years when her father worked for the U.S. military.

 Career 
Witherspoon was selected as a fashion model for a florist's television advertisements at age seven.  This made her want to take acting lessons. In 2018, she appeared alongside Oprah Winfrey and Mindy Kaling in support of the Time's Up (movement)|Time's Up movement against sexual assault in the workplace and in ''A Wrinkle in Time (2018 movie)|A Wrinkle in Time''.
