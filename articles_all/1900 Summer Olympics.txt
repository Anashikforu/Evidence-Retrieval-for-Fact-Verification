


The '''1900 Summer Olympics''', officially known as the '''Games of the II Olympiad''', were held in Paris, France.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics), 
The games were part of the 1900 Exposition Universelle (Paris World's Fair). The sports events were not well planned; and the games were not thought to be a success.Zarnowski, C. Frank. 
Participating nations
A total of 24 nations sent athletes to compete at the Paris games.Taking part in the games for the first time were Argentina, Belgium, Bohemia, Canada, flag|Cuba, India, Mexico, Netherlands, Norway, Romania, 
Russia, Spain.
