



'''Male''' is one of two genders. Most species have two sexes  male and female. Human males are man|men or boys; human females are woman|women or girls. 

The two sexes have different sex organ|sexual organs, and different secondary sex characteristics. They also often have different biological functions. The female gives birth to children with the semen provided by the male. In many human society|societies, females often were involved in gathering, while men used to hunt.
