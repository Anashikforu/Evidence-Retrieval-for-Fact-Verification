

'''Empire of the Sun''' is an Australian electronic music group which started in 2007. The pair achieved chart success with their first single, "Walking on a Dream", which got to number ten on the Australian charts, and reached 64 on the UK Singles Chart.

Their first album also called ''Walking on a Dream'', released in October 2008, won a platinum award in Australia.

Empire of the Sun is very different, Luke Steele said that "...we are not a band. We're anything we want it to be..." The two musicians play a lot of instruments and dress in elaborate stage costumes.
