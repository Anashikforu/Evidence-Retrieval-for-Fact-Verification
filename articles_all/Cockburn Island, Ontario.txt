

'''Cockburn Island''' is an island in the Canada|Canadian province of Ontario, found in the Manitoulin District, Ontario|Manitoulin District.  It has the least number of people living in it while still being a municipality of Canada.  It had a population of 2 people in 2006.  There is an Indian reservation, or place where Indians live, on Cockburn Island.
