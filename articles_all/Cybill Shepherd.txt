

'''Cybill Lynne Shepherd''' (born February 18, 1950) is an Americans|American actress. She  has acted in many situation comedy|sitcoms, television programs and movies. She starred in ''Moonlighting (TV series)|Moonlighting'' with Bruce Willis.

Shepherd had melanoma in 2002.
