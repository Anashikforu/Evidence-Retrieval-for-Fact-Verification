
'''Bay Minette''' is a city in Baldwin County, Alabama|Baldwin County, Alabama, United States. According to the United States Census, 2000|2000 census, the population of the city was 7,820. According to the 2007 U.S. Census estimates, the city had a population of 7726. The city is the county seat of Baldwin County, Alabama|Baldwin County.
