'''"Breakaway"''' is a 2004 song written by Avril Lavigne, It was recorded by pop rock singer Kelly Clarkson for the soundtrack to the film ''The Princess Diaries 2|The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement''''The Princess Diaries 2'' DVD extras. by The Walt Disney Company|Disney Records. It was also included on Clarkson's second album, ''Breakaway (Kelly Clarkson album)|Breakaway'' and was released as the album's first single (music)|single.
