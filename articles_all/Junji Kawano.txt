Biography
Kawano was born in Nakatsu, Oita|Nakatsu on July 11, 1945. After graduating from University of Tsukuba|Tokyo University of Education, he joined Sanfrecce Hiroshima|Toyo Industries in 1968. The club won the Japan Soccer League champions in 1968 and 1970. The club also won 1969 Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1976. He played 90 games and scored 8 goals in the league.

On March 31, 1968, Kawano debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Australia national soccer team|Australia. In October 1969, he was also selected Japan for 1970 FIFA World Cup|1970 World Cup qualification. He played 2 games for Japan until 1969.
