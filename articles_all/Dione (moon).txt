
'''Dione''' is the fourth largest Satellite (natural)|moon of Saturn (planet)|Saturn. It has a diameter of 1,123 km (698 miles). It was discovered in 1684 by Giovanni Domenico Cassini. It is named after the titan (mythology)|titan Dione (mythology)|Dione in Greek mythology. The moon has many craters and ice cliffs. It is tidal locking|tidally locked with Saturn, meaning the same face of Dione always faces Saturn.
