

'''Waldeck-Frankenberg''' is a ''Kreis'' (Rural Districts of Germany|district) in the north of Hesse, Germany.

 History 
The district was created in 1972 by merging the two districts Frankenberg and Waldeck. Most of the area of the district was previously part of the old Waldeck (principality)|principality of Waldeck.
