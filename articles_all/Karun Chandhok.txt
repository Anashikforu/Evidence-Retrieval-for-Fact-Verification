'''Karun Chandhok''' (born 19 January 1984 in Chennai) is an Indian Auto racing|racing driver. In  he began driving for the Hispania Racing|HRT F1 Team in Formula One. After the British Grand Prix|British race, HRT announced that Sakon Yamamoto would replace Chandhok at the German Grand Prix. 

Before Formula One, Chandhok won the Formula Asia championship in 2001, and he was the first Formula Asia V6 by Renault champion in 2006. He then drove in the GP2 Series for three years, winning two races.
