
'''Sienna Rose Diana Miller''' (born December 28, 1981) is a British people|British-Americans|American actress, model (person)|model and fashion designer. She has starred in ''Layer Cake'', ''Alfie (2004 film)|Alfie'', ''Factory Girl'', "G.I. Joe: Rise of the Cobra" and ''The Edge of Love''. She was nominated for the Orange Rising Star Award in 2008.

Early life
Miller was born on December 28, 1981 in New York City, United States to a South African mother and American father. She has lived in England for most of her life and has dual citizenship - American and British people|British.

Modeling
Before her professional acting career, Miller worked as a photographic model. She signed with Tandy Anderson of Select Model Management London.  She modeled for Coca-Cola, Italian ''Vogue (magazine)|Vogue'', and posed topless in the 2003 Pirelli Calendar.

Personal life
Miller's had a relationship with Jude Law, her co-star from ''Alfie (2004 movie)|Alfie''. On Christmas Day 2004, they became engagement|engaged.

In 2008, Miller had a highly publicized affair with married actor Balthazar Getty.

She was in a relationship actor Tom Sturridge between 2011 and 2015. They have a daughter, Marlowe Ottoline, born in July 2012.
