

'''Brent Seabrook''' (born April 20, 1985) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman. He currently plays for the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League (NHL) and is an Captain (ice hockey)|alternate captain. He was the Chicago Blackhawks first round draft pick in 2003. He played in the Western Hockey League|WHL for the Lethbridge Hurricanes where he played four seasons. On June 9, 2010, Seabrook won the Stanley Cup with the Chicago Blackhawks and also won a gold medal at the 2010 Winter Olympics. On June 24, 2013, He won his second Stanley Cup with the Blackhawks after they defeated the Boston Bruins 4 games to 2 in the 2013 Stanley Cup Finals. 

On September 17, 2015, Seabrook was named as an alternate captain for the Blackhawks.

His younger brother Keith Seabrook|Keith plays for the Rockford IceHogs of the American Hockey League|AHL.
