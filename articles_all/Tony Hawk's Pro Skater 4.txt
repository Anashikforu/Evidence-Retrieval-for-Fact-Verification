'''''Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 4''''' is a 2002 skateboarding game that was developed by Neversoft, Vicarious Visions, Babaroga (game company)|Babaroga and Aspyr Media and was published by Activision. It is the fourth game in the Tony Hawk (series)|Tony Hawk's series It was released on October 23, 2002 for the GameCube, Playstation, Playstation 2 and the Xbox and October 28, 2002 for the Game Boy Advance and August 27, 2003 for the Microsoft Windows|PC in North America and November 15, 2002 for the GameCube, Playstation, Playstation 2, Xbox and November 22, 2002 for the Game Boy Advance and August 27, 2003 in the PAL region.

The fifth sequels, called ''Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 5'' released in 2015.
