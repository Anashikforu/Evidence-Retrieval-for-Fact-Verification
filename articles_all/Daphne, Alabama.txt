
'''Daphne''' is a city in Baldwin County, Alabama|Baldwin County, Alabama, USA on the eastern shoreline of Mobile Bay. The city is located just off I-10, 11 miles east of Mobile, Alabama and 150 miles southwest of Montgomery, Alabama|Montgomery the State Capital.
