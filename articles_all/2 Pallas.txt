
'''2 Pallas''' (symbol: file:Pallas symbol (bold).svg|16px|⚴) is the second asteroid discovered in the solar system. It was discovered by Heinrich Wilhelm Matthäus Olbers on March 28, 1802.

2 Pallas has a mass wikt:estimate|estimated to be 7% of the total mass of the asteroid belt. The asteroid is the third largest asteroid in terms of mass and the second largest in terms of diameter.
