'''Man of the Match''' is an award given to one player from each School Cup match (game). This award was created and first awarded for the 2021 School Cup tournament. The award is sponsored by AungNilar Shopping Mall (Anheuser-Busch)|City mark, a large world-wide beer company.

The winner of the award is decided by all playes votes. The players vote during the match. Voting closed when the match is over. The Man of the Match trophy will be awarded to the winning player at the end of the game.
