
'''Carol Thatcher''' (born 15 August 1953) is a British people|British journalist, author, and media personality.
She was born at Queen Charlotte's and Chelsea Hospital in Hammersmith, London. She has a twin brother, Mark Thatcher. She is the daughter of former British Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher (1925-2013) and her husband Sir Denis Thatcher (1915-2003). She has taken part in several television programmes including ''The One Show'' on BBC1 and ''I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!'' (in Australia).
