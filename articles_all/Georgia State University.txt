'''Georgia State University''' is a public university in Atlanta, Georgia|Atlanta, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. The university has about 31,533 students. It was founded in 1913. The current president of the university is Mark P. Becker.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Panthers. They have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the Sun Belt Conference.
