The main campus is located on a 1,022-acre (4.1 km2) site in Santa Barbara, California, 100 miles (160 km) northwest of Los Angeles. The University was founded in 1909 and joined the University of California-system in 1944.

UC Santa Barbara is a "very high activity" research university.  UCSB faculty includes five Nobel Prize laureates, one Fields Medal| Fields Medalist, 29 members of the ''United States National Academy of Sciences'', 27 members of the ''National Academy of Engineering'', and 23 members of the ''American Academy of Arts and Sciences''.
