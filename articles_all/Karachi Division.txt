

'''Karachi Division''' () is an administrative division of the Sindh Province of Pakistan.

The Karachi Division was wikt:abolish|abolished in 2000 and five districts of Karachi were merged in City District Karachi.

On 11 July 2011, Sindh Government restored again 5 districts of Karachi division.
In November 2013, a new district (6th), Korangi District|Korangi was formed by splitting District Karachi East.
