
'''Sabana Larga''' (in English language|English, "Long Savanna") is a Dominican Republic|Dominican Provinces of the Dominican Republic|municipal district of Comendador, Elías Piña|Comendador in the Elías Piña Province|Elías Piña Province near the border with Haiti.

Population
The municipality had, in 2010, a total population of 2,056: 1,127 men and 929 women. The urban population was  of the total population.

History
Sabana Larga was made a municipal district of Comendador by the law 18 of 20 January 2004; a municipal district is a part of a municipality that has its own government.

Geography
Sabana Larga is to the northeast of the city of Comendador, Elías Piña|Comendador, on the northern side of the ''Macasías'' river, the main left tributary of the Artibonite River|Artibonite river.

Economy
The main economic activity of Sabana Larga is farming.
