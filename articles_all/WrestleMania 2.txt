
'''WrestleMania 2''' was the second annual WrestleMania professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by the World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation (although the first WrestleMania was only on pay-per-view in select areas). It took place on April 7, 1986 at three venues: the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Uniondale, New York, the Allstate Arena|Rosemont Horizon in Rosemont, Illinois, and the Los Angeles Memorial Sports Arena in Los Angeles|Los Angeles, California.
