'''Jamaican English''' may mean: Jamaican Patois

'''Jamaican English''' is a variety of English language|English spoken in Jamaica. It uses parts of both American English|American and British English.

A simple example of this would be "Ku pon dis mudda, dis mon hab a crazy 'ead, truss mi."
Which would mean "Look at this mom, this man is crazy, trust me."
