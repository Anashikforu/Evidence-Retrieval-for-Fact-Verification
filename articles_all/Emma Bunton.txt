
'''Emma Bunton''', also known as '''Baby Spice,''' is a British people|British singer-songwriter and television promoter. She was born on 21 January 1976. She was a member of girl group the Spice Girls. She gained her nickname from being the youngest member of the group and also because she looked young. She was a contestant in the 2006 series of ''Strictly Come Dancing''. She is also the host of the The Heart Network|Heart FM Saturday evening show from 4pm to 7pm.

On 12 August 2012, the Spice Girls performed a medley of "Wannabe" and "Spice Up Your Life" at the 2012 Summer Olympics closing ceremony. They got back together just for the event.

Personal life
Bunton's Domestic partnership|long-term partner since 1998 is singer Jade Jones (singer)|Jade Jones. The couple have two sons: Beau Lee Jones and Tate Lee Jones.
