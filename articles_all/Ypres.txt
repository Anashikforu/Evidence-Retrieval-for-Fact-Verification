
'''Ypres''' () is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of West Flanders. The municipality is made up of the city of Ypres and the villages of Boezinge, Brielen, Dikkebus, Elverdinge, Hollebeke, Sint-Jan, Vlamertinge, Voormezele, Zillebeke, and Zuidschote. In 2007, 34919 people lived in the municipality. Poison gas was first used here, in the Second Battle of Ypres in 1917.
