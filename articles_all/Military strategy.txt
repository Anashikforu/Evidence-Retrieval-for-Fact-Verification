
A '''military strategy''' is a set of ideas or policy used by Military|armed forces to get the results they want.Gartner, Scott Sigmund, ''Strategic Assessment in War'', Yale University Press, 1999. p.&nbsp;163 Strategy is used to win combat, battles and wars. It involves planning, logistics (the movement and use of forces and equipment), and the wikt:deception|deception of the enemy.

Carl von Clausewitz is considered the creator of modern military strategy.
