

The '''Cavalier King Charles Spaniel''' is a small breed of dog of the spaniel type.

History
These dogs were developed in England about 100 years ago and are closely related to their even smaller relation the King Charles Spaniels (known in the United States of America as ''Toy Spaniels'').  One of the main people involved in creating this breed was the British General officer|general and politician John Churchill, 1st Duke of Marlborough.

Colour
There are four different colours that this dog breed come in which are:
 Black and a brown colour called ''Tan'' in dogs.
 Ruby, which is a dark red.
 Blenheim, which is brown and white.
 Tricolour, which is black, white with some tan markings.

Temperment
Cavaliers are usually happy dogs that like to stay very close to their human minders, they also like lots of exercise as they still keep some of their gundog family instincts.

Health issues and lifespan
Cavaliers typically live about ten years. Heart problems are common in Cavaliers.
