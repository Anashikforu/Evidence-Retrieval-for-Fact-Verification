The '''New Orleans Privateers''' college sports teams for the University of New Orleans (UNO). They play in the NCAA Division I for college|intercollegiate Track and field athletics|athletics. The schools teams are a part of the Sun Belt Conference. The University of New Orleans does not currently have a American football|football team.

Sports
The university has teams in the following sports:
 Baseball (Men's)
 Basketball (Men's and women's teams)
 Golf (Men's) 
 Swimming and diving
 Tennis (Men's and women's teams) 
 Volleyball (Women's)

The school did have a football team from 1965 until 1970. During that time, the team had a record of 12 wins, 10 losses and 1 tie. They also won 3 district wikt:championship|championships.
