
'''''Fighting Temptation''''' is a single (music)|single released by rhythm and blues|R&B singer Beyoncé Knowles|Beyoncé and female rappers Missy Elliott, MC Lyte, and Free (rapper)|Free in 2003 to promote the film ''The Fighting Temptations'', in which Beyoncé has a part. The song is part of the official soundtrack.

As it was released just one month after the second single from Beyoncé's ''Dangerously in Love'', "Baby Boy", the song did not become a big hit and failed to enter the Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100 and other important U.S. charts. However, the single received some attention from European countries, eventually peaking at number thirteen in the Netherlands. The film was also an instant success, mainly due to Beyoncé's success in Europe at that time.
