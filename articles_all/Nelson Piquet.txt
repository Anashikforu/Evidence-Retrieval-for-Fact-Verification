 
'''Nelson Piquet Souto Maior''' (born August 17, 1952), more commonly known as '''Nelson Piquet''', is a Brazilian racing driver who was Formula One world champion in 1981 Formula One season|1981, 1983 Formula One season|1983, and 1987 Formula One season|1987. He is one of the few men to win at least three List of Formula One World Drivers' Champions|world championships in the history of Formula One. The others are Jack Brabham, Jackie Stewart, Niki Lauda, Ayrton Senna (3 each), Alain Prost (4), Juan Manuel Fangio (5), Lewis Hamilton (7) and Michael Schumacher (7).
