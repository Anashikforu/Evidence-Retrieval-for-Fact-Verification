The '''Cross-City Line''' is a suburban railway line in the West Midlands (region)|West Midlands regions of England|region of England. It runs from Redditch, Worcestershire, its southern terminus, to Lichfield, Staffordshire, its northern terminus. It goes through Birmingham New Street Station|Birmingham New Street. It services on the line are currently operated by London Midland.
