
'''Brian Knighton''' (April 21, 1971 &ndash; February 4, 2016) also known as '''Axl Rotten''' was an Americans|American professional wrestler that wrestled for the original ECW and WWE. Rotten was trained by retired wrestler Jim Leon and former World Championship Wrestling jobber Joey Maggs. He was born in Baltimore, Maryland.

Rotten was found dead at a McDonald's in Linthicum, Maryland at the age of 44.
