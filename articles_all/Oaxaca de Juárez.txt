

'''Oaxaca de Juárez''' is the capital city of the Mexico|Mexican state of Oaxaca. It is called '''La'a''' in the Zapotec language. '''Ñuu Nunduva''' is its name in the Mixtec language. Since 1872, when Benito Juárez died there, the city is called ''de Juárez'', in his honour. Currently, 258,000 people live in the city.
