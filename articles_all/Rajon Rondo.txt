
'''Rajon Pierre Rondo''' (born February 22, 1986 in Louisville, Kentucky|Louisville, Kentucky) is an Americans|American basketball player who currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association|NBA as a point guard. Rondo won an NBA Championpship with the Boston Celtics in 2008.  Rondo was selected with the 21st overall pick in the first round of the 2006 NBA Draft by the Phoenix Suns. Rondo played college basketball for the University of Kentucky.
