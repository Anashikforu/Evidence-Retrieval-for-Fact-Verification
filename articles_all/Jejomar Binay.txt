
'''Jejomar "Jojo" Cabauatan Binay''' (born November 11, 1942), also known as Jojo Binay or VNay, is a Filipinos|Filipino politician. He previously served as the 15th Vice President of the Philippines from June 30, 2010 to June 30, 2016. Before he became vice president, he was Mayor of Makati City from 1986 to 1998 and again from 2001 to 2010.

He ran for President of the Philippines|President in 2016, but lost to Rodrigo Duterte.
