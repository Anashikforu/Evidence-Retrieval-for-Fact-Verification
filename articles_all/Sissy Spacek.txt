'''Mary Elizabeth "Sissy" Spacek''' (born December 25, 1949) is an Americans|American actress and singer. She played Carrie White in the 1976 movie ''Carrie (1976 movie)|Carrie''. She also played country music|country singer Loretta Lynn in the 1980 movie ''Coal Miner's Daughter''. Spacek is known mainly as a dramatic actress, but she has acted in comedy|comedies. She was born in Quitman, Texas.
