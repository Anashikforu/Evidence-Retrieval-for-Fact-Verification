


The number '''five''' is a number that comes after four and before the number six. In Roman numerals, it is '''V'''. 

 Mathematics 
Five is the third prime number, after two and three, and before seven. The number five is also an odd number.

Most wikt:people|people have five fingers on each hand and five toes on each foot.

 Creation 

It is not known for wikt:sure|certain who and how the shape of the number five was created, but most people think it was made by the Brahmin Indians.
