
The '''Neolithic''' period is the last part of the Stone Age. After the Neolithic came the Bronze Age. During the Neolithic, Neolithic revolution|people invented farming and Domestication|started caring for animals, such as cows, sheep and pigs.

The Neolithic period started at different times in different places, since not everyone started farming at the same time.

The word "Neolithic" means “new Stone Age.” It comes from two words in Ancient Greek language|Greek meaning "new" and "lithic" ("stone").
