

'''''Jak and Daxter: The Precursor Legacy''''' is a platform game made by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. It is the first video game in the ''Jak and Daxter'' series, and can only be bought for the Sony PlayStation 2. It was released in winter of 2001, and has two sequels, ''Jak II'' and ''Jak 3''. It also has two spin-offs, ''Jak X: Combat Racing'' and ''Daxter''. Many people think of ''Jak and Daxter: The Precursor Legacy'' as the spiritual successor to Naughty Dog's ''Crash Bandicoot (series)|Crash Bandicoot'' series.
