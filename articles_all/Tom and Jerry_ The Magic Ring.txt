
'''''Tom and Jerry: The Magic Ring''''' is a 2001/2002 comedy-fantasy animated direct-to-video movie that stars Tom and Jerry. It is the first direct-to-video and the second animated film of ''Tom and Jerry'' animated movie to only be released only on video and never in a movie theater. Also, it was the final cartoon and the last ''Tom and Jerry'' cartoon to be co-executive produced by both, William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, as Hanna died on March 22, 2001, a few days before the movie's release.
