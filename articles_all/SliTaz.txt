



'''SliTaz GNU/Linux''' is a software project started by Christophe Lincoln in 2006. It is one of the smallest desktop  Linux distributions available 
 It can be loaded entirely into Random access memory|RAM or be installed to a hard drive.

 History 

SliTaz 1.0 was released on March 22, 2008. SliTaz shares many common goals with Damn Small Linux, but is smaller and based on the more recent Linux 2.6 kernel.
