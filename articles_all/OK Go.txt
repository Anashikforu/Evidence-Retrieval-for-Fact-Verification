
'''OK Go''' is a Rock and roll|rock band from Chicago. The band members are Damian Kulash (Vocals|lead vocals and guitar), Tim Nordwind (bass guitar and backing vocals), Dan Konopka (drum kit|drums and percussion) and Andy Ross (Keyboard instrument|keyboards, guitar and backing vocals). Their best known song is "Here It Goes Again".
