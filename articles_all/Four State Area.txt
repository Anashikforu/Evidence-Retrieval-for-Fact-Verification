The '''''Four State Area''''' is a term used to describe the four corner region of Arkansas, Kansas, Missouri and Oklahoma with the Joplin, Missouri Metropolitan Area|Joplin, Missouri and Fayetteville-Springdale-Rogers Metropolitan Area|Fayetteville-Springdale-Rogers, Arkansas metropolitan areas included within.
