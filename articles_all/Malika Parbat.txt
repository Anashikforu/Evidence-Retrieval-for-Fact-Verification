'''Malika Parbat''' (also known as '''Queen of the mountains''') (el. 5,290 metres (17,356&nbsp;ft) is a mountain in Kaghan Valley, Pakistan about six kilometres south of Saiful Muluk Lake near Ansoo Lake.
