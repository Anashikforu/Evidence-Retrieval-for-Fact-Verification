
'''Timothy Franz Geithner''' (pronounced /ˈɡaɪtnər/; born August 18, 1961), was the 75th United States Secretary of the Treasury from 2009 to 2013. He served under President of the Untied States|President Barack Obama. Before becoming Secretary of the Treasury, he was  president of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York City|New York.

Geithner was born in Brooklyn, New York.
