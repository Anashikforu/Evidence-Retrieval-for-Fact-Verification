 Tallest buildings in Australia 
This is a '''list of the 25 tallest buildings in Australia'''. Observation towers, radio masts, chimneys and other structures are not included. Heights are measured to the ''structural height'', which means to the top of spires, but not including communications spires or Antenna|radio antennas.
