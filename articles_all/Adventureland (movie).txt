

'''''Adventureland''''' is a 2009 United States|American romance movie|romantic comedy-drama movie. It is set in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania in summer 1987. It was movie producer|produced by Sidney Kimmel, Anne Carey and Ted Hope. It was screenwriter|written and movie director|directed by Greg Mottola. Adventureland was released on April 3, 2009 in North America. The movie received positive reviews with a 88% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 76 out of 100 from Metacritic.
