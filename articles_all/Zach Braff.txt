
'''Zachary Braff''' (born April 6, 1975, South Orange, New Jersey|South Orange, New Jersey) is an United States|American actor, film director, producer and screenwriter. He became celebrity|famous in the early 2000s playing the part of "Dr. John Dorian" on the NBC series ''Scrubs (TV series)|Scrubs''. Since then, he has written, starred in and directed the :Category:2004 movies|2004 movie ''Garden State''. He also has directed multiple music videos. Braff is a Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat and attended the 2012 Democratic National Convention in support of Barack Obama|President Obama's re-election. He directed his first movie. It is called ''Wish I Was Here''.
