The '''Communist Party of Bangladesh''' (বাংলাদেশের কমিউনিস্ট পার্টি) is a Marxism-Leninism|Marxist-Leninist political party in Bangladesh, founded in 1968 as the '''Communist Party of East Pakistan'''.
