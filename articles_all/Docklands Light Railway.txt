


The '''Docklands Light Railway''' ('''DLR''') is a light metro or light rail system in London, England. It opened on 31 August 1987 to serve the redeveloped London Docklands|Docklands area of East London. It currently  covers several areas of London. To the north, it reaches Stratford station|Stratford. To the south, Lewisham. To the west, Tower Gateway DLR station|Tower Gateway and Bank and Monument stations|Bank in the City of London financial district. To the east, Beckton, London City Airport and Woolwich Arsenal station|Woolwich Arsenal. The DLR is fully computer operated. It is automated and the only job is the emergency stop button.
