'''Daisy Duke''' is a fictional character from the ''Dukes of Hazzard''. She is an attractive young woman from Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. Her apparel includes hot pants|low cut blue jeans, which later led to the term "Daisy Dukes" being used for short shorts. She was portrayed by actress Jessica Simpson in the movie.
