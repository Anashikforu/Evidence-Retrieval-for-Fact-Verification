

'''Thirty-three''' is a natural or counting number. It comes between thirty-two and thirty-four, and is an odd number. It is divisible by 1 (number)|1, Three|3, Eleven|11, and 33.

 In movies 
The 33 is a Spanish movie about workers struggle to survive after a disaster strikes in copper and gold mine.

 In religion 
In the Bible, Noah's name was mentioned 33 times when God made a promise or covenant with him. In superstitions, 33 is believed to be an Angel Numbers|angel number which means growth and development.
