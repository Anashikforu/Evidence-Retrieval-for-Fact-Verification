A '''grove''' is a small group of trees with little or no wikt:undergrowth|undergrowth. For example: a sequoia grove, or a small orchard planted for the cultivation of fruits or nut (fruit)|nuts. Other words for groups of trees include ''woodland'', ''copse'', ''woodlot'', ''thicket'' or ''spinney''.
