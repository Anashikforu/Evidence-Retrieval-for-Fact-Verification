


"'''Rolling in the Deep'''" is a song by English singer-songwriter Adele (singer)|Adele. It is from her second studio album, ''21 (Adele album)|21''. The song was written by Adele and Paul Epworth. Adele has described it as a "dark blues-y gospel music|gospel disco tune". It was released as the lead single on 29 November 2010 in the Netherlands. "Rolling in the Deep'" became Adele's first number-one hit single in the United States and Canada. It was also her first top-five hit in several countries.

Critics have given positive reviews for the song. It reached number one in Belgium, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, and Switzerland. It also reached top ten in Austria, Canada, Denmark, Ireland, New Zealand, and Norway. The song debuted at number two in the United Kingdom. There, it became Adele's third top-ten single.

"Rolling in the Deep" became Adele's second single to chart in the United States. After that, it became her first single to go to top of a ''Billboard (magazine)|Billboard'' chart. This was when it reached number one on the Hot Adult Top 40 Tracks chart in May 2011. The song is her best-selling single in the country. It has sold more copies than her previous best-selling single "Chasing Pavements".

Composition
The song has "martial arts", "wikt:pound|pounding piano keys" (played by Neil Crowley) and "chanting backing vocalist|backing singers".

Critical reception
The song was praised by critics for Adele's powerful voice, the lyrics and the song's instrumentation. ''The Sun (United Kingdom)|The Sun'' called the song an "epic, foot-stomper of a pop anthem." They said it had a wikt:thumping|thumping piano. According to ''The Sun'', Adele's vocals seemed to be that of a wikt:veteran|veteran who has been singing for 20 years .

Music video

The music video was premiered on Channel 4 on 3 December 2010.
