'''Claudia Schiffer''' (born 25 August 1970) is a Germany|German model (person)|supermodel and actress. She was born in Rheinberg, North Rhine-Westphalia, West Germany. Schiffer is one of the world's most successful models. She has appeared on over 500 magazine covers.
