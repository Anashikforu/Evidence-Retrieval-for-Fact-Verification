'''Layyah District''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Layyah is the capital. 

Layyah District shares borders with the districts of Bhakkar (north), Muzaffargarh (south), Jhang (east) and D.G. Khan (west).  The District is of semi rectangle|rectangular shape and has sandy land. It is made up of the three tehsils of:

#Layyah Tehsil
#Chaubara Tehsil
#Karor Lal Esan Tehsil

Layyah District has an extremely hot climate. The temperature in winter is low because it is near the Koh-Suleman mountains.
