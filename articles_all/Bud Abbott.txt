
'''William Alexander “Bud” Abbott''' (October 2, 1895 – April 24, 1974) was an Americans|American actor, Film producer|producer and comedian born in Asbury Park, New Jersey|Asbury Park, New Jersey. He is famous for his role as the "straight man" of the comedy team of Abbott and Costello, with Lou Costello.
