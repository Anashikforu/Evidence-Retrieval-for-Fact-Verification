'''John Burningham''' (27 April 1936 &ndash; 4 January 2019) was a British children's book writer and illustrator.  He won many awards including the Kate Greenaway Medal in 1963 for his book called Borka: The Adventures of a Goose With No Feathers. He was also known for illustrating the book ''Chitty-Chitty-Bang-Bang''.

Burningham was born April 27, 1936 in Farnham, Surrey, England.
