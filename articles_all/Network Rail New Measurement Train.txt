The Network Rail '''New Measurement Train''' (NMT) is a special train used to check how good rail tracks are. Network Rail engineers use this information to decide which tracks to work on. The NMT is converted from InterCity 125|High Speed Train British Rail Class 43 (HST)|Class 43 power cars and British Rail Mark 3|Mark 3 coaches. It can check the condition of all main lines in Great Britain in two weeks.
