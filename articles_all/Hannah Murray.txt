
'''Tegan Lauren-Hannah Murray''' (born 1 July 1989) is an England|English actress. She is best known for playing the role of Cassie (Skins character)|Cassie, a gentle, 'spacey' teen girl with an apparent eating disorder and unstated mental illness in E4's drama/comedy, ''Skins (TV Series)|Skins''.

Murray attended North Bristol Post 16 Centre studying German language|German.
