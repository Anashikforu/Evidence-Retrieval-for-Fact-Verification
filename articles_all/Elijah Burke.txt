
'''Elijah Burke''' (born May 24, 1978) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is best known for wrestling for World Wrestling Entertainment on its Extreme Championship Wrestling (WWE)|ECW brand under his real name and for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling under the ring name '''"The Pope" D'Angelo Dinero'''. He currently works for Ohio Valley Wrestling.
