

'''Augustine Azuka Okocha''' (; born 14 August 1973), commonly known as '''Jay-Jay Okocha''', is a Nigerian former professional Association football|footballer who played as an attacking midfielder. He played 73 times for the Nigeria national football team|Nigeria national team between 1993 and 2006, scoring 14 times, and was a member of three FIFA World Cup squads. He is known as one of the greatest football players from Africa.
