

'''Dustin James Brown''' (born November 4, 1984 in Ithaca, New York|Ithaca, New York) is an National Hockey League|NHL ice hockey right winger that plays for the Los Angeles Kings where he is the current team captain. He was picked by the Kings with the 13th overall pick in the 2003 NHL Entry Draft. He also played in the Ontario Hockey League|OHL with the Guelph Storm.

He was on the Team USA's men's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics where they won the silver medal.
