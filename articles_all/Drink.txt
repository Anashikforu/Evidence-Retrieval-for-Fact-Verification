

A '''drink''' or '''beverage''' is a liquid that an organism can take into their body, by using their mouth. Typical drinks for humans include water, tea, milk, coffee, juice, soft drinks and alcohol|alcoholic drinks. 

All drinks are mainly water. All life needs water to live. If a living organism don't get enough water, they will Dehydration|dehydrate, and likely Death|die. Plants take in water through their roots, which are underground. Most Terrestrial animal|land animals get water by drinking.
