
'''Nelson Mandela Bay Stadium''' is a sports stadium in Port Elizabeth, South Africa. It was named after the former President of South Africa|South African president Nelson Mandela.

 FIFA World Cup 
The stadium co-hosted the 2010 FIFA World Cup. It is one of the 10 stadiums where World Cup matches were played.
