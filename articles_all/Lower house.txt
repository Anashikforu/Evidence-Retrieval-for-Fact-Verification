A '''lower house''' is one of two Chambers of parliament|chambers of a bicameral legislature. The other chamber is called the upper house.

Although it is called the "wikt:lower|lower" house, in many legislatures in the world it actually has more Influence|power than the "wikt:upper|upper" house.

 Lower Houses in the World 
 France: the National Assembly
 Pakistan: the National Assembly of Pakistan|National Assembly
 United Kingdom: the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons
 United States of America: the United States House of Representatives
