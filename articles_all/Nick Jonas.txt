'''Nicholas Jerry Jonas''' (born September 16, 1992) is an American singer-songwriter and actor.

Jonas formed a band with his older brothers, Joe Jonas|Joe and Kevin Jonas|Kevin, known as the Jonas Brothers. The group released their debut studio album ''It's About Time (Jonas Brothers album)|It's About Time''. The band became a Walt Disney Pictures|Disney favorite and later starred in ''Camp Rock'' (2008) and its sequel ''Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam'' (2010) as well as two of their own series, ''Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream'' (2008–2010) and ''Jonas (TV series)|Jonas'' (2009–2010).

In 2017, Jonas appeared in the adventure comedy movie ''Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle''.

Jonas is married to Indian actress and singer Priyanka Chopra. In January 2022, the couple had their first child, via surrogacy.
