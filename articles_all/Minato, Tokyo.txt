The Municipalities in Japan|municipality calls itself "Minato City" in English.
History
On 15 March 1947, the ward of Minato was formed by combining the wards of Akasaka, Tokyo|Akasaka, Azabu, and Shiba, Minato, Tokyo|Shiba.

Geography
Minato is on Toyko Bay. It borders Shinagawa, Tokyo|Shinagawa on the south.  Meguro, Tokyo|Meguro is southwest of the ward. Shibuya, Tokyo|Shibuya is on the ward's western border. Shinjunku, Tokyo|Shinjuku, Chiyoda, Tokyo|Chiyoda and Chūō, Tokyo|Chūō are on the norther border.
