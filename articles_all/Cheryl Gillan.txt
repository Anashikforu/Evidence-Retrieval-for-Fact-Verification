
'''Dame Cheryl Elise Kendall Gillan''' Order of the British Empire|DBE Member of Parliament|MP (21 April 1952 &ndash; 4 April 2021) was a United Kingdom|British politician.  She was the Secretary of State for Wales from 2010 to 2012. She was a Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party Member of Parliament (MP) for the Chesham and Amersham (UK Parliament constituency)|Chesham and Amersham constituency in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom from 1992 to 2021. Gillan was born in Cardiff, Wales.

She was first wikt:elect|elected in the 1992 general election and became the Shadow Secretary of State for Wales in 2005. When the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative-Liberal Democrats|Liberal Democrat coalition was formed on 11 May 2010, she became the Secretary of State for Wales.

She was the Chair of the 1922 committee from May until September 2019. This is the powerful committee of backbench Conservative MPs in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons.

Gillan was diagnosed with breast cancer in June 2020.
