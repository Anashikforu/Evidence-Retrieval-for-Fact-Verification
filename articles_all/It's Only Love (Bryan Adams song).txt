

"'''It's Only Love'''" is a hit rock duet recorded by Grammy Award winning singers Bryan Adams and Tina Turner. It was included on Bryan Adams' album ''Reckless (album)|Reckless'' and Tina Turner's live album ''Tina Live in Europe'', as well as being added to both artists' greatest hits compilations: Adams' ''Anthology (Bryan Adams album)|Anthology'' and Turner's ''All the Best (Tina Turner album)|All the Best''. It reached number 15 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the United States|U.S. and number 29 in the United Kingdom|UK. The 12" single included the 1985 live version that would later appear on the 1988 album ''Tina Live in Europe''.
