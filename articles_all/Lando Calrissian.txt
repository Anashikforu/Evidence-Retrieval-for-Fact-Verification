'''Lando Calrissian''' is a major character in the ''Star Wars'' fictional universe|universe. He's played by Billy Dee Williams in ''Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back'' and ''Star Wars Episode VI: Return of the Jedi''. He appears a lot in the ''Expanded Universe (Star Wars)|Star Wars Expanded Universe'', including a series of novels called ''The Adventures of Lando Calrissian''.
