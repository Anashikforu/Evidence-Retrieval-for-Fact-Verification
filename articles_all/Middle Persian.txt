'''Middle Persian''' is the Iranian languages|Iranian dialect of southwestern Khuzestan, Lorestan, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari provinces in Iran. During the Sassanid Empire|Sassanid times (224-654 CE) it became a prestige dialect. It came to be spoken in other regions as well. Middle Persian is classified as Western Iranian languages|Western Iranian language. It comes from Old Persian language|Old Persian and is the ancestor of Persian language|Modern Persian.
