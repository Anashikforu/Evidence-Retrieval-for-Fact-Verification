The '''North Caucasus''' is the northern part of the Caucasus region between Europe and Asia. The term is also used as a synonym for the Economic regions of Russia|North Caucasus Economical Region of Russia.

Politically, the Northern Caucasus (territory north of the Greater Caucasus Range) includes the Russian Republics of the North Caucasus as well as several regions of Georgia (country)|Georgia and Azerbaijan.
