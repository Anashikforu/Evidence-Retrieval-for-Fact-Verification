'''Lavaux''' was a district of the cantons of Switzerland|canton of Vaud in Switzerland. The capital was Cully, Vaud|Cully. As of 2006 it is part of the Lavaux-Oron (district)|Lavaux-Oron District.

It contains the following municipalities:

 Chexbres
 Cully, Vaud|Cully
 Epesses
 Forel, Vaud|Forel
 Grandvaux
 Lutry
 Puidoux
 Riex
 Rivaz
 Saint-Saphorin
 Savigny, Vaud|Savigny
 Treytorrens (Lavaux)|Treytorrens
 Villette, Switzerland|Villette
