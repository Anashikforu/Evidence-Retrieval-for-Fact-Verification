'''New jack swing''' is a type of music that combines Dance music|dance, Hip hop music|hip hop, Contemporary R&B|R&B and other musical genres. It was extremely popular between 1984 and 1997. The artists of new jack swing include TLC (group)|TLC, Bell Biv Devoe and New Edition. Teddy Riley started new jack swing. Michael Jackson's 1991 album ''Dangerous'' is the most successful new jack swing album.
