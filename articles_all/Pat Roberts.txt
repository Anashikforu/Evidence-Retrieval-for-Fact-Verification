
'''Charles Patrick "Pat" Roberts''' (born April 20, 1936 in Topeka, Kansas) is a former United States Senator|senator from Kansas. Before Roberts was a senator, he was a U.S. House of Representatives wikt:member|member and the chairman for the United States House Committee on Agriculture.

On January 4, 2019, Roberts announced that he will not seek reelection in 2020.
