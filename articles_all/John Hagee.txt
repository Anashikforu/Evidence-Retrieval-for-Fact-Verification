
'''John Charles Hagee''' (born April 12, 1940) is the founder and senior pastor of Cornerstone Church in San Antonio, Texas, a church with more than 19,000 members. Hagee is CEO at his non-profit corporation, Global Evangelism Television (GETV).

He is also founder and chairman of Christians United for Israel (CUFI). This is a Christian organization supporting Zionism.

Hagee is a Pentecostal, and wrote a lot of Christian books.

He is critical of Islam, European Union, communism, feminism, abortion and a man-made climate change.
