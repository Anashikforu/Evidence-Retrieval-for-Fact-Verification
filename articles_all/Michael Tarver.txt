
'''Tyrone Evans''' (born March 8, 1977 in Akron, Ohio|Akron, Ohio) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling with WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) under the ring name '''Michael Tarver'''. During his time with WWE he was a part of the first season of WWE NXT|NXT. He was eliminated along with Daniel Bryan in the first round. Tarver debuted on WWE Raw|Raw as a part of the professional wrestling stable, The Nexus. On June 13, 2011, He was released from his WWE contract. He currently wrestles for Florida Underground Wrestling.

Pro Wrestling Illustrated ranked Tarver '''#343''' of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the PWI 500 in 2010.

Tarver's finishing move is the Powerslam#Scoop powerslam|Tarver's Lightning which is a Reverse thrown scoop powerslam.
