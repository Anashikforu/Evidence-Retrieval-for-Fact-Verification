

'''Leigh Francis''' (born 30 April 1973) is an English people|English Comedian|comedy performer best known for his portrayal of wikt:foreign|foreign celebrity-obsessed fictional character|character Avid Merrion in Channel 4 comedy Television programme|programme ''Bo' Selecta!'', as well as Northern England|Northern businessman Keith Lemon in ''Keith Lemon's Very Brilliant World Tour'' and ''Celebrity Juice''. As Keith Lemon, he has also made many appearances on ''Big Brother UK|Big Brother's'' companion shows ''Big Brother's Little Brother'' and ''Big Brother's Big Mouth''.
