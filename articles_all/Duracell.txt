

'''Duracell''' is a brand of Battery|batteries made by Procter and Gamble. Duracell makes alkaline batteries of 9V, C, AA, AAA, and D sizes.

In 2014, Berkshire Hathaway bought the Duracell.
 History 
The company was founded in the 1920s by Samuel Ruben and Philip Rogers Mallory.

The Duracell became a brand appeared in 1964. bought Duracell in 1996.
