


'''Kim Yong-Sik''' (July 25, 1910 - March 5, 1985) was a South Korean professional association football|footballer and coach. He was a member of the Japan national football team|Japanese national team and the Korea Republic national football team|Korean national team.  He competed in two Summer Olympic Games.SportsReference.com (SR/Olympics), 
International career statistics
Kim was a member of the Japanese team at the 1936 Summer Olympics in Berlin.
