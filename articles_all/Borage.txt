
'''Borage''', also known as '''''starflower''''', is an Annual plant|annual herb.
The flowers are wikt:perfect|perfect with five narrow, triangle|triangular-pointed petals. Flowers are most often blue in color, although pink flowers are sometimes examined. White flowered types are also grown.

 Cooking 
As a fresh vegetable, borage, with a cucumber like taste, is often used in salads or as a garnish. The flower, which has the non-toxic pyrrolizidine alkaloid thesinine, has a Sweet (taste)|sweet honey-like taste and as one of the few truly blue-colored eatable things, is often used to decorate dessert.
