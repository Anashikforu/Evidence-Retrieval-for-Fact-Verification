

'''Momo''' is a fictional character in Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's anime|animated television programme|television series ''Avatar: The Last Airbender''. The character and series were created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko. Momo is voice actor|voiced by Dee Bradley Baker.
