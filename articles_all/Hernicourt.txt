

'''Hernicourt''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Nord-Pas-de-Calais in the Pas-de-Calais Departments of France|department in the north of France. It is 106 miles north of Paris on the River Ternoise. In 2004 there were about 424 people living in Hernicourt.
