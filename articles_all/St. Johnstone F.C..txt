

'''St. Johnstone Football Club''' is a Scotland|Scottish Association football|football club based in Perth, Perth and Kinross|Perth. Although it is officially recorded as being formed in 1884, research has shown that they did not play their first game until February 1885. The club plays its home games at McDiarmid Park.

St. Johnstone will now compete in the Scottish Premiership after finishing first and being Promotion and relegation|promoted in the 2008-09 season.
