

'''Nuevo León''' is a state in the northeast of Mexico and south of Texas. It is known as the finance|financial center of the country. It has a wide range of tourism|tourist attractions, such as museums, theme park|thematic parks, natural parks, grottos, as well as archaeological sites, gastronomy, and folklore. Its capital, Monterrey, the city of the mountains, is host venue of important international events, congresses, fairs, conventions and exhibitions.
