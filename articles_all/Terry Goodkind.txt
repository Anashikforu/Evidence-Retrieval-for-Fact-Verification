

'''Terry Goodkind''' (January 11, 1948 &ndash; September 17, 2020) was an American writer from the U.S. state of Nebraska. He was known for writing fantasy books. His most famous book is called ''Wizard's First Rule'' which is part of the ''The Sword of Truth'' series. He was born in Omaha, Nebraska.

Goodkind died on September 17, 2020 in Boulder City, Nevada at the age of 72.
