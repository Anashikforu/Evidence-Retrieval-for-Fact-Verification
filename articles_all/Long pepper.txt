
'''Long pepper''' (''Piper longum''), also known as the '''Indonesian long pepper''', is a flowering plant|flowering vine in the family ''Piperaceae'', known for its fruit, which is usually wikt:dry|dried and used as a spice.

 Uses 
Today, long pepper is a very rare ingredient in Europe|European cuisines, but it can still be found in Pakistan|Pakistani vegetable pickles, some North African spice mixtures, and in Indonesian and Malaysian cooking.
