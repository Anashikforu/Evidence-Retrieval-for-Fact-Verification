
 is a former Japanese Association football|football player. He played for the Japan national football team|Japan national team.

Biography
Mori was born in Nagasaki Prefecture on July 12, 1961. After graduating from Fukuoka University, he joined Japan Soccer League Division 1 club Shonan Bellmare|Fujita Industries in 1984. The club was relegated to Division 2 in 1990. In 1992, the club won the champions in Division 2. He retired in 1992. He played 131 games and scored 19 goals in the league.

On June 2, 1988, Mori debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against China national football team|China. In 1989, he played at 1990 FIFA World Cup|1990 World Cup qualification. He played 8 games for Japan until 1989.
