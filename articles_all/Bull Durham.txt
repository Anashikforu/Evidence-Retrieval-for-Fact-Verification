

'''''Bull Durham''''' is an 1988 United States|American romantic comedy baseball movie set in Durham, North Carolina. It was movie producer|produced by Thom Mount and Mark Burg and was movie director|directed by Ron Shelton. Bull Durham was released on June 15, 1988 in North America. The movie received positive reviews with a 98% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 73 out of 100 from Metacritic.
