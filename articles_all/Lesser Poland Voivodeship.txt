

'''Lesser Poland Voivodeship''', or '''Małopolska Province''',Arkadiusz Belczyk,
The province's natural borders are Świętokrzyskie Mountains (''Góry Świętokrzyskie'') on the north, ''Jura Krakowsko-Częstochowska'' (a broad range of hills stretching from Kraków to Częstochowa) on the west, and the Tatra mountains|Tatra, Pieniny Mountains|Pieniny and Beskidy Mountains on the south. The provinces and country|countries it is bordered by are the Silesian Voivodeship to the west, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship to the north, Subcarpathian Voivodeship to the east, and the country of Slovakia to the south.
