
"'''Halls of Illusions''''" is a 1997 midwest Hip hop music|hip hop song by the United States|American horrorcore band Insane Clown Posse. It is the first single from their fourth studio album ''The Great Milenko'' (1997). The song was produced by Insane Clown Posse and Mike E. Clark|Mike Clark. It featured Slash (musician)|Slash on the guitar.

The song is about the band's hatred towards child abuse and domestic violence.
The song has swearing but is loved by many Juggalos.
