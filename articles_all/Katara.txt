'''Katara''' is a fictional character in Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's anime|animated television programme|television series ''Avatar: The Last Airbender''. The character and series were created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko. 

Katara is voice actor|voiced by Mae Whitman and will be played by Kiawentiio Tarbell in the Avatar: The Last Airbender (upcoming TV series)|live action television remake.
