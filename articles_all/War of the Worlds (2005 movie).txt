
'''''War of the Worlds''''' is a 2005 movie that has Tom Cruise and Dakota Fanning as the main people. It was movie director|directed by Steven Spielberg. It is based on the H. G. Wells novel ''The War of the Worlds''.

 Plot 

Ray Ferrier's home town in New Jersey is attacked by Extraterrestrial life|alien tripods that were buried underneath the ground. He is then forced to flee the city with his two children and make it to Boston where his ex-wife is located.
