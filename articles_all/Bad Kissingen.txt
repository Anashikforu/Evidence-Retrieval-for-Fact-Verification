
'''Bad Kissingen''' is a city in Bavaria, in the country of Germany. About 21,000 people live there. 

 History 
It was first mentioned in 801 CE. It is a "spa town", a place where people go to rest and relax. The well that is the source of the spa was first mentioned in 823 CE.

 Well-known people 
 Jack Steinberger (born 1921), American physicist and Nobel Prize winner
