
'''Nico Rosberg''' (born 27 June 1985 in Wiesbaden, West Germany) is a Auto racing|racing driver for the Mercedes GP Formula One team. He races under the Germany|German flag in Formula One. Earlier in his career, he competed for Finland. He holds dual nationality of these two countries.
Rosberg won the 2005 GP2 Series for the ART team. Before that, he raced in Formula Three Euroseries for his father's team. Rosberg joined the Mercedes GP|Mercedes team for the  season.
