'''Eckerö''' is a municipality in the Åland Islands, Finland. As of March 2006, there are 926 people living in Eckerö.

Eckerö is the westernmost municipality of Finland. Mariehamn, the capital (city)|capital of Åland, is located 35 km away. 95 % of the people in Eckerö speak Swedish language|Swedish as their first language.
