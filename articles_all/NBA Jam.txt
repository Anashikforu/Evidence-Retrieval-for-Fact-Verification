
'''''NBA Jam''''' is an Arcade game|arcade basketball video game. The game was released in 1993. It was both developed and published by Midway Games|Midway. The gameplay for ''NBA Jam'' is based on the 1989 video game ''Arch Rivals'', which was another 2-on-2 basketball video game. The game is very popular in popular culture.

In the game if you enter a specific code you can unlock Bill Clinton. It is 2 on 2 arcade basketball.
