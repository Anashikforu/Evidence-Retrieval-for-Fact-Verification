
'''Montgomery County''' (county code '''MG''') is a County (United States)|county found in the U.S. state of Kansas. As of the 2010 United States Census|2010 census, 35,471 people lived there. and its most populous city is Coffeyville, Kansas|Coffeyville.

 History 
Montgomery County was created on February 26, 1867.
