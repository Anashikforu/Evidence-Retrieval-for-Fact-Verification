'''Nusa Penida''' is an island to the southeast of Bali, Indonesia. The island is a Subdistricts of Indonesia|subdistrict of the Klungkung regency. There are two small islands nearby: Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan. The Badung Strait separates the island from Bali while the Lombok Strait separates it from Lombok.
Diving is very popular around the island and there are many Macaques living on the island.
