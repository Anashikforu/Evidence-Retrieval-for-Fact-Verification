
'''''Wario Land: The Shake Dimension''''' (known as '''''Wario Land: Shake It!''''' in North America) is a platform game|platform video game made by Good-Feel and published by Nintendo for the Wii game console.

 Gameplay 
''The Shake Dimension'' is a side-scrolling platform game that uses 3D cel-shading to make it look hand-drawn. It stars Wario, and the player controls him by holding the Wii Remote like an Nintendo Entertainment System|NES controller. Wario can jump, do a shoulder bash, and at certain times, can use the Wii Remote's motion features to shake stuff or control stuff.
