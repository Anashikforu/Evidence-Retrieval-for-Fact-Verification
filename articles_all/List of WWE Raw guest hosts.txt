The '''WWE Raw Guest Host''' was started on June 29, 2009 by WWE Chairman Vince McMahon, with the first host being injured WWE superstar Dave Batista|Batista. The requirements for a guest host is a WWE Hall of Famer, a retired superstar, celebrity or injured wrestler.
