

'''''The Simpsons'' shorts''' is a series of one-minute television show|television shorts that ran on the variety show ''The Tracey Ullman Show'' for three seasons. The shorts were created by cartoonist Matt Groening.

 Season 1: 1987 


 Season 2: 1987-1988 


 Season 3: 1988-1989
