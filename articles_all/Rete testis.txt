
'''Rete testis''' is a network of small tubules found in the part of the testicle that carries spermatozoon|sperm. They can sometimes get cysts.

 Purpose 
The rete testis have concentrated sperm and fluid in them, and they help the epididymis to absorb this fluid. If the fluid is not absorbed, the person can be infertility|infertile.
