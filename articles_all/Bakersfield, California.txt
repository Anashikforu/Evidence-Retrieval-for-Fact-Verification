'''Bakersfield''' is a large city in the state of California, in the United States. It the county seat of Kern County, California|Kern County, in the southern part of the Central Valley. In the 2010 United States Census|2010 census, 347,483 people lived there.
California State Route 99|State Route 99, California State Route 58|State Route 58, and California State Route 178|State Route 178 run through or pass by the city. One major rail station, Bakersfield Station, is in the city. Meadows Field Airport is the airport that serves Bakersfield, and the rest of Kern County.
