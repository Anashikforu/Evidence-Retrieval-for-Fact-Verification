 
'''Franklin Roberto Lashley''' (born July 16, 1976) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler, Mixed martial arts|mixed martial artist and former collegiate Amateur wrestling|amateur wrestler. He is better known as '''Bobby Lashley'''. He is currently signed to the WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) where he is the WWE United States Champion|United States Champion. He is also known for his time in  Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA), where he competed under the ring name '''Lashley'''.

Lashley was released from the WWE on February 4, 2008. He is also an Mixed martial arts|MMA fighter, where he was considered a top draw for :en:Bellator_MMA|Bellator. He has not fought professionally since 2016.

 Wrestling career 
Lashley went to Missouri Valley College. While he was there, he won three national Sport wrestling|amateur wrestling championships between 1996 and 1998. During 1997 and 1998 he was the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics|NAIA National Wrestling Champion. He wrestled at the weight of 177 Pound (mass)|pounds (80 kilograms).

In November 2003, Lashley started professional wrestling. He began working for WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). WWE sent him to Ohio Valley Wrestling (OVW), where he started his training under Lance Storm. During 2005, Lashley appeared at four live events for both the WWE Raw|''RAW'' and WWE Friday Night SmackDown!|''SmackDown!'' brands. On September 23 2005, Lashley made his televised WWE debut on ''SmackDown!''

On November 14, Lashley moved to the Extreme Championship Wrestling (WWE)|ECW brand. Lashley signed a contract for the final spot in a six-man match for the ECW World Championship at ECW December to Dismember#2006|December to Dismember. Lashley won the match and became the new ECW World Champion. This made Lashley the first African American to hold the ECW title. Lashley lost the title to Vince McMahon in May of 2007. On June 3, 2007, he defeated McMahon to get the title back.

On the June 19, 2014 episode on ''Impact Wrestling'', Lashley defeated Eric Young to win his first TNA World Heavyweight Championship.
