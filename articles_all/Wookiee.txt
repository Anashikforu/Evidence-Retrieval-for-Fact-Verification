

A '''Wookiee''' is a Fictional character|fictional race of Extraterrestrial life|aliens from the Star Wars series of movies. Their home planet is Kashyyyk. The best known Wookiee Fictional character|character is Chewbacca.

 Origin 
When George Lucas was developing fictional characters for ''Star Wars'', he named Chewbacca after his dog, Chewbacca. The name "Wookiee" came from an Wikt:ad libitum|ad lib in Lucas's first feature movie, ''THX 1138''. The line was "I think I ran over a Wookiee back there."

Features
On Kashyyyk wookiees build their houses in mile-high trees.Jeanne Cavelos, ''The Science of Star Wars: An Astrophysicist's Independent Examination of...'' (New York: St. Martin's Press, 1999), p. 52 They have long arms and legs so they can climb the trees. Chewbacca is over seven feet tall and over 200 years old. By nature they are wikt:gentle|gentle and intelligent creatures. When angered they can pull a man's arms out of their Wikt:socket|sockets. Wookiees have a great sense of Olfaction|smell. They are fully covered with a thick coat of hair. Wookiees prize loyalty and Wikt:bravery|bravery above all else. 

 Language 
The Wookiee language is Shyriiwook. Wookiees are capable of understanding English language|English but cannot speak it. In the original Star Wars trilogy Chewbacca showed he could understand humans. His human partner, Han Solo, also shows knowledge of Shyriiwook. In the Star Wars expanded universe books, Chewbacca builds a wikt:miniature|miniature translator Android (robot)|droid ("Em Teedee") for his nephew Young Jedi Knights|Lowbacca. When Lowbacca begins training as a Jedi the droid helped him communicate with his fellow students.
