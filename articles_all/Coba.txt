

'''Coba''' () is a big wikt:ruin|ruined city of the Pre-Columbian Maya civilization. It is in the state of Quintana Roo, Mexico. It is about 90&nbsp;km east of the Maya site of Chichen Itza, about 40&nbsp;km west of the Caribbean Sea, and 44&nbsp;km northwest of the site of Tulum.  Coba was thought to have had around 50,000 people living in it.
