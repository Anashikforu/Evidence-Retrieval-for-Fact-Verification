

'''Salvador de Madariaga y Rojo''' (July 23, 1886 A Coruña, Spain - December 14, 1978 Locarno, Switzerland) was a Spain|Spanish diplomat, writer, historian and pacifism|pacifist. He was the father of Nieves Mathews and professor/historian Dr. Isabel de Madariaga.  He was the grandfather of Javier Solana.
