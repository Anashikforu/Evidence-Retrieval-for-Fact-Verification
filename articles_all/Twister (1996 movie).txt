
'''''Twister''''' is a 1996 United States|American disaster movie movie director|directed by Jan de Bont, and based upon a screenplay by Michael Crichton and Anne-Marie Martin. It follows a group of storm chasers attempting to prevent all tornadoes from destroying everything in Oklahoma. The movie has a famous scene of a flying cattle. It is a scene that was the subject of many parody|parodies. It was the first movie to be commercially released on DVD.

 Home media 
''Twister'' was released in VHS by Warner Bros. Home Entertainment|Warner Home Video in October 1996. It was later released on DVD on March 26, 1997. It is considered to be the first home release of a movie to use this now widely-used optical media technology.
