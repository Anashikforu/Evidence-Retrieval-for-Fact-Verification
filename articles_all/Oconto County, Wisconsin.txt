
'''Oconto County''' is a county (United States)|county  in the U.S. state of Wisconsin.  There were 35,634 people living in Oconto county as of the year 2000.  The county seat is Oconto, Wisconsin|Oconto.

Geography
The county has an area of 1,149&nbsp;square miles, says the United States Census Bureau.
