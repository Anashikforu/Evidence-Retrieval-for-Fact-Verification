The '''Cable News Network''' ('''CNN''') is an cable television|American cable news television channel. It was founded in 1980 by Ted Turner.
Shows
The hosts of its opinion shows are Don Lemon, Chris Cuomo,  Fredricka Whitfield,  Erin Burnett, Brianna Keiler and Brooke Baldwin.

CNN has been criticized by the right-wing Media Research Center for having a left-wing bias.
