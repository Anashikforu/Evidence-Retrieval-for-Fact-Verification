'''Aegukga''' (Aeguk-kka; 애국가 ; 愛國歌), meaning '''The Patriotic Song''' is the national anthem of the South Korea|Republic of Korea (South Korea). It should not be confused with Aegukka|North Korea's anthem, which has the same name.

 Lyrics 

The song is sang in the order of 1, refrain, 2, refrain, 3, refrain, 4, refrain.

 History 

 The song was first sang against the colonial Japanese.
 The song used to be a different tune than the current tune.
