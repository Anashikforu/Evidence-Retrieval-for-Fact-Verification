
'''Bioggio''' is a municipality of the district of Lugano (district)|Lugano, in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

Bioggio was created on 4 April 2004 from the merger of Bosco Luganese and Cimo, Ticino|Cimo. On 20 April 2008, Iseo, Switzerland|Iseo used to be part of the municipality of Bioggio.
