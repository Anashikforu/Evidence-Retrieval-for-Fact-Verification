
'''Leuk''' (French '''Loèche''') is a municipality of Switzerland and is the capital city of the district of Leuk (district)|Leuk in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Valais in Switzerland.

Since it controls access to the Gemmipass, it had some importance from the time of Roman Empire|Roman Raetia. The Leukerbad hot baths are just North of Leuk, towards the pass.

Loèche houses the installations of Onyx (interception system)|Onyx, the Swiss interception system for electronic intelligence gathering.

On 1 January 2013 the former municipality of Erschmatt merged into the municipality of Leuk.
