

'''Pope John XIX''' (; &nbsp; -died October 1032), born '''Romanus''', was an Italy|Italian bishop of the Roman Catholic Church and the List of popes|145th Pope from 1024 to 1032.
Early life
Romanus was born in Rome to the noble Tusculum family.The Tusculum family are also known as the Tuscolani He was the brother of Pope Benedict VIII and the uncle of Pope Benedict IX.
Pope
Romanus was elected pope in 1024. His family used his money and influence to make Romanus the pope. This was called simony.  He chose the name Pope John,

After his election, Pope John became a priest and a bishop.

John XIX is credited as the first pope to grant an indulgence.

His successor was his nephew, who became known as Benedict IX.
