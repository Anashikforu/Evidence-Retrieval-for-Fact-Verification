
'''Robert Anthony Plant''' (born 20 August 1948, West Bromwich, Staffordshire, England), is an English people|English Rock and Roll|rock singer and musician, and was known for being the lead vocals|lead vocalist for rock music|rock band Led Zeppelin. In 2007, he recorded an album with bluegrass music|bluegrass fiddler Alison Krauss, and toured with her in 2008.
