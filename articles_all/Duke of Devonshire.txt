'''Duke of Devonshire''' is a title in the Peerage of England held by members of the aristocracy|aristocratic House of Cavendish|Cavendish family. This branch of the Cavendish family has been one of the richest and most influential aristocratic families in England since the 16th century, and have been rivalled in political influence perhaps only by the Earl of Derby|Earls of Derby and the Marquess of Salisbury|Marquesses of Salisbury.
