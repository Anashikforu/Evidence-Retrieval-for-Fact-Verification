
'''Collaboration''' is when people work with each other to complete a task.. It involves co-operation and teamwork and the sharing of ideas, knowledge and skills to reach the same wikt:objective|objective.
Each person plays a tightly or loosely defined role in the process of collaboration. For instance, to make a movie a writer, Movie director|director, producer and actors are needed. There may also be a need for a costumer, a cameraman (if the director cannot do it all), an audio engineer, and a film editor if the project is big enough and the schedule tight enough.
