'''Bran Castle''' () is a castle near Bran, Braşov|Bran  in the area of Braşov, Romania.  It is a national monument and Landmarks|landmark in Romania.  The Fortification|fortress lies on the border between Transylvania and Wallachia, on Highway 73. 

The castle is known as "Dracula's Castle". It was used as the home of Dracula in Bram Stoker's ''Dracula''.
