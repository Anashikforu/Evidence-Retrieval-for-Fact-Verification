The '''Wii Balance Board''' (called '''Wii Board''' in Europe) is a balance board accessory made for Nintendo's Wii and Wii U game console."Wi Fit: How To Stay Balanced," ''GamePro'' 235 (April 2008): 19. It was first used for ''Wii Fit'', and was also used for other games, including ''We Ski''.
