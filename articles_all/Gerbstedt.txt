

'''Gerbstedt''' is a little town at the Mansfeld-Südharz Rural District|Mansfeld-Südharz rural district, Saxony-Anhalt in Germany. It is  north of Lutherstadt Eisleben|Eisleben. The little river Lobach flows through it.

Gerbstedt is about 30&nbsp;km northwest of Halle, Saxony-Anhalt. It was first mentioned in 985 as Gerbizstidi. In the area of Gerbstedt, there is copper schist. It is mined since 1730.
