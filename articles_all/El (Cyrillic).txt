'''El''' ('''Л''', '''л''') is the eleventh letter of the Cyrillic alphabet. It sounds like l. It comes from the Greek alphabet|Greek letter lambda and is the same as the Latin script|Roman L. It looks like Pe (Cyrillic)|Pe, (''П'', ''п''), another letter in the same alphabet but its left leg is curved.
