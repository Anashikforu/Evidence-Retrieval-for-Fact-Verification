

'''Kokkai-gijidōmae Station''' (国会議事堂前駅,こっかいぎじどうまええき) is a railway station of Tokyo Metro in Tokyo. This station is also connected by underground passageways to Tameike-Sanno Station. It is also the deepest station on the entire Tokyo Metro system.

"Kokkai-gijidōmae" means "'''In front of National Diet Building'''".
