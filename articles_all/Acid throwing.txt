

'''Acid throwing''' or '''vitriolage''' is a form of violence|violent assault. Sometimes the crime is called '''acid attack'''. The attackers throw acid or other Corrosive substance|corrosive substances at their Victim|victims. Usually they throw it at the targets' faces. They want to damage skin tissue, even to expose and to dissolve the bones. The consequences of these attacks include blindness and permanent scars of the face and body.

In India, the number of acid attacks have been rising.
Acid throwing is a crime in most countries in the world.  Many Islamic countries have the concept of qisas, or retributive justice. With this, a victim can ask that the attacker be disfigured in a similar way. That way, an Iranian court ordered that the attacker of a woman who was blinded by such an attack, be blinded as well.
