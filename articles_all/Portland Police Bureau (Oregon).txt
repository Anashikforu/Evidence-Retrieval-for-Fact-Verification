The '''Portland Police Bureau'''  is the law enforcement agency of the City of Portland, Oregon|Portland, the biggest city in the U.S. state of Oregon.

History
The Bureau, first named the Portland Metropolitan Police Force, was created in 1870 by the Portland City Council. The Council chose Phillip Saunders as its first chief of police.
