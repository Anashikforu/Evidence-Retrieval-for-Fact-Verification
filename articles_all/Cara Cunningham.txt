
'''Cara Cunningham''' (formerly '''Chris Crocker'''; born December 7, 1987) is an Americans|American internet celebrity and singer. She is best known for her viral video on video sharing website YouTube. For her video entitled "Leave Britney Spears|Britney Alone", she got over 30 million views. She has also appeared on the Maury Povich television programme|TV program. Prior to beginning her transition, Cunningham felt she could not be open or public about her gender identify and felt she had to present herself as gay, but she is heterosexual. She often spoken of how hard it was growing up gay or transgender in eastern Tennessee.
