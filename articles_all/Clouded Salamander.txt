The '''Clouded Salamander''' ('''''Hynobius nebulosus''''') is a type of salamander in the Hynobiidae Family (biology)|family of animals.  It can only be found in Japan.  The places they like to live are warm forests, rivers, swamps, freshwater spring (hydrosphere)|springs, and land that is being watered.
