
'''Michael Kevin Moore''' (born June 3, 1965) is a United Kingdom|British politician who was the Secretary of State for Scotland in the Conservative-Liberal Democrat coalition government. He was the Member of Parliament for Berwickshire, Roxburgh and Selkirk. He was a Liberal Democrats|Liberal Democrat MP from 1997 to 2015.

He was born in Belfast but grew up in Scotland.
