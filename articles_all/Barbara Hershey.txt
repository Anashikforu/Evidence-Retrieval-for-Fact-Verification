
'''Barbara Lynn Herzstein''' (b. February 5, 1948), better known as '''Barbara Hershey''', is an Americans|American movie and television actress.

She won the Golden Globe Award in 1991 for "Best Performance by an Actress in a Mini-Series or Motion Picture Made for TV". She was also in ''Black Swan (movie)|Black Swan'', ''The Last Temptation of Christ'' and ''Hannah and Her Sisters''.
