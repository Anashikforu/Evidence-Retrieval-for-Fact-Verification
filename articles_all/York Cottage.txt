'''York Cottage''' was the home of King George V of the United Kingdom|George V and Mary of Teck|Queen Mary when they were Duke of York|Duke and Duchess of York.  It is currently used as the Estate Office for Sandringham House.  Some of the building is also used as flats for estate employees and holiday accommodation.

King George V and his wife, Queen Mary, lived in the house after their marriage in 1893.
