

'''Timothy Francis "Tim" LaHaye''' (April 27, 1926 &ndash; July 25, 2016) was an American evangelicalism|evangelical Baptist minister, author, and Public speaking|speaker. He was best known for co-authoring the ''Left Behind (series)|Left Behind'' series with Jerry B. Jenkins. He wrote over 50 books, both fiction and non-fiction. Time (magazine)|''Time'' magazine named LaHaye "''one of the 25 most :wikt:influence|influental evangelicals in America''."

His wife was Beverly LaHaye, founder of Concerned Women for America.

LaHaye died of a stroke on July 25, 2016 in San Diego, California, aged 90.
