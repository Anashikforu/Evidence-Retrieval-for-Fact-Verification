
'''Danny Woodhead''' (born January 25, 1985 in North Platte, Nebraska) is an Americans|American American football|football  running back for the New England Patriots of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Woodhead made his NFL debut with the New York Jets in 2008 and was signed by the New England Patriots on September 18, 2010.
