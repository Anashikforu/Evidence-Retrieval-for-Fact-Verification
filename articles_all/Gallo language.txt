'''Gallo''' is one of the two regional languages of Brittany. It is an Langue d'oïl|Oïl language of the  Romance language family, with Latin at the base. Gallo is spoken in the eastern part of Brittany.

The other regional language of Brittany is Breton language|Breton. Breton is a Celtic language.
