Sombart was born in Ermsleben, Harz. He was the son of Anton Ludwig Sombart, a rich liberal politician, industrialist. 
Werner studied at the university|universities in Pisa, Berlin, and Rome, learning both law and economics. In 1888, he received his Doctor of Philosophy|Ph.D. from Berlin.
