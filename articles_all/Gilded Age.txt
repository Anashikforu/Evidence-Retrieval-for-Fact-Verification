

'''The Gilded Age''' is a time in the in History of the United States|history of the USA. It lasted from the end of the American Civil War, and up to the late 19th century. It corresponds to the latter part of the British Victorian era. 

The name Gilded Age was first used by writer Mark Twain in ''The Gilded Age: A Tale of Today'' (1873).  The time saw rapid progress in technology and railroad expansions and industrialization.
