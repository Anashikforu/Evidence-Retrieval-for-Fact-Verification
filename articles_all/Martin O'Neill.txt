'''Martin Hugh Michael O'Neill, Order of the British Empire|OBE''' (b. 1 March 1952) is an Northern Irish people|Irish association football|football manager. He is currently the manager of Sunderland F.C., and has previously managed Celtic F.C.. and Aston Villa F.C. O'Neill was born in Kilrea, Northern Ireland.
