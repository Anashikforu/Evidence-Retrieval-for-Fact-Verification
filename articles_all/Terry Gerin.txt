
'''Terrance "Terry" Gerin''' (born October 7, 1975) is an Americans|American professional wrestler and actor. He is best known for working with WWE|World Wrestling Federation, Extreme Championship Wrestling, and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling under the ring names '''Rhino''' and '''Rhyno'''. He currently works for Ring of Honor. His finishing move is called the Gore (Professional wrestling attacks#Spear|spear).
