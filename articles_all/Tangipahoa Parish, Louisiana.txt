

'''Tangipahoa Parish''' is a County (United States)|parish in the U.S. State of Louisiana. The parish was put together from parts of 4 other parishes in 1869. Before it was a parish, many Slavery in the United States|slaves were forced to work on sugar Plantation|plantations in the area. After the American Civil War|Civil War, the white people continued to be very violent towards the black people they had enslaved. All across Southern United States|the south, there were Lynching in the United States|lynchings against black people. Tangipahoa Parish had more lynchings than any nearby parish.
