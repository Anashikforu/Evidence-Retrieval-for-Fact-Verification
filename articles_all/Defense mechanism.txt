'''Defense mechanism''' is a psychological concept of the mind. It is the coping skill|technique individuals Unconscious mind|unconsciously use to protect themselves from getting hurt in unpleasant situations (e.g. conflicts, unhappiness). Without defense mechanisms, people may suffer from psychological and mental illness.
