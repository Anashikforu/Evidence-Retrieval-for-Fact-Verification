The '''Wittelsbach''' family is a European royal family and a Germany|German dynasty from Bavaria. 

Members of the family were rulers of Bavaria, either as Dukes, Prince-elector|Electors or Kings, from 1180 until the revolution in 1918, after the defeat of German Empire|Germany in World War I.
