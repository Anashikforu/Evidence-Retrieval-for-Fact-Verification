
'''Stuart "Stu" Alexander Bennet''' (born 10 August 1980) is an English people|English professional wrestling|professional wrestler and former Bare-knuckle boxing|bare-knuckle boxer. He was most recently signed to the WWE where he was known under the ring names, '''Wade Barrett''', '''Bad News Barrett''' and '''King Barrett'''.

Barrett was born in Penwortham. He lived in Preston until he was six. He then moved to Wales.

Barrett was the winner of the first season of ''WWE NXT|NXT''. He made his debut on the main roster on the June 7 episode of ''WWE Raw|Raw'' as the leader of Nexus (professional wrestling)|The Nexus. After he joined a new group The Corre.

On the April 28 episode of ''WWE Raw|Raw'', Barrett competed in the 2015 King of the Ring tournament. In the tournament, he defeated Dolph Ziggler in the first round after Sheamus distracted Ziggler.
