A '''battle''' is a fight between two or more groups where each group is trying to wikt:defeat|defeat (beat) the others.  Battles are most often fought during wars.  A small battle fought by only a small part of the armies is called a Skirmisher|skirmish.  Series of battles are called military campaigns.  The ground on which a battle is fought is called a battlefield.''Department of Defense Dictionary of Military and Associated Terms'', ed. Barry Leonard (Department of Defense; Joint Publication 1-02), p. 52

Battles can very important, for example the 1389 Battle of Kosovo, which was a draw in military terms, and the 1798 Battle of the Nile which the French lost, changed the history of  Europe and the world.
