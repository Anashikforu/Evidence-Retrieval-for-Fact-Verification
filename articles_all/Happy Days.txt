

'''''Happy Days''''' is an United States|American television sitcom. It was originally broadcast|aired on American Broadcasting Company|ABC between January 15, 1974 and June 12, 1984. It stars Ron Howard, and has several spin-offs. It is set in Milwaukee, Wisconsin in the 1950s and 1960s.

 History 
= Jumping the shark =
The term Jump the shark|"jumping the shark", meaning when a good show goes bad, usually because of one or more major mistakes or running out of ideas, originated in a two-part episode of ''Happy Days''. The episode was about Fonzie jumping a shark. This is thought to be the moment where the show started going downhill.
