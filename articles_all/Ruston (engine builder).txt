
'''Ruston''' was an industrial equipment manufacturer in Lincoln, Lincolnshire|Lincoln, England, the company's history going back to 1840. The company is best known as a manufacturer of narrow gauge railway|narrow and standard gauge diesel locomotives and also of steam shovels.
