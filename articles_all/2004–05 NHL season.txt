


The '''2004–05 NHL season''' would have been the 88th regular season of the National Hockey League (NHL). The season was canceled on February 16, 2005 because of an unfinished lockout (industry)|lockout that began on September 16, 2004. The 2004–05 NHL lockout|loss of the 2004–05 season made the NHL the first North American professional sports league to cancel an entire season because of a Labour (economics)|labour issue.
