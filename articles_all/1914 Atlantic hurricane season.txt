

The '''1914 Atlantic hurricane season''' was the shortest and least active hurricane season on record. This season had only one storm with two other possible storms. This season was also one of two to not have a hurricane with the other being 1907 Atlantic hurricane season|1907.



Tropical Storm One


This system form as a tropical wave just to the east of Florida on September 13. It became a Tropical Depression on the 14th, the storm made landfall in Georgia (U.S. State)|Georgia with 70mph winds. It moved westward and dissipated over Texas on the 19th.
