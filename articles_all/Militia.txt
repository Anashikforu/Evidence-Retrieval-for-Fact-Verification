
A '''militia''' is a group of people not in the military who do many things that the military does.  This includes collecting weapons, training soldiers, defending people or land, police|policing an area.  Militias are often made up of veterans who were once in the military.  

Some militias are organized by a government, like the United States National Guard.  This is a group of state militias who help with wars, natural disasters and other emergency|emergencies.  Others are not connected to a government, and may even be illegal. One common definition of a militia includes all men who are able to serve as soldiers.

At the beginning in the late 20th century, some militias acted as professional forces, while still being "part-time" or "on-call" organizations.
