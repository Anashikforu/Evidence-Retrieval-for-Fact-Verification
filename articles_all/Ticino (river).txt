The '''Ticino river''' is a river that originates in the Alps, near Nufenen. It then flows through the Switzerland|Swiss canton of Ticino and northern Italy. It flows into the Po River, near Pavia. It is 270 kilometre|km long. It gave its name to the canton. In Switzerland, it is dammed to make electricity. In Italy, it is mainly used for irrigation. The river flows through Lago Maggiore.
