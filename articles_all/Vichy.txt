

'''Vichy''' () is a Communes of France|''commune'' in central France, in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region, along the Allier (river)|Allier river. It is the ''commune'' in the department with second largest population after Montluçon. It is in the History|historic province of ''Bourbonnais''.

History
Vichy has lots of Spas and resorts. This site was inhabited before the Roman conquest of Gaul.Gros, J.F. (1884). 
In 1861, Napoleon III visited Vichy several times.Prince, Danforth ''et al.'' (2011). 
During World War II, it was the Capital (city)|capital city of Vichy France.Vinen, Richard. (2007). 
 Geography 
The ''commune'' of Vichy is on the southern part of the Allier department. The city is on the right side of the Allier (river)|Allier river.

= Climate =
The Köppen climate classification type for the climate at Vichy is an "Oceanic climate" (also known as Maritime Temperate climate) and of the subtype "Cfb".

 Administration 
Vichy is a Prefectures in France|subprefecture of the Allier department since 1941; before that year, the subprefecture was in Lapalisse. It is also the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of Arrondissement of Vichy|Vichy and the administrative centre () of two cantons:
 Canton of Vichy-1|Vichy-1 formed by 3 ''communes'' and part of Vichy, with 18,420 inhabitants (2014).
 Canton of Vichy-2|Vichy-2 formed by 2 ''communes'' and part of Vichy, with 18,763 inhabitants (2014).

It is part of the Communes of France|intercommunality ''Vichy Communauté''.
