

'''Gary Paul Davis''' (born March 1, 1969 in Upland, California|Upland, California), known by his stage name '''Litefoot''', is an Americans|American rapper and actor. He is of Cherokee and Chichimeca descent. He was the first Native American person to rap and he called his style of music, ''Tribalistic Funk''. 

David is also an actor and acted in the movies The Indian in the Cupboard (movie)|The Indian in the Cupboard in 1995, Mortal Kombat: Annihilation in 1997, Kull the Conqueror in 1997 and Adaptation (movie)|Adaptation in 2002.
