
'''Dwayne "D. J." King''' (born June 27, 1984) is a Canadians|Canadian former professional ice hockey winger (ice hockey)|left winger who played in the National Hockey League (NHL) with the St. Louis Blues and the Washington Capitals.

Before playing in the NHL, King played 4 seasons in the Western Hockey League; 3 seasons with the Lethbridge Hurricanes and 1 season with the Kelowna Rockets. He was drafted by the St. Louis Blues with the 190th overall pick in the 2002 NHL Entry Draft.

King is of Métis people|Métis heritage. He has a younger brother named Dwight King|Dwight, that plays for the Los Angeles Kings.
