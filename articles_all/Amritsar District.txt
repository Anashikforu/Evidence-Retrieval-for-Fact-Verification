'''Amritsar district''' (Doabi:ਤਾਲੁਕਾ ਅੰਮ੍ਰਿਤਸਰ) is one of 19 districts in the state of Punjab, India|Punjab in  West India. It has a population over 3,000,000  and covers an area of 5075&nbsp;km². The city of Amritsar is headquarters of this district.

History
During British India|British Rule Amritsar was part of Lahore Division and was subdivided into 3 tehsils, there were - Amritsar|Amritsar taluka, Tarn Taran taluka and Ajnala taluka.
The Principle Jatt Sikh clans according to 1911 census are Aulakh,  Bhangu, Bhullar, Chahal, Chhina, Deo, Dhariwal, Dhillon, Shergill, Hanjra, Hayer, Hundal, Khaira, Kang, Mahal, Maan, Pannu, Randhawa, Sandhu, Sidhu, Sohal, Virk, Waraich.
