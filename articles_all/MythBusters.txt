

'''''MythBusters''''' is an United States|American scientific method|scientific television show, which began on January 23, 2003. The hosts, Adam Savage and Jamie Hyneman along with Kari Byron, Tory Belleci and Grant Imahara attempt to test whether certain myths are ''true or false'', referred to on the show as ''confirmed or busted'', respectively. The show has had a total of 267 episodes as of September 5, 2015. Two more episodes are to come in January 2016.
