
'''Our Lady Peace''' is a Canada|Canadian Alternative rock band. They formed in 1992 in Toronto. The members of the group are Raine Maida, the singer, who also plays the rhythm guitar, Jeremy Taggart, who plays the drum|drums, Duncan Coutts, who plays the Bass guitar and Backing vocals and Steve Mazur, who plays the guitar and Backing vocals. ''A Decade'' is the name of their greatest hits album. They have a live audience DVD out also. They have many Billboard awards.
