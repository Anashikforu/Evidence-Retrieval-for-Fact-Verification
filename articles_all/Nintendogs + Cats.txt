


Nintendogs + Cats is a video game sequel of Nintendogs. It was released in North America on March 26, 2011. This game has a total of 3 versions. It is made for the Nintendo 3DS.

On April 26, 2011, just one month after this game was released in North America, Nintendogs + Cats have sold 1.7 million copies of the game.

 Ratings 
Common Sense Media gave this game 5 out of 5 stars.
 Features 
Nintendogs + Cats has more features than Nintendogs, like a RoboDog and cats.
