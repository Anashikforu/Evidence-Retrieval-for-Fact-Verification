"'''Baba O'Riley'''" is a song by the English rock music|rock band The Who, written by Pete Townshend. Roger Daltrey sings most of the song, with Pete Townshend singing the Thirty-two-bar form|middle eight: "Don't cry/don't raise your eye/it's only teenage wasteland". The song's title comes from this combination of the song's philosophical and musical influences: Meher Baba and Terry Riley.The Who: The Ultimate Collection, page 12
