'''Apotropaism''' or '''apotropaic magic''' is the use of Magic (paranormal)|magic rituals against evil or harmful influences like the evil eye or ''w:invidia|invidia'', which was thought to cause injury or bad Superstition|luck. This can be a long ritual, or it can take the form of simply wearing an amulet or other token, ''against bad luck''.
