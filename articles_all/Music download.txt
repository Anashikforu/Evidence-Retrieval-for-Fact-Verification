
A '''music download''' is the act of downloading a song from the Internet to a user's computer. This term can mean both legal downloads (by money|paying to download the music) and downloads of copyright material without wikt:permission|permission or paying.

As of 2006, digital music sales are estimated to have reached about US$2 billion.
There are many websites that let you download music. Some of these sites are iTunes, Google Play, Microsoft Windows|Windows Store, and Amazon.com. , ''FierceMobileContent''
