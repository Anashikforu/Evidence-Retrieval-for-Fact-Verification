 Traditional history 
Ninken was a grandson of Emperor Richū; and he was adopted  by the childless Emperor Seinei.Aston, William. (1998). ''Nihongi,'' Vol. 1, pp. 373-377.

 Events of Ninken's life 
Very little is known about the events of Ninken's life and reign.  Only limited information is wikt:available|available for study prior to the reign of the 29th monarch, Emperor Kimmei.Titsingh, pp. 34-36; Brown, pp. 261-262; Varley, pp. 123-124.
