
'''Bill Kopp''' (born April 17, 1962 in Rockford, Illinois|Rockford, Illinois) is an Americans|American Animation|animator and actor|voice actor. He performed the "Whammy" on the 1980s game show ''Press Your Luck'' and the title fictional character|character on Nelvana's ''Eek! The Cat''. He was an animator for The Simpsons' shorts on the The Tracey Ullman Show|Tracey Ullman Show, but left after the first season. He created ''The Schnookums and Meat Funny Cartoon Show and Mad Jack the Pirate''. He studied animation at the California Institute of the Arts.
