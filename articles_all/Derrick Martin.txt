
'''Derrick Martin''' (born May 16, 1985 in Westminster, Colorado|Westminster, Colorado) is an Americans|American American football|football strong safety that currently plays for the New England Patriots of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Martin was drafted out of the University of Wyoming by the Baltimore Ravens with the 208th pick in the sixth round of the 2006 NFL Draft.
