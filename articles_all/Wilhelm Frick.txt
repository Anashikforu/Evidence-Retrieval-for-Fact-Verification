
'''Wilhelm Frick''' (12 March 1877 in Alsenz - 16 October 1946 in Nuremberg) was a well-known Nazism|Nazi official. Between 1933 and 1943, he was a minister of the Third Reich. After the end of World War II, he was executed for war crimes.
