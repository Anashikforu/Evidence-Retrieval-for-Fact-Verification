

Saint '''Jude the Apostle''' was one of the Twelve Apostles of Jesus. He was also called '''Jude of James''' and '''Jude Thaddeus'''. He is the patron saint of Armenia, along with Bartholomew the Apostle|Saint Bartholemew. He is also the patron saint of lost causes and desperate situations.

St. Jude was martyred (killed for his beliefs) with another apostle, Simon the Zealot in Beirut, Lebanon, around AD 66 by a axe.  In the Roman Catholic church, Jude and Simon's feast day is celebrated on 29 October.
