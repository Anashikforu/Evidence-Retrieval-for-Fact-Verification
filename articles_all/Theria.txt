
'''Theria''' is a Subclass (biology)|subclass of mammals. It includes almost all living mammals: the ones that give birth to live young, and not by using a Eggshell|shelled Egg (biology)|egg.

The Theria are divided into two groups (infraclasses): Metatheria and Eutheria. The surviving Metatheria are the marsupials, and the Eutheria are the placental mammals.
