
'''Winfield''' is a city in Fayette County, Alabama|Fayette and Marion County, Alabama|Marion counties in the U.S. state of Alabama. The population was 4,717 at the United States Census, 2010|2010 census, the second largest city in Marion County.

Notable natives
W. Eugene Davis, judge of the United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit
Johnny Micheal Spann, an employee of the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) and the first American killed in combat after the Operation Enduring Freedom|U.S. invasion of Afghanistan.
Jimmy Miles, award winning Country musician
