'''Bakerhill''' or '''Baker Hill''' is a city in Barbour County, Alabama|Barbour County, Alabama, United States, near Eufaula, Alabama|Eufaula.  According to the 2005 U.S. Census estimates, the city had a population of 321.
 Geography 
Bakerhill is located at 31.88 degrees north, 85.15 degrees west (31.88495, -85.15390).
