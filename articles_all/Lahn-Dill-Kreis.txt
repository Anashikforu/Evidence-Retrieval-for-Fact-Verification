

'''Lahn-Dill''' is a ''Kreis'' (district) in the west of Hesse, Germany.
 History 
In 1977 the district Gießen Rural District|Gießen was merged with the districts Wetzlar Rural District|Wetzlar and Dillkreis to the new Lahn-Dill-Kreis. However this big district was not popular, so in 1979 Giessen was split from it.

 Geography 
The main rivers of the district are the Lahn and the Dill (river)|Dill, which also gave it the name.
