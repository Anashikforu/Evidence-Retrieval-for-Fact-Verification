'''Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey''' (also known as '''Rutgers University'''), is the largest institution for higher education in the U.S. state|state of New Jersey. It was originally chartered as ''Queen's College'' in 1766 and is the eighth-oldest college in the United States. 

 Ranking 
Rutgers was designated The State University of New Jersey by acts of the New Jersey Legislature in 1945 and 1956.
