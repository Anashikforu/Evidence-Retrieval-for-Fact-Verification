'''Geoffrey William Hoon''' (born 6 December 1953) is an English people|English politician and held several government posts. He was born in Derby. He was the Member of Parliament for the Ashfield (UK Parliament constituency)|Ashfield constituency in the United Kingdom from his first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1992|1992 general election until he retired in 2010.

Hoon is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
