

"''''Somewhere Out There''''" is a 2002 alternative rock song by the Canada|Canadian alternative rock band Our Lady Peace and is the first single from their fifth studio album ''Gravity (Our Lady Peace album)|Gravity'', which was released in 2002. The song was written by Raine Maida. The song was the most successful song on the album and was also the most internationally successful single ever released by the band.
