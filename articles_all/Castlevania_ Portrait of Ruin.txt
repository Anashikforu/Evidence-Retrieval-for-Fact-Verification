
'''''Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin''''', known in Japan as , is an Action RPG video game made by Konami for the Nintendo DS handheld game console|handheld video game console. It's the second game release for the system, and has a sequel that's going to be released called ''Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia''.

Gameplay
''Portrait of Ruin'' is a sidescrolling action game that has gameplay from platform games and console role-playing game|role-playing games like jumping to reach higher areas and leveling up.
