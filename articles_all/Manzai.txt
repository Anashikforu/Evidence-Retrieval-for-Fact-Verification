 History 
The history of ''manzai'' began in the Nara period.Kodansha. (1985). "Mansai,"  ''Encyclopedia of Japan,'' Vol. 5, p. 111.

In the Edo period, the traditional dialogue was performed on stage.

In modern times, ''manzai'' performances are televised in Japan.

=New Year's tradition=
In Japan's past, traditional ''manzai'' performers would go from house to house in the New Year's season.  They would sing songsYamaguchi, Kenkichi ''et al.'' (1964).
