

'''Dame Angela Brigid Lansbury'''  (born October 16, 1925) is an English character actress of stage, film, and television. She is notable for her roles as ''Jessica Fletcher'' in the long-running TV series ''Murder, She Wrote'' (1984-96), and as the voice of ''Mrs. Potts'' in the Disney film ''Beauty and the Beast'' (1991).
