
'''Luigino Celestino Di Agostino''' (born December 17, 1967) is an Italy|Italian disk jockey. He was born in Turin, Italy. Di Agostino is better known under his stage name '''Gigi D'Agostino'''. He is also known as the ''Violento Man''.
