'''Princess Leia Organa''', born '''Leia Amidala Skywalker''', is a fictional character|character from the ''Star Wars'' fictional universe|universe. Leia was played by Carrie Fisher in the original three ''Star Wars'' movies: ''Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope|A New Hope'', ''Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back|The Empire Strikes Back'', and ''Star Wars Episode VI: Return of the Jedi|Return of the Jedi''. She is also a main character in many novels that are set in the ''Star Wars'' universe.
