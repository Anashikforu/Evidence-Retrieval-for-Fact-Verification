
 is a platform game|platform video game made by Nintendo for the Game Boy handheld game console|handheld video game console|game console. It stars Mario who has to save his girlfriend Pauline from Donkey Kong, just like in the original ''Donkey Kong (video game)|Donkey Kong'' game for the Arcade game|arcades.
The gameplay in the first 4 levels is the same as the first ''Donkey Kong'' game, but then it becomes more like the ''Mario vs. Donkey Kong'' game in the next levels.
