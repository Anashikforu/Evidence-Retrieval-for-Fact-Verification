
'''Frank Vincent Zappa''' (December 21, 1940 - December 4, 1993) was an Americans|American musician and composer. He was known for his strange style of music, which was often weird and funny. He made various types of music and many albums. Zappa worked with many other musicans, most notably his 1960s group The Mothers of Invention and his friend and Blues music|blues singer Captain Beefheart.  Some people think he is the best composer in popular music or even any non-classical music.

Zappa was born in Baltimore, Maryland, but spent most of his life in Southern California. He grew up in Baltimore, in Florida, and in Monterey, California|Monterey, Claremont, California|Claremont and El Cajon, California. His mother was of France|French and Italy|Italian descent; his father was an immigrant from Sicily. Zappa had four children with his wife Gail. They gave them all very unusual names: Moon Unit Zappa|Moon Unit, Dweezil Zappa|Dweezil, Ahmet Zappa|Ahmet, and Diva.

Zappa was openly against Illegal drugs|drugs, Televangelism, and censorship|censoring music. He often tried to see what he could get away with.

Zappa died from prostate cancer in Los Angeles, California.
