

'''Squidbillies''' is a cartoon series on the Adult Swim block of the Cartoon Network. It is about a Poverty|poor family of squids living in the Appalachian Mountains|Appalachian region. It was first shown on October 16, 2005. The show was created because of the popularity of the Aqua Teen Hunger Force. So far 50 episodes have been made.
