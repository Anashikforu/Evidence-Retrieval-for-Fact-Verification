'''Binic''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Bretagne in the Côtes-d'Armor Departments of France|department in the west of France. Once a commune of its own, Binic is now part of the commune of Binic-Étables-sur-Mer, following a merger on 1 March 2016.
