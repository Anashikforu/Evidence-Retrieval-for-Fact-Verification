'''Violet Baudelaire''' is a fictional character in the popular children's book series ''A Series of Unfortunate Events'' by Lemony Snicket. Violet is the oldest of the Baudelaire Orphan|orphans. Her younger siblings are her brother Klaus Baudelaire|Klaus and her sister Sunny Baudelaire|Sunny. She is an inventor. She puts her hair up when she is inventing.
