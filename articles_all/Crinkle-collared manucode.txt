The '''crinkle-collared manucode''' (''Manucodia chalybatus'') is a medium-sized Birds of Paradise|bird of paradise, that grows up to 36cm long. It looks glossy greenish blue, black and purple with long tail, red Iris (anatomy)|iris and iridescence|iridescent green breast feathers. 

This species looks a little like the Jobi manucode in appearance. It is found in lowlands and hill forests in mainland New Guinea and Misool Island of West Papua (province)|West Papua. They eat mainly of fruits and figs.
