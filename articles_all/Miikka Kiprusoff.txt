
'''Miikka Sakari Kiprusoff''' (pronounced "MEE-kah KIHP-roo-sawf") (born October 26, 1976) is a Finland|Finnish retired professional ice hockey|hockey goaltender. He played 11 seasons in the National Hockey League|NHL mainly with the Calgary Flames.

The Calgary Flames announced on September 9, 2013 that Kiprusoff had retired.

Awards
He was named to the SM-liiga All-Star Team during the 1998–99 season.
Urpo Ylönen trophy (1998–99)
Jari Kurri trophy (1998–99)
He was named as a First Team All-Star during the 2005-06 NHL season
Vezina Trophy (2005-06 NHL season|2005–06)
William M. Jennings Trophy (2005-06 NHL season|2005–06)
Molson Cup (2005–06, 2006–07, 2008–09)
