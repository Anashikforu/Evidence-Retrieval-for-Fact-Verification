'''''Garage Inc.''''' is a cover version|cover album by United States|American heavy metal music|heavy metal band Metallica, released in 1998 in music|1998.

It includes recorded cover versions of other artists' songs, all of their b-side covers released up to that point, and the entire ''The $5.98 E.P.: Garage Days Re-Revisited'', which had gone out of print since its original release in 1987. The title is a combination of Garage Days Re-Revisited and their song Damage, Inc.  Over 5 million copies have been sold in the US as certified by the RIAA.

 Reception 
''Professional reviews'':
Rolling Stone (12/10/99, p.&nbsp;122) - 4 Stars (out of 5) -"Gloriously hard as the album is, you can't miss Metallica's good natured side coming through."
Entertainment Weekly (12/18/98, p.&nbsp;84) - "We'll have to wait until Metallica's next `proper' album to find out if this trip to the garage recharges their batteries. Still, all things considered, Garage Inc. is an intermittently exhilarating joyride." - Rating: B-
CMJ (12/21/98, p.&nbsp;29) - "Those who still relate to the adolescent angst of the 'Metallicats' earliest days will find plenty to like on Garage Inc."
