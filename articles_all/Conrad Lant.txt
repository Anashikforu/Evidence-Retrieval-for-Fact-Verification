

'''Conrad "Cronos" Lant''' (born 15 January 1963) is the singer|vocalist and bass guitar|bass player in black metal band Venom (band)|Venom. He was born in 1963 in London, England.

 History 
Cronos started to sing in 1978 in the band ''Guillotine''. Then he knew Jeffrey Dunn|Jeffrey "Mantas" Dunn and they founded Venom (band)|Venom.

In 1989 Cronos left the band to start a solo work. Venom could not go on without him and they stopped singing in 1993. Then Cronos returned in 1995 and rejoined the band.
