
A '''Deluge myth''' or '''Flood myth''' is a mythical story about a flood. Usually this flood is sent by a deity to destroy a civilisation as a punishment. The theme can be found in many cultures. Well-known examples that are believed to be myths include the story of Noah's Ark in the Bible, the Puranas|Hindu Puranic story of Manu (Hinduism)|Manu, Deucalion in Greek mythology or Utnapishtim in the Epic of Gilgamesh.
