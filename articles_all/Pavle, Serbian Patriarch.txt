

'''Patriarch Pavle (Paul)''' ( at spc.rs He was the spiritual leader of Eastern Orthodox Serbs from 1990 to his death. His full title was ''His Holiness the Patriarchate of Peć|Archbishop of Peć, Metropolitan of Belgrade and Sremski Karlovci|Karlovci, Patriarch of Serbia|Serbian Patriarch Pavle''. He was the oldest living leader of an Eastern Orthodox church. His health was bad. For this reason, he had to spend his last years in a Military Medical Academy in Belgrade. During this time, his duties were done by Amfilohije Radović|Metropolitan Amfilohije.
