"'''Electric Avenue'''" is a reggae song released by Guyana|Guyanese singer Eddy Grant on his ''Killer on the Rampage'' album in January 1983.  It is a reggae, synthpop, new wave, and disco song.  Grant wrote and produced the song himself.

It reached #2 on both the United States and UK singles charts.

The song is named after a market street in Brixton, London, England. It has been covered by many artists.
