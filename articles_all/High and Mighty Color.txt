


'''High and Mighty Color''' (ハイ・アンド・マイティ・カラー, ''Hai ando Maiti Karā''Help:Japanese|?) is a Japanese Rock music|rock band signed to Sony Music Entertainment Japan. Their single ''Ichirinno Hana'' was used as an OP song for the anime show, ''Bleach (anime)|Bleach''.

 Members 
 Maki – vocals
 Yuusuke – vocals
 Kazuto - guitar
 Meg (musician)|Meg – guitar
 Mackaz – bass guitar|bass
 Sassy (musician)|Sassy – drums

Awards
 47th Japanese Record Grand Prix Rookie of the Year Award for 2005
