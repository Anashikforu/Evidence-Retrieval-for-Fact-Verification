

'''Parker Brothers''' is a toy and game manufacturer and brand. Over almost 115 years, the company has created more than 1800 games; some of them are ''Monopoly (game)|Monopoly'', ''Cluedo'' (known as ''Clue'' in North America), ''Risk (game)|Risk'', Trivial Pursuit, Ouija, Aggravation (board game)|Aggravation and Probe (parlor game)|Probe.
