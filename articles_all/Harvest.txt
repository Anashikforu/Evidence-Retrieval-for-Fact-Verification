'''Harvest''' means to :wikt:collect|collect what has been planted and wikt:grow|grown in the ground. It is usually done by farmers, and in the autumn|fall season. This is also called picking crops. Crop failure (also known as harvest failure) is a loss or reduced crop yield due to plants being damaged, killed, or destroyed. Sometimes it brings famine.

"Harvest" can also mean to collect other things than plants such as aquaculture|cultivated bivalves including oysters, scallops, and other animals.
