

'''L''' is the twelfth (number 12) letter in the English language|English alphabet.

 Meanings for L 
 For clothes|clothing, L sometimes means "large size".
 In Roman numerals, L means the number fifty.
 The city of Chicago, Illinois|Chicago, Illinois has an elevated train called the "Chicago 'L'".
 A character from the manga and anime Death Note.
 In Wheel of Fortune (US game show)|Wheel of Fortune, L is one of the six letters given to any contestant participating in the "Bonus Round."
 L is the symbol for litre, a unit of volume.
 L can sometimes mean ‘lose’ as in “He took a big L.”
