'''The Fylde''' is a coastal plain in western Lancashire, England.  It is 13-mile (20-kilometre) Square (geometry)|square-shaped peninsula. It is bounded by Morecambe Bay to the north, the River Ribble|Ribble estuary to the south, the Irish Sea to the west, and the Forest of Bowland|Bowland hills to the east. The eastern boundary is near the M6 motorway.
