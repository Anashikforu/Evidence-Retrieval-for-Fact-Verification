
'''Frank White''' (born September 4, 1950 in Greenville, Mississippi|Greenville, Mississippi) is a retired Americans|American baseball player and is a former coach of the Kansas City Royals. He was a second baseman. He now is part of the television broadcast team for the Royals.

White won the World Series with the Kansas City Royals in 1985.

His number (#20) is retired by the Royals. He is one of only two Royals players to have his number retired by the team (the other is George Brett).
