
'''Dayton''' is a large city in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Ohio. It is where Wright brothers|Orville and Wilbur Wright grew up. The Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, also near Dayton, gets part of its name from the Wright brothers. The National Museum of the United States Air Force is inside the Wright-Patterson Air Force Base.

Dayton has a humid continental climate (''Dfa'' in the Koeppen climate classification).

Mass murder|Ten people 2019 Dayton shooting|were killed in a mass shooting in Dayton on 4 August 2019.
