

The '''Lepidosauria''' (Greek for ''scaled lizards'') are reptiles with overlapping Scale (zoology)|scales. They include the tuataras, lizards, snakes and amphisbaenians.

Lepidosauria is a superorder of Sauropsida. It includes the orders : 
  Squamata
  Sphenodontia / Rhynchocephalia
