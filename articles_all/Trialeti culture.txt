


The '''Trialeti''' culture was seen around the 2nd millennium B.C.Munchaev 1994, p. 16; cf., Kushnareva and Chubinishvili 1963, pp. 16 ff. In the late 3rd millennium B.C. settlements of the Kura-Araxes culture began to be replaced by early Trialeti culture sites.The Making of Bronze Age Eurasia - Page 266 by Philip L. Kohl The Trialeti culture was the second culture to appear in the Caucasus, after the Kura-Araxes culture.
