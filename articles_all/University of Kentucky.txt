The '''University of Kentucky''' sometimes shortened to '''UK''' is a public, co-educational university in Lexington, Kentucky|Lexington, Kentucky. The university has about 27,209 students. It was founded in 1865. The current president of the university is Dr. Eli Capilouto. 

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Wildcats. They have 19 varsity teams, almost all of which compete in the Southeastern Conference. The men's association football|soccer team plays in Conference USA, and the shooting sports|rifle team competes in the Great America Rifle Conference.

In the 2014-15 Kentucky Wildcats men's basketball|2014-15 season of the men's basketball team, the team won all 31 regular season games and started 38-0. However, they were upset by Wisconsin in the final four, who they played also the year before. They would finish 38-1.
