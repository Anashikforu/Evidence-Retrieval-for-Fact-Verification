
'''Hồ Ngọc Hà''' (born 25 November, 1984)
 Biography 
Hồ Ngọc Hà was born in Đồng Hới city, Quảng Bình province to a banking family. Her grandfather was of French people|French descent. She studied piano at Military College of Art (Vietnam)|Military College of Arts . She is now living in Ho Chi Minh City.
