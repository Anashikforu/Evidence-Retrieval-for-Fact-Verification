


The '''+44''', or '''Plus 44''', was a punk rock group that formed in 2004. It was started by former Blink-182 members Mark Hoppus and Travis Barker, with guitarist Craig Fairbaugh from the band Transplants, and Shane Gallagher from the Nervous Return. The name of the band is the title of the telephone code for the United Kingdom|UK, +44. The band broke up on January 13, 2009, when Blink-182 reformed.

 Members 
 Mark Hoppus (vocals, bass)
 Travis Barker (drums, keyboards)
 Craig Fairbaugh (rhythm guitar, vocals)
 Shane Gallagher (lead guitar)
