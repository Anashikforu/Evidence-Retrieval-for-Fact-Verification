'''Hartlepool Utd F.C''' are an English association football|football team playing in the The Football League|English Football League. They were founded in 1908 as ‘Hartlepools Utd’ and currently play at the Victoria ground. The well known football manager Brian Clough started his career as a manager with Hartlepool in 1965 before leaving to go on and manage Derby County F.C.|Derby County.
