

'''Peanut butter''' is a type of moist paste that is made of crushed roasted peanuts. There are two kinds of peanut butter: ''crunchy/chunky'' peanut butter and ''smooth/creamy'' peanut butter. It can be eaten, for example on bread, usually with jam|jelly to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich or a peanut butter sandwich with only peanut butter. Marcellus Gilmore Edson was the real inventor, not George Washington Carver.

January 24 is National Peanut Butter Day.
 Health 
Peanut butter can protect people from cardiovascular system|cardiovascular sickness. Peanut butter (and peanuts) have lots of protein, Vitamin E|E, wikt:magnesium|magnesium, folate, food fiber, and arginine. However, some people with peanut allergy|allergies can have a Shock (circulatory)|shock or allergic reaction from eating or smelling it, which has made some schools decide not to let their students eat peanut butter in their schools. Some oils put inside peanut butter to make it easier to spread can also make people have heart disease. Peanut butter can also carry salmonella and make people sick because of it.

Peanut butter has around 188 calories, with there being 71 percent of fat, 14 percent of protein, and 15 percent of carbohydrate.
