

'''''Amadeus''''' is a 1984 United States|American biographical movie|biographical period drama movie movie director|directed by Milos Forman. It won eight Academy Award|Oscars. This is a biography of two great music composers - Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and Antonio Salieri.  Salieri knows Mozart is a great composer but tries to stop him out of envy.

The movie was shot on location in Prague, Kroměříž and Vienna. Notably, Forman was able to shoot scenes in the Estates Theatre|Count Nostitz Theatre in Prague, where ''Don Giovanni'' and ''La Clemenza di Tito'' were first shown two centuries before. Several other scenes were shot at the Barrandov Studios.
