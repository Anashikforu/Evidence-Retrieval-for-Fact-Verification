
The '''Keihan Main Line''' (京阪本線, ''-honsen''Help:Japanese|?) is a railway line of Keihan Electric Railway. The line starts from Sanjo Station in Kyoto, and ends at Yodoyabashi Station in Osaka, and travels a distance of about 51 km. The line is not "the Main Line" but "'''the Keihan Main Line'''". Trains go direct to the Keihan Oto Line|Oto Line.

Stations
 ●: stop, ↓: pass
 ▲: K-Limited Express trains bound for Yodoyabashi stop at Hirakata|Hirakata-shi in the morning. Express trains pass through Hirakata-koen and Moriguchi|Moriguchi-shi from the first train until 8:00 a.m. (JST) and at midnight, and Express trains for Demachiyanagi stop at Yodo. Some express trains stop at Yodo during horse racing events. Sub express trains pass through Moriguchishi from the first train until 8:00 a.m. (JST).
 Local trains (普通) stop at all stations.
