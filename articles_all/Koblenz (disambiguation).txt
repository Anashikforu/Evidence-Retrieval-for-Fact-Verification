The name comes from the Latin  word "confluentiae" (= Eng. "confluence") and means the joining of two rivers. There are several places named '''Koblenz''':

the town Koblenz in the Rhineland at the confluence of the Rhine and Mosel 
the municipality Koblenz, Switzerland in Canton Aargau at the confluence of the Rhine and Aar
the neighbourhood Knappensee-Koblenz, in the municipality Knappensee, district (Landkreis) Kamenz, Bundesland Sachsen
the neighbourhood Koblenz (bei Schmölln, Thüringen) in the municipality Gößnitz (Thüringen), district (Landkreis) Altenburger Land, Bundesland Thüringen
