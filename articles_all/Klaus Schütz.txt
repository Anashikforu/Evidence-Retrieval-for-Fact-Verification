
'''Klaus Schütz''' (17 September 1926 in Heidelberg – 29 November 2012 in Berlin
 Biography 
He was Mayor of West Berlin from 1967 to 1977, and was President of the German Bundesrat|President of the Bundesrat of Germany|Bundesrat in 1967/68. After his resignation he worked as German Ambassador to Israel until 1981 and Director General of the Deutsche Welle broadcaster from 1981 to 1987. He was also president of the German Red Cross.
