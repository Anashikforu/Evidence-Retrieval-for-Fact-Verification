

'''Robert Remus''' (born March 27, 1948, in Parris Island, South Carolina|Parris Island, South Carolina) better known by his ring name, '''Sgt. Slaughter''', is a retired Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler that is best known for wrestling for World Wresting Entertainment|WWF/E under the ring name '''Sgt. Slaughter'''. 

He is a one-time WWE Championship|WWF Champion and was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2004 by Pat Patterson.

His finishing move was the Camel clutch and then the Cobra clutch.
