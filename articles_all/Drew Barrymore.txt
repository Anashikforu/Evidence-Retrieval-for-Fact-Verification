
'''Drew Blyth Barrymore''' (born February 22, 1975) is an Americans|American actor|actress and film producer. Born in Culver City, California, she is the youngest member of the Barrymore family. Lionel Barrymore was her great-uncle and John Barrymore was her grandfather. Barrymore made a breakout role in ''E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial''. She starred with Adam Sandler in ''The Wedding Singer'', ''50 First Dates'' and ''Blended (movie)|Blended''. She starred in the Netflix series ''Santa Clarita Diet''.

 Personal life 
Barrymore is bisexual.
