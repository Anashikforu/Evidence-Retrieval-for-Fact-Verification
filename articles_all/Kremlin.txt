'''Kremlin''' () is the Russian word for "fortress", "citadel" or "castle". It is used to refer to fortification|fortified central complexes in historic Russian city|cities. In Moscow it refers to the central square. The word is also used to mean the government of Russia.

The Necropolis for communism|communists is in one of the walls of the Moscow Kremlin. The mausoleum for Vladimir Lenin is outside.
