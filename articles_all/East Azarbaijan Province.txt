


'''East Azarbaijan''' or '''East Azerbaijan''' () is one of the 30 provinces of Iran|provinces of Iran. It is in the northwest of the country. It has borders with Armenia and the Azerbaijan|Republic of Azerbaijan, and the provinces of Ardabil province|Ardabil, West Azarbaijan, and Zanjan province|Zanjan. Its capital city|capital is Tabriz.
