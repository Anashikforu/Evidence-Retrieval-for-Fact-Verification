

'''Union County''' is a county in northern Florida. Union County is also the smallest county in Florida. The county was founded in 1921 after being cut off from Bradford County. In 2005 almost 15,000 people lived in Union County.
 Other cities 
There are only two other cities in Union County other than Lake Butler. The two other cities are:
 Raiford
 Worthington Springs
