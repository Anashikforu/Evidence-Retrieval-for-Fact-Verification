'''Samson''' was a British Heavy metal music|heavy metal band between 1977 and 2002. It was considered a part of the New Wave of British Heavy Metal. Its first album, ''Survivors'', received good reviews. This made the band more popular. Iron Maiden lead singer Bruce Dickinson was in the band before he joined Iron Maiden.
