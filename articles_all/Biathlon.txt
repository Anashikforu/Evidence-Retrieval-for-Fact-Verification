
'''Biathlon''' (not to be confused with duathlon) is a term used to describe any sporting event made up of two disciplines. However, biathlon usually refers specifically to the winter sport that combines cross-country skiing and rifle|rifle shooting. Another popular variant is summer biathlon, which combines cross-country running with riflery.
