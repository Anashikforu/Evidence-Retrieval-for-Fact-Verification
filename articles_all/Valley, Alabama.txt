
'''Valley''' is a city in Chambers County, Alabama|Chambers County, Alabama, United States. According to 2019 census, there are 9,141 people living there. 

 Geography 
Valley is located in the southeastern Chambers County at 32°48'40.993" North, 85°10'40.577" West (32.811387, -85.177938).

It is located on the Alabama-Georgia state line.

Valley has a larger Micropolitan Statistical Area known as the "Greater Valley Area" consisting of the three largest cities of Chambers County: Valley, :en:Lanett,_Alabama|Lanett, and :en:LaFayette,_Alabama|LaFayette. It also includes the city of :en:West_Point,_Georgia|West Point, Georgia.

 Climate 
It's climate usually has hot, humid summers and generally mild to cool winters.  According to the :en:Köppen_Climate_Classification|Köppen Climate Classification system, Valley has a humid Subtropics|subtropical climate. It is often abbreviated "Cfa" on climate maps.
