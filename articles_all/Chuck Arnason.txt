
'''Ernest Charles "Chuck" Arnason''' (born July 15, 1951 in Ashern, Manitoba) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|right wing who played 401 games over 8 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Montreal Canadiens, Atlanta Flames, Pittsburgh Penguins, Kansas City Scouts, Cleveland Barons (NHL)|Cleveland Barons, Colorado Rockies, Minnesota North Stars and Washington Capitals. He is the father of ice hockey player Tyler Arnason.
