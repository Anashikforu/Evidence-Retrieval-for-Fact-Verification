
'''La Bollène-Vésubie''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur in the Alpes-Maritimes Departments of France|department in the south of France. The inhabitants there are called Bollénois. In 2006, about 561 people lived in this town.
