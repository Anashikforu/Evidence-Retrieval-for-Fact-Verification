

'''''Carrie''''' is a novel by Stephen King. It was published in 1974. It was King's first published novel, although the fourth he had actually written.

In the book, Carrie White is a shyness|shy 17-year-old girl with psychic powers. When she gets angry, Carrie uses these telekinesis|telekinetic powers to get back at all of the classmates who have bullying|bullied her.
