


'''Embroidery''' is the art of decorating Textile|fabric or other materials with designs Embroidery stitch|stitched in strands of yarn|thread or yarn using a needle. Embroidery may also incorporate other materials such as metal strips, pearls, beads, quills, and sequins. Sewing machines can be used to create machine embroidery.
