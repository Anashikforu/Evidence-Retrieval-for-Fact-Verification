'''Bernina ''' is a district in the cantons of Switzerland|canton of Graubünden, Switzerland. It has an area of 237.2 square kilometre|km² and a population of 4,687 people (as of December 2004).

Bernina consists of two ''Kreise'' (sub-districts) Brusio and Poschiavo, each of which contains a single municipality of the same name.
