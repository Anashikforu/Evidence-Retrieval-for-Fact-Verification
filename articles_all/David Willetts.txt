

'''David Linsay Willetts''' (born 9 March 1956) is an English people|English politician. He was born in Birmingham. He is the current Minister of State for Universities and Science in the Department for Business, Innovation and Skills. He is the Member of Parliament for the Havant (UK Parliament constituency)|Havant constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1992|1992 general election.

He is a member of the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.
