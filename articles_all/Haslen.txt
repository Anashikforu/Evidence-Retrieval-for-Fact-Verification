

'''Haslen''' is a former municipality of the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Glarus (canton)|Glarus in Switzerland. On July 1, 2006, Haslen merged with the municipalities of Leuggelbach and Nidfurn. On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Haslen, Betschwanden, Braunwald, Elm, Switzerland|Elm, Engi, Linthal, Luchsingen, Matt, Switzerland, Mitlödi, Rüti, Glarus|Rüti, Schwanden, Glarus|Schwanden, Schwändi and Sool, Switzerland|Sool merged into the new municipality of Glarus Süd.
