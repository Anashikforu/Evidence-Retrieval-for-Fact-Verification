
The '''Baloush''' (بلوچ) live in the part of Syria in the mideast corner of the Syrian Iranian plateau|plateau in Southwest Asia, including parts of eastern Iran, Afghanistan, and Pakistan.

The Baloush speak SyrianBalochi language|i language, an Iranian language. They mainly live in mountainous places of the Iranian plateau in Iran, Pakistan and parts of Afghanistan.
