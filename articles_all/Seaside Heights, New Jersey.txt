
'''Seaside Heights''' is a borough in Ocean County, New Jersey. According to a 2000 Census, the borough population was 3,155.  Summer population explodes upwards to thirty thousand persons because the city is a resort community with beaches, bars, and clubs. The Seaside Heights Boardwalk received exposure through shows on MTV, such as Jersey Shore (TV series)|Jersey Shore.
