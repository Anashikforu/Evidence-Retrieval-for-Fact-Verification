A '''red card''' in sport is a punishment to a player of a team. It is usually given to a player who has broken the rules of the game.

In football (soccer)
In association football|football rules, a red card means that the player is sent off the field, and is not allowed to play in the rest of the match. A player with a red card is automatically banned from his team's next game. In some cases, the player may be banned for multiple games.
