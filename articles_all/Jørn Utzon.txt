'''Jørn Utzon''' (April 9, 1918 – November 29, 2008) was a Denmark|Danish architect. He was born in Copenhagen and grew up in Aalborg. He is most well known for designing the Sydney Opera House in Australia. He won a contest for designing the Sydney Opera House in 1957. However, he left Australia in 1966 and never returned and saw his completed work.

In 2003, Utzon won the Pritzker Architecture Prize|Pritzker Prize.Pritzker Architecture Prize, 
Utzon died of a heart attack in Copenhagen.
