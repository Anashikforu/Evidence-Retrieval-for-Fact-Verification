

'''Tim Russert''' (May 7, 1950 - June 13, 2008) was an Americans|American television journalist and lawyer. He was the longest serving moderator for NBC's ''Meet the Press'' beginning in 1991. He was the senior vice president of NBC News and a frequent correspondent and guest on NBC's ''The Today Show'' and ''Hardball''. He was born in Buffalo, New York. Russert died from a heart attack in Washington, D.C.
