
'''Pamela Denise Anderson''' (born July 1, 1967 in Ladysmith, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadians|Canadian-Americans|American actress, sex symbol, model (person)|glamour model, Television producer|producer, television personality, and author.

Anderson started her career in Labatt Brewing Company|Labatt Beer commercials. She was then offered to pose for Playboy and appeared in five issues. Anderson was the Playboy Playmate|Playmate of the Month in February 1990. Anderson has been a contestant in two series of ''Dancing with the Stars'' and in the 2013 series of ''Dancing on Ice''.

For a time, she was known as '''Pamela Anderson Lee''' during her marriage to the drummer for Mötley Crüe, Tommy Lee. The couple had two sons. She later married Kid Rock and Rick Salomon. She is a dual citizen: Canada|Canadian and United States|American. In 2002, she stated that she has been diagnosed with hepatitis C.

Anderson is a vegan and an animal rights' activist.
