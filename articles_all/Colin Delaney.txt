

'''Colin Matthew Delaney''' (September 7, 1986) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler  best known for working with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) on its Extreme Championship Wrestling (WWE)|ECW WWE Brand Extension|brand.

 Career 
Delaney made his World Wrestling Entertainment debut as a Job (professional wrestling)|jobber, seen wrestling on the Extreme Championship Wrestling (WWE)|ECW show.  On August 15, Delaney was released by WWE. 

 Personal life 
Colin has an older brother Ed who competes as "Hangin' Tough" Freddie Midnight, and younger brother Brian who competed as Mr. Bodacious.
