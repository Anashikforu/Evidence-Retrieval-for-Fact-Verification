
'''Bragging Rights (2009)''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) show made by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), that took place on October 25, 2009 at the Civic Arena (Pittsburgh)|Mellon Arena in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. It was the first WWE Bragging Rights|Bragging Rights event held by the WWE and was created to replace WWE Cyber Sunday|Cyber Sunday in the late October slot of WWE's pay-per-view calendar.
