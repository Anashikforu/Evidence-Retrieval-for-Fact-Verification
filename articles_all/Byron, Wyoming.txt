'''Byron''' is a town in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Wyoming.  It part of Big Horn County, Wyoming|Big Horn County. At the 2010 census, 593 people lived in Byron. 

In high school, students go to Rocky Mountain High School in Byron. Middle School students go to Rocky Mountain Middle School in Deaver and elementary school students attend Rocky Mountain Elementary School in Cowley. 

The town has an area of 0.9 square mile|mi². 0.1&nbsp;mi² of it (5.62%) is water.
