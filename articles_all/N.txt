

'''N''' is the fourteenth (number 14) letter in the English language|English alphabet.

 Meanings for N 
 On calendars, N sometimes means the month November
 In chemistry, N is the symbol for nitrogen
 In chess, N is a symbol for the Knight (chess)|knight piece.
 In physics, n is the symbol for a neutron
 In Wheel of Fortune (US game show)|Wheel of Fortune, N is one of the six letters given to any contestant participating in the "Bonus Round."
