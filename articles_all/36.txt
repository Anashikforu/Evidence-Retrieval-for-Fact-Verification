


'''36''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a leap year starting on Sunday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Allenius and Plautius'''.

 Events 
 Pontius Pilate is called back to Rome.  He had stopped a Samaritan uprising.
 L. Vitellius defeated Artabanus of Parthia in support of another clamaint to the throne, Tiridates III.
 Herod Antipas is remarried.  
 Marcellus becomes governor of Judaea and Samaria.
 In MesoAmerica, last Mesoamerican Long Count calendar|calendar monument created for about a century and a half.
