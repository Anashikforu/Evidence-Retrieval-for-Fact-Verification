
'''Backlash (2004)''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view List of WWE pay-per-view events|event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It was held on April 18, 2004 at the Rexall Place in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. It was the sixth annual Backlash PPV. It was a ''WWE Raw|Raw''-only event and was the first Backlash event that was held outside of the United States.
