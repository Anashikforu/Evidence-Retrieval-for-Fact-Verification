
 
The '''Toyota Corolla''' is a Sedan (automobile)|sedan automobile made by Toyota since 1966. 

 Sales 
It was first sold in United States|America in 1968. The Corolla is made in many countries all around the world including the United States. The Corolla once had a twin called the Sprinter and it was sold by Chevrolet in the ''United States'' as the ''Nova'' and Geo Prizm in the 1980s and 1990s. The Corolla is Toyota's best selling car besides the Toyota Hi-Lux|Hi-Lux pickup truck and the bigger Toyota Camry.
