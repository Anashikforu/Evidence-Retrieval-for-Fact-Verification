'''Lead poisoning''' is a condition where there is too much lead in the body. Lead interferes with many processes and is toxic to many organs and tissues. Treatment of the condition is usually focused on removing the source of lead. Some animals also die from lead poisoning. Mostly, children are at risk for this condition. Symptoms of Lead Poisoning include coughing,fatigue, and severe dehydration. If you believe your child has Lead Poisoning take them to a doctor immediately.
