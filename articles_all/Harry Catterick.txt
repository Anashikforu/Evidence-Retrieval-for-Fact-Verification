'''Harry Catterick''' was an England|English soccer|football player and manager. He was most successful when he managed Everton FC during the 1970s.

 Honours with Everton 

Old First Division Winners 1962–63 
Charity Shield Winners 1963 
FA Cup Winners 1966 
Old First Division Winners 1969–70 
Charity Shield Winners 1970
