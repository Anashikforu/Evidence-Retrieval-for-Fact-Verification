
'''No Way Out''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view event made by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It took place on February 23, 2003 at the Bell Centre in Montreal, Quebec. It was the 4th No Way Out event. Eight matches were scheduled on the event's Card (sports)|card.
