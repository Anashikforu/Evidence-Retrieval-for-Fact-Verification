

'''Wanda Sykes''' (born March 7, 1964) is an Americans|American Writer, Comedienne and Actress. She was born in Portsmouth, Virginia and raised in Washington, D.C. She starred in the sitcom ''Curb Your Enthusiasm'' on the HBO network. In November 2009 her late night talk show ''The Wanda Sykes Show'' premiered.

Personal life
Sykes is lesbian.
