
'''Dominici''' is a progressive metal band that started in 2005 by former Dream Theater vocalist, Charlie Dominici. Their only published works are the three albums that make up the O3: A Trilogy|O3 Trilogy.

Dominici also opened for three Dream Theater concerts, during Dream Theater's "Systematic Chaos#Tour|Chaos in Motion Tour."
