
A '''rockumentary''' is a documentary about rock music or its musicians. The word '''rockumentary''' is a combination (a portmanteau word) of the words "rock" and "documentary." Producer Bill Drake called the 1969 History of Rock & Roll radio broadcast a rockumentary.{{Citation
