
'''"Life on the Fast Lane"''', also known as "'''Jacques To Be Wild'''", is the ninth episode of the The Simpsons (season 1)|first season of ''The Simpsons'', which was first shown on March 18, 1990. It was written by John Swartzwelder and directed by David Silverman. Albert Brooks guest starred as Jacques, a French bowling teacher, with him being wikt:credit|credited as "A. Brooks". In this episode, Marge deals with becoming wikt:adminration|infatuated with Jacques. It won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Animated Program (for Programming Less Than One Hour) in 1990.
