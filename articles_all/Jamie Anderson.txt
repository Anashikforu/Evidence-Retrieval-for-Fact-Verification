'''Jamie Anderson''' is an Americans|American singer from Tucson, Arizona. She is based in Ottawa, Ontario.
As of 2010 she had released ten full-length albums. She is coming out|openly lesbian.
 Career 
Anderson first began touring the U.S. in 1987, and released her first album in 1989. She was voted Favorite New Performer by ''Hot Wire (magazine)|Hot Wire'' in 1990 and 1991, and played many women's music festivals, coffeehouses, colleges and other venues all though the 1990s and to the present.
