
'''''Castlevania''''', known in Japan as , is an Action game|action platform game|platforming video game made by Konami for the Nintendo Entertainment System. It's the first game in the ''Castlevania'' series. It stars Simon Belmont, a vampire hunter, who has to battle against Dracula (Castlevania)|Dracula and his minions. It's had many sequels and is still a popular series.
