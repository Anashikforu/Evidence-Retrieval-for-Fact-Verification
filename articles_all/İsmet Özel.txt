'''İsmet Özel''' (born 19 September 1944, in Kayseri) is a Turkey|Turkish poet. He was born in Kayseri, Turkey.

He started his university Academic degree|degree at Political Science Faculty of Ankara University. He Graduation|graduated from the French language department at Hacettepe University.

In 1970, he published a literature magazine ''Halkın Dostları'' with Ataol Behremoğlu, a Turkish poet and Russian language|Russian linguist. In fact, Ataol Behramoğlu purified his poetry by leaving out crude propaganda and naive didacticism.
