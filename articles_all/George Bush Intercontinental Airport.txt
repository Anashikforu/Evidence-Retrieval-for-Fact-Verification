

'''George Bush Intercontinental Airport''' is an airport|international airport in Houston, Texas. It opened in June 1969.

The airport has five runway|runways. At least 700 planes takeoff from the airport daily. These planes carried 40,007,345 passengers during the year of 2009. Planes fly from this airport to around 170 cities in the world.
