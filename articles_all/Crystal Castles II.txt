'''''Crystal Castles''''', also known as '''''Crystal Castles II''''', is the second album by electronic music group Crystal Castles. The album was released by Fiction Records on 24 May 2010.
Critical reception
''Crystal Castles'' has been met with mostly good reviews from people.

Track listing


Notes
 Ethan Kath sings "Not in Love", which is a cover of a song by 1980s new wave band Platinum Blonde (band)|Platinum Blonde.
 "Year of Silence" samples the vocal track from the Sigur Ros song called "Inní mér syngur vitleysingur".
 Both "Violent Dreams" and "Vietnam" contain parts of the song "A Walk In The Park" by Stina Nordenstam.
 The CD sleeve notes state that the album cover is a photo of X Tecumseh Clark taken by his father, avant-garde composer and poet Todd Tamanend Clark.
