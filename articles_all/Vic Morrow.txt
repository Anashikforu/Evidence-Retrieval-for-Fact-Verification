'''Victor Morrow''' (February 14, 1929 - July 23, 1982) was an Americans|American actor. He was famous for acting in the television series ''Combat!'' (1962 in television|1962-1967 in television|1967), in which he also worked as a television director.

Morrow was born in the Bronx, New York City. He was the father of actress Jennifer Jason Leigh. 

Morrow died while filming ''Twilight Zone: The Movie'' at an accident in Ventura County, California with a helicopter that crashed and killed him and two children. The accident made the United States Government|government make many reforms in United States|U.S. child labor laws and safety regulations on movie sets in California.
