


'''''Mario & Luigi: Partners in Time''''' is a game for the Nintendo DS. ''Mario & Luigi: Partners in Time'' was released in North America on November 28, 2005, in Japan on December 29, 2005, in Europe on January 11, 2006, in the United Kingdom on February 10, 2006, and in Australia on February 23, 2006. It is the second game in the ''Mario and Luigi RPG'' series, preceding ''Mario & Luigi: Superstar Saga''.

In this game, Princess Peach|Peach goes with two other Toad (Nintendo)|Toads in a time machine, but she never returns. Whenever the machine returns, a green alien comes out of it. After they beat it, Mario and Luigi go to various places in the past.
