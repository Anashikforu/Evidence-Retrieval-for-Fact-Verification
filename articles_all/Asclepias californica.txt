


'''''Asclepias californica''''' (commonly known as the '''California milkweed''') is a species of milkweed plant.  It is also known as '''Round-hooded milkweed'''. It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to California and Baja California Peninsula|Baja California.  It blooms from April to July.  This species is found on grassy or bushy hillsides.
