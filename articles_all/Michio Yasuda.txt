Biography
Yasuda was born on November 10, 1949. After graduating from Fukuoka University, he joined Japan Soccer League club Nippon Steel Yawata SC|Nippon Steel in 1972. He retired in 1980. He played 155 games in the league.

On August 23, 1979, Yasuda debuted for Japan national football team|Japan national team against North Korea national football team|North Korea.
