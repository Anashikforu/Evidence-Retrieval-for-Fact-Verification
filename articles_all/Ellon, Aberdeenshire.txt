
'''Ellon''' is a town in Aberdeenshire, Scotland, approximately 16 miles north of Aberdeen, lying on the River Ythan (pronounced ''eye-than'') which has one of the few undeveloped river Ythan Estuary|estuaries on the eastern coast of Scotland. It is in the ancient region of Formartine.

Famous people from Ellon
 Paul Sturrock, footballer
 Teddy Scott, footballer, coach and kit man for Aberdeen F.C.|Aberdeen
 Evelyn Glennie, percussionist
