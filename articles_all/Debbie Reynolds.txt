'''Mary Frances Reynolds''', better known as '''Debbie Reynolds''' (April 1, 1932 &ndash; December 28, 2016), was an Americans|American actress and singer. Reynolds was born in El Paso, Texas. She starred in many television programs and movies. She also had many songs and albums during her career. 

On December 28, 2016, Reynolds died at the Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, California from a cerebral hemorrhage with hypertension being a factor, aged 84. This was one day after her daughter Carrie Fisher died.
