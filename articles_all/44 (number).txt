

'''Forty-four''' is a number. It comes between forty-three and forty-five, and is an even number. It is divisible by 1 (number)|1, Two|2, Four|4, Eleven|11, Twenty-two|22 and itself, Forty-four|44.

Science

=Chemistry=

 Forty-four is the atomic number of ruthenium. 

=Astronomy=

 The saros number of the solar eclipse series which began on April 30, 1448 BC and ended on June 7, 168 BC.
