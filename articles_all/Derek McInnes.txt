

'''Derek John McInnes''' (born 5 July 1971 in Paisley, Renfrewshire|Paisley, Renfrewshire) is a former professional association football|footballer and current manager of Bristol City F.C.|Bristol City.

McInnes featured prominently for Greenock Morton F.C.|Greenock Morton, Rangers F.C.|Rangers, West Bromwich Albion F.C.|West Bromwich Albion and Dundee United F.C.|Dundee United during his playing career.

McInnes became manager of St. Johnstone F.C.|St. Johnstone in November 2007. He guided the club to promotion to the Scottish Premier League in 2009 and kept that status for two seasons. McInnes was made manager of Bristol City on 19 October 2011.
