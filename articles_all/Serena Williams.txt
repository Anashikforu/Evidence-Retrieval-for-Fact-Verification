
'''Serena Jameka Williams''' (born September 26, 1981) is an Americans|American professional tennis player. She is the younger sister of another former world no. 1 professional female tennis player, Venus Williams.

Personal life
The Williams sisters grew up in Los Angeles. She lives in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida|Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, United States with her husband Alexis Ohanian and daughter. She is a Jehovah's witness.
