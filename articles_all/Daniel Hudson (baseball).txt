


'''Daniel Claiborne Hudson''' (born March 9, 1987 in Lynchburg, Virginia|Lynchburg, Virginia) is an Americans|American baseball player. He currently plays as a pitcher (baseball)|pitcher for the Pittsburgh Pirates of Major League Baseball (MLB).

Hudson formerly played for the Chicago White Sox from 2009-2010 and was traded to the Diamondbacks on July 30, 2010 along with minor league pitcher David Holmberg for Edwin Jackson.
