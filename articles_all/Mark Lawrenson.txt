'''Mark Thomas Lawrenson''' (born 2 June 1957, in Preston, Lancashire) is a former professional Football (soccer)|football player, a defender in the Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool and  Football (soccer)|Irish football teams of the 1980s; he has since become a radio, television and internet pundit for the BBC. 

Despite being born in England, UK, he played for the Republic of Ireland national football team due to his Ireland|Irish ancestry.
