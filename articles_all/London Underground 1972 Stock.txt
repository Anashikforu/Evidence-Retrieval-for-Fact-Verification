

The London Underground '''1972 stock''' is a fleet of trains used on the London Underground. The 1972 Stock was ordered to make up the shortfall in trains on the Northern line's London Underground 1959 Stock|1959 Tube Stock fleet. It is, however, currently used on the Bakerloo line.
