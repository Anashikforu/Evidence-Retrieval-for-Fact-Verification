

'''''18 til I Die''''' is a Rock music|rock album by the Canada|Canadian singer/songwriter|singer and songwriter Bryan Adams. It was released in 1996 (see 1996 in music). It is the second album Adams co-produced with Mutt Lange|Robert Mutt Lange and features a more raw rock style, including some Blues style music.
