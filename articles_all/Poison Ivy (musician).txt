
'''Kristy Marlana Wallace''' (born February 20, 1953) is an Americans|American guitarist, songwriter, Arrangement (music)|arranger, Record producer|producer, and sometimes a Singer|vocalist. Wallace is better known by the stage names of '''Poison Ivy''' and sometimes '''Poison Ivy Rorschach'''. She was a co-founder of the American garage punk band The Cramps.

She was married to Lux Interior for 37 years until he died on February 4, 2009.
