'''Puurs''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Antwerp (province)|Antwerp. It includes the towns of Liezele, Ruisbroek, Breendonk and Puurs proper. There is also the Hamlet (place)|hamlet of Kalfort. On 1 January 2019, Puurs and Sint-Amands were merged into the new municipality of Puurs-Sint-Amands.

In 2007, 16250 people lived there.

It is at 51° 04 North, 04° 16 East.
