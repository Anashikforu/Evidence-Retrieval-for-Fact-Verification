


The '''Hyundai Accent''' is a car produced by Hyundai Motor Company since 1994. It is the successor of the Hyundai Pony|Pony, and it is now in its Third generation. The car was sold in Mexico as the Dodge Attitude until 2013 when Hyundai entered the Mexican market.
