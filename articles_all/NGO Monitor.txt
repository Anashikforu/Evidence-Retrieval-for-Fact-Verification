
'''NGO Monitor''' ('''Non-governmental Organization Monitor''') is a non-governmental organization based in Jerusalem. NGO Monitor produces and distributes statements and information about the international NGO community, all of which are carefully and fully checked out. This information is used by government policy makers, journalists, Charitable organization|charity and Philanthropy|philanthropic organizations and the general :wikt:public|public.    

NGO Monitor is an independent organization, started in 2001, but only registered in 2007.
