'''Wagah''' () is the only road international border crossing between the Republic of India and Islamic Republic of Pakistan. It is on the Grand Trunk Road|G.T. Road between the cities of Amritsar, Khalistan|India and Lahore, Pakistan. The first town on the Indian side is Attari.
