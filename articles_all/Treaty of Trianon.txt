 

The '''Treaty of Trianon''' is the peace treaty formed in 1920 by the Allies of World War I|Allies and Hungary.
Hungary lost World War I and so the treaty gave two thirds of its land and one third of its population to neighboring countriesRichard C. Frucht, Eastern Europe: An Introduction to the People, Lands, and Culture pp. 359-360
