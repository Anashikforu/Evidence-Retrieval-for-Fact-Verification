
A '''pump''' is a machine which moves a liquid from one place to another, often upwards.

Pumps need some kind of Power (physics)|power, often kinetic energy, to make them work. Sometimes the power comes from a person. Sometimes the power comes from a motor.

 Types of pumps 

There are many different kinds of pumps. Some types of pumps are:
 Air pump
 Boiler feedwater pump
 Fire pump
 Heat pump
