

'''''Doraha''''' is a 1967 Pakistani Urdu language|Urdu movie. It is a Black and white|black & white movie. ''Doraha'' is a classic Romance movie|romantic and melody|melodious movie of 1960s. It is one of the best movies of Pervaiz Malik's and Waheed Murad's careers. At the box office, the movie did not earn much money. It gained more importance after several years. The movie became much more well liked during 1980s and 1990s. ''Doraha'' was Producer|produced by Sohail Rana and Pervaiz Malik. Malik also movie director|directed the movie. The movie stars Murad, Shamim Ara, Deeba, Ibrahim Nafees and Talish. For ''Doraha'', Waheed Murad first cast member|casted Zeba, but after her marriage with Mohammad Ali, he picked Shamim Ara. The people that knew Murad knew that Shamim Ara was just a replacement. All the dialogue and songs were for Zeba
