
'''Shota Rustaveli''' was a Georgia (country)|Georgian poet of the 12th century, and the greatest classic of Georgian secular literature. He is the author of ''The Knight in the Panther's Skin'' ("Vepkhistqaosani" in Georgian), the Georgian national epic poetry|epic poem. Little is known about life of the poet.
 His work 

"The Knight in the Panther's Skin" (the actual name is Vepkhistkaosani, which means The wearer of '''Tiger's''' Skin) has been translated into many languages. It was first printed in 1712, in Tbilisi (capital of Georgia (country)|Georgia). It consists of 1576 four line stanzas with monorhyme.
