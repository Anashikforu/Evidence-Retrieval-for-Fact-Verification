
The '''Latin Wikipedia''' (In Latin language|Latin: '''Vicipaedia''' or ''Vicipaedia Latina'') is the Latin language edition of Wikipedia. Started in May 2002, this edition had over 80,000 articles as of October 2012.:la:Specialis:Census |Specialis:Census from the Latin Wikipedia

As of October 2015, it is the 47th largest edition of Wikipedia by number of articles.meta:List of Wikipedias|Wikimedia list of Wikipedias and their statistics.. Retrieved 25 October 2015.

''Vicipaedia'' is the largest Wikipedia in a dead language, (a language that is no longer anyone's mother tongue).
