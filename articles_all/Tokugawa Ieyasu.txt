Early life
At birth, his name was Matsudaira Takechiyo.  

In 1562, he changed his name to Matsudaira Motoyasu.  The second syllable -- ''-yasu'' -- comes from the name of his grandfather Kiyoyasu.Sadler, Arthur Lindsay. (2010). 
Shogun
Ieyasu was the first shogun of the Tokugawa shogunate.
