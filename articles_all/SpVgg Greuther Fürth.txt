
The '''Spielvereinigung Greuther Fürth''' is a Germany|German Association football|football club which plays in the country's top league, the Fußball-Bundesliga|Bundesliga. The club is playing in the Bundesliga for the first time after being promoted from the German 2. Bundesliga|Second Bundesliga as winners of that league in 2011–12.
