'''Donald McAlpine''' is an Australian Cinematography|cinematographer. He was born in 1934 in New South Wales, Australia. He has been involved in over fifty movies. His more recent work has been seen in Baz Luhrmann's hit musical ''Moulin Rouge!'' (2001), for which he earned an ''Academy Award'' wikt:nominate|nomination, Adam Sandler's ''Anger Management'' (2003), Universal Pictures' ''Peter Pan (2003 movie)|Peter Pan'' (2003) and the Walt Disney Pictures|Walt Disney/Walden Media-produced ''The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe'' (2005).
