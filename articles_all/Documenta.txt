
'''''documenta''''' is one of the most important exhibitions of modern art in the earth|world. Since 1955, it takes place every five years in Kassel, Germany. More than 1.2 million people visited the last one, documenta&nbsp;14, which was held in 2017. The next one, documenta&nbsp;15, will be from June 18 to September 25, 2022.
