
'''Francisco''' "'''Frankie'''" '''Muniz IV''' (born December 5, 1985) is an Americans|American actor. He was born in Ridgewood, New Jersey and grew up in Wood-Ridge, New Jersey, Raleigh, North Carolina and Burbank, California|Burbank, Los Angeles. He is best known for his role as Malcolm in the television Situation comedy|sitcom ''Malcolm in the Middle''.
