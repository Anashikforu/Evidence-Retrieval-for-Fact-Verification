

The '''Slovak Wikipedia''' (In Slovak language|Slovak: ''Slovenská Wikipédia'') is the Slovak language|Slovak-language edition of Wikipedia. This edition began in October 2003.

As of October 2015, it is the 37th largest edition of Wikipedia by number of articles.meta:List of Wikipedias|Wikimedia list of Wikipedias and their statistics.. Retrieved 25 October 2015.

It has over 120,000 articles.:sk:Špeciálne:Statistics|Statistics at Slovak Wikipedia
