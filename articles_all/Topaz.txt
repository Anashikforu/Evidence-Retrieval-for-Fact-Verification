

'''Topaz''' is a silicate mineral. Along with silicate, it includes the chemical elements aluminium and fluorine with the chemical formula aluminium|Al2silicon|Sioxygen|O4(fluorine|F,hydroxide|OH)2.

Color and varieties
Pure topaz is colorless and transparent but they usually have colors made by small impurities. The wikt:typical|typical topaz is wine, yellow, pale gray, reddish-orange, or blue brown. It can also be made white, pale green, blue, gold, or pink (rare).

''Orange topaz'', also known as wikt:precious|precious topaz, is the traditional November birthstone, the symbol of friendship, and the state gemstone for the US state of Utah.
''Imperial topaz'' is yellow, pink (rare, if natural) or pink-orange. Some imperial topaz stones can fade if they are wikt:expose|exposed too much in the sunlight.
''Blue topaz'' is the US state Texas' gemstone.
''Mystic topaz'' is colorless topaz which has been artificially coated, making it rainbow-colored.
