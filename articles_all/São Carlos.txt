'''São Carlos''' is a Brazil|Brazilian city in the state of São Paulo (state)|São Paulo. In 2017, it had 246,088 people living in it. It is about 230&nbsp;km (143 miles) away from the city of São Paulo and has an area of 1,137 km² (441 mi²).
