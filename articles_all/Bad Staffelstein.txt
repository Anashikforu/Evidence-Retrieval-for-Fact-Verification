

'''Bad Staffelstein''' is a small town in the Bavarian Regierungsbezirk|Administrative Region of Upper Franconia in Germany. It has about 10,000 residents.

Geography
=Location=
The municipal territory of Bad Staffelstein extends on both sides of the Main (river)|Main river. It is northeast of Bamberg in Lichtenfels (district)|Lichtenfels.
