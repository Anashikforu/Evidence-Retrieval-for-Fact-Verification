
'''''Nerf N-Strike''''' is an video game that was made by Electronic Arts.
History
In August 2007, Electronic Arts gained the rights to some Hasbro brands, like ''Monopoly (game)|Monopoly'', ''Littlest Pet Shop'', and Nerf.  In February, EA Casual Entertainment announced that their EA Salt Lake Studio would develop and release video games for ''Littlest Pet Shop (video game)|Littlest Pet Shop'' and ''Nerf N-Strike''.Sinclair, Brandon.
