
'''Csaba László''' (born 13 February, 1964 in Odorheiu Secuiesc, Harghita County, Romania) is a Romanian-Hungarian football (soccer)|football Head coach|manager and former player. In 2012 he was appointed as a head coach of Lithuanian national football team. He was previously manager of Scottish Premier League club Heart of Midlothian F.C.|Hearts and also had spells at Ferencvárosi TC, FC Sopron and the  Uganda national football team|Ugandan national team.
