
'''Management''' means directing and controlling a group of people or an organization to reach a goal. Management often means the deployment and :wikt:manipulation|manipulation of human resources, Finance|financial resources, Technology|technological resources, and natural resources. In other word '''Management''' can also mean the person or people who manage, the managers.  European Management is specifically defined by academic Andreas M. Kaplan as a cross-cultural, societal management approach based on interdisciplinary principles.
