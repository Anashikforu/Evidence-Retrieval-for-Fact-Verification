
'''David Silverman''' (born 15 March 1957 in New York City, New York) is an Animation|animator and screenwriter|writer. He is best known for movie director|directing many episodes of the animation|animated television programme|television series ''The Simpsons'', where he would go on to be the supervising director of animation for several years, as well as animating on all of the original Simpsons ''The Simpsons shorts|Tracey Ullman shorts''.
