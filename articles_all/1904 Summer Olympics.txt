


The '''1904 Summer Olympics''', officially known as the '''Games of the III Olympiad''', were held in St. Louis, Missouri, United States.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics), 
The games were part of the Louisiana Purchase Exposition (St. Louis World's Fair). The sports events were not well planned; and the games were not thought to be a success.Zarnowski, C. Frank. 
 Participating nations 
Athletes from 12 nations took part in the St. Louis games.
