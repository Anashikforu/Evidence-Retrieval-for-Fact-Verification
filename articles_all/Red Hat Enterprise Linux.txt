'''Red Hat Enterprise Linux''' ('''RHEL''') is a Linux distribution developed by Red Hat for the commerce|commercial market. Red Hat Enterprise Linux is released in server versions for x86-64, Power ISA, ARM64, and IBM Z and a desktop version for x86-64. All of Red Hat's official support and training, together with the Red Hat Certification Program, focuses on the Red Hat Enterprise Linux platform.
