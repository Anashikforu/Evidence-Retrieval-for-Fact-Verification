
'''Yury Mikhailovitsch Luzhkov''' () (September 21, 1936 &ndash; December 10, 2019) was a Russian politician. He was a member of the United Russia. He became the Mayor of Moscow in 1992, when Gavriil Kharitonovich Popov quit the job since he could not handle the problems that came with it. He resigned in 2010. Luzhkov was born in Moscow. 

Luzhkov died at a Munich hospital from heart surgery-related problems on December 10, 2019 at the age of 83.
