
The '''Florida Panhandle''' is the region of the state of Florida which includes the westernmost 10 to 18 county|counties in the state. It is a narrow strip lying between Alabama and Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia to the north and the Gulf of Mexico to the south. While some definitions only include the counties west of the :en:Apalachicola River|Apalachicola River, others include the eight counties east of the Apalachicola that are part of the Big Bend (Florida)|Big Bend region. Culturally and in terms of history and climate, the region is more closely tied to the :en:Deep South|Deep South than to peninsula|peninsular Florida.''Florida'', DK Eyewitness Travel Guides, 2004, pg. 20
