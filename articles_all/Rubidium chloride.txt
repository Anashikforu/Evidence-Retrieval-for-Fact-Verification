'''Rubidium chloride''' is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is RbCl. It is composed of rubidium and chloride ions. It is similar to other alkali metal halides, such as sodium chloride. It is made by chemical reaction|reacting rubidium hydroxide with hydrochloric acid. It has several medicinal uses, including fighting cancer and Depression (mental illness)|depression, medical research, and medicine.
