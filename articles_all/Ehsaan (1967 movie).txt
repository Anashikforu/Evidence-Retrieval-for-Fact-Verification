


'''''Ehsaan''''' is a Pakistani Urdu black and white movie. It was a presentation by Waheed & Malik combination. The movie is a love story, starring Waheed Murad, Zeba, Nirala, Rozina, Azad Nafees|Azad and Ibrahim Nafees. The movie was produced by Waheed Murad and directed by Pervaiz Malik.
