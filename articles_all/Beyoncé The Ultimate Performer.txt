

'''''Beyoncé The Ultimate Performer''''' is a DVD by Contemporary R&B|R&B artist Beyoncé Knowles. It was released in the US on November 24, 2006. It features some of her exclusive footages in France, Japan, United Kingdom, New York (state)|New York among other places during her worldwide tour, the makings and music video.
