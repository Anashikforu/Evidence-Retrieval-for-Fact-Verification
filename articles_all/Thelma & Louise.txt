
'''''Thelma & Louise''''' is a 1991 United States|American adventure movie|adventure crime movie|crime road movie starring Geena Davis as Thelma and Susan Sarandon as Louise. Also starring in the movie are Michael Madsen, Harvey Keitel and Brad Pitt. It was among the highest-grossing movies of 1991.
