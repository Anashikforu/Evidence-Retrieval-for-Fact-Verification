The '''Santa Susana Mountains''' are a mountain range in southern California, in the United States. They are north of the city of Los Angeles and the Los Angeles River; and south of the Santa Clara River. The west part of the mountains is in Ventura County, California|Ventura County while the east part is in Los Angeles County.
