'''Steven Vincent Buscemi''' (born December 13, 1957) is an Americans|American actor, movie director|director, movie producer|producer, writer and former firefighter. He is best known for his roles as Mr. Pink in ''Reservoir Dogs'', Carl Showalter in ''Fargo (movie)|Fargo'', and Seymour in ''Ghost World''. With a long career, he has worked in several movies and television shows like ''The Sopranos'' and ''The Simpsons''. He was married to Jo Andres from 1987 until her death in 2019. They have a son, Lucian Buscemi.
