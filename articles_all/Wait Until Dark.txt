
'''''Wait Until Dark''''' is a suspense-Thriller (genre)|thriller movie from 1967. Audrey Hepburn plays a blind woman who is terrorized by three criminals looking for a doll stuffed with heroin. The movie also stars Alan Arkin and Richard Crenna.
