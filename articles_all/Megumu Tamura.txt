Biography
Tamura was born on January 10, 1927. He played for Nippon Oil & Fats SC|Nippon Oil & Fats.

In March 1951, Tamura was selected Japan national football team|Japan national team for Japan team first game after World War II, Football at the 1951 Asian Games|1951 Asian Games. At this competition, on March 7, he debuted against Iran national football team|Iran. He played 3 games for Japan in 1951.

On October 8, 1986, Tamura died of cancer in Chuo, Tokyo|Chuo, Tokyo Metropolis|Tokyo at the age of 59.
