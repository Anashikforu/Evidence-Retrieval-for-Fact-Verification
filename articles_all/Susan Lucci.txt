
'''Susan Lucci''' (born December 23, 1946) is an Americans|American actress. She is best known for cast member|playing Erica Kane on the award winning soap opera ''All My Children'' on American Broadcasting Company|ABC. Some information sources say Lucci was born during 1946, while others say that she was born in 1948.

Lucci was born in Scarsdale, New York. She has been married to Helmut Huber since 1969. She has a daughter, actress Liza Huber and a son, Andreas Huber.
