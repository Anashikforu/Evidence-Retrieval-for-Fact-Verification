

'''Chevenez''' is a former municipality of the district of Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Chevenez, Damvant, Réclère and Roche-d'Or merged into the new municipality of Haute-Ajoie.
