

A '''secret police''' force is an agency most often used in dictatorships. It is used to scare people living under a dictatorship into agreeing with the dictator's policies. Not all dictatorships, however, use a secret police force. Fidel Castro's Cuba and Mao Zedong's People's Republic of China|China were able to maintain a dictatorship without using secret police.
