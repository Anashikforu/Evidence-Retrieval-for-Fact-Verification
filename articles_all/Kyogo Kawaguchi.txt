'''Kyogo Kawaguchi''' (born October 1, 1974 in Sano City, Tochigi Prefecture, Japan) is a Japan|Japanese singer-songwriter. 
He is now working towards world peace with "Chikyukyoudai" ("World Siblings"). His notable works include "Sakura".
