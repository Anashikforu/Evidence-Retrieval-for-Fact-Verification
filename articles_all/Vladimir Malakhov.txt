'''Vladimir Malakhov''' (born 27 November 1980) is a chess Grandmaster (chess)|grandmaster from Russia.

He was a member of the gold medal-winning Russian team at the 2009 World Team Chess Championship in Bursa.

Malakhov is qualified as a Nuclear physics|nuclear physicist, and he used to be an amateur chess player. Now he's a professional chess player who works part-time as a nuclear physicist.
