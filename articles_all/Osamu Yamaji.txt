Biography
Yamaji was born in Hyogo Prefecture on August 31, 1929. After graduating from Waseda University, he played for Kashima Antlers|Sumitomo Metal. He also played for Osaka SC and won the 2nd place at Emperor's Cup 3 times (1951, 1952 and 1953).

In March 1954, Yamaji was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for 1954 FIFA World Cup|1954 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on March 7, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea.
