A '''precancerous''' (or premalignant) '''condition''' is not malignant. However, it has the potential to become cancer if proper treatment is not taken in time.

A premalignant lesion is a visually (Morphology (biology)|morphologically) altered Tissue (biological)|tissue in which cancer is more likely to occur than its normal counterpart.
