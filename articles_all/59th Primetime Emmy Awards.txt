'''The 59th Annual Primetime Emmy Awards''' were held at the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles, California on September 16, 2007 and were live on television on Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox at 8:00 p.m. North American Eastern Time Zone|EDT (00:00 Coordinated Universal Time|UTC) for the first time in high-definition television|high definition (on Broadcast delay|tape delay three hours later on the West Coast of the United States at 8:00 p.m. Pacific Time Zone|PDT/3:00 UTC). The ceremonies were hosted by Ryan Seacrest.
