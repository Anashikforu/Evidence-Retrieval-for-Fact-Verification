
'''PepsiCo, Inc.''' is an American multinational corporation|multinational food, snack and beverage corporation headquartered in Harrison, New York, in the hamlet of Purchase, New York|Purchase. PepsiCo manufactures, marketing, and distribution of grain-based snack foods, beverages, and other products. PepsiCo was formed in 1965 with the merger of the '''Pepsi-Cola Company''' and Frito-Lay, Inc.
