
'''Heath Miller''' (born July 15, 1983 in Pineville, West Virginia|Pineville, West Virginia) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He currently works with the WWE where he wrestles under the ring name '''Heath Slater'''. He was a member of the List of professional wrestling terms#S|stables '''3MB''' and Nexus (professional wrestling)|The Nexus.

Slater has won the WWE Tag Team Championship three times with Justin Gabriel. He also won a Slammy Award in 2010 for the debut of The Nexus. Slater's finishing move is the ''Smash Hit'' which is a spinning lifting DDT.
