

'''Eve Myles''' (born 1978 in Ystradgynlais, Powys), is a Wales|Welsh actress. She is most known for playing lead fictional character|characters in ''Belonging (TV series)|Belonging'' and ''Doctor Who'' spin-off television programme|series ''Torchwood''.
