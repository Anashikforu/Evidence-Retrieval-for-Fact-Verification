

'''''Kenny vs. Spenny''''' was a Canada|Canadian reality-comedy television series created by Kenny Hotz. It starred Kenny Hotz and Spencer Rice.

In Canada reruns are still shown on the Showcase (TV channel)|Showcase. The series had six seasons and ran from August 26, 2003 to December 23, 2010. Matt Stone and Trey Parker, creators of ''South Park'', were executive producers for 10 episodes in the fourth season of the show.

The show spawned many many replicas including ''Frank vs Girard'' in French Canadian on Vrak.TV|VRAK.TV, ''Ed vs. Spencer'' in the United Kingdom, ''Juan vs. Roman'' in Latin America, ''Elton vs. Simon'' in Germany, ''Katja vs. Bridget'' and ''Dennis vs. Valerio'' in the Netherlands, and ''Sid vs. Varun'' in India.

TV.com rated the series a 8.4. It was ranked as at #8 among the top 10 Canadian television shows of the 21st century.
