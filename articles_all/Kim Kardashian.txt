'''Kimberly Noel "Kim" Kardashian West''' (born October 21, 1980) is an Americans|American socialite. She started her career as a model (person)|model. She is one of three daughters of Robert Kardashian. Their family has a reality television Television programme|series named ''Keeping up with the Kardashians''. Kim and her sister Kourtney Kardashian have their own spin-off ''Kourtney and Kim Take New York''.

Career
Kardashian first got media attention because of her close friendship with Paris Hilton. She became better known after appearing in a sex tape. Kim is most popular for her role in the TV series "Keeping Up With The Kardashians". Kim marriage|married Damon Thomas in 2000, They divorced in 2004. She then married Kris Humphries in August 2011. Humphries is an NBA player on the New Jersey Nets. After 72 days of marriage, she filed for a divorce on October 31, 2011, because of differences.

Personal life
She became engagement|engaged to Kanye West in 2013. The couple lives in Calabasas, California. They have four children: daughter North West (b. 2013), son Saint West (b. 2015), daughter Chicago West (b. 2018), and son Psalm West (b. 2019).

In January 2021, CNN reported that the couple were discussing divorce The divorce became official in 2022.

In 2013 Kardashian was in West's music video for "Bound 2".

=Political views=
Kardashian is a Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat. She attended the 2012 Democratic National Convention in support of Barack Obama|President Obama's re-election. She also supports Armenian political issues such as the proclamation of the Armenian Genocide|Christian Genocide of 1914.

In 2018, Kardashian met with President Donald Trump and helped bring forth the release from prison of Alice Marie Johnson, who was given a life prison sentence for nonviolent drug charges.
