
The '''Åland Islands''' (Swedish language|Swedish: ''Åland'' (pronounced ), Finnish language|Finnish: ''Ahvenanmaa'') form an archipelago in the Baltic Sea. It is situated at the entrance to the Gulf of Bothnia and forms an Federacy|autonomous, Demilitarized zone|demilitarized, Monoglottism|monolingually Swedish language|Swedish-speaking Provinces of Finland|administrative province, Regions of Finland|region and historical provinces of Finland|historical province of Finland. It is the smallest province of Finland, comprising 0.5% of Finland's population and 0.49% of land area.
