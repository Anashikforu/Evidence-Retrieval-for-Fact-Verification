
'''Sherman''' is a city in northern Texas near Dallas and Oklahoma.  It is the county seat of Grayson County, Texas|Grayson County.

History
Sherman was created in 1846 by the Texas legislature.
The city of Sherman had some human and environmental problems over the years.  For example, a huge tornado happened in 1896.

However, Sherman continued to grow, especially when an Army Air Field for pilot training, Perrin Air Force Base, was built there in 1941. Perrin Field was closed in 1971.

Present
Sherman is about 65 miles north of Dallas at the intersection of U.S. Highway 75 and U.S. Highway 82. It has a number of public schools and colleges. It even has its own symphony orchestra, which is not common for a city of its size. Manufacturing is a big part of the local economy. Also, several national companies have plants in Sherman. It is a good city for tourism and international students.
