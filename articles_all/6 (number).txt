




The number '''six''' is a natural number that comes after the number 5 (number)|five and before the number 7 (number)|seven. Six is also the first perfect number which means that the sum of its factors (1, 2 and 3) are equal to the number itself (6). The next perfect number is 28 (number)|28. In Roman numerals, it is '''VI'''.

 Mathematics 
In mathematics, the number six is an even number.
