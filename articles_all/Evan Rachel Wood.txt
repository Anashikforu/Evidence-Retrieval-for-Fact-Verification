
'''Evan Rachel Wood''' (born September 7, 1987)

Wood continued acting in mostly art film|independent films, including ''Pretty Persuasion'' (2005), ''Down in the Valley'' (2006), and in the big studio production ''Across the Universe (movie)|Across the Universe'' (2007). Critics have praised Wood's acting, such as ''The Guardian'' newspaper describing her as being "wise beyond her years" and as "one of the best actresses of her generation". Her personal relationship with singer Marilyn Manson (musician)|Marilyn Manson received media coverage. She is coming out|openly bisexual.
