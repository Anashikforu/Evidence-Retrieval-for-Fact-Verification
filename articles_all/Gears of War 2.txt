'''''Gears of War 2''''' is a third-person shooter video game made by Epic Games and published by Microsoft. It can only be played on Microsoft's own Xbox 360 Video game console|console. It is the sequel to the popular Xbox 360 game ''Gears of War''. The game was released around the world on November 7 2008. By November 12 it had already sold over 2 million copies.
