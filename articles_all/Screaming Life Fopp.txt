


'''''Screaming Life/Fopp''''' is a compilation album by the Grunge music|grunge band Soundgarden. It was released on May 11, 1990 through Sub Pop Records.

Overview
The album combines the band's first two Extended play|EPs: ''Screaming Life'' (1987) and ''Fopp (EP)|Fopp'' (1988). It was released in 1990 (see 1990 in music) by Sub Pop. There are two cover version|cover songs on the album; "Swallow My Pride" is a Green River (band)|Green River cover and "Fopp" is an Ohio Players cover.
