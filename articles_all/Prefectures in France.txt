A '''prefecture''' in France can refer to :
the ''Chef-lieu de département'', the town in which the administration of a departments of France|''département'' is located ;
the ''Chef-lieu de région'', the town in which the administration of a Regions of France|''région'' is located ;
the jurisdiction of a ''préfecture'' ;
the official residence or headquarters of a ''Prefect (France)|préfet''.
