
'''East''' is one of the 4 main directions on a compass (called Cardinal direction|cardinal directions). East is normally to the Position|right on most maps. For example: Japan is east of China, which is itself east of Pakistan. When “The East” is described: it usually refers to the countries of Asia (especially from Pakistan eastwards like China and India). The sun and moon are also seen to rise in the east.
