
'''Everett''' is a city in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Washington.  It is the county seat of Snohomish County, Washington|Snohomish County. 103,019 people live there.

The city is where Boeing builds many of its airplanes, like the Boeing 747 and Boeing 787. It is also where the United States Navy has a base called Naval Station Everett.
