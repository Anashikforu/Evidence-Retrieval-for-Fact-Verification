


'''''Star Trek: First Contact''''' is the eighth movie set in the Star Trek fictional universe|universe. It was made in 1996 by Paramount Pictures. The Borg (Star Trek)|Borg travel back in time to destroy ''Phoenix'', the Earth's first warp technology|warp Spacecraft|ship. Captain Jean-Luc Picard|Picard and his crew must follow them to save the Earth from Cultural assimilation|assimilation by the Borg.

The movie was very successful at the box office. The movie also got very good reviews from movie critics.
