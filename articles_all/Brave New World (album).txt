

'''''Brave New World''''' is an album by Iron Maiden.  It is the band's twelfth studio album, and it was released on 30th May of 2000.

The album was recorded in Paris, France. And it was producer|produced by Kevin Shirley.

It has sold around 307,000 copies in the USA.
