

'''Andrew "Andy" Frank Millen''' (born 10 June 1965, in Glasgow) is a Scottish people|Scottish retired professional football (Soccer)|footballer who played in Defender (association football)|defence. He is the coach (sport)|assistant manager of St Mirren F.C.|St. Mirren. During his career, Millen played for St Johnstone F.C.|St Johnstone, Alloa Athletic F.C.|Alloa Athletic, Hamilton Academical F.C.|Hamilton Academical, Kilmarnock F.C.|Kilmarnock, Hibernian F.C.|Hibernian, Raith Rovers F.C.|Raith Rovers, Ayr United F.C.|Ayr United, Greenock Morton F.C.|Greenock Morton, Clyde F.C.|Clyde and finished his career with St. Mirren in the Scottish Premier League.

He currently holds the record for oldest player to have played in Scottish Premier League in a match playing for St. Mirren vs Heart of Midlothian F.C.|Hearts, at the age of 42 years 279 days, on 15 March 2008.
