
'''William David Trimble, Baron Trimble''', Her Majesty's Most Honourable Privy Council|PC (born 15 October 1944, in Belfast), is a politician from Northern Ireland.  He was Leader of the Ulster Unionist Party (UUP; 1995–2005), the first First Minister of Northern Ireland (1998–2002), and was a Member of the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons (1990–2005).

He is a life peer for the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.  Trimble was awarded the 1998 Nobel Peace Prize, along with John Hume because of effort toward Belfast Agreement.
