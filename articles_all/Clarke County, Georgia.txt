'''Clarke County''' is a county in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. The county town|county seat of Clarke County is Athens, Georgia. Clarke county was formed on December 5, 1801 and named after Revolutionary War hero Elijah Clarke. The University of Georgia is in Clarke County. Clarke county has 104,439 people.
