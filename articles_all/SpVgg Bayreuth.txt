'''SpVgg Bayreuth''' is a germany|German football (soccer)|football team in Bayreuth, Bavaria. It was :wikt:found|founded in 1921. The club was banned in 1933 by the Nazism|Nazis but restarted shortly after World War II. SpVgg Bayreuth plays in one of the Fourths Divisions, the Bayernliga. In the season 2005/2006, they played in the Third Division, the Regionalliga. Today the play in the Fifth Division (Bayernliga Nord).
