
'''Lucia Godoy Alcayaga''', known by pen name '''Gabriela Mistral'''Named after Gabriele d'Annunzio and Frédéric Mistral (7 April 1889 – 10 January 1957) was a Chilean educator, poet, diplomat, and feminist. In 1945, she received the Nobel Prize in Literature. Her best known works include ''Tala (poem)|Tala'', ''Desolación (poem)|Desolación'', and ''Lagar''.
