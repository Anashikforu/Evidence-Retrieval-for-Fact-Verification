'''Romain Maes''' (10 August 1912 in Zerkegem– 22 February 1983 in Dilbeek|Groot-Bijgaarden) was a Belgium|Belgian cyclist. He won the 1935 Tour de France. Some claim that he also won the 1936 Paris-Roubaix race, and that Georges Speicher was credited as winning by mistake.
