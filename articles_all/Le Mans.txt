

'''Le Mans''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Pays de la Loire Regions of France|region in the Sarthe Departments of France|department in the west of France.

'''Le Mans''' is a city in France. It has about 143,000 inhabitants. It takes about one hour to go to Paris by TGV.

Sport
 Le Mans is the start of the seventh stage of the Tour de France|2011 Tour de France.
 The city is well known for the 24-hour car race of Le Mans.
