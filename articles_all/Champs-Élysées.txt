
The '''Champs-Élysées''' is a long and very famous street in Paris.


The street is 1910 meters long and 70 meters wide.It links the Arc de Triomphe and the Place de la Concorde. It is a very popular place: there are many famous cinemas, Bar (business)|bars, boutiques, and restaurants. It is also where the Tour de France ends. It is the second most expensive street of the world. Rent is more than 900 000/100m² in a year.

On 20 April 2017, two ISIS-claimed gunmen 2017 shooting of Paris police officers|opened fired and killed one police officer and injured another at Champs-Élysées.
