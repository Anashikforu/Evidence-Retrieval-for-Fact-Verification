
'''Marie Picasso''' (born 24 April 1979) is a Swedes|Swedish reality show star, model and singer who rose to fame in 2002 when she was a housemate on Big Brother (TV series)|Big Brother. She later got a job as a game show Presenter|host on TV4 leading two different game shows. In 2007 she entered Idol 2007 where she is currently in the top 10 of the Swedish version of Pop Idol. Picasso won Pop idol on 7 December 2007 in Globen Arena in Stockholm with 51,3% of the votes and she won over the first runner up Amanda Jenssen. Her debut single is called "This Moment".
