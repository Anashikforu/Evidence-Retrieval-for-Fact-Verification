

'''Louis Victor Pierre Raymond, (7th) Duke of Broglie''', (August 15, 1892 in Dieppe – March 19, 1987 in Louveciennes) was a France|French physicist. He discovered that electrons are similar to both Wave–particle duality|particles and waves. He won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1929.
