
'''Alizée Jacotey''' (born 21 August 1984 in Ajaccio, Corsica) is a French people|French singer. Her stage name is Alizée — which is the feminine form of ''alizé'' and means "trade wind". While working with Mylène Farmer and Laurent Boutonnat, Alizée put out two albums. Both of which were hits both inside and outside of France. After the first two albums she broke up with Mylène and Laurent. She has released three more albums. On 6 November 2003 she married Jérémy Chatelain. In 2012 they divorced.
