
'''Arthur Tudor''' (19 or 20 September 1486 - 2 April 1502) was the oldest son of Henry VII of England|Henry VII and Elizabeth of York.

As the oldest son, Arthur was to be King of England after the death of his father. In 1501, he married Catherine of Aragon, a princess from Spain. He died a few months later. His younger brother, Henry VIII of England|Henry, became heir to the throne. After Henry became king in 1509, he married Arthur's widow, Catherine.
