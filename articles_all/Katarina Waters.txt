


'''Katarina Waters''' (born 10 November 1980) is a Germany|German-born English people|English professional wrestling|professional wrestler and valet that currently wrestles for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling under the ring name, '''Winter'''. She is best known from  World Wrestling Entertainment, wrestling on its WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brand with the ring name '''Katie Lea'''.

 Career 
Before signing with WWE, she spent many years on the independent circuit, particularly in the Frontier Wrestling Alliance, where she used the ring name Nikita. After signing with WWE, she competed in Ohio Valley Wrestling, where she became a OVW Women's Championship#Title history|two time OVW Women's Championship|Women's Champion before being called up to the main roster.
