

'''''High School Musical 2''''' is a 2007 Disney Channel|Disney television movie movie. It is a sequel to ''High School Musical''. The movie was very popular. It had a record number of viewers for a made-for-television movie. ''High School Musical 2'' also set the record for most viewers for a pay-television movie in Australia.
Soundtrack
''What Time Is It?'' is a song off the soundtrack of this movie. It is about the kids who go to East High School leaving school for the summertime.
