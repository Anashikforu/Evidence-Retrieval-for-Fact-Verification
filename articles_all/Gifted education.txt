

'''Gifted education''' is a term for special ways to wikt:education|educate (teach) children who have been wikt:identify|identified as talent|gifted. There is no definition of a gifted student which everyone agrees on.

Gifted and talented education has a long history of thousands of years. Handbook of gifted education (2nd ed.). New York: Allyn and Bacon. p. 5 In China's Tang Dynasty (580-618 Common Era|CE), child prodigies were summoned to the imperial court for specialized education. Throughout the Renaissance, those who showed creative talent in art, architecture, and literature were supported by both the government and private patronage.

In 2011, the National Association for Gifted Children published a position paper that stated what a gifted student is. The word "gifted," describes people who demonstrate outstanding aptitude or competence in one or more wikt:domain|domains. An "aptitude" is defined as an exceptional ability to learn or reason. "Competence" is defined as documented performance or achievement in the top 10 percent of the population.National Association for Gifted Children. (2011).
