
'''''Fantasia''''' is the third List of Disney animated movies|animated movie produced by The Walt Disney Company|Walt Disney Productions. It was first released on February 29, 1940. It was originally released by itself instead of RKO Pictures, which was the distributor of Disney from ''Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs (1937 movie)|Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs'', until ''Peter Pan (1953 movie)|Peter Pan''.

''Fantasia'' contains eight classical music pieces. One of them is from Igor Stravinsky|Igor Stravinsky's ''The Rite of Spring''. It tells the story of life on Earth until the extinction of the dinosaurs. Another famous work that appears in the movie is Johann Sebastian Bach's Toccata and Fugue in D minor for organ (music)|organ. 

A sequel to this movie, ''Fantasia 2000'', was released in 1999.
