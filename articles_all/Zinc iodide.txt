'''Zinc iodide''' is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is ZnI2. It contains zinc and iodide ions.

Properties
Zinc iodide is a colorless solid. It dissolves in water. It reacts with base (chemistry)|bases to make zinc hydroxide. It is a reducing agent. When it is oxidation|oxidized it makes iodine.

Preparation
It is made by reacting zinc with iodine. 

Uses
It is opaque (not clear) to X-rays. It can be used as a stain in microscope slides.
