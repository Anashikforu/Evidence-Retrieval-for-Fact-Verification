
'''Brunswick County''' is a county in the U.S. state of North Carolina.  In 2000, 73,143 people lived there.  Its county seat is Bolivia, North Carolina|Bolivia.

 History 
The county was made in 1764 from parts of Bladen County, North Carolina|Bladen County and New Hanover County, North Carolina|New Hanover County.

 Government 
Brunswick County is part of the local Cape Fear Council of Governments.

 Connected counties 
These counties are connected to Brunswick County:
Pender County, North Carolina - north-northeast
New Hanover County, North Carolina - east
Horry County, South Carolina - southwest
Columbus County, North Carolina - northwest
