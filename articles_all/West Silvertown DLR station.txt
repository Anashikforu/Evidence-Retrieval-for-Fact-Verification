
'''West Silvertown''' is a station on the Docklands Light Railway which opened in December 2005. It is on the Woolwich Arsenal DLR station|Woolwich Arsenal branch. Trains run Westbound to Bank and Monument stations|Bank in the City of London and Eastbound to Woolwich Arsenal DLR station|Woolwich Arsenal, passing through London City Airport DLR station|London City Airport station.

The station is in the London Borough of Newham and is in Travelcard Zone 3.
