'''Dana Elaine Owens''' (born March 18, 1970 in Newark, New Jersey), best known under the name '''Queen Latifah''' is an Americans|American, rapper singer, actress and model (person)|model. She has won a Golden Globe award, two Screen Actors Guild Awards, two Image Awards, a Grammy Award, six additional Grammy nominations, an Emmy Award nomination and an Academy Award nomination.
