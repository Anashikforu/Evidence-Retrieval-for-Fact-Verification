

'''Byron Moreno''' (born 23 November 1969) is a former Ecuadorian Association football|football referee. He has been suspended for his job, because he showed illegal red cards. He was also arrested in New York for the possession of the illegal drugs|illegal drug heroin and was fined for drunk driving.
