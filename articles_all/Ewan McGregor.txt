'''Ewan Gordon McGregor'''  He played the lead role in Danny Boyle's ''Trainspotting (movie)|Trainspotting'', Obi-Wan Kenobi in the prequel trilogy of ''Star Wars'', and in his motorcycle adventures with friend Charley Boorman. 

McGregor starred as Christopher Robin in the 2018 Disney movie ''Christopher Robin (movie)|Christopher Robin''. He also starred as the villain Black Mask in the 2020 DCEU movie ''Birds of Prey (2020 movie)|Birds of Prey''.

McGregor lives in Los Angeles, California. He also has a home in North London, England.
