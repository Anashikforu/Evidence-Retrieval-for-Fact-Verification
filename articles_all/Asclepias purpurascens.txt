


'''''Asclepias purpurascens''''' (also known as the '''purple milkweed''') is a species of milkweed plant.  The species ''Asclepias cordifolia'' is also sometimes called purple milkweed.  It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to North America.  It blooms from June to July.  The flowers are a pink-purple color.  This species is found in savannas, prairies, and meadows.  Purple Milkweed likes to grow in clay or sandy soil and in sunny or partly shaded places.
