

'''Tarawih''' (تراويح) also spelled '''Taraweeh''', are extra prayers which Muslims read at night in the month of Ramadan. Tarawih prayers are made up of a number of prayer phrases and actions, raka'ah|raka‘āt, said in pairs. Hanafis and Shafi'i say there are 20 raka‘āt, some Hanabalis say it is only eight, while others say 20. Maliki|Malikis say there are 36, Andy Carroll|and the Ahl al-Hadeeth say it is 8.
