
'''''Exempli gratia''''' is a Transition (linguistics)|linking word, mostly known in English-speaking country|countries by its abbreviation '''e.g.''', means "for example" in Latin.

The literal translation is "for the sake of example". Gratia means "for the sake of" and when used with the genitive case.
''Exempli gratia'' is sometimes confused with ''id est'' (''i.e.'').'' Id est'' means "that is (to say)" in the sense of "that means", or "which means", or "in other words", or sometimes "in this case".
