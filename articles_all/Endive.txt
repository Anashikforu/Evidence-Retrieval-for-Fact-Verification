

'''Endive''' (''Chicorium endivia'') is a leafy plant of the family (biology)|family Asteraceae. The plants are either annual, or they live for two years. Originally, these plants grew in the countries around the Mediterranean. There are many wikt:varieties|varieties, but they can be split into two groups

 the curly-leaved. or narrow-leaved endive
 Batavian or broad-leaved endive

Since Antiquity, these plants have been used to make salads. Sometimes they are cooked, and prepared similar to spinach or chard.
