

'''Kelly Michelle Lee Osbourne''' (born 27 October 1984, in London) is an England|English singer and actress. She is the daughter of the singer Ozzy Osbourne.

Osborne is one of the Presenter|co-presenters in the E! television programme|programme ''Fashion Police''. She also presents a programme on BBC Radio 1 called ''The Surgery'' on Sunday evening. Her duet song with her father, singer Ozzy Osbourne, "Changes", received much praise.
