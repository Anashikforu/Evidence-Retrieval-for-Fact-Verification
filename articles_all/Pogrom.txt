'''''Pogrom''''' (from Russian language|Russian:''погром'' (pogrom); from "громить" international phonetic alphabet|IPA: - to wreak havoc, to demolish violence|violently) is a form of riot attacking people and property. The target of the attack is a group of people because of their ethnic identity or religion, usually Antisemitism|Jews. In a pogrom, the local authorities such as the police do not arrest the rioters and do not come to the aid of the victims.

In a pogrom, the target people's homes, businesses, and places of worship are destroyed. People of the target group are physically attacked and some are murdered.
