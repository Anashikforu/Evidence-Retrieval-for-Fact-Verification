


'''Christopher Charles Mintz-Plasse''' (born June 20, 1989 in Woodland Hills, Los Angeles, California) is an Americans|American Actor. He is famous for playing the character ''Fogell/McLovin'' in the movie, Superbad (movie)|Superbad and ''Chris D'Amico/Red Mist'' in the movie, Kick-Ass.
