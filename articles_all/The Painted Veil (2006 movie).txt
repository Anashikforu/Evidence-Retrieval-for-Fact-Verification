

'''''The Painted Veil''''' is a 2006 romance movie|romantic drama movie that was directed by John Curran. It was based on the 1925 novel with The Painted Veil (novel)|the same name. This is the third movie version of the novel the first The Painted Veil (1934 movie)|in 1934 and the second in 1957 called ''The Seventh Sin''. This movie won a Golden Globe Award for Best Original Score in 2006.

 Sally Hawkins as Mary
 Liev Schreiber as Charles Townsend
