

'''''Saratoga Trunk''''' is a 1945 United States|American Romance movie|romance movie directed by Sam Wood and written by Edna Ferber and Casey Robinson. Flora Robson who played Ingrid Bergman's servant in the movie was nominated for a Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.

Cast
Gary Cooper as '''Colonel Clint Maroon'''
Ingrid Bergman as '''Clio Dulaine'''
Flora Robson as '''Angelique Buiton'''
Jerry Austin as '''Cupidon'''
John Warburton as '''Bartholomew Van Steed'''
Florence Bates as '''Sophie Bellop'''
Curt Bois as '''Augustin Haussy'''
John Abbott (actor)|John Abbott as '''Roscoe Bean'''
