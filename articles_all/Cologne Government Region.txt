
'''Cologne Government Region''' (German language|German: ''Regierungsbezirk Köln'', Kölsch (dialect)|Kölsch: ''Rejierungsbezirk Kölle'') is one of the five Regierungsbezirk|governmental districts of the Germany|German States of Germany|Federal State of North Rhine-Westphalia. It is in the southwest of that state. It covers the hills of the Eifel as well as the Bergisches Land.

It was made in 1815, when Prussia reorganised its districts.
