

'''Publius Vergilius Maro''' (21 September 19 BC),) in English, was a poet in the Latin language. His poems are about gods and their mythology.  Virgil's most famous epic poem is called the ''Aeneis|Aeneid''.

 Life 
Tradition is that Virgil was born in the village of Andes, near Mantua in Cisalpine Gaul.The epitaph on his tomb in Posilipo near Naples was ''Mantua me genuit; Calabri rapuere; tenet nunc Parthenope. Cecini pascua, rura, duces'' ("Mantua gave birth to me, the Calabrians took me, now Naples holds me; I sang of pastures the Eclogues, country the Georgics and leaders the Aeneid").
