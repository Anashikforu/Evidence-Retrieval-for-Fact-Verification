

'''78violet''' (formerly '''Aly & AJ''') are an United States|American music group. The members are Amanda Michalka and her sister, Alyson Michalka|Alyson who is two years older. 

Members
 Alyson (nicknamed "Aly"): helps writing the music and songs. She is one of the two guitarists.
 Amanda (nicknamed "AJ"): helps writing the songs and constructing the music.

Aly & AJ lived in Seattle, Washington in the early 1990s, then moved back to Southern California. They learned to play the piano at the age of six. They both were in the movie ''My Super Sweet 16 The Movie''.

 Acoustic Hearts of Winter (2006)
