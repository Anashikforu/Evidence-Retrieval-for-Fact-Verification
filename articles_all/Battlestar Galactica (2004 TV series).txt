

'''''Battlestar Galactica''''' is an United States|American science fiction television programme|television series filmed in Vancouver. It was created by Ronald D. Moore that first aired in a Battlestar Galactica (TV miniseries)|three-hour miniseries in December of 2003 on the Sci Fi Channel (United States)|Sci Fi Channel.

The series has gained a wide range of critical acclaim. It won a Emmy Award and Saturn Awards.
