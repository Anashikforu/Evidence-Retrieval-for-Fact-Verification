
'''Robert "Rob" Alexander Szatkowski''' (born December 18, 1971 in Battle Creek, Michigan|Battle Creek, Michigan), better known by his ring name, '''Rob Van Dam''', is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler who currently works with WWE. He has also wrestled with Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) from 1996 to 2001 and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) from 2010 to 2013. His finishing move is the Five-Star Frog Splash.
