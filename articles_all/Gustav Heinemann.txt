
'''Gustav Walter Heinemann''' (born 23 July 1899 in Schwelm; died 7 July 1976 in Essen) was a Germany|German politician. He was Ministry of the Interior of Germany|Interior Minister under Chancellor of Germany|Chancellor Konrad Adenauer. He resigned when Adenauer started getting ready to set up the German Army. He later resigned from the CDU and joined the SPD. He also served as Ministry of Justice of Germany|Justice Minister before he was elected President of Germany|Federal President in 1969. He served for one term, and left office in 1974.
