
'''The Rose Bowl Game''' is an United States|American college football Bowl Championship Series|bowl game at the Rose Bowl (stadium) in Pasadena, CA|Pasadena, California.  It is known as the "granddaddy of them all" because it has been played every year since 1916.  It is almost always played on January 1 after Pasadena's Rose Parade.  For many years, the two teams who played were the winner of the Pac-10 (a football wikt:conference|conference of colleges on the West Coast) and the winner of the Big 10 (a football conference of collleges in the Midwest).  In 1998, the game became part of the Bowl Championship Series.  University of Southern California|USC has won the game 24 times.
