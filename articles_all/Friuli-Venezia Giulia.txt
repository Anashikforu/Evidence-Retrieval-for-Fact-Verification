

'''Friuli-Venezia Giulia''' () is a region in the north-east of Italy on the Adriatic Sea. The Capital city|capital is Trieste. The population is 1,191,588.  FVG has a border with the Italian region of Veneto to the west, Austria to the north and Slovenia on the east.

People in this region speak the Italian language|Italian, Friulian, Slovene language|Slovene and German language|German languages.

 Cuisine 
Friulani, Giuliani, Austrian, Slovene and Hungarian influences over the centuries have created a wide range of food and drink typical of the region.  Some examples include:
 Jota (Istrian stew)
 Radicchio
 Goulash
 Gnocchi alla spinache
