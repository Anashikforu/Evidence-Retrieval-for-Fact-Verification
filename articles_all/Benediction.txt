'''Benediction''' is a short prayer for help and blessings from God. They are usually delivered at the end of a worship service. Roman Catholicism|Roman Catholics have many more benedictions, usually with many candles (even poor churches have at least ten),). This was made popular by Martin Luther in his Deutsche Messe (German Mass). It is a tradition in most Lutheran Churches.Precht, Fred L. ''Lutheran Worship History and Practice''. St. Louis: Concordia Publishing House, 1993. p. 434. F. Scott Fitzgerald also wrote a short story called ''Benediction'' in 1920.
