
'''Great North Eastern Railway''' ('''GNER''') was a Great Britain|British List of companies operating trains in the United Kingdom|train operating company, owned by Sea Containers Ltd. It operated high-speed rail|high-speed express train services on the East Coast Main Line from 1996 until the takeover in 2007 of the franchise by National Express East Coast on 9 December 2007.
