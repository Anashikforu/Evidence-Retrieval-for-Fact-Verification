

The British Rail '''Class 108''' diesel multiple units were built by Derby Carriage and Wagon Works|BR Derby from 1958 to 1961, with a final production quantity of 333 vehicles.

These units stayed in were used till the 1990s after which they were wikt:withdraw|withdrawn from traffic.
