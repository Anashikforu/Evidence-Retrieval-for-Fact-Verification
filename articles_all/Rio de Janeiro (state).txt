
'''Rio de Janeiro''' is one of the States of Brazil|26 states of Brazil. Its capital is the city of Rio de Janeiro.

Rio de Janeiro is in the Southeast Region, Brazil|Southeast Region of Brazil. It shares borders with Minas Gerais, Espírito Santo and São Paulo (state)|São Paulo as well as the Atlantic Ocean. Rio de Janeiro has an area of .

The state's largest cities are Rio de Janeiro, Nova Iguaçu, Niterói, Duque de Caxias, São Gonçalo (Rio de Janeiro)|São Gonçalo, São João de Meriti, Campos dos Goytacazes, Petrópolis and Volta Redonda. Its main rivers are the Guandu River, the Piraí River|Piraí, the Paraíba do Sul River|Paraíba do Sul, the Macaé River|Macaé and the Muriaé River|Muriaé. Its climate is Tropics|tropical.

 Geography 
The state is made up of both mountains and plains. It is between the Mantiqueira Mountains and the Atlantic Ocean. The bays of Guanabara Bay|Guanabara, Sepetiba Bay|Sepetiba, and Ilha Grande Bay|Ilha Grande are on the coastline of Rio de Janeiro.

Rio de Janeiro is the smallest state in the Southeast Region and one of the smallest in Brazil. Its coastline is , the third longest coastline in the country.
