


'''''Most Haunted Live''''' is a United Kingdom|British paranormal television program based on investigating possible paranormal activity.  It is shown on the Satellite television|satellite and Cable television|cable channels Living (channel)|Living, Living2 and Virgin 1 (Formerly Ftn), mainly for the UK market. It is the Live version of ''Most Haunted'' which is going into its 10th series on February 19, 2008. It is made by Antix Productions, and is led by Yvette Fielding and her husband Karl Beattie.

Production
The team's latest series leads them to travel to Italy, Turin. Investigating possible paranormal 'hotspots' for 24 hours witihn the city.
