


'''Yuri Vladimirovich Andropov''' was a Soviet Union|Soviet politician. He was born in Nagutskoye, Russian SFSR.

Andropov was Director of the KGB for an unusually long time:  19671982 (Jan-May). He was General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union|General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union|CPSU from November 12, 1982 until his death fifteen months later. He had diabetes mellitus and died of renal failure in Moscow, Russian SFSR.
