'''Adam Craig Gilchrist''' (born 14 November 1971 in Bellingen, New South Wales|Bellingen, New South Wales) is a former Australian cricketer. He was a wicket keeper and a left handed batsman. In his test cricket|test career, Gilchrist hit 100 six (cricket)|sixes, something nobody else in history has done. He holds the record of the second fastest century maker in history.
