

"'''Boulevard of Broken Dreams'''" is a song by the United States|American punk rock band Green Day, from their seventh studio album, ''American Idiot''.

The song depicts a solemn walk of a lonely Jesus of Suburbia (The protagonist of the album) down a road after the happenings of Holiday (Green Day song)|Holiday. The song is considered by many to be the best song ever written by Green Day, and it has received many awards.
