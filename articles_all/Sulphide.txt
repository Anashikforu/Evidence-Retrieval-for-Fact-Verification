A '''sulphide''' ('''sulfide''' in US English) is an anion of  sulphur in its lowest oxidation number of -2.  Sulphide is also a term for thioethers, a common type of  Chemical compound|compound which are known for their bad odours (example: H2S = hydrogen sulphide).

The dianion S2- exists only in strongly alkaline water|aqueous solutions.
