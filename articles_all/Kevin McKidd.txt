
'''Kevin McKidd''' (born 9 August 1973) is a Scotland|Scottish actor. He has played many acting roles such as Lucius Vorenus (character of Rome)|Lucius Vorenus on ''Rome (TV series)|Rome'', as Dan Vasser on ''Journeyman (TV series)|Journeyman'', as Poseidon on ''Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief, and as Dr. Owen Hunt on ''Grey's Anatomy''.
