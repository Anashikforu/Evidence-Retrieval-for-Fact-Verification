
'''Sir Run Run Shaw''' Order of the British Empire|CBE, Grand Bauhinia Medal|GBM (23 November 1907 &ndash; 7 January 2014) was an important name in Hong Kong movie theatre|cinema. He made many films with his brother Runme Shaw. Later in life, he donation|donated a lot of money to charitable organization|charity, schools, and hospitals.

He died at his residence on the morning of 7 January 2014 at 6:55am (HKT) at the age of 106 from natural causes, peacefully with his family by his side.
