#redirect Mojang Studios

'''Mojang AB''' is a Sweden|Swedish video game company. It was created by Markus Persson. He is known for making ''Minecraft''. The company was started in May 2009. It is the company that created and updates ''Minecraft''. It also develops some other games.

On September 15, 2014, Microsoft bought Mojang for 2.5 billion US dollars. After the deal was made, Markus Persson announced that he would leave Mojang.
