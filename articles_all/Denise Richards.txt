

'''Denise Lee Richards''' (born February 17, 1971) is an Americans|American Actor|actress and former fashion model. She came to fame in the late 1990s, after many movies that showed her beauty, including ''Starship Troopers (movie)|Starship Troopers'', ''Wild Things'' and ''The World Is Not Enough''.
