
'''Xavier Rudd''' (born 1978) is an Australian musician. He grew up in the small town of Torquay, Victoria, Australia|Victoria, Australia. When Rudd was young, he learned to play the didgeridoo on a vacuum cleaner hose. 

Rudd is known for playing at music festivals and concerts in Australia and in North America. He has many wikt:fan|fans in Australia, his home, as well as Canada, where he has recorded a few live albums. 

Some of his songs have politics|political themes that deal with the father of 3 sons environment and the Human rights|rights of Australian Aborigine|Aboriginal Australians.

Personal life
Rudd is a father of three sons, Joaquin, Finojet, and Jundi. A husband to his wife, Ashley Freeman.. 

Rudd also describes himself as a full-time "barefooter", which means that he does not own any shoes or other footwear. Rudd is also a vegetarian.
