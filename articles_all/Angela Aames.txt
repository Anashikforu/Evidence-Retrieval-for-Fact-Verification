

'''Angela Aames''' (27 February 27, 1956 in Pierre, South Dakota &ndash; November 27, 1988 in West Hills, California) was an Americans|American actress. She was famous for her role in the movie ’’Bachelor Party’’. She died of heart problems at the age of 32.
