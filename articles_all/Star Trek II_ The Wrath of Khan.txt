
'''''Star Trek II: The Wrath of Khan''''' is the second ''Star Trek'' movie. It was made in 1982. It is a sequel to ''Star Trek: The Motion Picture''. It is considered to be the best of the ''Star Trek'' movies. Most of the original actors are in the movie and it marked the return of Khan, a villain from the first season of the series. It won 2 Saturn awards, another 8 nominations. A notable scene in the movie consists of Kirk screaming the name of Khan very loudly when he is trapped in a planet.
