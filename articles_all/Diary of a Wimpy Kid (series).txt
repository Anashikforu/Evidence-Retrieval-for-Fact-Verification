'''''Diary of a Wimpy Kid''''' is an ongoing series of currently sixteen books written by Jeff Kinney. The series is about a middle school student named Greg Heffley. The books are written in the style of Greg's diary. They contain many drawings, all with no color.


These books are about Gregory Heffley, most commonly known as Greg, who struggles with middle school life and family life, he is very insociable and its hard for him to communicate and be part of the popular group which is proved time and again only having 1 close friend, Rowley Jefferson. He goes to public school where he has multiple crushes including Holly Hills, also known as Piper Elizabeth Mattews in the online version.

Characters
=Main=
'''Greg Heffley''' is a middle school student who wants to be popular. He gets upset when things don't usually go his way.
'''Rodrick Heffley''' is Greg's older brother. He is a teenager who likes heavy metal music. Rodrick plays the drums in his band, Löded Diper.
'''Manny Heffley''' is Greg's younger brother. Greg thinks that his parents like Manny more than him and Rodrick.
'''Frank Heffley''' is Greg's father. He thinks Greg is weak. Frank wants Greg to stop playing video games and play sports instead.
'''Susan Heffley''' is Greg's mother. She is nice, but does not know very much about things that Greg and Rodrick like. She does not like technology and smartphones.
'''Rowley Jefferson''' is Greg's best friend. Greg thinks he is not mature. Rowley is nicer than Greg. Rowley benefits in multiple ways because of Greg. Rowley has also made journals (he was inspired by his best friend Greg). His journal is about his and Greg's middle school life and just their ordinary lives.
