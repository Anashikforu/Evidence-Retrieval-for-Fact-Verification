'''Reba McEntire''' (born March 28, 1955) is an Americans|American country music singer, songwriter and actress. She has many hit songs. She acted in a TV comedy called "Reba".

Early life
McEntire was born in Kiowa, Oklahoma. She was raised in Denison, Texas.
