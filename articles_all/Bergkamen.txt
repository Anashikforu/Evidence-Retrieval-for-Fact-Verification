
'''Bergkamen''' is a town in the district of Unna Rural District|Unna, in North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It is near the river Lippe River|Lippe, approx. 15&nbsp;km north-east of Dortmund and 15&nbsp;km south-west of Hamm.

Bergkamen, a German town in the East part of the Ruhr Area|Ruhrgebiet and south of the Münster Government Region|Münsterland, was founded in 1966 by the merging of at first five smaller communities.

Twin towns
  Hettstedt, since 1990
  Taşucu, since 1994
  Gennevilliers, since 1995
  Wieliczka, since 1995
