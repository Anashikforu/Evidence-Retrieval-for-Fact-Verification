

'''''Ella Enchanted''''' is a Newbery Honor book. It was written by Gail Carson Levine. It was published in 1997. It is also the title of the United States|American Ella Enchanted (movie)|movie based on the novel, released April 9, 2004. The movie was directed by Tommy O'Haver and starred Anne Hathaway (actress)|Anne Hathaway and Hugh Dancy. The story is a retelling of ''Cinderella'' featuring various mythical creatures including elf|elves, ogres, Dwarf (mythology)|dwarves and giant (mythology)|giants.
