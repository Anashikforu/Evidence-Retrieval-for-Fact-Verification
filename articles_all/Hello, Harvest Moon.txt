

'''''Hello, Harvest Moon''''' is a children's picture book written by Ralph Fletcher with drawings by Kate Kiesler.  It was first :wikt:published|published in 2003 by Clarion Books.

 Story 
The moon rises and shines through a girl's bedroom window.  It then shines on a silent street, corn and wheat fields and :wikt:fall|fall trees.  A young girl and her cat play a game by its light, a :wikt:pilot|pilot flies a :wikt:plane|plane using its light.  The moon sets in the :wikt:daylight|daylight as the young girl and her cat say goodnight.
