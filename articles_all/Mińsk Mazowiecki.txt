'''Mińsk Mazowiecki''' (Minsk, miɲsk mazɔ'vi̯eʦki) is a town in Poland. It is the capital (city)|capital of Mińsk County. 

 Location 
It has been located in the Mazovia Voivodship since 1999. 

 History 
The town of Mińsk was founded in 1421. It has always been a town based on trade|commerce. Before the Holocaust, it was a Poland|Polish - Judaism|Jewish town. The main religion in Mińsk is Roman Catholicism. Mińsk have 37000 inhabitants.
