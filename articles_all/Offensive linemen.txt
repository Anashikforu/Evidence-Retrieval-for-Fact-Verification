'''Offensive linemen''' (also '''OL''') are American football players who defend the quarterback or running backs by blocking defensive players.

 Details 
OL are separated from 4 positions.
Center (American football)|Center (C) (1)
Guard (G) (2)
Tackle (T) (2)
Tight end (E) (0~2)

 Rules 
OL (without C) must not touch a ball.
Do not grab defensive players from behind.
In pass play, don't cross the line of scrimmage.
OL must wear uniform which the number is 50 to 79.

Category:American football positions
