 is a town in Miyagi Prefecture. Fishing is an important business in this town. The JR East Ishinomaki Line stops here at Onagawa Station. The town was badly damaged by the 2011 Tōhoku earthquake and tsunami.

2011 Tōhoku earthquake and tsunami
Image:Wide view of Onagawa after tsunami.jpg|thumb|none|750px|View of Onagawa one month after the tsunami, 10 April 2011

The tsunami reached a maximum of 15 metres in height and went as much as one kilometer in from the coast.

Energy
There are three nuclear reactors in Onagawa. They can produce up to 2,174 megawatts of electricity. The plants automatically shut down after the March 11 earthquake without any accidents. There is no plan to start the plants again during 2011.
