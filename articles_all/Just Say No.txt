
 
"'''Just Say No'''" was an advertising campaign, part of the U.S. "War on Drugs", wikt:dominant|dominant during the 1980s and early 1990s. It wanted to discourage children from using illegal drug(s) offering wikt:various|various wikt:ways|ways of saying no. "Just Say No" was also used to discourage violence and premarital sex. The slogan was created by First Lady of the United States Nancy Reagan.
