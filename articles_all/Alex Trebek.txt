
'''George Alexander''' "'''Alex'''" '''Trebek'''  (July 22, 1940 &ndash; November 8, 2020) was a Canadians|Canadian-Americans|American television game show host. He hosted ''Jeopardy!'' from 1984 until his death in 2020. He was born in Greater Sudbury, Ontario. He became a naturalized American citizen in 1998. In 2011, he snapped his achilles tendon while trying to stop a thief trying to rob his San Francisco hotel room.

In March 2019, Trebek announced that he had been diagnosed with Cancer staging|stage IV pancreatic cancer.
