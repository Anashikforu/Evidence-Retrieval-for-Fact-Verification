'''Kennesaw State University''' is a public, coeducational university in Kennesaw, Georgia|Kennesaw, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. The university has about 23,452 students. It was founded in 1963. The current president of the university is Daniel S. Papp.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Owls and they have 15 varsity teams. Most teams compete in the Atlantic Sun Conference (A-Sun). The American football|football team, which began play in 2015, plays in the Big South Conference because the A-Sun does not sponsor football.
