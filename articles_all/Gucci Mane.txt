
'''Radric Delantic Davis''' (born February 12, 1980), known professionally as '''Gucci Mane''', is an Americans|American rapper and record executive.
Gucci Mane has a large tattoo of an ice cream cone tattooed onto his face which he got after he was released from the hospital.
