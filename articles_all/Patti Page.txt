
'''Clara Ann Fowler''', better known as '''Patti Page''', (November 8, 1927 - January 1, 2013) was an Americans|American singer. She is one of the best known female singers of pop music|traditional pop. She was the best selling female artist in the 1950s. She will win an honorary Grammy Award in 2013.

Page was born in Claremore, Oklahoma|Claremore, Oklahoma.
