The '''Leader of the Conservative Party''' is the most senior politician within the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party of the United Kingdom. The post is currently held by Boris Johnson who is also the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister at the head of a Conservative majority government. There was no proper leader of the Conservative Party until 1922:
