
'''Treehouse of Horror''' is the third List of The Simpsons episodes|episode in The Simpsons (season 2)|season 2 of ''The Simpsons''. It was first shown on October 25, 1990. It is the first-ever "Treehouse of Horror" episode and has since been followed by a yearly "Treehouse of Horror" episode.  This episode features three stories. The last one is the famous ''The Raven'' by Edgar Allan Poe.
