
'''Sonoma County''' is a county in the U.S. state of California. It is located north of the San Francisco Bay. It is the wikt:northernmost|northernmost of the nine San Francisco Bay Area counties. The county seat is in Santa Rosa, California|Santa Rosa.

Sonoma County was one of the original counties formed when California became a state in 1850.

The U.S. Census Bureau wikt:estimate#Verb|estimates that the population was 500,292 in 2014.

Cities
Some of the cities in Sonoma County include:

 Petaluma, California|Petaluma
 Santa Rosa, California|Santa Rosa
 Sonoma, California|Sonoma
