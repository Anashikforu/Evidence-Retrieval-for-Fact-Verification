


'''Betty Aberlin''' (born '''Betty Kay Ageloff''' December 30, 1942, New York City) is an Americans|American Actor|actress, poetry|poet, and writer. She had a regular role on ''Smothers Brothers|The Smothers Brothers Show''. Aberlin worked on many television programme|television programs. She played Lady Aberlin on ''Mister Rogers' Neighborhood''. Her movie roles include work in ''Dogma (movie)|Dogma'' and ''Jersey Girl (movie)|Jersey Girl''.
