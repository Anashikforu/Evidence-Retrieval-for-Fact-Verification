The '''cayenne pepper''' is a type of chili pepper.  It is very hot and is used as a spice. Cayenne pepper is the basis for the hot sauce known as Tabasco, as well as being an ingredient in many other wikt:commercial|commercially made hot sauces, such as Frank's Red Hot and Cholula. It can also be used in a dried powder form.
