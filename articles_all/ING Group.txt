'''ING Group''', or just '''ING''' is a world wide Finance|financial company. ING started in the Netherlands. ING offers Finance|banking, Investment|investments, life insurance and retirement services. ING means: '''I'''nternationale '''N'''ederlanden Groep. 

It is a member of Inter-Alpha Group of Banks. 

 History 
The first bank of ING Group was founded in 1743.  

Then it was established in 1991 after the Mergers and acquisitions|merger of NMB Postbank Groep and Nationale-Nederlanden.
