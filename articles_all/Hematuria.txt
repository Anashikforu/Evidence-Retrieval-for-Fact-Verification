'''Hematuria''' is the presence of blood in the urine. It may have a benign cause, but in some cases can be caused by an illness that could cause death.  

Causes

Renal cell carcinoma
Cystitis
Bladder cancer
Pyelonephritis
Prostate cancer
Urethritis
Wilms tumor
Kidney stones
Glomerulonephritis
Possibly, allergies.
