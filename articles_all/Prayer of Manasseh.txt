

The '''Prayer of Manasseh''' is a short work of 15 verses of the penitential prayer of the Kingdom of Judah|Judean king Manasseh of Judah|Manasseh.  Manasseh is recorded in the Bible as one of the most idolatrous (Books of Kings|2 Kings 21:1-18); however, after having been taken captive by the Assyrians, he prays for mercy (Books of Chronicles|2 Chronicles 33:10-17) and turns from his ways of worshiping idols.
