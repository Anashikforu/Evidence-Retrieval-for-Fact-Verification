
'''Brian Orser''', Order of Canada|OC (born December 18, 1961 in Belleville, Ontario|Belleville, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadians|Canadian figure skater. He won two silver medals (second place) at the Olympic Games|Olympics: the first in 1984 and the second in 1988.

At the 1988 Olympics, he was part of the ''Battle of the Brians''. He and Americans|American Brian Boitano were both favorites to win the competition. Boitano won it in a very close call over Orser.
