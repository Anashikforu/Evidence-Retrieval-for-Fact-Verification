'''Popol Vuh''' (Quiché for "Council Book" or "Book of the Community"; Popol Wuj in modern spelling) is the book of scripture of the Quiché, a kingdom of the post classic Maya civilization in highland Guatemala.

''Popol Vuh'' may also refer to:

'''Music'''
Popol Vuh (German band), a 1970s German cosmic music band
Popol Vuh (Norwegian band), a Norwegian progressive rock band that later changed its name to ''Popol Ace'' to avoid confusion
