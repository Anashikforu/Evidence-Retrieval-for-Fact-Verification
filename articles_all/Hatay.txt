'''Hatay''' is a city in the southern part of Turkey, on the Mediterranean coast. Hatay is bordered by Syria on the south and east.

In ancient times the city was called Antioch. It joined Turkey in 1939.  From 1938 to 1939, the city was independent as Hatay State.
