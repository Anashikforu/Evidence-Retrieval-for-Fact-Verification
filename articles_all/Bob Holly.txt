

'''Robert William "Bob" Howard''' (born January 29, 1963) better known by his ring name '''Hardcore Holly''', is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is best known for his 15-year job he had with  World Wrestling Entertainment.

While in World Wrestling Entertainment|WWF/E, Holly has held the WWE Hardcore Championship|WWF/E Hardcore Championship six times, the World Tag Team Championship (WWE)|WWF/E Tag Team Championship three times (with the Sean Waltman|1-2-3 Kid, Mike Lockwood|Crash Holly and Cody Rhodes), and the NWA World Tag Team Championship once with Mike Barton|Bart Gunn.
