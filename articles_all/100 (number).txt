'''100''' (also written as '''one hundred''') is the natural number after 99 (number)|99 and before 101 (number)|101. It is the first three-digit number above zero. The number 100 is an abundant number. The letter C is the old Roman numeral for 100.

Usage
100&nbsp;m running competition.
In binary and bcd -code, 100 means number 4 (number)|four.
