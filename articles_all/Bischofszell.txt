

'''Bischofszell''' is a municipality of the district of Bischofszell (district)|Bischofszell in the cantons of Switzerland|canton of Thurgau in the country of Switzerland.  It has a population of 5,500 people.

The local industry is Bischofszell Konserven (now belonging to Migros, makes jams, fruit juices, various cans, convenience food, among them Anna's Best)).
