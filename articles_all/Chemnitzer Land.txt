
'''Chemnitzer Land''' was a Districts in Saxony|district in the Saxony|Free State of Saxony, Germany. It ended in 2008.

The district has a very big population because the East Germany|East German government tried to make the area very Industry|industrial.

The district was created in 1994 by joining the old districts of Glauchau and Hohenstein-Ernstthal. In 2008, part of city of Zwickau will become part of the district. The rest of the city will become part of Zwickauer Land.
