


'''''Kirby & the Amazing Mirror''''' is a ''Kirby (series)|Kirby'' video game released in 2004. It is for the Game Boy Advance. It is ''Kirby'''s last game for the system. It is the only ''Kirby'' title where Kirby's enemy King Dedede does not make an appearance. The game has been re-released in Europe for Club Nintendo members only.
