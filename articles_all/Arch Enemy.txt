

'''Arch Enemy''' is a death metal band from Sweden. They started in 1996. The members are: Alissa White-Gluz (vocals), Michael Amott (guitar, vocals), Jeff Loomis (guitar), Sharlee D'Angelo, (Bass guitar|bass) Daniel Erlandsson (drums).
