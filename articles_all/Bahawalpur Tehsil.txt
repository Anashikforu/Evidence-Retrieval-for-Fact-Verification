'''Bahawalpur Tehsil''' (also Bhawalpur or Bhawulpore) () is a city of (1998 pop. 403,408) in Bahawalpur District, Punjab Pakistan. Seraiki is the local language of the area. Urdu and English language|English are also spoken by the people. Bahawalpur is located south of the Sutlej River and it lies in the Cholistan region. It is situated 90&nbsp;km from Multan, 420&nbsp;km from Lahore and about 700&nbsp;km from the national capital Islamabad. It was the capital of the former princely state of '''Bahawalpur'''.
