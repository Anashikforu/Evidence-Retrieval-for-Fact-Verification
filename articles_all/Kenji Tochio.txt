Biography
Tochio was born on May 26, 1941. After graduating from Fujieda Higashi High School, he joined JEF United Chiba|Furukawa Electric. In 1965, Furukawa Electric joined new league Japan Soccer League. He retired in 1970. He played 61 games and scored 1 goal in the league.

On May 28, 1961, Tochio debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Malaysia national football team|Malaya. He played 2 games for Japan in 1961.
