'''Munmu of Silla''' (). 

He was the son of King Muyeol of Silla.  Before his father died, he sought an alliance with Tang Dynasty|Tang China. In 660, with the help of the Tang army, he and Kim Yushin conquered Baekje. Japan sent reinforcements for Baekje.



Munmu was with the Tang army at the attack of Koguryo and the destruction of it in 668.
