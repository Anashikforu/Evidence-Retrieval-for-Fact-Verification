
'''Milo Anthony Ventimiglia''' (born July 8, 1977) is an Americans|American actor. He is best known for playing the hero, Peter Petrelli, on the television series, ''Heroes (TV series)|Heroes''. He played Jess Mariano on the television series Gilmore Girls.  He was born in Anaheim, California|Anaheim, California.
