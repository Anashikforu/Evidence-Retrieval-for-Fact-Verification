'''Madeline L'Engle''' (November 29, 1918) was an Americans|American writer. Her book ''A Wrinkle in Time'' won the Newbery Medal. ''A Wrinkle in Time'' was made into a movie in 2003.

L'Engle was born in 1918 in New York City, New York. She died at her home in Connecticut on September 6, 2007.
