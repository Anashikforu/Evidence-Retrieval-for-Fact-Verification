A '''marching band''' is a band of musical instrument|instrumental musicians who march while playing instruments.

They combine movement – usually some type of marching and other movements – with their musical performance. Its instruments typically are brass instruments, woodwind instrument|woodwinds, and percussion instruments.  The music usually has a strong rhythmic component suitable for marching.  Marching bands use a uniform, often bearing the name and emblem of their organization.  Many bands have a color guard, who perform with flags and other props. 

In addition to traditional parade performances, many bands also perform field shows at special events (such as American football|football games) or at competitions.
