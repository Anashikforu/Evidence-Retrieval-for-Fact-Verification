
'''Don Adams''' (April 13, 1923 – September 25, 2005), born '''Donald James Yarmy''', was an Americans|American actor, comedian and director. He was best known for his role as Maxwell Smart (Agent 86) in the TV situation comedy ''Get Smart'' (1965–1970, 1995), for which he also directed and wrote. Adams won three consecutive Emmy Awards for his role (1967–1969). He was also famous for voicing the cartoon characters Tennessee Tuxedo and Inspector Gadget.
