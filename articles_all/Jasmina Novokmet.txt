'''Jasmina Novokmet''' (born 8 September 1969 in Pristina, Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia|Yugoslavia) is a Serbian Conductor (music)|conductor, professor of conducting and former associate dean at the Faculty of Arts (Priština)|Faculty of Arts of Priština.  She is a former student of Jovan Šajnović.
