
'''Backlash (2006)''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view List of WWE pay-per-view events|event produced by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It was held on April 30, 2006 at the Rupp Arena in Lexington, Kentucky. It was the eighth annual Backlash PPV. The pay-per-view received 273,000 more buys then last year's Backlash pay-per-view.
