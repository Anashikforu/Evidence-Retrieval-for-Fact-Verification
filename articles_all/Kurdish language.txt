
The '''Kurdish language'''  (Kurdish: kurd√Æ) is a language spoken by the Kurdish people in an area called Kurdistan, including by Kurdish people in parts of the countries Iran, Iraq, Syria and Turkey. Kurdish has two main dialects: Kurmanji and Sorani. It belongs to the same language group as the Iranian languages. Another well-known Iranian language is Persian language|Persian. It is considered an Indo-European language.
