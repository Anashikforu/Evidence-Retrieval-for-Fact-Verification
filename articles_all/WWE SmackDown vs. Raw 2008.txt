


'''''WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2008''''' is a wrestling video game that was published by THQ. The video game was released for the PlayStation 3, PlayStation 2, PlayStation Portable, Nintendo DS, Wii and Xbox 360 systems. The video game was also released as a cell phone|mobile game. The video game was released on November 9, 2007 in Europe and on November 13, 2007 in both North America and Australia The game was also released on November 9, 2007 in Europe, November 13, 2007 in North America and on November 22, 2007 in Australia for the PSP.

The game was the first of the SmackDown vs. Raw games to feature the newly revived ECW (WWE)|ECW brand.

It is part of the WWE (video game series)|WWE SmackDown vs. Raw video game series.
