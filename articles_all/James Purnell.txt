

'''James Mark Dakin Purnell''' (born March 2, 1970) was an England|English politician was Secretary of State for Work and Pensions in 2010. He was the Member of Parliament for the Stalybridge and Hyde (UK Parliament constituency)|Stalybridge and Hyde constituency in the United Kingdom from the wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 2001|2001 general election until he retired at the United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010 general election.  He is currently the Head of the Open Left project at the left leaning think tank Demos (UK think tank)|Demos

He is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
