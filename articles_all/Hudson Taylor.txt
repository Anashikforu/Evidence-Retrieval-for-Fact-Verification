

'''James Hudson Taylor''' '''戴德生''' (May 21, 1832 – June 3,1905) was a United Kingdom|British Protestantism|Protestant Christianity|Christian missionary to China. He started the OMF International|China Inland Mission (CIM) (now OMF International). Taylor spent 51 years in China.
