

'''Alexander Rae "Alec" Baldwin III''' (born April 3, 1958) is an Americans|American actor, comedian, movie producer, and killer.
Early life
Alec Baldwin was born in Amityville, New York and raised in Massapequa, New York.
2021 shooting incident

On October 21, 2021, Baldwin was filming on the set of Western movie ''Rust (upcoming movie)|Rust'' at the Bonanza City, New Mexico|Bonanza Creek Ranch in Santa Fe, New Mexico, when he fired a gun used as a prop, accidentally killing cinematographer Halyna Hutchins and wounding movie director Joel Souza. Investigations as to what happened, are ongoing.
