'''Camp Nou''' (also known as '''the Nou Camp''') is a soccer|football stadium used by FC Barcelona that was opened in 1957. It can hold 99,354 people, making it the largest stadium in Europe and the second largest List of football (soccer) stadiums by capacity|football stadium in the world. 

The stadium was used to host the 1982 FIFA World Cup and the 1992 Summer Olympics. It also hosted the 1999 UEFA Champions League Final between Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United and FC Bayern Munich|Bayern Munich.
