
'''Joel Norman Quenneville''' (born September 15, 1958 in Windsor, Ontario) is a retired Canadians|Canadian ice hockey player. He is the current head coach for the Florida Panthers of the National Hockey League|National Hockey League (NHL). He won the Stanley Cup when he was an assistant coach for the Colorado Avalanche in 1996. He became head coach of the Blackhawks in 2008. He replaced Denis Savard.
