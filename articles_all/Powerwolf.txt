

'''Powerwolf''' is a Germany|German power metal band formed in 2003 by the ex-members of the Rock music|rock band Red Aim. Powerwolf have released 7 albums: ''Return in Bloodred'' from 2005, ''Lupus Dei'' from 2007, ''Bible of the Beast'' from 2009, ''Blood of the Saints'' from 2011, ''Preachers of the Night'' from 2013, ''Blessed & Possessed'' from 2015, and ''The Sacrament of Sin'' from 2018. Their lyrics are based on Transylvanian werewolf and vampire legends.
