

'''Sulfurous acid''' is a weak acid with the chemical formula H2SO3. It is produced by dissolving sulfur dioxide in water. Base (chemistry)|Bases deprotonation|deprotonate (remove the Hydrogen ion) it to produce sulfites. It tends to turn back into sulfur dioxide and water again. It is a weak reducing agent.
