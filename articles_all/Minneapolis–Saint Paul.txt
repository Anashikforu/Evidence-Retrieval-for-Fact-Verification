

'''Minneapolis-Saint Paul''' is the most populous List of United States urban areas|urban area in the state of Minnesota, United States, and is made up of 186 cities and townships. Built around the Mississippi River|Mississippi, Minnesota River|Minnesota and St. Croix River (Wisconsin-Minnesota)|St. Croix rivers, the area is also nicknamed the '''Twin Cities''' for its two largest cities, Minneapolis, Minnesota|Minneapolis and Saint Paul, Minnesota|Saint Paul.
