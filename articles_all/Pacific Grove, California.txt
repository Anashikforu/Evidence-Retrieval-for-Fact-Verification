'''Pacific Grove''' is a coastal town in Monterey County, California, US. The total wikt:population|population was 15,522 in the 2000 census. Pacific Grove is wikt:location|located between Point Pinos and Monterey, California|Monterey. Its elevation is 151 feet (46 m).

Pacific Grove is known for its Victorian homes, Asilomar State Beach, its artistic legacy and the wikt:annual|annual migration of the Monarch butterfly|Monarch butterflies. The city has more Victorian architecture|Victorian houses per person than anywhere else in United States. Some of them have been turned into bed and breakfast inns.

Point Pinos Lighthouse, the oldest wikt:continuous|continuously working lighthouse on the West Coast, is in Pacific Grove.

John Denver died near Pacific Grove.
