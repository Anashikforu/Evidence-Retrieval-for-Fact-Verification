
'''Piedmont''' is a city in Calhoun County, Alabama|Calhoun and Cherokee County, Alabama|Cherokee counties in the U.S. state of Alabama.  The population was 5,120 at the United States Census, 2000|2000 census. It is included in the Anniston, Alabama|Anniston-Oxford, Alabama Metropolitan Statistical Area.

On March 27, 1994, a 1994 Palm Sunday tornado outbreak|F4 tornado struck here, destroying the Goshen United Methodist Church and killing 22 people including the groom's 4-year-old daughter.
