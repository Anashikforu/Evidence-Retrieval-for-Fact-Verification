


'''Tbilisi''' is the capital, and the largest city in Georgia (country)|Georgia. It was founded in the 5th century by the Georgian king, Vakhtang Gorgasali. According to legend, when he was hunting, he killed a pheasant which dropped into hot water. Gorgasali discovered there were a lot of hot Spring (water)|springs in this place, which he called Tbilisi as ''tbili'' means '''hot''' in Georgian.
