The climate of the province is a tropical climate, hot most of the year, but it is cooler on the mountains.

Economy
The main economic activity of the province is agriculture; the main products are coffee in the mountains, rice in the southern and eastern part of the province and Cacao tree|cacao in the low mountains and those parts of the valley close to the mountains. Vegetables and fruits are very important in the valleys in the mountains.

Other important economic activity is cattle raising. There are several Industry|industries, especially textiles and food, in La Vega.

Tourism has become a very important economic activity, mainly in Jarabacoa and Constanza.
