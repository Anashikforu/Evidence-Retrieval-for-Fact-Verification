



'''Heart of Midlothian Football Club''' (commonly known as ''Hearts'') is a professional Association football|football club based at Tynecastle|Tynecastle Stadium in Edinburgh, Scotland. They play in the Scottish Premier League along with their Edinburgh|city rivals Hibernian F.C.|Hibernian. Hearts were one of the teams invited to join the Scottish Football League when it first started in 1890. Hearts have won the Scottish Cup on 8 occasions, the League title 4 times and the League Cup 4 times.
