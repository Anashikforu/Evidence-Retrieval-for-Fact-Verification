
'''Graham John Yost''' (born September 5, 1959) is a Canadians|Canadian movie and television screenwriter. His most famous works are the 1994 movie ''Speed (movie)|Speed'', ''Broken Arrow'', and ''Hard Rain''. Yost did not write the wikt:dialog|dialog for ''Speed''. The dialog was written by Joss Whedon.

He has also written for the television programme|television series ''Herman's Head'', ''Band of Brothers'', and ''Boomtown (TV series)|Boomtown''. More recently, he is the creator of the NBC drama ''Raines'' starring Jeff Goldblum that premiered on March 15, 2007.

Yost won an Primetime Emmy Award|Emmy award for his part in the mini-series ''From the Earth to the Moon'' and for his involvement in ''The Pacific''. Graham also won a Golden Globe for his work on HBO miniseries, ''Band of Brothers''. Graham was one of the writers for the show.
