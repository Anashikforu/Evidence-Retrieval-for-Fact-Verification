
'''Björn Borg''' (born June 6, 1956) is a retirement|retired professional tennis player from Sweden. He won Wimbledon Championships|Wimbledon five years in a row, more than any other player until Roger Federer did the same. Borg also won six French Open titles, which was later also done by Rafael Nadal, who in the 21st century won nine titles.
Borg was famous for his unusual and exciting playing style. He also set many records.

Since retiring, Borg has run a clothing line. He has two sons, Robin (born 1985) and Leo (born 2003).

Trademark
'''Björn Borg''' has given the name to the fashion brand Björn Borg. Besides underwear, it includes other clothing, shoes, bags, glasses and perfume|fragrance for both men and women.
