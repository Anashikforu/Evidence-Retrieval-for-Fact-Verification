
The '''Kia Soul''' is a car produced by Kia Motors|Kia since 2008. It is based on the Hyundai i20 and Kia Venga's platform. It entered its second generation in 2013. 

The Soul is available with 1.6 and 2.0 litre petrol engines with 93 kW/126 hp and 106 kW/144 hp respectively, and a 1.6 litre turbocharger|turbo diesel engine with 94 kW/128 hp.
