

'''''Vampires Suck''''' is a 2010 United States|American parody movie directed and written by Jason Friedberg and Aaron Seltzer. It is a parody of Stephenie Meyer's series, ''Twilight (series)|Twilight''. The movie was be released in North America on August 8, 2010, August 26, 2010 in Australia and October 15, 2010 in the United Kingdom. ''Vampires Suck'' achieved $4 million at the box office.
