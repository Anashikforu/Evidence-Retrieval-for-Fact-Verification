'''Marco Camenisch''' (born 21 January 1952) is a Switzerland|Swiss anarchism|anarchist and environmentalism|environmental activist. He was born in Schiers. He served several years in prison for the 1989 murder of a border guard in Brusio, Switzerland and other offences.
Camenisch has maintained his politics|political ideas during his time in prison, and he has remained a political activist.
