
'''Kenneth Anderson''' (born March 6, 1976 in Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin|Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler who is currently wrestling for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling|TNA Wrestling under the ring name, '''Mr. Anderson''' and is the current reigning TNA World Heavyweight Champion. Anderson also wrestled for WWE from 2005 to 2009 as '''Mr. Kennedy''' before being released by the company because it was reported that Kennedy had injured his wrist during his last match with the company, and a stiff back suplex almost injured Randy Orton.
