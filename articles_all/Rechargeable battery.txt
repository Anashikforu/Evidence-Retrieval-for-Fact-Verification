A '''rechargeable battery''', also called  a '''storage battery''' or a '''secondary cell''', is a battery that can be recharged  over and over again with electricity. 

Rechargeable batteries are not always chosen.  Each battery costs more to make, and has more dangerous chemicals than disposable batteries.

Rechargeable batteries can be used for many smaller electronics such as MP3 players and flashlights.  Almost all mobile phones and laptop computers use them.  They are also used for Car battery|car batteries.
