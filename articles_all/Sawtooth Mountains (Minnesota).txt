The '''Sawtooth Mountains''' are low ridges on the North Shore (Lake Superior)|North Shore of Lake Superior in the U.S. state of Minnesota. They extend about 30 miles (50 km) from Carlton Peak near Tofte, Minnesota|Tofte on the west, to Grand Marais, Minnesota|Grand Marais on the east.Upham, pp. 3, 147.
