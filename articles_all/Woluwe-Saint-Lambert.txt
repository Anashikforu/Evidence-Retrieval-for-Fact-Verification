'''Woluwe-Saint-Lambert''' (in French language|French) or '''Sint-Lambrechts-Woluwe''' (in Dutch language|Dutch) is a municipality in Belgium, in the Brussels-Capital Region.

In 2007, 48315 people lived there.

It is at 50° 51 North, 04° 25 East.
