
'''Sarah Sutton''' (born 12 December 1961 in Basingstoke, Hampshire, England) is an English people|English actress. She is well known for her role as Nyssa in the BBC television series ''Doctor Who''.  Nyssa was a Companion (Doctor Who)|companion of Tom Baker and Peter Davison's Doctor (Doctor Who)|Doctors from 1981 to 1983. She was the youngest female actor to play a companion in the series.
