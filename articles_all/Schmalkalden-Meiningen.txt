
'''Schmalkalden-Meiningen''' is a ''Landkreis'' (Rural Districts of Germany|rural district) in the west of Thuringia, Germany.

 History 
The district was created in 1994 by joining the old districts of Schmalkalden, Meiningen and part of Suhl-Land.

 Partnerships 
The district has a twin town|partnership with the Vogelsbergkreis in Hesse, as well as with the Pest (county)|Komitat Pest in Hungary.
