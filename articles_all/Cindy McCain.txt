
'''Cindy Lou Hensley McCain''' (born May 20, 1954) is an Americans|American businesswoman and politician. McCain is the List of ambassadors of the United States to the United Nations Agencies for Food and Agriculture|U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Agencies for Food and Agriculture since 2021. She was the wife of Republican Senator John McCain. During the Donald Trump presidency, she became a critic of the president and the United States Republican Party|Republican Party.

In the 2020 United States presidential election|2020 presidential election, she was one of the few key Republicans who supported form Vice President of the United States|Vice President Joe Biden over President Trump.

In late November 2020, McCain was seen as a possible pick for an ambassador role in the Joe Biden|Biden administration.
