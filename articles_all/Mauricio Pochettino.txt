


'''Mauricio Roberto Pochettino Trossero''' (; born 2 March 1972) is a retired Argentina|Argentine Football (soccer)|football player and current manager of French club Paris Saint-Germain F.C.

In 2019, Pochettino led Tottenham Hotspur to the 2019 UEFA Champions League Final|UEFA Champions League Final after beating AFC Ajax in 2018–19 UEFA Champions League|the semi-final for the first time in the team's history. Tottenham later lost to Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool 1-0 in 2019 UEFA Champions League Final|the final.
