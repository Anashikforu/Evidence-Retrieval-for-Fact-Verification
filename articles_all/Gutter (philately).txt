
'''Gutter''' in wikt:philately|philately (stamp collecting) is a space between printed stamps. Gutters may separate sheet of stamps in panes. They may, or may not, have some printing. The gutter between the stamps is often the same size as the postage stamp.

There are other related wikt:philately|philatelic terms:
 '''Gutter pairs''' are two stamps separated by a gutter.
 '''Gutter block''' is a block of at least four stamps separated by a gutter.
 '''Gutter margin''' is a margin dividing a sheet of stamps into separate panes.
