



'''Atalanta Bergamasca Calcio''', often called '''Atalanta''' (nicknamed ''La Dea'', the ''Nerazzurri'', or the ''Orobici'') is a professional Association football|football club based in Bergamo, Lombardy, Italy. The club plays in Serie A ever since it got promoted from Serie B in 2010–11 Serie B|2010–11. They finished 3rd in the 2018–19 Serie A season, meaning they could play in the UEFA Champions League for the first time in the club's history.  They play at the Stadio Atleti Azzurri d'Italia|Gewiss Stadium, which has a capacity of 21,300. They have a long-standing rivalry with nearby club Brescia Calcio|Brescia.
