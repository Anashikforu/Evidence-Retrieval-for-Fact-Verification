
The '''Ford Taurus''' was a car produced by Ford Motor Company from 1986 through 2006. Ford ended production of the Taurus shortly after due to falling sales and increased demand for crossover models and SUV's.

Versions
The Ford Taurus has had several different changes to the way it was built. Each different version is called a ''Generation''.
