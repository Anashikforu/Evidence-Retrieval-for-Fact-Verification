The '''CONCACAF Champions League''' (originally called the '''CONCACAF Champions' Cup''') is a football (soccer)|football competition between the best club Football team|teams in North America|North and Central America and the Caribbean. The teams are selected by the Central American and Caribbean Association of Football (the CONCACAF) because of their good performances in matches.
