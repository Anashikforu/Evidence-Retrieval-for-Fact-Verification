
'''Tulsa''' is the second largest city in Oklahoma, United States. There are 387,807 people living in Tulsa. It is the 47th largest city in the United States. It is the county seat of Tulsa County, Oklahoma|Tulsa County; and the most densely populated county in Oklahoma. Tulsa has 563,299 people living in it.
