
'''"Bart Gets Hit by a Car"''' is the tenth episode of ''The Simpsons'' second season. The episode first started on January 10, 1991.

This episode has the first wikt:appearance|appearance of "Dr. Nick Riviera", "Lionel Hutz", and the "Blue-Haired Lawyer". Also, recurring (appearing in several episodes) guest star Phil Hartman and the show's then-script wikt:supervisor|supervisor Doris Grau made their first episode in the show.
