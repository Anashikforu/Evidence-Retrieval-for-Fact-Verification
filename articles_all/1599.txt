
The year '''1599''' was a common year which started on Friday.

 Events 
 January 8 &ndash; The Jesuit educational plan known as the ''Ratio Studiorum'' is issued.
 June 20 &ndash; The Synod of Diamper is convened.
 August 15 &ndash; First Battle of Curlew Pass: Ireland|Irish forces defeat the English.
 September 21 &ndash; The first reported performance of William Shakespeare|Shakespeare's ''Julius Caesar (play)|Julius Caesar'' at the Globe Theatre in London, is recorded by Swiss traveller Thomas Platter the Younger.
  October 18 &ndash; Battle of Sellenberk: Michael the Brave, Prince of Wallachia, defeats the army of Andrew Báthory near Șelimbăr, leading to the first recorded uniting of the Romanians.
