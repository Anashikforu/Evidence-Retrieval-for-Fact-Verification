'''Treptower Tollensewinkel''' was an ''Amt (political division)|Amt'' in the former Demmin Rural District|district of Demmin. Since 4 September 2011, it is an Amt in the Mecklenburgische Seenplatte Rural District. Both districts were/are in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Germany.
