
'''''A Song of Ice and Fire''''' is an award-winning series of fantasy novels written by George R. R. Martin. He began writing the series in 1991. Five books were released in the series and two were unfinished. It was translated into over twenty languages and is a best-seller, with the fourth volume reaching the top of the ''New York Times'' bestseller lists when it came out.
