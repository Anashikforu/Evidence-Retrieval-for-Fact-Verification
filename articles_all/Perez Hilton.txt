
'''Mario Armando Lavandeira, Jr.''' (born March 23, 1978), known as '''Perez Hilton''', is an Americans|American blogger and television presenter. He writes and gossips about musicians, actors and celebrities. His name is a play on the name "Paris Hilton".
Hilton was born in Miami to Cuban parents.

He was a housemate in the January 2015 series of Celebrity Big Brother (UK).

Personal life
Hilton is coming out|openly gay.
Hilton is a Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat and attended the 2012 Democratic National Convention in support of Barack Obama|President Obama's re-election.
