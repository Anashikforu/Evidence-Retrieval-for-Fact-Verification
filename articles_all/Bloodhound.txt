

The '''Bloodhound''' is a large breed of dog famous for its wikt:tracking|tracking ability by scent. It stands 23–27 inches (58–69 cm) tall and weighs 80–110 Pound (mass)|pounds (36–50&nbsp;kg). It has a long nose, large ears with Wrinkle (skin)|wrinkles on its face. Its fur is short and its coat (dog)|coat is usually a brown and black color. Bloodhounds are known to be a gentle and affectionate breed. This breed lives an average of six years and nine months.
