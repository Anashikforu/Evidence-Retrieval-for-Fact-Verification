

'''Zhang Ziyi''' (, born February 9, 1979) is a China|Chinese movie actress.  She is best known for her work in award-winning movies such as ''Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon'' (2000), ''Rush Hour 2'' (2001), ''Hero'' (2002), ''House of Flying Daggers'' (2004), ''2046'' (2004) and ''Memoirs of a Geisha'' (2005).
