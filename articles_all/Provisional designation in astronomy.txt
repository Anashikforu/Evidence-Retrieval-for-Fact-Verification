A '''provisional designation in astronomy''' is the naming wikt:convention|convention of astronomical objects as soon as they are found. The wikt:provisional|provisional designation is usually replaced by a wikt:permanent|permanent name once a good orbit has been Calculation|calculated. With asteroids, so many have been found that many will never be named by the people who found them.
