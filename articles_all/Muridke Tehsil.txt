

'''Muridke Tehsil''' is an administration|administrative subdivision (tehsil) of Sheikhupura District, Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab, Pakistan. The tehsil was formerly part of Ferozewala Tehsil until 2005, when it was separated from Ferozewal and became another of Sheikhupura's tehsils. It is headquarters|headquartered at the city of Muridke. Besides various villages it includes the town of Narang Mandi.
The 'Ravi Rayon' factory  complex is very famous industry in Muridke.
Rice Reasurch Institute is popular place in Kala Shah Kaku.
