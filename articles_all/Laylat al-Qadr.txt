

'''Laylat al-Qadr) (also known as '''Shab-e-Qadr'''), basically  the '''Night of Decree''' or '''Night of Measures''', is the anniversary of two very Important Cultural Properties of Japan|important dates in Islam. They occurred in the Ramadan (calendar month)|month of Ramadan. It is the anniversary of the night Muslims believe the first verses of the Qur'an were revealed to the Islamic prophet Muhammad.
