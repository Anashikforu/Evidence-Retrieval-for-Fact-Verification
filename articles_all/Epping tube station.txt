
'''Epping''' on the London Underground is the north-eastern terminus of the Central Line.  The station before Epping is Theydon Bois tube station|Theydon Bois. Epping station is in the Epping Forest (district)|Epping Forest District of Essex. It is one of eight London Underground stations in this District and is in Travelcard Zone 6.
