
'''Kemmental''' is a municipality of the district of Kreuzlingen (district)|Kreuzlingen in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Thurgau in Switzerland.

History
The municipality was created on January 1, 1996 by mergxer of the formar municipalities of Alterswilen, Altishausen, Ellighausen, Lippoldswilen, Neuwilen, Siegershausen, Dotnacht and Hugelshofen.
