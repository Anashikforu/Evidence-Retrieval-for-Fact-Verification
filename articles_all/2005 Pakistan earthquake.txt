

The '''2005 Kashmir Earthquake''' (also known as the '''Great Pakistan earthquake''') was a major earthquake epicenter|centered in Pakistan-administered Kashmir and in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa near the city of Muzaffarabad. It occurred at 08:52:37 Pakistan Standard Time (03:52:37 Coordinated Universal Time|UTC) on 8 October 2002

87,351 peoples died, 75,266 peoples injuried, and 2.4 million people were left homeless. Kashmir, Pakistan, and Southern part of India were all affected.
