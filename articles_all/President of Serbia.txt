

The '''President of Serbia''' is the head of state of the Serbia|Republic of Serbia. Prior to the title of President, head of state which King of Serbia|ruled Serbia was called a King. Presently serving as the head of state is President of Serbia, '''Aleksandar Vučić'''. President Tadic was elected for a second term with a 50.31% majority in the Serbian presidential election, 2008|2008 Serbian presidential election.
