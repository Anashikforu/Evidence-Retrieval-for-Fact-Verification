
'''Rachael Ray''' (born August 25, 1968 in Glen Falls, New York) is an Emmy Award-winning television personality and author. Her family roots are from Sicily, Italy. Her TV shows include ''30 minute Meals'', ''$40 a Day'', ''Rachael Ray's Tasty Travels'' and ''The Rachael Ray Show in September 2006 First Episode in Rachael Ray''. In September 2010, ''Rachael Ray's Week In a Day'' began airing on the Cooking Channel. She has written 12 cookbooks.
