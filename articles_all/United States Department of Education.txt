
The '''United States Department of Education''' (also referred to as '''ED''', for Education Department) is a United States Cabinet|Cabinet-level department of the United States government of the United States|government. Created by the Department of Education Organization Act (Public Law 96-88), it was signed into law by President Jimmy Carter on October 17, 1979 and began operating on May 4, 1980.
