

'''Montsevelier''' is a former municipality of the district of Delémont (district)|Delémont in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Montsevelier, Vermes, Switzerland|Vermes and Vicques, Switzerland|Vicques merged into the new municipality of Val Terbi.
