
'''No Way Out''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view event made by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It took place on February 15, 2004 at the Cow Palace in Daly City, California|Daly City, California. It was the 5th No Way Out event. Seven matches were scheduled on the event's Card (sports)|card. The main event between Eddie Guerrero and Brock Lesnar was quoted by Kevin Sowers from PWTorch  as being ''"one to remember for a long time"''.
