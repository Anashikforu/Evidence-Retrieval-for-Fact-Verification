

'''Whitchurch''' is a small market town in the north of the county of Shropshire, England. It is the oldest town inhabited in Shropshire.  The town lies 2 miles (3 km) east of the Wales|Welsh border. As of 2011, the town has a population of 9,781. 

The town was founded by the Ancient Rome|Romans and there are still some Roman artefact|artefacts that can be visited in a Museum.

The town has a Whitchurch railway station (Shropshire)|railway station on the Welsh Marches Line.
