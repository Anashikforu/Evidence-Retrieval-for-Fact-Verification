
'''Coral''' is a reddish or orangeish shade of Pink (color)|pink.  The color is named after the sea animal also called corals.

The first written use of ''coral'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1513. Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930--McGraw Hill Page 193; Color Sample of Coral: Page 27 Plate 2 Color Sample J10 

 Meaning of coral 

 Coral and coral blue color for interior design|painting a room to give a calm feeling of warmth.

 In some sects of Hinduism, coral red instead of red is shown as being the color which represents the first (''muladhara'') chakra in Hindu philosophy.
