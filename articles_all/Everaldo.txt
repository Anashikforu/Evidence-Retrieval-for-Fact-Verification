
'''Everaldo''' (11 September 1944 – 28 October 1974) is a former Brazilian Football (soccer)|football player. He has played for Esporte Clube Juventude|Juventude, Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense|Grêmio and Brazil national football team|Brazil national team. He is regarded as one of the best left-backs of all time.
