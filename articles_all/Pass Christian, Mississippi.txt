
'''Pass Christian'''
   The name ''Pass Christian'' is pronounced, in the French
   manner, as "pass kris-CHAN" or "pass kris-chee-ANN"
   (rather than "pass KRIS-chin" as in ''Christianity'').
   It is often mentioned in radio or television weather
   bulletins for hurricane advisories along the U.S. Gulf Coast.
 is a city in Harrison County, Mississippi|Harrison County, Mississippi, United States, along the Gulf of Mexico.  It is part of the Gulfport-Biloxi metropolitan area|Gulfport-Biloxi, Mississippi Metropolitan Statistical Area. The population was 4,613 at the 2010 census.
