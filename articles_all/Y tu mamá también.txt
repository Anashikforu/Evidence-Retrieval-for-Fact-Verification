
'''''Y tu mamá también''''' (''And your mother too'') is a 2001 award-winning Mexico|Mexican road movie. It was movie director|directed by Alfonso Cuarón and written by him and his brother Carlos Cuarón.

It was released without a movie ratings|rating in the United States because the movie's distributors believed a market-limiting NC-17 was unavoidable. This movie was released during 2002 in the US.
