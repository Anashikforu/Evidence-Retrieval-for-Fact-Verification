
'''Damien Varley''' (born 29 October 1983 in Limerick) is an Ireland|Irish rugby union player for Munster Rugby|Munster in the Pro 12 and Heineken Cup. He plays as a Hooker (rugby union)|Hooker. Varley plays his club rugby with Garryowen Football Club|Garryowen.  Before he played for the London Wasps.
