
'''Robert Peter Bodak''' (born May 28, 1961 in Thunder Bay, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey player who played in the National Hockey League for the Calgary Flames and Hartford Whalers.  A career minor-leaguer, Bodak played only three games with the Flames in 1987–88 NHL season|1987–88 and one more with the Whalers in 1989–90 NHL season|1989–90.  He did not record a point, but had 29 penalty minutes in his four games.
