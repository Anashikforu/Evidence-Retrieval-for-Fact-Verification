

'''Hòa thượng Thích Quảng Đức''' (1897–June 11, 1963) was a Buddhism|Buddhist Monk from Hội Khánh, French Indochina who committed suicide by Self-immolation|burning himself to death while sitting in an intersection (crossroads). He was protesting the Buddhist crisis|persecution of Buddhists by South Vietnam's Ngô Đình Diệm administration.
