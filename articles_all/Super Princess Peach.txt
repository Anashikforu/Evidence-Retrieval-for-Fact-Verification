


'''''Super Princess Peach''''' (スーパープリンセスピーチ in japan|Japanese) is a 2005 Platform game|platform video game. It was developed by TOSE and published by Nintendo. In 2009, this game was re-released as a Nintendo Selects title. It was released for the Nintendo DS.

Unlike most ''Mario (series)|Mario'' video games, Bowser has decided to kidnap Mario. The player takes control of Princess Peach|Peach who must go out and save Mario.
