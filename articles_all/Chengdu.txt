
'''Chengdu''' (also called The Hibiscus City), is the fourth largest city in the  People's Republic of China. It is the capital of Sichuan province and is on top of the Chengdu Plain. The city is home to the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding. The city has a famous food culture, so much so that UNESCO awarded the city as a City of Gastronomy. Chengdu is known for hot pot and Teahouse|teahouses and for adding Chili pepper|chili peppers in much of their food.
