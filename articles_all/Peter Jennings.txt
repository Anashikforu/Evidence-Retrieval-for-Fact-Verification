
'''Peter Jennings''' (29 July 1938 &ndash; 7 August 2005) was a Canadians|Canadian journalist, born in Toronto, Ontario. 

He was a journalist and news anchor for ''World News Tonight'' on ABC News|ABC between 1983 and 2005. 

He died in Manhattan, New York City, in 2005 of complications from lung cancer.
