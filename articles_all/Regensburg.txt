



'''Regensburg''' (German pronunciation: Help:IPA/Standard German|ʁɛ:gənsbʊɐ̯k; Czech language|Czech: ''Řezno'', Austro-Bavarian: Help:IPA|ʁɛŋsbʊɐ̯k; ''Rengschburg'' or ''Rengschbuach'' Help:IPA|ʁɛŋsbʊax) is a city in the south of Germany, in the east of the state of Bavaria.
It has about 138,000 inhabitants and a university.

Important people from Regensburg
Konrad of Megenberg died in the city in 1374.
