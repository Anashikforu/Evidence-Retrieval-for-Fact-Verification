
'''Joseph Steven Sakic''' (born July 7, 1969 in Burnaby, British Columbia) is a Canadians|Canadian retired ice hockey Centre (ice hockey)|centre Sakic played 20 seasons in the National Hockey League (NHL). He played with the Quebec Nordiques/Colorado Avalanche organizations. Sakic was able to win the Stanley Cup in 1995–96 NHL season|1996 and 2000–01 NHL season|2001.

He retired from playing professional ice hockey on July 9, 2009.

He was a fan favourite in his hometown of Burnaby and even had a street named "Joe Sakic Way" in his honour.
