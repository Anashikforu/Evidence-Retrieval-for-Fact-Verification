

'''Dr. Fahmida Mirza''' (), sometimes also spelled as '''Fehmida Mirza''' (born December 20, 1956), is a medical doctor, agriculture|agriculturist and businesswoman from Hyderabad, Sindh|Hyderabad, Sindh, Pakistan. She was elected as the first female Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan|Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan on March 19, 2008. She is also the first female parliamentary speaker in the Muslim world.
