

'''''Mario Kart: Super Circuit''''' is a Game Boy Advance racing game released in 2001 and is the third game in the Mario Kart|''Mario Kart'' series. It was later released in 2011 for the ''Nintendo 3DS|3DS'' Virtual Console and the Wii U Virtual Console in 2014. It has playable characters from the Mario (series)|''Mario'' series. The game contains all the courses from ''Super Mario Kart'' along with newcoming courses. It can be played single player (modes: Grand Prix, Extra Super Mario Kart, Quick Run, and Time Trials) multiplayer (modes: Grand Prix, "Link It Up!", V.S., and Battle)
