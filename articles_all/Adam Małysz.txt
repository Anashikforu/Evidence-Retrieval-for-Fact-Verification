

'''Adam Henryk Małysz''' (born 3 December 1977 in Wisła, Poland) is a Poland|Polish former ski jumping|ski jumper. He has four Olympic Games|Olympic medals (3 silver, 1 bronze). His longest jump was 230,5 meters. Since 2011 he is a rally driver.
