
'''Saint Ursanne''' (population about 1,000) is a city and was a municipality, in the new municipality of Clos du Doubs and Porrentruy (district)|district of Porrentruy in the canton of Jura, Switzerland which has kept its medieval style.

Its name refers to Ursicinus of Saint-Ursanne|Saint Ursicinus, a 7th century monk who made a monastery there.
