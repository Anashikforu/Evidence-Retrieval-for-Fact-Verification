
'''Jan Peter Balkenende''' (born 7 May 1956) is a Dutch people|Dutch politician. He was the Prime Minister of the Netherlands from 2002 until 2010. He is a member of the Christian Democratic Appeal political party|party.

In other country|countries, he is perhaps most famous because he looks like Harry Potter.
Balkenende lives in Capelle aan den IJssel, Netherlands.
