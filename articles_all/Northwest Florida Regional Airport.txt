'''Northwest Florida Regional Airport''' (airport code '''KVPS''') serves the city of Fort Walton Beach, Florida.

The following Airlines serve Northwest Florida Regional Airport: American Airlines

 Facilities and aircraft 

Northwest Florida Regional Airport and Eglin Air Force Base share two runways: 12/30 with a 12,005 x 300&nbsp;ft (3,659 x 91&nbsp;m) asphalt/concrete surface and 1/19 with a 10,012 x 300&nbsp;ft (3,052 x 91&nbsp;m) asphalt pavement. For the 12-month period ending March 31, 2006, the airport had 126,060 aircraft operations, an average of 345 per day: 71% military aviation|military, 19% airline|scheduled commercial, 8% general aviation and 2% air taxi.

The airport has a 110,000 sq. ft. airport terminal|airport passenger terminal building. It has three second-level gates with passenger jet ways and three ground-level commuter gates with regional jet boarding bridges
