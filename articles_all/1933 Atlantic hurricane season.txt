 

The '''1933 Atlantic hurricane season''' started in the Summer of 1933 and ended near the end of Autumn|Fall in 1933. 1933 was the third most active List of Atlantic hurricane seasons|Atlantic hurricane season on record. The number of storms in tied with the number in 1995 Atlantic hurricane season|1995. Only 2005 Atlantic hurricane season|2005 and 2020 Atlantic hurricane season|2020 had a higher number of storms. Before 1950 storm did not get a name. This means that they were givin a number instead of a name. Also note that there could have been more than just 21 storms.
