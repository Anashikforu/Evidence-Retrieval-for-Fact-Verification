


'''Oxfam Internationa''' is a group of 14 organisations working with over 3,000 partners in around 100 countries to find lasting solutions to poverty and injustice.
Oxfam began in Oxford in 1942 as the '''Ox'''ford Committee for Famine|'''Fam'''ine Relief. It was started by a group of Quakers (which included Marcus Tite), activism|social activists, and Oxford academics.
The first overseas Oxfam began in Canada in 1963. The committee changed its name to its telegraph address, '''OXFAM''', in 1965.
