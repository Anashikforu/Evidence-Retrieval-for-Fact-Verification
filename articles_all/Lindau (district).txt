
'''Lindau (district)''' is a rural district in Swabia (Government Region)|Swabia in southwest Bavaria, Germany. Neighbor districts are: Oberallgäu and in Baden-Württemberg: Ravensburg (district) and the Bodensee (district); In the south of the district are the Austrian state Vorarlberg and Lake Constance. Lindau is the capital (city)|capital of Lindau (district).
