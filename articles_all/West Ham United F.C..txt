
'''West Ham United Football Club''' is an England|English Association football|football club.

The club plays in the English Premier League|Premier League. It was started in 1895 and its home stadium is the London stadium East London. The club is also called "the Hammers" and "the Irons" because it was started in a London Ironworks. Their main rivals are Millwall F.C.|Millwall and other rivals are Tottenham Hotspur F.C.|Tottenham Hotspur, Chelsea F.C.|Chelsea and Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal 

West ham currently are managed by David Moyes, their former care-taker manager for the 2017/18 season in Premier League.
