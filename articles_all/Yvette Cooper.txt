
'''Yvette Cooper''' (born 20 March 1969) is a British people|British politician.

Cooper is the Member of Parliament (MP) for the Normanton, Pontefract and Castleford (UK Parliament constituency)|Normanton, Pontefract and Castleford constituency in the United Kingdom. She is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party. Her husband, Ed Balls, represents the neighbouring constituency of Morley and Outwood (UK Parliament constituency)|Morley and Outwood.

She served in the Cabinet of the United Kingdom|Cabinet between 2008 and 2010 under Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister Gordon Brown. She was Chief Secretary to the Treasury and then Secretary of State for Work and Pensions. After Labour lost the United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010 general election, Cooper was appointed Shadow cabinet|Shadow Foreign Secretary, then in 2011 Shadow Home Secretary.
