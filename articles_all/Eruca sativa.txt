

'''''Eruca sativa''''' (syn. ''E. vesicaria'' subsp. ''sativa'' (Miller) Thell., ''Brassica eruca'' L.), also known as '''arugula''' or '''rocket''', is a plant that can be Eating|eaten. It is a leaf vegetable with dark green, spiky, dandelion-like leaves and a strong, spicy, Black pepper|peppery flavor.
