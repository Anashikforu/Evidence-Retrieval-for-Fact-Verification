
'''Wexford''' (coming from Old Norse ''Veisafjǫrðr'' meaning "mudflat, stagnant pool", also Irish language|Irish: ''Loch Garman'', Latin: ''Menapia'') is the county town of County Wexford in the Republic of Ireland. It is found near the south-eastern tip of Ireland, close to Rosslare Europort. 

The town is connected to the capital (city)|capital, Dublin, via the N11 road|N11 Roads in Ireland#National Primary Routes|National Primary Route (European route E1), and the rail transport in Ireland|national rail network. It also has a major ferry port with modern terminal, Rosslare Europort. The port is located in South East Wexford.
