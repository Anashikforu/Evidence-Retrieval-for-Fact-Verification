
'''Deval Laurdine Patrick''' (born July 31, 1956) is an American politician. He was elected to be the governor of Massachusetts in 2006. He is the first African Americans|African-American to be elected governor of that state. He is only the third African-American to be elected a governor of any state in the United States. Patrick ran as a Democrat.

He was born in Chicago, Illinois on 31 July 1956, is a lawyer, and holds two degrees from Harvard University.

On November 11, 2019, ''The New York Times'' reported that Patrick was thinking of a 2020 United States presidential election|2020 President of the United States|presidential candidacy.
