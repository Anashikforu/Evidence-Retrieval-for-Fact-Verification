'''Simon John Pegg''' (né Beckingham, born 14 February 1970) is an English people|English actor, comedian, film director|director, film producer|producer and screenwriter. His best known roles are ''Spaced'', ''Shaun of the Dead'', Hot Fuzz, ''Paul (movie)|Paul'' and the Ice Age (franchise)|Ice Age franchise. He is also known for working with Nick Frost.

Personal life
Pegg married Maureen McCann on 23 July 2005. On 21 February 2009, Pegg said his wife was five months pregnant with the couple's first child. Pegg has said on his personal Twitter feed that he is an Atheism|atheist.
Along with Jonny Buckland, Pegg is godfather to Apple, daughter of close friends Chris Martin and Gwyneth Paltrow. Pegg's parents and sister briefly appeared in ''Spaced''. His mother was in both ''Shaun of the Dead'' and ''Hot Fuzz''.

Pegg was awarded an honorary fellowship by the University of Gloucestershire on 4 December 2008.
