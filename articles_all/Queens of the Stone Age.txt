
'''Queens of the Stone Age''' are a rock music|rock band from California, USA. They formed in 1997. The band has released five studio albums, three extended plays (EP), fifteen single (music)|singles and one live/music video|video album. Between 2003 and 2008, they were wikt:nominate|nominated for the Grammy Award four times.
