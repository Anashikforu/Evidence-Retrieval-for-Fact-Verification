
'''Hauteville-Lompnes''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes in the Ain Departments of France|department  in the east of France.

It is about 20&nbsp;km east of Ambérieu-en-Bugey and 38&nbsp;km southeast of Bourg-en-Bresse.

On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of Plateau d'Hauteville.
