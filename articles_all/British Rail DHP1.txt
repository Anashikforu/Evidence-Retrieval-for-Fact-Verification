
'''DHP1''', meaning '''D'''iesel '''H'''ydraulic '''P'''rototype number '''1''', was a prototype '''Type 3''' mainline diesel locomotive built in 1965 by Clayton to demonstrate their wares to British Railways. It was designed for mixed traffic work, having steam heating facilities for working passenger trains. It was painted red, with the cab area above bonnet-level height painted cream.
