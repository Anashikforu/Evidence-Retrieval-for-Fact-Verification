
'''Nelson Frazier, Jr.''' (February 14, 1971 â€“ February 18, 2014) was an Americans|American professional wrestler.  He is best known for wrestling with World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation/World Wrestling Entertainment under the ring names, '''Mabel''', '''Viscera''', '''Big Daddy V''', and '''King V'''. He wrestles for All Japan Pro Wrestling under the ring name, '''Big Daddy V''' and '''Big Daddy Voodoo'''. He was forced to retire from WWE for good as WWE did not want to be responsible for his health problems.
 
Frazier was in the 2009 movie ''The Legend of Awesomest Maximus'' co-starring
Will Sasso and Rip Torn.

On the night of February 18, 2014, it was reported that Frazier had died following a myocardial infarction|heart attack, four days after turning 43.
