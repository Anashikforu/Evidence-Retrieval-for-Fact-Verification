
The '''Chow Chow''' is a breed of dog that wikt:origin|originated in China and is believed to be one of the oldest dog breed. It has a large head, a curly tail and a blue tongue. It has a thick coat (dog)|coat of fur and can be either wikt:rough|rough or wikt:smooth|smooth. Most Chow Chows are a reddish brown or black color. It stands about 17–20 inches (43–51&nbsp;cm) and weighs 45–70 Pound (mass)|pounds (20–32&nbsp;kg).
