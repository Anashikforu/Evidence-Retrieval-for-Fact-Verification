
'''Walloon''' is a Romance language. It belongs to the family of the ''Langues d'oïl|oïl languages'' along with French language|French, Picard language|Picard and Norman language|Norman.

Walloon is spoken in Wallonia in Belgium and in some villages in Wisconsin in the United States.

It has about 3,400,000 speakers.
