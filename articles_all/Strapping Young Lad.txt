
'''Strapping Young Lad''' was a Heavy metal music|metal band from Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. They played extreme metal. These people played in the band: Devin Townsend (guitar, vocals), Jed Simon (guitar, vocals), Byron Stoud (Bass guitar|bass, vocals), Gene Hoglan (drums). The band went on an extended hiatus and would eventually disband in 2007.
