

In Hinduism, '''Kali Yuga''' (Devanāgarī: , lit. "Age of Kali (demon)|Kali", "age of vice"), is one of the four stages or yugas that the world goes through.According to the ''Surya Siddhanta'', Kali Yuga began at midnight (00:00) on 18 February 4th millennium BCE|3102 BCE in the Julian calendar or 23 January 4th millennium BCE|3102 BCE in the Gregorian calendar. Traditionally, most Hindus believe that the Kali Yuga is 432,000 years long. Kali Yuga is also recognized and stated in Sikhism, in the Guru Granth Sahib.
