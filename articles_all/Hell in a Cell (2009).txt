

'''Hell in a Cell''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) List of WWE pay-per-view events|event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), which took place on October 4, 2009 at the Prudential Center in Newark, New Jersey. It was the first event in the WWE Hell in a Cell|Hell in a Cell series.

 Background 

Hell in a Cell contained professional wrestling matches, with the Card (sports)|main event matches being held in a Hell in a Cell match. The events involved wrestlers from all three WWE Brand Extension|Brands, WWE Raw|Raw, WWE SmackDown and ECW (WWE)|ECW. The wrestlers portrayed either a good guy or a bad guy as they followed a series of events which lead to a wrestling match or a series of wrestling matches.
