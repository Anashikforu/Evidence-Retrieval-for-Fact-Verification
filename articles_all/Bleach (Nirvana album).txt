'''''Bleach''''' is the first album by the United States|American rock music|rock band, Nirvana (band)|Nirvana. It did not become popular when it was released, but when Nirvana's second album ''Nevermind'' was released in 1991, many Nirvana fans discovered ''Bleach''. It is the best-selling release ever on the Sub Pop label.
