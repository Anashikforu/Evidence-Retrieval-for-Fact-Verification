
'''Haines City''' is a city in Polk County, Florida|Polk County, Florida, United States. As of 2007, the Economic and Business Research at the University of Florida estimated that the city's population was 18,748. Thus making it the third largest city by population in the county.

 Parks 

The city went without a public park as both Lake Eva Park and Railroad Park were both undergoing some sort renovations. From July 2008 through July 2009.

Schools
The city has many schools in the area, including Haines City High School.

Notable residents
Wayne Gandy - National Football League|NFL star for the Atlanta Falcons, born and raised in Haines City.
