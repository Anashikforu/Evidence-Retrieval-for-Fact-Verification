'''Ottakring''' is part of Vienna. It is the 16th district of Vienna, created in 1893 from the villages of Ottakring (village)|Ottakring and Neulerchenfeld. Ottakring can be reached by public transit; it is west of the city centre.
 
Today, there are fewer people in this district than in the past. In 1910, about 179,000 people lived there, but in 2019 there were only 103,000.
