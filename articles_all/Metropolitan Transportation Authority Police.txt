

The '''Metropolitan Transportation Authority Police''', or '''''MTA Police''''', is the police force of New York's Metropolitan Transportation Authority (New York)|Metropolitan Transportation Authority local railroad, or MTA.  Officers of the MTA Police are full police officers in New York State and in the state of Connecticut. The force was started in 1998.
