




'''La Mayenne''' is a department the northwest of France. It was named after the river Mayenne (river)|''la Mayenne'' and its number is 53. It is in the region Pays de la Loire. Its population is 285,338 (1999) and its land area is 5,175&nbsp;km². The population density is 55/km². It has 261 Communes of the Mayenne department|communes, its prefecture is Laval, Mayenne|Laval ans its subprefectures are Mayenne (town)|Mayenne and Château-Gontier. The inhabitants are called the ''Mayennais''.
