'''Princess Iman bint Al Hussein''' (born April 24, 1983) is the daughter of the late Hussein of Jordan|King Hussein of Jordan and Queen Noor. She is the half-sister of the present Jordanian king, Abdullah II of Jordan|Abdullah II. Princess Iman was Education|educated at the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst in England.
