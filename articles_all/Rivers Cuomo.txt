'''Rivers Cuomo''' (born June 13, 1970) is an Americans|American musician. He is best known for being a member of the Rock music|rock band Weezer. Cuomo plays electric guitar|electric and acoustic guitar. He also knows how to play drum kit|drums, bass guitar, clarinet, harmonica, Keyboard instrument|keyboards, trumpet and piano. In 2007 he released an album on his own called ''Alone: The Home Recordings of Rivers Cuomo''. It is a collection of eighteen demos he made between 1992 and 2007.

Personal life
Cuomo married Kyoko Ito June 18, 2006.
