

'''Ensiferum''' is a folk metal band from Helsinki, Finland. The name comes from the Latin word ''ensĭfĕrum'' which means "sword bearer". (someone who carries a sword) The members of the band call their music "heroic folk metal." Ensiferum has made four albums, one Extended Play|EP, one compilation album|compilation, three single (music)|singles, and three demo albums.

Musical style
Ensiferum's music has wikt:harsh|harsh vocals. They are normally mixed with clean vocals for Refrain|choruses and bridge (music)|bridges. The band often plays heavy metal music|Metal and folk music|folk song|tunes on guitar|lead guitar or keyboard instrument|keyboard with heavier traditional heavy metal sounds such as gallop (drumming)|gallop rhythm. It is common for them to use acoustic guitar at the start of songs. Their lyrics often deal with fantasy|fantastical, or history|historic stories. These stories usually have heroic themes.
