
The '''Africanis''' is a group of rare South African dogs. They are not recognized as a breed. The Swahili language|Swahili name for the breed is ''''. This means ''common'' or ''Mixed-breed dog|mixed-breed'' or "traditional dog".

Description

The Africanis is a medium-sized dog. It is well-muscled and is a bit longer than it is tall. It has a short coat (dog)|coat of fur that can be of any color. This dog can run very fast and also is training|trainable.
