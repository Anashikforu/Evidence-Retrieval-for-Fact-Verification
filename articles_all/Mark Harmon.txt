

'''Mark Thomas Harmon''' (born September 2, 1951) is an Americans|American actor. He has been acting since the 1970s. From 2003-2021, he has played Gibbs on the CBS crime television series|series ''NCIS (TV series)|NCIS''. He also played Ryan in ''Freaky Friday (2003 movie)|Freaky Friday''.
