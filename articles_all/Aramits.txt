
'''Aramits''' is a Commune in France|commune of the Pyrénées-Atlantiques ''departments of France|département'' in the region of Nouvelle-Aquitaine, in southwestern France.

The people living in Aramits are called ''Aramitsiens'' or ''Aramitsiennes''.
At the 2009 Census, the commune had a population of 677.
