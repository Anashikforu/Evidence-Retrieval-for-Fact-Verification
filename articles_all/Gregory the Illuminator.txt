'''Saint Gregory the Illuminator''' or '''Saint Gregory the Enlightener''' (Armenian language|Armenian: Գրիգոր Լուսաւորիչ translit. Grigor Lusavorich, Greek: Γρηγόριος Φωστήρ or Φωτιστής, Gregorios Phoster or Photistes), the founder and patron saint of the Armenian Apostolic Church, he was born about 257.

He belonged to the royal line of the Arsacid Dynasty of Armenia|Arsacid Dynasty, being the son of a Parthian named Anak, who assassinated Chosroes I of Armenia|Chosroes I of Kingdom of Armenia|Armenia, and thus brought ruin on himself and his family.
