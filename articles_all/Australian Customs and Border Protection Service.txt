The '''Australian Customs and Border Protection Service''' is the Government of Australia|Australian Federal Government agency with the duty of protecting the Australian border, helping with the movement of legal international travellers and goods, and collecting border related Duty|duties and taxes.

The Service has nearly 6,000 people working for it in Australia and other countries and is based in Canberra, Australia.

wikt:protect|Protecting Australia by stopping illegal and possibly harmful goods, such as Illicit drug|drugs and weapons, is a main concern and many methods and equipment are used to help with this task. This includes using intelligence, and computers, Australian Customs Detector Dog Program|detector dogs, Smartgate, x-rays, Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) and other methods. 

In doing its job, Customs works with other government agencies such as the Australian Federal Police, the Australian Quarantine and Inspection Service, the Department of Immigration and Citizenship (Australia)|Department of Immigration and Citizenship, the Department of Defence (Australia)|Department of Defence and the Australian Fisheries Management Authority.
