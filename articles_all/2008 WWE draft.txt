The '''2008 World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) WWE Draft Lottery|Draft''' was the fifth yearly (and sixth overall) Draft held by WWE. The Draft was held on June 23, 2008 in San Antonio, Texas from the AT&T Center.

Report
=Background=
The Draft was announced by WWE Chairman, Vince McMahon on the May 26 2008 episode of ''Raw''.
