
'''Crystal Gayle''' (born '''Brenda Gail Webb''', January 9, 1951 in Paintsville, Kentucky) is an Americans|American country music|country singer. She had twenty one country hits in the 1970s and 1980s. She is the younger sister of singer Loretta Lynn. Crystal Gayle has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, near Loretta Lynn's star. She is well known for her hair, which is floor length.
