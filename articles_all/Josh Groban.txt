
'''Joshua Winslow "Josh" Groban''' (born February 27, 1981) is an Americans|American singer, songwriter, musician, actor, and record producer. He has won awards.

Early life
Groban was born in Los Angeles, California. His parents are Lindy (nee Johnston), a school teacher, and Jack Groban, a businessman.

Groban debuted as a singer in the seventh grade.
