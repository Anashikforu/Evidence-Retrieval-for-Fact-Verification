

'''Vincent "Vinny" Guadagnino''' (born November 11, 1987) is an American reality television personality and actor. He is best known for his time on MTV's'' Jersey Shore (TV series)|Jersey Shore''.

In the November 8, 2011 episode of ''90210 (TV series)|90210'', he played a small role that he reprised in the episode titled "O Holly Night". 

MTV has also produced Guadagnino's own talk show, ''The Show with Vinny'', which debuted in May 2013.
