'''Dieter Gunther Bohlen''' is a Germans|German musician, born on 7 February 1954. He was born in Berne, Germany|Berne, Lower Saxony. He was a member of Modern Talking, but now appears in the TV programme "Germany Searches for a Superstar" , which is the German version of Pop Idol and American Idol.
