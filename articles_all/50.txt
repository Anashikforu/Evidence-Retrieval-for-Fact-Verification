


'''50''' (Roman numerals: '''L''') is a common year of the Gregorian calendar. It started on a Saturday.

It is one of only seven years to use just one Roman numeral. The seven are 1|1 AD (I), 5|5 AD (V), 10|10 AD (X), '''50 AD''' (L), 100|100 AD (C), 500|500 AD (D), and 1000|1000 AD (M).

 The Romans create a city called London|Londinium on the River Thames
