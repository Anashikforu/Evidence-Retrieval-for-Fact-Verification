
'''Forchheim''' (; East Franconian German|East Franconian: ''Forchham'') is a Germany|German town in Upper Franconia Government Region|Upper Franconia. Next bigger cities are Nuremberg|Nürnberg, Bamberg, Fürth, Erlangen and Coburg. There live is about 32.000 people.

It is 465 meters high and has an area of 44,46 km².
