'''Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB)''' is a public government-money|funded, higher education, technology school located in Bandung, Java|West Java, Indonesia. Institut Teknologi Bandung are the one of most popular university in Indonesia, together with Gadjah Mada University and University of Indonesia. The symbol of Institut Teknologi Bandung is Ganesha, a Hinduism|Hindu deity|god representing wisdom.
