'''Guru Angad Dev''' is(, 1504 - Sept 7, 1539) was one of the ten gurus who founded Sikhism. He became a Guru because of the wikt:obedience|obedience he showed to his Guru (Guru Nanak,the first guru in Sikh religion). Guru Angad Dev invented the present form of the Gurmukhi script.
