'''Kurt Paul Schmitt''' (7 October 1886 in Heidelberg – 2 November 1950 in Heidelberg) was a Germany|German economist and minister for economics of the Third Reich. He was also a member of Hitler's Cabinet.

He fought in the First World War and got the Iron Cross.
