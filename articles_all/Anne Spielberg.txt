'''Anne Spielberg''' (born December 25, 1949) is an Americans|American screenwriter. She is the sister of movie director Steven Spielberg. 

She was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in 1949. After working for her brother's production company, Amblin Entertainment, she co-wrote the movie ''Big (movie)|Big'' in 1988. For this, she was wikt:nominate|nominated for an Academy Award for "Best Writing of an Original Screenplay". She was also a co-movie producer|producer on the movie, along with noted television and movie producer James L. Brooks. She was also an uncredited cowriter of the movie ''Small Soldiers (movie)|Small Soldiers''. Sue Spielberg and Nancy Katz are her sisters.
