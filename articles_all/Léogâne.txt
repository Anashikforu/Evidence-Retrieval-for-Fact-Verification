'''Léogâne''' is the name of both a coastal city and a district in Ouest Department, Haiti. The city of Léogâne is around . The arrondissement of Léogâne contains three commune (subnational entity)|communes: Petit-Goâve, Grand-Goâve, and the city of Léogâne.
