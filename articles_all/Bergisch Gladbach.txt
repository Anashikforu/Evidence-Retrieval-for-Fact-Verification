

'''Bergisch Gladbach''' (; Ripuarian language|Ripuarian: ''Jläbbisch Gläbbich'')  is a city in the Germany|German States of Germany|state of North Rhine-Westphalia.
It is near to Cologne and has about 110,016 inhabitants.
