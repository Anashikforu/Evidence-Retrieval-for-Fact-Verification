
'''Greencastle''' is a city in Greencastle Township, Putnam County, Indiana|Greencastle Township, Putnam County, Indiana|Putnam County, Indiana, United States, and the county seat of Putnam County.  Before Greencastle became a city in 1861, it was a town.  Greencastle, Indiana was founded in year 1821 by a man named Ephraim Dukes after he purchased and later sold some land to the local government.  This type of purchase is called a land grant. Ephraim Duke named the settlement for his hometown of Greencastle, Pennsylvania.
