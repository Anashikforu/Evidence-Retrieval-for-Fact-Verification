The '''sooty mangabey''' (''Cercocebus atys''), is an Old World monkey of Guinea Bissau, Gabon, Senegal and Ghana. It has social groups of four to twelve individuals.

 Disease 
Sooty mangabeys are naturally infected with a strain of Simian Immunodeficiency Virus (SIV). Due to extensive human-mangabey contact in sub-Saharan Africa, the virus has zoonosis|jumped from this species into humans on many occasions. This resulted in the HIV#HIV-2|HIV-2 virus.
