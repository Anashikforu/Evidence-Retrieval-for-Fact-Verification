'''Yotsuya Kaidan''', also known as ''Tōkaidō yotsuya kaidan'', is one of the most famous Japanese ghost stories of all time.Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). 
The tale takes place in the Tōkaidō (region)|Tōkaidō region.

Plot
The story is a tale of murder and ghostly wikt:revenge|revenge. It continues to be an influence on Japanese horror today.
