 Retirement 
Newman retired from acting in May 2007. He said "You start to lose your memory, you start to lose your confidence, you start to lose your invention. So I think that's pretty much a closed book for me."
He came out of retirement to narrator|narrate the 2007 documentary movie ''Dale'', about the life of NASCAR driver Dale Earnhardt. He also narrated the 2008 BBC documentary ''Meerkats: The Movie''. It was recorded at a studio near his home shortly before his death. ''The Meerkats'' was the last movie credit of Newman's long career.
