
'''Sodium sulfite''' is a chemical compound. It is made of sodium and sulfite ions. It is one of the sulfites used to food preservation|preserve dried food. It is used to remove chlorine from pools. It reacts with acids to produce sulfur dioxide. It is a weak reducing agent. When it is oxidation|oxidized, it is converted to sodium sulfate. Its chemical formula is Na2SO3.
