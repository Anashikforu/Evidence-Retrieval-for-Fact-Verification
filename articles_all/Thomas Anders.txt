'''Thomas Anders''' (born '''Bernd Weidung''', 1 March 1963, Koblenz, West Germany) is a Germans|German singer, composer and record producer. Anders was the lead singer of Modern Talking, a famous Germany|German pop duo, between 1984–1987 and 1998–2003.
