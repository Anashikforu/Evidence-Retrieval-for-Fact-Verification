
A '''commune''' is a minor subnational entity|administrative subdivision corresponding to an urban or rural zone, or mixed. It is the equivalent of a municipality, a council, or other instances of local administration.

 Communes in the world 
Many countries have adopted the name of commune (or similar) for their minor and basic administrative unit. Some countries are Germany (''Gemeinde''), Belgium (''commune''/''gemeente''), Chile (''commune of Chile|comuna''), Colombia (''commune of Colombia|Comuna''), Denmark (kommune), France (''Communes of France|commune''), Italy (''comune''), Luxembourg (''commune''), Netherlands (''gemeente''), Poland (''gmina''), Sweden (''kommun'') and Switzerland (''Gemeinde''/''commune''/''comune'').
