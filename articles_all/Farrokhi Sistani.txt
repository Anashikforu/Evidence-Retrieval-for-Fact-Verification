'''Abul Hasan Ali ibn Julugh Farrukhi Sistani''' (c. 980 - 1037 or 1038) () or '''Farrokhi''' is a Persian people|Persian poet. Farrokhi was the best poet in the court of Mahmud Ghaznavi. All of his poems are in Persian language|Persian. His poems are collected in a Divan.
