'''Da Nhay Beach''' is a beach on the shore of South China Sea. Da Nhay in Vietnamese language|Vietnamese means ''the rocks that jump''. The beach was called this because of the geographical appearance of it; it has thousands rock reaching the sea and surrounding small fine sand beaches.

 Location 
It is about 40&nbsp;km north of Đồng Hới city, Quảng Bình Province, Vietnam.
