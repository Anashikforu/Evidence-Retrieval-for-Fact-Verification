
The '''Lamborghini Jalpa''' is a sports car. Lamborghini built it from 1981 to 1988. The Jalpa came after the Lamborghini Silhouette|Silhouette. The Jalpa was designed by Carrozzeria Bertone.

History
The Jalpa was introduced at the 1981 Geneva Auto Show. It was based off the Lamborghini Silhouette|Silhouette. Lamborghini designed it to be less expensive than the Lamborghini Countach|Countach.
