

'''Khayrpur''' or '''Khairpur''' was a princely state on the Indus River in what is now Pakistan, with its capital city at Khairpur, Pakistan|Khayrpur. Together with Bahawalpur (princely state)|Bahawalpur to the north-east, the state was included with the Punjab States Agency|Punjab states rather than the neighbouring Rajputana states (now Rajasthan) to the east.
