


'''Neo-Nazism''' means the ideology of some politics|political groups who want to return to the beliefs and practices of Nazism after the end of World War II. Different groups have their own sets of beliefs and practices. Neo-Nazis believe in loyalty to Adolf Hitler, hating Jews (anti-Semitism), racism, belief in a Germanic master race, xenophobia (hating and fearing people from different countries), extremist nationalism, White supremacy|supremacy, militarism, and hatred of homosexuals (homophobia).  

Neo-Nazis often use the symbols of Nazi Germany such as the swastika. Neo-Nazism has been linked to Far-right politics|far-right political parties in England such as the National Front and the British Nationalist Party as well as being linked to the skinhead sub-culture.
