
'''Rafik Baha ad-Din Hariri''' (; November 1, 1944 â€“ February 14, 2005) was a Lebanese billionaire and businessman. He was Prime Minister of Lebanon from 1992 to 1998, and he became Prime Minister again in 2000. But in October 20, 2004, he quit his job. He helped a lot in rebuilding Beirut, which was damaged from the fighting during the Lebanese Civil War|civil war.

Hariri was killed along with 21 other people on February 14, 2005. A thousand kilograms of Trinitrotoluene|TNT exploded car bomb|in a van near his car. His car was passing through St. George Hotel in the Lebanese capital, Beirut. People are still trying to learn who did it, and the United Nations have helped to investigate. Serge Brammertz is leading this investigation. Many people in Lebanon said that the government of Syria and its allies did it.
