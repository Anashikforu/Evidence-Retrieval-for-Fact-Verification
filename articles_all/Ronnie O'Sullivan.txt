
'''Ronnie O'Sullivan''' (born 5 December 1975) is an English people|English professional snooker player. He was born in Wordsley, West Midlands (county)|West Midlands. He grew up in Chigwell, Essex and still lives there. He was List of world snooker champions|World Champion in 2001, 2004, 2008, 2012, 2013 and 2020.

O'Sullivan made his first century break at age 10.

He has won the UK Championship six times. This was in 1993, 1997, 2001, 2007, 2014 and 2017. He has won the Masters seven times, in 1995, 2005, 2007, 2009, 2014, 2016 and 2017.
