'''Falun Gong''' (also known as '''Falun Dafa''') is a spiritual discipline from China with exercises similar to Tai chi chuan. It was founded by Li Hongzhi in 1992.
The police started arresting Falun Gong followers, and according to human rights organizations, over 3000 people have been jailed and tortured to death.Mary-Anne Toy, 
It is not known how many people practice Falun Gong. The Chinese government suggests there were 70 million practitioners in China in 1998.
Reporter Ethan Gutmann said about 65,000 Falun Gong people were killed for their body parts from 2000 to 2008.Ethan Gutmann (10 March 2011)
