


'''Sagwa''' is the name of a Siamese (cat)|siamese cat in the children's book ''Sagwa the Chinese Siamese (cat)|Siamese Cat'' by Amy Tan. Sagwa's popularity with Child|children prompted an educational animated series of the same name on PBS Kids. In the series, which is set circa 1900 during the Qing dynasty, Sagwa has fun in her day-to-day life while learning and teaching valuable life Ethics|lessons. The show is notable for its setting and messages about family and loyalty.
