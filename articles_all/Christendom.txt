'''Christendom''' or the '''Christian world''',Marty, Martin E. 
People have used the term '''Christendom''' for the Middle Ages|medieval and Renaissance understanding of the Christian world as one single ''Body of Christ'' with Christ as the head.  With the rise of Modernity and the Reformation during the early 16th century the understanding changed to a modern idea of a tolerant and diverse society that consists of many different communities.
