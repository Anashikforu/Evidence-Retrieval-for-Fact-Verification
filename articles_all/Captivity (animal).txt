Animals who live under human control or care are in '''captivity'''. Captivity can be used as a term meaning the keeping of either Domestication|domesticated animals (livestock, pets) or wikt:Wild|wild animals. This may include for example farms, private homes and zoos. Sometimes humans take care of critically endangered animals and therefore these animals are in captivity.
