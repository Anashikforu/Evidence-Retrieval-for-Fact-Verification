

'''Jedward''' are an Irish people|Irish pop music|pop rap duo based in the Republic of Ireland. They are twins '''John Paul Henry Daniel Richard Grimes''' and '''Edward Peter Anthony Kevin Patrick Grimes''' (born 16 October 1991). Thay have since released two more albums, Victory (2011) and Planet Jedward (2012). The twins are releasing a single titled Free Spirit on May the 16th which was first played on Australia's KIIS 106.5.  They are also set to release an album later this year. They were contestants in the Celebrity Big Brother 2011 (UK)|2011 edition of ''Big Brother (TV series)|Big Brother''.
