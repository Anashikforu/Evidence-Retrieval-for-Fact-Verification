
'''Yaoundé''' is the capital (city)|capital city of Cameroon|the Republic of Cameroon. It is the second largest city in the country after Douala.

 History 
Yaoundé was :wikt:found|founded in 1888 by Germany|German traders. It was created to be used as a base for the ivory trade and an agriculture|agricultural research station. The city was under the control of Belgium|Belgian troops during World War I. After the war, it became the capital of French Cameroons. It has remained the capital of the Republic of Cameroon until the present day.
