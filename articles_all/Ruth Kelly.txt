
'''Ruth Maria Kelly''' (born May 9, 1968) is now an economist working for the HSBC|HSBC Bank. She was a United Kingdom|British politician and had been a Cabinet of the United Kingdom|Cabinet Minister. 

She was the Member of Parliament for the Bolton West(UK Parliament constituency)|Bolton West constituency in the United Kingdom from the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election to the United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010 general election

She is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
