
'''Büyükada''' () is an island in the Sea of Marmara, near Istanbul, Turkey.

Büyükada is a Tourism|tourist location for people from Istanbul for holidays. In Büyükada there are no cars allowed. The only cars are owned by the police, fire department and the other local authority|authorities. The bicycle is the most common transportation|transport used in the island. Romanlar live there and works as Coachmen.
