



'''Amy MacDonald''' is a Scotland|Scottish singer and songwriter.  She became famous in 2007 with her first album ''This Is The Life'' and her first single (music)|single "Poison Prince".  She has become even more successful in Europe since her single "This Is The Life" charted at number 1 in many European countries.
