
'''Sikkimese people''' live in the Indian province of Sikkim. The Native (ecology)|native Sikkimese consist of the Lepcha people|Lepcha and Limbu people|Limbu people; they migrated from Tibet. They also include the Bhutias. These are Ancestor|descendants of Buddhism|Buddhists who arrived from Nepal in 15th century; they migrated from the Kham district of Tibet in the 14th century.
