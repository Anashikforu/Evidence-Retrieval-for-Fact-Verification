A vibration in a string is a wave. A '''vibrating string''' mostly produces a sound with constant frequency. As frequency characterizes the Pitch (music)|pitch, the sound produced is a constant note (music)|note.

Vibrating strings are the basis of any string instrument like guitar, cello, or piano.
