


'''Alyson Rae Stoner''' (born August 11, 1993) is an Americans|American actress, dancer, singer and former model (person)|model. She was known for her roles in ''The Suite Life of Zack & Cody'' (as Max), ''Cheaper By The Dozen'' (as Sarah Baker), and ''Step Up (movie)|Step Up'' (as Camille Gage)'', Phineas and Ferb'' (for the voice of Isabella Garcia)'', Camp Rock'' and ''Camp Rock 2:The Final Jam'' (as Caitlyn Geller). Stoner is from Toledo,Ohio.
