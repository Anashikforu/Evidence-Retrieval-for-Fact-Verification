'''Mary Kathleen Turner''' (born June 19, 1954) is an Americans|American actress. She became famous during the 1980s with Hollywood movies including: ''Body Heat'', ''Romancing the Stone'' and ''Peggy Sue Got Married''. Turner has starred in many television programs and cinema|theatrical movies. She was born in Springfield, Missouri. She is a Democrat.
