

'''FM Static''' is a Pop punk band that makes albums with ''Tooth & Nail Records''. The band was started by Thousand Foot Krutch (another band) wikt:lead|lead man Trevor McNevan and Thousand Foot Krutch drummer Steve Augustine. In 2003, they made their first album called (''What Are You Waiting For?''). This album has sold over 60,000 copies so far and had the singles "''Definitely Maybe''" and "''Crazy Mary''". On August 1, 2006, FM Static started selling their second album called ''Critically Ashamed'', which has the single "''Waste of Time''" on it. Their third album was announced in late April 2008. The two piece finished recording "Dear Diary" in early August 2008. It was released on April 7, 2009. 

FM Static wikt:won|won the ''Best Website Ever Made'' wikt:award|award from the ''Webgrammys'', and they have been wikt:nominate|nominated for the ''GMA Canada'' ''Covenant Awards''.
