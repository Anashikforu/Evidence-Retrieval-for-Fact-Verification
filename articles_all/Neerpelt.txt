'''Neerpelt''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Limburg (Belgium)|Limburg. On 1 January 2019, Neerpelt and Overpelt were merged into the new municipality of Pelt (municipality)|Pelt.

In 2007, 16156 people lived there.

It is at 51° 13 North, 05° 26 East.
