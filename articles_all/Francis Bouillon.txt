

'''Francis Bouillon''' (born October 17, 1975 in New York City, New York) is an Americans|American professional ice hockey defenceman for the Nashville Predators of the National Hockey League (NHL). He played in the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League|QMJHL with the Laval Titan. Bouillon has also played for the Montreal Canadiens for 8 seasons before signing a one-year contract with the Predators.
