

'''''Grandpa Never Lies''''' is a children's book written by Ralph Fletcher with pictures drawn by Harvey Stevenson.  It was first :wikt:published|published in 2000.

Plot summary
A young girl describes her special :wikt:bond|bond with her grandfather over four :wikt:seasons|seasons.  The Grandfather tells :wikt:creative|creative stories in answer to her questions.  Each story is followed by her saying "And Grandpa never lies, so I know it's so".  Then Grandma suddenly dies and the little girl and her grandfather are sad and comfort each other.
