'''Napoleon''' is a Fictional character|character in the book Animal Farm. Together with Snowball (Animal Farm)|Snowball, he is a boar that Mr. Jones is breeding for sale at the market. Napoleon is the only Berkshire|Berkshire boar on Manor Farm. He is fierce-looking, large, and very stubborn, wanting things to be done his own way. Napoleon is very selfish and only cares about his own good.

 Symbolism 
Napoleon is based on Joseph Stalin|Stalin of the Russian revolution, who harshly ruled the entire Russia. Napoleon's name is derived from the French conqueror Napoleon I of France|with the same name.
