The '''Atlanta Flames''' were an ice hockey team in the NHL from Atlanta, Georgia|Atlanta, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia, USA from 1972–73 NHL season|1972 to 1979–80 NHL season|1980. The team was moved to Calgary, Alberta|Calgary, Alberta, Canada for the start of the 1980–81 NHL season and were renamed the Calgary Flames. The team was named after the Atlanta in the Civil War#The fall of Atlanta|burning of Atlanta, led by U.S. Army general William Sherman during the American Civil War.
