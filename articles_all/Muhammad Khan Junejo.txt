

'''Mohammad Khan Junejo''' (Urdu language|Urdu:  Sindhi language|Sindhi :محمد خان جوڻيجو, August 18, 1932 – March 16, 1993) was the eleventh Prime Minister of Pakistan. He was appointed by the Zia military regime, but later turned against it and tried to improve democracy in Pakistan. For this, he was removed from power by the Pakistan Army.
