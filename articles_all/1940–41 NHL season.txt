The '''1940–41 NHL season''' was the 24th Season (sport)|season of the National Hockey League. Seven teams each played 48 games. The Boston Bruins were the List of Stanley Cup champions|Stanley Cup winners as they beat the Detroit Red Wings four games to none in the final series.
