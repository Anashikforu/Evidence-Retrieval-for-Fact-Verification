

'''Stewart Jason Downing''' (born 22 July 1984) is an English people|English Association football|footballer. He started playing for the Middlesbrough F.C.|Middlesbrough first team in 2001. He went on loan to Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland in 2003, and spent the next six years at Boro. When Boro were relegated, in 2009 he left to join Aston Villa F.C.|Aston Villa, and spent the next two years there. He had a two-year spell with Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool from 2011, and joined West Ham United F.C.|West Ham United in 2013. He re-joined Boro in 2015, and has played there since.

He began playing for England national football team|England in 2005, and played 35 matches with them, but has not played since 2014. It is suggested that he has retired from international football.

 Football career 

= Start with Boro =
He joined the Middlesbrough F.C.|Middlesbrough first team in 2001, having played for the Academy. In his first two seasons, he played in five Premier League matches, and failed to score any goals during that time.

 Sunderland loan 
In 2003, he joined Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland on loan, and stayed there till 30 November 2003. He made three goals in seven matches.

 Next six years with Boro 
He became a regular at Boro, and spent the next six seasons there. They had some highs and some lows, but were relegated to the Football League Championship|Championship at the end of the 2008/09 season. This made Stew leave Boro.

 Start with England team 
In 2005, he began playing for the England national football team|England team.

= Move to Villa =
After Boro's relegation, he joined Aston Villa F.C.|Aston Villa, in the 2009/10 Premier League. He played there for two seasons.

= Joining Liverpool =
He joined Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool in 2011 and stayed there for 2 seasons.

= Move to London with West Ham =
In 2013, he joined London club West Ham United F.C.|West Ham United, where he spent the next 2 seasons.

 Latest with England team 
He has not played for England since 2014.

= Back to Boro =
He re-joined Boro in 2015, and has stayed there since.
