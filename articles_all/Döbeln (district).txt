


'''Döbeln''' was a district in the Free State of Saxony, Germany. It ended in 2008.

 History 
The region was originally populated by Sorbs|Sorbian peoples.
The present borders of the district were made in 1952, when the government of East Germany formed the new districts. Döbeln is one of the few districts which have not been changed after the German reunification.

 Geography 
The district is on the banks of the Mulde|Freiberger Mulde in the triangle between the cities of Dresden, Leipzig and Chemnitz.
