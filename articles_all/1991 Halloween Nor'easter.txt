
The '''1991 Halloween Nor’easter''', also known as the '''Perfect Storm''', was an atypical nor'easter|nor’easter which was extratropical cyclone|extratropical, absorbed one hurricane, and ultimately evolved into a small hurricane late in its life cycle. The unnamed hurricane of 1991 was the last tropical cyclone of the 1991 Atlantic hurricane season, and its 4 hurricane. By November 2, Atlantic Canada experienced the effects of a landfalling tropical storm. Damage totaled $208 million (1991 USD)
