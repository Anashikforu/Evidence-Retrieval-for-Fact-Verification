'''Line 2''', also known by the names of its termini, currently '''Paral·lel''' - '''Pep Ventura''', is a Barcelona metro|metro line in Barcelona operated by Transports Metropolitans de Barcelona|TMB, coloured purple and sometimes simply called ''línia lila''. 

Chronology
1995 - Sant Antoni-Sagrada Família section opened.
1996 - Sant Antoni-Paral·lel section opened.
1997 - Sagrada Família-La Pau section opened.
2002 - La Pau-Pep Ventura section moved from Barcelona metro line 4|L4 to this line.
