
The '''Claremont Colleges''' are a group of Private university|private colleges and university|universities in Claremont, California|Claremont, California, near Los Angeles. They include:

Pomona College
Claremont Graduate University
Scripps College
Claremont McKenna College
Harvey Mudd College
Pitzer College
Keck Graduate Institute
Put together, they have about 8500 students.
