
'''Eintracht Frankfurt''' is a Germany|German sports club from Frankfurt|Frankfurt am Main, best known for the Association football|football section.

The club plays in the German Bundesliga|Bundesliga. They have won the German Championship once and German Cup (DFB-Pokal) 5 times (last time in 2018) and the UEFA Cup once.

They were founded in 1899. The team colours are red, black and white. They play at Deutsche Bank Park (former Waldstadion). The current captain of the side is defender Sebastian Rode from Germany.
The coach of Eintracht Frankfurt is Oliver Glasner.

Eintracht has also 18 other sections such as Athletics (track and field)|athletics, basketball and ice hockey.
