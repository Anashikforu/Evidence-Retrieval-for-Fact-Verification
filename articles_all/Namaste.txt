

'''Namaste or Namaskar''' is a neutral greeting used a lot in many East Asian countries. It is used when Hello and Hi are used in English language|English, though it has a very different meaning.

Namaste is kind of like a praise to the person receiving the namaste.

The word's meaning is “I bow to the divine in you.” Namaste is a traditional Indian greeting of respect and thank you, with spiritual and symbolic meaning.
