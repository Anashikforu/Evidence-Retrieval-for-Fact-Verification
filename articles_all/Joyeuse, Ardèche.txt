

'''Joyeuse''' ()is a ''Communes of France|commune'' in the France|French Departments of France|department of Ardèche in the Regions of France|region of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes. It is the seat of the Cantons of France|canton of Joyeuse.  Joyeuse has a population of 1,487 people as of the year 1999.

Geography
Joyeuse can be found in the  region of Bas-Vivarais that has a lot of history.  It can be found in the valley of the Beaume River|Beaume, which is a tributary of the Ardèche River.
