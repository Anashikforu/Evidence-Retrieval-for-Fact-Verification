'''Purple Haze''' is a type of marijuana that has buds that look like purple hairs all around it making it more potent, that is, it has more THC in it. It came into recognition when Jimi Hendrix wrote a song about it and its effects after he had it in the Himalayan region of Nepal. This herb is naturally found on the higher hilly region and himalayan region of Nepal.
