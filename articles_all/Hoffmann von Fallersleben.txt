'''August Heinrich Hoffmann''' (April 2, 1798 - January 19, 1874), who used '''Hoffmann von Fallersleben''' as his pen name, was a Germany|German poet. He is best known for writing "Das Lied der Deutschen", which is now the national anthem of Germany, and a number of popular children's songs.
