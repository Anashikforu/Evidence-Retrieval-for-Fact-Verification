
'''Freeport''' is a city in Brazoria County, Texas|Brazoria County, Texas within the Houston Metropolitan Area|Houston – Sugar Land – Baytown Metropolitan Area in Southeast Texas. As of the 2000 U.S. census, the city population was 12,708. Freeport is about sixteen miles from Angleton, Texas|Angleton.

Dow Chemical is the major employer in Freeport, as the company operates a large chemical plant complex in Freeport.
