

'''Melbourne Central''' is a railway station|train station in Melbourne, victoria, Australia|Victoria, Australia. It is one of three underground stations on the City Loop, Melbourne|Melbourne Underground Rail Loop (City Loop).  The station is underneath Melbourne Central Shopping Center. Melbourne Central has four platforms and four railway track|tracks. It was known as '''Museum Station''' when the Melbourne Museum was at the State Library of Victoria, across the street. Melbourne Central opened on January 24, 1981.
