

'''''Law & Order: Special Victims Unit''''' (often shortened to '''Law & Order: SVU''') is an United States|American TV show|television spin-off of the original police drama series ''Law & Order'' which is set in New York City, inside the fictional 16th Police precinct|precinct of Manhattan.
