 Traditional history 
According to ''Nihon Shoki'', Nintoku was the fourth son of Emperor Ōjin and the father of three emperors — Emperor Richū|Richū, Emperor Hanzei|Hanzei, and Emperor Ingyō|Ingyō .

 Events of Nintoku's life
