
'''Margaret "Jane" Pauley''' is a news anchor and reporter. She hosted ''NBC Today'' between 1976 and 1989 and ''Dateline NBC'' starting in 1992 and ending in 2003. Pauley was born 31 October 1950 in Indianapolis.
