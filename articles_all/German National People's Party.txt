
The '''German National People's Party''' (German language|German: ''Deutschnationale Volkspartei'' and short: ''DNVP'') was National conservatism|national-conservative party of the time of the Weimar Republic and the Third Reich. This party was founded in 1918, after World War I. In June 1933, the DNVP merged with the NSDAP.

 Chairman 
 1918 to 1924 Oskar Hergt (1869-1967)
 1924 to 1928 Kuno Graf von Westarp (1864-1945)
 1928 to 1933 Alfred Hugenberg (1865-1951)
