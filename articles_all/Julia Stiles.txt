'''Julia Stiles''' (born March 28, 1981, New York City) is an Americans|American actress. She played Nicky Parsons in the ''Jason Bourne|Bourne'' movie series. She is also a theater|stage and television actress. On March 17, 2001, Stiles hosted ''Saturday Night Live''. She is a feminist.
