
'''Clarence Clifton Young''' (November 7, 1922 &ndash; April 3, 2016)  was an Americans|American politics|politician from the U.S. state|state of Nevada. He was a member of United States House of Representatives from January 3, 1953 to January 3, 1957. He was from the Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party.

Young died in Reno, Nevada on April 3, 2016, aged 93.
