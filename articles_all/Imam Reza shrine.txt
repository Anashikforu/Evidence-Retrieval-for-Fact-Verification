'''Imām Rezā shrine''' () is a complex in Mashhad, Iran. It contains the mausoleum of Imam Ridha|Imām Ridhā, the eighth Twelve Imams|Imām of Twelvers|Twelver Shi'ites. Also in the complex are the Goharshad Mosque, a museum, a library, Seminary|seminaries, a cemetery, a dining hall for pilgrims, vast prayer halls, and other buildings.

This complex is the center of tourism in Iran. It is visited by 15 to 20 million pilgrims every year.
