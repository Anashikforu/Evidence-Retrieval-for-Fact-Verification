


'''Marie McDonald McLaughlin Lawrie''' (born 3 November 1948), best known by her stage name '''Lulu''', is a Scottish people|Scottish pop music|pop singer and actress. Her most famous hit is the song "''Shout (song)|Shout''". Lulu's first hit was the song "''To Sir with Love (song)|To Sir with Love''", which was the title song of the 1967 movie ''To Sir, with Love'', which was also her movie debut. She sang the title song of the 1974 James Bond movie ''The Man with the Golden Gun (movie)|The Man with the Golden Gun''.

She was a contestant in the 2011 series of ''Strictly Come Dancing''.
