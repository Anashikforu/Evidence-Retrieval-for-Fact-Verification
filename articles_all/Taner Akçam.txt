 
'''Altuğ Taner Akçam''' (born October 23 1953) is a Turkish people|Turkish historian, Sociology|sociologist and author. He is one of the first Turkish academics to acknowledge and discuss openly the Armenian Genocide by the Ottoman Empire|Ottoman Turkish government in 1915.
