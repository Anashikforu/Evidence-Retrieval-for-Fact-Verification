'''Stetson University''' is a private, co-educational university in DeLand, Florida|DeLand, Florida. The university has about 2,200 students. It was founded in 1883. The current president of the university is Wendy B. Libby.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Hatters. They compete in the Atlantic Sun Conference.
