 Meanings for K 


 Degrees Kelvin. The kelvin (symbol: K) is the SI unit of temperature. 
 In chemistry, K is the symbol for potassium (from its German language|German name ''kalium'').
 in chess, K is a notation symbol for the King (chess)|king piece
 In a deck of playing cards, the letter K is used to mark each of the king (playing card)|kings.
