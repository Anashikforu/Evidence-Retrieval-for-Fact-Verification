



'''Desmond "Des" Bernard O'Connor''' (12 January 1932 &ndash; 14 November 2020) was an English people|English television presenter and singer. He was born in Stepney, London. O'Connor presented the long running game show Countdown (game show)|Countdown on Channel 4 in the United Kingdom, from January 2007 to December 2008.

O'Connor died on 14 November 2020 from problems caused by a fall, aged 88.
