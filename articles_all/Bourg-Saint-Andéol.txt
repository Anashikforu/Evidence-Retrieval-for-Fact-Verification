

'''Bourg-Saint-Andéol''' is a small Communes of France|commune of the Ardèche ''Departments of France|département'' in the Rhône Valley in the southern part of France.  It is the Capital (city)|capital of a canton in the arrondissement of Privas, with a population of about 8,000 as of the year 1999.

Twin cities
Monschau in Germany (Eifel) since 1975
Albertirsa in Hungary since 1998
Gaggiano in Italy since 2002
