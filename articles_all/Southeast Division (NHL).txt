=2013 realignment=
The NHL dissolved the Southeast Division as the league realigned into two conferences with two divisions each. The Winnipeg Jets moved to the Western Conference (NHL)|Western Conference and the Central Division (NHL)|Central Division. The Florida Panthers and Tampa Bay Lightning were placed into the new Atlantic Division (NHL)|Atlantic Division. The Carolina Hurricanes and Washington Capitals were placed in the newly formed Metropolitan Division.
