'''Alberto Núñez Feijoo''' (born September 10, 1961) is a Spanish politician. He is the List of Presidents of the Regional Government of Galicia|7th and current President of the Regional Government of Galicia since 2009. He is the leader of the Galician PP, the main conservative political party which defeated the socialists in the elections of 2009 and again in the elections of 2012. Feijoo is a strong admirer of Manuel Fraga, although he does not govern Galicia in the same manner.

Núñez Feijoo studied law in Santiago de Compostela. He is married to the journalist Carmen Gámir.
