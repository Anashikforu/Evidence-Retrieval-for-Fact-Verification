A province is the second largest administrative division in Chile, after a Regions of Chile|region. Each region is divided in provinces. Chile has 54 provinces in total. Each province is ruled by a governor (''gobernador'') designated by the President of Chile|president. The provinces are also divided into Communes of Chile|communes which are ruled by a municipality.
