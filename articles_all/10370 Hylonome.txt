

'''10370 Hylonome''' is an asteroid orbiting in the farther part of the solar System|solar system. It belongs to the group of icy minor planets called ''centaur (planetoid)|centaurs'', with an orbit that crosses the orbits of Neptune and Uranus. It was found on February 27, 1995.

Sightings with the Spitzer Space Telescope show that it's diameter is about 70 km (43 miles) plus or minus 20&nbsp;km (50 to 90&nbsp;km in diameter), or a 35&nbsp;km radius.
