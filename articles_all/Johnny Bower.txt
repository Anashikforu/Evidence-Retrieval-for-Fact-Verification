'''John William "Johnny" Bower''' (November 8, 1924 &ndash; December 26, 2017), nicknamed "The China Wall",  was a Hockey Hall of Fame goaltender.

Bower died of pneumonia on December 26, 2017 in Toronto at the age of 93.
Awards
 Hap Holmes Memorial Award (best AHL goaltender, 3 times)
 Vezina Trophy (2 times, at that time awarded for allowing the fewest goals throughout a season).
 NHL First Team All-Star in 1961.
 Les Cunningham Award as the AHL's Most Valuable Player three times consecutively, one of only four players in North American professional hockey history (Bobby Orr, Wayne Gretzky and Paul Polillo being the others) to do so at any level of play.
