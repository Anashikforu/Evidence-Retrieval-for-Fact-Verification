'''Cremona''' is a city in northern Italy. Cremona is in the Lombardy Region with a population of 71,533 inhabitants. It is in the Po valley|Po Valley on the North side of the Po (river)|Po River.

 History 
In the year 218 BCE, Romans founded the city. Before that, it was a village of the Gauls.
