

The '''World Tag Team Championship''' is a professional wrestling tag team championship (professional wrestling)|championship in World Wrestling Entertainment. It is the original world tag team title of WWE. Currently, it is exclusive to the WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brand and is :en:Championship unification#Professional wrestling|unified with the WWE Tag Team Championship to make up the '''Unified WWE Tag Team Championship'''.

 History 
The championship was first introduced into the then ''World Wide Wrestling Federation (WWWF)'' in 1971. Following its introduction into the WWF Grady Johnson|Luke Graham and Camille Tourville|Tarzan Tyler became the first ''WWWF Tag Team Champions'' on June 3. In 1979 the title became known as the ''World Wrestling Federation (WWF) Tag Team Championship'' when the promotion renamed itself World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation

In 2001 at the Survivor Series 2001|Survivor Series Pay-per-view during the WWE Invasion Storyline|Invasion Storyline The Hardys|The Hardy Boyz, Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy who were the current ''World Tag Team Champions'' and were competing for the ''World Wrestling Federation'' took on The Dudleys|The Dudley Boys, Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley who were the current WCW Tag Team Championship|WCW World Tag Team Champions and were competing for ''The Alliance'' in a title Unify|unification match inside a Steel Cage Match|Steel Cage, The Dudleys|The Dudley Boys defeated The Hardys|The Hardy Boyz and were named the last WCW Tag Team Championship|WCW Tag Team Champions while also becoming the ''WWF Tag Team Champions''

After the World Wrestling Entertainment#World Wrestling Entertainment|WWF/WWE name change in 2002, the championship was referred to as the ''World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) Tag Team Championship''. After a period where the championship was to be freely defended on either WWE Raw|WWE Monday Night Raw or WWE Friday Night SmackDown!|WWE Thursday Night Smackdown the championship became exclusive to the WWE Raw|Raw Brand. The current General Manager at this time Stephanie McMahon created new WWE Tag Team Championship|WWE Tag Team Titles which were to be exclusive to the WWE Friday Night SmackDown!|Smackdown Brand.
