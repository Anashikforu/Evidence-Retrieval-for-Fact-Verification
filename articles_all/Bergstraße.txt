'''Bergstraße''' or '''Bergstrasse''' can mean many things, including:

Bergstraße Route – The Mountain route in the Odenwald of Baden-Württemberg and Hesse
Bergstraße Rural District|Kreis Bergstraße – A rural district in Hesse
Hessische Bergstraße – A Hessian winegrowing region
Badische Bergstraße – A winegrowing region in Baden-Württemberg
