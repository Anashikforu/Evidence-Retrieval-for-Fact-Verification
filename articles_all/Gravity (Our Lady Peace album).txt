

'''''Gravity''''' is the fifth studio album by Canadian Rock music|rock band Our Lady Peace. It was released on June 18, 2002. The producer of the album was Bob Rock. This album featured new guitarist Steve Mazur who replaced Mike Turner (musician)|Mike Turner when he left the band in 2001.
