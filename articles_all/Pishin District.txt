'''Pishin''' () is a Districts of Pakistan|district in the north west of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province of Pakistan. 

Administration
The district of Pishin is administratively subdivided into the following tehsils:

 Barshore Tehsil
 Bostan Tehsil
 Hurramzai Tehsil
 Karezat Tehsil
 Pishin Tehsil
