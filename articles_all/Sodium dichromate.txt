
'''Sodium dichromate''' is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is Na2Cr2O7. It consists of sodium and dichromate ions. It is an orange crystalline solid. It is an intermediate in making chromium. It is used to make leather. It is also used in making organic chemistry|organic chemical compounds. It is a strong oxidizing agent and a toxic carcinogen.
