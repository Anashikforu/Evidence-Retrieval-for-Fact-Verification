The '''Louisiana State University and A&M College''' is a public university in Baton Rouge, Louisiana|Baton Rouge, Louisiana; it belongs to the Louisiana State University System of higher education as a land-grant and sea-grant institution. Colloquially, most people call the university "Louisiana State University" or "LSU". In sports competitions, "LSU" is almost always associated with Louisiana State University and A&M College''' '''or the LSU Athletics Department.
