

'''''The Exterminators''''' (renamed ''Billy the Exterminator'' in 2010) is an United States|American television programme|television program. The program follows Vexcon employees, ''Billy'' and ''Ricky Breatherton'' as they try to rid pests and unwanted animals from clients homes and businesses in Louisiana.
