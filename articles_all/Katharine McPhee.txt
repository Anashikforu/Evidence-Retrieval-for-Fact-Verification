
'''Katharine Hope McPhee''' (born March 25, 1984) is an Americans|American pop music|Pop/R&B singer. She was the runner-up to Taylor Hicks on the fifth season of the television programme|television show American Idol in 2006. Her first album, named ''Katharine McPhee'', was released January 30, 2007.
