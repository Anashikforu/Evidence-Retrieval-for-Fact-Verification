
'''Paul James Hartley''' (born October 19, 1976) is a scotland|Scottish Football (soccer)|football Footballer|player. He currently plays for Scottish_Football_League#Celtic_F.C.|Celtic and the Scottish national team.

Hartley used to play for Hamilton Academical, Millwall, Raith Rovers, Hibernian, Greenock Morton, St Johnstone and Hearts.
