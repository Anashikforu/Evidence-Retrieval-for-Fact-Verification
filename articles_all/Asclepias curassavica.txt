

'''''Asclepias curassavica''''' (also known as the '''scarlet milkweed''', '''Mexican butterfly weed''', or '''bloodflower''') is a species of milkweed plant.  It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to Central America and South America.  It is now naturalized around the world.  It gets flowers from spring to fall.
