

'''Boysetsfire''' is an United States|American post-hardcore rock band from Newark, Delaware. Boysetsfire has five members in the band; the current band members are Nathan Gray, Chad Istvan, Robert Ehrenbrand, Josh Latshaw and Matt Krupanski.

 Band members 

;Current
Nathan Gray – lead vocals, keyboard instrument|keyboard 
Josh Latshaw – guitar, backing vocals
Chad Istvan – guitar, backing vocals, piano, keyboard 
Robert Ehrenbrand – bass guitar, backing vocals
Matt Krupanski - drums 

;Former
Rob Avery - Bass guitar
Darrell Hyde - Bass guitar
