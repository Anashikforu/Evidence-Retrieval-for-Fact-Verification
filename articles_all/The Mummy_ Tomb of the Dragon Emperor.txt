

'''''The Mummy: Tomb of the Dragon Emperor''''' is a 2008 in movies|2008 United States|American adventure movie. It is a sequel to  ''The Mummy (1999 movie)|The Mummy'' and ''The Mummy Returns''. It was released on August 1, 2008 in the United States. Jet Li is in the movie also.

 Theatres 
The movie opened in Los Angeles on July 27, 2008.

 Critics ideas 
Most movie criticism|critics said the movie is bad. Roger Ebert of the ''Chicago Sun-Times'' liked it as "plain dumb fun."
