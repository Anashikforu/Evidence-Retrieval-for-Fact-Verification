

'''Martin Lee Truex Jr.''' (born June 29, 1980 in Mayetta, New Jersey|Mayetta, New Jersey) is an Americans|American racecar driver. Truex Jr. drives the No. 19 Toyota Camry for Joe Gibbs Racing in the NASCAR#Sprint cup|Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series. Truex won the 2004 and 2005 Nationwide Series championships. Truex's brother Ryan Truex is also a race car driver.
