
The '''Colorado Avalanche''' are an American ice hockey in the National Hockey League (NHL). They play their home games at Ball Arena in Denver, Colorado. They play in the Central Division (NHL)|Central Division of the Western Conference (NHL)|Western Conference. The team has won the Stanley Cup championship two times, in 1995–96 NHL season|1996 and in 2000–01 NHL season|2001.
