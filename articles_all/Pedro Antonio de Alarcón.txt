'''Pedro Antonio de Alarcón''' (10 March 1833 – 19 July 1891) was a Spain|Spanish writer. He was born in Guadix, near Granada. He was a 19th century novelist. He gained his first Literature|literary recognition with ''A Witness' Diary of the African War (1859-1860)'', a patriotism|patriotic account of the war|campaign.
