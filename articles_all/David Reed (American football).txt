
'''Brandon David Reed''' (born March 22, 1987 in Dubuque, Iowa|Dubuque, Iowa) is an Americans|American American football|football wide receiver who currently plays for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Reed was drafted out of the University of Utah by the Baltimore Ravens with the 156th pick in the fifth round of the 2010 NFL Draft. He won Super Bowl XLVII with the Ravens when they defeated the San Francisco 49ers 34-31.
