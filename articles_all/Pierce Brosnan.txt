
'''Pierce Brendan Brosnan'''  (born 16 May 1953) is an Irish people|Irish-Americans|American actor. Brosnan holds both Irish and United States|American citizenship. He was born in Drogheda, County Louth. He used to play the part of James Bond. He played the roles of Mr. Brunner and Chiron in ''Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief'', Stu Dunmeyer in ''Mrs. Doubtfire'', and was in ''Mamma Mia!''. He will play Doctor Fate in the 2022 DC Extended Universe movie ''Black Adam (movie)|Black Adam''.
