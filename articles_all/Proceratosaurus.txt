'''''Proceratosaurus''''' was a small dinosaur from the Middle Jurassic, about 165 million years ago. It was found in Gloucestershire, England, in 1910. It was a  carnivore|carnivorous theropod.

''Proceratosaurus'' was first believed to be an early ancestor of ''Ceratosaurus'', but it was actually an ancestor of the coelurosaurs.
