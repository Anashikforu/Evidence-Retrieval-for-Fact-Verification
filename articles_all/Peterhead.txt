

'''Peterhead''' is a town in Aberdeenshire, Scotland. It is Aberdeenshire's largest settlement, having a population of 17,947 at the United Kingdom Census 2001|2001 Census.
Sport
Peterhead F.C. are a Scottish Football League side who currently play in the Scottish Football League Second Division|Second Division.
