'''Collingwood Football Club''', the Magpies, is a club which plays Australian Rules Football in the Australian Football League|AFL. It comes from Collingwood, Victoria|Collingwood, a suburb of Melbourne in Victoria (Australia)|Victoria. In the AFL/VFL, it has won 15 premierships.

 History 

The Collingwood Football Club was formed in February 1892.  Collingwood played its first game in the Victorian Football Association on 7 May 1892, against Carlton Football Club|Carlton.  In 1902, Collingwood won its first premiership against the Essendon Football Club.

The Victorian Football League (VFL) formed in 1897.  Collingwood was one of the original teams (along with Fitzroy Football Club|Fitzroy, Melbourne Football Club|Melbourne, St Kilda Football Club|St Kilda, Carlton, Essendon Football Club|Essendon, South Melbourne Football Club|South Melbourne and Geelong Football Club|Geelong).  In the early years of the VFL, Collingwood was the most successful team; it won 11 premierships in the first 40 years. They won the most recent AFL premiership against St Kilda in 2010. The teams had to play a second match after the scores were equal at the end of the first Grand Final game. Triple M
