'''Greater Nottingham''' is a conurbation based around the city of Nottingham in Nottinghamshire, England.  

Unlike Greater Manchester, which is a metropolitan county and Greater London, which is a regions of England|region, Greater Nottingham not officially an administrative area.  But it is an own '''Nottingham Urban Area''' with a population of 666,358 at the time of the United Kingdom Census 2001|2001 census.

The Nottingham Urban Area includes Nottingham itself and the surrounding towns and villages of Arnold, Nottinghamshire|Arnold, Beeston, Nottinghamshire|Beeston, Breaston, Carlton, Nottinghamshire|Carlton, Clifton, Nottinghamshire|Clifton,  Eastwood, Nottinghamshire|Eastwood, Heanor, Hucknall, Ilkeston, Kimberley, Nottinghamshire|Kimberley,  Long Eaton, Ripley, Derbyshire|Ripley, Ruddington, Stapleford, Nottinghamshire|Stapleford, and West Bridgford, Nottinghamshire|West Bridgford.  This Nottingham Urban Area is separated from the Derby Urban Area only by a narrow gap between Breaston and Borrowash.  Similar narrow gaps exist between the Nottingham urban area and the Mansfield Urban Area and Alfreton Urban Area.
