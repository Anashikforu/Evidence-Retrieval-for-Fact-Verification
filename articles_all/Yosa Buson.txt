{{nihongo|'''Yosa Buson''' or '''Yosa no Buson'''|与謝 蕪村||extra=1716 &ndash; January 17, 1784 was a Japanese Haiku poet and painter, usually referred to simply as "Buson". He is one of the greatest poets of the Edo period, comparable with Matsuo Basho and Kobayashi Issa.

He was born in Osaka, his real family name was '''Taniguchi''' ().
