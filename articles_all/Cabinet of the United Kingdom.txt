In the government of the United Kingdom, the '''Cabinet''' is a group of 22 people led and chosen by the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Minister who are the main decision-makers in the British government. People in the cabinet have to be members of either the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons or the House of Lords.

All the Cabinet members are also members of the governing Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.
