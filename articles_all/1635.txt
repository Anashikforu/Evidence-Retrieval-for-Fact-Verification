
The year '''1635''' was a common year which start on Monday.

Events
 February 22 &ndash; The ''Académie française'' in Paris is founded as the national academy for the preservation of the French language.
 July 31 &ndash; The Royal Mail service is made available to the public by King Charles I of England.
 August 25 &ndash; The Great Colonial Hurricane strikes Narragansett Bay as a possible Category 3 hurricane, killing over 46 people.

=Date unknown=
 Dominica is claimed by France.
