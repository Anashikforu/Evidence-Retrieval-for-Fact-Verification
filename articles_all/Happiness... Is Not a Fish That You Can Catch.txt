

'''''Happiness... Is Not a Fish That You Can Catch''''' is the third studio album by Canadian Rock music|rock Our Lady Peace. It was released on September 21, 1999. The producer of the album was Arnold Lanni. It peaked at #2 on the Canadian Albums Chart.
