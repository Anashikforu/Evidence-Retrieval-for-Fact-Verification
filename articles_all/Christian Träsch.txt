

'''Christian Träsch''' (born 1 September 1987 in Ingolstadt, Bavaria) is a Germany|German footballer.

 Career 
Träsch came to the youth teams of TSV 1860 Munich in 2003. He played for their second senior team in the Regionalliga in 2006 and 2007. In 2007 he came to the second team of VfB Stuttgart, and on 3 February 2008 he played his first match in Stuttgart's Bundesliga team. 

Träsch joined the Germany national football team|German national football team for a trip to Asia in 2009 and played his first international match against the United Arab Emirates national football team|United Arab Emirates on 2 June 2009.
