
'''Braives''' is a Wallonia|Walloon municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Liège (province)|Liège. On January 1, 2006 Braives had a total population of 5,579. The total area is 44.00&nbsp;km² which gives a population density of 127 inhabitants per km².
It is at 50° 37 North, 05° 08 East.
