The '''Democratic National Committee''' ('''D.N.C.''' or '''DNC''') is a group of people who coordinate the activities of the United States Democratic Party|U.S. Democratic party in all fifty U.S. state|states. The D.N.C. is headquartered in the nation's capital (city)|capital, Washington, DC. It also has state United States Democratic Party|Democratic party headquarters in each of the 50 state capitals.

The current chairman is Jaime Harrison of South Carolina and the Deputy Chairman is Gretchen Whitmer of Michigan.
