'''The Fiesta Bowl''' is an United States|American college football Bowl Championship Series|bowl game at the University of Phoenix Stadium near Phoenix, Arizona.  It is the newest of the BCS games, as it was first played in 1971.  It has usually matched a team from the Western Athletic Conference or the Big 12 against a very good team from another :wikt:conference|conference.
