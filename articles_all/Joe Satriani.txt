
'''Joseph Satriani''' (born July 15, 1956 in Westbury, New York|Westbury, New York) is an Americans|American musician. He can play different musical instrument|instruments. He is most well known for his work as a rock music|rock guitarist. 

Early in his career, Satriani worked as a guitar teacher. Some of his former students have become famous with their guitar skills. These include Steve Vai, Larry LaLonde, Kirk Hammett, Charlie Hunter, Kevin Cadogan and Alex Skolnick. 

Satriani has been important in the music made by other musicians.  He was a founder of the ever-changing touring trio, G3 (tour)|G3, as well as performing in various positions with other musicians.

Awards and nominations
Satriani has the second most Grammy Award nominations of any artist without winning.
