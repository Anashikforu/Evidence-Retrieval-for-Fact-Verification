
'''Felicity Huffman''' (born December 9, 1962) is an Academy Award-nominated and Emmy Award-winning United States|American movie and television actress and convicted felon. She is well known for her role as Lynette Scavo, a busy mother on the American Broadcasting Company|ABC television show ''Desperate Housewives''. A year later, her role as a transwoman in the Art film|independent film ''Transamerica (movie)|Transamerica'' was praised by many critics and earned her a Golden Globe Award.

Personal life
Huffman dated actor William H. Macy on and off for 15 years before they married on September 6, 1997. They have three children, Sophia, Georgia and James. She has appeared on television, in movies, and on stage many times with her husband; the couple each received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame on March 7, 2012.

On March 12, 2019, Huffman was arrested in connection with an alleged 2019 college admissions bribery scandal|nationwide college entrance exam cheating scandal, charged with conspiracy to commit Mail and wire fraud|mail fraud and honest services mail fraud, and released on $250,000 bail.
