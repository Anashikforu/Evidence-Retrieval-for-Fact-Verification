
'''Lance Edward Armstrong''' (born September 18, 1971 in Plano, Texas, USA) is an Americans|American former bicycle rider. He used performance-enhancing drugs|drugs which helped him in competition. In 2012 the US Anti-Doping Agency (USADA) took away all his titles from 1998 to 2006. In 2012, he was also banned forever from cycling competitions.

Before his titles were taken away, he won the Tour de France seven times in a row, from 1999 to 2005.Maese, Rick. 
Armstrong retired from professional cycling in 2011.
