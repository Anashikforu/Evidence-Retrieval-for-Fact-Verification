
'''Alexei Yuryevich Zhamnov''' (born October 1, 1970 in Moscow, Soviet Union|URS) is a Russians|Russian retired professional ice hockey Centre (ice hockey)|centre. He is currently the head coach for the Kontinental Hockey League|KHL's Vityaz Chekhov. Zhamnov played a combined 14 seasons in the National Hockey League|NHL with four teams, 4 while part of the Winnipeg Jets (1972–96)|Winnipeg Jets, 8 while part of the Chicago Blackhawks, 1 while part of the Philadelphia Flyers and 1 while part of the Boston Bruins.

Zhamnov was also the captain of the Chicago Blackhawks from 2002-2004.
