'''Double reeds''' are a group of musical instruments in the woodwind family. Instead of a mouthpiece, the player blows air through a pair of Reed (instrument)|reeds that vibrate against each other. 

Examples of double reed woodwinds are the bassoon, oboe and Cor anglais (English horn).
