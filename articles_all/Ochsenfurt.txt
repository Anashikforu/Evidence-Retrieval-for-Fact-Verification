

'''Ochsenfurt'''  () is a town in Würzburg (district)|Würzburg, in Bavaria, Germany. Ochsenfurt is on the left edge of the River Main (river)|Main. This makes it the largest town in the Würzburg district.

Geography
=Place=
The town is on the left edge of the River Main (river)|Main,  south of Würzburg.

=Subdivision=
The ''Ortsteil|districts'' of Ochsenfurt are: Darstadt, Erlach, Goßmannsdorf, Hohestadt, Hopferstadt, Kleinochsenfurt, Tückelhausen, and Zeubelried.
