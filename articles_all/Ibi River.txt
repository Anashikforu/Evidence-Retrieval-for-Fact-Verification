Along with the Nagara River|Nagara and Kiso rivers, the Ibi is the third of the Kiso Three Rivers of the Nōbi Plain. It is one of Japan's first class rivers. The former Tōkaidō (road)|Tōkaidō post station of Kuwana-juku was on the western banks of this river during the Edo period.
