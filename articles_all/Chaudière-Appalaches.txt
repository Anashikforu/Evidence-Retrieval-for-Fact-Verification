 
'''Chaudière-Appalaches''' is region of Quebec, Canada. It is named for the Chaudière River and the Appalachian Mountains.

According to the Statistics Canada|Canadian 2006 census 393,669 people live in Chaudière-Appalaches. It also has an area of . Its main cities are Lévis, Quebec|Lévis, Saint-Georges, Quebec|Saint-Georges, Thetford Mines, Sainte-Marie, Quebec|Sainte-Marie and Montmagny, Quebec|Montmagny.
