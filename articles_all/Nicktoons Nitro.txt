

'''''Nicktoons Nitro''''' is a video game which is a sequel to ''Nicktoons Racing'' and ''Nicktoons Winners Cup Racing''. It features characters from the TV shows: ''SpongeBob SquarePants'', ''Danny Phantom'', ''The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius'', ''The Fairly OddParents'', ''Invader Zim'', ''Avatar: The Last Airbender'', ''Back at the Barnyard'', ''The Mighty B!'', ''Ricky Sprocket: Showbiz Boy'', ''Kappa Mikey'', ''Dora the Explorer'', ''Go, Diego, Go!'', ''Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!'', ''Ni Hao, Kai-Lan'', ''Maggie and the Ferocious Beast'', and ''My Life as a Teenage Robot'', The game features ''Mario Kart'' like gameplay. The game was released in 2009 as an arcade game by developer Raw Thrills.
