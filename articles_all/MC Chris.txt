

'''Christopher Ward''', better known as '''MC Chris''' (stylized as '''mc chris'''), is an Americans|American rapping|rapper, voice actor, writer, and comedian.

He has appeared and contributed original lyrics to many of Williams Street Studios' shows on Adult Swim, most notably ''Aqua Teen Hunger Force''.  Mc Chris is the voice of MC Pee Pants
