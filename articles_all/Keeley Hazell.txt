
'''Keeley Rebecca M. Hazell''' (born 18 September 1986) is a United Kingdom|British former Page Three girl|Page 3 Girl and glamour photography|glamour model. She was born in Lewisham  She went to Ravensbourne School (Bromley)|Ravensbourne School in Bromley. Her mother, Amber, is a lunch lady|dinner lady and her father, Roy, is a window fitter; they separated when she was thirteen.
