
'''Sindhi''' ( سنڌي, ''Sindhī'') is the language of the Sindh region of South Asia, which is a province of Pakistan. It is an Indo-Aryan languages|Indo-Aryan language spoken by about 41 million people in Pakistan and 12 million in the India|Republic of India. It was the "mother tongue" of Mohandas Gandhi|Gandhi and Muhammad Ali Jinnah.
