The '''Gulfport-Biloxi Metropolitan Statistical Area''' is a metropolitan area in the Mississippi Gulf Coast|Gulf Coast region that covers three county|counties â€“ Hancock County, Mississippi|Hancock, Harrison County, Mississippi|Harrison, and Stone County, Mississippi|Stone. As of the United States Census, 2000|2000 census, the MSA had a population of 246,190. The area was significantly impacted by Hurricane Katrina in 2005. A July 1, 2006, estimate placed the population at 227,904.  Prior to the hurricane, the area had experienced steady to moderate population growth. It is also part of the larger Gulfport-Biloxi-Pascagoula Combined Statistical Area.
