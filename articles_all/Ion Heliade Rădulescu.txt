'''Ion Heliade Rădulescu''' or '''Ion Heliade''' (also known as '''Eliad''' or '''Eliade Rădulescu''' January 6, 1802 – April 27, 1872) was a Wallachian-born Romanian academics|academic, Romantic and Classicist poet, essayist, short story writer, newspaper editor and politician.
