

A '''Beretta 92''' is a pistol and is one of the most famous pistols of the Italian company Beretta. It fires 9x19mm Parabellum|9x19mm cartridge (firearms)|cartridges and it can hold 15 of them. It is the standard side-arm of many police and military units and the United States Army. In the US Army, the pistol got the name '''M9'''.

After the US military ended its ban on foreign-made Weapon|weapons, its Alliance|allies in NATO pressured the US to adopt a 9mm Pistol|handgun to replace the M1911 pistol|M1911 because NATO has a rule that all NATO Country|countries must use weapons with the same kinds of ammunition.
