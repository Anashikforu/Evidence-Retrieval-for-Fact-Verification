The '''Audi A6''' is an automobile produced by Audi. It replaced the Audi 100 in 1994, and entered its fourth generation in 2011.

It is available in both Sedan (automobile)|sedan and station wagon bodystyles. It is also available in SUV bodystyle, called Audi Q7 quattro.
