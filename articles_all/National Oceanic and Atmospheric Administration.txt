


The '''National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration''' ('''NOAA''') is a science|scientific agency of the United States government that is focused to monitor the oceans surrounding the United States and the Earth's atmosphere|atmosphere. NOAA warns people for dangerous weather, charts seas and sky, guides the use and protection of ocean and coastal Natural resource|resources, and conducts research to help understanding and stewardship of the natural environment|environment. In addition to its civilian employees, NOAA research and operations are supported by 300 uniformed service members who make up the NOAA Corps.
