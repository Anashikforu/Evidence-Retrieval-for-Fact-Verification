The '''Animikie Group''' of sediments were deposited between 2.5 and 1.8 billion years ago, within the Animikie Basin. This group of formations are divided into the Gunflint Range, the Mesabi Range|Mesabi and Vermilion Range (Minnesota)|Vermilion ranges, and the Cuyuna Range.
