

'''Andrew David Lansley''' (born 11 December 1956 in Hornchurch, Essex, England) is a British people|British politician who was the Secretary of State for Health in the Conservative-Liberal Democrat coalition government from 2010 - 2012. He was the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party Member of Parliament (MP) for the South Cambridgeshire constituency in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected as an MP in the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election. He stepped down at the United Kingdom general election, 2015|2015 general election. 

He was made a Privy Council of the United Kingdom|Privy Counsellor on 13 May 2010.
