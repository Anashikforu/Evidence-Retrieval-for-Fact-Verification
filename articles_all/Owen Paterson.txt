
'''Owen William Paterson''' (born June 24, 1956 in Shropshire, England) is a United Kingdom|British politician who was the Secretary of State for Northern Ireland. He was a Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party Member of Parliament (MP) for the constituency of North Shropshire in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom.

He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election and first entered the shadow cabinet in 2007.
