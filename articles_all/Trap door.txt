

A '''trap door''' or '''trapdoor''' is a door in the ground or in a floor,  that one enters vertically. Some can open when someone is standing on it. People typically put small doors disguised as something normal to Fear|scare a person falling into a trapdoor. They can be used for malicious purposes like murder. However, they can be used to hide things from other people.  

Trap doors are popular in wikt:mystery|mystery themed movies. The victim who falls down the trap door often is met with danger or a hidden wikt:treasure|treasure.
