
'''F minor''' is a minor scale based on F. The harmonic minor raises the E.  Its key signature has four flats.

Its Relative key|relative major is A-flat major, and its parallel key|parallel major is F major.

F minor is a key that people often link with passion. Some famous pieces in the key of F minor are Ludwig van Beethoven|Beethoven's ''Piano Sonata No. 23 (Beethoven)|Appassionata Sonata'', Joseph Haydn|Haydn's Symphony No. 49 (Haydn)|Symphony No. 49 in F minor, ''La Passione'', and Antonio Vivaldi|Vivaldi's The Four Seasons.

E-sharp minor

The enharmonic equivalent of F minor is '''E-sharp minor'''. It is a minor scale based on the musical Note (music)|note E♯. Its key signature has six sharps and one double sharp.
