An '''allergen''' is something that causes allergies in humans.

Dust, pollen and pet dander are all common allergens. It is possible to be allergic to anything from chlorine to perfume. Food allergies are not as common, but some foods, like peanuts, Nut (fruit)|nuts, seafood and shellfish cause serious allergies in lots of people.
