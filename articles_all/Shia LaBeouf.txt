
'''Shia Saide LaBeouf''' (born June 11, 1986) is an Americans|American actor, comedy|comedian and movie director|director. He is best known for his work in the movies ''Transformers (movie)|Transformers'' (2007) and ''Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull'' (2008) and the Disney Channel Television programme|series ''Even Stevens''. His parents are Jeffery LaBeouf and Shayna Saide. LaBeouf is an only child. He started his career doing comedy|stand up comedy in local places such as Café|coffee houses. He wikt:graduate|graduated from high school in 2003 and hopes to attend college, preferably Yale University. He won the Orange Rising Star Award in 2008. He played Jerôme in the 2013 movie ''Nymphomaniac''.

 Family life 
LaBeouf is named after his grandfather who was also a stand up comedian. His parents are divorced. He lives with his mother in Los Angeles.
