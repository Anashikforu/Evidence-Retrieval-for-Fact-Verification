
'''Daniel Hamhuis''' (born December 13, 1982 in Smithers, British Columbia|Smithers, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey defencemen. He currently plays for the Nashville Predators of the National Hockey League|National Hockey League (NHL).

Hamhuis formerly played for the Vancouver Canucks for 6 seasons.

Hamhuis played in the Western Hockey League|WHL for the Prince George Cougars for 4 seasons and played for the Milwaukee Admirals in the American Hockey League|AHL.
