'''Lake Okeechobee''' is the largest lake in the U.S. state of Florida. Lake Okeechobee covers about 730 square miles (about half the size of Rhode Island). The lake is shared by Glades County, Florida|Glades, Okeecobee County, Florida|Okeechobee, Martin County, Florida|Martin, Palm Beach County, Florida|Palm Beach, and Hendry County, Florida|Hendry counties.
