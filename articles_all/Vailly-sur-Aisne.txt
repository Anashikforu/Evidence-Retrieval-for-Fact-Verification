


'''Vailly-sur-Aisne''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is in the Picardie Regions of France|region in the Aisne Departments of France|department. This in the north of France. In 2012, 2,055 people lived there.

In July 1429, Charles VII of France along with Joan of Arc and after his coronation in Reims, stopped in Vailly where he spent the night there.
