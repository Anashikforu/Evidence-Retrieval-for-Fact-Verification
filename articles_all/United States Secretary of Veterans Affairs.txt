
The '''United States Secretary of Veterans Affairs''' is the head of the United States Department of Veterans Affairs, the department in charge of taking care of the wikt:affair|affairs of  war veterans. The Secretary is a member of the United States Cabinet|Cabinet and second to last at 17th in the United States presidential line of succession|line of succession to President of the United States|the presidency (the position was last until the United States Department of Homeland Security was added in 2006
When the position of Secretary is vacant, the United States Deputy Secretary of Veterans Affairs. Accessed 2008-01-13. or another person picked by the President serves as Acting Secretary until the President picks and the United States Senate confirms a new Secretary.
