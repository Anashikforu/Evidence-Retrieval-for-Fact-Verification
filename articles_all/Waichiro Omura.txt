Biography
Omura was born in Shizuoka Prefecture on January 1, 1933. After graduating from Rikkyo University, he played for Tanabe Mitsubishi Pharma SC|Tanabe Pharmaceutical.

In June 1956, he was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for Football at the 1956 Summer Olympics|1956 Summer Olympics qualification. At this qualification, on June 3, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea. In November, he played at 1956 Summer Olympics in Melbourne. He also played at Football at the 1958 Asian Games|1958 Asian Games. He played 5 games for Japan until 1958.
