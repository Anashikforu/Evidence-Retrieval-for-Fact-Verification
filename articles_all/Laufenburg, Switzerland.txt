

'''Laufenburg''' is a municipality of the Switzerland|Swiss Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Canton of Aargau|Aargau. It is the capital city of the district with the same name.

Since 2010, Sulz, Aargau|Sulz is part of the municipality.
