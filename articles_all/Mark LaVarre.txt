
'''Mark LaVarre''' (born February 21, 1965 in Evanston, Illinois|Evanston, Illinois) is a retired American professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|right winger who played a career total of 78 games in the National Hockey League|NHL. He played his entire NHL career with the Chicago Blackhawks|Chicago Black Hawks. He was drafted by the Chicago Black Hawks with the 119th overall pick in the sixth round of the 1983 NHL Draft.
