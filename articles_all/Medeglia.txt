


'''Medeglia''' is a former municipality of the district Bellinzona (district)|Bellinzona in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 21 November 2010 the former municipalities of Bironico, Camignolo, Medeglia, Rivera, Switzerland|Rivera and Sigirino merged to form the new municipality of Monteceneri.
