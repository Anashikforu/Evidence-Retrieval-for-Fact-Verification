

'''''Until the End of Time''''' is a Rhythm and blues|R&B song written by Justin Timberlake, Timbaland|Tim Mosley, and Danjahandz|Nate Hills and is the first single from the deluxe edition of his studio album ''FutureSex/LoveSounds''.

 Music video 
A music video has been shot at the three major destinations: Philippines, Indonesia and Los Angeles (US) on October 24, 2007. The video was released on MTV, November 22, 2007.
