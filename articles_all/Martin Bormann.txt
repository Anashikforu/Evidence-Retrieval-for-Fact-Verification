
'''Martin Ludwig Bormann''' (17 June 1900 in Halberstadt – 2 May 1945 in Berlin) was a Germans|German politician and member of the Nazi Party. He was the private secretary of Adolf Hitler. He was also a member of Hitler's Cabinet.

In 1923, he took part in the Coup d'état|Munich putsch. At this time, he became one of Hitler's closet advice|advisors.
