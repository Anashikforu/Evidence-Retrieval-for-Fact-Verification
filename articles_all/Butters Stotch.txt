
'''Leopold "Butters" Stotch''' (voiced by Matt Stone) is a popular fictional character in the Animation|animated television programme|television series ''South Park''. His nickname is a play on the word ''butterscotch''. The creators have said in more than one DVD commentaries that Butters is one of their "top 3 favorite characters". Butters is based on Eric Stough, the animation director of the show.
