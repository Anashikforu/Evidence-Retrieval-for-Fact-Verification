'''The Christian Community''' (original German language|German name: ''Die Christengemeinschaft'') is a Christian denomination. It was founded in 1922 in Dornach (Switzerland) by a group of Theology|theologians and pastors, led by the Protestantism|Protestant pastor Friedrich Rittelmeyer. He was helped by Rudolf Steiner, the Austrian philosopher and founder of anthroposophy.
