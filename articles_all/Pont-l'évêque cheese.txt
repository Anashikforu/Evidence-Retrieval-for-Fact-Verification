
'''Pont-l'évêque''' is a soft cheese from Normandy. It is produced in the village with the same name. It has had an Appellation d'Origine Contrôlée|AOC since 1976, which means it can only be made in Pont-l'Évêque, Calvados. It is made from the milk of cows.
