
'''Philology''' considers both morphology (linguistics)|form and Meaning (linguistics)|meaning in writing, such as text or manuscript, and linguistic expression. It combines linguistics and literary studies.

Classical philology is the philology of the Ancient Greek|Greek, Latin and Sanskrit languages.
Any classical language can be studied philologically, and indeed describing a language as "classical" is to :wikt:imply|imply the existence of a philological tradition associated with it.
