
'''David John Mellor,''' Queen's Counsel|QC (born March 12, 1949 in Wareham, Dorset, England) is a United Kingdom|British politician who was an MP from 1979 to 1997, as a member of the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.

Mellor became a barrister in 1972, and a Queen's Counsel in 1987 but is currently not practising. He became a Member of Parliament (MP) representing the London constituency of Putney, at the 1979 General Election. In 1990, John Major made him Chief Secretary to the Treasury but after a sex scandal in 1992, Mellor was forced to resign and lost his seat to the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party in the 1997 General Election.
