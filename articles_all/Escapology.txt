'''Escapology''' is the wikt:practice|practice of escaping from physical restraint|restraints or other traps. Escapologists (also called '''escape artists''') escape from handcuffs, straitjackets, cages, steel boxes, barrels, bags, burning buildings, aquarium|fish-tanks and other such traps, usually doing more than one of these at once. One well-known escapologist is Harry Houdini.
