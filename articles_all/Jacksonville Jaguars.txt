
The '''Jacksonville Jaguars''' are a professional American football team in Jacksonville, Florida. They are currently members of the American Football Conference South Division|Southern Division of the American Football Conference (AFC) in the National Football League (NFL). The Jaguars, and the Carolina Panthers, became part of the NFL as expansion teams in 1995.

Head coaches
 Tom Coughlin (1995–2002)
 Jack Del Rio (2003–2011)
 Mel Tucker (interim, 2011)
 Mike Mularkey (2012)
 Gus Bradley (2013-)
