
The '''2028 Summer Olympics''', which will be officially known as the '''Games of the XXXIV Olympiad''', is a sporting event that will happen from July 21 to August 6 in Los Angeles, California, United States.

Applying to host the games was supposed to begin in 2019. The host city was supposed to be announced in 2021. In July 2017, The International Olympic Committee decided to award 2024 and 2028 Games at the same time.

Candidate City
 '''Los Angeles, California, United States''' (chosen)
