The '''diamondback rattlesnake''' is a very poisonous rattlesnake. Diamondbacks are found from Florida to California. The bite from a big rattlesnake can hold enough poison to kill a person. However, if the victim gets to a Medical doctor|doctor quickly enough, he or she can usually survive. People who spend time in regions where diamondback rattlesnakes live should take a few wikt:precaution|precautions. People must be especially careful around caves. Rattlesnakes sometimes wikt:hibernate|hibernate in caves during cool weather. People can listen carefully for its unusual rattling noise, to know where it is hiding.
