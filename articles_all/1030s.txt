
The '''1030s''' is the fourth decade of the 11th century.

 Events 
= Europe =
 1030 &ndash; On July 29, during the Battle of Stiklestad of Norway, Olav Haraldsson lost to the people he ruled and is killed in the fight. The people he lost to were called "''pagan''" because they were not Christianity|Christian. Later on, Olav is made a Canonization|saint and is called the patron saint of Norway and ''Rex perpetuum Norvegiae'' ('the eternal king of Norway', or 'King of Norway forever').
 1030 &ndash; The city of Kaunas in Lithuania is founded.
 1030 &ndash; The city of Tartu in Estonia is founded.
 1030 &ndash; Thalwil, Switzerland, is talked about for the first time
 1030 &ndash; Henry I of France fights against his father Robert II of France|Robert.
 1031 &ndash; Collapse of the Moors|Moorish Caliphate of Caliph of Córdoba|Córdoba.
 1031 &ndash; Byzantine Empire|Byzantine general George Maniaces captures Edessa, Mesopotamia|Edessa.
 1031 &ndash; Henry I of France|Henry I becomes List of French monarchs|King of France
 1031 &ndash; Bezprym seizes the throne of Poland from Mieszko II Lambert
 1034 &ndash; On April 11, Empress Zoe of Byzantium marries her chamberlain and elevates him to the throne of the Byzantine Empire|Eastern Roman Empire as Michael IV.
 1034 &ndash; Free County becomes subject to the Holy Roman Empire.
 1035 &ndash; Harold Harefoot|Harald I becomes List of English monarchs|King of England.
 1035 &ndash; Harthacanute becomes king of Denmark.
 1035 &ndash; Magnus I of Norway|Magnus I becomes king of Norway.
 1035 &ndash; William II (the future William I of England) becomes duke of Normandy.
 1035 &ndash; Construction on the cathedral of Saint Sabino begins in Bari.
 1035 &ndash; Koper is awarded town privileges|town rights.
 1036 &ndash; Edward the Confessor's younger brother Alfred Aetheling is blinded and murdered in an apparent attempt to seize the throne of England from Harold Harefoot|Harold I.
 June 4, 1039 — Henry III, Holy Roman Emperor|Henry III becomes King of Germany. 
 1039 &ndash; The Abbey of Bec is founded.
 1039 &ndash; Bretislav I of Bohemia invades Poland.

= Asia =
 1030 &ndash; The Song Dynasty China|Chinese historian and scholar Ouyang Xiu earned his ''jinshi'' degree at the age of 22 by passing the Imperial examinations|highest level tests in the country. This was his third time to take the test. This degree let him work for the government as a scholar-bureaucrat|scholar-official.
 1030 &ndash; Byzantine Emperor Romanus III attacks Syria.
 1030 &ndash; Masud begins to rule the Ghaznavid Empire after the death of his father Mahmud.
 1036 &ndash; Emperor Go-Suzaku ascends the throne of Japan.
