

'''Maiasaura''' was a large, plant-eating, duck-billed dinosaur. Maiasaura was the first dinosaur that was found alongside its young, Egg (biology)|eggs, and nests. This suggests that Maiasaura nurtured its young.

 About Maiasaura 
Maiasaura lived about 80 to 65 million years ago in the late Cretaceous period. It was described as a kind and good natured dinosaur who ate plants, leaf|leaves and berry|berries. It ate no meat at all since it was a herbivore. Its name Maiasaura means “Good Mother Lizard”. It was named this because scientists discovered that it looked after its young. Maiasaura measured about 9&nbsp;metres long, 2-2.5 metres tall and weighed around 1800 kilogram|kg.

 Where they live 
Maiasaurs fossils were found in Montana, United States.
