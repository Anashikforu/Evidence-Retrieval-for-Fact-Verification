'''Michael John "Mike" Myers''',  (born 25 May 1963) is a Canadians|Canadian actor, comedian, Movie director|director, producer and screenwriter. He was a comedian on ''Saturday Night Live'' from 1989 to 1995. He has acted in movies including: ''Wayne's World'', ''Austin Powers'', ''Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me'', ''Austin Powers in Goldmember'', ''Shrek'' and ''The Love Guru''.

Myers was born to English people|English parents and raised in Scarborough, Toronto|Scarborough, Ontario, Canada. He holds three citizenships: Canadians|Canadian, British people|British and Americans|American.
