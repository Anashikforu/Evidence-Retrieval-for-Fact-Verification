

'''D''' is the fourth (number 4 (number)|4) letter in the alphabet. It is derived from the Greek alphabet|Greek Delta and the Phoenician alphabet|Phoenician Dalet.

 Meanings for D 
 In education, D is a barely passing Grade (education)|grade.
 In electronics, D is a standard size dry cell battery (electricity)|battery.
 In music, D is a Note (music)|note.
 In Roman numerals, D also means the number 500 (number)|500.
 In computer programming, D is a programming language.
