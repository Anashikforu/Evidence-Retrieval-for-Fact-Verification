

'''Northern Rhodesia''' was a territory in southern Africa|south central Africa. It was founded in 1911. It became wikt:independent|independent in 1964 as Zambia.

The term "Rhodesia (name)|Rhodesia" in geography meant a region which is now Zambia and Zimbabwe. From 1964 on, it only meant Southern Rhodesia.
