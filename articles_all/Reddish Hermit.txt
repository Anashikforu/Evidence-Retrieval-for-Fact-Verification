

The '''reddish hermit''' (''Phaethornis ruber'') is a species of hummingbird. It is found in Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, Venezuela, and in the Guianas. The reddish hermit is 7.5&nbsp;cm in length, which makes it one of the smallest hermits. Its wikt:natural|natural habitats are forest and woodland, mostly Humidity|humid.
