'''Surf City''' is a town in Pender County, North Carolina|Pender and Onslow County, North Carolina|Onslow counties in the U.S. state of North Carolina. As of 2000, 1,393 lived in the town.

Surf City is located at  (34.428137, -77.543198). According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 5.3&nbsp;square miles (13.7&nbsp;km²). 4.2&nbsp;square miles (10.9&nbsp;km²) of it is land and 1.1&nbsp;square miles (2.8&nbsp;km²) of it (20.45%) is water.
