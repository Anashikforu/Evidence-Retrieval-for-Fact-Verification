
 Events 
 Roman Emperor Trajan completes his invasion of Parthia by capturing the cities of Seleucia on the Tigris|Seleucia, Babylon, Ctesiphon and Susa, marking the high-water mark of the Roman Empire's eastern expansion.
 Trajan removes Osroes I of Parthia|Osroes I as king of Parthia, and appoints his son Parthamaspates of Parthia|Parthamaspates in his place. Parthamaspates Romanizes his name to ''Parthicus''.
 A Jewish uprising against Rome fails.
