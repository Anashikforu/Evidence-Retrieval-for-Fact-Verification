The '''Invasion of Grenada''', codenamed '''Operation Urgent Fury''', was an invasion of the nation of Grenada ordered by President of the United States|President Ronald Reagan. Grenada is an island in the Caribbean Sea, 100 miles north of Venezuela, and over 1,500 miles south east of the United States. It was invaded by the combined force of troops from the United States (nearly 10,000 troops), Jamaica and members of the Regional Security System (RSS) (approximately 300 troops).
