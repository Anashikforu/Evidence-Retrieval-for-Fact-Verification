
'''Robert Laurence "Bob" Barr, Jr.'''

(born November 5, 1948) is the Libertarianism|Libertarian Party nominee for President of the United States in the United States presidential election, 2008|2008 election.  He is  a  former federal prosecutor
and a former member of the United States House of Representatives.

 He represented Georgia's 7th congressional district as a Republican Party (United States)|Republican from 1995 to 2003.

Barr is known as one of the leaders of the impeachment of President Bill Clinton.  Barr joined the Libertarian Party in 2006,
and until May 2008 served on its Libertarian National Committee|National Committee.
