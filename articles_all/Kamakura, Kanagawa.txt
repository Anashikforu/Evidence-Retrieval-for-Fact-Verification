In 1250, Kamakura was the 4th largest city in the world with 200,000 people.
Kamakura was made a municipality on November 3, 1939.  

Today, Kamakura is famous as an Upper class|upper-class area for people to live. This is because of the history and because it is close to the mountains and the sea.

Tsurugaoka Hachimangu Shrine is one of the three major Hachimangu located in Kamakura. It is the most popular spot in Kamakura, especially, Yabusame in September is very famous. Tsurugaoka Hachimangu was built in 1063 and became the capital and center of Kamakura Government established by Minamoto no Yoritomo. Turugaoka Hachiman-gu has many beautiful maple and ginkgo trees.
