
'''Catawba County''' is a county in the U.S. state of North Carolina.  In 2000, 141,685 people lived there.  Its county seat is Newton, North Carolina|Newton.

 History 
The county was made in 1842 from Lincoln County, North Carolina|Lincoln County.

 Government 
Catawba County is part of the local Western Piedmont Council of Governments.
