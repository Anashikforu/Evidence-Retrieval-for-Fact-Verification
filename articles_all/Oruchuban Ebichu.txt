In 1990 to 2007, the manga was published in the magazine Giga&chan (ギガ/Shan). It was published in the magazine ACTION PIZAZZ (アクションピザッツ) in 2007 and 2008. After 1999, The manga was published in book form. In 1999, it was shown on Television on DirecTV).

 Plot 
this story is about a housekeeper hamster named Ebichu and her master, Gosujinjama,  who she calls Office Lady (OL).
Ebichu is a hamster. She is a faithful and very good housekeeper. She often does not have any wikt:Tact|tact. This often leads to her being abused yo OL. Gosujinjama is 25 years old (29 in the manga). She is has a bad temper.
