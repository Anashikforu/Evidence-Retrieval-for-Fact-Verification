
'''Telugu Cinema''' (Telugu: తెలుగు సినీపరిశ్రమ), means movies and movie-making in the Telugu language. Another word for Telugu Cinema is '''Tollywood'''. This is Portmanteau word|portmanteau made of the words "Telugu" and "Hollywood".

'''Tollywood''' movies are produced in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana  states in the India|Republic Of India.
