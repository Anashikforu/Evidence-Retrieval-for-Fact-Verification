Biography
Ohashi was born in Hiroshima Prefecture on April 21, 1934. After graduating from Waseda University, he played for Yuasa Batteries SC|Yuasa Batteries and his local club Sanfrecce Hiroshima|Toyo Industries. In 1965, Toyo Industries joined new league Japan Soccer League. He retired in 1967. He played 3 games and the club also won the championship for 3 years in a row (1965-1967) in the league.

In May 1958, Ohashi was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for Football at the 1958 Asian Games|1958 Asian Games. At this competition, on May 26, he debuted against Philippines national football team|Philippines.

After retirement, in 1971, Ohashi became a manager for Sanfrecce Hiroshima|Toyo Industries as Yukio Shimomura successor. He managed the club until 1975.

Ohashi died on December 21, 2015 at the age of 80.
