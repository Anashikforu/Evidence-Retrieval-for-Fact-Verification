Biography
Nakamura was born in Fujieda, Shizuoka|Fujieda on April 8, 1955. After graduating from Hosei University, he joined Japan Soccer League club Kawasaki Frontale|Fujitsu in 1978. In 1978 season, however he played 11 games and scored 3 goals, the club was relegated to Division 2. He retired in 1981.

On March 4, 1979, Nakamura debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against South Korea national football team|South Korea. In this match, he scored a goal and Japan won the match. He played 5 games and scored 1 goal for Japan in 1979.
