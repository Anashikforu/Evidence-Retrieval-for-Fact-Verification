
'''Michael Greg "Mike" Mizanin''' (born October 8, 1980), is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler, reality television star, and actor. He currently competes for the WWE, where he wrestles under the ring name '''The Miz''', where he is the current WWE Intercontinental Championship|WWE Intercontinental Champion in his seventh reign. Before joining the WWE, The Miz was a cast member on MTV's The Real World: Back to New York and its spinoff series, The Challenge (TV series)|Real World/Road Rules Challenge. He was also the first runner-up during the fourth season of WWE's WWE Tough Enough|Tough Enough.

The Miz is a one-time WWE World Heavyweight Champion|WWE Champion, seven-time WWE Intercontinental Championship|Intercontinental Champion, two-time WWE United States Championship|United States Champion, three-time WWE Tag Team Championship|WWE Tag Team Champion with John Morrison (wrestler)|John Morrison, Big Show, and John Cena, two-time World Tag Team Championship (WWE)|World Tag Team Champion with John Morrison and Big Show, Raw's Money in the Bank ladder match|Mr. Money in the Bank in 2010, two-time Slammy Award winner and the 25th Triple Crown Champion.
