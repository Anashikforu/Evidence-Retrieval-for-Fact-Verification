The '''Provincially Administered Tribal Areas''' (PATA) are administrative subdivisions in the Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa provinces of Pakistan. 

 List of PATA 
According to article 246(b) of the 1973 Constitution of Pakistan, the following areas are PATA:

= Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK) =
 Chitral District
 Dir District
 Swat (Pakistan)|Swat District (including Kalam)
 The Tribal Area in Kohistan District, Pakistan|Kohistan District
 Malakand District
 The Tribal Area adjoining (Batagram, Allai and Black Mountain of Hazara),Mansehra District.
 The former state of Amb (princely state)|Amb.

= Balochistan =
 Zhob District
 Loralai District (excluding Duki Tehsil)
 Dalbandin Tehsil of Chagai District
 Kohlu District|Marri Tribal Territory in Sibi District
 Dera Bugti District|Bugti Tribal Territory in Sibi District
