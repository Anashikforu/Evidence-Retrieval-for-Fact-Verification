
'''Gotha''' is a Kreis (Rural Districts of Germany|rural district) in the middle of Thuringia, Germany.

 History 
The district was made on October 1, 1922, when the newly created state of Thuringia was divided into 15 rural districts ().

Partnerships
Since 1990 the district has had a partnership with Main-Kinzig Rural District in Hesse, Germany.
