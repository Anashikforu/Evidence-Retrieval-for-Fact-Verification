
'''HTC Corporation''' is a manufacturer of smartphones in Taiwan. It used to be called High Tech Computer Corporation and now is called '''HTC''' by lots of people. The company first made smartphones based on Microsoft's Windows Mobile operating system, but in 2009 it started to make smartphones based on Android (operating system)|Android operating system.

HTC is also a member of the Open Handset Alliance, a group of manufacturers and mobile network operators helping to improve the Android (operating system)|Android operating system.
