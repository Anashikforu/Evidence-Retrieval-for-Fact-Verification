

'''''Jackass''''' was an United States|American reality television comedy television series|series which was first shown on MTV on October 1, 2000. The series centers around the cast and celebrity guests performing lots of dangerous, crude, ridiculous, and self-injuring stunts and pranks. The show has spawned three movies ''Jackass: The Movie'', ''Jackass 2'' and ''Jackass 3-D''. The show has also made several spin-off shows including ''Viva La Bam'', ''Wildboyz'', ''Homewrecker (TV series)|Homewrecker'', ''Dr. Steve-O'' and ''Blastazoid'' and a video game called Jackass: The Game.
