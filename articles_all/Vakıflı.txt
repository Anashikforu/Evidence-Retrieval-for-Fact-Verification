'''Vakıflı Köyü''' ( — Vakif) is the only ethnic Armenians|Armenian village in Turkey. It is on the slopes of Musa Dagh in the Samandağ district of Hatay Province. It has a view of the Mediterranean Sea. From the village, people can see the Syrian border. It has a population of about 130 people.
