
'''Midland''' is a city in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Texas. It is called Midland, because it is the midway point between two bigger cities in Texas, Fort Worth, Texas|Fort Worth and El Paso, Texas|El Paso. Oil was discovered in the region in 1923 and is still a larger part of the economy.

Midland is also famous as the hometown of former First Lady of the United States|First Lady Barbara Bush|Barbara Walker Bush. She and her husband, former president George H. W. Bush lived there, with their children including the later President (United States)|US President George W. Bush and his wife Laura Welch Bush|Laura, and his brother Jeb Bush, the former Governor of Florida.
