

'''Taylor Reuben Hicks''' (born October 7, 1976) is an United States|American singer, songwriter and musician. In 2006, he won the fifth season of ''American Idol''. His runner-up was Katharine McPhee. After he won, he was signed to Arista Records. His album called ''Taylor Hicks'' was released on December 12, 2006.
