'''Stone Sour''' was a Rock music|rock band from Iowa, United States. Their singer is also the lead singer in Slipknot (Corey Taylor). They are signed to Roadrunner Records and have released six albums. They have been wikt:nominate|nominated for three Grammy Awards, one in 2005 and two in 2006.
