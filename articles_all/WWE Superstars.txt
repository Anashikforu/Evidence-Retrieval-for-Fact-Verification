'''''WWE Superstars''''' was a professional wrestling television program for WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) that features wrestlers from all WWE's brands: WWE Raw|Raw, WWE SmackDown|SmackDown and, at one time, ECW (WWE)|ECW competing against each other. It was aired between April 16, 2009 and November 25, 2016.
