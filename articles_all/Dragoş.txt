

'''Dragoş''' (''Drágfi of Béltek'') was a Maramureş region|Maramureş Voivode ruling over the lands of what was to become Moldavia (between 1351 and 1353). He left Maramureş by orders from the Kingdom of Hungary|Hungarian List of Hungarian rulers|King Louis I of Hungary|Louis I, in order to establish a defense line against the Golden Horde. He was succeeded by his son, Sas of Moldova|Sas (''Szász'' or ''Sas of Béltek'') (ruled 1354-1358).
