The '''Green Monster''' (often known simply as '''The Monster''') is the nickname of the green, 37-foot, two-inch (11.3 m) left field wall at Fenway Park baseball park, where the Boston Red Sox play home (sports)|home games.  Seats were added on top of the Green Monster just in time for the 2003 MLB season.
