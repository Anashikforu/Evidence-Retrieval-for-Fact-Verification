


'''Teal''' is a deep blue-green color; a dark cyan.

''Teal'' gets its name from the colored area around the eyes of the common teal, a member of the duck family.  

The first written use of ''Teal'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1917. Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 205; color sample of Teal: Page 101 Plate 39 Color Sample L7
