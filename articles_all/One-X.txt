

'''''One-X''''' is the second studio album by Canada|Canadian rock music|rock band, Three Days Grace. It was released on June 13, 2006. The producer of the album was Howard Benson. The album peaked at #5 on the U.S. Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200 and #2 on the Canadian Albums Chart. The songs "Riot (song)|Riot" and "Animal I Have Become" were featured in the video game, ''WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2010''.
