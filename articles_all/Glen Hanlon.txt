
'''Glen A. Hanlon''' (; born February 20, 1957 in Brandon, Manitoba) is a Canadians|Canadian retirement|retired National Hockey League (NHL) goaltender and a former head coach of the Washington Capitals of the NHL, Dynamo Minsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL), and the Belarusian national ice hockey team. He was a goaltender scout for the Vancouver Canucks. On March 24, 2010 he was named head coach of Slovak national ice hockey team.
