


'''''Asclepias fascicularis''''' (also known as the '''narrow-leaf milkweed''' or '''Mexican whorled milkweed''') is a species of milkweed plant. It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to the western United States.  It blooms from June to September.  This species is found in dry areas and valleys.
