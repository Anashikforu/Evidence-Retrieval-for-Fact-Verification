'''John Felix Anthony Cena Jr.''' (born April 23, 1975) is an Americans|American  professional wrestling|professional wrestler and actor. He currently works for the WWE. Cena has won the WWE Championship|WWE Championship/WWE World Heavyweight Championship thirteen times, the World Heavyweight Championship (WWE)|World Heavyweight Championship thrice, the WWE United States Championship|United States Championship five times and the World Tag Team Championship (WWE)|World Tag Team Championship twice. Also, Cena won the Royal Rumble in 2008 and 2013. As for his musical career, Cena has released an album called ''You Can't See Me|you cant see me''. Cena has also been featured in the movies ''The Marine'', ''12 Rounds (movie)|12 Rounds'' and ''Legendary (movie)|Legendary''. He will star as Peacemaker (comics)|Peacemaker in the 2022 movie ''The Suicide Squad (movie)|The Suicide Squad'' and his own Peacemaker (TV series)|television series for HBO Max. His most famous catchphrase is "You Can't See Me" which has become a very well-known meme in Western Society. 

In Baseball, he is a Boston Red Sox|Red Sox fan turned Tampa Bay Rays|Rays fan. 

 Career 
= World Wrestling Entertainment (2001-present) =
Cena was signed by then Person in charge of Talent Relations, Jim Ross on behalf on WWF. According to Ross, when he returned to Connecticut after signing Cena he walked into Vince McMahon's office and told him "I just signed your main event for WrestleMania 22|WrestleMania in 5 years." Cena would eventually make his WrestleMania debut 3 years later at WrestleMania XX winning the WWE United States Championship from Big Show|The Big Show, and his Main event WrestleMania debut WrestleMania 21|4 years later winning the WWE Championship, from John Layfield|John "Bradshaw" Layfield.

During the match between Barrett and Orton, Orton won so Cena was forced to retire. He was rehired by Wade Barrett and defeated him at TLC: Tables, Ladders and Chairs Pay Per View.

He was drafted to SmackDown as the first pick in the WWE Draft|2011 WWE Draft, but before the end of the night, he was drafted back to Raw.

He was defeated by Dwayne Johnson|The Rock at WrestleMania XXVIII. He won his second Royal Rumble in January 2013. Thus he got another match at WrestleMania XXIX against The Rock for WWE Championship. He won that match and became the WWE Champion. He retired the next night on Raw after getting defeated by the debuting Dominik Mysterio, the son of professional wrestler Rey Mysterio. He was inducted in the 1938 WWE Hall of Fame.
