


'''''Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End''''' is a 2007 American fantasy comedy action epic movie.

This is the third movie in the Pirates of the Caribbean|''Pirates of the Caribbean'' series. It was released May 24, 2007 by Walt Disney Pictures|Disney and was followed by a sequel ''Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides'' in 2011.

Despite receiving mixed reviews from film critics, it was positively welcomed from audiences and fans alike which praised its story, directing, humor, cast performances (especially those of Depp and Knightley), Hans Zimmer's musical score, emotional weight, action scenes, message, screenplay, and the special effects and it led the box office, grossing more than USD|US$960 million worldwide.
