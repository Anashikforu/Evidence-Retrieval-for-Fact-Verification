
'''Parliament''' is an underground Train station|railway station in Melbourne, Australia. It is part of the List of Melbourne railway stations|suburban train network. It is one of the five stations on the City Loop, Melbourne|City Loop. The city loop goes around the Melbourne central business district|central business district.

Parliament has 4 platforms on two level|levels, like the other to underground stations on the city loop. Each level is for the trains leaving. The different levels are for trains going to the north and west suburbs or to the south and east suburbs. The station opened on January 22 1983.

Many people who work for the government use the station because it is underneath the Parliament House of Victoria. It is also at the intersection of Bourke Street, Melbourne|Bourke Street and Spring Street, Melbourne|Spring Street. The List of Melbourne tram routes|tram routes 86, 95 and 96 on Bourke Street, tram routes 11, 12, 31, 42, 109, and 112 on Collins Street, Melbourne|Collins Street and the free City Circle (tram route)|City Circle tram stop near the station.

The lower platforms are 38 meters low. That means that they are the lowest in the city loop. Its escalators were the longest in the southern hemisphere, when the station opened.
