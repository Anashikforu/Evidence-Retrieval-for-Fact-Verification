
'''Saint-Trivier-de-Courtes''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department in the east of France. In 2014, 1,099 people lived in the commune. It is located 34&nbsp;km northeast of Mâcon. The inhabitants are the ''Trivicourtois''.
