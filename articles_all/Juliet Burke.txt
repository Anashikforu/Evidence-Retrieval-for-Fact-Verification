'''Juliet Burke''' is the name of a fictional character, a main character on the American Broadcasting Company|ABC's TV series '''''Lost (TV series)|Lost'''''.  She is played by the actress Elizabeth Mitchell. Juliet's character was introduced in the third season's premiere.  

Character's background
Juliet is a fertility doctor. She arrived on the Island in order to help pregnant women, as all of them die before giving birth to their child.
