'''Sohrab Sepehri''' (; October 7, 1928 - April 21, 1980) was an Iranian poet and painter. He was born in Kashan, Iran. His poems are in Persian language|Persian. His most famous poetry book is Hasht Ketab (Eight Books). His poems have been translated to French language|French, English language|English, Arabic language|Arabic, Italian language|Italian, Swedish language|Swedish, Hindi and Russian language|Russian. Sohrab Sepehri died in Tehran.
