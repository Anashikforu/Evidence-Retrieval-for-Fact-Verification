'''The Supreme Governor of the Church of England''' is a title which British Monarchs have to show their leadership over the Church of England. The title was first granted to Queen Elizabeth I of England|Elizabeth I after she passed the Act of Supremacy, which required all officials in the Church of England to state an oath of allegiance to her.
