


'''Rupert Alexander Lloyd Grint'''; born 24 August 1988) is an English people|English actor. He is best known for his role as Ron Weasley, one of the three main Fictional character|characters in the Harry Potter (movie series)|''Harry Potter'' series of movies.  He was cast as Weasley at age eleven. Before that, Grint had acted only in school Play (theatre)|plays and his local Theatre|theatre group. From 2001 to 2011, he starred in all eight ''Harry Potter'' movies.

Grint started to work outside of the ''Harry Potter'' franchise in 2002. He had a main role in ''Thunderpants''. In 2006, he was in ''Driving Lessons'', a Comedy-drama|dramedy movie. He was also in ''Cherrybomb (movie)|Cherrybomb'' in 2010. Grint was in the comedy movie|comedy ''Wild Target''  with Bill Nighy and Emily Blunt. His first movie project after the ''Harry Potter'' series was in the 2012 Anti-war movement|anti-war movie ''Into the White.'' In 2013, his movie ''CBGB (movie)|CBGB'' was released. That same year, he was cast in CBS's television program|program ''Super Clyde''. His first work on theatre|stage was in ''Mojo (play & movie)|Mojo'' in October 2013. It was at the Harold Pinter Theatre in London. In 2014, he was the voice of the character of Josh in ''Postman Pat: The Movie''. From 2017 to 2018, he Executive producer|executive-produced and starred in the programme ''Snatch (TV series)|Snatch'', It was based on the Snatch (movie)|movie of the same name. Since 2019, he has been in the Apple TV+ horror series ''Servant (TV series)|Servant''.

Grint supports several Charitable organization|charitable causes. Since 2011, he has been in a relationship with actress Georgia Groome. They have a daughter.
