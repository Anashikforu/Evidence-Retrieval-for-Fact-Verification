'''Holy Week''' (Latin: ''Hebdomas Sancta'' or ''Hebdomas Maior'', "Greater Week"; Ancient Greek|Greek: ''Μεγάλη Ἑβδομάς'', ''Megale Hebdomas'') is the commemoration of the last week of Jesus Christ's ministry on earth.  Holy Week is the last week of Lent.  It begins with Palm Sunday and leads up to Easter Sunday.
