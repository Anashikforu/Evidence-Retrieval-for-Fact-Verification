
'''Theodor Eike''' (October 17, 1892 in Hampont – February 26, 1943 near Kharkov) was an Obergruppenführer of the SS and General officer|General of the Waffen-SS.

He was the commander of the Dachau concentration camp|concentration camp of Dachau. During the WWII|World War II was he the leader of the 3rd SS Division Totenkopf|Totenkopf Division.

Eike was on behalf of the Nazi Party a member of the Reichstag (institution)|Reichstag. He was also a member of the Sturmabteilung|SA.
