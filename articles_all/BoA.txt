

'''Kwon Bo-ah''' (born 5 November 1986 in Guri, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea) is a South Korean singer. She is commonly known by her stage name '''BoA'''. She has been active in both South Korea and Japan. Aside from Korean language|Korean, BoA also speaks Japanese language|Japanese and English language|English and has released songs in those languages. She also released some Chinese language|Chinese songs, but she does not know Chinese. BoA succeeded not only in Korea, but in all of East Asia as well.

BoA was born and raised in Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. She was discovered by SM Entertainment talent agents when she accompanied her older brother to a talent search. In 2000, after two years of training, she released ID; Peace B, her debut Korean album. Two years later, she released her debut Japanese album, Listen to My Heart.
