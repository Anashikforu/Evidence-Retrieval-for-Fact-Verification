

'''''Eyes Open''''' is the fourth full-length album by the alternative rock band Snow Patrol. It was released May 1, 2006 internationally, and May 9, 2006 in the United States. The "Special Edition" of the album has a bonus DVD with music videos of "You're All I Have". ''Eyes Open'' was the best-selling album in the United Kingdom|UK in 2006, and sold more than one and a half million copies.

History

The album reached #1 in its 11th week on the New Zealand chart and reached 2x Music recording sales certification|Platinum there for shipping over 30,000 units.
