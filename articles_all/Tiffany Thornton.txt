

'''Tiffany Dawn Thornton''' (born February 14, 1986 in College Station, Texas|College Station, Texas, United States|U.S.) is an Americans|American actor|actress, comedian and singer, best known for her co-starring role as Tawni Hart on the Disney Channel Original Series, ''Sonny with a Chance.''

 Career
Thornton made her television acting debut on the pilot episode of the Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox sitcom, ''Quintuplets''. She then went on to guest star on ''8 Simple Rules'', ''American Dreams'', ''The O.C.'', ''Desperate Housewives'', ''That's So Raven'', ''Jericho (TV series)|Jericho'', ''Wizards of Waverly Place'' and ''Hannah Montana''. 

In 2009, she began her role as Tawni Hart on the Disney Channel Original Series, ''Sonny with a Chance'' and then went on to play a part in the Disney Channel Original Movie, ''Hatching Pete''.

 Personal life 
Thornton became engaged to her boyfriend of one year, Christopher Carney, a court officer, in December 2009. She has openly stated, "I am a Christian. I would have nothing without my faith."
