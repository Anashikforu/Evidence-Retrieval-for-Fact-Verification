
'''Pandemic Studios''' was a video game developer that was started in 1998. It became owned by Electronic Arts from 2007 to 2009. It was an Australian and United States|American company, with offices in Brisbane and Los Angeles.

Pandemic Studios developed the first two main titles of the successful Star Wars: Battlefront (series)|''Star Wars: Battlefront'' series; those were ''Star Wars: Battlefront'' and ''Star Wars: Battlefront II''. It also developed ''Full Spectrum Warrior'', the Mercenaries (series)|''Mercenaries'' series, and the last game in the ''Army Men'' series.

Their office in Brisbane was closed in February 2009.
