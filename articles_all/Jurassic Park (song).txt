"'''Jurassic Park'''" is a song by "Weird Al" Yankovic. It is a parody of "MacArthur Park (song)|MacArthur Park" by Jimmy Webb (as performed by Richard Harris (actor)|Richard Harris). It is a humorous retelling of the plot in the novel and film ''Jurassic Park''. It is on the album Alaplooza released in 1993.
