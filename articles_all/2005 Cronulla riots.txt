
The '''2005 Cronulla riots''' were a series of violent events between Lebanon|Lebanese and white Australian wikt:mob|mobs. Riots started in and around Cronulla, New South Wales|Cronulla, a suburb on the beach in Sydney, Australia's biggest city. The violence was soon also in other suburbs around Sydney. The riots happened on 11, 12 and 14 December. Many people committed crimes there.

Several people were sent to prison for being involved in the riot.
