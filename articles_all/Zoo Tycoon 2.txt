


'''''Zoo Tycoon 2''''' is a 2004 simulation video game|computer game. In the game, the player must create and run a zoo. This is done by managing its finances, employees and animal exhibits. The goal is to become a "zoo tycoon".

The game is a sequel the 2001 game ''Zoo Tycoon''. Six expansion packs have been created for the game. The latest is ''Zoo Tycoon 2: Extinct Animals''.
