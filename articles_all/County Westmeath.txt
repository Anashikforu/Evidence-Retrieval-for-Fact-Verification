'''Westmeath''' is a Counties of Ireland|county in the middle of Ireland. Westmeath and County Meath|Meath once formed a fifth Provinces of Ireland|province in Ireland known as Mide. Westmeath is known as the '''lakeland county'''. Its main town is Mullingar.
