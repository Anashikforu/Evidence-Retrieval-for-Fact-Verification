
'''Exposé''' is an United States|American pop music|pop-rock music|rock group. The group was formed in 1984. Members of the group included Ann Curless, Jeanette Jurado and Gioia Bruno. They had success in the late 1980s and early 1990s. Exposé are the first music group to have four top 10 hit songs on the Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100. The group have had over 25 million album|records worldwide.
