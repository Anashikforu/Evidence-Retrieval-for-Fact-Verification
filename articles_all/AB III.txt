
'''''AB III''''' is the third studio album by American Rock music|rock band Alter Bridge. It was released on October 8, 2010 in Germany and Australia, since then released worldwide through late 2010 and early 2011. It received critical acclaim, and debuted at #17 in the Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200 with more than 28,000 copies sold.

It contains one of Alter Bridge most successful songs, "Isolation (Alter Bridge song)|Isolation" which peaked at #1 at the Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks chart.
