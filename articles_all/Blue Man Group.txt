
'''The Blue Man Group''' is a performing arts group. It was formed in 1987 in New York City. The members paint themselves in blue, wear black clothes and wear caps that make their heads look bald. The band plays rock music and is very entertainment|entertaining and comedy|humorous. Family|Families of all ages can enjoy their performances.
