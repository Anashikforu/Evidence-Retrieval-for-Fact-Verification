'''Tatyana Ali''' (born January 24, 1979) is an United States|American R&B singer and actress. She is best known for playing Ashley Banks in ''The Fresh Prince of Bel Air''. She later played Roxanne in ''The Young and the Restless''.

Ali was born in North Bellmore, New York, a suburb of New York City.
