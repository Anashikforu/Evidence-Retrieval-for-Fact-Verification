
A '''brass band''' is a musical group that is made up of only brass instruments (such as the trumpet, tuba, and trombone) and a percussion|drum section.

There are several different types of brass bands. One of the more popular types of brass band is the England|British brass band. A British brass band usually has 25 or 26 players that play: cornets, flugelhorns, tenor horns, euphoniums, tenor trombone, bass trombone and tuba.
