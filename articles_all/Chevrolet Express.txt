'''Chevrolet Express''' and '''GMC Savana''' are full sized passenger or cargo vans made via General Motors. These vans seat between eight and fifteen passengers. The cargo vans are used for Commerce|commercial and business purposes. The Express/Savana vans were first produced around 1996.
