
'''Carteret County''' is a county in the U.S. state of North Carolina.  In 2000, 59,383 people lived there.  Its county seat is Beaufort, North Carolina|Beaufort. Most of the county is part of the Crystal Coast.

 Government 
Carteret County is part of the Eastern Carolina Council of Governments.
