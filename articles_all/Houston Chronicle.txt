
The '''''Houston Chronicle''''' is the largest daily newspaper in Texas, United States|USA. As of March 2007, it is the ninth largest newspaper circulating in the United States. With the demise of its long-time rival the ''Houston Post'', its nearest major competitors are located in Dallas, Texas|Dallas-Fort Worth, Texas|Fort Worth.
