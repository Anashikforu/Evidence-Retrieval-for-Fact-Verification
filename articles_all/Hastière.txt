'''Hastière''' is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Namur (province)|Namur. The area has seven sub-municipalities. They are called Hastière-Lavaux, Hermeton-sur-Meuse, Waulsort, Hastière-par-delà, Blaimont, Heer, and Agimont.

In 2007, 5278 people lived there. This grew to 5979 by 2018.
It is at 50° 13 North, 04° 49 East.

Hastière is on the Meuse|River Meuse, and its attractions include a church, gardens and the Castle of Freÿr.
