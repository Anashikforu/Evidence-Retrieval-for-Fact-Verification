

'''Noel Thomas David Gallagher''' (born 29 May 1967) is a British people|British musician and singer-songwriter. 

Gallagher was the former lead guitarist and main songwriter for the band Oasis (band)|Oasis. He left the band in 2009 following an argument with his younger brother and Oasis' frontman Liam Gallagher|Liam. 

In 2011 he released his first solo album ''Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds''. The band's second album ''Chasing Yesterday'' was released 2 March 2015.
