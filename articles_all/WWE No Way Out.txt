'''WWE No Way Out''' is a yearly pay-per-view made by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). The event is usually produced in February and is typically the last pay-per-view before WrestleMania (PPV series)|WrestleMania. The first ''No Way Out'' event was in 1998 as an WWF In Your House|In Your House event, called '''No Way Out of Texas: In Your House'''. The event did not take place in 1999, 2010 or 2011 but was brought back in 2000 and 2012.
