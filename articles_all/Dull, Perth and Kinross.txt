'''Dull''' is a village located in the county of Perth & Kinross in Scotland.  It is in the Highland part of the county. There is only one street of houses in Dull, on the north side of the valley of the River Tay.  The place-name means 'meadow' in Scottish Gaelic language|Gaelic.
