
"'''Not Afraid'''" is a 2010 Hip hop music|hip hop song by the United States|American rapper Eminem, and is the first single from his seventh studio album ''Recovery (Eminem album)|Recovery'', which was released in 2010. The song was written by Eminem, Boi-1da, Luis Resto (musician)|Luis Resto, J. Evans and M. Burnett.

The song is about how Eminem tried to overcome the troubles in his life from the past few years and mainly his drug addiction and rehabilitation.
