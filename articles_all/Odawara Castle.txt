 is a landmark in the city of Odawara, Kanagawa|Odawara in Kanagawa prefecture|Kanagawa Prefecture. It was the fortress of various daimyo during the History_of_Japan#Muromachi_Period|Muromachi period of History of Japan|Japanese history.

Odawara Castle was destroyed by the Meiji government. However, present Odawara Castle was rebuilt in 1960. It serves as a museum and is designated as an important historical monument. Odawara Castel premise is a park, and there are zoo and small amusement park in addition to historical site such as Castel tower. It was voted the 100 best historic parks in Japan in October, 2006.
