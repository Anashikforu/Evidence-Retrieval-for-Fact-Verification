
'''''The Rage: Carrie 2''''' is a 1999 United States|American horror movie|horror science fiction sequel movie. It follows classic 1976 horror movie ''Carrie (1976 movie)|Carrie''.

 Background 
The movie director|director said that the movie shows how difficult it is to be a young woman today.
 Plot summary 
Rachael/Rachel, like Carrie, is a high school outcast who attends Bates High School, except that it is a new building because Carrie destroyed the first one. Also like Carrie, she has telekenesis|telekinetic powers, the ability to move things with your mind, powers. Throughout the movie, there is flashbacks to events that happened in the first movie.
 Reception 
Rob Nelson, of City Pages, said that the movie's obsessive focus on predation|predatory teen-male human sexuality|sexuality — and female fragility in the face of it — clearly pegs this as the work of a feminism|feminist. Marc Savlov, of Austin Chronicle said that the movie does not even deliver one good shock.
