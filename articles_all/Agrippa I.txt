'''Agrippa I''' also called the Great (10 BC – 44 Anno Domini|AD), King of the Jews, was the :wikt:grandson|grandson of Herod the Great, and son of Aristobulus IV and Berenice (daughter of Salome)|Berenice.). He was, according to Josephus, known in his time as "Agrippa the Great".Josephus, ''Antiquities of the Jews|Antiquitates Judaicae'' xvii. 2. § 2
