
'''Alexander Auld''' (born January 7, 1981) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey goaltender currently playing for the Montreal Canadiens of the National Hockey League (NHL). Auld has also played for the New York Rangers, Vancouver Canucks, Florida Panthers, Phoenix Coyotes, Boston Bruins, Ottawa Senators and Dallas Stars of the NHL. He has appeared internationally for Canada national men's ice hockey team|Team Canada on three occasions – the 2001 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships|2001 World Junior Championships, the 2004 Spengler Cup, and the 2006 Men's World Ice Hockey Championships|2006 World Championships.
