

'''''Armaan''''' (), the Pakistani Urdu drama|melodramatic black and white movie. It was movie producer|produced by Waheed Murad and movie director|directed by Pervaiz Malik. It was the first Pakistani movie to complete 75 weeks in cinemas and, thus, became the first Pakistani 'Platinum Jubilee' movie. The movie was picturized in black and white prints and in beautiful locations of Karachi.
