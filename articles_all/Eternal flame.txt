
An '''eternal flame''' is a flame that burns for a eternity|very long time. They are used for creating a powerful symbol. There was an eternal flame in the ancient Greek temple at Delphi, Greece|Delphi. Other famous eternal flames include the one at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Paris, the John F. Kennedy Eternal Flame at his grave in the Arlington National Cemetery,
