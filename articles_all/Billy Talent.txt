
'''''Billy Talent''''', before called '''Pezz''', is a Canada|Canadian punk rock band from Mississauga, Ontario. The name Billy Talent was inspired by the guitarist "Billy Tallent" from the '''Michael Turner''' novel ''Hard Core Logo''. They performed and recorded together for about 10 years before becoming famous.

Members
