

'''Taylor William Pyatt''' (born August 19, 1981 in Thunder Bay, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey player who currently plays for the New York Rangers. Drafted out of the Ontario Hockey League (OHL), he played Junior hockey#Major junior|major-junior with the Sudbury Wolves. He has previously played for the New York Islanders, Buffalo Sabres, Vancouver Canucks and Phoenix Coyotes in the NHL.
