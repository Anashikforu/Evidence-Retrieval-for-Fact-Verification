
'''Christopher Arnold Terreri''' (born November 15, 1964 in Warwick, Rhode Island) is an Americans|American former professional ice hockey goaltender who played for four professional teams of the National Hockey League|NHL. He won the Stanley Cup two times with the New Jersey Devils. He is currently the goaltending coach of the New Jersey Devils of the NHL.
