

The '''Toronto Raptors''' are a Canada|Canadian professional basketball team in the National Basketball Association (NBA). They are based in Toronto. They were started in 1995. They play their home games at Scotiabank Arena. They are the only NBA team currently playing in Canada.

For the 2019 season, the Raptors won the NBA Conference Finals|Eastern Conference finals for the first time in the team's history. Kawhi Leonard was named the Finals MVP for the second time in his career.
