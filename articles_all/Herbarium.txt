
In botany, an '''herbarium''' is the building where the specimens are wikt:storing|stored, or the Science|scientific Institution|institute that not only stores but researches these specimens. A wikt:dry|dried plant wikt:mount|mounted on a sheet also is an herbarium.
In scientific literature herbaria are wikt:recognize|recognized by acronyms,
for example NY specimens are kept in New York Botanical garden.
