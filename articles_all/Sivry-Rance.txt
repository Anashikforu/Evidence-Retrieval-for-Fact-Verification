
'''Sivry-Rance''' is a municipality found in the Belgium|Belgian province of Hainaut. Annie Debruxelles is a mayor.

In 2018, its population was 4,833.

It is found at 50° 10 North, 04° 10 East.

The area is 72.97 km2.

It has 5 sub-municipalities: Grandrieu, Montbliart, Rance, Sautin, and Sivry.
