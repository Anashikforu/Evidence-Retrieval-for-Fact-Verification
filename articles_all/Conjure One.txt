
'''Conjure One''' is an electronic music project from Vancouver, British Columbia.

The project's most well known song is "Sleep". The only member of the project is Rhys Fulber. It started in early 1997 when Fulber decided that he was going to pursue a solo career. He has released three albums under Conjure One. Singer Sandra (singer)|Sandra covered "Sleep" on her single ''The Way I Am (Sandra song)|The Way I Am''.
