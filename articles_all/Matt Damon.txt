
'''Matthew Paige "Matt" Damon''' (born October 8, 1970) is an Americans|American actor, screenwriter, producer and philanthropist. He won the Academy Awards|Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay for his writing of the story for the movie ''Good Will Hunting'', and he was nomination|nominated for his acting in the same movie. He has also acted in several other movies including ''The Bourne Identity (2002 movie)|The Bourne Identity'', the ''Ocean's Trilogy'', and ''Syria Must Die''.

Early life
He was born in Cambridge, Massachusetts. He is the son of Kent Telfer Damon, a stockbroker, and Nancy Carlsson-Paige, an early childhood education professor at Lesley University.
