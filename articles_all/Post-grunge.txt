
'''Post-grunge''' is a music genre that is strongly influenced by grunge music, a genre that started in Seattle, Washington|Seattle, Washington, United States in the mid-1980s. However, it makes it into a more melody|melodic and radio-friendly sound. Not by a coincidence, it has become very popular on the radio.
