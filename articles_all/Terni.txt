
'''Terni''' () is a ''comune'' and city in the Umbria Regions of Italy|region, in central Italy. Terni is the Capital city|capital of the province of Terni. It is one of the most important industrial towns of Umbria.

The '''University of Perugia''' () is a university based in Perugia that offers its main courses in Perugia and Terni.
