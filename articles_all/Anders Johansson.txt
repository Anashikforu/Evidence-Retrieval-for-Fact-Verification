


'''Anders Johansson''' (born 25 May 1962 in Gothenburg) is a Swedish people|Swedish musician. He is the son of Jan Johansson, jazz pianist, and brother of keyboardist Jens Johansson. He is the current drummer for the Swedish power metal band HammerFall.
