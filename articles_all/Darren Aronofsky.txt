

'''Darren S. Aronofsky''' (born February 12, 1969 in Brooklyn, New York) is an Americans|American movie movie director|director. His best known movies that he has directed are ''Requiem for a Dream'', ''The Wrestler (2008 movie)|The Wrestler'' and ''Black Swan (movie)|Black Swan''. He attended Harvard University where he wikt:graduate|graduated in 1991 and majored in social anthropology. He is Jewish and has Ukrainians|Ukrainian and Russian ancestry|descent. He will be directing ''Bond 25'' and it will star Daniel Craig as 007, Clive Owen and Sophie Ellis-Bextor.
