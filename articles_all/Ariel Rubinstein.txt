
'''Ariel Rubinstein''' (born April 13, 1951 in Jerusalem) is an Israeli economist. He is a professor of economics at Tel Aviv University and New York University. Rubinstein writes about game theory. He has a well-known John Forbes Nash|John Nash speech which he gives all around the world. He currently lives in Tel-Aviv.
