
'''Mehmet Okur''' (born May 26, 1979 in Yalova, Turkey) is an Turkey|Turkish basketball player. He was drafted by the Detroit Pistons with the 37th pick in the 2nd round of the 2001 NBA Draft. He currently plays for the Utah Jazz as a Center (basketball)|center and Power forward (basketball)|power forward. He has also played for the Detroit Pistons from 2002 to 2004 until he signed with the Jazz after the Pistons were unable to pay his salary due to salary cap limitations.
