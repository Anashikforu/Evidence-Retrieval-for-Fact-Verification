The '''district of Mendrisio''' is the southernmost district of Canton of Switzerland|canton of Ticino and of Switzerland.

 Circles and municipalities 

The district of Mendrisio has 27 municipality|municipalities divided in 5 circles (''circoli''):
 Circle of Mendrisio: Mendrisio, Coldrerio, Genestrerio, Salorino;
 Circle of Balerna: Balerna, Casima, Castel San Pietro, Monte, Chiasso, Pedrinate, Morbio Inferiore;
 Circle of Caneggio: Caneggio, Cabbio, Vacallo, Sagno, Morbio Superiore, Bruzella, Muggio;
 Circle of Stabio: Stabio, Novazzano, Ligornetto.
