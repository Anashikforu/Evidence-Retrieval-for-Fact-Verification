

The '''jalapeño''' is a type of Mexican  Capsicum|pepper. It is related to the Family (biology)|family of the Chili pepper. It is pronounced ''hala-PAYN-nyo'' or ''hala-PEE-nyo''. It is a reasonably spicy pepper.

A Chipotle (food)|chipotle is a smoked, dried jalapeño.
