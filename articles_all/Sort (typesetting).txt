In typesetting by hand compositing, a '''sort''' is a piece of type representing a specific Letter (alphabet)|letter or symbol, casting|cast from a matrix (printing)|matrix mould and assembled with other sorts bearing additional letters into lines of type to make up a ''forme'', from which a page is printed.
