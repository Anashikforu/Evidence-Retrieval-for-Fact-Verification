

"'''Gotta Get Away'''" is a song by the California|Californian punk rock group The Offspring. It was released as the third and last single from the album ''Smash (album)|Smash'' (1994). The song was a modest hit in several countries, while it peaked at #6 on Billboard's Modern Rock charts in the United States. "Gotta Get Away" was inspired by an early track, "Cogs", written while the band was still named Manic Subsidal.''Greatest Hits (The Offspring album)|The Offspring's Greatest Hits'' DVD Extra The lyrics refer to the pressure that lead singer Dexter Holland was under to finish the album on time.''Greatest Hits (The Offspring album)|The Offspring's Greatest Hits'' "Gotta Get Away"
