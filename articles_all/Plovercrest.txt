The '''plovercrest''' (''Stephanoxis lalandi'') is a species of hummingbird. It is found in Argentina, Brazil, and Paraguay. 

Its habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forest,Montane = highlands below subalpine zone. and degraded former forest. The Plovercrest has a blue chest, green back and white flanks.
