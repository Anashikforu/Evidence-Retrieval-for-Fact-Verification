

'''Lasse Lindh''' (born 27 March 1974) is a Sweden|Swedish indie pop musician. His first album, ''Bra'', came out on EMI in 1998. It had three singles which made the record chart|charts in Sweden. Lasse Lindh at Allmusic.com Lindh began singing in English language|English for his second album, ''You Wake Up at Sea Tac'' . The title is a reference to a line in ''Fight Club (movie)|Fight Club''. The album  was released in 2002. Lindh's relationship with South Korea dates back to 2006. His songs "The Stuff" and "C'mon Through" were used in the MBC TV (South Korean TV channel)|MBC Television program|TV series ''Soulmate''. In 2014, his song "Run to You" was used in the Seoul Broadcasting System|SBS drama ''Angel Eyes (TV series)|Angel Eyes.''
His most recent album is ''Sparks''. It was released in 2009.
