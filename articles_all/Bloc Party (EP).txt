

'''''Bloc Party''''' is an Extended Play|EP of songs in Bloc Party's first two (United Kingdom|UK) single (music)|singles: "She's Hearing Voices" and "Banquet/Staying Fat". The EP was released on May 24, 2004 throughout Europe. It was Bloc Party's first V2 Records EP. This EP was released later in Japan in August, and then in the United States in September of the same year.
