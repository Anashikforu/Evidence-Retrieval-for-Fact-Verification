The '''36th Ryder Cup Matches''' were held between 22 and 24 September 2006 at The Kildare Hotel and Golf Club|The K Club, Straffan, County Kildare|Co. Kildare, Republic of Ireland|Ireland. Team Europe won the competition by a score of 18½ to 9½, equalling their record winning margin of 2004 Ryder Cup|2 years earlier. This was the first time Europe had achieved three successive victories.The sequence from 1985-1987-1989 included a tie in 1989, retaining the trophy for Europe. Swedes|Swedish rookie Henrik Stenson made the winning putt, just moments after Luke Donald had sunk a putt to ensure Europe retained the trophy.
