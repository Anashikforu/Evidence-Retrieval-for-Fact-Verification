




'''11''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a common year starting on Thursday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Manius Aemilius Lepidus (consul 11)|Lepidus and Titus Statilius Taurus|Taurus'''.

 Events 
 Germania Inferior and the Rhine secured by Germanicus
 Augustus ends his plan to create a defensive border at the Elbe. This is to reinforce the Ancient Rome|Roman defence along the Rhine and the Danube.
 Artabanus III of the Arsacid Dynasty becomes ruler of Parthia
 In India, Satakarni begins his reign as Emperor of the Andhra Empire (11-29)
