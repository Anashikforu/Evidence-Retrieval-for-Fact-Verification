
'''Cyber Sunday (2008)''' was the fifth WWE Cyber Sunday|Cyber Sunday (the third under this name) professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It took place on October 26, 2008 at the US Airways Center in Phoenix, Arizona.
