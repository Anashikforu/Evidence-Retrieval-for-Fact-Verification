
'''Peter Michael Ainsworth''' (November 16, 1956 &ndash; April 6, 2021) was an England|English politician and was Shadow Minister|Shadow Secretary of State for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs. He was the Member of Parliament for the East Surrey (UK Parliament constituency)|East Surrey constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1992|1992 general election, but decided not to stand for reelection in 2010.

He was a member of the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.

Ainsworth died on April 6, 2021 at the age of 64.
