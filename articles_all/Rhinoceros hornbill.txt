

The '''rhinoceros hornbill'''  (''Buceros rhinoceros'') is one of the largest hornbills: adults are about the size of a swan. It lives in parts of Malay Peninsula|Malaya and Thailand. 

It usually wanders in the forest, where it lives, in search of fruits and Nut (fruit)|nuts to eat. Hornbills use their big bills to open the tough husks of nuts and to handle large fruit.
