



'''''Rango''''' is a 2011 American computer-animated action comedy movie that was produced by Gore Verbinski, Graham King and John B. Carls and was directed by Gore Verbinski. ''Rango'' was released on March 4, 2011 in North America. ''Rango'' was positively received from audience. It won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.
