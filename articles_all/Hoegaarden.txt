'''Hoegaarden''' is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Flemish Brabant.

In 2007, 6226 people lived there.

It is at 50° 46 North, 04° 53 East.

A Hoegaarden Brewery|beer, named after that town, is made there.
