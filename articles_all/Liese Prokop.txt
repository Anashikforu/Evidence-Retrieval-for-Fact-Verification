
'''Liese Prokop''' (March 27, 1941 – December 31, 2006) was an Austrian Sportsperson|athlete and politician. Prokop was born in Vienna, Austria. Before being Marriage|married to Gunnar Prokop, her name was '''Liese Sykora'''.  The two had two sons and a daughter. Prokop won the silver medal in the Pentathlon in the 1968 Summer Olympics in Mexico City. Prokop was the first female Austrian Cabinet|Minister for Interior affairs. She was in charge of the wikt:security|security of Austria, including its police|police force. She was a member of the Österreichische Volkspartei|ÖVP, Austrian people's party.

She died unexpectedly December 31, 2006 of a torn aorta
