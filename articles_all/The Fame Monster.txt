

'''''The Fame Monster''''' is an extra long EP (extended play) recording created by United States|American artist Lady Gaga in 2009. ''The Fame Monster'' reached the top of the charts in Australia, Germany, Ireland, New Zealand, Poland and the United Kingdom, while reaching the top ten in other major markets. Lady Gaga did a tour called the Monster Ball that lasted from late 2009 until mid-2011.

Gaga has said that the album represents the darker side of fame, and each song shows a certain fear.
