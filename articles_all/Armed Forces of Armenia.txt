The '''Armed Forces of the Republic of Armenia''' is the Armenian Army|Army, Armenian Air Defense|Air Defense, and Armenian Border Guard|Border Guard. Some parts were formed out of the former Red Army|Soviet forces in the Armenian SSR. 

The Commander-in-Chief of the military is the President of Armenia, currently Robert Kocharyan. The Armenian Ministry of Defense|Ministry of Defense is in charge of politics|political leadership. It is currently headed by Mikael Harutyunyan. Military command is in the hands of the General Staff, headed by the Chief of Staff, who is Lieutenant-General Seyran Ohanyan.  Armenia established a Ministry of Defense on 28 January 1992. The Border guards purpose is to patrol Armenia's borders with Georgia (country)|Georgia and Azerbaijan, while Russian troops continue to watch its borders with Iran and Turkey.
