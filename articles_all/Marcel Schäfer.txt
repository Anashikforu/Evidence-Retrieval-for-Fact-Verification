
'''Marcel Schäfer''' (born 7 June 1984 in Aschaffenburg) is a Germany|German footballer.

 Career 
Schäfer came to the youth teams of T.S.V. 1860 München|1860 Munich when he was 16 years old. In 2003 he played his first and only Bundesliga match for Munich. After the German Bundesliga 2003-04|Bundesliga season 2003-04, 1860 Munich was promotion and relegation|relegated to the German 2. Bundesliga|2. Bundesliga. Schäfer played three more years for Munich. 

In 2007 he went back to the Bundesliga and came to V.f.L. Wolfsburg. They won their first German Championship in 2009.

Schäfer plays for the Germany national football team|German national football team since the year 2008. His first international match was against England national football team|England on 19 November 2008.
