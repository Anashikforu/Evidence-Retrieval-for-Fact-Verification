'''''Giardia lamblia''''' (also known as  ''Lamblia intestinalis'' and ''Giardia duodenalis'') is a parasite that infects the gastrointestinal tract|intestines and causes '''giardiasis''' or 'beaver fever'. ''Giardia'' affects humans, cats, dogs and birds, cows, beavers, deer, and sheep.
