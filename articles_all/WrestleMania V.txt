

'''WrestleMania V''' was the fifth annual WrestleMania professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by the World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation (WWF). It took place on April 2, 1989 at the Boardwalk Hall|Trump Plaza in Atlantic City, New Jersey.
