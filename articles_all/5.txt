



'''5''' (Roman numerals|'''V''') is a common year of the Julian calendar which started on a common year starting on Thursday|Thursday. It was known as the ''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Messalla and Cinna''.  It was the 5th year of the 1st century.

It is one of only seven years to use just one Roman numeral. The seven are 1|1 AD (I), '''5 AD''' (V), 10|10 AD (X), 50|50 AD (L), 100|100 AD (C), 500|500 AD (D), and 1000|1000 AD (M).
