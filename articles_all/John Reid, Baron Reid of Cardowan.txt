
'''John Reid, Baron Reid of Cardowan''' (born 8 May 1947) is a Scottish football club chairman and a Scottish Labour Party|Labour Party politician.  He was the Member of Parliament for the Scotland|Scottish  constituency of Airdrie and Shotts (UK Parliament constituency)|Airdrie and Shotts in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected to the British House of Commons|House of Commons in the United Kingdom general election, 1987|1987 general election for the Motherwell North (UK Parliament constituency)|Motherwell North constituency. The boundary of the constituency was changed, and in the 1997 election he was elected to the new constituency of Hamilton North and Bellshill (UK Parliament constituency)|Hamilton North and Bellshill. After more boundary changes in 2005 he was elected to the new constituency of Airdrie and Shotts.

He is the former Home Secretary.
Reid did not stand in the United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010 election, and was made a life peer instead.

He is Chairman of Celtic F.C.|Celtic Football Club in Glasgow.

He is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
