

'''Campo Blenio''' was a municipality of the district of Blenio (district)|Blenio in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

On 25 January 2005, the people in charge announced that Campo Blenio would merge with Aquila, Switzerland|Aquila, Ghirone, Olivone and Torre, Switzerland|Torre to form a new municipality to be called Blenio. This union was carried through on 22 October 2006.
