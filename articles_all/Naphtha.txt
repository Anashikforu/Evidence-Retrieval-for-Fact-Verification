
'''Naphtha''' is the name for a number of intermediary product (chemistry)|products that wikt:occur|occur when refining crude oil. They are liquid hydrocarbons. They are used to produce high octane rating|octane gasoline products. The petrochemical industry also uses them to make olefins and solvents.

Typical naphtas are used for:
Solvents in cleaning fluid.
As an ingredient of Shoe polish.
As an ingredient for some of the fuels used in lighters.
Possibly one of the things in Greek fire.
