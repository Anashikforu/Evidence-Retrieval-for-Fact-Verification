The '''Q Awards''' are the United Kingdom's annual music awards run by the music magazine Q (magazine)|Q. Since they began in 1990, the Q Awards have become one of Britain's biggest and most advertised music awards, helped by the often bad behaviour of the celebrity|celebrities who turn up. In the ceremony of 2004, Elton John accused Madonna (entertainer)|Madonna of cheating fans by miming on stage, after she had been nominated for a Best Live Act award.
