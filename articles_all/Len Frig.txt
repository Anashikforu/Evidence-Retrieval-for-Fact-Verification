'''Leonard Elroy Frig''' (born October 23, 1950 in Lethbridge, Alberta) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey defenceman (ice hockey)|defenceman. He played a total of 311 games in the National Hockey League (NHL). He played for the Chicago Blackhawks|Chicago Black Hawks, Oakland Seals, Cleveland Barons (NHL)|Cleveland Barons, and St. Louis Blues (hockey)|St. Louis Blues.
