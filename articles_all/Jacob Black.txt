
'''Jacob Black''' is a science fiction|fictional character in the series of books and movies called ''Twilight Saga''. In the movies, the character is played by Taylor Lautner. He lives in the U.S. state of Washington (U.S. state)|Washington. He has a romance with Bella and tries to protect her from the vampires and Edward Cullen the head vampire who is in love with Bella. Jacob can also turn into a wolf.
