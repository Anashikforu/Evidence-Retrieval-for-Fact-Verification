

'''Kassel''' is a ''Landkreis'' (Rural Districts of Germany|Rural District) in the north of Hesse, Germany. The district-free city Kassel is nearly completely surrounded by the district.

 History 
1972 the previous districts of Kassel, Hofgeismar and Wolfhagen were joined to form the newly established district of Kassel.
