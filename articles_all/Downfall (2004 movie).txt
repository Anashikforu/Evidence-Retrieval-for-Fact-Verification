
'''''Downfall''''' (German language|German: '''''Der Untergang''''') is a 2004 Germany|German war movie about the final days of Adolf Hitler, the Nazi dictator of Germany, in the Führerbunker. It is set in Berlin in 1945. The movie was first released in German on 16 September 2004.

The film was nominated for the Academy Awards|Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.

A scene in the film, where Hitler become angry and goes into a furious tirade when he finally realizes that the war is truly lost is known for being parodied and has an internet meme.
