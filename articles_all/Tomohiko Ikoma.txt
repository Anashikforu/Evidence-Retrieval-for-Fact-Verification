Biography
Ikoma was born in Hyogo Prefecture on August 25, 1932.
On January 2, 1955, when Ikoma was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Myanmar national football team|Burma. He played 5 games for Japan in 1955.

On April 27, 2009, Ikoma died in Kobe at the age of 76.
