

'''Lichtenberg''' is a Boroughs of Berlin|borough of Berlin, Germany. In 2001 it took over the old borough of Hohenschönhausen. Lichtenberg now has an area of  and a population (as of 2003) of about 260,200 inhabitants.

The present mayor of the borough is Christina Emmrich (The Left (Germany)|Die Linke).

Lichtenberg contains the Tierpark Friedrichfelde|Tierpark Berlin, the larger of Berlin's two zoological parks. Lichtenberg was also the site of the headquarters of the Stasi, the East Germany|East German intelligence service. The complex is now a museum.

The Berlin-Hohenschönhausen Memorial is on the site of the main prison of the Stasi.
