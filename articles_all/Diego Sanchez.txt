
'''Diego "The Nightmare" Sanchez''' (born December 31, 1981 in Albuquerque, New Mexico|Albuquerque, New Mexico) is a Mexico|Mexican United States|American Mixed martial arts|mixed martial artist (MMA). He has a background in wrestling and Gaidojutsu, a system of submission wrestling developed by his longtime trainer Greg Jackson. Sanchez currently fights as a welterweight with the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).
