


'''Rebecca St. James''' (born '''Rebecca Jean Smallbone'''; 26 July 1977) is a Christian rock singer. She is signed to Forefront Records.

St. James has sold a total of two million albums.

Personal life
St. James is originally from Australia.  She now lives in Nashville, Tennessee.

On 3 January 2011 she announced her engagement to Jacob Fink.  He is the bassist of the band Foster the People.
