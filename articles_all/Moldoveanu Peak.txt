'''Moldoveanu Peak''' (Romanian language|Romanian: ''Vârful Moldoveanu'') is the highest mountain in Romania. It is in the Făgăraş Mountains of the Southern Carpathians.

The most-used paths to reach Moldoveanu are over the Vistea Mare Peak (2527m), by paths coming from Podragu, Sambata, or by the Vistea Valley. 
The closest wikt:locality|locality is Victoria, Romania|Victoria, on the north side. On the south side it is accessible from northwest of Câmpulung.
