
'''Nick Young''' (born June 1, 1985 in Los Angeles, California) is an Americans|American basketball player that currently plays for the Denver Nuggets of the National Basketball Association|NBA. He was selected by the Washington Wizards with the 16th pick in the 1st round of the 2007 NBA draft. Young played four seasons with the Wizards before being traded to the Los Angeles Clippers for a future second-round pick on March 15th, 2012.
