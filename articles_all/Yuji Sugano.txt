
 is a former Japanese Association football|football player. He played for the Japan national football team|Japan national team.

Biography
Sugano was born in Aichi Prefecture on April 14, 1961. After graduating from Osaka University of Commerce, he joined his local club Nagoya Grampus|Toyota Motors (later ''Nagoya Grampus Eight''). In 1992, Japan Soccer League was folded and founded new league J1 League. However, he retired in 1992 without playing in J1 League.

On February 2, 1988, Sugano debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Oman national football team|Oman.
