Biography
Sato was born in Amagasaki on February 5, 1932. After graduating from Kwansei Gakuin University, he played for Kwangaku Club (All Kwangaku) which was consisted of his alma mater Kwansei Gakuin University players and graduates. He won 1955, 1958 and 1959 Emperor's Cup.

On January 2, 1955, when Sato was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Myanmar national football team|Burma. In 1956, he was selected Japan for Football at the 1956 Summer Olympics|1956 Summer Olympics in Melbourne. He also played at Football at the 1958 Asian Games|1958 Asian Games. He played 15 games for Japan until 1959.

Sato died on January 1, 1988 at the age of 55.
