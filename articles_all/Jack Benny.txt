
'''Jack Benny''' (born '''Benjamin Kubelsky'''; February 14, 1894 &ndash; December 26, 1974) was an Americans|American comedian and actor. 

He was born in Chicago and grew up in Waukegan, Illinois. His mother was a Lithuanian immigrant; his father was a Jewish immigrant from Poland. 

From 1932 to 1955, Benny had a radio show. The first title of the show was ''The Canada Dry Ginger Ale Program'', but it was later called "The Jack Benny Program".

Death

Benny died of pancreatic cancer in Beverly Hills, California at the age of 80.
