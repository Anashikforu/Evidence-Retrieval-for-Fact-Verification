'''Spartacus''' (circa 120 BC(Russian) Валентин Лесков. ''Спартак''. М.: Молодая гвардия, 1987 – circa 70 BC, at the end of the Third Servile War), according to ancient Rome|Roman historians, was a gladiator-slave who became the  leader of an unsuccessful slave uprising against the Roman Republic.
