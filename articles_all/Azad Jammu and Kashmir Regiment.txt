The '''Azad Jammu and Kashmir Regiment''' is one of the six infantry regiments of Pakistan Army. Its ''Azad Jammu and Kashmir Regimental Centre'' is located at Mansar (Pakistan), Attock District, Punjab (Pakistan)|The Pak-Punjab.
