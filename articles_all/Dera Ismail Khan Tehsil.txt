'''Dera Ismail Khan Tehsil''' is an administrative subdivision (tehsil) of Dera Ismail Khan District in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. 

Administration
The tehsil is administratively divided into 21 Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils, four of which form the headquarters - Dera Ismail Khan.
