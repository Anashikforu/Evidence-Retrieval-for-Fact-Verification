A '''palindrome''' is a word, sentence, or number that reads the same from left to right as from right to left.


You can read it horizontally, backwards, even vertically!

 Examples of Palindromes 
= Words =
 Mum
 Dad
 Deed
 Level
 Radar
 Kayak
 Eye

= Sentences =
 Was it a cat I saw?
 Do geese see God?
 Rats live on no evil star.
 Never odd or even.
 Madam, I'm Adam.
 Go Hang a salami I'm a lasagna hog
 Top spot
 Nurses run

= Numbers =
 1881
 1991
 2002
