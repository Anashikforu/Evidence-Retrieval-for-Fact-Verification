

The '''''Gringo Gazette''''' is a newspaper in the English language founded by Carrie Duncan, published every other week for the United States|American expatriate communities in Baja California (state)|Baja California and Baja California Sur, Mexico.

It has been published since 2001. Most of its contributors are Americans living in Mexico or Americans with a second home in Mexico. Most of the subscribers of this newspaper are located in San Felipe, Baja California|San Felipe, La Paz, Baja California Sur|La Paz and Cabo San Lucas.
