
'''Thomas Andrew "Tom" Felton''' (born 22 September 1987) is an English people|English actor and singer. Felton was born in Epsom, Surrey. He is best known for portraying the character Draco Malfoy in the Harry Potter movie series. Felton has also won a MTV Movie Award for ''Best Villain'' in the film version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince in 2010.
