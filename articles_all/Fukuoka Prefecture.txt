 Geography 
Fukuoka Prefecture faces the sea on three sides.  The prefecture borders Saga Prefecture|Saga, Ōita Prefecture|Ōita, and Kumamoto Prefecture|Kumamoto prefectures.  Fukuoka is near Yamaguchi Prefecture across the Kanmon Straits.

Fukuoka includes the two largest cities on Kyūshū, Fukuoka, Fukuoka|Fukuoka and Kitakyūshū. Small islands are near the north coast of the prefecture.
