

'''John David Ashcroft''' (born May 9, 1942) is an Americans|American consultant, lobbyist and politician. He was the 79th United States Attorney General. He served during the first term of President of the United States|President George W. Bush from 2001 until 2005. Ashcroft was previously the Governor of Missouri (1985–1993) and a United States Senate|U.S. Senator from Missouri (1995–2001). Ashcroft was born in Chicago, Illinois.
