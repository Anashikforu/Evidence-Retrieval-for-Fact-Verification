
'''Amagerbro station''' is a Copenhagen Metro|Metro station in Copenhagen in Denmark, established in 2002.

The station is operated by the Metro service M2 (metro)|M2 and Movias bus services 77, 78, 2A, 5A, 350S, 871, 872 and 81N.
