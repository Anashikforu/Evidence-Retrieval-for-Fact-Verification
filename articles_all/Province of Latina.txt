
The '''Province of Latina''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Lazio Regions of Italy|region of Central Italy. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Latina, Lazio|Latina.

History
The province of Latina was created in December of 1934 with the name of '''Littoria'''.

Geography

The province of Latina has an area of , the smallest province of the Lazio region.

The province is in the southwest of the Lazio region. It borders to the north with the Province of Frosinone|Frosinone province, to the northwest and west with the Metropolitan City of Rome Capital|Metropolitan City of Rome, to the southeast with the Campania region (Province of Caserta|Caserta), and to the south with the Tyrrhenian Sea (Mediterranean Sea).
