
'''Mastung District''' () is a Districts of Pakistan|district in the northwest of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province. Pakistan. Prior to 1991 Mastung was part of Kalat District. For administrative purposes Mastung was separated from Kalat and made a new district in 1991.
