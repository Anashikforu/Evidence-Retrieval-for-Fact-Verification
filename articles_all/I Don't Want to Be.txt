"'''I Don't Want to Be'''" is the debut single by Gavin DeGraw on his first album Chariot (album)|''Chariot''. The single was released in 2004. The song is in the Alternative rock genre. 

 Chart Rankings 
U.S. ''Billboard'' Hot 100 gave the single "I Don't Wanna Be" a ranking of #10, U.K. Singles Chart gave the single a ranking of #32, and Dutch Singles Chart ranked it at #9.
