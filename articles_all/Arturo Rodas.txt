'''Arturo Rodas''' (born 1954) is a leading Ecuadorian composer.Béhague 2001, 7:872. He has lived in London since 1989.

 Biography 

Rodas has composed ''24.5 Preludes'' for piano; ''Climax'' for orchestra, ''Atonal fugue I'' for oboe ''d'amore'' & piano and electronic music works.

"Since emigrating to England in 1990, he has composed works such as ''The Book of the Orchestra'',(2002-2003) work renamed ''El árbol de los pájaros'' which is intended to be performed in a house with many rooms so as to allow an “audience” to hear different instrumental groups as they wander from room to room".Walker 2007.
