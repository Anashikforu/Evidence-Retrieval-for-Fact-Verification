'''Royal Museum''' '''of Scotland''' is on Chambers Street (Edinburgh)|Chambers Street, Edinburgh, United Kingdom|UK. It is part of the National Museum of Scotland|National Museums of Scotland, the other part being the adjacent Museum of Scotland. Both names merged with each other in 2007 to become the '''National Museum of Scotland'''.

It was designed by Francis Fowke and built from 1861-1888.
