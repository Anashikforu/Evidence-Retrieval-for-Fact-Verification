
'''''Breakaway''''' is the second album by Americans|American singer Kelly Clarkson; it was released on November 30, 2004.

The album debuted at number three on the Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200 for selling 250,000 copies. It has sold over 6 million copies in the United States and has been certified six times Platinum by the RIAA.

Five singles were released from the album, "Since U Been Gone", "Behind These Hazel Eyes", "Because of You", "Walk Away", and "Breakaway".
