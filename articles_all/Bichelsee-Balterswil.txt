

'''Bichelsee-Balterswil''' is a municipality of the district of Münchwilen (district)|Münchwilen in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Thurgau in Switzerland. It was made from the union on January 1, 1996 of Bichelsee and Balterswil. The lake Bichelsee (lake)|Bichelsee is in the municipality.
