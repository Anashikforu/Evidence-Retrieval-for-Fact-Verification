
'''Phillip Paul Lloyd''' (born March 3, 1981) is a South African professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is best known for his time in WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) where he wrestled under the ring name '''Justin Gabriel'''. He is currently wrestling for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) and Global Force Wrestling (GFW) under the ring name '''PJ Black'''.

Lloyd was the first South African wrestler to compete in the WWE. While performing in Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW), he was a one-time FCW Florida Heavyweight Championship|FCW Florida Heavyweight Champion and a one-time FCW Florida Tag Team Championship|FCW Florida Tag Team Champion with Brian Cage|Kris Logan.
