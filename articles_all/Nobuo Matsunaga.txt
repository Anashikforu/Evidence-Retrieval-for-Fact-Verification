Biography
Matsunaga was born in Shida District, Shizuoka|Shida District, Shizuoka Prefecture|Shizuoka on December 6, 1921.
In March 1954, Matsunaga was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for 1954 FIFA World Cup|1954 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on March 14, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea. He also played at Football at the 1954 Asian Games|1954 Asian Games. He played 4 games for Japan until 1955.

After retirement, Matsunaga became a manager for Hagoromo Club|Nippon Light Metal. In 1972, he promoted the club to new division, Japan Soccer League Division 2.

On September 25, 2007, Matsunaga died of lymphoma in Fujieda, Shizuoka|Fujieda at the age of 85.
