

The '''Class 185''' (branded by its operators as the '''Pennine Class 185''') is a diesel multiple unit|diesel multiple-unit passenger train built by Siemens Transportation Systems in Germany for the United Kingdom|British train operating company TransPennine Express|First TransPennine Express.
