

'''Laney Amplification''' is a company from Great Britain that makes electric guitar|guitar and bass guitar amplifiers. It was founded in 1967 when Lyndon Laney started to make amplifiers in his parents' garage. Laney is popular for its good quality amplifiers for low prices.
