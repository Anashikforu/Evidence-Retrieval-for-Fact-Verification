

'''Terry Ugene Bollea''' (born August 11, 1953), better known by his ring name  '''Hulk Hogan''', is an Americans|American semi-retired Professional wrestling|pro wrestler and actor.

He is best known for his time in WWE|World Wrestling Federation/Entertainment, World Championship Wrestling and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling. He is a six-time WWE Championship|WWF/E Champion and six-time WCW World Heavyweight Championship|WCW World Heavyweight Champion.

His first match in Japan was in May 1980 for New Japan Pro Wrestling. He was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame by Sylvester Stallone in 2005.
