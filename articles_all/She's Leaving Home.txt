
"'''She's Leaving Home'''" is a song, written by Lennon/McCartney|John Lennon and Paul McCartney and released in 1967 on The Beatles album ''Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band''. McCartney wrote and sang the verse and Lennon the chorus. This was one of a handful of songs of the Beatles in which the members did not play any instruments.
