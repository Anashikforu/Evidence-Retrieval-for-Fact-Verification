
'''Pharaoh's Island''' is an island on the River Thames, in Surrey, England. 

 Location 
The island is above Shepperton Lock. It can only be reached by boats.

 History 
The island was given to Horatio Nelson|Admiral Nelson as a reward for winning Battle of the Nile, and he used it as a place to fish.
