The British Rail '''Class 444''' (or SR Multiple Unit Numbering and Classification#DC Third Rail EMUs|5Des) "Desiro" electrical multiple units were built by Siemens AG from 2003-04. Forty-five of these units were built for express services for South West Trains. The "Desiro" family also includes units of Classes British Rail Class 350|350, British Rail Class 360|360 and British Rail Class 450|450.
