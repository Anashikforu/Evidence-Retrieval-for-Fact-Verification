

'''Michael Thomas "Tom" Green''' (born July 30, 1971) is a Canadians|Canadian actor, comedian, rapping|rapper, movie director|director, movie producer|producer and writer. He was born in Pembroke, Ontario. He had his own comedy television program|program, ''The Tom Green Show'', on MTV. He directed, co-wrote and starred in ''Freddy Got Fingered''. He was also a member of the short lived hip hop group Organized Rhyme where he performed under the stage name "MC Bones".
