'''Kruishoutem''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of East Flanders. On 1 January 2019, Kruishoutem and Zingem were merged into the new municipality of Kruisem. In 2007, there were 8,152 people living there.

It is at 50° 54 North, 03° 31 East.
