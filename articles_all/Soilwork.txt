
'''Soilwork''' is a melodic death metal band from Helsingborg, Sweden. They started in 1996. They have made 7 albums until now. The members are: Bjørn „Speed“ Strid (vocals), Daniel Antonsson (guitar), Ola Flink, (Bass guitar|bass), Dirk Verbeuren (drums) and Sven Karlsson (Keyboard instrument|Keyboard).
