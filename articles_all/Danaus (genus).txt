

'''''Danaus''''' is a genus of butterfly|butterflies.  ''Danaus'' is in the Family (biology)|family Nymphalidae.  Species in this genus are commonly known as '''Tigers''' or '''Monarchs'''.

 ''Danaus genutia'' Cramer, 1779 - Common Tiger
 ''Danaus gilippus'' Cramer, 1775 - Queen
 ''Danaus ismare'' Cramer, 1780 - Ismare Tiger
 ''Danaus melanippus'' Cramer, 1777 - White Tiger
