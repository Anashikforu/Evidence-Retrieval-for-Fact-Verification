
'''Centre-Val de Loire''' is one of the Regions of France|administrative regions of France. Its Capital city|capital is Orléans but its largest city is Tours.

It was formed in 2014 from the Territory|territories of three History|historical provinces: Touraine (Indre-et-Loire), Orléanais (Loiret, Eure-et-Loir, Loir-et-Cher), and Berry (province)|Berry (Cher (department)|Cher, Indre (department)|Indre).

Geography
