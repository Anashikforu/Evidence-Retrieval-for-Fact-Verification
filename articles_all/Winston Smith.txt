'''Winston Smith''' is a fictional character and the protagonist of George Orwell's 1949 novel ''Nineteen Eighty-Four''. In the book, Winston is a clerk for the Ministry of Truth, where his job is to historical revisionism (negationism)|rewrite historical documents so that they match the current Party line (politics)|party line, which changes on a daily basis.
