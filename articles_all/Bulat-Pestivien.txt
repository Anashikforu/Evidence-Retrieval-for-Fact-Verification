'''Bulat-Pestivien''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Bretagne in the Côtes-d'Armor Departments of France|department in the west of France.

Bulat-Pestivien is twinned with  Ballyferriter (Baile an Fheirtéaraigh), Ireland, since 1992.
