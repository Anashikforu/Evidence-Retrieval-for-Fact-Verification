'''Ewoks''' are a fictional Species|race of small, bear-like animal|creatures from George Lucas' ''Star Wars'' fictional universe|universe. They first appeared in the film ''Star Wars Episode VI: Return of the Jedi|Return of the Jedi''. 

They live on the forests on the Satellite (natural)|moon of the Planet Endor. They often wear the skins or skulls of animals they have hunted. The most famous Ewok is Wicket W. Warwik, a brave Ewok who destroyed an entire Imperial shield projector. The Ewoks were also well known for their victory against the Empire in the "Battle of Endor".
