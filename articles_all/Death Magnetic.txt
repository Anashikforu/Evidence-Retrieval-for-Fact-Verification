'''''Death Magnetic''''' is the ninth studio album by heavy metal music|heavy metal band Metallica. The album was released September 12, 2008 with the hits ''The Day That Never Comes'', ''Cyanide'', ''My Apocalypse'', ''The Unforgiven III'', and ''The Judas Kiss''.

 Awards 
= 2009 Grammy nominations =
''Death Magnetic'' was nominated for four Grammy awards. Rick Rubin won "Producer of the Year, Non-Classical" for his work on ''Death Magnetic'', as well as other albums this past year.
 Best Rock Album
 Best Metal Performance ("My Apocalypse")
 Best Rock Instrumental Performance ("Suicide & Redemption")
 Best Recording Package

The album won two Grammys for "Best Metal Performance" and "Best Recording Package" at the 51st Grammy Awards on February 8, 2009.
