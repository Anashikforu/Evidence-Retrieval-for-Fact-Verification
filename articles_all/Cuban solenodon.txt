

The '''Cuban solenodon''' or '''almiqui''' (''Solenodon cubanus'') is a species of Soricomorpha|soricomorph that is endemic to Cuba. It belongs to the family Solenodontidae along with a similar species, the Hispaniolan Solenodon (''Solenodon paradoxus''). The solenodon is unusual among mammals in that its saliva is Venom (poison)|venomous. Since its discovery in 1861 by the Germany|German naturalist Wilhelm Peters.
