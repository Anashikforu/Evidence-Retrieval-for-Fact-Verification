

The '''Mexican Grand Prix''' (Gran Premio de Mexico) is a Formula One Auto racing|motor race held at the Autodromo Hermanos Rodriguez in Mexico City.  It first appeared as a non-championship event in 1962 before being held as a championship event from 1963-1970, 1986-1992 and since 2015.
