'''West Forsyth High School''' is a high school in North Carolina. It has 1611 students. It is the third largest school in Forsyth County, North Carolina|Forsyth County and the 53rd largest school in North Carolina. Its principal is Kurt Telford. It is under the Winston-Salem/Forsyth County Schools' district.
