'''Uherské Hradiště''' is a town in the Czech Republic. About 26,280 people live there (2008). It is located in the South-East of the historical region Moravia in the Zlín region. It was founded on October 15 1257 by the Czech kingdom|Czech king Přemysl Otakar II.
