A '''bastard''' is a person who was born of parents who are not marriage|married to one another. It is considered by some cultures and individuals as a nasty or insulting word today, or sometimes even profanity, with the term '''illegitimate''' being a little more polite. Some cultures see illegitimate children as dirty or bad, and only accept sexual intercourse|sexual relations between marriage|married couples.
