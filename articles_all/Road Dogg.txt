
'''Brian James''' is an American Professional wrestling|pro wrestler currently working for the WWE as a commentator on the web series ''Are You Serious?'' and as an agent. James is also known for his work in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling, but perhaps best known for his work with the World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation (WWF) as '''"The Road Dogg" Jesse James''' or just '''Road Dogg'''. He is a former member of D-Generation X and the tag team partner of Monty Sopp|Billy Gunn, with whom he made the New Age Outlaws in the WWF and New Age Outlaws#The James Gang (2006)|The James Gang and Voodoo Kin Mafia in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). In the WWF, James is a List of WWE Intercontinental Champions|one time WWE Intercontinental Championship|Intercontinental Champion, a List of WWE Hardcore Champions|one time WWE Hardcore Championship|Hardcore Champion, and a List of World Tag Team Champions (WWE)|five time World Tag Team Championship (WWE)|Tag Team Champion with Monty Sopp|Billy Gunn. In TNA, he is a NWA World Tag Team Championship|two time NWA World Tag Team Championship|NWA World Tag Team Champion with Konnan and Ron Killings.
