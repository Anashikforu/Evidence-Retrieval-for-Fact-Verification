

"'''Nobody's Home'''" is Avril Lavigne's third Single (music)|single from her second album ''Under My Skin''. Daniel Stern played guitar for it. It was produced by Lavigne and Don Gilmore.

The song was written by Lavigne and Ben Moody, ex-member of Evanescence. It is a "Rock music|rock Ballad (music)|ballad".
