
'''José Nicholas Théodore''' (born September 13, 1976 in Laval, Quebec|Laval, Quebec) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey goaltender. He plays for the Florida Panthers of the National Hockey League. Before that he played for the Montreal Canadiens where he won the Vezina Trophy|Vezina and Hart Memorial Trophy|Hart trophies in 2001–02 before being traded to the Avalanche in 2006.
