
'''Burlington Airpark'''  is an airport in Burlington, Ontario, Canada. It has two runways. The airport is privately owned by Vince Rossi.

History

The airport was created by Victor and Gwen Kovachik in 1962. It is a very popular airport for Toronto-area pilots.
