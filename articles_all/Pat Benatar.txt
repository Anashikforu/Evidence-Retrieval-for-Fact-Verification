

'''Pat Benatar''' (born '''Patricia Mae Andrzejewski''' on 10 January 1953) is a four-time Grammy Award-winning Americans|American rock music|rock singing|singer with seven platinum''RIAA''  and three gold albums to her credit, as well as 19 Top 40 singles. Pat Benatar was eligible for the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2004, she was nominated in 2020 and was not elected and will be nominated again in 2022.
Benatar is also known for her mezzo-soprano vocal range.
