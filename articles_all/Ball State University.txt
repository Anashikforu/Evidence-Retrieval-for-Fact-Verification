'''Ball State University''' is a public, coeducational university in Muncie, Indiana|Muncie, Indiana. The university has about 21,401 students. It was founded in 1918. The current president of the university is Jo Ann M. Gora.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Cardinals. They have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the Mid-American Conference.
