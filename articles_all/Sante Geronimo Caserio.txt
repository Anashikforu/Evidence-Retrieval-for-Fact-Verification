
'''Sante Geronimo Caserio''' (September 8, 1873 - August 16, 1894) was an Italy|Italian anarchist who assassination|assassinated the President of France|president of the French Third Republic, Marie François Sadi Carnot, by stabbing.

Death
Caserio was execution|executed by guillotine in Lyon at 5 am on August 16, 1894. In front of the guillotine, he shouted "''Coraggio, compagni — evviva l' anarchia!''" ("Courage, comrades — Long live anarchy!")
