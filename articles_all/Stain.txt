A '''stain''' is a spot that has another color than the material on which it is found. It can be occur without intention in the case of domestic stains on textile|fabric, cloth, or other material, or it can be intentional. Stains are used intentionally in a variety of fields, including research (staining (biology)|biochemical staining), technology (metal staining), and art (wood staining, stained glass.)

Different types of material can be stained by different substances, and #Stain resistance|stain resistance is an important characteristic in modern textile engineering.  Various laundry techniques exist to attempt to remove or minimize existing stains, and #Stain removers|stain removers are an important type of chemical in laundry detergents. Some stand-alone stain removers also exist.
