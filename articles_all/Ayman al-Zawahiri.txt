'''Ayman Mohammed Rabie al-Zawahiri''''' was born June 19, 1951. He is an Egyptian physician,

Since the September 11 attacks, U.S. State Department has offered a US$25 million reward for information leading to al-Zawahiri's arrest.

Earlier life
al-Zawahiri studied medicine in Cairo. He successfully completed his studies in 1974. For three years, he worked as a surgeon in the Egyptian army. After that, he worked in a hospital in Cairo. In 1980 and 1981, al-Zawahiri travelled to Pakistan to help the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement|Red Crescent movement.

al-Zawahiri is the author of several works including numerous al-Qaeda statements. He speaks Arabic language|Arabic, English language|English and French language|French.
