
'''Adair County''' is a county in the U.S. state of Oklahoma.  As of 2010, 22,286 people lived there.  Its county seat is Stilwell, Oklahoma|Stilwell.

 History 
The county was created in 1907.

 Geography 
The county has a total area of 577 square miles.
