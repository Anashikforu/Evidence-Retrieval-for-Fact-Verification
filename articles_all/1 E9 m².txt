'''1 E9 m²''' is a term used to group together areas of similar size. It is used for places that are between 1,000 km² and 10,000 km² in area. The term is based from the scientific notation for 1 x 109metre|m².

 1,000&nbsp;km² is equal to:
 100,000 hectares
 approx. 386 square miles 
 approx. 247,105 acres.
 a circle of radius of approx. 18&nbsp;km.
 a square of side of approx. 32&nbsp;km.
