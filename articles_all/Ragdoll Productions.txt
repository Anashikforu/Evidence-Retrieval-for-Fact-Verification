'''Ragdoll Productions''' (or '''Ragdoll Ltd.''')  (and '''Ragdoll Productions Animations.''') is a United Kingdom|British company that produces children's television shows. It was founded by Anne Wood in 1984, who later made many of their successful television series, seen (in the case of ''Teletubbies'') in 111 countries and translated into 41 languages.
