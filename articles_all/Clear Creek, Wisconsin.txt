

'''Clear Creek''' is a town in Eau Claire County, Wisconsin|Eau Claire County, Wisconsin, United States.  About 710 people were living in Clear Creek as of the year 2002.  The community|communities of Allen, Wisconsin|Allen, and Foster, Eau Claire County, Wisconsin|Foster can be found in Clear Creek.  Clear Creek has an area of 35.9 square miles, most of it land.
