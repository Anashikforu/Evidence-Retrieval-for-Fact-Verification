Biography
Furuta was born in Hiroshima on October 27, 1952. After graduating from Waseda University, he joined his local club Sanfrecce Hiroshima|Toyo Industries (later ''Mazda'') in 1975. The club won second place at 1978 Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1984.

On August 13, 1971, when Furuta was a Waseda University student, he debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Iceland national football team|Iceland. It was the youngest player to play for Japan national team at the age of 18 years and 29 days until Daisuke Ichikawa made new record in 1998. He played at Football at the 1974 Asian Games|1974 and Football at the 1978 Asian Games|1978 Asian Games. He played 32 games for Japan until 1978.
