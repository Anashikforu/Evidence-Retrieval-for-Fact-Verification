


'''''Archelon''''' was a huge marine turtle (Testudines) that was the size of a car. It lived in North America 74 million years ago (from the Upper Cretaceous) in the Epeiric sea|epeiric inland sea called the Western Interior Seaway. Fossils have been found in the U.S. states of Kansas and South Dakota.

''Archelon'', like other turtles, had a solidly-roofed skull with no opening (except for eyes, and nostrils).
