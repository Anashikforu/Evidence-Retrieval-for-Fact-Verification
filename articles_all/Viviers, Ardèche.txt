

'''Viviers''' is a Communes of France|commune in the French ''Departments of France|département'' of Ardèche.  The commune has a population of about 3,400 people as of the year 1999.

 History 
Viviers became the capital of the Gaulish Helvii tribe following the falling of nearby Alba-la-Romaine.
