'''Shinzō Abe''' (born 21 September 1954) is a Japanese politician.

He was first made the 90th Prime Minister of Japan following Junichiro Koizumi in 2006."
In 2012 he was once again made party leader and his party went on to win the election in December 2012 by a wide margin. This brought him back to power as Prime Minister.

He was born in Shinjuku, Tokyo, but his Constituency is in Yamaguchi Prefecture. It was inherited from his father Shintarō Abe. His grandfather was Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi.

On 28 August 2020, Abe announced his plans to resign as Prime Minister because of his bad health with ulcer|ulcerative colitis. He was replaced by Yoshihide Suga on 16 September 2020.
