
'''''Pretty Woman''''' is a 1990 United States|American romantic comedy movie set in Los Angeles. It stars Julia Roberts as a street prostitution|street prostitute and Richard Gere as a rich businessman. It became one of the highest-grossing movies of 1990. Roberts won a Golden Globe Award for her performance.
