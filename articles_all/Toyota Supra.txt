
The '''Toyota Supra''' was a sports car made by Toyota from 1979 until 2002. The Supra was called the '''Celica Supra''' until 1986, when it became a separate model. Toyota cancelled the manufacturing of the Supra in the United States in 1998 and Canada in 1995 Until 2020 when they released the GR Supra or A90 Supra (Fifth Generation).
