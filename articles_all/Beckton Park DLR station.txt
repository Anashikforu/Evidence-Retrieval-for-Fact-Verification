
'''Beckton Park DLR station''' is a station on the Docklands Light Railway (DLR) in the London Docklands|Docklands area of east London. The station is located by the north quay (wharf) of the Royal Albert Dock (though no foot access is possible). The station is opposite Beckton District South Park, which is open space leading to housing in South Beckton.
