
'''B'z''' is a Japanese rock music|hard rock band. It is made up of Takahiro Matsumoto (guitar) and Koshi Inaba (singer). B'z started playing on September 21, 1988. The band has sold more than 70 million Compact disc|CDs. They have had 19 albums sell over 1 million copies each.
