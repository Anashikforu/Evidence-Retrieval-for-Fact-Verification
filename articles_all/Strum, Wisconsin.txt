



'''Strum''' is a village in Trempealeau County, Wisconsin|Trempealeau County of the U.S. state|state of Wisconsin in the United States.  It can be found along the Buffalo River (Wisconsin)|Buffalo River. 1,001 people were living in Strum as of the 2000 census. Strum has an area of 1.2 square miles, says the United States Census Bureau.
