
'''Varg Vikernes''' (legal name '''Louis Cachet''', born '''Kristian Vikernes''' on February 11, 1973 near Bergen, Norway) is a black metal musician, convicted murderer, fire|arsonist and political activist. In 1991, Vikernes created the one-man music project Burzum. Burzum quickly became important within the early Norwegian black metal scene. Later he became a part of the Heathen Front and wrote several writings on Germanic neopaganism.

In the early 1990s, there were black metal bands who believed in burning churches to prove their hatred for Christianity. Vikernes was found Guilt (law)|guilty of arson and the murder of Mayhem band member Euronymous|Øystein 'Euronymous' Aarseth.
On July 16th 2013, Vikernes and his wife, a French national, were arrested in Corrèze, France, on suspicion of planning acts of terrorism because his wife had purchased four rifles.
