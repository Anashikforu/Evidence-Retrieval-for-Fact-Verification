'''Hollywood Undead''' is a rap rock music|rock band from Los Angeles, California. They became a band in 2005 when members Aron Erlichman (Deuce) and Jorel Decker (J-Dog) released the song "The Kids" on their MySpace account. Their first album ''Swan Songs'' was released in 2008 and became a hit claiming Gold in the United States.
