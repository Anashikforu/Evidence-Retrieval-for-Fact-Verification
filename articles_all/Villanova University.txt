The '''Villanova University''' is a private university in Villanova, Pennsylvania|Villanova, Pennsylvania. The university has about 9,535 students. It was founded in 1842. The current president of the university is Rev. Peter M. Donohue, O.S.A. .

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Wildcats. They have 24 varsity teams, most of which compete in the Big East Conference. The American football|football team plays in the Colonial Athletic Association, and the women's rowing team is a member of the American Athletic Conference.
