
'''Sarah Jessica Parker''' (born March 25, 1965) is an Americans|American television and movie actress, producer, and fashion designer. She was born in Nelsonville, Ohio.

Parker is partly of Jewish descent (her father is Jewish) and considers herself Jewish.
She played Carrie Bradshaw in the television series ''Sex and the City''. She has also starred in many movies, such as the 1984 movie ''Footloose''.
