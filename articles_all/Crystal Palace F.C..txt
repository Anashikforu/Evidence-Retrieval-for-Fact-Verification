
'''Crystal Palace Football Club''' ('''Crystal Palace''') is an England|English Association football|football club. It is based in South Norwood in the London Borough of Croydon. The club was founded in 1905. The home stadium of the club is Selhurst Park.

Crystal Palace has played in the English Premier League|Premier League since 2013. They were runners-up in the FA Cup in 1990 and 2016.
