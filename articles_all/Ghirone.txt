
'''Ghirone''' was a municipality of the district Blenio (district)|Blenio in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 22 October 2006 the former municipalities of Aquila, Switzerland|Aquila, Campo (Blenio), Ghirone, Olivone and Torre, Switzerland|Torre merged to form the new municipality of Blenio.
It is no longer a municipality.
