
'''Jamie Benn''' (born July 18, 1989 in Victoria, British Columbia|Victoria, British Columbia) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|left winger . He currently plays for the Dallas Stars of the National Hockey League (NHL). His older brother Jordie Benn also plays for the Dallas Stars.

Before playing in the NHL, Benn played 2 seasons with the Kelowna Rockets of the Western Hockey League (WHL). He won a gold medal with Canada at the 2009 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships when they defeated Sweden in the Finals. He also scored the game-winning goal in Canada's first game against Norway
