

'''Brett W. Gurewitz''' (born May 12, 1962 in Los Angeles, California), nicknamed '''Mr. Brett''', is an Americans|American guitarist. He is the guitarist and a songwriter of the punk rock band, Bad Religion. He is also the owner of the music label Epitaph Records. He left and returned to Bad Religion twice and is still with the band today.
