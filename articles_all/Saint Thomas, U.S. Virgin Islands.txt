

'''Saint Thomas''' is an island in the Caribbean Sea. It is a county and constituent Districts and sub-districts of the United States Virgin Islands|district of the United States Virgin Islands (USVI). The territorial capital and port of Charlotte Amalie, U.S. Virgin Islands|Charlotte Amalie is on the island. As of the 2000 census, Saint Thomas had 51,181 people
