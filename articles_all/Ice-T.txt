'''Tracy Marrow''' (born February 16, 1958), better known by his stage name '''Ice-T''', is an Americans|American hip hop|rapper, actor, record producer, singer and songwriter. He was born in Newark, New Jersey|Newark, New Jersey and moved to Summit, New Jersey during his childhood. He moved to Los Angeles, California in his early teens. His movies include ''Ricochet'' and ''New Jack City''. He is best known for his role in the TV series ''Law & Order: Special Victims Unit''.
