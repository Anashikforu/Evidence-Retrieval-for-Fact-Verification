Legacy
In ancient Japan, there were four noble clans, the ''Gempeitōkitsu'' (源平藤橘).  One of these clans, the Minamoto clan are also known as Genji (源氏). The Saga Genji (嵯峨源氏) are said to be descended from Emperor Saga.
