In photography, a '''slide'''  is a thin translucent photograph bound in paper, plastic or similar frame and often used for projection with use of a slide projector for a slideshow. The terms '''reversal film''' and '''transparency''' are also used. 

One can wikt:distinguish|distinguish it from negative (photography)|negative and photographic printing|print.
