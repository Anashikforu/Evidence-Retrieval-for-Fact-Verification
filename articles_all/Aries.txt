 Mythology 
In mythology Aries is related to Greek mythology|Greek myth of the sheep|ram which carried Athamas's son Phrixus and daughter Helle (mythology)|Helle to Colchis away from their evil stepmother Ino. This ram is also related to Theseus, from the Greek myth of the Minotaur.

 Astrology
