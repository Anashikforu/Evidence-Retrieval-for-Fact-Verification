

'''''Mechanical Animals''''' is the third studio album by United States|American rock music|rock band, Marilyn Manson (band)|Marilyn Manson. It was released on September 14, 1998. The producers of the album were Michael Beinhorn, Marilyn Manson (musician)|Marilyn Manson and Sean Beavan. The album received controversy from Wal-Mart because of the album's artwork which features Manson covered from head to toe in latex paint.
