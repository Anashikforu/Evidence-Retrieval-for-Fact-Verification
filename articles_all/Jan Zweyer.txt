
'''Jan Zweyer''' (real name '''Rüdiger Richartz'''; born December 12, 1953 in Frankfurt)
 Career 
First he studied architecture and finished it with an exam. Then he studied social sciences and got an exam in it, too. and worked as a clerk for science. After that he worked as a  journalist who is only paid when he can sell his stories.
