'''Hydrochloric acid''' is a chemical compound. It is a solution of hydrogen chloride (Hydrogen|HChlorine|Cl) gas that is Solvation|dissolved in water. In the past, people called it '''muriatic acid'''. It is widely used in industry, and it is also made by the stomach to help Digestion|digest food. Hydrochloric acid, like all acids, react with base (chemistry)|bases to produce salts (chlorides).

Safety


The Environmental Protection Agency says hydrochloric acid is a toxin.
