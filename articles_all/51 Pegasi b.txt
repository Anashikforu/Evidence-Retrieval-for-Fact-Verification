

'''51 Pegasi b''', also called '''Bellerephon''' or '''51 Peg b''', is the first extrasolar planet discovered orbiting a main-sequence star,  51 Pegasi. Michel Mayor and Didier Queloz announced the discovery on 6 October 1995. It is 50 light years away in the direction of the constellation Pegasus (constellation)|Pegasus.Deborah Byrd and Eleanor Imster 2017. Today in science: 1st planet orbiting a sunlike star.
