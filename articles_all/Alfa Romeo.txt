'''Alfa Romeo Automobiles S.p.A.''' is an Italy|Italian car manufacturer created on June 24, 1910 in Milan. and since February 2007 a part of Fiat Group Automobiles S.p.A. The company was originally known as A.L.F.A., which stands for Anonima Lombarda Fabbrica Automobili (English: Lombard Automobile Factory, Public Company).
