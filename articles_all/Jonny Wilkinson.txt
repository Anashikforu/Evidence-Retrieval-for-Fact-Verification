
'''Jonathan Peter Wilkinson''' OBE (born 25 May 1979) is an English people|English rugby union player who now plays in France for RC Toulonnais|Toulon. 

He was born at Frimley Park Hospital in Frimley, Surrey, He was grew up in Farnham 

He played for England when the team won the 2003 Rugby World Cup. He was awarded the MBE in 2003 and then the Order of the British Empire|OBE in 2004.
