'''Line 7''' or '''L7''' coloured brown (currently known as '''Plaça Catalunya - Avinguda Tibidabo'''), is a rapid transit|metro in the Barcelona Metro network that is ran by Ferrocarrils de la Generalitat de Catalunya|FGC. It is also called '''Línia de Balmes''', because it climbs Carrer de Balmes uphill into Tibidabo. It shares three stations with Barcelona Metro line 6|L6.
