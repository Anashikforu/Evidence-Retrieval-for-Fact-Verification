

"''''4 AM''''" is a 1998 alternative rock song by the Canada|Canadian alternative rock band Our Lady Peace and is the fourth single (music)|single from their second studio album ''Clumsy (album)|Clumsy'', which was released in 1997. The song was written by Raine Maida and was produced by Arnold Lanni. They thought of the name 4 AM because they wrote the song at 4:00 AM and Raine Maida's father, Anthony Maida has the initials "AM".
