

'''Fujian''' () is a Political divisions of China|province on the southeast coast of People's Republic of China|China. The capital is Fuzhou. 

Most of Fujian is administered by the People's Republic of China. However some Fujian Province, Republic of China|Fujian areas like Kinmen (also called Quemoy) and Matsu Islands|Matsu are under the control of the Republic of China. The PRC however, still claim that Kinmen and Matsu to be part of Fujian Province of the PRC.

 Geography 
Fujian is separated from Taiwan by the 180km wide Taiwan Strait. Some of the small islands in the strait are also part of the province.
Small parts of the province, namely the islands of Quemoy|Kinmen and Matsu Islands|Matsu, are under the administration of the Republic of China. The area of Fujian is about 121,400 square kilometers.

 Administrative divisions

The People's Republic of China controls most of the province, and divides it into nine Political divisions of China#Prefecture-level|prefecture-level divisions.



All of these cities except Longyan, Sanming, and Nanping are found along the coast.

 Economy 


Fujian is one of the powerhouses in Chinese economy. In 2008, Fujian's nominal GDP rose by 13% from the previous year.
 Demographics 
The population is 34.71 million (2000 population census). Han Chinese make up most of the population.

= Dialects =

 There are several local dialects spoken by people from different regions in Fujian. People from the South (Xiamen, Quanzhou, etc.) speak Min, also known as Hokkien.
