


'''''Asclepias cordifolia''''' (also known as the '''heart-leaf milkweed''' or '''purple milkweed''') is a species of milkweed plant. It is not the same as ''Asclepias purpurascens'', which is also known as purple milkweed.  It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to western North America.  Heart-leaf Milkweed is found in woodlands, Rock (geology)|rocky slopes, and lava flows.
