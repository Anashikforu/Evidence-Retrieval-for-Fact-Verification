'''Cattenom Power Plant''' is a nuclear power power plant|plant. It is near Metz, Grand Est, France. It has four pressurized water reactors. Each has a capacity of 1300 megawatts. In 2006, it produced the third most electrictity of all power plants in France, behind that of Gravelines Nuclear Power Plant (near Calais) and Plaluel nuclear power plant|Plaluel (near Dieppe).
