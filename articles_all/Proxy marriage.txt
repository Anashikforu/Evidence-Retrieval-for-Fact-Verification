A '''proxy marriage''' is when two people are getting married separately, but are getting married to one another. It was common for European kings and nobles to be wedding|wed by proxy marriage. Napoleon I of France was wed by proxy marriage to Marie Louise, Duchess of Parma. Catherine of Aragon wed Arthur, Prince of Wales|Prince Arthur by proxy.
