
'''Taylor Daniel Lautner''' (; born February 11 1992) is an Americans|American actor. He is best known for playing Jacob Black in the ''Twilight Saga'' movie series, though he has also been in family movies and done voiceover work for animated cartoons.

 Early life 
Lautner was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan. His parents are Deborah, who works for a software development company, and Daniel Lautner, a commercial airline pilot.
