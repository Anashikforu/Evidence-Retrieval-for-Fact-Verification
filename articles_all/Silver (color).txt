



'''Silver'''  is the color of the metal silver.  It is a grayish-white color.

The first written use of ''silver'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1481.Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 204; Color Sample of Silver: Page 97 Plate 37 Color Sample A2 


 Meaning of silver 

 Like the color Gold (color)|gold, silver represents wealth because the metal silver is Value|valuable, although not quite as valuable as gold.
