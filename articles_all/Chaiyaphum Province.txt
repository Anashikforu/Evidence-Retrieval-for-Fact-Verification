
'''Chaiyaphum''' (Thai: ชัยภูมิ) is one of the Isan (''north-eastern'') Provinces of Thailand|provinces (''changwat'') of Thailand. As of 2000, 1 million people live there, and has an area of 12,778.3&nbsp;km². The Governor is Thawon Phrommichai.

Geography
 Chulabhorn Dam
 Administrative divisions 

The province has 16 districts (amphoe); 124 subdistricts (tambon) and 1393 villages (muban).
