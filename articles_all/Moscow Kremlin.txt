
The '''Moscow Kremlin''', usually just called the '''Kremlin''', is a fortress in the center of Moscow, where most of the Russian government works.  It is where the President of Russia lives. It is on the left bank of the Moskva River and southwest of Red Square.

The Kremlin and Red Square are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
