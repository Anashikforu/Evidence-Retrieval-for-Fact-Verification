
The '''Governor of the State of Oklahoma''' is the head of state for the Oklahoma|State of Oklahoma, United States. 

The office was created in 1907 when Oklahoma was officially admitted to the United States as the 46th U.S. state|state. Prior to statehood in 1907, the office was preceded by a President of the United States|Presidential appointed List of governors of Oklahoma|Governor of Oklahoma Territory with similar powers. The 28th and current Governor of Oklahoma is Kevin Stitt, a member of the Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party.
