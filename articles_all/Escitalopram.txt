'''Escitalopram''' is an antidepressant medication. It is used as a drug to help treat Depression (mental illness)|depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder, social anxiety disorder and other mental illnesses. It is commonly known and sold as ''Lexapro'' or ''Cipralex''.
 Side Effects 
Escitalopram can cause sleeping problems, dry mouth and diarrhea. It can also cause sexual problems.
