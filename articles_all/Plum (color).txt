'''Plum''' is a color similar to purple. The name comes from the fruit called a plum.

The first written use of ''plum'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1805.Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 202; Color Sample of Plum: Page 117 Plate 47 Color Sample J9 Note: The color designated plum (color #8E4585) that is displayed in the large color swatch above at the top of the page matches the color identified as plum in the color sample in Maerz and Paul
