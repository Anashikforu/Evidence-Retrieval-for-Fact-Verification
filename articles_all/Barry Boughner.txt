
'''Barry Michael Boughner''' (born January 29, 1948 in Delhi, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|right winger. He played 20 National Hockey League games with the California Golden Seals|Oakland Seals/California Golden Seals, going scoreless with 11 penalty minutes.

Boughner served as head coach for the Niagara Falls Flyers of the Ontario Hockey Association for a portion of the 1979–80 OMJHL season|1979–80 season.
