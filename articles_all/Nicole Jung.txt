'''Nicole Yongju Jung''' (Hangul: 정니콜; Korean name: '''Jung Yong-Ju''', Hangul: 정용주), better known mononymous person|mononymously as '''Nicole''', was born on October 7, 1991. She is a Korean-American singer. Born and raised in Glendale, California but moved to South Korea to pursue her dreams. 
Biography
Nicole was born on October 7, 1991, in Glendale, California. She moved to Korea with her mom. Her mother has a ramyeon restaurant called "Red Kitchen". She is also very popular with a lot of fans and is a member of Korea's K-POP "KARA"

Career
=Kara=


Kara debuted on March 29th 2007 with "Break It" on M! Countdown as a 4 member. Nicole was one of the four along with Park Gyu-ri|Gyuri, Han Seungyeon|Seungyeon, and Kim Sunghee|Sunghee. Kara was often compared to Fin.K.L because of their concept and the fact that they are both from the same company(DSP Entertainment). Later on Sunghee left the group in March 2008 due to parental pressure, just when Kara intended their comeback. Sunghee failed her college exams and left the group. On July 24th of 2008, Kara came back with two new members, Goo Hara and Kang Jiyoung|Jiyoung. Their new concept was "cute" and "playful".

In December 4, 2008, Kara have their comeback starting on M! Countdown with "Pretty Girl (song)|Pretty Girl".In February 2009, Kara released their second mini-album "Honey (Kara song)|Honey". "Honey" became the group's first #1 single when it topped the ''M! Countdown'' and The Music Trend.. ''JoongAng Ilbo|Joins.com''. March 9, 2009. Retrieved March 9, 2009.

In late July 2009, Kara revealed their second album named "Revolution (Kara album)|Revolution". 
=Solo activities=

Nicole joined ''Star Golden Bell'' on December 20, 2008, hosting a segment called "Level With Me (Season 2)" in which the guests have to guess a term or title from her clues; the challenge comes due to her comparative lack of skill and experience with the Korean language. The segment ended on October 17, 2009.

In April 2009, she sang a duet with former Noel (band)|Noel member Kang Kyun-seong called "Happy and".

In August 2009, she and 2PM ex member Jaebeom became part of historical culture variety show Nodaji, replacing Choi Min Yong and Kim Tae Hyun. But the show was canceled due to low ratings on October 2009.

In November 2009, She joined ''Nicole The Entertainer's Introduction to Veterinary Science''.

In July 2010, she joined ''Good Sunday - Yeongwunghogeol (영웅호걸)''.
