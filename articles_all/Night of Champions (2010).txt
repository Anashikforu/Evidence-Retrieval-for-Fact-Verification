
'''Night of Champions (2010)''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), that took place on September 19, 2010 at the Allstate Arena in Chicago, Illinois. It is also the first Night of Champions event to feature a non-title match.

 The lumberjills were: Alicia Fox, Brie Bella, Eve Torres, Gail Kim, Jillian Hall, Kelly Kelly, Layla El|Layla, Maryse Ouellet|Maryse, Natalya Neidhart|Natalya, Nikki Bella, Rosa Mendes and Tamina Snuka|Tamina.
