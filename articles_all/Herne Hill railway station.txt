
'''Herne Hill railway station''' is a train station in Herne Hill, a location in the London Borough of Lambeth, South London, England. Its location is . 

Transport for London has thought of making the Victoria line go to Herne Hill to give faster turnaround at the southern end of the line. The extension is not a high thing for TfL as it has a weaker business case than other projects.
Services

The station is looked after by Southeastern (train operating company)|Southeastern train operating company. The off-peak service pattern  is:

 Two trains per hour (tph) to Luton around Elephant & Castle#FCC_May2012|First Capital Connect 2012, p. 115. 
 2&nbsp;tph to St Albans around Elephant & Castle
 4&nbsp;tph to London Victoria station|London Victoria around Brixton#SE_May2012|Southeastern 2012, p. 52.

 2&nbsp;tph to Sutton around Wimbledon#FCC_May2012|First Capital Connect 2012, p. 107.
 2&nbsp;tph to Sutton around Mitcham Eastfields

On Sunday, northbound Sutton Loop trains change between stopping at Blackfriars and Luton.#FCC_May2012|First Capital Connect 2012, p. 120. Late-night Sutton loop services (21:57 onwards) run to Bedford from Monday-Saturday and finish at Blackfriars on Sunday.#FCC_May2012|First Capital Connect 2012, p. 116.

First Capital Connect and Southeastern jointly have extra Rush hour#United_Kingdom|peak trains from Herne Hill to Beford (morning and evening peaks),#FCC_May2012|First Capital Connect 2012, pp. 126,115. Beckenham Junction Kent House, Rochester and Swanley (evening peak only).#FCC_May2012|First Capital Connect 2012, pp. 107,123.

Buses

Herne Hill has buses by London bus which routes are 3, 37 (24 hours), 68, 196, 201, 322, 468, N3 and N68. The nearest London Underground station is Brixton tube station|Brixton.
