
'''Walter Bruce Willis''' (born March 19, 1955 in Germany) is an Americans|American actor and film producer. 

Willis was married to actress Demi Moore and they had three daughters before their divorce in 2001. They were married for thirteen years. He has received many awards and honors in his career. Willis supports the Military of the United States|United States armed forces and has been a spokesman several times.

Some of his best-known movies are ''Die Hard'', ''The Player'', ''Pulp Fiction'' and ''Sin City''.
