'''Jhang District''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Jhang is the capital.

Administration
The district of Jhang is administratively subdivided into 3 tehsils, these are:

 Chiniot District (2009)
 Jhang Tehsil
 Shorkot Tehsil
 Ahmad Pur Sial Tehsil
 Athara Hazari Tehsil
