
'''Mickey Rooney''' (born '''Joseph Yule'''; September 23, 1920 &ndash; April 6, 2014) was an Americans|American actor and producer.

In his career, he won many awards, such as an Academy Award|Oscar, a Golden Globe and an Emmy Award. Best known for his work as the Andy Hardy character, Rooney had one of the longest movie careers of any actor.

Rooney married eight times and had nine children. He separated from his eighth wife in 2012.
Rooney died after a long illness on April 6, 2014 at his home in Los Angeles, California. He was 93 years old.
