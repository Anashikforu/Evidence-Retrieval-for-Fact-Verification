'''Donald John Judson Aiken''' (January 1, 1932 &ndash; November 2, 2021) was an Americans|American ice hockey player. He played one game for the Montreal Canadiens in the National Hockey League between the 1957 and 1958 season. He was born in Arlington, Massachusetts|Arlington, Massachusetts.

Aiken died on November 2, 2021 in Reading, Massachusetts at the age of 89.
