'''Telephone tapping''' or '''wire tapping'''/'''wiretapping''' is when somebody hearing|listens to another person's telephone|telephone calls. They usually use a listening device called a ''bug'' to listen to and record the conversation so that another person can then listen to it later.

This is legal|illegal in many country|countries, including the United Kingdom|UK and United States|US because it means that telephone calls are not private. Sometimes, the police need to 'tap' into telephone calls to catch terrorists or other criminals. Evidence from illegal tops may be used in criminal trials in some countries.
