

'''Flock''' is a web browser based on Mozilla Firefox. Flock is also the name of the company making the browser.

Awards
Upon leaving beta, Flock has won a number of awards:

''Webby Award'' in social networking, 2008
''SXSW'' community Award, 2008 
''Open Web Award'' for Applications and Widgets, 2007
