
'''Queen Silvia of Sweden''' (born '''Silvia Renate Sommerlath''') (born 23 December 1943) and of half German, half Brazilian descent is the Queen consort of Carl XVI Gustaf of Sweden|King Carl XVI Gustaf, Sweden's monarch, and the mother of the heir apparent to the throne, Princess Victoria of Sweden|Crown Princess Victoria. Their other two children are
 Prince Carl Philip of Sweden and
 Princess Madeleine of Sweden

She is styled ''Her Majesty The Queen'' and is in Sweden most often referred to as ''Queen Silvia''. 

She also make efforts for charitable organizations, human rights and sign language and opposes child sexual abuse and child pornography.
