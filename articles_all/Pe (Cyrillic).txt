'''Peh''' ('''П''', '''п''') is the fifteenth letter in the Cyrillic alphabet. It sounds like p. Its wikt:italic|italic wikt:lowercase|lowercase form is ''п''.

It comes from the Greek alphabet|Greek letter pi (letter)|pi and stands for the Roman alphabet|Roman letter P.
