'''Salat al Eid''' () is the special prayer offered to commemorate two Islamic festivals. Both the Eids are collectively known as Eidain (عيدين) in Arabic language. The two Islamic festivals are:

Eid Al Fitr, () is celebrated on the 1st day of Shawwal, the 10th month of Islamic calendar.
Eid Al Adha, () is celebrated on the 10th day of Dhu al-Hijjah, the 12th month of Islamic calendar.
