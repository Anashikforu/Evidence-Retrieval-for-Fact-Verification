
'''Harnett County''' is a county in the U.S. state|state of North Carolina, United States.  In 2000, 91,025 people lived there.  Its county seat is Lillington, North Carolina|Lillington.

 History 
The county was made in 1855 from Cumberland County, North Carolina|Cumberland County.  It was named for Cornelius Harnett.

 Government 
Harnett County is a member of the Mid-Carolina Council of Governments.
