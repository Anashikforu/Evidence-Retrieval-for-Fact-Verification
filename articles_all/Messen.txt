
'''Messen''' is a municipality in the Bucheggberg (district)|district Bucheggberg, in the canton of Solothurn, Switzerland. On 1 January 2010 the former municipalities of Balm bei Messen, Brunnenthal, Switzerland|Brunnenthal, Messen and Oberramsern merged to form the municipality of Messen.
