

The order (biology)|order '''Lamiales''' is a taxon in the Asteridae|asterid group of dicotyledonous flowering plants. It includes about 11,000 species divided up into about 10 family (biology)|families. A few well-known members of this order are the lavender, the lilac, the Olive (fruit)|olive, the jasmine, the ash tree, the teak, the snapdragon, and a number of table herbs such as mentha|mint, basil, and rosemary.
