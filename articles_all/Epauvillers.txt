

'''Epauvillers''' is a village and former municipality in the district of Franches-Montagnes (district)|Franches-Montagnes in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland. On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Epauvillers, Epiquerez, Montenol, Montmelon, Ocourt, Saint-Ursanne, Seleute merged into the new municipality of Clos du Doubs.
