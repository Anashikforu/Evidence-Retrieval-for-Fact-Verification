

The '''Reichstag''' was the Germany|German parliament during the Holy Roman Empire, the North German Confederation, the German Empire, the Weimar Republic and the Third Reich until 1945.  They met in the Reichstag (building)|Reichstag building from 1894 to 1933.  The Reichstag is now called German Bundestag|Bundestag.
