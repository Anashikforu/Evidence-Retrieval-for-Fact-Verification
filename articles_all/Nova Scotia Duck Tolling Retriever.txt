
The '''Nova Scotia Duck-Tolling Retriever''' is a breed of sport and hunting dog that wikt:origin|originated in Canada in the 19th century to help hunters with wikt:attract|attracting ducks within gunshot range and bringing the duck back to the hunter. They stand 17-21 inches (43 to 53 cm) and weigh 43 to 51 Pound (mass)|pounds (19 to 23 kg). Its thick coat (dog)|coat of fur protects it from the icy cold water.
