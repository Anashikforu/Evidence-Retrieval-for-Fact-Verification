'''Le Locheur''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Basse-Normandie in the Calvados (department)|Calvados Departments of France|department in the northwest of France. About 277 people lived there in 2006. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of Val d'Arry.
