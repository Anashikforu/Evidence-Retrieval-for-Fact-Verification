'''NASDAQ''' is a stock exchange in New York City. It stands for '''National Association of Securities Dealers Automated Quotations'''. About 3,800 companies trade on it. It is owned by a company called Nasdaq, Inc.

NASDAQt was founded in 1971 by the National Association of Securities Dealers (NASD).
