'''Dhudial''' is a village and Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council, an administrative subdivision, of Chakwal District in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province of Pakistan, it is part of Chakwal Tehsil. It is one of the largest villages of Chakwal District and is located at 33°3'52N 72°58'24E
Tribes
