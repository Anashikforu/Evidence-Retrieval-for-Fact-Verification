

The '''New York City Transit Police Department''', that was started in 1953, was a transit police wikt:department|department for the New York City Subway and bus lines. It became part of the NYPD in 1995 as the NYPD#Transit Bureau|Transit Bureau of the New York City Police Department by wikt:decision|decision of New York City Mayor Rudolph W. Giuliani.
