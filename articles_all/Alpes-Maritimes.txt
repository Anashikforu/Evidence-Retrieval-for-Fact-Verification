
'''Alpes-Maritimes''' () is a Departments of France|department of the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur Regions of France|region in the extreme southeast corner of France.

The name Alpes-Maritime comes from ,  a Ancient Rome|Roman military district created in the region by Augustus in 14 Anno Domini|BC.

History

The department of the Alpes-Martimes was created on 4 February 1793 with the territory of the County of Nice that was Annexation|annexed in January of the same year. The Capital city|capital was Nice. It was divided in three districts: Menton, Nice and Puget-Théniers. In 1794, district of Menton was changed to district of Fort-d'Hercule (Monaco).

In 1800, with the creation of the Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'' in France, the three districts were changed into three ''arrondissements'': Nice, Monaco and Puget-Théniers.

After the annexation of Sanremo|San Remo, the ''arrondissement''  of San Remo was created in 1805. By the Peace of Paris (1814)|Peace of Paris in 1814, San Remo passed to Genoa and the rest of the department passed to the Kingdom of Sardinia.

When Nice became part of France in 1860, the department was created again with Nice as the capital and with the ''arrondisement'' of Grasse that was part of Var (department)|Var department. In the same year, the department was divided in two ''arrondissements'': Grasse and Puget-Théniers.

On 10 September 1926, the ''arrondissement'' of Puget-Théniers was eliminated.

Administration
Alpes-Maritimes is managed by the ''Departmental Council of Alpes-Maritimes'' in Nice. The department is part of the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region.

=Administrative divisions=
There are 2 Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'' (districts), 27 Cantons in France|''cantons'' and 163 Communes in France|''communes'' (Municipality|municipalities) in Alpes-Maritimes.

Demographics
The inhabitants of Alpes-Maritimes are known, in French language|French, as ''Maralpins'' (women: ''Maralpines'').

Alpes-Maritimes had a population, in 2014, of 1,083,312, for a population density of  inhabitants/km2. The ''arrondissement'' of Grasse, with 560,045 inhabitants, is the ''arrondissement'' with more inhabitants.
