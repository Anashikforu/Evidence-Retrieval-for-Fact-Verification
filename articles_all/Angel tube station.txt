'''Angel tube station''' is a London Underground station in The Angel, Islington|The Angel, Islington. It is on the Bank and Monument stations|Bank branch of the Northern line, between Old Street station|Old Street and King's Cross St. Pancras tube station|King's Cross St. Pancras stations. It is in Travelcard Zone 1.
