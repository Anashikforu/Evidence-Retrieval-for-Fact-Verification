
'''Scott Brown''' (born 25 June 1985 in Dunfermline, Fife) is a Scotland|Scottish association football player, currently playing for Scottish Premier League club Aberdeen F.C.|Aberdeen. Brown, who started his professional career with Hibernian F.C.|Hibernian, is a regular in the Scotland national football team|Scottish national team. His transfer to Celtic from Hibs in the summer of 2007 broke the record for the biggest transfer fee paid between two Scottish clubs.
