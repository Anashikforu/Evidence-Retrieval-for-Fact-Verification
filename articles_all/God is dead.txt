"'''God is dead'''" (German language|German: ) is a well known phrase by the German philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche. It is also known as the '''death of God'''. The phrase is not meant literally. Instead it is about what value destruction has done to people's belief in Christianity. Some religious thinkers do take it literally though.
