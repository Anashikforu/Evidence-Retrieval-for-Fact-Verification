
'''Jack Sparrow''' is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the ''Pirates of the Caribbean'' movie series. The character was created by screenwriters Ted Elliott (screenwriter)|Ted Elliott and Terry Rossio. He is played by Johnny Depp.

He later appears in the sequels ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest|Dead Man's Chest'' (2006), ''Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End|At World's End'' (2007), ''Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides|On Stranger Tides'' (2011), and ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales|Dead Men Tell No Tales'' (2017).

Biography
Sparrow is one of the nine pirate lords in the Brethren Court, the Pirate Lords of the Seven Seas. He is the caption of the ''Black Pearl'' and his main enemy is Hector Barbossa. Later, he tries to escape his blood debt to the legendary Davy Jones (Pirates of the Caribbean)|Davy Jones while fighting the East India Company|East India Trading Company.
