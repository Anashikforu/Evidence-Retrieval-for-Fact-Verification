
'''Calcium hydroxide''', also known as '''slaked lime''', is a chemical compound with the formula Ca(OH)2. It dissolves slightly in water. It is a base (chemistry)|base. It can be made by the chloralkali process. It can be dehydrated (remove the water) to calcium oxide by heat. It can be used to neutralization|neutralize acidic soils.
