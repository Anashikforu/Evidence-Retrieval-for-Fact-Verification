



'''Däniken''' is a municipality in the Olten (district)|Olten district in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Solothurn (canton)|Solothurn in Switzerland. Rich people live in the upper half of Däniken. Others live in the lower half. That half has a nuclear power plant. Its name is Gösgen. Däniken has a lot of industry.
