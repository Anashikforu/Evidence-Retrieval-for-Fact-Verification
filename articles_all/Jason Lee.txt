
'''Jason Michael Lee''' (born April 25, 1970 in Orange, California) is an Americans|American professional Skateboarder turned actor. In 2005, he gambled on accepting the lead in the ''My Name is Earl'' television series. The sitcom ran for four years. In 2007, he played Dave Seville in ''Alvin and the Chipmunks (movie)|Alvin and the Chipmunks''. He played the role again in the 2009 sequel.
