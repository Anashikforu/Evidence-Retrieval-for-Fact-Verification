


'''''Ice Road Truckers''''' is a Canada|Canadian-United States|American Documentary film|documentary-style reality television television series|series that first appeared on television on The History Channel on June 17, 2007 (first season). The show follows the activities of truck drivers which operate on routes that contain frozen lakes and rivers in the remote Arctic territories of Canada and Alaska.
