
'''Harold Hugh "Cammie" Cameron''' (February 6, 1890 – October 20, 1953) was a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman (ice hockey)|defenceman who played professionally for the Toronto Arenas|Toronto Blueshirts, Toronto Arenas, Ottawa Senators (original)|Ottawa Senators, Toronto St. Pats and Montreal Canadiens. Cameron won three Stanley Cups in his career. His first as a member of the 1913–14 NHA season|1913–14 Toronto Blueshirts, his second as a member of the 1917–18 NHL season|1917–18 Blueshirts and his third as a member of the 1921–22 NHL season|1921–22 Toronto St. Pats (all predecessor clubs of the Toronto Maple Leafs).

Awards and achievements
 Inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1962.
