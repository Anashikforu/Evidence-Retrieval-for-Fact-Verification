'''Atlanta FC''' is an United States|American association football|football team based in Sandy Springs, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia, United States. Started in 2007, the team plays in National Premier Soccer League (NPSL), a national wikt:amateur|amateur league at the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid, in the Southeast Division.

The team plays its home games at Hoskyn Stadium on the grounds of Riverwood High School, where they have played since 2008. The team's colors are Orange (color)|orange, white, and sky blue.
