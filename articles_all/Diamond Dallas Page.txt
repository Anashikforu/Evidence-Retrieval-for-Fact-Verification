
'''Dallas Page''' (born '''Page Joseph Falkinburg, Jr.'''; April 5, 1956 in Point Pleasant, New Jersey|Point Pleasant, New Jersey) is a retired Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler, fitness instructor and actor who is probably best known for wrestling with World Championship Wrestling (WCW), World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation (WWF), Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) under the ring name, '''"Diamond" Dallas Page''' or "'''DDP'''" for short.
