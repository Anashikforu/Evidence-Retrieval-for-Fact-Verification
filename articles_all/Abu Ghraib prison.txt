'''Abu Ghraib prison''' is a prison in Abu Ghraib, a city 32&nbsp;km west of Baghdad, Iraq. It became known when Saddam Hussein tortured and executed dissidents there. After the United States had invaded Iraq, there was also a scandal where US military|forces tortured prisoners.
