
'''Marisa Tomei''' is an Americans|American movie, Theatre|stage and television actress. She was born in Brooklyn, New York City. She is of Italians|Italian ancestry. She has starred in a number of television programs, movies and stage plays. She starred in the comedy ''My Cousin Vinny'' in 1992.
