

'''''The Oprah Winfrey Show''''' was an United States|American talk show which had Oprah Winfrey as the Presenter|host. It began on September 8, 1986, and has since become one of the most famous talk shows in the world. The show ended on May 25, 2011 after 25 seasons. It won 47 Daytime Emmy Awards.

 Guests 
Notable guests on her show were:
 Gayle King &ndash; Oprah's friend (140 appearances)
 Celine Dion &ndash; Singer (27 appearances)
 Tom Cruise &ndash; Actor, known for jumping in the show's couch. He also appeared in the show's last episode.
 Nelson Mandela &ndash; President of South Africa.
