

'''Luis Enrique Martínez García''' (; born 8 May 1970), known as '''Luis Enrique''', is a Spanish professional Association football|football manager and former player. He is the current head coach of the Spain national football team|Spain national team. He is widely considered as one of the best managers in the world.
