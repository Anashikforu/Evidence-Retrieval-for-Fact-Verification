In Christianity, the words '''born again''', '''regenerated''' or '''transformed''' are synonymous with spirituality|spiritual rebirth and sometimes Salvation (Christianity)|salvation from the Holy Spirit. It means having a "personal" faith in Jesus|Jesus Christ. The term is most often used by Evangelicalism|Evangelical, Fundamentalism|Fundamentalists, Pentecostalism|Pentecostal and some Mainline (Protestant)|Mainline branches of Protestantism|Protestant Christianity.
