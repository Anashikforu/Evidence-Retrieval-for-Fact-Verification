The '''United States Secretary of Health, Education, and Welfare''' was the head of the United States Department of Health, Education, and Welfare.  The Secretary was a member of the President of the United States|President's United States Cabinet|Cabinet. In 1979, there was a division of the Secretary of Health, Education, and Welfare's position into separate secretaries of United States Secretary of Health and Human Services|Health and Human Services and United States Secretary of Education|Education.
