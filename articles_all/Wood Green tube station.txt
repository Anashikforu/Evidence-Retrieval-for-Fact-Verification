
'''Wood Green''' is a London Underground station on the Piccadilly Line. The station is between Turnpike Lane tube station|Turnpike Lane and Bounds Green tube station|Bounds Green stations and is in Travelcard Zone 3. It is located at junction of Green Lanes|Wood Green High Road and Lordship Lane, Haringey|Lordship Lane. It serves The Mall (Wood Green)|Wood Green Shopping City and the nearby London Borough of Haringey|Haringey Council administrative complex as well as a densely populated residential area.
