
'''Macon''' is a city in central Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia, USA. It is among the largest metropolitan areas in Georgia, and the county seat of Bibb County, Georgia|Bibb County. It is near the center of Georgia, more or less 75 miles (129&nbsp;km) south of Atlanta, Georgia|Atlanta, that's why the city's called the ''Heart of Georgia''. As of the United States Census, 2000|2000 census, Macon had a population of 97,255. Macon is the sixth-largest city by population of the state.
