

'''George Gideon Oliver Osborne''' Order of the Companions of Honour|CH Privy Council of the United Kingdom|PC (born May 23, 1971) is a British people|British politician. He was the Chancellor of the Exchequer from 2010 through 2016 in the David Cameron Cameron ministry|government. He is the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party Member of Parliament for the Tatton constituency in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the 2001 general election.

George Osborne was born in Paddington, London, England and was educated at the University of Oxford. He became an MP in 2001 and entered the shadow cabinet in 2004. In 2005, Osborne was made the Shadow Chancellor of the Exchequer by Michael Howard and held this post when David Cameron became leader of the Conservative Party. Osborne became Chancellor of the Exchequer on 11 May 2010 and has then has outlined pound sterling|£6.2 billion worth of cuts to help reduce the budget Economic deficit|deficit. He left office on 13 July 2016 when Theresa May became Prime Minister. He was replaced by Philip Hammond. In April 2017 he said he would not stand in the United Kingdom general election, 2017|2017 general election.

Osborne is part of the old Anglo-Irish aristocracy, known in Ireland as the ''Protestant Ascendancy|Ascendancy''. He is the heir to the Osborne baronet|baronetcy (of Ballentaylor, in County Tipperary, and Ballylemon, in County Waterford).
