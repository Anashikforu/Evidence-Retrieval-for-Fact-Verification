

'''Dido Florian Cloud de Bounevialle O'Malley Armstrong''' (born 25 December 1971) is a female English people|English singer who has released four albums. The single (music)|singles "Here with Me", "Hunter", "Thank You", and "All You Want" came from ''No Angel'' (1999). "White Flag", "Life for Rent", "Don't Leave Home", and "Sand in My Shoes" came from ''Life for Rent'' (2003). "Don't Believe in Love", "Quiet Times", and "It Comes and It Goes" came from ''Safe Trip Home'' (2008). In 2013 Dido's fourth album ''Girl Who Got Away'' was released. She is of French language|French descent.

Personal life
Dido is married to Rohan Gavin.
