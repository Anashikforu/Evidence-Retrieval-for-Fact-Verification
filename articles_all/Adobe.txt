

'''Adobe''' is a building material made from soil and often organic matter. It can also mean any kind of earth construction. 

Adobe is one of the earliest building materials ever used. It is found all over the world. The techniques used in adobe construction have not changed since the 16th century in the Southwestern United States. Because they are not fired in a kiln they never become hard. They wikt:shrink|shrink and wikt:swell|swell as the water content in them changes. Also, as they get wikt:wet|wetter, they become wikt:weak|weaker.

Adobe does not permanently stick to wood, metal or stone because it moves more than they do. This causes Cracking joints|cracks and wikt:seperation|seperation from other materials. Stone is sometimes used as a foundation (engineering)|foundation and wood for the roof. These are usually held in place by their own wikt:weight|weight or the weight of the adobe pressing down on them. But adobe construction is cheap and the materials are usually easy to find.
