

'''Priscilla Ahn''' (born 1984) is an Americans|American singer-songwriter. 

In 2008, Blue Note Reco.rds released her first full-length album, ''A Good Day''.  Ahn went on a national tour that June.  Ahn has toured with Willie Nelson, Amos Lee, Ray LaMontagne, Devotchka and Joshua Radin.  Ahn's songs have been on television shows and movies.  She has made appearances on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Dancing with the Stars and National Public Radio|NPR.
