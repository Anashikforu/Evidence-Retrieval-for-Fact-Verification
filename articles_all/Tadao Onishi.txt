Biography
Onishi was born in Kyoto on April 18, 1943. After graduating from Kyoto University of Education, he joined Urawa Reds|Mitsubishi Motors in 1967. The club won Japan Soccer League champions in 1969 and 1973. The club also won 1971 and 1973 Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1974. He played 83 games in the league.

In October 1969, Onishi was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for 1970 FIFA World Cup|1970 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on October 10, he debuted against Australia national soccer team|Australia.
