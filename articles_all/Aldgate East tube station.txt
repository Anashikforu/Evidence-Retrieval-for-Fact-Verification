
'''Aldgate East''' is a London Underground station. It is between Aldgate and Whitechapel in the London Borough of Tower Hamlets.

The station is in Travelcard Zone 1. It is on the Hammersmith & City Line between Liverpool Street station|Liverpool Street and Whitechapel tube station|Whitechapel, and the District Line between Tower Hill tube station|Tower Hill and Whitechapel tube station|Whitechapel.
