"'''Love the Way You Lie'''" is a 2010 Hip hop music|hip hop song by the United States|American rapper Eminem and featured Barbados|Barbadian singer Rihanna, and is the second Single (music)|single from his seventh studio album ''Recovery (Eminem album)|Recovery'', which was released in 2010. The song was written by Eminem, A. Grant, H. Hafferman, M. Riddick.

H. Hafferman is Holly Brook Hafermann who now writes and records as Skylar Grey.

The song's music video stars Megan Fox and Dominic Monaghan as a couple in a domestic violence|violent abusive relationship|abusive love-hate relationship.
