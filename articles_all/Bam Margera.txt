

'''Brandon Cole "Bam" Margera''' (born September 28, 1979 in West Chester, Pennsylvania) is famous as a skateboarder, stunt performer|daredevil and for being on television and radio. He made a number of videos for the CKY Videos|CKY. He became famous when he joined MTV's ''Jackass (TV series)|Jackass'' crew. He has also been in MTV's ''Viva La Bam'' and ''Bam's Unholy Union'', both Jackass: The Movie|''Jackass'' movies, and ''Haggard: The Movie|Haggard'', which he helped write and movie director|direct. In ''Viva La Bam'', he starred with his family: Phil Margera (dad), April Margera (mom), and Vincent Margera (uncle).
