

'''Jessica Simpson''' (born July 10, 1980) is an Americans|American singer and Actor|actress. She is known for her reality television show, Nick and Jessica: Newlyweds. In the late 2000s, she was married to Nick Lachey. In 2008, she filed for divorce and she married Eric Johnson. She is the daughter of Tina and Joe Simpson and older sister of singer and actress Ashlee Simpson.

She has starred in the movies ''The Dukes of Hazzard (movie)|The Dukes of Hazzard'' (2005) and ''Employee of the Month (2006 movie)|Employee of the Month'' (2006)

Early life
Jessica Simpson was born in Abilene, Texas. She is the daughter of Tina Ann and Joseph Truett. She has a sister whose name is Ashlee Simpson who is also a singer.
At first, Simpson sang at a local church. At age twelve, she auditioned for The Mickey Mouse Club, but was not selected.

 Personal life 
Simpson was married to musician Nick Lachey from 2002 until they divorced in 2006.
