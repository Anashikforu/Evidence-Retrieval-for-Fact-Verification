'''Iodate''' is an ion. Its chemical formula is IO3-. The iodine is in the +5 oxidation state. It can be made by reduction (chemistry)|reduction of periodate. They are similar to chlorates. They react with acids to make iodic acid. They are salts of iodic acid. An example would be sodium iodate.
