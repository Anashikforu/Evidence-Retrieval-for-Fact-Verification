
The '''Hyundai Tucson''' is a compact luxury crossover|SUV that is produced by Hyundai Motor Company. In some markets it is sold as the Hyundai ix35|ix35, but the ix35 name is expected to be dropped. It is based on the Kia Sportage's unibody platform.
