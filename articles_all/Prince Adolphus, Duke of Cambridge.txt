 '''Prince Adolphus, Duke of Cambridge''' was the eighth son of King George III of the United Kingdom|George III. He married Princess Augusta of Hesse-Cassel in 1818. They had three children. One of them was Princess Mary Adelaide, the mother of Mary of Teck, who became the consort of George V of the United Kingdom.
