

'''José de San Martín''' (1778 - 1850) was an Argentina|Argentine general and politician.  He was born in Yapeyú, Corrientes Province|Corrientes, Spain. He liberated Argentina, Peru and Chile from Spain. In 1817, he crossed the Andes from Mendoza Province|Mendoza to Chile. Together with Simón Bolívar, San Martín is called one of the Liberators of South America.
