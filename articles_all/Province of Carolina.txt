The '''Province of Carolina''' was a United Kingdom|British colony in North America from 1663 to 1729. It covered the present U.S. state|states of North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia, and a part of Florida. An argument over how the it would be government|governed led to its becoming the Province of North Carolina and the Province of South Carolina. Officially, these two provinces did not become separate colonies until 1729.
