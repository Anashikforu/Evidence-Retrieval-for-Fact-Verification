

'''Andrés Arturo García Menéndez''' (born April 12, 1956), professionally known as '''Andy Garcia''', is an Cuban American actor and Movie director|director born in Cuba. He became famous in the 1990s, when he acted in many successful Hollywood movies, including ''The Godfather: Part III'', ''When a Man Loves a Woman (film)|When a Man Loves a Woman'', ''Ocean's Eleven'', and ''Ocean's Thirteen''. He was nominated for several awards for his work, including the Academy Award|Oscar.
