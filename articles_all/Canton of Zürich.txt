'''Zürich''' (Alemannic German|Swiss German: ''Züri'', French language|French: ''Zurich'', Italian language|Italian: ''Zurigo'', Romansh language|Romansh: ''Turitg'') is a Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Switzerland. About 1.2 million people live there. The canton is in the northeast of Switzerland. The city of Zürich City|Zürich is its capital. The official language is German language|German, but people speak the local Swiss German dialect called ''Züritüütsch''. English writers often write the name of the Canton of Zürich as '''Canton of Zurich'''.
