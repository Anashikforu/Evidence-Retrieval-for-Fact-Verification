
The '''Audi A2''' was a 5-door hatchback automobile produced by Audi from 1999 until 2005. The car was made from aluminium which made it light and economical but was expensive to produce, and demand for it fell later in its life, only one generation was made and had an indirect successor in 2010 with the A1.
