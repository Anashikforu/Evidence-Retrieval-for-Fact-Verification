

The '''Governor of the State of Kansas''' is the head of state for the Kansas|State of Kansas. Under the Kansas Constitution, the Governor is also the head of government. The Governor is the Commander-in-Chief of the Kansas National Guard when not called into Federal Government of the United States|Federal use.

Kansas has had 47 governors since becoming a state.

The current governor is Laura Kelly. She started on January 14, 2019.

Since 1962, the governor lives in the Cedar Crest (mansion)|governor's mansion.
