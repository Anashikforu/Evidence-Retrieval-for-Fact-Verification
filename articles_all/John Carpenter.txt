
'''John Howard Carpenter''' (born January 16, 1948) is an Americans|American movie director, movie producer|producer, screenwriter|writer and actor. He was born in Carthage, New York. He is known for horror movie|horror and science fiction movies including ''Halloween (movie)|Halloween'', ''The Thing (1982 movie)|The Thing'' and ''Escape from New York''.
