'''Dehwari''' is a Southwestern Iranian language spoken by over 20,0000 people in Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan, Pakistan. Most of the Dehwari speakers are concentrated in Mastung District|Mastung , Khuzdar District|Khuzdar, Nushki District|Nushk, Kharan District|Kharan, Sarlath District|Sarlath, Dalbandin District|Dalbandin, and Kalat District|Kalat.
