'''Sir Anthony Joseph Francis O'Reilly''' (born May 7, 1936) billionaire born in Dublin, Ireland. He is the chairman of Dublin's Independent News & Media Group and was once the chief executive officer|CEO of the H.J. Heinz Company.

O'Reilly played Rugby football|rugby for the Leicester Tigers and London Irish in England. He played for Ireland between 1955 and 1963.
