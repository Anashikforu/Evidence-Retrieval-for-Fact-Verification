

The '''Diprotodontia''' is a large order of about 120 Australasian marsupial mammals. It includes the kangaroos, Wallaby|wallabies, possums, koala, wombats, and many others. 

Extinct members include the giant Diprotodon family, and ''Thylacoleo'', the so-called "marsupial lion".
