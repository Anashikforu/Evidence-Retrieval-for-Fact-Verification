


'''Cetartiodactyla''' is the Taxonomy|taxonomical order (biology)|superorder which includes whales (and dolphins) and even-toed ungulates.  The name was created by merging the name for the two orders, Cetacea and Artiodactyla, into a single word. 

Cetartiodactyla is used because it is thought that whales evolution|evolved from the artiodactyls.  Because of this, their closest living land relative may be the hippopotamus.
