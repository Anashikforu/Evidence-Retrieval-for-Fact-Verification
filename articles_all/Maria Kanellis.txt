
'''Maria Louise Kanellis''' (born February 25, 1982 in Ottawa, Illinois) is an Americans|American female Professional wrestling|professional wrestler. She is best known for her time with WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment from 2004 to 2010. She joined WWE again in June 2017 and she will be with real-life husband, Mike Kanellis.
