
'''Unterallgäu''' is a rural district in Swabia (Government Region)|Swabia in southwest Bavaria, Germany. Neighbor districts are: Neu-Ulm (district), Günzburg (district), Augsburg (district), Ostallgäu and Oberallgäu and in Baden-Württemberg:  Ravensburg (district) and Biberach (district); West of the district is the Bavarian district-free city Memmingen. Mindelheim is the capital (city)|capital of the Unterallgäu.
