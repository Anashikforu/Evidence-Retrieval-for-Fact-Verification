In Germanic paganism, '''Baduhenna''' is a goddess of war. She is only referred to by Tacitus in the "Annals (Tacitus)|Annals," where he tells of a Sacred grove|grove in Frisia that was dedicated to her, near where 900 Roman Military|Roman prisoners were killed in 28 Common Era|CE.Simek (2007:26).
