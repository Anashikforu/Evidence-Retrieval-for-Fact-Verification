

'''Chelsea Victoria Clinton''' (born February 27, 1980) is the only child of Bill Clinton, former President of the United States, and his wife, former United States Secretary of State|Secretary of State, Hillary Clinton.

 Early life and education  
Clinton was born at Baptist Health Medical Center in Little Rock, Arkansas. She moved in to The White House when she was twelve years old when her father, Bill Clinton, became president.  She has helped her mother, Hillary Clinton, many times by campaigning for people to Election|elect her mother as the first woman President of the United States.

Clinton has a Bachelor of Arts in History. She also has a Master of Philosophy qualification. She went to university at Oxford University and Stanford University. 

Clinton is Vegetarianism|vegetarian. 

 Personal life 
On November 30, 2009, it was announced that Clinton was engaged to Marc Mezvinsky, an investment banker. They married on July 31, 2010. On November 14, 2011, Clinton announced joining as a "special correspondent" for NBC News. She will continue as a Doctor of Philosophy|PhD student.

On September 2014, Clinton gave birth to a daughter Charlotte. 

Her youngest son, Jasper, was born in New York on July 2019.
