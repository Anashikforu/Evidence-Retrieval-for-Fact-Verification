
The '''Vice Chancellor''' is the deputy of the Federal Chancellor of Germany|Chancellor. He acts for the Chancellor if he cannot act, or if there is no chancellor. The Vice Chancellor is also a federal minister, and in a coalition government is usually the leader of the minority party.
