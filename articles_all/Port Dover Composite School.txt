'''Port Dover Composite School''' was a public high school in Port Dover, Ontario|Port Dover, Ontario, Canada. The high school was open to both men and women. There were no school uniforms and it is free for Canada|Canadian citizens to attend from ages 14 to 21 years of age. In the past, the school hosted a dinosaur music party in french language|French as well as other special events.

This high school was officially closed on January 31, 2013 due to an increasing amount of elderly people in the area and a decreasing amount of children and young people.
