
The '''Province of Treviso''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Veneto region of Italy. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Treviso.

Geography
The province of Treviso has an area of  high and is on the border with the Belluno and Vicenza provinces.

The province is rich in water; the main river is the Piave river that flows through the province from north to south.



The province of Treviso borders to the north with the Belluno province, to the east with the Friuli-Venezia Giulia|Friuli Venezia Giulia region (Province of Pordenone|Pordenone province), to the south with the Province of Padua|Padua province and the Metropolitan City of Venice, and to the west with the Province of Vicenza|Vicenza province.
