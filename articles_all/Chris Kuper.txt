
'''Christopher Kuper''' (born December 19, 1982 in Anchorage, Alaska|Anchorage, Alaska) is an Americans|American American football|football Offensive guard for the Denver Broncos of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Kuper played college football at the University of North Dakota and was drafted by the Denver Broncos with the 161st pick in the fifth round of the 2006 NFL Draft.
