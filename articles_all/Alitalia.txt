

'''Alitalia''' was an Italy|Italian airline It was once the flag carrier and largest airline of Italy. It ended operations on October 14, 2021 and was replaced as Italy's flag carrier by ITA Airways, which began operations the next day.

 History 
The "first" Alitalia was founded on September 16, 1946. In June 2014, Etihad Airways bought 49% of Alitalia. On January 1, 2015, a "third" Alitalia began. This one is 49% owned by Etihad and 51% owned by Italian shareholders.
 Destinations 
Alitalia flew to 102 destinations in 41 country|countries. 28 of these cities are in Italy. Alitalia's main hub wasat Rome Fiumicino Airport.
