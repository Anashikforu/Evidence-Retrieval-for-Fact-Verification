

'''Yucca''' is a family (biology)|family of shrubs and trees, related to the agaves. There are between 40 and 50 different species|kinds of Yucca. All come from the heat|hot and :wikt:dry|dry places in North America|North and Central America, as well as the Caribbean. 

They have a very special way of pollination. There is an animal, the Yucca moth, which does the pollination. It also lays its Egg (biology)|eggs in the plant. The larvae will eat some of the seeds, but not all of them.

In many parts of the world, yuccas are grown as ornamental flowers.
