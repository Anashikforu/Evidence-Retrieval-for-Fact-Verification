
The '''Province of Terni''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Umbria region of Central Italy. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Terni.

History
The province of Terni was created in 1927 when the old ''Province of the Umbria'', created in 1860, was divided in two provinces, the province of Perugia|Perugia and Terni provinces.

Geography
The province of Terni has an area of , one-third of both the area and population of the Umbria region.

The province is bordered to the north with the province of Perugia, to the east, south and west with the Lazio (the province of Rieti|Rieti and province of Viterbo|Viterbo provinces) and to north-west with the Tuscany (province of Siena).



The province is in the southwest of the Umbria region, in the last part of the Nera (Italy)|Nera river valley near the confluence with the Velino river, and the eastern part of the valley of the Tiber River|Tiber river.
