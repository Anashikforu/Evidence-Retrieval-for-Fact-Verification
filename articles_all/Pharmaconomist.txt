'''Pharmaconomist''' means '''expert in pharmaceuticals''' (expert in medicine).

A pharmaconomist is a Denmark|Danish person who works in a pharmacy. Just like pharmacists, pharmaconomists have to be trained to work there, and have lots of knowledge about the drugs and treatments that they give out.
