
'''Super League Greece''' () is the top division Association football|football league in Greece. The current version was formed on 16 July 2006 and replaced the ''Alpha Ethniki''. The first championship was won by Aris Thessaloniki F.C.|Aris Thessaloniki. The club which has won the most championships is Olympiacos C.F.P.|Olympiacos with 44 titles, next is P.A.E. Panathinaikos|Panathinaikos with 20 titles and third is  A.E.K. Athens F.C.|AEK Athens with 11 titles. The league currently has 16 teams.
