

'''James William "Jim" Ross''' (born January 3, 1951) is a professional wrestling Sports commentator|commentator. He is also known by his nicknames '''Good Ol' J.R.''' or simply '''J.R.'''. Ross is a restaurateur, former professional wrestling commentator and former company executive. He is most well known for working for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) as a play-by-play commentator. He was born in Fort Bragg, California.

He was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2007 and is widely regarded as one of the greatest commentators in wrestling history.

In October 2021, Ross tweeted that he was diagnosed with skin cancer.
