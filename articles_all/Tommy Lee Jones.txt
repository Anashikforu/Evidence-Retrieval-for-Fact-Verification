
'''Thomas "Tommy" Lee Jones''' (born September 15, 1946) is an Americans|American actor and movie director. He won the Academy Award|Academy and the Golden Globe Award|Golden Globe awards in 1993 for his role in the movie ''The Fugitive (1993 movie)|The Fugitive''.

Early life
He was born in Texas in 1946. He was raised in Midland, Texas.
He finished university in 1969 and moved to New York City|New York to be an actor. His first role was in the Broadway theatre|Broadway play, ''A Patriot For Me.'' He had many small parts until he got the main role in the movie ''JFK''. He won the Academy Award for the best actor in a supporting role for the movie ''The Fugitive''.
