
'''Wellingborough''' is a town in Northamptonshire, England. In 2001 there were 72,519 people living in the town. The town is near the A45 road (Great Britain)|A45 road.

Wellingborough is an Anglo-Saxon town. It and was settled in the 6th century|sixth century by the Saxons. It was first known as ''Wendeling burh'' - "the stronghold of Waendel's people".
