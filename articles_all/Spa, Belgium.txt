
'''Spa''' is a municipality of Belgium. It is in the Wallonia|Walloon Region and Liège (province)|Province of Liège. It is in a valley in the Ardennes mountain chain. Spa is 35&nbsp;km (22 miles) southeast of Liège (city)|Liège, and 45&nbsp;km (28 miles) southwest of Aachen.

 History 

Spa is famous as a location of healing hot springs. It was a frequent watering-place since the 14th century. 

In 1918, the German Army established its main Headquarters in Spa. German delegates went to the French lines to meet Ferdinand Foch|Marshal Foch. This led to the Armistice with Germany (Compiègne)|Armistice which ended the First World War. 

The town is perhaps best known today as home to the Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps, the host the annual Formula One Belgian Grand Prix. It is also the location of mineral water producer Spa (mineral water)|Spa.

Spa hosted the finish of Stage 2 of the 2010 Tour de France on July 5.
