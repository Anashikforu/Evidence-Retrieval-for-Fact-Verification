'''Waldpolenz Solar Park''', is a photovoltaics|photovoltaic (PV) power plant, to the east of Leipzig in Germany. The power plant is a 40-megawatt solar cell|solar power system using new technology. It is in the Muldentalkreis district in the state of Saxony in eastern Germany. The investment cost for the Waldpolenz solar park amounts to some Euro 130 million.
