
The '''Province of Piacenza''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy. Its largest town and capital is the city of Piacenza.

Geography

The province of Piacenza has an area of . It is the most western of the nine provinces in the region of Emilia-Romagna in northwestern Italy. It is divided in two zones from north to south; the northern part is largely flat (the ''Pianura Padana'' or valley of the Po (river)|Po river) but the southern two thirds are hilly and gets up the Apennines mountains.

The province is bordered to the north by the region of Lombardy (Province of Cremona|Cremona, Province of Lodi|Lodi and Province of Pavia|Pavia provinces), to the east by the Province of Parma|Parma province, to the south by the region of Liguria (Metropolitan City of Genoa) and to the west by the region of Piedmont (Province of Alessandria|Alessandria province).
