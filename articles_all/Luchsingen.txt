
'''Luchsingen''' is a former municipality, in the municipality of Glarus Süd and Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Glarus (canton)|Glarus in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2004 the former municipalities of Hätzingen and Diesbach, Glarus|Diesbach were merged into the municipality of Luchsingen.

Oberblegisee is in the municipality.
