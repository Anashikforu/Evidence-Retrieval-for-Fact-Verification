Biography
Yokoyama was born in Nagasaki Prefecture on April 10, 1956. After graduating from Shimabara Technical High School, he joined Japan Soccer League Division 1 club Nippon Steel Yawata SC|Nippon Steel in 1975. However the club performance was not good, the club was relegated to Division 2 in 1982. He retired end of 1986/87 season. He played 105 games and scored 29 goals in Division 1.

On August 23, 1979, Yokoyama debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against North Korea national football team|North Korea. In 1980, he played in all matches included Football at the 1980 Summer Olympics|1980 Summer Olympics qualification and 1982 FIFA World Cup|1982 World Cup qualification. In 1983 and 1984, he also played at Football at the 1984 Summer Olympics|1984 Summer Olympics qualification. This qualification was his last game for Japan. He played 31 games and scored 10 goals for Japan until 1984.
