
The '''Region of Amazonas''' is a region of Peru. It is in the northwest part of the country. Its capital is the city of Chachapoyas, Peru|Chachapoyas.

Geography


The '''Amazonas Region''' has a lot of rainforests and mountain ranges. The rainforest zone covers 72.93% of the area, and it goes to the north, up to the border with Ecuador.
