

'''Discovery Channel''' (formerly '''The Discovery Channel''') is an Asian Satellite television|satellite and cable TV channel which provides Documentary film|documentary programming. Its documentaries focused mainly on popular science, technology, history and culture, similar to that of the National Geographic Channel (Asia)|National Geographic Channel and History (South East Asian TV channel)|History.

It is owned and operated by Discovery Communications#Discovery Networks International|Discovery Networks Asia, a division of Discovery Communications. Discovery Channel SE Asia now divided into two channel feeds.
