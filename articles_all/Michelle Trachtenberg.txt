

'''Michelle Christine Trachtenberg''' (born October 11, 1985) is an Americans|American television and movie Actor|actress. She is best known for her role as Buffy the Vampire Slayer#Characters|Dawn Summers from the television program|television series ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer''. Trachtenberg was born in New York City, New York. She is of Russian judaism|Jewish descent on her mother's side of the family.

 Career 
Trachtenberg has been acting since 1988 at the age of four. She has acted in several television shows and many movies.
