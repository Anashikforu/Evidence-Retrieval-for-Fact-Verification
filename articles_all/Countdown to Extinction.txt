

'''''Countdown to Extinction''''' is the fifth Album|studio album by American thrash metal band Megadeth. ''Countdown to Extinction'' was released on July 6, 1992. It was released through Capitol Records. The album debuted at #2 on the Billboard 200 and sold 128,000 copies in the first week.

The album was nominated for Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance|Best Metal Performance at the 1993 Grammy Awards.
