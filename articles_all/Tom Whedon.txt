'''Tom Whedon''' is an United States|American television Screenwriter|writer. He has written for many television programme|series, for example, ''Benson (television)|Benson'', ''Alice (television)|Alice'' and ''The Golden Girls''. Whedon also wrote for the children's series ''The Electric Company''. 

He is the father of Deadwood (TV series)|Deadwood script writer Zack Whedon. Whedon is also the father of writer/producer/film director|director Joss Whedon, the creator of ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer'', ''Angel (TV series)|Angel'', and ''Firefly (TV series)|Firefly''.
