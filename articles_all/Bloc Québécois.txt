
The '''Bloc Québécois''' ('''BQ''') is a federalism|federal political party based in Canada that mainly believes and advocates Quebec should secession|secede from Canada, also known as Quebec#Leaving Canada|Quebec sovereignty. The party is considered to be center-left. The leader of the party is currently Yves-François Blanchet.

 History 
The Bloc was always the party with the biggest number of seats in Quebec between 1993 and 2011. They were usually the second or third biggest party in the House of Commons of Canada|House of Commons. The party won only four seats in parliament after the 2011 Canadian federal election|2011 federal election. In the 2019 Canadian federal election|2019 federal election, the party won 32 seats in the House of Commons of Canada|House of Commons.
