'''Crawford Wilson''' (born 19 June 1990) is an USA|American actor and voice actor, from Memphis, Tennessee. He is best known for playing the lead role in the Nintendo GameCube Console role-playing game|RPG ''Baten Kaitos Origins'', List of Baten Kaitos Origins characters|Sagi. He also voiced List of Avatar: The Last Airbender major secondary characters|Jet in the animated series ''Avatar: The Last Airbender.''

He provided a voice in the ''Family Guy'' episode "Prick Up Your Ears (Family Guy)|Prick up your ears."
He has been in commercials and print ads around the United States. He also did the voice of Daisukenojō Bitō in the Nintendo DS game ''The World Ends with You'', by Square Enix. Wilson also voiced Robin (comics)|Robin in "The Color of Revenge!" on ''Batman: The Brave and the Bold''.

He played Dwayne Dill in the 2009 Disney Channel original movie, ''Hatching Pete''.
He played Jake James in the movie ''The Secrets Of The Mountain''.
