'''Richard Morgan Fliehr''' (born February 25, 1949, in Memphis, Tennessee) better known by his ring name, '''Ric Flair''', is a semi-retired Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler that is currently signed to Total Nonstop Action Wrestling|WWE.
He wrestled for 36 years. His last WWE match was against Shawn Michaels at WrestleMania XXIV. He is a two time WWE Hall of Famer.

His nickname is ''"The Nature Boy" Ric Flair''.
