


'''Craig William Levein''' (born 22 October 1964 in Dunfermline) is a Scotland|Scottish former professional football (soccer)|footballer and current Coach (sport)|football manager. Levein currently manages Scottish Premier League club Heart of Midlothian F.C.|Hearts where he is also the Director of Football. During his playing career he played for Cowdenbeath F.C., Heart of Midlothian F.C.|Heart of Midlothian and won 16 cap (football)|caps for the Scotland|Scotland national team.
