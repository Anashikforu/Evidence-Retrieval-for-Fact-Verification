'''Tseh''' ('''Ц''', '''ц''') is the twenty-first letter in the Cyrillic alphabet. It sounds like ts. Its wikt:lowercase|lowercase wikt:italic|italic form is ''ц''.

It stands for the Roman alphabet|Roman letter C.
