

'''''Dirty Harry''''' is a 1971 United States|American crime thriller movie. It was movie producer|produced and movie director|directed by Don Siegel. It was the first in the Dirty Harry series. Clint Eastwood plays the main role. Eastwood acts as a San Francisco Police Department Inspector "Dirty" Harry Callaghan, trying to catch a serial killer. The character is based on real life cop Dave Toschi hunting for the Zodiac Killer.
Dirty Harry was followed by four sequels: ''Magnum Force'' in 1973, ''The Enforcer (1976 movie)|The Enforcer'' in 1976, ''Sudden Impact'' in 1983 (directed by Eastwood), and ''The Dead Pool'' in 1988.
