
'''Interstate H-2''' (abbreviated '''H-2''') is an intrastate interstate highway located on the island of Oahu|O‘ahu in Hawaii|Hawai‘i, USA.  H-2 is also known as the '''Veterans Memorial Freeway'''. Despite the number, this is a north–south interstate–the 'H'-series (for Hawaii) numbering reflects the order in which routes were given money and built. Its southern terminus is at Interstate H-1 in Pearl City, Hawaii|Pearl City, and its northern terminus is at Wilikina Drive (Hawaii Route 99|Route 99) in Wahiawa, Hawaii|Wahiawā near Schofield Barracks, Hawaii|Schofield Barracks and Wheeler Army Airfield. The route is  long.
