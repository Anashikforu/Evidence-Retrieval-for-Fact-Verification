

'''Denali''', also formerly known as '''Mount McKinley''', is the highest mountain peak in both the United States and North America. It is exactly 20,310 Foot (unit of length)|feet (6,190.5 metres|m) high.
 Location 
Denali is in the middle of the Alaska Range. It is about 130 miles (209 km) north-northwest of Anchorage, Alaska|Anchorage and 155 miles (249&nbsp;km) southwest of Fairbanks, Alaska|Fairbanks. The summit is about 35 miles (56&nbsp;km) from the nearest major road, the George Parks Highway.
