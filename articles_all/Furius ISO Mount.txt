


'''Furius ISO Mount''' is a software|program to mount (computer)|mount and Burn (computer)|burn iso Computer file|files.

 Features 
Ability to mount .iso|ISO, IMG, BIN, MDF and .nrg|NRG image files
Automatically creates a mount point in the users home directory.
Automatically saves the history of the last 10 images mounted.
Ability to burn ISO and IMG Files to optical disks
Ability to generate MD5 and SHA1 checksums
Automatically retrieves any previously unmounted images.
Automatically generates a log file of all commands needed to mount and unmount images manually.
