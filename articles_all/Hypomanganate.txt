'''Hypomanganate''' is an ion. Its chemical formula is MnO43-. It is bright blue. It disproportionation|disproportionates into manganates and manganese(IV) oxide easily. This can be stopped by adding a lot of a base (chemistry)|base. They are both reducing agents and oxidizing agents. They are made by reduction (chemistry)|reducing manganates with sulfites and hydrogen peroxide.
