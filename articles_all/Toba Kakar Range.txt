The '''Toba Kakar Mountains''' are a Southern offshoot of the Himalayas in the Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan region of Pakistan. The historical route through the mountains is known as the Bolan Pass, and though the mountains are sometimes inaccurately referred to as the "Brahui Mountains", Brahui language|Brahui is the term for an Ethnic groups of Pakistan|ethnic group that lives in the region, and their language.
