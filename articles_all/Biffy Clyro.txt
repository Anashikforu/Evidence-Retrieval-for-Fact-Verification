

'''Biffy Clyro''', often called '''Biffy''' by fans, are a Scotland|Scottish Rock music|rock band. The band are signed to Roadrunner Records and have released four albums and an EP. Their fourth album ''Puzzle'' got them much mainstream and popular attention.
