

'''''Guitar Hero: Aerosmith''''' is a rhythm game made by Neversoft with other help from Activision. It is part of the Guitar Hero (series)|''Guitar Hero'' franchise. The game was released in North America on June 29 2008, and two days earlier in Europe.

Some of the songs featured in the game include "Rocks (album)|Combination",Joe Perry, Guitar World interview (April 2008) Page 68 "Kings and Queens (Aerosmith song)|Kings and Queens",Game Informer - Issue 180 (April 2008) Page 53-54 "Train Kept A Rollin"
