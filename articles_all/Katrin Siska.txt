

'''Katrin Siska''' (born December 10, 1983 in Tallinn, Estonia) is an Estonians|Estonian singer and member of the Estonian girlpower pop music|pop-rock music|rock-group Vanilla Ninja. Siska was born to a Russians|Russian mother Leena and an Estonians|Estonian father Toomas. She has a younger sister Triin-Ketlin. She was a member of a choir while in school and started playing the piano when she was 7 years old.
