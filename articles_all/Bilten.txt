

'''Bilten''' was a municipality in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Glarus (canton)|Glarus in Switzerland. On 1 January 2011 the municipalities of Bilten, Filzbach, Mollis, Mühlehorn, Näfels, Niederurnen, Oberurnen and Obstalden joined together to become the new municipality of Glarus Nord.
