

'''''Mickey's House of Villains''''' is a Filpino-American-Canadian direct-to-video animation|animated movie created by The Walt Disney Company|Disney. It is the movie adaptation of the Disney Channel animated television series ''Disney's House of Mouse'', starring Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck, Minnie Mouse, Goofy, Daisy Duck, and characters and villains that have appeared in past Disney productions. It was released on both VHS and DVD by Walt Disney Home Video on September 3, 2002.
