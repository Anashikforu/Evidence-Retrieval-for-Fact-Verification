

'''Tan Son Nhat International Airport''' (. The airport can handle up to 17 million passengers per year in the two airport terminal|terminals (Domestic Airport|Domestic, International airport|International). The new international Terminal station|terminal was opened on 14th August 2007.

 History 
This airport was built by the French colonists in 1930s. During Vietnam War, the United States made the airport larger to serve the war. After the Fall of Saigon in April 1975, this airport was made commercial.
