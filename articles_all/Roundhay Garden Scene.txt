

'''''Roundhay Garden Scene''''' is a short movie made on October 14, 1888 in the garden of ''Oakwood Grange'', Roundhay, Leeds, West Yorkshire, England. It was made by a French people|French man named Louis Le Prince. It was silent. The movie had Adolphe Le Prince, Sarah Whitley, Joseph Whitley, and Harriet Hartley walking in the garden of Oakwood Grange. It is believed to be the oldest surviving movie by many people. It is 1.66 seconds long. The digitalized version lasts 2.11 seconds.
