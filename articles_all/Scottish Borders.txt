'''Scottish Borders''' is a council area (region) in south Scotland. Its name is because it is on the border with England.

The name is usually shortened to    " ''the Borders".''

It is made up of the counties of :-

 Berwickshire
 Peeblesshire
 Selkirkshire

The main town of the region is Newtown St. Boswells|Newtown St Boswells.
