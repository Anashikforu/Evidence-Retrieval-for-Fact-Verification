
'''Xanten''' (Latin: ''Castra Vetera'' or ''Xantum'') () is a historic town in the Wesel Rural District|Wesel district of the North Rhine-Westphalia states of Germany|state of Germany. Xanten is known for the Archaeological Park as well as its mediaeval picturesque city centre with Xanten Cathedral and visited by approximately 800,000 tourists a year.

Xanten is the only German town whose name begins with "X".
