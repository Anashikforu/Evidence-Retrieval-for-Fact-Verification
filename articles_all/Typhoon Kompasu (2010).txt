
'''Typhoon Kompasu''' was noticed on August&nbsp;28, the United States Air Force base in Okinawa, Japan, began preparing for what was then Tropical Storm 08W. Tropical cyclone warnings and watches|Tropical Cyclone Condition of Readiness (TCCOR) level three was issued the following morning.

Throughout Okinawa, Kompasu brought strong winds and heavy rains, leaving an estimated 32,000 residences without power. The highest rainfall total was measured in Motobu, Okinawa|Motobu at  Sion Pine Tree was the last of its kind on the island and was once the symbol of the northern Okinawa Region.
