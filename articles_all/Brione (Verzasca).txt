

'''Brione''' is a former municipality of the district of Locarno (district)|Locarno in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

On 17 October 2020 the former municipalities of Brione (Verzasca), Corippo, Frasco, Sonogno and Vogorno merged to form the new municipality of Verzasca, Switzerland|Verzasca.
