
'''Oloron-Sainte-Marie''' is a city and Communes of France|commune in southwestern France in the Pyrénées-Atlantiques Departments of France|department, of which it is a Prefectures in France|sub-prefecture.

In 1858, the ''communes'' of Saint-Marie-Legugnon and Oloron were combined to make the new ''commune'' of Oloron-Sainte-Marie.
