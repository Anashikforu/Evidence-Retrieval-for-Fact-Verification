

'''Martin Adams''' (born 1956) is an England|English professional darts player.  His nickname is ''Wolfie'', referring to his hairy face and the fact that he likes wolf|wolves.

Adams is a top-ranked darts player. He is a three-time BDO World Darts Championship |BDO World Champion and three-time Winmau World Masters|World Masters champion. He was on the Board of directors|board of the British Darts Organisation for a short time.
