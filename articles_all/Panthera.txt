'''''Panthera''''' is a genus of the family (biology)|family Felidae.
It has five living species, which are the biggest cats in the Felidae.  The tiger is the largest ''Panthera'' species, and the largest of all cats. The leopard is the smallest ''Panthera'' species.Turner A. 1997. ''The big cats and their fossil relatives''. Columbia University Press.
