
'''''The Good, the Bad and the Ugly''''' (1966) is a Spaghetti Western movie movie director|directed by Sergio Leone and starring Clint Eastwood. It is the third and final movie in the Dollars trilogy and was released in the United States in 1967. The first movie in the trilogy is ''A Fistful of Dollars''; the second is ''For a Few Dollars More''.

This movie and ''Once Upon a Time in the West'' are Films considered to be the greatest ever|considered to be the greatest western movies ever.
