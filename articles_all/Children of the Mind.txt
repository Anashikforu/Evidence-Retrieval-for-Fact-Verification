


'''''Children of the Mind''''' (1996) is the fourth, and currently the last, book of Orson Scott Card's popular Ender's Game series|''Ender's Game'' series, a series of science fiction novels that focus on the character Ender Wiggin. Although Card has written some additional novels that take place with in the Ender Saga, they take place in the middle of the storyline and therefore are not sequals to ''Children of The Mind''.
