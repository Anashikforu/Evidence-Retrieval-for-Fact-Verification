

'''Sami Helenius''' (born January 22, 1974 in Helsinki, Finland) was a professional ice hockey defenceman who played for the Pelicans (ice hockey)|Pelicans of the SM-liiga and has played in the National Hockey League|NHL for the Chicago Blackhawks, Dallas Stars, Calgary Flames, Tampa Bay Lightning and the Colorado Avalanche. He was the team captain of the SM-liiga team Jokerit.
