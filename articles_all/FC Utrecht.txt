
'''FC Utrecht''' is a Association football|football club which plays in the Netherlands.

It was a merger of three clubs from the city of Utrecht (city)|Utrecht: '''Door Oefening Sterk''', '''USV Elinkwijk''' and '''Velox''' in 1970.
