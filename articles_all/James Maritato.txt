

'''James Maritato''' (born March 12, 1972 in Sicily, Italy) is an Italy|Italian-born United States|American professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) and Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW). Nunzio is currently wrestling with the National Wrestling Alliance.

Championships
Nunzio has won many belts with different wrestling promotions:

'''Chaotic Wrestling'''
CW Tag Team Championship (one time) with Luis Ortiz

'''East Coast Pro Wrestling'''
ECPW Hall of Fame in 2009

'''Extreme Championship Wrestling'''
ECW World Tag Team Championship (two times) with Tony Mamaluke and Tracy Smothers

'''Jersey All Pro Wrestling'''
JAPW Tag Team Championship (one time) with Tracy Smothers

'''USA Pro Wrestling'''
USA Pro Tag Team Championship (two times) with Rahul Kay and Kid Kruel
USA Pro United States Championship (one time)

'''World Wrestling Entertainment'''
WWE Cruiserweight Championship (two times)
