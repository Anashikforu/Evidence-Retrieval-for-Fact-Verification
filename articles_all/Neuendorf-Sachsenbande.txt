
'''Neuendorf-Sachsenbande''' is a municipality in Wilstermarsch, in the district of Steinburg Rural District|Steinburg, in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. The municipality has an area of below sea level.

 History 

The town was made on April 15, 2003, when the municipalities of ''Neuendorf bei Wilster'', ''Sachsenbande'', and ''Bredensee'' were all joined together.
