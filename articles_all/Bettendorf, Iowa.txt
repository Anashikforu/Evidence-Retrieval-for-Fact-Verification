'''Bettendorf''' is a city in Iowa in the United States.  It is in Scott County, Iowa|Scott County. Bettendorf is one of the Quad Cities, along with neighboring Davenport, Iowa|Davenport and the Illinois cities of Moline, Illinois|Moline, East Moline, Illinois|East Moline and Rock Island, Illinois|Rock Island. In 2011, Bettendorf was named America's 95th Best Town by CNNMoney.
