

'''Morena Baccarin''' (born June 2, 1979) is a Brazilian Actor|actress. She is best known for her role as Inara Serra in the science fiction|sci-fi television programme|television series ''Firefly (TV series)|Firefly'' and the movie ''Serenity (film)|Serenity''. Morena was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Her father is of Italy|Italian descent. Her mother is Brazilian theatre|stage and television actress Vera Setta.

When she was 10, Baccarin moved with her family to Greenwich Village, New York, in the United States. She later attended the Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts (the High School in the movie ''Fame (film)|Fame'') before she studied theater at Juilliard School|Juilliard.

 Interviews 
 When asked in an interview "if she's up for the role of ''Wonder Woman (film)|Wonder Woman'', " Baccarin replied: "I’m not going to say no to a superhero but it’s not up to me."
