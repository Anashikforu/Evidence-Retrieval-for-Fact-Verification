

'''Ludwigsstadt''' is a Town#Germany|town in Kronach (district)|Kronach, in Upper Franconia, Bavaria, Germany.

Geography
It is in the valley of the Loquitz River, a tributary of the Saale, in the Thuringian-Franconian Highlands of the Thuringian Slate Mountains and the Franconian Forest mountain ranges. Being  down the Loquitz. Ludwigsstadt is the only municipality of the State of Bavaria north of the Rennsteig ridge.
