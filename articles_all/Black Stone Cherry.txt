
'''Black Stone Cherry''' are a Rock music|rock band from Kentucky, USA. They are signed to Roadrunner Records and have released two albums, called ''Black Stone Cherry'' and ''Folklore and Superstition''.  The band has had several songs reach the top 30 of Billboard charts|Billboard's Top Mainstream Rock Songs, including "Lonely Train", which reached #14 on that chart in 2006.

Members of the band
Chris Robertson (lead vocals, guitar)
Ben Wells (guitar, backing vocals) 
Jon Lawhon (bass guitar, backing vocals)
John Fred Young (drums, backing vocals)
