


The '''Republican People's Party''' (, '''CHP''') is the oldest political party in Turkey. Their ideologies are social democracy and Mustafa Kemal Atatürk|Kemalism.

They are currently the second largest party in the Grand National Assembly of Turkey|Turkish Parliament, with 132 out of 550 seats and they form the Official Opposition|Main Opposition of Turkey. Their founder is Mustafa Kemal Atatürk and their current leader is Kemal Kılıçdaroğlu.

On September 7, 1919, Turkish nationalists established a resistance organisation against the invasion of Turkey. On September 9, 1923, the organisation officially declared itself as a political party and it was the Republican People's Party.
