The '''Intercity-Express''' (abbreviated: '''ICE'''; sometimes stylized as ''InterCityExpress''), is a category of high speed Railway|trains, which are operated by DB Fernverkehr since 1991. It is the highest service and fare category (Class A) of rail in Germany and the flagship train of the German state railway, Deutsche Bahn. There are currently 259 trainsets in use.

The trains have an operating speed of up to 300 km/h. They are one of fastest trains in the world. Some of the high-speed lines in Germany are:
