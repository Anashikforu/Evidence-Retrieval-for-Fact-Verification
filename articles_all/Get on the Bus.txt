
"'''Get on the Bus'''" was the third single (music)|single from girl group Destiny's Child.  The song featured rapper/producer Timbaland and is lifted from the original motion picture soundtrack of ''Why Do Fools Fall in Love (movie)|Why Do Fools Fall in Love'' (1998).  It was the second single to be lifted from the soundtrack following Melanie B's debut single, "I Want You Back (Melanie B song)|I Want You Back".  Produced by Timbaland, it peaked at number twelve on the UK and Netherlands charts, and at number sixty on the German chart.  The single was not commercially released in the U.S. and other major markets, It did receive some minor US Urban radio airplay, peaking at #58 on the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Airplay chart. The music video was directed by Earle Sebastian.
