

The '''Cia-Cia language''' (Hangul: 바하사 찌아찌아, Latin alphabet|Roman: Bahasa Ciacia), also known as '''South Buton(ese)''', is an Austronesian language spoken principally around the town of Bau-Bau on the southern tip of Buton Island off the southeast coast of Sulawesi in Indonesia.
