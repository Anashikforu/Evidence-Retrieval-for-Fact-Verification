'''Funk metal''' is a subgenre of funk rock that fuses elements of Heavy metal music|heavy metal and funk. Allmusic has claimed that, "Funk metal evolved in the mid-'80s when Alternative rock|alternative bands like the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Faith No More, Infectious Grooves, Mordred (band)|Mordred and Fishbone began playing the hybrid with a stronger funk underpinning than metal."Faith No More have also been described as funk metal that dabbled in rap-metal.
Rage Against the Machine's mix of funk and metal not only included rap, but also elements of punk rock. Primus (band)|Primus, a band that has never had a clear genre, have been called things such as "thrash metal-funk meets Don Knotts, Jr.Living Colour have been cited by ''Rolling Stone'' as "Black-funk-metal pioneers."

Certain bands not from an alternative background, such as Bang Tango and Extreme (band)|Extreme, have also frequently incorporated funk into their musical style.
