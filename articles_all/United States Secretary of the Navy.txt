The '''United States Secretary of the Navy''' ('''SECNAV''') is the civilian head of the United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy. The position was a member of the President of the United States|President's United States Cabinet|Cabinet until 1947, when the Navy, Army, and newly created Air Force were placed in the United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense and the Secretary of the Navy was placed under the United States Secretary of Defense|Secretary of Defense.
