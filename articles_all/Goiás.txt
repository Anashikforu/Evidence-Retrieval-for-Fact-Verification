
'''Goiás''' is a States of Brazil|state of Brazil. It is in the central part of the country. It shares borders with Tocantins (state)|Tocantins, Bahia, Minas Gerais, the Brazilian Federal District|Federal District, Mato Grosso do Sul and Mato Grosso. The capital (city)|capital and largest city in the state is Goiânia. With an area of 341,289 Square kilometre|km², Goiás is about the size of the Republic of the Congo; smaller than Germany and larger than Finland.
