


The '''United States Grand Prix West''' was a Formula One Auto racing|motor race held at Race track#Street circuit|street circuit in Long Beach, California|Long Beach, California. It was a Formula 5000 race in 1975, and a F1 world championship event from 1976 to 1983. After 1983 the event became the Long Beach Grand Prix of the CART series.
