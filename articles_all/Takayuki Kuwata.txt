Biography
Kuwata was born in Hiroshima on June 26, 1941. After graduating from Waseda University, he joined his local club Sanfrecce Hiroshima|Toyo Industries in 1965. The club won Japan Soccer League champions for 4 years in a row (1965-1968). The club also won 1965, 1967 and 1969 Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1969. He played 62 games and scored 34 goals in the league. He was selected Best Eleven in 1966 and 1967.

On May 28, 1961, when Kuwata was a Waseda University student, he debuted and scored a goal for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Malaysia national football team|Malaya. In 1962, he also played and scored a goal at Football at the 1962 Asian Games|1962 Asian Games. He played 5 games and scored 2 goals for Japan until 1962.
