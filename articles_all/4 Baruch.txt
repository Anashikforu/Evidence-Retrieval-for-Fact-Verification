'''''4 Baruch''''', also called ''Paraleipomena of Jeremiah'' (''Paralipomena Jeremiae''), contains the Rest of the Words of Baruch and the Epistle of Jeremy. 

This religion|religious text is said to be a part of the Old Testament by the Ethiopian Orthodox Church. Other Christian denominations do not include in the Bible.
