
The '''20th century''' began on January 1, 1901 and ended on December 31, 2000. It was the century that saw two world wars, the Soviet Union rise and fall, the coming of the telephone, sound recording, film and television, aeroplane|airplanes, atomic bomb|atomic weapons, genetics and DNA, computers and electronics in general. Science and industrialisation|industrialization spread; medicine became more scientific.  The human population increased more during this century than any previous one.
