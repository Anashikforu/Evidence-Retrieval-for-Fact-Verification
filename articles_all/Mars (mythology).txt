

'''Mars''' is the god of war in Roman mythology. Mars is the son of Juno (mythology)|Juno and Jupiter. Mars is the father of Romulus and Remus. The planet Mars (planet)|Mars and the month March are named after him. He was the god of war, murder, and bloodshed. His Greek name is Ares. He tried many times to start wars between the gods.
