

'''Engelbert Humperdinck''' (1 September 1854 – 27 September 1921) was a Germany|German composer. Today, he is seen as part of the Romanticism|Romantic movement. Most people remember him for his opera, ''Hänsel und Gretel (opera)|Hänsel und Gretel'' which was first performed in 1893.

British singer, Engelbert Humperdinck (singer)|Arnold George Dorsey (born 1936), uses the name "Engelbert Humperdinck" as his stage name. He wanted the name to make him seem more cultured and refined.
