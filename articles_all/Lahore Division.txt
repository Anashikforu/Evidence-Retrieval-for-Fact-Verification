'''Lahore Division''' () was a former Divisions of Pakistan|division of the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province of Pakistan. In 2000 the government of Pakistan ended divisions.

History
Lahore Division was also an administrative division of Punjab (British India)|Punjab Province of British India. It extended along the right bank of the Sutlej River from the Himalaya to Multan, and was made up of the six districts of Sialkot District|Sialkot, Gujranwala District|Gujranwala, Montgomery District|Montgomery, Lahore District|Lahore, Amritsar District|Amritsar, and Gurdaspur District|Gurdaspur. With the Partition of India|Partition of British India into the independent states of India and Pakistan in 1947, Lahore Division was divided among the two countries.
