'''Montsûrs''' is a Communes of France|commune of 2,020 people (1999). It is found in the region Pays de la Loire in the Mayenne department in the northwest of France.  On 1 January 2017, it was merged with Saint-Céneré and formed the short-lived commune Montsûrs-Saint-Céneré.
