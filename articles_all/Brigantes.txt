
The '''Brigantes''' were a British Celtic tribe (people), that used to live in the North of Roman Britain; between the rivers Tyne and Humber. They occupied what was later Northumbria.David Hughes, ''The British Chronicles'', Volume 1 (Westminster, MD: Heritage Books, 2007), p. 78

Nearby tribes
The tribes that used to live next to the Brigantes were the Carvetti, the Parisii, the Cornovii (Midlands)|Cornovii, and the Corieltauvi.

Related tribes
There was a Brigantes tribe who used to live in the South of Ireland as well. They may both be Kinship|related to the ''Brigantii'' of the eastern Alps.
