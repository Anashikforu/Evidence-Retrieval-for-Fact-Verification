
'''David Greenwalt''' (born October 16, 1946) is an Americans|American screenwriter. He was born in Los Angeles, California. He has worked as writer, television director|director and Television producer|producer on many television programme|television series.

Greenwalt is best known as a writer on ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer'' and co-creator of its spinoff, ''Angel (TV series)|Angel''. He is also co-creator of the short lived television series ''Profit (TV series)|Profit''.

On June 1, 2007, The Hollywood Reporter announced that Greenwalt would serve as executive producer and showrunner on ''Moonlight (TV series)|Moonlight'', a new series that will debut on CBS in the fall of 2007. He later had to leave the show before it was first aired for health reasons. Executive producer Joel Silver said that his name would remain: "He worked really hard on the arc of the series. He really helped us focus and get started".
