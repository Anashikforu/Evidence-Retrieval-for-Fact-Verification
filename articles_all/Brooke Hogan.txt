'''Brooke Hogan''' (born May 5, 1988 in Tampa, Florida) is an Americans|American singer, actress and model (person)|model who currently has her own show on VH1 and MuchMore called ''Brooke Knows Best'' it is a spin-off of the show her parents were on called ''Hogan Knows Best''. Her father is semi-retired professional wrestler Hulk Hogan. She released her debut album in 2006 entitled ''Undiscovered''.
