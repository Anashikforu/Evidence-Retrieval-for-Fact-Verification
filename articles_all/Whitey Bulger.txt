

'''James Joseph "Whitey" Bulger, Jr.''' (September 3, 1929 &ndash; October 30, 2018) was an Americans|American former Mobster|mob boss that worked out of South Boston, Massachusetts. He was on the ten top most wanted fugitives in the United States from 1999 until 2011. His story was on ''America's Most Wanted'' on Fox Broadcasting Company|FOX. He was captured by police on 22 June 2011.
On October 30, 2018, Bulger was transferred to the United States Penitentiary, Hazelton in West Virginia. Later that day, he was found dead in the prison, aged 89.
