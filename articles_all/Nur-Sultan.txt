'''Nur-Sultan''', previously known as Akmolinsk (Russian: Акмолинск, romanized: Akmolinsk), Tselinograd, and Astana  in 2019.

Nur-Sultan has an estimated population of 1,136,000.
On 23 March 2019, a unanimous vote in Kazakhstan's parliament agreed to the new name. The city was renamed Nur-sultan, after former president Nursultan Nazarbayev.
