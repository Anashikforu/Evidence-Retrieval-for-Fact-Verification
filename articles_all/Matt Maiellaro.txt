

'''Matt Maiellaro''' is the co-creator and writer of the cult television Cartoon series|animated Adult Swim shows ''Aqua Teen Hunger Force'' and ''Perfect Hair Forever.'' He is also the creator of ''12 Oz. Mouse''. Maiellaro was a producer and writer for ''Space Ghost Coast to Coast'' since the show started in 1994.
