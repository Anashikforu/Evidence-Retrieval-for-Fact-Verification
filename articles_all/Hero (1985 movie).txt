

'''''Hero''''' is 1985 a Pakistani Urdu language|Urdu movie. It stars Waheed Murad, Babra Sharif, Mumtaz, Aslam Pervaiz, Saqi, Adil Murad and Nadeem. The movie was producer|produced by Murad and directed by his friend Iqbal Yousuf.
