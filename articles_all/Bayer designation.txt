The '''Bayer designation''' for stars uses :en:Greek_alphabet|Greek letters to assign names to stars. For example:

α Canis Major(constellation)|Canis Majoris
θ Orion (constellation)|Orionis
ζ Reticulum|Reticuli

It usually starts with the letter alpha (α) assigned to the brightest star in the constellation, then going down the :en:Greek_alphabet|Greek alphabet to assign letters for other stars (beta, gamma, delta, etc.) in order of decreasing brightness.
