'''Sapphire''' is a deep blue or bright blue color that is a representation of the color of a sapphire. Sapphire Gemstone|gems are most commonly found in a range of blue shades.  Other names for variations of the color ''sapphire'' are '''blue sapphire''' or '''sapphire blue''', shown below.

The first written use of ''sapphire'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1430.Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 204; Color Sample of Sapphire: Page 97 Plate 37 Color Sample L8

Meaning of sapphire
 In the Old Testament of the Bible, in the Book of Ezekiel, it is stated that God sits upon a sapphire throne in Heaven.Book of Ezekiel|Ezekiel 1:26 It is possible that this may be the source of the importance of the color blue in Judaism.
