
'''Exclusive disjunction''' (also called '''exclusive or''', '''XOR''') is a logic operation on two values. It is often represented by the symbol \veebar (or \oplus).

The best way to remember a XOR operation is: "One or the other, but not both".

Because of that, this is wikt:difference|different from inclusive disjunction.
