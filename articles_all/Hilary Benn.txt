

'''Hilary James Wedgwood Benn''' (born 26 November 1953) is an English people|English politician. He was born in Hammersmith, west London. Since October 2010, he has been the Shadow Leader of the House of Commons. He is the Member of Parliament for the Leeds Central (UK Parliament constituency)|Leeds Central constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in 1999 in a by-election. He is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party. He is the middle son of Tony Benn (1925-2014) and Caroline Benn (1926-2000).
