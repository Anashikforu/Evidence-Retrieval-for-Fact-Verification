

'''Thiamine''' (or thiamin) is '''vitamin B1'''. It is a water-soluble vitamin of the B vitamin complex|B complex. Its phosphate derivatives take part in many cellular processes. Thiamine pyrophosphate (TPP) is a coenzyme in the catabolism of sugars and amino acids. 

also it can be found in Food and Manufactured Dietary supplement|Dietary supplements also in Medication|Medications Foods that provide Thiamine include Whole grain|whole grains, Legume 

Lack of thiamine causes beriberi.
