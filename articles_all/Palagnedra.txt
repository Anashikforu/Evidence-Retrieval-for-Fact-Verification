
'''Palagnedra''' was a municipality of the district Locarno (district)|Locarno in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. Lago di Palagnedra is found below the village, on the Melezza river. On 25 October 2009 the former municipalities of Borgnone, Intragna, Switzerland|Intragna and Palagnedra merged into the municipality of Centovalli.
