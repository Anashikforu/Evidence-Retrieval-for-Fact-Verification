
'''Glay''' is a Japanese rock and roll|rock band. There are 4 men in the band. The member's names are Takuro (guitar & piano), Teru (vocals), Hisashi (guitar), and Jiro (bass guitar|bass). They are one of the most popular rock bands in Japan. They are from Hakodate in Hokkaido.  "HOWEVER" is their most famous song. This song made them famous. Many of their compact disc|CDs and albums have sold millions of copies .

Since 2007, Teru has been in Advertising|ads shown on television and in movie theater|theaters to raise awareness about HIV/AIDS.
