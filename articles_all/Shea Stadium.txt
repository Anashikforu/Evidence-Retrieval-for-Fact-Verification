
'''William A. Shea Municipal Stadium''', or more commonly '''Shea Stadium''' (often referred to in short as '''Shea'''), was a Major League Baseball|major league ballpark that was the home for the New York Mets 1964 to 2008 when the Mets left for their new home Citi Field. Shea was also the home of the New York Yankees while Yankee Stadium (1923)|Yankee Stadium was being wikt:renovate|renovated for the 1974 and 1975 seasons. It was demolished between October 14, 2008 and February 18, 2009.
