


'''3 World Trade Center''' (also known as '''175 Greenwich Street''') is the name for a new skyscraper, as part of the World Trade Center reconstruction in New York City. The new Three World Trade Center that opened in June 2018 is located at 175 Greenwich Street's pinnacle height. It is taller than the Empire State Building's roof and it is the fourth tallest building in New York City as measured to its pinnacle.
