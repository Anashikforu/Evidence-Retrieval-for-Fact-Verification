

'''Franz Joseph I''' () (August 18, 1830 – November 21, 1916) of the Habsburg|Habsburg Dynasty was Emperor of Austrian Empire|Austria, King of Hungary, King of Bohemia from 1848 until 1916. His brother Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria would have been his successor but he was assassinated. Charles I of Austria became emperor.

Franz Joseph's 68-year reign is the third-longest in the recorded history of Europe (after those of Louis XIV of France and Johannes II, Prince of Liechtenstein|Ioannes II, Prince of Liechtenstein).
