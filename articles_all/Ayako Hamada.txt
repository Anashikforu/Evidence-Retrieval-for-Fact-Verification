
'''Ayako Valentina Hamada Villareal''' (born February 14, 1981 in Mexico City, Mexico) is a Japanese-Mexico|Mexican professional wrestler currently wrestling on the independent circuit. She is probably best known for wrestling for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling|TNA Wrestling where she won the TNA Knockout Tag Team Championship twice once with Kia Stevens|Awesome Kong and once with Shantelle Malawski|Taylor Wilde.
