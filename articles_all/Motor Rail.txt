'''Motor Rail''' was a British locomotive-building company, based in Bedford. Formed in 1911 as The Motor Rail & Tramcar Co Ltd, they built Petrol engine|petrol and Diesel engine|diesel engined locomotives, mainly narrow gauge. During World War I over 900 locos were supplied for use on temporary War Department Light Railways|military supply railways. The trade name '''Simplex''' was used from the early days; in 1972 the company was renamed Simplex Mechanical Handling Ltd.
