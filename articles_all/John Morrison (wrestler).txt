
'''John Randall Hennigan''' (born October 3, 1979) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is currently working on the independent circuit. He is best known for working for World Wrestling Entertainment under the ring names '''John Morrison''' and '''Johnny Nitro'''.

 Personal life 
Hennigan grew up in Los Angeles, California with his two younger sisters, Kelly and Elise. Hennigan once dated wrestler Melina Perez. The fictional character|characters they play on television started in a relationship.
