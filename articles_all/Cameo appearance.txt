A '''cameo role''' or '''cameo appearance''' (often shortened to just '''cameo''') is a brief appearance of a known person in a work of the performing arts, such as Play (theatre)|plays, movies, video games and television. Such a role does not have to be an actor: short appearances by movie directors, politicians, sportsperson|athletes, and other Celebrity|celebrities are common. 

Some examples would be Alfred Hitchcock briefly appearing in some of his movies or Stan Lee always briefly appearing in Marvel Entertainment|Marvel-related movies.
