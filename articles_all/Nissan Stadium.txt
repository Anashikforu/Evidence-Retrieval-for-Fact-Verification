The '''Nissan Stadium''' (previously called the '''International Stadium of Yokohama''') is a sports stadium in Yokohama, Japan. It was built for the 2002 FIFA World Cup Final.

 Events 
 2002 FIFA World Cup Final
 FIFA Club World Cup
