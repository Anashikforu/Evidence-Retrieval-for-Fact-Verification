The  is a Japanese flute which is played vertically (up-down) like a recorder, instead of horizontally (from side to side) like the Western world|Western transverse flute. Its name means "1.8 foot", its size. It is traditionally made of bamboo, but ones made of wood or plastic exist today. It was used by the monks of Zen Buddhism in the practice of ''suizen'' (blowing meditation).
