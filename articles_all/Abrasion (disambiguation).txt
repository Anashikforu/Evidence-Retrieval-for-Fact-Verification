
'''Abrasion''' may mean:

Abrasion (medical), damage to the skin, generally not deeper than the epidermis
Abrasion (dental), the loss of tooth structure by mechanical forces from a foreign element
Abrasion (geology) (''corrsion''), mechanical scraping of a rock surface by friction between rocks and moving particles during their transport in wind, glacier, waves, gravity or running water.
'''Abrasion''' (materials science), Wear (erosion)#Abrasive wear|abrasive wear is the erosion of material from a solid surface by the action of another solid.
Abrasion (band), a heavy metal trio formed in Melbourne, Australia.
