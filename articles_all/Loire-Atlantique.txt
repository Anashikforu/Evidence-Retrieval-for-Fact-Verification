


'''Loire-Atlantique''' is a department in France in the region Pays de la Loire. Its capital is Nantes which is also the region's capital. The department gets its name from the river which gos through it (''la Loire River|Loire)'', and because it wikt:border|borders the Atlantic Ocean.

 History 
Loire Atlantique is one of the 83 first departments of France created in 1790. It used to be called ''Loire-Inférieure'' and got its new name in 1957.

The department used to be part of Britanny until 1941 when the Vichy Governement sparated it. Many people still think it as Britanny's capital and some want to join it back to Britanny again.

 Geography 
Loire-Atlantique borders 4 departements:
 Vendée
 Maine-et-Loire
 Ille-et-Vilaine
 Morbihan

West of it is the Atlantic Ocean.
