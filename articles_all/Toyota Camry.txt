

The '''Toyota Camry''' (; Japanese: トヨタ・カムリ) is a made by Toyota since 1980. The name "Camry" comes from the Japanese language|Japanese word ''kanmuri'' (冠, かんむり), which means ":wikt:simple:crown|crown".

Sales
The Camry is Toyota's best-selling car in the United States.
