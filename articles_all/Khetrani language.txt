
'''Khetrani''' is an Indo-Aryan language. It is spoken in northeast Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province (Pakistan). About 4,000 people speak it. It is member of Lahnda group. It is related to Saraiki language|Saraiki and to Sindhi language|Sindhi. The literacy rate for First language|native speakers is below 1% and below 5% for those speaking it as a second language.
