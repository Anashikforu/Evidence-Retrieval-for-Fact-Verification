
'''Sprite''' is a lemon-lime Soft drink|soda, similar to 7 UP and Sierra Mist. It is made by the Coca-Cola|Coca-Cola Company. It has no caffeine. In 2009, its logo was changed.

Variants
There are many variants of Sprite. The two popular Sprite variants are:
 Sprite, lemon-lime carbonated drink with no caffeine
 Sprite Zero, the no calorie and no sugar added version of Sprite
