'''Atlantic Canada''', also known as the '''Atlantic provinces''', is the region of Canada which is made up of four Provinces and territories of Canada|provinces on the Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic coast: the three Maritimes|Maritime provinces – New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Prince Edward Island – and Newfoundland and Labrador. The population of the Atlantic provinces was 2,324,893 in 2007.
