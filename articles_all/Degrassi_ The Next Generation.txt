

'''''Degrassi: The Next Generation''''' is a Canada|Canadian teen drama created by Yan Moore and Linda Schuyler. It was shown on CTV Television Network|CTV, MuchMusic and MTV Canada. The series has had fourteen seasons and had made 385 episodes.

The TV series spawned two movies: ''Degrassi Goes Hollywood'' in 2009 and ''Degrassi Takes Manhattan'' in 2010.

TV.com rated the series a 8.8.

In June 2014, it was announced that Degrassi would end.
