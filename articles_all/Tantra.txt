'''Tantra''' (Sanskrit: तन्त्र "weave" meaning ''continuity''Norbu, p. 49), '''tantricism''' or '''tantrism''' is the name for a number of traditions from Indian religions. These traditions are usually esotericism|esoteric in their nature. Tantra exists in Hindu, Bönpo, Buddhist, and Jain forms. Tantra in its various forms has existed in South Asia, China, Japan, Tibet, Korea, Cambodia, Burma, Indonesia and Mongolia.White (2000), p. 7

Tantra is derived from 2 words - "Tanoti" (Liberation of energy) and "Trayati" (Expansion of consciousness).
