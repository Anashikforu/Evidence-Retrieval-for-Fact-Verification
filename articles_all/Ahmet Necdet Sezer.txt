

'''Ahmet Necdet Sezer''' (born September 13, 1941 in Afyonkarahisar) is a Turkish politician who was the tenth President of Turkey|President of Turkey. He was president from May 16, 2000 until August 28, 2007. He was a Republicanism|republican and supporter of secularism in Turkey.
