
A '''defender''' in association football|football is a player, who plays in at the back of the formation. There are many positions classed as a defender, mainly:

 Left Back
 Central Defender
 Right back
 Sweeper
 Full Back

The defenders are the first number when referring to formations, for example:

 '''4-3-3''' is the formation. This formation shows that there are four '''defenders''', three midfielders and three striker|attackers.
 '''4-4-2''' is the formation. This formation shows that there are four '''defenders''', four midfielders and two striker|attackers.
 '''3-4-3''' is the formation. This formation shows that there are three '''defenders''', four midfielders and three striker|attackers.
