'''Samuel Benjamin Harris''' (born April 9, 1967) is an Americans|American author. He is a philosopher,  atheist and humanist . He is the co-founder and Chief executive officer|CEO of Project Reason.

Harris, Christopher Hitchens, Richard Dawkins, and Daniel Dennett are commonly known as the "Four Horsemen of Atheism".
