Biography
Kishioku was born in Muroran, Hokkaido|Muroran on April 2, 1954. After graduating from Muroran Otani High School, he joined Japan Soccer League club Nippon Steel Yawata SC|Nippon Steel in 1973. He played 121 games and scored 1 goal in Japan Soccer League Division 1.

On May 31, 1979, Kishioku debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Indonesia national football team|Indonesia. He played at Football at the 1980 Summer Olympics|1980 Summer Olympics qualification. He played 10 games and scored 2 goals for Japan until 1980.
