'''Stroud''' is a city in Oklahoma in the United States.In the 2010 census it had a population of 2,690.
It is 12.5 square miles in land area and has a population density of 220 people per square mile. It is twined with Stroud, England|Stroud in the United Kingdom

History

Stroud was founded in 1892 and named for James Wrexel Stroud, a developer. Early in its history, Stroud lay in Oklahoma Territory, where alcoholic drinks could be sold legally.
