The '''Onager''' (''Equus hemionus'') is a large mammal belonging to the Equidae family. It is native to the deserts of Syria, Iran, Pakistan, Kashmir, Israel and Palestine, and Tibet|western China. It is sometimes known as the '''Asian Wild Ass'''.
