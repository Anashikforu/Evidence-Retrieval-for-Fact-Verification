Biography
Watanabe was born in Hiroshima on September 24, 1924. After graduating from Kokugakuin University, he played for Chugoku Electric Power SC|Chugoku Electric Power.

In March 1954, Watanabe was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for 1954 FIFA World Cup|1954 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on March 14, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea. He also played at Football at the 1954 Asian Games|1954 Asian Games. He played 2 games for Japan in 1954.

On October 12, 2011, Watanabe died of pancreatic cancer in Hiroshima at the age of 87.
