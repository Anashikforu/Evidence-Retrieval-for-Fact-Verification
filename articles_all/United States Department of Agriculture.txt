The '''United States Department of Agriculture''' (also called the '''Agriculture Department''', or '''USDA''') is a United States federal executive departments|United States Federal Executive Department (or ''United States Cabinet|Cabinet Department''). Its purpose is to develop and execute policy on farming, agriculture, and food.
