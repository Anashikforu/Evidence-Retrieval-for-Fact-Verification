
The '''New Wave of British Heavy Metal''' ('''NWOBHM''') was a heavy metal music|heavy metal movement that started in the late 1970s, in  Great Britain|Britain.  It was well known around the world by the early 1980s.  The movement developed as a reaction in part to the decline of early heavy metal bands such as Deep Purple, Led Zeppelin and Black Sabbath
