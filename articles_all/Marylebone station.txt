
'''Marylebone station''', also known as '''London Marylebone''', is a London station group|central London railway terminus and London Underground station. It is halfway between the mainline stations at Euston railway station|Euston and Paddington station|Paddington, being about 1 mile (1.6&nbsp;km) from each. It was opened on 15 March 1899 by the Great Central Railway.Butt (1995), p. 156
