



An '''amphitheatre''' (or '''amphitheater''')  is a type of structure. It is a flat area, surrounded by an area that ascends gradually. In the ascending area, people can be seated. Today, such structures are used for presentations, but also Audience|spectator sports.

In Ancient Rome, these structures were used to entertain the population. Gladiator combats, Sport|athletics and executions were staged there.

Natural amphitheatre
A natural amphitheatre is a natural Formation (geology)|formation of rock (geology)|rocks or cliffs, which resemble a man-made amphitheatre.
