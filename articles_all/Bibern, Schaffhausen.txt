

'''Bibern''' is a former municipality in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Schaffhausen (canton)|Schaffhausen in Switzerland. On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Bibern, Altdorf, Schaffhausen|Altdorf, Hofen, Switzerland|Hofen and Opfertshofen merged into the municipality of Thayngen.
