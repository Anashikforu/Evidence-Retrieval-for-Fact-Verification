

'''Type O Negative''' was a doom metal band from Brooklyn, New York. The band was started in 1989 and ended in 2010.

 Members 
Josh Silver – Keyboards, synthesizer, sound effects, programming and backing vocalist|backing vocals
Kenny Hickey – Lead guitars, rhythm guitars and backing vocals
Johnny Kelly – Drums, percussion and backing vocals
