
'''Ishmael''' (إ‎ ʾIsmāʿīl) was the first son of Abraham and :fr:Agar_(Bible)|Hagar, an Egyptian servant. He and his mother had settled in the valley and was the meeting of :fr:Jurhum|Jurhum, nomads of Yemen and descendants of :fr:Yoktan|Joktan who had also spotted the water source and incorporate. He is the father of the ''North Arabs'' (:fr:Adnan|Adnanites).

It was derived from Abraham, descendant of :fr:Arpakshad|Arpachshad, son of :fr:Sem|Shem, son of Noah, grandson of Methuselah and coming from line of Seth, son of Adam and Eve, first human.
