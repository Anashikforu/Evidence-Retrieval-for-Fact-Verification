'''Troy University''' is a public university in Troy, Alabama|Troy, Alabama. The university has about 30,000 students. It was founded in 1887. The current chancellor of the university is Jack Hawkins, Jr.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Trojans. They have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the Sun Belt Conference.
