

'''''K-9 and Company''''' was a 1981 television spin-off of the original television programme|series of ''Doctor Who''. It featured Sarah Jane Smith, played by Elisabeth Sladen, and K-9 (Doctor Who)|K-9, a robotic dog. Both characters had been companions of the Doctor (Doctor Who)|Fourth Doctor, but they had not appeared together before. A single episode, "A Girl's Best Friend", was produced, but the series was not taken up.
