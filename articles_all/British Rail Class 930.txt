This former British Rail Class 405|4Sub unit was converted to a sandite/de-icing unit in 1979. It was withdrawn in mid-2004 and has since been scrapped.
The British Rail '''Class 930''' was reserved for former Southern Region electrical multiple units and diesel multiple unit|diesel-electric multiple units converted for departmental use. Originally the series was reserved for de-icing and, later, sandite units. In recent years, however, other types have also been given numbers in this series. Electric and diesel units are dealt with separately
