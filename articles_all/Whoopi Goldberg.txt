
'''Whoopi Goldberg''' (born '''Caryn Elaine Johnson'''; November 13, 1955) is an United States|American actress, comedienne, author and talk show host. She made her movie debut in ''The Color Purple (movie)|The Color Purple'' for which she won a Golden Globe Award. Goldberg was partners with Frank Langella from 1996 until they separated in 2001. She is currently a co-host on ''The View''.

Early life
Goldberg had a daughter when she was 18 years old. She became a grandmother when she was 34.
