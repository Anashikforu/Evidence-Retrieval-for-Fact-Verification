
'''Larkana Taluka''' is an administrative subdivision (tehsil|taluka) of Larkana District in the Sindh province of Pakistan. The city of Larkana is the capital.

History
During British Raj|British rule Larkana became headquarters of Larkana District and Larkana Taluka - and for a time was part of the Bombay Presidency of British India.
In 1901 the population was 100,827, compared with 90,15l in 1891. At that time the taluka contains one town, Larkana and 72 villages. 

Administration
Larkana tehsil|Taluka is administratively subdivided into 18 Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils.
