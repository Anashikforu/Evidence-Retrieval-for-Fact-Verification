'''Christina Claire Ciminella''' (born May 30, 1964), aka '''Wynonna Judd''', is an American singer and songwriter. In 1992 she had her first number one album on Billboard's Top Country Albums.
She first rose to fame during the 1980s as part of the duo The Judds with her mother Naomi Judd, who is also a singer. The duo had seven albums via Curb Records. Wynonna has had many solo albums.
