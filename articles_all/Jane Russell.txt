
'''Ernestine Jane Geraldine Russell''', known simply as '''Jane Russell''' (June 21, 1921 - February 28, 2011) was an Canadian-American actress, born in Minnesota. She starred in almost two dozen films throughout her career. Russell died in Santa Maria, California|Santa Maria, California of a Respiratory system|respiratory related illness.

 Notable films 
 ''The Outlaw'' (1943)
 ''The Paleface'' (1948)
 ''Gentlemen Prefer Blondes'' (1953)
