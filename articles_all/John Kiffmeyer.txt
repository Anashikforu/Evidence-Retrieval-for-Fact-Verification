

'''John Kiffmeyer''' (born July 11, 1969) also known as '''Al Sobrante''' is an Americans|American drummer. He was the first drummer for the punk rock band Green Day. He has also been the drummer for Isocracy (band)|Isocracy. He got his nickname from the fact he is from El Sobante, California|El Sobrante (California).
