
The '''Serbia national football team''' (Serbian language|Serbian: Фудбалска репрезентација Србије, ''Fudbalska reprezentacija Srbije'') is the national football (soccer)|football team of Serbia.

Both FIFA and UEFA consider the Serbian national team to be the direct successor of the Yugoslavia national football team|Yugoslavia and Serbia and Montenegro national football team|Serbia and Montenegro teams.
