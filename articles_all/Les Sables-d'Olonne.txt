'''Les Sables-d&#39;Olonne''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the region Pays de la Loire in the Vendée department in the west of France. On 1 January 2019, the former communes of Olonne-sur-Mer and Château-d'Olonne joined together to become the commune of Les Sables-d'Olonne.
