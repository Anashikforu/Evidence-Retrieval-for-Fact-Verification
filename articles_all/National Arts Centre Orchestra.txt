The '''National Arts Centre Orchestra''' is an orchestra in Ottawa, Canada's capital.  Since 1998 their conducting|conductor (musical director) has been Pinchas Zukerman.   The orchestra was started in 1969.  It has made more than 40 recordings and toured widely throughout the world.
