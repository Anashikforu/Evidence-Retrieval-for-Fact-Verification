'''Over the Limit''' was a professional wrestling event that was held yearly by WWE, and aired live television|live only on pay-per-view (PPV). The first one was held in 2010, taking the place of WWE Judgment Day|Judgment Day in the May slot of WWE's pay-per-view calendar. The last event took place on May 20, 2012 at the PNC Arena in Raleigh, North Carolina|Raleigh, North Carolina. It was replaced by WWE Battleground in 2013.

As is the case with most lower level PPV's made by WWE, the event was held only in arenas in the United States. Championship (professional wrestling)|Championship bouts were scheduled on every card (sports)|card, with the lower level title matches on the undercard and the top level featured on the main event|main card. Under the WWE Brand Extension|Brand Extension, WWE assigns its performers to either WWE Raw|Raw or WWE SmackDown|SmackDown which cause matches to be set up between wrestlers on the same show.
