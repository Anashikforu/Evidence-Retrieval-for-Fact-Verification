


'''Nu''' (uppercase/lowercase '''Ν ν''') is a letter of the Greek alphabet used to represent the "n" sound in Ancient Greek|Ancient and Modern Greek language|Modern Greek. In the system of Greek numerals, it has a value of 50. Letters that came from it include the Roman N and Cyrillic Н.

In statistics, the lowercase \nu can be used to represent Degrees of freedom (statistics)|degrees of freedom.
