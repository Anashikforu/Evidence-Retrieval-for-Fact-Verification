The '''Lesser Antilles''' (also known as the '''Caribbees'''"West Indies."  ''Merriam-Webster's Geographical Dictionary'', 3rd ed.  2001.  ()  Springfield, MA: Merriam-Webster Inc., p. 1298.) are a group of islands between the eastern edge of the Caribbean Sea and the western Atlantic Ocean. They are east and south of the Greater Antilles. Some of the islands are also just off the northern coast of South America.
