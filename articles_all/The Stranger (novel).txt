

'''''The Stranger''''' (sometimes known as '''''The Outsider''''') is an existentialism|existentialist novel written by French writer Albert Camus. It was written in 1942.

The title is "''L'Etranger''" in French language|French. It discusses philosophy|philosophical ideas.
