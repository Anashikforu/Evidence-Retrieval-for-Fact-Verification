
The '''1985 Santiago earthquake''' was registered the Sunday March 3, 1985 at 22:47 UTC (19:47 local time). Its epicenter was on the coastline of the Valparaiso Region, Chile, near Algarrobo, Chile|Algarrobo. It had a magnitude of 8.0 on the Richter scale.
