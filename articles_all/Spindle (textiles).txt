
A '''spindle''' (sometimes called a '''drop spindle''') is a wooden spike (or shaft) that has at one end a weight, mostly a circular Whorl (spindel)|whorl.

Sometimes it has a hook at either end of the spike. It is used for spinning (textiles)|spinning wool and other fibres into yarn|thread. Spindles or parts of them have been found in very, very old archaeological sites; they may represent one of the earliest pieces of technology available to humankind.
