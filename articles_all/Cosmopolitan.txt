'''Cosmopolitan''' is a word considered first made by Diogenes of Sinope and is made of two parts, ''cosmos'' and ''politics''.  It can be used for:
A place or person that is proud of its multicultural heritage
Global citizenship|World citizens think that all the people of the world should have and follow the same ideals or international law, especially on international human rights law|human rights and that the idea of nation-states will be therefore outdated, before international organizations.
''Cosmopolitan (magazine)'', an American women's magazine.
