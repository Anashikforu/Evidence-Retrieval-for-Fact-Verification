'''Chatuchak Weekend Market''' (also spelled '''Jatujak''') is a market in Bangkok.
Nowadays Chatuchak Weekend Market has been made buying and selling wikt:culture|culture and be famous around the world as the center of selling products. It has more than 112,000 square meters. Foreign tourists know Jatujak market as JJ market or Chatuchak Weekend Market.
