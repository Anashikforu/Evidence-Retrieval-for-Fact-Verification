'''Medellín''' is a village in the provinces of Spain|province of Badajoz (province)|Badajoz, Extremadura, Spain. It is notable as the birthplace of Hernán Cortés in 1485 and the site of the Battle of Medelin|Battle of Medellín, during the Peninsular War.  

The city was named after the Roman general Quintus Caecilius Metellus Pius who founded it as a military base for his operations in western Iberia.  It was originally called Metellinum.
