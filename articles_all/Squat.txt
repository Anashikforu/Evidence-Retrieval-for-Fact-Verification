


'''Squat''' is a renowned element of many folk dances. 
In powerlifting and bodybuilding, as well as in other sports, '''squat''' is an exercise that strength training|strengthens the muscles of the thighs, hips and buttocks, as well as the bones, ligaments and insertion of the tendons throughout the lower body.
