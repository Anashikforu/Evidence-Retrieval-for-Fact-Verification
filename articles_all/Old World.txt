


'''Old World''' is a term about the three continents, Europe, Asia and Africa, whose people knew about each other before Christopher Columbus.

In 1492 Spain sent Columbus to the Americas|New World. They found Native Americans, and then they became Friendship|friends and traded.

They had foods and animals Native Americans had not seen before, like sugarcane, pigs, and horses. But, they did not know that they were also giving disease|sickness. They accidentally killed many Native Americans. They also killed many of them on purpose, in conquering them.

The people of the Old World called the Americas the New World.
