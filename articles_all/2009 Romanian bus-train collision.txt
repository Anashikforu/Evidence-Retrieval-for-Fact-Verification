
The '''2009 Romanian bus-train collision''' (), happened on 14 August 2009 at 14:10pm in Romania. A bus and train wikt:collided|hit each other on a level crossing. Forteen people were killed. It happened in Scânteia, Iaşi|Scânteia of Iaşi County in Romania on County Road 248C

 Accident 
The accident happened when a minibus carrying 16 wikt:passengers|passengers was moving across a level crossing. The minibus was hit by a train as it was crossing the level crossing near Scânteia. Everyone that was killed in the accident was on the bus, which included a child. Two other people were badly injured. The bus was hit in the center of it and was pushed a total of 350 meters by the train
