
'''Robert Michael Schneider''' (born October 31, 1963) is an Americans|American actor, comedian, screenwriter, producer and Movie director|director.

 Early life 
Schneider was born in San Francisco, California and grew up in the nearby suburb of Pacifica, California|Pacifica. He is the son of American Jews|Jewish-American real estate broker Marvin Schneider, and Pilar, a former kindergarten teacher and ex-school board president; Schneider graduated from Terra Nova High School (Pacifica)|Terra Nova High School in 1982.

 Career 
After becoming a famous stand-up comedy|stand-up comedian and expert at NBC sketch comedy series ''Saturday Night Live'', Schneider now has a career in movies, including main roles in the comedies ''Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo'', ''Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolo'', ''The Animal'' and ''The Hot Chick''.
