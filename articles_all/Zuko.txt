'''Zuko''' is a fictional character in Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's anime|animated television programme|television series ''Avatar: The Last Airbender''. The character and series were created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko. His powers were once powered by Hatred and anger but once he started to develop a better mentality he changed energy that powered his powers.

Zuko is voice actor|voiced by Dante Basco. He will be played by Dallas Liu in the Netflix Avatar: The Last Airbender (upcoming TV series)|live action remake.
