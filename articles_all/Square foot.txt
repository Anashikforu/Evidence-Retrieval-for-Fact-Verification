A '''square foot''' is a way to measure area. It is the area made by a Square (geometry)|square that has 1 foot (unit of length)|foot sides. It is used mainly in the United States and partially in Bangladesh, Canada, Ghana, Hong Kong, India, Liberia, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan, Singapore, and the United Kingdom.
