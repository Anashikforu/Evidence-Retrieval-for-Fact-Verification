

'''Ashland''' is an United States|American city in the U.S. state|state of Alabama. It is the county seat of Clay County, Alabama|Clay County. The city has a population of about 2,000 people. Ashland is the hometown of Alabama|Alabama's 52nd governor Bob Riley.  Supreme Court Justice Hugo Black is from Ashland.

Geography
Ashland has an area of 7.2 miles. The city is Alabama|Alabama's highest elevation|elevated county town|county seat at 1130 feet.
