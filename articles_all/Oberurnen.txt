
'''Oberurnen''' is a former municipality of the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Glarus (canton)|Glarus in Switzerland. On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Oberurnen, Bilten, Filzbach, Mollis, Mühlehorn, Näfels, Niederurnen and Obstalden merged into the new municipality of Glarus Nord.
