


A '''charitable organization''' is an organization that supports a cause by fundraising or through wikt:donation|donations and Charity (practice)|charity. The organisations arrange for all the distribution of any money to each cause.

There are many well-known charities all over the world. Two of the most well-known charities are UNICEF and the Red Cross.
