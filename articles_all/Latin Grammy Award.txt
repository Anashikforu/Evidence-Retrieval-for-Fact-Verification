
The '''Latin Grammy Awards''' are awards given yearly by the Latin Academy of Recording Arts & Sciences|Latin Recording Academy (an association of professionals who work in the Latin music industry) for outstanding work in the Latin music field. One major difference between the Grammy Awards and the Latin Grammy Awards is that a recording that does not have to be released in the United States. To be considered for a Latin Grammy nomination, the song or album must be sung in Spanish language|Spanish or Portuguese language|Portuguese.
