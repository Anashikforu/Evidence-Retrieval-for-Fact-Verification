
'''''Elite Beat Agents''''' is a Music video game|music video game made by iNiS and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo DS handheld game console|handheld video game console|game console. It's the Spiritual successor|spiritual sequel to ''Osu! Tatakae! Ouendan''. The gameplay has the player tap and drag on spots on the touch screen in beat to the rhythm of the music to score points. It was released under the Touch! Generations label in North America. In South Korea, it's called ''Dowajweo! Rhythm Hero'' (; Help! Rhythm Hero).
