
'''Antananarivo''' is the capital (city)|capital city of Madagascar. It is the largest city in the country. The city is the administration|administrative, communications and economy|economic center of Madagascar.

Antananarivo has a population of over 1.4 million people. Including an Anglican church|Anglican church|cathedral and a Roman Catholic Church|Roman Catholic church|cathedral, there are about fifty churches in the city and its suburbs.  History 
Antananarivo was :wikt:found|founded in about 1625 by King Andrianjaka. The name, Antananarivo, means ''the City of the Thousand''. It comes from the number of soldiers Andrianjaka used to guard the city.

Until 1869 all buildings in the city were of wood or rushes. Since the introduction of stone and brick, the whole city has been rebuilt. It now has many notable structures. For example, the Royal family|royal palaces, the Anglican and Roman Catholic cathedrals, several stone and brick churches, colleges, schools, hospitals, courts of judiciary|justice and other government buildings.

The city was captured by the France|French in 1895. Since then, good roads have been built in the city.
