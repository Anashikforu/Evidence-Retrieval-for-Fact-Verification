This is a '''list of the tallest buildings in Sydney''', New South Wales, Australia. Spires on top of buildings count onto its height, but antennas do not.



Notes
:'''A.''' Height for buildings is measured by spire height, or if there is no spire, roof height.  Antenna height is not used.
:'''B.'''  Sydney Tower is not ranked because it cannot be occupied all the time by people. It is only used for observation.
