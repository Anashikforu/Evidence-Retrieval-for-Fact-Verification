'''Butler University''' is a private, co-educational university in Indianapolis, Indiana|Indianapolis, Indiana. The university has about 4,512 students. It was founded in 1855. The current president of the university is Bobby Fong. 

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Bulldogs. They have 17 varsity teams, most of which compete in the Big East Conference. The American football|football team plays in the Pioneer Football League.
