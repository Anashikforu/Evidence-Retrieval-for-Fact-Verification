
'''Lucy Katherine Pinder''' (born 20 December 1983) is an English people|English glamour model. She is from Winchester, Hampshire.

From 2 January 2009, Pinder appeared in the Celebrity Big Brother 2009 (UK)|sixth series of ''Big_Brother_(UK)#Celebrity_Big_Brother|Celebrity Big Brother'' UK. She revealed that she is "a bit of a Conservative Party (UK)|Tory bird" and that 'thick' people irritate her. She was the first housemate to be voted out, on 9 January (Day 8) with 57% of the public vote.
