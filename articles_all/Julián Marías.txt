
'''Julián Marías Aguilera''' (June 17, 1914 – December 15, 2005) was a Spain|Spanish philosophy|philosopher. He is the author of ''History of Philosophy'' (1941) written in Spanish language|Spanish on the subject of the history of philosophy.  He was a pupil of the Spanish philosopher José Ortega y Gasset.
