
'''Taste buds''' are small 'bumps' on the tongue. They are the sense organs which wikt:detect|detect the taste of things that go into the mouth. They are involved in detecting the five (known) elements of taste perception: salty, wikt:sour|sour, bitterness|bitter, Sweet (taste)|sweet, and umami.
