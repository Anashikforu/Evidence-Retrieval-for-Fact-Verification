
'''Jessica Claire Biel''' (born March 3, 1982) is an Americans|American actress, 

Biel became known for her early television role of Mary Camden in the long-running family drama Television series|series ''7th Heaven''. She is perhaps best known for appearing in several Hollywood movies. These include ''Summer Catch'', the remake of ''The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (2003 movie)|The Texas Chainsaw Massacre'' and ''The Illusionist''. 

Biel is also known for her beauty and is considered a sex symbol. In 2005 Esquire magazine declared her the "Sexiest Woman Alive".

In 2012, she married singer Justin Timberlake.
