'''Force India Formula One Team Limited''' or '''Force India F1 Team''', often called '''Force India''' is a Formula One motor racing team. The racing team was formed by Indian businessman Vijay Mallya. Mallya bought the Spyker F1 racing team in October 2007, and changed the name to Force India.
