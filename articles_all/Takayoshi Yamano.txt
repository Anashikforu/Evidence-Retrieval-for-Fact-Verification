Biography
Yamano was born in Osaka Prefecture on April 5, 1955. After graduating from Osaka University of Commerce, he joined his local club Cerezo Osaka|Yanmar Diesel in 1978. The club won the Japan Soccer League champions in 1980. The club also won 1983 and 1984 JSL Cup. He played 125 matches for the club until 1984. In 1986, he signed with Japan Soccer League Division 2 club Osaka Gas SC|Osaka Gas. He retired in 1991.

On June 11, 1980, Yamano debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against China national football team|China. He played 2 games for Japan in 1980.
