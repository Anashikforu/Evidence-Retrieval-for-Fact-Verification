
The '''Leader of the Opposition''' (sometimes known as the '''Leader of the Opposition in the House of Commons''') in the United Kingdom is the politician who leads Official Opposition (United Kingdom)|Her Majesty's Loyal Opposition. The Leader of the Opposition is normally the leader of the second largest political party|party in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons.

The current Leader of the Opposition is Keir Starmer, the leader of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
