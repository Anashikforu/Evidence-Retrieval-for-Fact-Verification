
'''Troy Brouwer''' (born August 17, 1985 in North Delta, British Columbia|North Delta, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|right winger. He currently plays for the St Louis Blues of the National Hockey League|National Hockey League (NHL). He also played in the NHL for the Chicago Blackhawks, Florida Panthers, Calgary Flames, Washington Capitals, and Chicago Blackhawks. He played in the Western Hockey League|WHL for the Moose Jaw Warriors where he played 5 seasons and was named team captain his last season with the Warriors.

On June 9, 2010, Brouwer won the Stanley Cup with the Chicago Blackhawks.

Awards
He won the Bob Clarke Trophy (WHL leading scorer) in 2006.
He was named to the American Hockey League|AHL Second All-Star Team in 2007.
He was named to the AHL All-Rookie Team in 2007.
He won the Stanley Cup in 2010. (Chicago Blackhawks)
