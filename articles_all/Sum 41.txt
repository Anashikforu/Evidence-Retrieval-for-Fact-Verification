'''Sum 41''' (sometimes called '''The Sums''') is a Canadian rock punk band from Ajax, Ontario. The people currently in the band are Deryck Whibley (singer|lead vocals, guitar, keyboard instrument|keyboards), Cone McCaslin (bass guitar, backing vocalist|backing vocals), and Steve Jocz (drum kit|drums, percussion instrument|percussion, and backing vocals). The band's most known lineup is the same, except with Dave Baksh on lead guitar and backing vocals.

In 1999, the band signed a record deal with Island Records. Since then, the band has released four studio albums. Their most successful album to date is ''All Killer, No Filler.'' The second single off the album, "In Too Deep (Sum 41 song)|In Too Deep", also did well.

They often perform more than 300 times in a single year. That is why they are known for their long and worldwide tours, most of which last more than a year. Sum 41 has received many awards and nominations. They have been nominated for seven Juno Awards (the Canadian equivalent of a Grammy Award), winning twice.
