

'''R''' is the eighteenth (number 18) letter in the English language|English alphabet.

 Meanings for R 
 On calendars, R is sometimes used as an abbreviation|short letter for Thursday instead of T, to avoid mistakes with Tuesday.
 In chess, R is a notation symbol for the Rook (chess)|rook piece.
 In geometry, R is most times a variable for the radius of a circle
 In navigation, R means the direction "right."
 In movies, R means "restricted."
 In Wheel of Fortune (US game show)|Wheel of Fortune, R is one of the six letters given to any contestant participating in the "Bonus Round."
