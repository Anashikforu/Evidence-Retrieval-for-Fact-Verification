
'''Wayne Simmonds''' (born August 26, 1988 in Scarborough, Ontario|Scarborough, Ontario) is an Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|right winger. He currently plays for the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League (NHL). He has also played for the Los Angeles Kings, Philadelphia Flyers, Nashville Predators, New Jersey Devils.

He was drafted by the Los Angeles Kings with the 61st overall pick in the 2007 NHL Entry Draft. He also played in the Ontario Hockey League|OHL with the Owen Sound Attack and Sault Ste. Marie Greyhounds. He is African American and is one of a small number of black hockey players in the league.
