
'''Niklas Oskar Bäckström''' (born February 13, 1978) is a Finns|Finnish professional ice hockey goaltender. He plays for the Minnesota Wild of the National Hockey League (NHL). He has also played in the Liiga|SM-liiga for HIFK (ice hockey)|HIFK, SaiPa and Kärpät and in the Swedish Hockey League|Elitserien for AIK IF|AIK. He won the William M. Jennings Trophy in 2007 along with Manny Fernandez. He also played for Finland at the 2006 Winter Olympics where he won a silver medal and at the 2010 Winter Olympics where he won a bronze medal.
