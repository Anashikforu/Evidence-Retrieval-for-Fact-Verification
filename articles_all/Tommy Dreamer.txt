
'''Thomas James "Tom" Laughlin''' (born February 13, 1971) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler, promoter and actor. He is better known by his ring name, '''Tommy Dreamer'''. He is best known for his time in Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) and WWE|World wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He has also worked for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). He is both the founder and owner of the wrestling promotion House of Hardcore.

Dreamer is a one-time ECW World Heavyweight Championship|ECW Champion and fourteen-time WWE Hardcore Championship|Hardcore Champion. He has been called the "heart and soul" of Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) and the "Innovator of Violence".
