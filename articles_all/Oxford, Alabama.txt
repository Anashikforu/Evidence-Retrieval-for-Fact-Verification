
'''Oxford''' is a city in Calhoun County, Alabama|Calhoun and Talladega County, Alabama|Talladega counties in the U.S. state of Alabama.  The population was 14,592 at the United States Census, 2000|2000 census.  Oxford is one of two principal cities of and included in the Anniston-Oxford Metropolitan Area|Anniston-Oxford Metropolitan Statistical Area.
