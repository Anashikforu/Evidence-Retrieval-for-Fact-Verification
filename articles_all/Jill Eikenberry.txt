'''Jill Eikenberry''' (born 21 January 1947) is an Americans|American actress. She was born in New Haven, Connecticut. Her most well known role was playing Ann Kelsey in ''L.A. Law'' (1986-1994). She appeared in many television movies in the 1980s.
