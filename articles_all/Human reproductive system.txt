A '''reproductive system''' is the part of an organism that makes them able to sexual reproduction|sexually reproduce. Humans and other animals use their reproductive systems to have sexual intercourse as well as reproduce. The reproductive system is one of the human Organ system|organ systems. 

The illustrations here show only human reproductive systems. Other mammals have similar reproductive systems.
