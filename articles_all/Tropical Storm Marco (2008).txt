

'''Tropical Storm Marco''' was the smallest tropical cyclone ever. It was the 13th Tropical cyclone#Naming|named storm of the 2008 Atlantic hurricane season. Marco started from a large Low pressure area|storm over the northwestern Caribbean Sea during late September 2008. It later became a tropical depression. Marco reached its peak strength with winds of 65&nbsp;miles per hour (100&nbsp;km/h) on October&nbsp;7. Marco hit land near Misantla, Veracruz. Because it was a very small storm, Marco caused little damage.
