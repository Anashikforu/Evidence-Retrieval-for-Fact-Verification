

'''Matador Records''' is an United States|American indie (music)|indie record label with many bands like Modest Mouse, Pavement (band)|Pavement, and Guided by Voices.  Matador Records is founded in 1989 by Chris Lombardi, who operates out of his New York apartment. It becomes a label dedicated to unique alternative acts. Half of the company will be purchased by Beggars Banquet in 2002.
