
'''Martin Bell''', Order of the British Empire|OBE (born 31 August 1938) is a British people|British UNICEF (UNICEF UK) Ambassador, a former broadcast war reporter and former independent politician. He was an Member of parliament|Member of Parliament (MP) for Tatton (UK Parliament constituency)|Tatton from 1997 to 2001. Sometimes, he is called "the man in the white suit".
