



'''22''' is a year in the 1st century.  It was a common year starting on Thursday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Consulship of Agrippa and Galba'''.

 Events 
 Gaius Sulpicius Galba becomes Roman consul|consul.
 Roman law replaces Celtic customs in Gaul.
 Beginning of Later Han Dynasty in China.

 Births 
 Valeria Messalina, third wife of Roman Emperor Claudius

 Deaths 
 Junia Tertia
