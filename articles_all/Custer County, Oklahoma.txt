
'''Custer County''' is a county in the U.S. state of Oklahoma.  It was named in honor of General George Armstrong Custer.  As of 2000, 26,142 people lived there.  Its county seat is Arapaho, Oklahoma|Arapaho.

 Geography 
The county has a total area of 1,002 square miles.
