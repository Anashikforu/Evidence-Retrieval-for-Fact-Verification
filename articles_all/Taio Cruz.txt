
'''Jacob Taio Cruz''' is an English people|English R&B singer. He has released three studio albums and his single, "Break Your Heart" which was featured on his ''Rokstarr'' album was a number one hit in both the U.K. and the U.S. In 2014 his fourth album ''#Black'' will be released.
