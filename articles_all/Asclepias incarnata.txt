


'''''Asclepias incarnata''''' is a species of milkweed plant.  It is also known as the '''swamp milkweed''', the '''rose milkweed''', the '''pink milkweed''', the '''swamp milkweed''', and '''white Indian hemp'''.  It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to North America.  It blooms from June to August.  The flowers are pink. Swamp milkweed is found in wet meadows, prairies, swamps, and marshes.  It likes moist soil and sunny or partly shaded places.
