
'''''Machair''''' was a Scottish Gaelic language|Scottish Gaelic television soap opera produced by Scottish Television|Scottish Television Enterprises between January 1993 and April 1999.

''Machair'' was written in English language|English and translated into Gaelic, then given English subtitles and broadcast during primetime. Audiences liked the show too. ''Machair'' got 30% audience share and made it into the Top Ten of programmes viewed in Scotland, even though fewer than 2% of the Scottish population can speak Gaelic.  It was nominated for awards for production and writing from ''The Celtic Film Festival'' and Writers Guild of Great Britain.
