
'''East Germany national football team''' was the national football (soccer)|football team of the German Democratic Republic (GDR or East Germany) from 1952 to 1990. The East Germany team is now the Germany national football team|Germany team.

 Record
= FIFA World Cup =

East Germany only qualified for one FIFA World Cup|World Cup, and they only made it to 1974 FIFA World Cup|round 2.

 1950 FIFA World Cup|1950 - ''Did not enter''
 1954 FIFA World Cup|1954 - ''Did not enter''
 1958 FIFA World Cup|1958 to 1970 FIFA World Cup|1970 - ''Did not qualify''
 1974 FIFA World Cup|1974 - Round 2
 1978 FIFA World Cup|1978 to 1990 FIFA World Cup|1990 - ''Did not qualify''

= European Championship =

East Germany never qualified for a UEFA European Football Championship tournament.

 1960 European Nations' Cup|1960 to UEFA Euro 1988|1988 - ''Did not qualify''
 UEFA Euro 1992|1992 - ''Left to qualification round''
