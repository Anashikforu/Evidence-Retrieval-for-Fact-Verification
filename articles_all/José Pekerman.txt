
'''José Néstor Pekerman''' (born 3 September 1949) is a former Argentina|Argentine Association football|football player and current manager (head coach) of  Colombia national football team|Colombia national team. He only played for two clubs in his entire career, which are A.A. Argentinos Juniors|Argentinos Juniors, Deportivo Independiente Medellin. He is of Jewish descent.
