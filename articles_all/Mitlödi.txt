

'''Mitlödi''' is a former municipality of the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Glarus (canton)|Glarus in Switzerland. On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Mitlödi, Betschwanden, Braunwald, Elm, Switzerland|Elm, Engi, Haslen, Linthal, Luchsingen, Matt, Switzerland|Matt, Rüti, Glarus|Rüti, Schwanden, Glarus|Schwanden, Schwändi and Sool, Switzerland|Sool merged into the new municipality of Glarus Süd.
