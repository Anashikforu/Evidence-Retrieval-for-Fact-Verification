The '''Wampanoag''' were a Native American tribe. They lived in what is now the United States|American region of New England. The Wampanoag tribe helped the English settlers to survive, after they arrived in the ''Mayflower (ship)|Mayflower''.

They caught a bacterial infection called leptospirosis or Weil's syndrome in the early 17th century. It caused a many deaths and damaged their society greatly, though their descendents still survive today.
