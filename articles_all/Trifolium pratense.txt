

'''Red Clover''' (''Trifolium pratense'') is a species of plant. It is wikt:native|native to Europe, Africa, and Asia. It has been introduced species|introduced to North America, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico. It blooms from spring to early fall. It is found in fields, pastures, and near roadsides. Red Clover likes to grow in thick, dry soil.
