'''Bahawalpur District''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Bahawalpur is the capital. The Riasti dialect of the language of the Punjab is spoken here and the neighboring areas in the Bahawalpur area of Cholistan desert|Cholistan.

Administration
The district is administratively divided into five tehsils and 107 Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils:

 Ahmedpur Sharqia Tehsil
 Bahawalpur Tehsil  City
 Hasilpur Tehsil
 Khairpur Tamewali Tehsil
 Yazman Mandi Tehsil
 Bahawalpur Tehsil Sadar
