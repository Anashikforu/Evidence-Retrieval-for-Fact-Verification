'''Sea caves''' are a hollow chamber in a coastal cliff, made by the pounding power of crashing waves. "''Natural arch|Sea arches''" and "''sea stacks''" are also carved from these Rock (geology)|rocky cliffs.Sea waves carry on both erosional and depositional activities .The process of sea erosion is called marine erosion.
