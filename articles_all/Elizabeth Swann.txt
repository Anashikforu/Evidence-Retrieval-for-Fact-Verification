
'''Elizabeth Swann''' (later '''Elizabeth Turner''') is a fictional character and the tritagonist of the Pirates of the Caribbean (movie series)|''Pirates of the Caribbean'' movie series. She appears in ''Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl|The Curse of the Black Pearl'' (2003) and three of its sequels, ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest|Dead Man's Chest'' (2006), ''Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End|At World's End'' (2007) and ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales|Dead Men Tell No Tales'' (2017). She is played by Keira Knightley. She is married to Will Turner.

Elizabeth is the only child to List of Pirates of the Caribbean characters|Weatherby Swann, the love interest (later wife) to Will Turner, mother to List of Pirates of the Caribbean characters|Henry Turner and daughter-in-law to List of Pirates of the Caribbean characters|William “Bootstrap Bill” Turner.

Swann becomes the Pirate king and captain of Singapore's pirate fleet in ''At World's End''.
