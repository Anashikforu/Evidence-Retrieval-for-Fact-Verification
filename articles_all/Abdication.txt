

'''Abdication''' is the act of resigning from an office (official job), especially from being the leader of a country. The word is normally used for kings and queens who decide to give up their position which brings an end to a wikt:reign|reign.
A similar term for an elected or appointed official is resignation.

Edward VIII of the United Kingdom|King Edward VIII of the United Kingdom is an example of a king who abdicated.
