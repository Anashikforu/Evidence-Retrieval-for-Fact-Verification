
'''C. B. Fry''' is a former sportsman. He played Cricket and Football (soccer)|football for England. He played in an FA Cup Final. Fry held a world record for long jump for many years. He also played Rugby union.

Later in his life, he was a writer and diplomat. He was asked to become king of Albania, but refused it.
