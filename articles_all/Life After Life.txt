
'''''Life After Life''''' is a 1975 book written by psychiatrist Raymond Moody. The book presents the author's account of what it is like to die.Clifton D. Bryant (2003). 
''Life After Life'' sold more than 13 million copies
Moody wrote a second book, ''Reflections on Life After Life'', in 1977.Lee Worth Bailey and Jenny L. Yates (1996).
