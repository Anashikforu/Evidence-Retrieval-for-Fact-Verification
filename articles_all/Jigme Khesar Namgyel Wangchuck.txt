
'''Jigme Khesar Namgyel Wangchuck''' (born 21 February 1980) is the son of Jigme Singye Wangchuck and the 5th and current reign|reigning Druk Gyalpo|Dragon King (Druk Gyalpo) of the Kingdom of Bhutan. He became king on 9 December 2006. He was officially crowned on 1 November 2008.
