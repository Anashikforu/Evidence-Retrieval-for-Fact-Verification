The '''Citroën Berlingo''' is a series of almost identical vans and leisure activity vehicles produced by Citroën in three generations since 1996. It is builded in cooperation with Peugeot.

Peugeot make the same vehicle but with a different name, The name is: '''Peugeot Partner'''.

 First generation (1996–2012) 
Citroën created the first generation '''Berlingo I''' in July 1996 and Peugeot created '''Peugeot Partner I''' in 1996.
