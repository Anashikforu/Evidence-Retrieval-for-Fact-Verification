The '''British Rail Class 450''' third rail Direct current|DC Multiple unit|EMU (or 4Des) began service during 2003. They are a part of the Siemens AG|Siemens ''Desiro'' modular train family and are more popularly known as the 'Blue Desiro'. In standing with requirements of all new rolling stock for the South East region, provision has been made for future conversion to 25 kV Alternating current|AC overhead lines|overhead supply or dual voltage, although at present no trains have been fitted with a pantograph (rail)|pantograph.
