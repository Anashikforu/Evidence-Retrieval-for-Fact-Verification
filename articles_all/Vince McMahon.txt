'''Vincent Kennedy McMahon''', better known as '''Vince McMahon''' or '''Mr. McMahon''' (born August 24, 1945) is an Americans|American businessman, Professional wrestling|wrestling wikt:promote|promoter, professional wrestling|professional wrestler, and Film producer|movie producer. He is the Chief executive officer|Chairman of the board and main owner of WWE|WWE, Inc.. McMahon has been both the ECW World Champion and the WWE Championship|WWE Champion.

Forbes estimates his networth|net worth to be around $2 billion.

Career
McMahon started his wrestling career in 1971 as an wikt:announce|announcer. He worked for the "World Wide Wrestling Federation" (WWWF) which later changed its name to "World Wrestling Federation" (WWF). The name was changed again to World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). The company was originally owned by his father, Vince J. McMahon. McMahon bought the company from his father in 1984. McMahon took the business from a local promotion in the north eastern part of the United States and made it a Earth|global company.

McMahon played the role of an evil boss for many years.

 Personal life 
McMahon marriage|married his wife, Linda McMahon, on August 26 1966 in New Bern, North Carolina. They have two children: Shane McMahon|Shane and Stephanie McMahon|Stephanie. In 2016, President-elect of the United States nominated McMahon's wife Linda as Administrator of the Small Business Administration.
