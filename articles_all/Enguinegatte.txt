
'''Enguinegatte''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Nord-Pas-de-Calais in the Pas-de-Calais Departments of France|department in the north of France. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of Enquin-lez-Guinegatte.
