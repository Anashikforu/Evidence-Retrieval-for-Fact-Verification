


'''Dwight Eversley Yorke''' Chaconia Medal|CM (born 3 November 1971) is a retired Trinidad and Tobago|Trinidadian Association football|football player. He has played for Trinidad and Tobago national football team|Trinidad and Tobago national team. He won the 1998–99 Premier League#Top scorers|1998–99 Premier League Premier League Golden Boot|Golden Boot, along side Jimmy Floyd Hasselbaink and Michael Owen. This made him the joint first foreign Premier League Golden Boot|Golden Boot winner.
