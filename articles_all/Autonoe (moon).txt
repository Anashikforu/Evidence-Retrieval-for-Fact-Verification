'''Autonoe''' or '''
Autonoe is about 4&nbsp;kilometres in diameter, and orbits Jupiter at an average distance of 24,264,000&nbsp;km in 772.168 days, at an inclination of 151° to the ecliptic (150° to Jupiter's equator), with an orbital eccentricity of 0.369.

It belongs to the Pasiphaë group, non-Sphere|spherical retrograde moons orbiting Jupiter at distances ranging between 22,800,000 and 24,100,000&nbsp;km, and with inclinations ranging between 144.5° and 158.3°.
