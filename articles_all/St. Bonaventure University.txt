'''St. Bonaventure University''' is a private, Catholic Church|Roman Catholic university in Allegany, New York|Allegany, New York. The university has about 2,500 students. It was founded in 1858. The current president of the university is Sr. Margaret Carney, OSF STD.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Bonnies. They have 14 varsity teams and they compete in the Atlantic 10 Conference.
