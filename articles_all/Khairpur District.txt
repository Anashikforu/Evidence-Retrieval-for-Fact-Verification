'''Khairpur District''' () is a Districts of Pakistan|district in the Sindh province of Pakistan. The district has an area of 15,910 square kilometres and is headquartered at the city of Khairpur, Pakistan|Khairpur.

Administration
The district has eight Tehsil|Talukas (counties):

 Khairpur
 Nara Tehsil
 Kot Diji Tehsil
 Sobho Dero Tehsil
 Mirwah Tehsil
 Kingri Tehsil
 Faiz Ganj Tehsil
 Gambat Tehsil
