

The '''Israel Defense Forces''' (shortened: ''IDF'') (, ''"Army for the Defense (military)|Defense of Israel"'', known in Israel by the Hebrew acronym צה"ל, ('''Tzahal'''), is the name of Israel's military forces. It includes the Israel Army, Israel Air Force and Israel Navy.
