'''La Mayenne''' is a river in the northwest of France. Its source in the Orne, about 15&nbsp;km west of Alençon. It joins the Sarthe (river)|Sarthe and Loire River north of Angers to form the Maine.

It is 200&nbsp;km long and its surface area is about 5 820&nbsp;km². It has an average discharge of 50 m³/s.
