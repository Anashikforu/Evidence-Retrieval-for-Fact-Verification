

'''''Little Miss Sunshine''''' is a 2006 United States|American independent comedy movie|comedy-drama movie|drama road movie that was shown at the Sundance Film Festival.  It is about a family who drives 800 miles from Albuquerque, New Mexico through Arizona to Redondo Beach, California in a Volkswagen Type 2 van so that the daughter to compete in a child beauty pageant|beauty contest for children.  On the way they have many problems, and learn to assist and help each other.
