

'''WHSV-TV''', channel 3, is a television station in Harrisonburg, Virginia. The station broadcasts television programs from the American Broadcasting Company|ABC Television Network. Also known as '''WHSV News 3''', the station is owned by Gray Television and broadcasts from an antenna west of the town of Stanley, Virginia|Stanley on Massanutten Mountain. The station goes to fourteen counties in the Shenandoah Valley of Virginia and West Virginia.

History
WHSV-TV first was WSVA-TV, which meant '''S'''erve '''V'''irginia '''A'''griculture and had its first broadcast on air in October 1953. In 1976, the "call letters" the station used were changed to WHSV-TV; these are the call letters the station uses now.
