Melbourne's '''Luna Park''' is a historic amusement park near the beach at St Kilda, Victoria (Australia)|Victoria Australia. It was designed and developed by Frederick Ingersoll. It opened in 1912 and has been operating ever since. It is the first of two Luna Parks still operating in Australia; the other is on Sydney Harbour.
