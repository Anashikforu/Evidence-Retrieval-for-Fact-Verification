

'''Vengeance: Night of Champions''' was the seventh yearly professional wrestling pay-per-view event by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) with the name WWE Night of Champions|Vengeance. It featured wrestlers from the WWE Raw|Raw, WWE Friday Night SmackDown|SmackDown, and ECW on Sci Fi|ECW WWE Brand Extension|brands. The event took place on June 24, 2007 at the Toyota Center (Houston)|Toyota Center in Houston|Houston, Texas.

The Card (sports)#Main event|main event featured the Raw brand. John Cena defended the WWE Championship against Mick Foley, Bobby Lashley, Randy Orton, and Booker Huffman|King Booker. Cena won the match and kept the WWE Championship after Pin (professional wrestling)|pinning Foley following an Powerslam#Fireman's carry powerslam|FU.
