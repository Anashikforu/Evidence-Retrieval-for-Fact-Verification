


A '''compass''' is a navigational tool that uses magnetism. It has an arrow which points in the direction of the North Magnetic Pole.  or other places where there are few landmarks.

The first compass was invented by Ancient China|ancient Chinese in the Han Dynasty. It was a large spoon-like magnetism|magnetic object made of magnetite ore set upon a square bronze plate.
