
'''Devin Star Tailes''' (born July 2, 1989), best known by her stage name '''Dev''', is an Americans|American singer. Her associated acts include Far East Movement. Her song "Booty Bounce" was sampled in the Far East Movement's "Like a G6". Her most successful single (music)|single was "In the Dark".

Dev was born in Tracy, California. She later moved herself to Los Angeles.
