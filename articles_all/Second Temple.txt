The '''Second Temple''' (Hebrew: בית המקדש meaning 'Holy House') was the reconstructed Temple in Jerusalem which stood between 516 BC and 70 AD. It is  also known as '''Herod's Temple'''. During this time, it was the center of Judaism|Jewish worship. The Roman Empire|Romans destroyed Jerusalem and its Second Temple on August 4, 70 A.D.
