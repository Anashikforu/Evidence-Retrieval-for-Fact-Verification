
'''Harry Smith''' is a Canadians|Canadian Professional wrestling|professional wrestler of English descent who currently works for New Japan Pro Wrestling under the name '''Davey Boy Smith, Jr.'''.

He also worked for World Wrestling Entertainment. He wrestled on the ECW WWE Brand Extension|brand using the name '''David Hart Smith''', as a member of The Hart Dynasty, alongside TJ Wilson|Tyson Kidd and Nattie Neidhart|Natalya.

He has wrestled on its WWE Raw|Raw brand and in Florida Championship Wrestling under the name '''DH Smith''' ("D" for Davey Boy Smith, and "H" for the Hart wrestling family|Hart family). On August 5, 2011, WWE released him from his wrestling contract. He currently holds the PWA World Heavyweight title.

His grandfather is Stu Hart who was a wrestling promoter. His father was Davey Boy Smith. His first public wrestling match was at age 10.  He started wrestling professionally on February 22, 2002 at age 16 and has been continuing since then.
