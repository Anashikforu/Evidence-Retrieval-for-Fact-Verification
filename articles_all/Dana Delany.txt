


'''Dana Welles Delany''' (born March 13, 1956) is an Emmy Award-winning United States|American movie and television actress. She is well known for her role as Colleen McMurphy on the American Broadcasting Company|ABC television show ''China Beach'' (1988–1991), ''Katherine Mayfair'' on ''Desperate Housewives'' (2007–2010) and ''Megan Hunt'' on ''Body of Proof'' (2011–present).
