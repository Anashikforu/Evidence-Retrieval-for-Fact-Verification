'''The Open Championship''' - often known as the '''British Open''' outside the United Kingdom and Ireland - is the oldest of the four men's major golf championships|major championships in men's golf. The event is hosted annually by one of several prestigious golf clubs in the United Kingdom and is administered by The Royal and Ancient Golf Club of St Andrews|The R&A regardless of its location. The next tournament, in 2013, will take place at Muirfield in Scotland.
