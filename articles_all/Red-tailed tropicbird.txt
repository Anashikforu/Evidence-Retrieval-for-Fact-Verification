The '''red-tailed tropicbird''' (''Phaethon rubricauda'') is a seabird that nests across the Indian Ocean|Indian and Pacific Oceans. It is the rarest of the tropicbirds, but is  widespread and not considered wikt:threaten|threatened. It nests in colonies on oceanic islands.
