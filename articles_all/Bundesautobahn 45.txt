
The '''Bundesautobahn 45''' (''in german language|German: A 45''), is a road|Motorway from the Ruhr area till Hanau.

The highway begins in Dortmund, North Rhine-Westphalia and crosses Hesse. The '''A 45''' ends in Bavaria.

 Cities along the A 45 

 Dortmund
 Hagen
 Lüdenscheid
 Siegen
 Wetzlar
 Giessen|Gießen
 Hanau
