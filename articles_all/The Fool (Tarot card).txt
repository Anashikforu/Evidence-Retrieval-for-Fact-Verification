
'''The Fool''' or '''The Jester''' ("Il Matto" in Italian) is one of the 78 playing cards|cards in a Tarot deck. It is one of the 22 Trump cards that make up the Major Arcana and the only one of the major arcana found in the modern playing cards in the role of the joker. The Fool is often numbered 0 (number)|0. It is used in divination as well as in Tarot card games|game playing.
