


'''''The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian''''' is a 2008 fantasy movie by Walt Disney Pictures|Disney and Walden Media.  It was released on May 9, 2008. It is part of the The Chronicles of Narnia movie series|''Chronicles of Narnia'' movie series.

This movie is a follow-up of the 2005 movie ''The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe''.
