
'''Gatcombe Park''' is the private country home of Anne, Princess Royal. It is in England between the Gloucestershire villages of Minchinhampton and Avening. It is five miles (8&nbsp;km) south of Stroud, Gloucestershire|Stroud and around six miles (10&nbsp;km) north of Highgrove, the country residence of Charles, Prince of Wales|Prince Charles.
