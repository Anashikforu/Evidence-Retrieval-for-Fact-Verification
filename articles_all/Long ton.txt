The '''long ton''' is the '''imperial ton''' or 'weight ton'. It is used in the United Kingdom and several other Commonwealth of Nations|Commonwealth countries. In their avoirdupois or Imperial unit|Imperial system of measurements, it is equal to  of salt water with a density of 64 lb/ft3 (1.025 g/ml). Many other countries use the Metric system|metric tonne. It has some limited use in the United States, for measuring the Displacement (ship)|displacement of ships. It was the unit recommended for warships by international agreements between the world wars, for example battleships were limited to 35,000 long tons.
