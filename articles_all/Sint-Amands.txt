'''Sint-Amands''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Antwerp (province)|Antwerp. It includes the towns of Lippelo, Oppuurs and Sint-Amands proper. On 1 January 2019, Puurs and Sint-Amands were merged into the new municipality of Puurs-Sint-Amands.

In 2007, 7819 people lived there.

It is at 51° 03 North, 04° 12 East.
