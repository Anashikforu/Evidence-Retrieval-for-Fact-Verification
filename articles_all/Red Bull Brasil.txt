
'''Red Bull Brasil''', also known as '''RB Brasil''', is a Brazilian association football club from Campinas, São Paulo (state)|São Paulo. The club was founded in 2007 by initiative of energy drink-maker Red Bull GmbH. Red Bull Brasil plays in the top tier of Campeonato Paulista, the São Paulo (state)|São Paulo state league. In 2017, Red Bull Brasil makes their second appearance in the fourth tier of Campeonato Brasileiro (the first was in 2015).
