
'''''Post''''' is an album by Iceland|Icelandic Rock and roll|rock singer Björk. She sent it out on June of 1995. It includes techno and dance music|dance songs.

Post was one of the most popular albums of Björk. It was ranked #26 in Spin (magazine)|''Spin'' magazine's "100 Greatest Albums, 1985-2005".
