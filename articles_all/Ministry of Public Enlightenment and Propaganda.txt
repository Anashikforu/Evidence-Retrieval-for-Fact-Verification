
The '''Reich Ministry for Public Enlightenment and Propaganda''' (German language|German: ''Reichsministerium für Volksaufklärung und Propaganda'' or ''Propagandaministerium'') was a Ministry (government department)|ministry in Weimar Germany and  Nazi Germany that wikt:enforce|enforced Nazi Party Nazism|ideology in Germany. It :wikt:regulated|regulated its culture and society. The ministry was founded on 13 March 1933, by Adolf Hitler's new National Socialist government. The head of this ministry was Dr. Joseph Goebbels. He was responsible for controlling the press and culture of  Weimar Republic and Nazi Germany.

This ministry existed until 1945, when the WWII|Second World War ended.
