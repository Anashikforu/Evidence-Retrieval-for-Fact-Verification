'''''Sonic the Hedgehog''''' is a Japanese platform game|platform video game series and media franchise created by Sega and featuring their mascot, Sonic the Hedgehog (character)|Sonic. It is a series of fast platform games. Sonic is the main character. He is a blue hedgehog with Supersonic|supersonic speed. Usually, Sonic must stop the villain, Doctor Eggman|Doctor Eggman's, plans for taking over the world. Sonic is often helped by his friends like Sonic the Hedgehog#Other Sonic characters|Tails, Amy Rose, and Knuckles.

The Sonic the Hedgehog (1991 video game)|first ''Sonic the Hedgehog'' game was released in 1991. It was created by Sega's Sonic Team division after Sega wanted a new mascot character. This was because they wanted to replace Alex Kidd, and they wanted to compete with Nintendo's mascot, Mario. Its success made many sequels, and it helped Sega become one of the biggest video game companies during the History of video game consoles (fourth generation)|16-bit era of the early 1990s. The first major 3D ''Sonic'' game, ''Sonic Adventure'', was released in 1998 for the Dreamcast.
