'''''Final Fantasy X''''' is a fantasy role-playing game|role-playing video game made by Square Enix. It is the 10th game in the ''Final Fantasy'' video game series. The game was released in 2001, and is the first ''Final Fantasy'' game that was made for the Sony PlayStation 2 video game console.
