
'''Phil Knight''' is an Americans|American businessman. He was one of the first owners of Nike, Inc. with Bill Bowerman. In 2008, he had a net worth of US$ 10.5 billion and was the 31st richest person in the United States. Phil Knight also ran Track and field athletics|track in high school and college.  Bowerman was his coach at the University of Oregon.
