'''London Luton Airport'''  (previously called '''Luton International Airport''') north of London.  The airport is 2 miles (3&nbsp;km) from junction 10a of the M1 motorway. It is the fourth largest airport serving the London area after London Heathrow Airport|Heathrow, London Gatwick Airport|Gatwick and London Stansted Airport|Stansted and is one of London's five international airports along with London City Airport.

In 2007, total passengers at London Luton increased by 5.3% to 9,927,321 
London Luton Airport is widely known as a result of the Airline (UK TV series)|''Airline'' and Luton Airport (TV series)|''Luton Airport'' television series.
