

'''Sibiu''' (; Hungarian language|Hungarian: ''Szeben'') is a county (judeţ) of Romania, in the old area of Transylvania. The capital of Sibiu County is Sibiu.

 Geography 
Sibiu County has an area of 5,432 square kilometers.

 Divisions 
Sibiu County has 2 municipalities, 9 towns and 53 communes.
