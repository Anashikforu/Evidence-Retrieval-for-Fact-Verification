
'''Liu Xing''' is a China|Chinese hurdling athlete. He was born on 13 July 1983 in Shanghai, China. He is 189&nbsp;cm tall and weighs 74&nbsp;kg. He is an Olympic gold medal winner. Liu broke the World Junior and Asian record for hurdles in 2002.
