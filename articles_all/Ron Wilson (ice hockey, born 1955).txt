
'''Ronald Lawrence Wilson''' (born May 28, 1955) is a former ice hockey player. He was the head coach of the Toronto Maple Leafs from 2008 to 2012. He also was the coach for the San Jose Sharks, Washington Capitals, Vancouver Canucks, and Anaheim Ducks|Anaheim Mighty Ducks of the National Hockey League|NHL. Wilson was born in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. He is a citizen of both Canada and the United States.
