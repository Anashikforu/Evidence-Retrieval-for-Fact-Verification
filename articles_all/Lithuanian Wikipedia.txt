

The '''Lithuanian Wikipedia''' is the Lithuanian language edition of Wikipedia. This edition was started in 2003.

As of October 2015, it is the 40th largest edition of Wikipedia by number of articles.meta:List of Wikipedias|Wikimedia list of Wikipedias and their statistics.. Retrieved 25 October 2015.

It currently has over 128,476 articles.:lt:Specialus:Statistika|Statistics at Lithuanian Wikipedia
