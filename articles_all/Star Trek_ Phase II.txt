
'''''Star Trek: Phase II''''' was a planned television series based on the characters of Gene Roddenberry's ''Star Trek: The Original Series|Star Trek'', which had run from 1966 to 1969. It was set to air in early 1978 on a proposed Paramount Television Service (a precursor/forerunner to UPN). The series was to follow the adventures of the ''Starship Enterprise|Enterprise'' crew on a second five-year mission, and be a continuation of the ''Star Trek'' franchise.
