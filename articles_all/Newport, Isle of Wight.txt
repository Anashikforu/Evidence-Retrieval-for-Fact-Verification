
'''Newport''' is the county town of the Isle of Wight, an island off the south coast of England.  Newport has a population of 23,957 according to the United Kingdom Census 2001|2001 census.  The town is slightly to the north of the centre of the island, at the head of the navigable section of the River Medina, which flows northward to the Solent, and on which the town has a quay.
