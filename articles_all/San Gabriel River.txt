The '''San Gabriel River''' is a river in Los Angeles County|Los Angeles and Orange County, California|Orange counties in the state of California. It is about  long. It begins in the San Gabriel Mountains and flows southwest into the Los Angeles Basin, then flows into the Pacific Ocean near Long Beach, California|Long Beach and Seal Beach, California|Seal Beach. Like the Los Angeles River, it is heavily polluted and is lined with concrete in many places.
