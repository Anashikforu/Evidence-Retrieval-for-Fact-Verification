The British Rail '''Class 460''' (or SR multiple unit numbering and classification|8Gat) electrical multiple units were built by Alstom at Washwood Heath between 1999 and 2001. These units were part of Alstom's "Juniper (train)|Juniper" family of units, which also includes Classes British Rail Class 334|334 and British Rail Class 458|458.

All have since been converted to British Rail Class 458|Class 458/5 units (other than four surplus driving vehicles used as spares donors) for operation by South Western Railway (train operating company)|South Western Railway.
