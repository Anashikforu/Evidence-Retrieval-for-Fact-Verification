
'''Misa Telefoni Retzlaff''' (born Hermann Theodor Retzlaff, 21 May 1952) is the Deputy Prime Minister of Samoa. He is of German people|German-Samoan descent.

Retzlaff went to school at King's College, Auckland|King's College in Auckland, New Zealand.

In February 2011 Telefoni said he was retiring from politics at the 2011 election.
