'''Mandi Bahauddin District''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Mandi Bahauddin is the capital.

The district has three tehsils, these are:

 Malakwal Tehsil
 Mandi Bahauddin Tehsil
 Phalia Tehsil
