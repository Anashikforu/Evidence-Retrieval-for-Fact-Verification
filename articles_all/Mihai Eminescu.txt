


'''Mihai Eminescu''' (pronunciation in Romanian language|Romanian: ) (January 15, 1850 – June 15, 1889), born ''Mihail Eminovici'', was a late Romanticism|Romantic poet, the best-known and most influential Romanian language|Romanian poet. Famous poems include ''Luceafărul (poem)|Luceafărul'' ("Evening Star"), ''Odă în metru antic'' (''Ode in ancient meter''), and the five ''Scrisori'' (''Epistles/Satires''). Eminescu was active in the ''Junimea'' literary society, and served as editor of ''Timpul'', the official newspaper of the Conservative Party (Romania, 1880-1918)|Conservative Party.
