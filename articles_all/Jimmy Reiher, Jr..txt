
'''James William Reiher, Jr.''', (born September 1, 1971) better known as '''Jimmy Snuka, Jr.''', is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler and the son of professional wrestler Jimmy Snuka|"Superfly" Jimmy Snuka. He is probably best known for wrestling with World Wrestling Entertainment where he worked on its WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brand under the ring name '''Deuce'''. He is a former WWE Tag Team Championship|WWE Tag Team Champion with his former partner Cliff Compton|Domino.
