'''Heatseekers Albums''' (formerly '''Top Heatseekers''') is a weekly music chart for albums introduced by ''Billboard (magazine)|Billboard'' in 1993. It was created to Publishing|publish sales by new and developing musical recording artists.  Albums that appear on Top Heatseekers may also appear on the Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200 at the same time.

Top Heatseekers contains fifty chart positions."
