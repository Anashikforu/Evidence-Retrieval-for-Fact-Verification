

'''Timothy James "Tim" Curry''' (19 April 1946), is an English people|English actor and singer. He is well known for his roles in ''The Rocky Horror Picture Show'', ''Clue (movie)|Clue'', ''It (miniseries)|It'', ''Home Alone 2: Lost in New York'' and ''The Three Musketeers (1993 movie)|The Three Musketeers''. He has released three albums.

Personal life and stroke
Curry resides in Toluca Lake, Los Angeles|Toluca Lake, Los Angeles, California.
In July 2012, Curry suffered a major stroke.
