
'''D'Anjou''' (also known as the '''poire d'Anjou''' or '''Beurr√© d'Anjou''') is a type fruit in the family (biology)|family Rosaceae. It is a type of pear that first came from Anjou,France. D'Anjou pears usually cost less than other types of pears, but are not as Sweet (taste)|sweet. There are two main varieties of D'Anjou:

'''Green Anjou''' has a green skin that may turn yellow as the pear ripens.
'''Red Anjou''' has a red skin that darkens as it ripens.
