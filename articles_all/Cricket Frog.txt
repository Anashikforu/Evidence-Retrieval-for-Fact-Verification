



The '''cricket frog''', genus ''Acris'', is a small small, North American  frog of the family Hylidae. They stay more in water than other members of the family and generally live near permanent bodies of water with surface vegetation. The common name|common and scientific names refer to their call, which resembles that of a cricket (insect)|cricket.
