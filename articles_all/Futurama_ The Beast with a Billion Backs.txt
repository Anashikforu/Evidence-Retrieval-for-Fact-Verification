

'''''Futurama: The Beast with a Billion Backs''''' is an animation|animated movie. It's the sequel to ''Futurama: Bender's Big Score'', and is the second ''Futurama'' movie. It takes place right after the events of ''Bender's Big Score'', and is about a giant monster who is in love with the entire universe. It has two sequels that were released, the first called ''Futurama: Bender's Game'', and then ''Futurama: Into the Wild Green Yonder''.

 Plot summary 

Everyone is worried and afraid of the rift in space that was opened at the end of ''Bender's Big Score''.
