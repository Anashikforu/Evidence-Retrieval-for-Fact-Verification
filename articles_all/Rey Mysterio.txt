
'''Óscar Gutiérrez''' (born December 11, 1974) better known by his ring name '''Rey Mysterio''', is an Mexican American|Mexican-American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is currently working for Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA). He is best known for his time in the WWE. He wrestled for Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) from 1995 to 1996, World Championship Wrestling (WCW) from 1996 to 2001, and the WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment/WWE from 2002 to 2015. Gutierrez is a devout Roman Catholic and crosses himself before every match.

On February 26, 2015, Mysterio's contract with the WWE ended and he left after 13 years with the company.

AAA Return (2015–present)
On March 3, 2015, it was announced that Mysterio resigned with Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA).

On March 20, 2015, Mysterio teamed with Tigre Uno|Extreme Tiger to face El Hijo del Perro Aguayo and T.J. Perkins|Manik. In the match, Mysterio used a Professional wrestling throws#Headscissors takedown|headscissors takedown to throw Aguayo out of the ring. When Aguayo got back into the ring, Mysterio dropkicked him in the head to set up for his finisher, the Professional wrestling attacks#Tiger feint kick|619 which caused Aguayo to land on the middle rope. Aguayo appeared to be limp while hanging on the middle rope. The match was finished when Mysterio pinned Manik. Aguayo was later brought out of the ring and was rushed to the Del Prado hospital. He was pronounced dead around 1:00am on March 21.
