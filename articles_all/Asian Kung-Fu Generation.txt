
'''Asian Kung-Fu Generation''' (アジアン・カンフー・ジェネレーション, Ajian Kanfū Jenerēshon) is a J-pop|Japanese pop-rock music|rock band signed by Ki/oon Records, a subsidiary of Sony Music Entertainment Japan. They performed the second opening for the anime Naruto.They are called "AKG" or "AKFG."

 Members 
Masahumi Gotoh: singer|vocals and guitar
Kensuke Kita: guitar and vocals
Takahiro Yamada: bass guitar|bass and vocals
Kiyoshi Idachi: drums
