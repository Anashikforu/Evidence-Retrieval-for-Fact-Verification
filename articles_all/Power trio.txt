The '''power trio''' is a popular type of a rock and roll band format since the 1960s. The usual power trio has three people playing the guitar, bass guitar|bass, and drums. It does not include the rhythm guitar or Keyboard instrument|keyboard often included in other rock music. In more recent years, the term has come to mean any type of a three-person band.
