'''Southampton Central railway station''' is the main train station in Southampton. It is served by First Great Western, South West Trains, CrossCountry & Southern (train operating company)|Southern.Butt, R.V.J. (1995). ''The Directory of Railway Stations'', Patrick Stephens Ltd, Sparkford, , p. 214.

It has services terminating for Portsmouth and Southsea, Great Malvern, London Waterloo station|London Waterloo, Newcastle upon Tyne|Newcastle and other services.
