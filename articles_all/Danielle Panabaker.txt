
'''Danielle Nicole Panabaker''' (born September 19, 1987) is an Americans|American actor|actress. She is known for acting in many movies. These include ''Sky High'' and ''Mom at Sixteen''. She has also acted on the television series including ''Shark (TV series)|Shark''. Panabaker is the older sister of child actress Kay Panabaker|Kay. Both girls act in Disney Channel television programme|programs.
