
'''Porno Graffitti''' is a Japanese J-Pop band. The members of the band are Akihito Okano (singer|vocals) and Haruichi Shindo (guitar). The band is from Innoshima in Hiroshima. The band first started playing in 1999 with three members. The third member, Tama, left the band in 2004. The band has become one of the duos leading the music in Japan.
