
'''Manuel Friedrich''' (born September 13, 1979 in Bad Kreuznach) is a Germany|German footballer with the position of the Defender. He has played for the team 1.FSV Mainz 05 and at the moment he playes for Bayer Leverkusen. Friedrich has played nine matches for the German national football team since 2006. During that time he has scored one wikt:goal|goal.
