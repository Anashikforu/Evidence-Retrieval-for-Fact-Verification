
 

The '''Olt River''' (Romanian language|Romanian and Hungarian language|Hungarian; German language|German: ''Alt''; Latin: ''Aluta'' or ''Alutus'') is a river in Romania. It flows in the Romanian counties Harghita County|Harghita, Covasna County|Covasna, Braşov County|Braşov, Sibiu County|Sibiu, Vâlcea County|Vâlcea and Olt County|Olt. The river was known as ''Alutus'' or ''Aluta'' in Roman Empire|Roman times.
