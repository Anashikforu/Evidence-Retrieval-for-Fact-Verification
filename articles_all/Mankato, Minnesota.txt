
'''Mankato''' is a city in Blue Earth County, Minnesota|Blue Earth and Nicollet County, Minnesota|Nicollet counties of Minnesota in the United States.  It is the county seat of Blue Earth County.  About 32,400 people were living in Mankato as of 2000.  It can be found by the Minnesota River where it meets up with the Blue Earth River.  It has a sister city called North Mankato, Minnesota|North Mankato.  The city of Skyline, Minnesota|Skyline can be found inside of Mankato.
