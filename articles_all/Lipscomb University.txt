'''Lipscomb University''' is a private, coeducational university in Nashville, Tennessee|Nashville, Tennessee. The university has about 3,742 students. It was founded in 1891. The current president of the university is L. Randolph Lowry III.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Bisons and they compete in the Atlantic Sun Conference.
