
'''Alexander Mikhaylovich Ovechkin''' (born September 17, 1985) is a Russians|Russian professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|left winger. He plays for the Washington Capitals of the National Hockey League (NHL) and serves as their Captain (ice hockey)|captain.

Ovechkin was picked first overall in the 2004 NHL Entry Draft. He also played for the HC Dynamo Moscow|Dynamo Moscow of the Russian Superleague|RSL from 2001 to 2005 and again in 2012 because of the 2012-13 NHL lockout.
