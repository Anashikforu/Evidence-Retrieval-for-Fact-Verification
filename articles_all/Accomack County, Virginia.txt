
'''Accomack County''', which used to be called Accomac Shire, is a County (United States)|U.S. county in the Virginia|Commonwealth of Virginia. The United States Census, 2000|2000 census said that 38,305 lived there.  Its county seat is Accomac, Virginia|Accomac.

Accomack and Northampton County, Virginia|Northampton Counties comprise the Eastern Shore of Virginia, part of the Delmarva Peninsula.
