'''Air Europa''' is a Spanish airline based on the Balearic Islands|Balearic island of Mallorca. It operates flights within Spain but also to other places in Europe and further away, it concentrates on the holiday market.

 Destinations 

Air Europa flies to destinations in Europe, Africa and both North America|North and South America.

 Fleet 
The Air Europa fleet is:

6x Airbus A330-200
26x Boeing 737-800
2x Boeing 767-300ER
6x Embraer E-Jets|Embraer E-195
