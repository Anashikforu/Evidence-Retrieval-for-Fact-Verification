

'''Jackson''' is an United States|American city in the U.S. state|state of Wyoming. It is in Teton County, Wyoming|Teton County. The current mayor of Jackson is Mark Barron. As of 2000, 8,647 people lived there. Many tourists go through Jackson when they visit the nearby Grand Teton National Park, Yellowstone National Park, and the National Elk Refuge.
