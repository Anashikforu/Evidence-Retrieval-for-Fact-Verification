

'''Patrick "The Little Ball of Hate" Verbeek''' (born May 24, 1964 in Sarnia, Ontario) is a former Canada|Canadian National Hockey League|NHL ice hockey right winger who played 21 seasons in the NHL.

He was given the nickname "The Little Ball of Hate" by Glenn Healy because teammate Ray Ferraro was nicknamed "Big Ball of Hate".
