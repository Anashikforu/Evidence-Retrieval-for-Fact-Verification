'''Scooby-Doo''' is the main character in the long running series ''Scooby-Doo''. He is a speaking dog that has a very sweet tooth. His best friend is Shaggy Rogers (Scooby-Doo)|Shaggy.

Portrayals
Scooby was first voiced by Don Messick before being replaced by Neil Fanning and Frank Welker
