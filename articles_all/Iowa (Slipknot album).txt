
'''''Iowa''''' is the second studio album by American heavy metal music|heavy metal band Slipknot. It was released on August 28, 2001. The producers were Ross Robinson and the band itself. The album's title came from the band's home state Iowa. single (music)|Singles include "Left Behind" and "My Plague".
