
'''Watford''' (pronounced ''wot-fud'') is a large town and borough in Hertfordshire in the East of England. 

In 2011, it had a population of 131,928 people, making it Hertfordshire's largest settlement. Despite being Hertfordshire's largest town, the county town is Hertford. 

Watford is located 17.5 miles north west of Central London and 16.7 miles (27.02 km) south west of Hertford and is on the banks of the River Colne, Hertfordshire|River Colne.

Transport
Watford is one of the few places that are not in London to have a London Underground station and to be on the ''Tube Map''. Watford has four mainline train stations, Watford Junction railway station|Watford Junction, Watford High Street railway station|Watford High Street, Watford North railway station|Watford North and Garston railway station|Garston, as well as the Watford tube station|tube station.

Watford Junction railway station|Watford Junction is on the West Coast Main Line between Euston railway station|London Euston and Glasgow Central railway station|Glasgow Central and is also on the London Overground Watford DC line to Euston railway station|London Euston. Watford High Street railway station|Watford High Street is also on this line. Watford North railway station|Watford North and Garston railway station (Hertfordshire)|Garston are on the Abbey Line to St Albans Abbey railway station|St Albans Abbey. Watford tube station is on the Metropolitan line.

Sports
Watford has a professional association football|football club, Watford F.C.|Watford FC, who have played in the Premier League. They play their home matches at Vicarage Road Stadium.
