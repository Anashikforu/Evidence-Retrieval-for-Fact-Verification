
According to the New Testament, the '''Ascension of Jesus''' happened at the end of Jesus' life on earth.  The Acts of the Apostles says that after Jesus told his Twelve Apostles|apostles to teach the gospel to everyone, he went up to heaven to be with God#God in Christianity|God. 

Ascension Day is often celebrated on a Thursday, the fortieth day of Easter, although some Christian denominations have moved the observance to the following Sunday.
