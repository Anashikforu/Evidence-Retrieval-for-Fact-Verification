
'''Iragna''' is a former municipality of the district Riviera (district)|Riviera in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 2 April 2017 the former municipalities of Cresciano, Iragna, Lodrino, Switzerland|Lodrino and Osogna merged into the new municipality of Riviera, Ticino|Riviera.
