
'''Limburg-Weilburg''' is a Landkreis (rural districts of Germany|rural district) in the west of Hesse, Germany.
 History 
1867 the ''Oberlahnkreis'' with the capital Weilburg was created. In 1886 ''Limburg an der Lahn'' district was set up, with the capital Limburg an der Lahn|Limburg. In 1974 both districts were joined together.

 Geography 
The district is located between the two mountain areas Westerwald and Taunus. The main river is the Lahn.
