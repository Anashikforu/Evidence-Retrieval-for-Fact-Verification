'''Veh''' ('''В, в''') is the third letter of the Cyrillic alphabet. It is said as v.

It comes from the Greek alphabet|Greek Beta (letter)|beta, along with the Cyrillic Be (Cyrillic)|beh. Its old name was ''vede''. Although it looks like a B, it stands for the Roman alphabet|Roman V and Beh (Б, б) stands for B.
