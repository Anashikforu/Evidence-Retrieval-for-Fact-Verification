


The '''England football team''' are a team who play football for England. They play their matches in England at Wembley Stadium. 

They played their first game in 1872 against Scotland football team|Scotland.

They won the FIFA World Cup in 1966 when they beat West Germany by 4 goals to 2 at the Wembley Stadium (1923)|old Wembley Stadium.
