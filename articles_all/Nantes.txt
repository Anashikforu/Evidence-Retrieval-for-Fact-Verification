'''Nantes''' is a city in France, the Prefectures in France|prefecture of the Pays de la Loire Regions of France|region and the Loire-Atlantique Departments of France|department, on the Atlantic Ocean. From the 16th century|16th to the 19th century|19th centuries, it was the busiest Slave trade|slave trading port in France. Before 1941, Nantes was part of Brittany. The Gallo language|Gallo and Breton language|Breton languages are spoken in the city. Jules Verne was a famous writer from Nantes.

Nantes has an oceanic climate (''Cfb'' in the Koeppen climate classification).
