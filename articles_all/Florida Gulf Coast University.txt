'''Florida Gulf Coast University''' is a public, coeducational university in Fort Myers, Florida|Fort Myers, Florida. The university has about 12,024 students. It was founded in 1991. The current president of the university is Wilson G. Bradshaw.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Eagles and they have 15 varsity teams. They compete in the Atlantic Sun Conference.
