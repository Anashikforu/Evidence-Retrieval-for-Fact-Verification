Biography
Watanabe was born on April 5, 1954. After graduating from Sendai University, he joined Japan Soccer League club Sanfrecce Hiroshima|Toyo Industries (later ''Mazda'') in 1977. The club won the 2nd place in 1978 Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1982. He played 81 games and scored 3 goals in the league.

On June 16, 1979, Watanabe debuted for Japan national football team|Japan national team against South Korea national football team|South Korea. He played 6 games and scored 1 goal for Japan in 1979.
