

'''''Fairly Legal''''' was an United States|American legal drama television series that was created by Michael Sardo. Fairly Legal debuted on January 20, 2011 on the USA Network. Filming for the show takes place in Vancouver. The story is about a woman named Kate Reed who is a former lawyer turned mediator. She works at the San Francisco, California|San Francisco law firm that her father has started and left her and the law firm to deal with his death.
