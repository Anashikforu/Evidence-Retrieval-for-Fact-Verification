


'''Orion's Belt''' or '''Belt of Orion''' is belt of 3 stars in one line in the constellation Orion (constellation)|Orion. Ancient Arabic names of these 3 stars are Alnitak, Alnilam and Mintaka. These 3 stars are named by astronomers as Zeta Orionis|ζ Ori (Alnitak), Epsilon Orionis|ε Ori (Alnilam), and Delta Orionis|δ Ori (Mintaka). These 3 stars are best visible in the night sky during the month of January.
