
'''Ahmed Mohamed Mahamoud Silanyo''' ) (born 1936) is a Somaliland politician. He is the current Chairman of the Peace, Unity and Development Party (Kulmiye) and President of Somaliland|President of Somaliland. Standing as an opposition candidate, Silanyo was elected as President during the Somaliland presidential election, 2010|June 2010 presidential election.
