
'''Rutland County''' is a county in the U.S. state of Vermont. In 2005, 63,743 people lived there.  Its county seat is Rutland (city), Vermont|Rutland. The United States Census Bureau|U.S. Census Bureau says that the county has 945&nbsp;square miles (2,447&nbsp;km²) of total area. Out of that, 933&nbsp;square miles (2,415&nbsp;km²) is land and 12&nbsp;square miles (32&nbsp;km²) is water.
