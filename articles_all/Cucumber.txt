
The '''cucumber''' (''Cucumis sativus'') is a widely grown plant in the family Cucurbitaceae. This family also includes squash (fruit)|squash. A cucumber looks similar to a zucchini (British English: courgette). 

To a botanist, cucumbers are biologically fruits even if they are commonly thought of as vegetables: that is their value as a food. They can be cooked, eaten raw, or Pickled cucumber|pickled. Some people hollow out the cucumber and fill it with other food, like Tomato|tomatoes or feta cheese.
