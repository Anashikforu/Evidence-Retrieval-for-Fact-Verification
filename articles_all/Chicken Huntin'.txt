

"'''Chicken Huntin''''" is a 1995 midwest Hip hop music|hip hop song by the United States|American Horrorcore band Insane Clown Posse and is the first single from their third studio album ''Riddle Box'', which was released in 1995. The song was produced by Insane Clown Posse and Mike E. Clark|Mike Clark.

The song is about meeting and killing multiple racist rednecks, who the band referred to as 'Chickens'.
