
'''Strontium-90''' is a radioactive decay|radioactive isotope of strontium. It has a Half-life (element)|half life of roughly 29 years. Strontium-90 and strontium-89 have medical applications. They are used for radiation therapy,  in the treatment of certain bone cancers. Because its application generates a lot of heat it is sometimes used instead of Plutonium in nuclear energy|nuclear power plants.
