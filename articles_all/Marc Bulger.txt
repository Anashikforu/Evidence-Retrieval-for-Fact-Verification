
'''Marc Robert Bulger''' () (born April 5, 1977 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania|Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania) is the starting quarterback for the Baltimore Ravens in the National Football League. He was voted Most Valuable Player of the 2004 Pro Bowl.
