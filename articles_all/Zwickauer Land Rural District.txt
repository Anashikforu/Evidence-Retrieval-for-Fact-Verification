

'''Zwickauer Land''' was a Landkreis (rural districts in Saxony|district) in the south-west of the Saxony|Free State of Saxony, Germany. The independent city of Zwickau is in the middle of the district. It is nearly completely surrounded by the district. In July 2008, the city lost its right to administration|administer itself, and become a city under the control of the district government.

The rural district was created on 1 August 1994. Its capital (city)|capital is Werdau. On January 1, 1999, the district was reorganised. Cainsdorf, Mosel, Oberrothenbach and Schlunzig were made a part of to Zwickau even though the people living in those towns wanted to stay independent.
