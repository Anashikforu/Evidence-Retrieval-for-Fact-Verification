"'''Empire State of Mind'''" is a song by Jay-Z and Alicia Keys. It was released as the third single from Jay-Z's 11th music  album ''The Blueprint 3'' (2009). The song is about New York City, and has a music Sampling (music)|sample of "Love on a Two-Way Street" (1970) by Ray, Goodman & Brown|The Moments. The title of the song takes its name from Billy Joel's "New York State of Mind" (1976).
