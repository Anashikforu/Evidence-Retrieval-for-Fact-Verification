
'''Alexander Emmanuel Rodriguez''' (born July 27, 1975 in New York City, New York (state)|New York). In the past, he played as shortstop for the Seattle Mariners and Texas Rangers (baseball)|Texas Rangers. 
 
He is currently first in run (baseball)|runs scored and total bases; second in extra base hits and RBI, and 4th in Hit (baseball)|hits among all players in baseball history since their 31st birthday. Also, Rodriguez has more HR, RBI, runs scored and more base hits than all-time leaders Hank Aaron (RBI), Barry Bonds (HR), Rickey Henderson (runs scored), and Pete Rose (hits) did before their 31st birthdays.

He has been said to be one of the best all-around players currently in baseball. 

In 2009, he admitted to using steroids from 2001-2003, during his time with the Texas Rangers (baseball)|Texas Rangers, in order to become better at baseball.

His playing career ended in August of 2016.
