
Image:SS Yongala wreck.jpg|right|thumb|180px|A shipping buoy marks the site of the SS ''Yongala''
'''SS ''Yongala''''' was a passenger ship that was launched in 1903. It sank off Cape Bowling Green on 23 March 1911 on its way to from Melbourne, Australia to Cairns she entered into a cyclone and sank without a trace south of Townsville.
