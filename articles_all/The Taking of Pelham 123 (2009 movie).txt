

'''''The Taking of Pelham 123''''' is a 2009 United States|American crime movie|crime Thriller (genre)|thriller movie. It was Movie director|directed by Tony Scott and stars Denzel Washington and John Travolta. It is based on the novel of the same name written by Morton Freedgood (writing under the pseudonym John Godey) and 1974 movie ''The Taking of Pelham One Two Three (1974 movie)|The Taking of Pelham One Two Three''. It is set in New York City.
