
The '''Arabic Wikipedia''' () is the Arabic language version of Wikipedia. It started in September 2001.

It was the 23rd largest edition of Wikipedia by article count, but grew to 17th largest edition.meta:List of Wikipedias|Wikimedia list of Wikipedias and their statistics.. Retrieved 25 October 2015.m:List of Wikipedias#100 000.2B articles|List of Wikipedias by number of articles

As of August 2014, it has over 300,000 articles, over 1,350,000 pages, over 625,000 registered users and over 15,000 files.:ar:خاص:إحصاءات|ar:Staticstics

It has been blocked in Syria.
