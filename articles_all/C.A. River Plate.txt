'''Club Atlético River Plate''', commonly known as '''River Plate''', is an Argentine professional Association football|football club. They are based in Buenos Aires, and play their home games at Estadio Monumental. 

The name of the club is English for Río de la Plata. They are the most succcessful club in Argentina, with 36 league titles. River Plate has a very strong rivalry with Boca Juniors, mainly because both teams are from Buenos Aires and also are considered the biggest teams in Argentina. Matches between them sometimes involve violence between supporters.
