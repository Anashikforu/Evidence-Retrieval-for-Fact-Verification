The '''list of popes''' begins with St. Peter and continues through the present day.
Peter was called the Bishop of Rome.
 Chronological list of popes 
The list sometimes says ''interregnum''. This is Latin for "between reigns". It means there was no Pope; as in a Pope had passed away, and his successor hadn't yet been elected.
