
The '''Middle class''' describes people who are not Aristocracy|aristocrats nor working class or Poor|underclass. Depending on class model used, the ''middle class'' may make up anywhere from 45% to 49% of households in the United States. There is a lower middle class and an upper middle class. In Marxism, middle class is the class below the ruling class. The word ''bourgeoisie'' may also be used as a synonym of middle class.
