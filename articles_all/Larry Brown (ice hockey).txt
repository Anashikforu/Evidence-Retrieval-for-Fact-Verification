
'''Larry Wayne Brown''' (born April 14, 1947 in Brandon, Manitoba) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey Defenceman (ice hockey)|defenceman. His career included playing in the National Hockey League (NHL) with the New York Rangers, Detroit Red Wings, Philadelphia Flyers and Los Angeles Kings.

Awards
MJHL Second All-Star Team (1967)
