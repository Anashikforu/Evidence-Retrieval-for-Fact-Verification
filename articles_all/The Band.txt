
'''The Band''' was a rock music band from 1964 to 1976 and again from 1983 to 1999. The original group was made up of four Canadians (Robbie Robertson, Richard Manuel, Garth Hudson, and Rick Danko) and one United States|American, (Levon Helm).

This band was the subject of the 1978 movie The Last Waltz.
