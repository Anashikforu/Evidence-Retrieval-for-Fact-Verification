'''Hasselt''' is a city in Belgium. It is the capital city|capital of the Limburg (Belgium)|Belgian province Limburg. It lies about half way between Liège and Antwerp. The city lays at the river demer and the albertchannel. The city has city privileges since 1232. Today, about 79.089 people live there, the people who live there are called Hasselaars.Most of them are native Dutch language|Dutch speakers.

Well-known people from Hasselt include Hendrick van Veldeke, probably one of the first writers who did no longer write in Latin language|Latin, but rather used the German language|German of the time, 12th century. Willy Claes, who was NATO secretary in 1994 and 1995, and Axelle Red a contemporary singer-songwriter.

It is located at 50° 55 North, 05° 20 East.
