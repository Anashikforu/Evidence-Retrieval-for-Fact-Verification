

'''''As the World Turns''''' ('''''ATWT''''') is an United States|American television soap opera that airs each weekday on CBS.

The show went from black-and-white to color in the mid-1960s, with the final black-and-white episode shown on February 10, 1967.{{Citation

April 2, 2006 was the 50th anniversary of the soap opera on CBS. The show aired its 13,000th episode April 23, 2007; the 10,000th episode aired on May 12, 1995.  ''As the World Turns'' is well known for having been taped in New York City{{Citation
