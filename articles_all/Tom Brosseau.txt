

'''Thomas Anderson "Tom" Brosseau''' (born November 3, 1976) is an United States|American singer-songwriter and guitarist. 

 Life 
He was born and grew up in Grand Forks, North Dakota|Grand Forks, North Dakota. His song "How to Grow a Woman from the Ground" was cover version|covered by Chris Thile, who released a 2006 How to Grow a Woman from the Ground|album of the same name.

Right now, Brosseau is touring the United States playing his new album, ''Cavalier (album)|Cavalier''.
