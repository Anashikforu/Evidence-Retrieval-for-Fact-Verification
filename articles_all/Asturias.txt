

The '''Principality of Asturias''' () is an autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community. It is in the kingdom of Spain or the former Kingdom of Asturias in the Middle Ages. It is on the Spanish north coast facing the Cantabrian Sea. The capital of the province is Oviedo. Gijón is the biggest city. There is an Asturias Airport|airport in Asturias, from where you can fly to a few places in Spain and in Europe. The region has borders with Galicia, León (province)|León and Cantabria.
