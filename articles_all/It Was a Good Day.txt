
'''"It Was a Good Day"''' is a 1993 single (music)|single by United States|American rap|rapper Ice Cube. It was released from the album (music)|album ''The Predator''. "It Was a Good Day" peaked at number seven on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs|''Billboard'' Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs and number fifteen on the Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100.
