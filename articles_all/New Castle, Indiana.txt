


'''New Castle''' is a city in Henry County, Indiana|Henry County, Indiana, 44 miles (71&nbsp;km) east of Indianapolis, Indiana|Indianapolis, on the Big Blue River (Indiana)|Big Blue River. In 1900, 3,406 people lived in the town; in 1910, 9,446; in 1920, 14,458; and in 1940, 16,620. The population was 17,780 in 2000. The city is the county seat of Henry County, Indiana|Henry County. New Castle is home to New Castle Fieldhouse, the largest high school  in the world.
