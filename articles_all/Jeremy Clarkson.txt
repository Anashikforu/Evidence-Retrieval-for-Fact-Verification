'''Jeremy Charles Robert Clarkson''' (born 11 April 1960) is an English people|English author, broadcaster, journalist, talk show host. He is best known for presenting BBC television show ''Top Gear (TV series)|Top Gear'' with Richard Hammond and James May. He is also known for controversy|controversial remarks.

He also writes columns for The Sunday Times and The Sun (United Kingdom)|The Sun.

Clarkson was ranked 49th on Motor Trend Magazine's Power List for 2011. It is a list of the fifty most important figures in the automotive industry.

On 26 March 2015, while Clarkson was not allowed to be at work, the BBC announced it will not keep paying him after he said cruel things to and attacked a producer.
