'''Thomas Wedgwood''' (1685-1739) was an england|English potter. He was a member of the Wedgwood family, who were very successful potters.  He was the son of Thomas Wedgwood II and Mary Leigh.

Thomas Wedgwood was married to Mary Stringer.  Their children were:

Thomas Wedgwood IV
Josiah Wedgwood
