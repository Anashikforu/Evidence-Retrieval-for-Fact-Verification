
'''''The Border''''' is a 1982 United States|American crime movie|crime drama movie by movie director|director Tony Richardson. It stars Jack Nicholson, Warren Oates and Harvey Keitel.

The movie is set in Texas and is about a group of US Border Patrol agents who look after the border between the US and Mexico.
