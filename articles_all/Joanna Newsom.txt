
'''Joanna Caroline Newsom''' is an indie (music)|indie singer and a harpist. She was born in Nevada City, California in 1982.

Early life
Newsom was not allowed to watch television or listen to the radio when she was a child. She went to a Waldorf education|Waldorf school. After high school Newsom studied composition and creative writing at Mills College in Oakland, California.

Career
Newsom's first album ''The Milk-Eyed Mender'' was released by Drag City on the 23rd of February 2003. Pitchfork gave the album a score of 8 out of 10.

Personal life
She married comedian Andy Samberg in September 2013.
