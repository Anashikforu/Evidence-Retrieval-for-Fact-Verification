

'''''Advance Wars: Days of Ruin''''', known in PAL regions as '''''Advance Wars: Dark Conflict''''' and in Japan as '''''Famicom Wars DS 2''''', is a turn-based tactics video game made by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo DS game console.George Jones, "''Advance Wars: Days of Ruin'': New features, multiplayer, and a dark story line make this sequel shine," ''GamePro'' 235 (April 08): 78. It's a lot different from other games in the series, which are cartoony, while this is gritty and realistic.
