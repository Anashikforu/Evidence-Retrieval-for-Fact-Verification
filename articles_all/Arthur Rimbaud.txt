
'''Jean Nicolas Arthur Rimbaud'''  (20 October 1854 – 10 November 1891) was a French people|French poet, born in Charleville-Mézières|Charleville. He wrote his best works when he was 15–18 years old.  He was wikt:restless|restless and moved around the world a lot. He was homosexual. He died of bone cancer just after his 37th birthday.
