

'''Rachel Hannah Weisz''' (born 7 March 1970) is an Academy Award-winning English people|English actress; she is also a fashion model. She became famous after her roles in the Hollywood movies ''The Mummy (1999 movie)|The Mummy'' and ''The Mummy Returns'', and has since continued appearing in major movie roles.

She is of Jewish descent.
