'''Food processing''' means the ways that are used to change raw wikt:ingredient|ingredients into food so that they can be eaten by humans or animals. The food processing industry uses these processes. Food processing often takes clean, harvested or slaughtered and butchered wikt:component|components and uses these to produce attractive and marketing|marketable food products. Similar process are used to produce animal feed.

 Industries 
Food processing industries and practices include the following:
 Cannery
 Cooking
 Industrial rendering
 Meat packing plant
 Slaughterhouse
 Sugar#Production|Sugar industry
 Vegetable packing plant
