The '''Rump Parliament''' was the name given to Parliament until 1660 of the Parliament of England|English Parliament that followed the Long Parliament, after Pride's Purge of December 6 1648 had removed those Members of Parliament that were against the plan to try King Charles I of England|Charles I for high treason.
