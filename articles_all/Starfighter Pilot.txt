


'''''Starfighter Pilot''''' was the debut, or first, Extended play|EP from the alternative rock band Snow Patrol, and was finished in 1997. It was released on the Electric Honey (label)|Electric Honey record label.  The song "Starfighter Pilot" was also put on the band's first full-length album, ''Songs for Polarbears''.
