

'''Clyde Best''' is a former Association football|football player. He has played for Bermuda national football team|Bermuda national team.

He was awarded an Order of the British Empire.

 Biography 
When Clyde Best was 16, he moved from Bermuda to England.

He played for West Ham United F.C.

Best made his first goal on 3 September 1969. He had 218 games and 58 goals with West Ham.

Then he played in the Netherlands|Dutch Eredivisie for the Feyenoord. Lated he joined some United States|American teams (Tampa Bay Rowdies, Toronto Blizzard and Portland Timbers).
