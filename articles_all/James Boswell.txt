
'''James Boswell''' (October 29, 1740 - May 19, 1795) was a Scotland|Scottish lawyer and author. He is best known for having written the Life of Johnson, a biography of his close friend Samuel Johnson, published in 1791. In the 1920s and 1930s, a large number of his personal papers and Diary|journals were discovered. They have since then been published by Yale University.
