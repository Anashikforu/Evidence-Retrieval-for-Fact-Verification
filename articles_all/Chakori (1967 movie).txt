


'''''Chakori''''' is a Pakistani Urdu movie. This movie was released on March 23, 1967, on Eid al-Adha|Eid-ul-Azha Day in Dacca, Bangladesh#History|East Pakistan (now Bangladesh). The movie was movie director|directed by Captain Ehtisham. It was Nadeem Baig's and Shabana's debut movie.
