

'''Wesley Trent Snipes''' (born July 31, 1962) is an Americans|American actor, martial arts|martial artist and movie producer. He is best known for his role as the vampire hunting|hunter in the ''Blade (movie)|Blade'' trilogy movies.

Snipes was born in Orlando, Florida and spent most of his childhood in The Bronx, New York City. He moved back to Orlando when he was a teenager.
