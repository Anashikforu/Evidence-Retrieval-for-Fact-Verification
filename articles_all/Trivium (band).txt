

'''Trivium''' is a Heavy metal music|heavy metal band from Orlando, Florida. They started in 1999 and became very successful. The members are: Matt Heafy (vocals, guitars), Corey Beaulieu (guitars), Paolo Gregoletto (Bass guitar|bass) and Alex Bent (drums).
