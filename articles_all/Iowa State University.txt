'''Iowa State University of Science and Technology''' or as it is better known, '''Iowa State University''' is a public university in Ames, Iowa|Ames, Iowa. The university has about 33,000 students. It was founded in 1858. The president of the university is Wendy Wintersteen. George Washington Carver was a student at Iowa State.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Cyclones. They have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the Big 12 Conference.
