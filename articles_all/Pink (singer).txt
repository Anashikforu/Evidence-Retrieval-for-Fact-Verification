'''Alecia Beth Moore''', better known as Pink (or P!nk, born September 8, 1979), is an Americans|American singer-songwriter and actress. She was at first an Contemporary R&B|R&B singer but then added pop and rock to her music. Her father is of English, Jamaican, German, Irish and Scottish descent, and her mother is Jewish (of Bohemian Jewish, Czech Jewish, Lithuanian Jewish and Russian Jewish descent). As of 2012, she sold over 40 million records and 70 million singles worldwide. She has won 3 Grammy Awards and 1 Brit Award.

 Early life 
Alecia Beth Moore was born in Abington Township, Montgomery County, Pennsylvania|Abington, Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, and raised in Doylestown, Pennsylvania|Doylestown, Pennsylvania, in Bucks County.  Her parents divorced when she was three years old.  She was formerly a member of the girl group Choice.

 Personal life 
In 2006, she married motocross racer Carey Hart in Costa Rica, after proposing to him at the X Games in Philadelphia, near her hometown of Doylestown, Pennsylvania. On June 2, 2011, they had a baby girl named Willow Sage Hart.
