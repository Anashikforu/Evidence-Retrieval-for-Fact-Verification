

'''''Buried Alive: The Elements of Love''''' is a Young-adult fiction|young adult book of :wikt:poetry|poetry by Ralph Fletcher, with pictures by Andrew Moore.  It was first wikt:published|published in 1996.

 Summary 
This book is a collection of thirty six free verse poems about :en:wikt:teenage|teenage love divided into four elements: earth, water, air and fire.
