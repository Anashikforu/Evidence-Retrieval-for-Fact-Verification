
'''Head-Smashed-In Buffalo Jump''' is a buffalo jump in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains about 18&nbsp;km northwest of Fort Macleod, Alberta, Canada. The plains Native Americans|Indians used Head-Smashed-In Buffalo Jump for over 5,500 years to kill Bison|buffalo. Head-Smashed-In Buffalo Jump is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and became one in 1981.
