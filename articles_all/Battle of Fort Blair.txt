The '''Battle of Fort Blair''' (also known as the '''Battle of Fort Baxter''') was a American Civil War|Civil War battle. It was fought in Cherokee County, Kansas, on October 6, 1863. The Union (American Civil War)|Union lost 103 peoples, while the Confederate States of America only lost three.
