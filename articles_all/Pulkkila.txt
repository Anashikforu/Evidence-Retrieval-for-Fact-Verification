'''Pulkkila''' is a former municipality in Northern Finland. It was in Northern Ostrobothnia, ''Pohjois-Pohjanmaa'', Oulu province, Finland, and its original name (with diacritic|diacritics) is Pulkkila. The population of Pulkkila is about 1,600. Pulkkila is best known for its metal works.
