

Image:NintokuTomb.jpg|thumb|Daisen Kofun, the largest tomb of Emperor Nintoku in Sakai, Osaka|Sakai, Osaka Prefecture (5th century)

 are wikt:earthen|earthen tombs or tumulus|tumuli in Japan.  They were constructed between the early 3rd century and early 7th century. 

These tumulus|burial mounds are referenced in the name of the Kofun period (middle 3rd century - early-middle 6th century). 

Many of the kofun have a wikt:keyhole|keyhole-shaped hill|mound () which is wikt:unique|unique to ancient Japan.
