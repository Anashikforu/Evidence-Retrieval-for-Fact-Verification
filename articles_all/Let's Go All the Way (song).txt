

"'''Let's Go All the Way''''" is a 1986 dance-pop/New Wave music|new wave song by the United States|American Pop music|pop band Sly Fox (band)|Sly Fox. It is the first single from their only album ''Let's Go All the way (album)|Let's Go All the Way'', which was released in 1985. The song was produced by Ted Currier and written by Gary "Mudbone" Cooper.

The song was used in the movie Ferris Bueller's Day Off and Loverboy (1985 film)|Loverboy and was also covered in 1999 by The Wondergirls and again in 2000 by the horrorcore band Insane Clown Posse.
