


'''Manatee Palms Youth Services''' is a hospital. It has 60 patients at its location in Bradenton, Florida. Manatee Palms Youth Services has been shut down

1990s abuse
The first time abuse at Manatee Palms that is on public record happened in November 1991 when a staff worker sexually hurt a teenage patient.
2000s abuse
In October 2004, a sexual assault happened at the facility and the staff member admitted it.

In November 2004, four patients ran away from the hospital, stole a car, caused crashes, and almost hit a sheriff's deputy.  

Manatee Palms was in the news after people found out that a sex offender was employed in 2003. The 41-year-old asked a 15-year-old girl to engage in sexual acts with him and record it with a video camera.

Closures
In April 2007, Manatee Palms was closed by the State of Florida for hiring staff members with criminal records, staff abusing patients, buildings falling apart, and threatening some people in Florida. due to staff members hurting patients by breaking their bones.
