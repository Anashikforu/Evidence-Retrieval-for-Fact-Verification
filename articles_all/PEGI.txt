
'''Pan European Game Information''' ('''PEGI''') is a European self-regulatory organization that assigns Video game content rating system|age and content ratings to consumer video games. The PEGI system is used in more than thirty countries. PEGI has rated more than 15,000 video games from 2003 to 2010.
