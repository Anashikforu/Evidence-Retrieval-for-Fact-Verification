The largest city in the region is its capital, Rancagua, with 206,971 inhabitants (2002 census). Other cities are San Fernando, Chile|San Fernando (49,519) and Rengo (30,891).

Administration
The O’Higgins region is divided in three provinces: Cachapoal Province|Cachapoal, Cardenal Caro Province|Cardenal Caro and Colchagua Province|Colchagua.
