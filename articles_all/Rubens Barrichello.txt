
'''Rubens Gonçalves "Rubinho" Barrichello''' (born May 23, 1972 in São Paulo (city)|São Paulo) is a Brazilian Formula One racing driver. Driving for the Brawn GP team, Barrichello finished third in the 2009 championship. He raced for WilliamsF1 in 2010, and will race for Williams in 2011.
