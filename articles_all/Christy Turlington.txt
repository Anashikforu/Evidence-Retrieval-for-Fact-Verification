


'''Christy Nicole Turlington''' (born January 2, 1969) is an Americans|American supermodel. She grew up in Danville, California|Danville, California. Her father was United Kingdom|British commercial pilot. Her mother was a El Salvador|Salvadoran flight attendant. 

Turlington is best known for representing Calvin Klein perfumes. She has done this since 1987. She has also worked on various campaigns for Maybelline and Giorgio Armani. She has appeared in several movies about the fashion industry.
