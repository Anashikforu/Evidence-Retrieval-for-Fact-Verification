
'''Megadeth''' is a heavy metal music|heavy metal band from the United States. The band was created by Dave Mustaine in 1983 after he was fired from Metallica. Megadeth is a member of "The Big Four" in thrash metal along with Metallica, Slayer and Anthrax (US band)|Anthrax. Megadeth is considered to be one of the creators of thrash metal. They have released 15 albums of music.
