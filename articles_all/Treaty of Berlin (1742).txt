The '''Treaty of Berlin''' was signed on July 28, 1742 between Austria|Austrian archduchess Maria Theresa of Austria|Maria Theresa and Frederick II of Prussia. 

 Terms 
Based on the treaty, Maria Theresa gave most of Silesia to Frederick. This treaty, along with the Treaty of Breslau, officially ended the First Silesian War.
