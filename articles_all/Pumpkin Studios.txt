'''Pumpkin Studios''' was a company that made Computer game|computer and video games. It was founded in 1996. It was in Bath, England. They made one game: ''Warzone 2100''. It was a real-time strategy game for Personal computer|PC and PlayStation. It used a few new ideas such as machines that use radio waves to find other objects (radar technology) and machines that shoot objects (artillery). The game also used movies to tell the story to the player. These movies are called Full motion video|full motion videos.

The company was closed on 13 March 2000.
