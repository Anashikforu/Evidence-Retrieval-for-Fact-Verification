
'''Freital''' is the biggest town in the Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge Rural District|Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge district, in the Free State of Saxony, Germany. It is situated on the small river Weißeritz, 8 km southwest of Dresden.
