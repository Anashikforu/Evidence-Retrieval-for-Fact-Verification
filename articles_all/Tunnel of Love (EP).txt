
'''''Tunnel of Love''''' is the fourth extended play|EP by the United States|American Horrorcore band, Insane Clown Posse. It was released on June 11, 1996. The producer of the EP was Mike E. Clark. A XXX Version which contained different cover art and a bonus track was also released at the same time.
