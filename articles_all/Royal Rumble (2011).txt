
'''Royal Rumble (2011)''' was the twenty-fourth annual Royal Rumble professional wrestling pay-per-view event made by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It took place on January 30, 2011 at the TD Garden in Boston, Massachusetts. As always, the WWE Royal Rumble|Royal Rumble match winner, Alberto Del Rio got a main event match at WrestleMania XXVII for his choice at either the WWE Championship or the World Heavyweight Championship (WWE)|World Heavyweight Championship.

Six Mock combat|professional wrestling matches were on the event's Card (sports)|supercard, a scheduling of more than one Card (sports)|main event. The Card (sports)|main event was the 40-man Royal Rumble|Royal Rumble match instead of the original 30-man match which featured wrestlers from both brands.
