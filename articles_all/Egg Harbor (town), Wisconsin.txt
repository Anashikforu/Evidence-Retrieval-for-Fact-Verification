'''Egg Harbor''' is a town in Door County, Wisconsin|Door County in the U.S. state|state of Wisconsin in the United States. The unincorporated area of Carlsville, Wisconsin|Carlsville is also within the town.

 Geography 
The United States Census Bureau says that Egg Harbor has a total area of 102.9 square miles, 64.47% of which is water.
