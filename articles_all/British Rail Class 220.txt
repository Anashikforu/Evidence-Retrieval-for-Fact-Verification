The '''Class 220 ''Voyager''''' is a class of diesel-electric high speed train|high-speed multiple unit|multiple-unit Railway|trains built by Bombardier Transportation for the United Kingdom|British train operating company Virgin Trains, but are now operated by CrossCountry. They are air-conditioned throughout, with powered doors and a top speed of . They were introduced to replace the thirty-year-old Intercity 125|High Speed Train and British Rail Class 47|Class 47 fleets. The trains were built between 2000 and 2001 and the first train entered service on 5 June 2001.
