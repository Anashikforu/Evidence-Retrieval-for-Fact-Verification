'''Bhakkar District''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Bhakkar is the capital.

Administration
The district is administratively divided into four tehsils and 42 Union Councils of Pakistan

 Bhakkar Tehsil
 Darya Khan Tehsil
 Kaloorkot Tehsil
