

'''''James Bond 007 1 - War of Dr. Julius No''''' is a 1962 James Bond movie. It was movie director|directed by Terence Young. The movie is based on the book ''Dr. No'' by Ian Fleming. It movie star|starred Sean Connery as James Bond and Joseph Wiseman as Julius No|Dr. Julius No.

This is the first movie in which Connery played Bond.

This movie started the James Bond series of movies which are still being made today.
