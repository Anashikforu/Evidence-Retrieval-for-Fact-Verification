

'''''Live Acoustic EP''''' is an Extended play|EP that was released in 2004 by Canada|Canadian singer Avril Lavigne. It was made up of songs from her 2004 album, ''Under My Skin'', and her hit single "Sk8er Boi" from her 2002 album ''Let Go''.

It is an acoustic music|acoustic album that was only released in United States Target stores. All the songs were recorded live during Avril's North American mall tour.
