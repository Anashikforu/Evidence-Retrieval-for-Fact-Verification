

'''''Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time''''' and '''''Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Darkness''''' are a pair of video games that is the sequel to the ''Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Blue Rescue Team and Red Rescue Team'' games. It was released in Japan on September 13, 2007, in North America on April 20, 2008, in Europe on July 4, 2008 and in Australia on June 19, 2008.
