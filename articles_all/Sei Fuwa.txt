Biography
Fuwa played for Waseda University. He won 1938 Emperor's Cup with Shogo Kamo, Sekiji Sasano, Kunitaka Sueoka, Hidetoki Takahashi and so on. He also played for Waseda WMW which was consisted of his alma mater Waseda University players and graduates.

In 1936, Fuwa was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for Football at the 1936 Summer Olympics|1936 Summer Olympics in Berlin, but he did not play in the match behind regular goalkeeper Rihei Sano. At this competition, Japan completed a come-from-behind victory first game against Sweden national football team|Sweden. The first victory in Olympics for the Japan and the historic victory over one of the powerhouses became later known as "Miracle of Berlin" (:ja:ベルリンの奇跡|ベルリンの奇跡) in Japan. In 2016, this team was selected Japan Football Hall of Fame.
