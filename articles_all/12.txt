




'''12''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a leap year starting on Friday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Augustus Caesar|Caesar and Capito'''.

 Events 
 Annius Rufus is appointed Prefect of Judea.
 Germanicus and Gaius Fonteius Capito become List of Early Imperial Roman Consuls|Roman Consuls.
 Quirinius returns from Judea to become a counselor to Tiberius
 Ovid stops writing Fasti because of the lack of resources (being far from the libraries of Rome). He completes 6 books that detail festivals found in the Roman Calendar.
