

'''British American Racing''' was a Formula One constructor that competed in the sport from 1999 to 2005.  BAR began by acquiring Tyrrell Racing|Tyrrell, and used Supertec engines for their first year. Subsequently they formed a partnership with Honda Racing F1|Honda which lasted for the next six years.
