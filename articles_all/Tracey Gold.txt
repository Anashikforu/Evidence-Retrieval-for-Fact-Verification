'''Tracey Claire Fisher''', better known as '''Tracey Gold''' (B. 16 May, 1969) is an United States|American actress. She is best known for playing Carol Seaver in the sitcom ''Growing Pains'' from 1985 until 1992. She also acted in other television programs. The actress was born in New York City.
