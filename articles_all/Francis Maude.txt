

'''Francis Anthony Aylmer Maude''' (born 4 July 1953) is an English people|English politician and the current Minister for the Cabinet Office and Paymaster General. He is the Member of Parliament for the Horsham (UK Parliament constituency)|Horsham constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1983|1983 general election for the North Warwickshire (UK Parliament constituency)|North Warwickshire constituency but lost in 1992. He returned to parliament in the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election.

Maude was born in Abingdon-on-Thames, Berkshire. He is a member of the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.
