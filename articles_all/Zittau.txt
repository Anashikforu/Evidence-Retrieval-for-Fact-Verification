
'''Zittau''' (Czech language|Czech: ''Žitava'', Polish language|Polish: ''Żytawa'', Upper Lusatian dialect: ''Sitte'') is a city in the southeast of the Free State of Saxony, Germany. It is close to the border triangle between Germany, Poland and the Czech Republic. It is part of the Görlitz Rural District|Görlitz rural district.
