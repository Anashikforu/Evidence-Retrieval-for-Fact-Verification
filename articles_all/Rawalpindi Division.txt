'''Rawalpindi Division''' () was one of the administrative subdivisions of the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan, forming part of the third tier of government below the federal and provincial levels. Divisions were composed of districts which formed the fourth tier of government, but the local government reform of 2000 abolished administrative Divisions of Pakistan|divisions and raised the Districts of Pakistan|districts to become the new third tier of government.
