
'''Avegno''' was a municipality of the district of Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 20 April 2008, Avegno and Gordevio merged to form the new municipality of Avegno Gordevio.
In 1982, the Swiss Heritage Society gave the Wakker Prize to Avegno for the saving of its historical buildings.
