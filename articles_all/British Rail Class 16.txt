
The '''British Rail Class 16''', originally known as the North British List of British Rail power classifications|Type 1 was a type of diesel locomotive ordered under British Rail|British Railways' British_Rail#1955-63:_The_Modernisation_Plan|1955 Modernisation Plan. Like other List of British Rail power classifications|Type 1 designs, they were relatively small locomotives intended primarily for local freight traffic.
