"'''Heart-Shaped Box'''" is a song by the United States|American Grunge music|grunge band Nirvana (band)|Nirvana. It was written by lead singing|singer and guitarist Kurt Cobain. It was released on August 23, 1993 as the first single from their third studio album ''In Utero''.

Cobain's wife Courtney Love thought the song was about her, although it was actually written about children with cancer.Cross, Charles R. ''Heavier than Heaven: A Biography of Kurt Cobain'', p.281
