
'''Jacquelyn Suzanne Gayda-Haas''' (born November 3, 1981 in Strongsville, Ohio|Strongsville, Ohio) is a semi-retired American professional wrestler who was best known for wrestling with World Wrestling Entertainment and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). She won the second season of WWE Tough Enough|Tough Enough. She is married to Charlie Haas who she married in 2005.

Championships and awards
Gayda won many things during her professional wrestling career:

'''World Wrestling Entertainment'''
Tough Enough II (with Linda Miles)

'''Total Nonstop Action Wrestling'''
Knockout of the Year in 2005

'''Wrestling Observer Newsletter awards'''
Worst Worked Match of the Year in 2002 (with Christopher Nowinski vs. John Layfield|Bradshaw and Trish Stratus on ''WWE Raw|Raw'' on July 7).
