A '''sheave''' (pronounced "shiv") is a wheel or roller with a groove along its edge for holding a belt, rope or Wire rope|cable.  When hung between two supports with a belt, rope or cable, one or more sheaves make up a pulley.  The words ''sheave'' and ''pulley'' are sometimes used interchangeably.
