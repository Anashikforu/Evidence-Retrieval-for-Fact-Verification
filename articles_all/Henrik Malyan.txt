
'''Henrik Malyan''' (Armenian language:Հենրիկ Մալյան; September 30, 1925 in Telavi, Georgia (country)|Georgia – March 14, 1988 in Yerevan, Armenia) was an Armenians|Armenian movie director.

His 1977 movie ''Life Triumphs'' (''Nahapet'') is considered to be one of the most important Armenian movies to deal with the Armenian Genocide.
