
'''Alexei Nikolayevich Kosygin''' ( – 18 December 1980) was a Russians|Russian man who was Premier of the Soviet Union.  He was a friend of the previous Prime Minister, Nikita Khrushchev. He and Leonid Brezhnev led the country together until he died.
