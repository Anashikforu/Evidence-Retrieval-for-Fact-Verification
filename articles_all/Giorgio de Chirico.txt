

'''Giorgio de Chirico''' (10 July 1888 &ndash; 20 November 1978) was an Italy|Italian painter and writer. In the years before World War I, he founded the ''Scuola metafisica'' art movement. The name means "Metaphysics|Metaphysical school". The ''Scuola metafisica'' was a big influence on the surrealism|surrealist painters. After 1919, he became interested in more traditional ways of painting. He began to paint in a neoclassicism|neoclassical style.''Encyclopaedia Britannica'' (1998). 
De Chirico was born in Volos, Greece. His parents were Italian. He studied art first in Athens and then in Florence. In 1906 he went to Munich in Germany where he studied at the Munich Academy of Fine Arts.
