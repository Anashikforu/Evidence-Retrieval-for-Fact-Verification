


'''Bonnie Lynn Raitt''' (born November 8, 1949) is an Americans|American blues singer-songwriter who was born in Burbank, Los Angeles County, California|Burbank, California.  Raitt is best known for her songs "Nick of Time (song)|Nick of Time", "Something to Talk About", and the ballad "I Can't Make You Love Me."   Raitt is also an avid political activist and has received nine Grammy Awards in her career.
