
'''Eric Ken Shinseki''' (born November 28, 1942) is a retired United States Army four-star General (United States)|general who was serving as the 7th United States Secretary of Veterans Affairs.  His last U.S. Army position was as the 34th Chief of Staff of the United States Army|Chief of Staff of the Army (1999-2003).
