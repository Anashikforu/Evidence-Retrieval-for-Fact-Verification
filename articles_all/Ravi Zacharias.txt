
'''Frederick Antony Ravi Kumar Zacharias''' (March 26, 1946 &ndash; May 19, 2020) was an Indian-born Canada|Canadian-United States|American Evangelicalism|Evangelical Christianity|Christian Christian apologetics|apologist and evangelism|evangelist. Zacharias was the author of many Christian books, such as the Gold Medallion Book Award winner ''Can Man Live Without God?''

Zacharias died of spinal cancer on May 19, 2020 at his home in Atlanta, Georgia at the age of 74.
