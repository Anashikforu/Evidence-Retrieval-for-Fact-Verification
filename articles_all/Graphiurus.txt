The '''African dormouse''', '''''Graphiurus''''',  is a genus of dormouse that lives throughout sub-Saharan Africa in different habitats.  They are good at climbing. They have bushy tails.  They eat invertebrates and small vertebrates.Kingdon, Jonathan  2004. ''The Kingdon pocket guide to African mammals, '' Russel Friedman.
