The British Rail '''Class 122''' diesel multiple units were built by Gloucester Railway Carriage and Wagon Company|Gloucester RC&W in 1958.  Twenty single-car driving motor vehicles, called "Bubble Cars", were built, numbered 55000-55019. These were supplemented by ten trailer vehicles, numbered 56290-299 (later renumbered 54290-299).
