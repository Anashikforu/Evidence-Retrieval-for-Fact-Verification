
'''Graveson''' is a Communes of France|commune of 3,836 people (2008). It is found in the Regions of France|region Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur in the Bouches-du-Rhône Departments of France|department in the south of France.

The village is very attractive for holidaymakers, because it is located between Arles and Avignon. Neighbour villages are Châteaurenard, Maillane and Rognonas.

Graveson is birthplace of the painter Auguste Chabaud.
