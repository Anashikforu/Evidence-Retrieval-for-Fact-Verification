'''Eastern religions''' is the name for a number of religions that had originated in Southern Asia|South, East Asia|East and Southeast Asia. They are grouped together because their ways of thinking, and worship are different from what are called the ''Abrahamic religion|Western'', ''African traditional religion|African'' and ''List of religions#Iranian religions|Iranian religions'', which originated elsewhere.
