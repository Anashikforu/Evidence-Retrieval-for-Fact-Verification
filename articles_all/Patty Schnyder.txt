
'''Patty Schnyder''' (born 14 December 1978, Basel) is a retired professional female tennis|tennis player from Switzerland. She played on the Women's Tennis Association|WTA tour from 1993 to 2011 and is a former World ''No.7''. She has had important wins over List of WTA number 1 ranked players|World No. 1's.

During her long career, she reached four Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slam quarterfinals, and one Grand Slam semifinal. She has won 10 Women's Tennis Association|WTA singles titles and 4 WTA doubles titles and has made over ''$6,906,000'' in prize money.
