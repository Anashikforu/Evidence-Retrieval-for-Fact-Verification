

'''Octave Mirbeau''' (1848&ndash;1917) was a France|French writer. His most famous novels are ''Le Jardin des supplices'' (in English language|English: ''The Torture Garden'') (1899) and ''Le Journal d'une femme de chambre'' (in English language|English: ''Diary of a Chambermaid'') (1900). His best known comedy is ''Les affaires sont les affaires'' (in English language|English: ''Business is business'') (1903).
