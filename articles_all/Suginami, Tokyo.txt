The Municipalities in Japan|municipality calls itself "Suginami City" in English.
Geography
Suginami is in the western part of Tokyo's special wards. It is next to Setagaya, Tokyo|Setagaya to the south. Its western neighbors are the cities of Mitaka, Tokyo|Mitaka and Musashino, Tokyo|Musashino. Nerima, Tokyo|Nerima is on the northern border. Nakano, Tokyo|Nakano is to the east.

The Kanda river runs through the ward.
