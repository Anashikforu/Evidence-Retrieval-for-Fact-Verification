
'''"Only Girl (In the World)"''' is a song performed by Barbados|Barbadian recording artist Rihanna. It is taken from her fifth studio album, ''Loud (Rihanna album)|Loud''. It was written by Cristyle|Crystal Johnson, Mikkel S. Eriksen and Tor Erik Hermansen from the Norway|Norwegian production team Stargate (production team)|Stargate and Sandy Vee.
