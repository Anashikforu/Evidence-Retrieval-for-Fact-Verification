

'''Alan Samuel Colmes''' (September 24, 1950 &ndash; February 23, 2017) was an American radio personality|radio/television host, former comedian and author. His talk radio|talk-radio show is distributed by Fox News Radio. He was the writer of the book, ''Red, White and Liberal: How Left is Right and Right is Wrong''.
Colmes died at a Manhattan medical center on February 23, 2017, at the age of 66 after suffering from lymphoma.
