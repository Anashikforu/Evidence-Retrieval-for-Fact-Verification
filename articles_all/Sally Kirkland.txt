

'''Sally Kirkland''' (born October 31, 1941) is an United States|American movie and television actress. Kirkland was named after her mother. Her mother was a fashion editor for ''Vogue (magazine)|Vogue'' and ''Life (magazine)|LIFE'' magazines). She had small roles for 1970s movies (''The Sting'', ''The Way We Were'' and ''A Star is Born''). She also starred in many television cast member|roles in the soap operas ''Valley of the Dolls'' and ''Days of Our Lives''.

Kirkland was born in New York City.
