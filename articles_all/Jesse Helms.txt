
'''Jesse Helms''' (October 18, 1921 - July 4, 2008) was a Senator from North Carolina. He was also the longest serving senator from that state, serving five terms. He opposed school integration, the Civil Rights Act, the Voting Rights Act, interracial marriage, civil rights, feminism, gay rights, disability rights, affirmative action, tax increases, abortion, the United Nations, foreign aid, communism, and giving government money to art that had nudity in it.  He and Strom Thurmond were leaders of the extreme conservatives (people who believe very strongly in right-wing beliefs).  Before being a Senator, he was a writer.

Death
He had many problems with his health, including prostate cancer and Paget's disease. He remained in poor health after retirement. He began to have failing memory and confusion. At last, he was moved to a nursing home near his home in Raleigh, North Carolina  He is buried in Historic Oakwood Cemetery in Raleigh, North Carolina.
