

'''Vâlcea''' (also spelt ''Vîlcea''; ) is a county (judeţ) of Romania. It can be found in the Historical regions of Romania|historical regions of Oltenia and Muntenia (both regions are split apart by the Olt River). The capital of Vâlcea County is Râmnicu Vâlcea.

Geography
Vâlcea County has an area of 5,765&nbsp;km².

Divisions
Vâlcea County has 2 municipalities, 9 towns and 78 communes.
