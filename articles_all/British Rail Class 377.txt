


The '''Class 377 Electrostar'''s are electric multiple units built by Bombardier Transportation (previously ADtranz) at their Derby Carriage and Wagon Works|Derby Works, from 2001-2014. The Electrostar family, which also includes Classes British Rail Class 357|357, British Rail Class 375|375, British Rail Class 376|376, British Rail Class 378|378, British Rail Class 379|379 and British Rail Class 387|387 and is the most numerous type of EMU built in the post-privatisation period of Britain's railways.
