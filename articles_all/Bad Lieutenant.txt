
'''''Bad Lieutenant''''' is a 1992 United States|American crime movie|crime drama movie starring Harvey Keitel in the title role as a New York City police detective. The movie is rated NC-17, though an R-rated version of the movie exists. It was movie director|directed by Abel Ferrara. Also appearing in the movie are Zoe Lund and Victor Argo.
