

'''Emily Jane Browning'''
Browning is also known for her roles in the horror movie ''The Uninvited (2009 film)|The Uninvited'' (2009), the action movie ''Sucker Punch (2011 film)|Sucker Punch'' (2011), and the independent movie ''Sleeping Beauty (2011 film)|Sleeping Beauty'' (2011). Her role in ''Sleeping Beauty'' was highly praised and in 2011 was named the Breakthrough performer of the year by Hamptons International Film Festival.
