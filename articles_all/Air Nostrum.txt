'''Air Nostrum''' (trading as Iberia Regional) is a regional airline based in Spain. It is not actually owned by Iberia Airlines|Iberia however it works in very close partnership with them. Another interesting fact is that Air Nostrum's main base is Valencia Airport, not Madrid Airport the main base of Iberia Airlines.

 Destinations 

As well as flying to many airports in Spain Air Nostrum operates flights to other European countries, for example Italy, Belgium, France, Croatia and Portugal.
