
The '''Anti-Comintern Pact''' was a pact between Nazi Germany and the Empire of Japan, which would later joined by more countries, in Berlin, Germany, on November 25, 1936. It was set up directly against the Comintern, or Communist International, an organization that was led by the Soviet Union. On November 6, 1937, Fascist Italy joined the pact,Robert Melvin Spector. ''World Without Civilization: Mass Murder and the Holocaust, History, and Analysis'', p. 257 which was the beginning of the Axis Powers.
