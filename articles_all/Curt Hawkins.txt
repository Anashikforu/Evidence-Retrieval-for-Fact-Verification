
'''Brian Joseph Myers''' (born April 20, 1985 in Glen Cove, New York|Glen Cove, New York) is an Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is best known for his time working with the WWE. He made his debut in 2004 and is now better known by his ring name '''Curt Hawkins'''. Hawkins was also known for being part of many tag teams with Zack Ryder (The Major Brothers, Curt Hawkins and Zack Ryder and The Edgeheads along with Edge (wrestler)|Edge). Hawkins is a one-time WWE Tag Team Championship|WWE Tag Team champion with Zack Ryder.

On June 12, 2014, Hawkins was released from his WWE contract.
