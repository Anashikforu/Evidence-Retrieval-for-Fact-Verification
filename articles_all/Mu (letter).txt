


'''Mu''' (uppercase/lowercase '''Μ μ''') is a letter of the Greek alphabet used to represent the "m" sound in Ancient Greek|Ancient and Modern Greek language|Modern Greek. In the system of Greek numerals, it has a value of 40. Letters that came from it include the Roman M and Cyrillic М.

In mathematics and statistics, the lowercase μ is used to represent population mean and the Möbius function.
