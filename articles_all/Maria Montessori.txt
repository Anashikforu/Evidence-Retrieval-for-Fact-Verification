

'''Maria Montessori''' was an Italy|Italian educator and Medical doctor|doctor. She was born on 31 August 1870 in Chiaravalle, Italy. 

Maria Montessori created the first Montessori school. It was a method of education which respected the natural development of the child. Its main feature was to allow as much independence as possible to the individual child. Another feature was to allow mixed ages in the same classroom.Montessori, Mario 1966. The human tendencies and Montessori education. Amsterdam: Association Montessori Internationale.
