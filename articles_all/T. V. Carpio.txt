'''Teresa Victoria Carpio''', more commonly known as '''T. V. Carpio''', is a China|Chinese / Filipino people|Filipina singer, songwriter and actress.  She is the daughter of Asian singing star Teresa Carpio, with whom she has performed on stage as a backup singer. 

Carpio played Prudence in the 2007 musical movie ''Across the Universe (film)|Across the Universe''.  She has also appeared on television, including a TV movie and episodes of ''The Jury (TV series)|The Jury'' and ''Law & Order''.  She made her Broadway debut in ''Rent (musical)| Rent'' as Alexi Darling and others, appearing from December 2006 until April 2007.
