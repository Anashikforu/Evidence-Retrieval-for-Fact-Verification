
'''Jenson Alexander Lyons Button''', Order of the British Empire|MBE (born 19 January 1980
Formula One
In 2000, Button joined the WilliamsF1|BMW WilliamsF1 Team at the age of 20, with Ralf Schumacher. He became the youngest driver to score a point at the 2000 Brazilian Grand Prix|Brazilian Grand Prix. He scored points six times during 2000, his highest place finish being in 2000 German Grand Prix|Germany where he finished fourth. His highest place on the grid for the start of the race was 3rd position in 2000 Belgian Grand Prix|Belgium. He scored 12 points that year, and finished 8th in the championship. 

In 2001, he changed teams to the Benetton Formula|Benetton team, and raced alongside Giancarlo Fisichella. He did not have much success. His best finish was 5th place at 2001 German Grand Prix|Hockenheim. Some people thought that Button would move teams because of his bad results, but stayed at the team for 2002, which was now called Renault F1|Renault. His results were much better than in 2001, scoring in the points many times and doing better than his teammate Jarno Trulli. He almost scored a podium result in 2002 Spanish Grand Prix|Barcelona, but could not finish the race. Even though Button was doing well, Briatore signed Fernando Alonso for 2003, and Button had to find a new team. In July 2002, he signed for the British American Racing|BAR team, and would race with Jacques Villeneuve, who won the championship in 1997. Button finished the season in seventh with fourteen points, just ahead of Trulli.
