

'''William Turner Jr.''' is a fictional character in the Pirates of the Caribbean (movie series)|''Pirates of the Caribbean'' movies. He appears in ''Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl|The Curse of the Black Pearl'' (2003), ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest|Dead Man's Chest'' (2006), ''Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End|At World's End'' (2007), and ''Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales|Dead Men Tell No Tales'' (2017). He is played by Orlando Bloom.

William Turner is a blacksmith's working in Port Royal, Colony of Jamaica|Jamaica. His love interest is Elizabeth Swann, the governor's daughter and upper class. Will is the son of Bootstrap Bill Turner. He works to free his father from service to Davy Jones (Pirates of the Caribbean)|Davy Jones. 

He marries Elizabeth Swann in ''At World's End'', and they have a son named Henry (played by Brenton Thwaites).
