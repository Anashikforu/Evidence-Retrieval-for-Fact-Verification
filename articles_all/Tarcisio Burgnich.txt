
'''Tarcisio Burgnich''' (25 April 1939 &ndash; 25 May 2021) was an Italy|Italian Football (soccer)|football player. He played for Italy national football team|Italy national team.

Burgnich died on 25 May 2021 at a hospital in Forte dei Marmi, Italy from stroke-related problems, aged 82.
