'''Jötunheimr''' (or '''Jotunheim''') is the land of the Jotun|giants in the Norse mythology. Two kinds of giants (or Jotuns) live in Jötunheimr, Rock (geology)|rock giants and ice|frost giants. From here they can attack the humans in Midgard and the Æsir|gods in Asgard. Jotunheim is ruled by King Thrym.
