
This list includes events from the '''1240s'''.

 Events 
 Batu Khan and the Golden Horde sack the Kievan Rus'|Ruthenian city of Kiev.
 Mandinka people|Mandinka prince Sundiata Keita defeats Sosso king Soumaoro Kanté at the Battle of Kirina, beginning the Mali Empire.
 July 15 1240 – Russians|Russian prince Alexander Nevsky defeats the Sweden|Swedes in the Battle of the Neva, saving Russia from a full-scale enemy invasion from the North.
 End of the civil war era in Norway.
 Saint Maurice is started to be portrayed as Moor.
 April 5 1241 – Mongols of Golden Horde under the command of Subotai defeat feudal Polish nobility, including Knights Templar, in the battle of Legnica|battle of Liegnitz
 April 27 1241 – Mongols defeat Bela IV of Hungary in the battle of Sajo.
