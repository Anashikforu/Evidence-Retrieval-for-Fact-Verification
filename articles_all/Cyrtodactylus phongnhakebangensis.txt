'''''Cyrtodactylus phongnhakebangensis''''' (Vietnamese: ''Thằn lằn Phong Nha-Kẻ Bàng'') is a type of gecko native to Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park, Quang Binh province, Bac Trung Bo, Vietnam. It was found and described in 2002.
