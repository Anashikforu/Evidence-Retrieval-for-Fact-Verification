'''Travertine''' is a Land|terrestrial sedimentary rock. It is a natural chemical precipitation|precipitate of carbonate minerals; aragonite or calcite. This happens from solution in ground and surface waters, and/or  hot spring|geothermally heated hot-springs.''Dictionary of geological terms'', 1962. A Dolphin Reference Book''A glossary of karst terminology'', 1970. Geol. Surv. Water-Supply Paper 1899-K, U. S. Gov. Print. Off., Washington. Similar (but extremely wikt:porous|porous) deposits formed from ambient-temperature water are known as tufa. 

The Colosseum built by the Roman Empire|Romans, was made of travertine.
