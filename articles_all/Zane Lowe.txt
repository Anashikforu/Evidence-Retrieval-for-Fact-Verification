
'''Alexander Zane Reid Lowe''' (born 7 August 1973) is a New Zealand radio disc jockey in the United Kingdom. He presents an early evening show on the Great Britain|British music radio station BBC Radio 1 on Mondays to Thursdays. Lowe also presented shows on the music Television network|television station MTV2.
