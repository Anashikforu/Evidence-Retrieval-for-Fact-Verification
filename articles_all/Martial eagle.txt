The '''martial eagle''' (''Polemaetus bellicosus'') is a bird of prey. It lives in Africa and is the biggest eagle in Africa, 76 to 96&nbsp;cm (30–38&nbsp;in) long, and its wingspan is 188–260&nbsp;cm (6.2–8.5&nbsp;ft) long. Because of its size, it hunts large prey, including birds as large as flamingos and mammals as large as young impala.
