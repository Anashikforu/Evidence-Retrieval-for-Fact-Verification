'''Norfolk County''' is a city in Ontario, Canada that is largely agriculture|agricultural. It has many primary schools and high schools and the majority of citizens are Christian. Its communities include:
 Simcoe, Ontario|Simcoe
 Delhi, Ontario|Delhi
 Port Dover, Ontario|Port Dover
 Pine Grove
 Gilbertville, Ontario|Gilbertville

The current mayor is Charlie Luke who was elected on November 13, 2014.
