Biography
Sugimoto was born in Kobe on December 4, 1926.
In March 1951, Sugimoto was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for Japan team first game after World War II, Football at the 1951 Asian Games|1951 Asian Games. At this competition, on March 7, he debuted against Iran national football team|Iran. He played 3 games for Japan until 1954.

On April 2, 2002, Sugimoto died of kidney failure in Nishinomiya, Hyogo|Nishinomiya at the age of 75.
