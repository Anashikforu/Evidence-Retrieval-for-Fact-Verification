'''The Honourable Society of Gray's Inn''' is one of the four Inns of Court around the Royal Courts of Justice in London. They may call members to the Bar association|Bar and allow them to practice as barristers. The other Inns are Middle Temple, Lincoln's Inn and Inner Temple.

Gray's Inn is in Holborn, in the London Borough of Camden, on the border with the City of London and the City of Westminster.
