'''''Batman''''' is a 1960s United States|American television series, based on the DC Comics|DC comic book character Batman. It aired on the American Broadcasting Company|ABC network for two and a half seasons from 1966 to 1968. Even though it was on TV for such a short time, the series had two new episodes each week, giving the show a total of 120 episodes (the equivalent of roughly five regular seasons).

 Cast

 Burt Ward - Robin (comics)|Robin/Dick Grayson - Batman's faithful partner and "boy wonder".
