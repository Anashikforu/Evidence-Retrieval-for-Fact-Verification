'''''True Star: A Private Performance''''' is a two-track extended play|EP by United States|American rhythm and blues|R&B singer Beyoncé Knowles|Beyoncé. Produced by herself and Tommy Hilfiger, it was released exclusively in Austria in 2004 (see 2004 in music), solely available with limited edition purchases of her fragrance by Hilfiger entitled True Star.
