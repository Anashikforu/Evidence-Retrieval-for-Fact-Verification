
'''Franz Ferdinand''' is an Indie (music)|indie Rock music|rock band from Glasgow, Scotland. The group is named after Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria|Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria. Franz Ferdinand has released five albums, ''Franz Ferdinand'', ''You Could Have It So Much Better With Franz Ferdinand'', ''Tonight: Franz Ferdinand'', ''Right Thoughts, Right Words, Right Action'', and their most recent release, ''Always Ascending''.

The band members are Alex Kapranos, Robert Hardy, Julian Corrie, Dino Bardot and Audrey Tait.
