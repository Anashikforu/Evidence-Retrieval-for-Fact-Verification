The '''Bethlehem Baptist Church''' ('''BBC''') is a megachurch in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The church was started in 1871 as the '''First Swedish Baptist Church of Minneapolis'''. Since 1980, their pastor has been John Piper (theologian)|John Piper.
