'''Sint-Agatha-Berchem''' (in Dutch) or '''Berchem-Sainte-Agathe''' (in French) is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian Brussels-Capital Region.

In 2007, 20431 people lived there.

It is at 50° 51 North, 04° 17 East.
