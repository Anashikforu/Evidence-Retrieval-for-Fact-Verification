
'''''Laverne & Shirley''''' was an American situation comedy which aired from January 1976 to May 1983 on American Broadcasting Company|ABC. Penny Marshall played Laverne; Cindy Williams played Shirley. The series was set between 1959 and 1967 and was made under Paramount Television.
