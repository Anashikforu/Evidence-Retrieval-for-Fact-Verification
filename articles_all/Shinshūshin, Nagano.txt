'''Shinshushinmachi''' is a town in Nagano Prefecture, Japan.  It is south of Nagano, Nagano|Nagano city.  It is famous for mutton, beautiful fireworks display|fireworks on August 15th, wasabi, soba, canoe polo, the Sai River, apples, and the fossil museum.
