

The '''Coat of arms of Romania''' is the coat of arms that has been wikt:adopt|adopted by the Parliament of Romania|Romanian Parliament on 10 September 1992.  The design for it came from the coat of arms that the Kingdom of Romania created back in 1922. It displays a golden Eagle (heraldry)|aquila that is holding a cross in its beak.  It also shows a Mace (club)|mace and a sword in its claws.  It contains the three colors of the Flag of Romania: red, yellow, and blue.
