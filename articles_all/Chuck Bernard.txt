
'''Joseph Charles "Chuck" Bernard''' (August 29, 1911 in Chicago, Illinois &ndash; March 1962) was a professional American football Center (football)|center who played for the Detroit Lions in 1934 and was a two-time All-American at the University of Michigan.

NFL Career
After graduating from the University of Michigan, Bernard played 10 games for the Detroit Lions in 1934.
