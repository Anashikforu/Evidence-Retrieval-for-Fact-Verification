'''Ratatouille'''  is an old France|French Provence|Provençal stewed vegetable dish, coming from Nice. The full name of the dish is '''''ratatouille niçoise.'''''Ratatouille. ''Oxford English Dictionary'', 2nd edition (1989) The word ''ratatouille'' may come from the word ''touiller'', which means "to stir up". It includes tomato sauce and cucumber.
