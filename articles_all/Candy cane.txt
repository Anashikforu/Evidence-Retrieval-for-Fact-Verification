
A '''candy cane''' is a hard cane-shaped candy stick. It is usually white with red wikt:stripe|stripes and tastes like peppermint. It is also made in many other flavors and colors.  The candy cane is a traditional candy of the Christmas holiday in the United States, but you can find them during the rest of the year. It is a symbolic figure in the Christmas season.

In the 17th century, white sticks of candy were popular, but they were not colorful.. The colors are also said to be symbolic. The white part represents purity, and the red represents the blood of Jesus.
