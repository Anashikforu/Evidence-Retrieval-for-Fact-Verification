'''Red huckleberry''' ('''''Vaccinium parvifolium''''') is a species of ''Vaccinium,'' a flowering plant. It grows in North America. There, it is often found in forests from southeastern Alaska and British Columbia, through western Washington (U.S. state) |Washington and Oregon to central California. In the Oregon Coast Range, it is the most-seen ''Vaccinium''.
