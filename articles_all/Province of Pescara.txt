
The '''province of Pescara''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Abruzzo region of Southern Italy, along the Adriatic Sea. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Pescara.

Geography

Pescara is the smallest province in Abruzzo with an area of . The province is bordered to the northeast by the Adriatic Sea, to the north by the province of Teramo, to the east by the province of Chieti, and to the south and west by the province of L'Aquila.



The final part of the Aterno-Pescara river flows through this province; other rivers are smaller and most of them are Tributary|tributaries of the Aterno-Pescara river. The city of Pescara, the capital of the province, is at the mouth of this river.

The highest mountain in the province is ''Monte Amaro'',( high and is the highest mountain of three provinces: Chieti, L'Aquila and Pescara of the Abruzzo.
