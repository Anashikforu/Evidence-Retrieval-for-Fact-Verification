
'''Euston Square''' is a London Underground station at the corner of Euston Road and Gower Street (London)|Gower Street, just north of University College London (not to be confused with nearby Euston tube station). The station, which is within walking distance of Euston railway station, is on the Circle line (London Underground)|Circle, Hammersmith & City line|Hammersmith & City and Metropolitan line|Metropolitan lines, between Great Portland Street tube station|Great Portland Street and King's Cross St. Pancras tube station|King's Cross St. Pancras. It is in Travelcard Zone 1|Zone 1 of the underground.  The postcode is NW1 2LU.
