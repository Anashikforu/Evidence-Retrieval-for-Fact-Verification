'''Nationwide Arena''' is a sports arena in Columbus, Ohio|Columbus, Ohio. The arena opened in 2000, and right now the Columbus Blue Jackets of the National Hockey League|National Hockey League (NHL) and the Columbus Destroyers of the Arena Football League (AFL) play there. The arena was built at the location of the old Ohio Penitentiary, a prison that was destroyed in 1998.
