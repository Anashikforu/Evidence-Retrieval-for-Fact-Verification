'''Sergey Karjakin'''  (born 12 January 1990 in Simferopol) is a Russian (formerly Ukraine|Ukrainian) chess Grandmaster (chess)|grandmaster. 

He was a chess prodigy and holds the record for the youngest grandmaster in history, at the age of twelve years and seven months. 

On 25 July 2009 Karjakin adopted Russian citizenship and afterwards played for Russia.
He won Candidates Tournament 2016 and earned the right to challenge for the World Chess Championship.
