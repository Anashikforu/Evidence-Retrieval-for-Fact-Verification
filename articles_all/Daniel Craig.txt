
'''Daniel Wroughton Craig''' (born 2 March 1968) is an English people|English television and movie actor and movie producer. He was born in Chester, Cheshire, England.
Craig is married to actress Rachel Weisz.

On 12 June 2008, Craig sliced the top of one of his fingers off while filming ''Quantum of Solace''. 

In September 2015, Craig donated $47,000 to the Bernie Sanders presidential campaign, 2016|presidential campaign of Bernie Sanders.
