
'''Anthony "Tony" Norris''' (born June 6, 1963), better known by his ring name, '''Ahmed Johnson''', is an Americans|American former Professional wrestling|pro wrestler. He is best known for his career in the World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation, which lasted from October 1995 to February 1998. He is a List of WWE Intercontinental Champions|one time WWE Intercontinental Championship|Intercontinental Champion and is the first African American to ever hold the title. He was also the winner of the WWF's first ever Kuwait Cup.
