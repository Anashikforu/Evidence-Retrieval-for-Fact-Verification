Biography
Tsugitani was born in Hyogo Prefecture on June 25, 1940. After graduating from Kwansei Gakuin University, he joined Urawa Reds|Mitsubishi Motors in 1963. In 1965,Mitsubishi Motors  joined new league Japan Soccer League. He retired in 1967. He played 40 games and scored 16 goals in the league.

On August 15, 1961, when Tsugitani was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Indonesia national football team|Indonesia. He played at Football at the 1962 Asian Games|1962 Asian Games. In 1964, he was selected Japan for Football at the 1964 Summer Olympics|1964 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, but he did not play in the match. He played 12 games and scored 4 goals for Japan until 1965.

On June 2, 1978, Tsugitani died of cirrhosis in Kobe at the age of 37.
