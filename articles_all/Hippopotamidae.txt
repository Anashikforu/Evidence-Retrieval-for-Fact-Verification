

'''''Hippopotamidae''''' is a group of even-toed ungulate mammals. They form the family (biology)|family '''Hippopotamuses'''. There are two living species of Hippopotamuses.

Taxonomy
 Family ''Hippopotamidae''
 Hippopotamus (''Hippopotamus amphibius'')
 Pygmy Hippopotamus (''Hexaprotodon liberiensis'' or ''Choeropsis liberiensis'')
