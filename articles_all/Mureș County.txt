


'''Mureş''' () is a county (judeţ) of Romania, in the Historical regions of Romania|historical region of Transylvania. The capital of Mureş County is Târgu Mureş.

 Geography 
Mureş County has an area of 6,714&nbsp;km².

 Population 
In 2002, about 582,000 people lived in Mureş County.

 Divisions 
Mureş County has 4 municipalities, 7 towns and 91 communes.
