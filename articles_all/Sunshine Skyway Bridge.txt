The '''Bob Graham Sunshine Skyway Bridge''', also known as the '''Sunshine Skyway Bridge''' or the '''Skyway''', is a highway bridge in Tampa Bay, Florida, that crosses 29,040 foot (length)|feet (5.5 miles or about 8.85 Kilometre|km).
