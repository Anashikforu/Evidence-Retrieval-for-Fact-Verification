
'''Vung Tau''' (Vietnamese language|Vietnamese: ''Vũng Tàu'') is a city in Bà Rịa-Vũng Tàu Province|Ba Ria-Vung Tau Province, Vietnam. This city has a population of 240,000 and an area of 154.0 km2. This is a petroleum and tourism centre of Vietnam. 

 History 
Vung Tau is located about 120&nbsp;km northeast of Ho Chi Minh City. Vung Tau town was founded by France|French Colony|colonists under the administration of Paul Doumer, then the Governor General of French Indochina. This city borders Vietnam Sea and has several beautiful beaches.
