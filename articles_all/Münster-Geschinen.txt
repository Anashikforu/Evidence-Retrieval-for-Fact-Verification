

'''Münster-Geschinen''' is the capital city of the district of Goms (district)|Goms in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Valais in Switzerland. On 1 January 2004 the former municipalities of Münster, Valais|Münster and Geschinen merged into the new municipality of Münster-Geschinen and On 1 January 2017 the former municipalities of Münster-Geschinen, Blitzingen, Grafschaft, Niederwald, Switzerland|Niederwald and Reckingen-Gluringen merged into the new municipality of Goms, Valais|Goms.
