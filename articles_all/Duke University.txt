

'''Duke University''' is a private university in Durham, North Carolina, founded in 1838. It was formed by Methodists and Quakers in the present-day town of Trinity, North Carolina|Trinity.

It has advanced research.  Some celebrity|famous people including former President of the United States, Richard Nixon
Duke has good Library|libraries, such as Bostock Library. It also has a famous medical school and law school. The school's colours are blue and white and its president is Richard Brodhead.

Duke University owns 220 buildings on  Duke Forest.

Duke University has own Academic journal|press ''Duke Univeristy Press''.

 Athletics 
Duke has 26 varsity sports teams, known as the Duke Blue Devils|Blue Devils.  Duke's athletic programs are considered among the very best in the NCAA.  Duke's teams play in the Atlantic Coast Conference.

Its Men's Basketball Team under coach Mike Krzyzewski has won the NCAA Tournament four times, and bought out the 2015 title as well.
