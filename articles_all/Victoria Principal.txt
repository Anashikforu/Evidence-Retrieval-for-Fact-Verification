'''Victoria Principal''' (born January 3, 1950) is an Americans|American actress and businessperson. She was born in Japan, where her father was stationed in the U.S. forces.

In 1970, Principal moved to Hollywood. She won her first film role as Marie Elena, a Mexican mistress, in John Huston's ''The Life and Times of Judge Roy Bean'' (1972) (opposite Paul Newman). She got a Golden Globe Award|Golden Globe nomination as Most Promising Newcomer for this role.
She starred in many movies and television programme|TV programs. Her most notable acting was playing Pamela Barnes Ewing in the nighttime television series ''Dallas (TV series)|Dallas''. She spent nine years on the long-running series, leaving in 1987.
