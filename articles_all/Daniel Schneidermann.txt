
'''Daniel Schneidermann''' (born April 5, 1958 in Paris) is a France|French journalist. He mainly reports about television|televised media. He is most active in weekly columns. Schneidermann has written in ''Le Monde'' and is presently in ''Libération''. He also can be seen on the television programme|television program ''Arrêt sur images'' (''Freeze-frame''), broadcast by the public television network|television channel France 5.
