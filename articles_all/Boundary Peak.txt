'''Boundary Peak''' is a mountain, (or ''peak''), in the southwestern U.S. State of Nevada. The county is Esmeralda. It is the List of U.S. states by elevation|highest point in Nevada. It is less than two miles from the California|Cal/Nevada|Nev state line, which is why it is called ''Boundary Peak''. Boundary Peak is  tall, and the closest town is Dyer, Nevada|Dyer, a famous ghost town.
