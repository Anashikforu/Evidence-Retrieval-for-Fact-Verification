'''Attock District''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Attock is the capital.

 Administration 
The district of Attock is administratively subdivided into six tehsils, each with an elected Nazim:

 Attock Tehsil
 Fateh Jang Tehsil
 Hasan Abdal Tehsil
 Jand Tehsil
 Pindi Gheb Tehsil
 Hazro Tehsil
