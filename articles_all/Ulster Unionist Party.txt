The '''Ulster Unionist Party''' or '''UUP''' for short is a centre-right-wing|right political party in Northern Ireland. It wants Northern Ireland to stay in the United Kingdom and is also moderately against the European Union. It has 10 out of 90 seats in the Northern Ireland Assembly and 1 of 3 Northern Ireland seats in the European Parliament but has no seats in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom. Its current leader is Robin Swann.

During the United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010 general election and the European Parliament election, 2009 (United Kingdom)|2009 European Parliament Election, the party allied itself to the Conservative Party (UK)|UK Conservative Party and called itself ''Ulster Conservatives and Unionists - New Force''.
