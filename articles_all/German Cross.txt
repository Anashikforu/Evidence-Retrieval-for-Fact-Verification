
The '''German Cross''' () was begun by Adolf Hitler on 16 November 1941 as an award that was a higher level than the Iron Cross First Class but below the Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross. It was given in two wikt:class|classes: gold and silver (the color of the laurel wreath around the swastika), the gold was given out for wikt:bravery|bravery, the silver was given out for wikt:excellence|excellent wikt:service|service.


This award was also wikt:available|available in cloth, which made for easier wear on the combat uniform.

The title of the award is taken from the fact that the swastika is a type of cross.
