
'''Real Sociedad de Fútbol''' is a spain|Spanish Association football|football team.  The team is in Donostia-San Sebastián in the Spanish provinces of Spain|province of Guipuzcoa. It was created in 1909. In the 2006/07 season, the team was in La Liga. After 40 years in this division, in the season 2007/08 Real Sociedad plays in Segunda División. The colors of the team are blue and white.

 Anoeta 
Anoeta Stadium is the stadium of Real Sociedad. Anoeta has a capacity of 32 000. Anoeta Stadium was opened in 1993.
