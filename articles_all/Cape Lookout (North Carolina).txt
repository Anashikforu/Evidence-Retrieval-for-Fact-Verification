''' Cape Lookout''' is southern point of the South Core Banks, one of the natural barrier islands on the Atlantic coast of North Carolina, United States|USA. It delimits Onslow Bay to the west from Raleigh Bay to the east. An area of the banks to either side of it has been designated the Cape Lookout National Seashore.

Cape Lookout stands at 34°36'19"N, 76°32'10"W, and is part of Carteret County, North Carolina|Carteret County, 11 miles southeast of Beaufort, North Carolina|Beaufort, NC.
