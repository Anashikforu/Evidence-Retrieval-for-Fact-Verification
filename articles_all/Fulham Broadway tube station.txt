

'''Fulham Broadway''' is a London Underground station on the Wimbledon station|Wimbledon branch of the District Line. It is famous as the nearest station to Stamford Bridge (stadium)|Stamford Bridge stadium. The stadium is the home of Chelsea F.C.|Chelsea Football Club. The station was opened as '''Walham Green''' on 1 March 1880.
