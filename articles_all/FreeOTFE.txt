

'''FreeOTFE''' is a free software|free, open source, computer program used to automatically encryption|encrypt and decrypt information stored on a hard disk.

FreeOTFE was originally written for Microsoft Windows and was later copied to Windows Mobile (FreeOTFE4PDA).

It can encrypt information using a large number algorithms including Advanced Encryption Standard|AES (aka Rijndael), Blowfish (cipher)|Blowfish, CAST6, MARS, RC6, Serpent and Twofish.

Sarah Dean is the developer of FreeOTFE.
