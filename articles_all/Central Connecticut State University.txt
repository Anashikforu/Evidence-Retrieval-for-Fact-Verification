'''Central Connecticut State University''' is a public university in New Britain, Connecticut|New Britain, Connecticut. The university has about 12,315 students. It was founded in 1849. The current president of the university is Jack Miller. 

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Blue Devils. They have 14 varsity teams and they compete in the Northeast Conference.
