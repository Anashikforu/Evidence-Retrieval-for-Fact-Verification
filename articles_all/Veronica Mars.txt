


'''''Veronica Mars''''' is an United States|American teen drama/wikt:mystery|mystery-film noir|neo-noir television programme|series set in Southern California. It was first shown on UPN on September 22, 2004. The series was shown for its first two seasons on the UPN before moving to The CW Television Network on October 3, 2006. The show stars Kristen Bell as Veronica Mars (character)|Veronica Mars: a student who also works as a private investigator with the help of her detective father. The series is said to be "a little bit Buffy the Vampire Slayer|Buffy and a little bit Humphrey Bogart|Bogart",.
The show starts with Veronica and her friends in high school. It follows them to college in later seasons of the series.
