
'''Leoš Friedl''' (born January 1 1977 in Jindřichův Hradec) is a Czech Republic|Czech professional tennis player best known for his doubles play with František Čermák. He is coached by Lubomir Gerla. In his career, he has won 15 top-level doubles titles and United States Dollar|$1,027,625 in prize money. Having turned professional in 1997, Friedl won the 2001 Wimbledon Championships|2001 Wimbledon mixed doubles title with Daniela Hantuchová, where they beat Mike Bryan and Liezel Huber, 4–6, 6–3, 6–2.
