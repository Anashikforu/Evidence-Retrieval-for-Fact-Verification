
'''''Misery''''' is a song by United States|American band Maroon 5. It was released on June 22, 2010 by A&M/Octone Records via the iTunes Store. The song was released in advance of the band's mid-2010 tour, and is the first single (music)|single from their third studio album, Hands All Over (album)|Hands All Over, which was released on September 21, 2010.
