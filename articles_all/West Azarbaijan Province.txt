

'''West Azarbaijan''' or '''West Azerbaijan''' (Persian language|Persian:
آذربایجان&nbsp;غربی ''Āzarbāijān-e Gharbī''; Kurdish language|Kurdish: ''Azerbaycanî Rojawa''; Azerbaijani language|Azeri: ''Qərbi Azərbaycan'') is one of the 30 provinces of Iran.

The province of West Azarbaijan covers an area of 39,487&nbsp;km² (or 43,660&nbsp;km² including Lake Urmia). The capital city of the province is Urmia.
