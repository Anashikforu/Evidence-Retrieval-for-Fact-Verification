
'''Schalkau''' is a Germany|German town in the south of Thuringia. Schalkau is in the Sonneberg Rural District|district of Sonneberg, in Thuringia, Germany. It is 13&nbsp;km west of Sonneberg, and 15&nbsp;km north of Coburg.

The population of Schalkau is about 3,500. The mayor of the city is Rheinhard Zehner.

The former municipality Bachfeld was merged into Schalkau on 31 December 2019.
