
'''Simon Peyton Jones''' (1958-) born in South Africa and is a British computer scientist. He does research on (Lazy evaluation|lazy) Functional programming language|functional programming languages, such as Haskell. He works at Microsoft Research in Cambridge, England. He is married to Dorothy, a priest in the Church of England. They have six children.

Simon Peyton Jones, Simon Marlow and others have developed the Glasgow Haskell Compiler, a compiler for Haskell.

He has also worked on C--.
