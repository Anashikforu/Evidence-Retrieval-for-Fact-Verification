

'''Joachim Löw''' (born 3 February 1960) is a former Germany|German Association football|football player who formerly managed the Germany national football team after being eliminated by England national football team|England in the UEFA Euro 2020 Round of 16. He became a FIFA World Cup|World Cup winning manager when his team won the 2014 FIFA World Cup.
