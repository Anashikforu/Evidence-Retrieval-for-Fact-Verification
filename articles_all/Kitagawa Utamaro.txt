

'''Kitagawa Utamaro''' (&nbsp;– 31 October 1806), also called '''Utamaro''',  was a Japanese artist. He was a master of the ukiyo-e style of woodblock prints and painting.Nussbaum, Louis Frédéric ''et al.'' (2005). 
Career
Utamaro is known for his studies of women (''bijinga''). He also made nature studies. 

His work was popular in Europe.  He influenced artists in Europe who were called Impressionism|Impressionists.
