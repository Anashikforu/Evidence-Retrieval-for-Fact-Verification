'''''Final Fantasy VII: Advent Children''''' is a computer animation|animated movie. It was released in 2005 by Square Enix.

Story
The movie is set two years after ''Final Fantasy VII'' . A mysterious illness has spread against humanity, Cloud Strife and the children are infected. He does not tell anyone he is infected. The villains included Kadaj, Loz, and Yazoo, are trying to find the "Cells of JENOVA". The Cells are a lifeform which fell to the planet hundreds of years ago and tried to destroy it.
