

"'''Jesus Christ Pose'''" is a 1991 heavy metal music|heavy metal song by the United States|American grunge music|grunge band Soundgarden, from their third studio album ''Badmotorfinger'', which was released in 1991. The song was written by Matt Cameron, Chris Cornell, Ben Shepherd and Kim Thayil and is a downloadable song in the video game, Rock Band (video game)|Rock Band.

The music video for Jesus Christ Pose received a lot of controversy from Christians since many of the band's listeners were Christians and they felt that the band was showing the song and music video as anti-Christian.
