


'''''Star Trek: Generations''''' is the seventh movie set in the Star Trek fictional universe|universe. It was made in 1994 by Paramount Pictures. It is the first movie to have the crew from the television series ''Star Trek: The Next Generation''. Captain James T. Kirk|Kirk and Jean-Luc Picard|Picard meet in a place called the Nexus where time is meaningless.
