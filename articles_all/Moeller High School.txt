

'''Archbishop Moeller High School''' is an all-boys, Catholic Church|Catholic school near Cincinnati, Ohio, United States. It opened in 1960 and is run by the Society of Mary (Marianist Order). The school is famous for good sports teams: in its first 25 years, they won seven state American football|football championships and five national football championships with Gerry Faust as head coach.
