
'''''Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles''''' is a television series that appeared on the Fox Broadcasting Company|Fox Television Network from January 13, 2008 until 2009. It is a spin-off of ''Terminator 2: Judgment Day''.

It is about Sarah Connor, her teenage son, and a good robot named Cameron trying to stop the evil computer Skynet from ever being created.
