
'''Robert F. "Bobby" Roode, Jr.''' (born May 11, 1976 in Peterborough, Ontario|Peterborough, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian professional wrestler. He is currently signed to World Wrestling Entertainment (2016-Present) where he is part of the RAW brand, teaming up with Dolph Ziggler as the Dirty Dawgz. He is also best known on TNA/IMPACT Wrestling as Bobby Roode. 

He was a member of the tag team Beer Money, Inc. along with James Storm. Roode also wrestled in dark matches with World Wrestling Entertainment.
