

'''Carroll M. Ebright''' (March 20, 1894 - November 1979), sometimes known as '''Ky Ebright''' was a  coach for the University of California, Berkeley rowing|crew team. He is the only man to coach three Olympic Games|Olympic gold medal winning eight oared boats. Ky Ebright died in Berkeley, California at the age of 85.
