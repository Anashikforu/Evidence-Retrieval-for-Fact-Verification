'''Locarno''' is a district of the cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

 Circles and municipalities 
The district of Locarno has 40 municipalities divided in 7 circles:
 circles of Locarno: Locarno, Orselina, Tegna, Verscio, Cavigliano;
 circles of the Isole: Ascona, Brissago, Ronco sopra Ascona;
 circles of Onsernone: Comologno, Auressio, Berzona, Crana, Loco, Switzerland|Loco, Mosogno, Russo, Switzerland|Russo, Vergeletto;
 circles of the Gambarogno: Magadino, Casenzano, Caviano, Contone, Gerra (Gambarogno), Indemini, Piazzogna, , Sant'Abbondio, Vairano, Vira (Gambarogno);
 circles of Melezza: Borgnone, Intragna, Switzerland|Intragna, Palagnedra, Losone;
 circles of Navegna: Tenero-Contra, Minusio, Mergoscia, Brione sopra Minusio, Gordola, Cugnasco;
 circles of Verzasca: Lavertezzo, Vogorno, Corippo, Brione-Gerra, Frasco, Sonogno.
