
'''Calypso''' is a small Satellite (natural)|moon of Saturn (planet)|Saturn. It was discovered by a few astronomers in 1980, based on observations from the Earth. It is named after Calypso (mythology)|Calypso in Greek mythology. Calypso is a Trojan moon, as it orbits together with larger moon Tethys (moon)|Tethys. It orbits behind Tethys by 60 degree (geometry)|degrees in its orbit around Saturn. Telesto (moon)|Telesto is another moon which shares Calypso's orbit. Calypso was named after Calypso, a nymph in Greek mythology.
