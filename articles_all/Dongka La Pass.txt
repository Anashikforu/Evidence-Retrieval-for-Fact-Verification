'''Dongkha''' (el. 3060 m./12,000&nbsp;ft.) is a high mountain pass in the Himalaya connecting Sikkim in Republic of India with Tibet.

The pass offers a view of the Tibetan Plateau. The pass is located in North Sikkim. Near the pass is the placid Cholamo Lake, 6.5&nbsp;km long and 2.5&nbsp;km wide.
