
'''Herefordshire''' (known as '''County of Herefordshire''') is a county  in the West Midlands (region)|West Midlands Regions of England|region of England. It borders the English counties of Shropshire to the north, Worcestershire to the east, Gloucestershire to the south east and the Wales|Welsh Preserved counties of Wales|counties of Gwent (county)|Gwent to the south west and Powys to the west. It is not to be confused with Hertfordshire, a county north of London.
