'''Allan Hunter''' (born 30 June 1946 in Northern Ireland) is a former Northern Ireland|Northern Irish Association football|football player.

He was a Defender (football)|defender. He also spent most of his career at Ipswich Town F.C.|Ipswich Town. He made over 350 appearances in eleven years.
