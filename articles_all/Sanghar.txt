'''Sanghar''' () is a city in the Sindh province of Pakistan. The city is headquarters of Sanghar District and Sanghar Taluka (a subdivision of the district). It is an agriculture|agricultural town about 265km from Karachi. Unfortunately, there is no railway station and airport in the city. But the city does have road links with Hyderabad (Pakistan)|Hyderabad, Nawabshah, Mirpur Khas and other major cities of Sindh.
