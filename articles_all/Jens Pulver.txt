'''Jens Johnnie "Little Evil" Pulver''' (born December 6, 1974 in Sunnyside, Washington) is an Americans|American Mixed martial arts|mixed martial artist (MMA). He was the first  Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) Lightweight (MMA)|lightweight champion, and was a coach on ''The Ultimate Fighter 5'' reality show, which aired on Spike (TV network)|Spike TV. He is currently signed as a Featherweight (MMA)|featherweight with World Extreme Cagefighting (WEC), which is owned by Zuffa (the parent company of the UFC).
