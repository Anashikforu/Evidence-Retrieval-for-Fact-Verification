'''Gili Air''' is the smallest of the Gili Islands|Gilis and it is closest to the Lombok mainland, making it popular with honeymoon couples and travelers seeking a quiet Retreat (spiritual)|retreat. It has population of about 1,000 and administratively lies in the West Nusa Tenggara province. The island offers excellent snorkeling and SCUBA diving off its east coast, and turtles can be seen along the coral reef.
