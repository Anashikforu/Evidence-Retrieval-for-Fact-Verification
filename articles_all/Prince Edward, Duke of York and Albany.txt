
'''Prince Edward, Duke of York''' (Edward Augustus) lived from 1739 to 1767. He was the youngest brother of George III of the United Kingdom.  He was heir apparent|heir presumptive to the throne until his nephew George IV of the United Kingdom was born in 1762.
