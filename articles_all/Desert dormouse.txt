The '''desert dormouse''' (''Selevinia betpakdalaensis'') is a species of rodent in the dormouse family (biology)|family Gliridae.
When it was first found, the species was placed in its own family, the Seleviniidae, but now it is wikt:consider|considered to be a dormouse. It is the only species in the genus ''Selevinia''.

It is Endemism|endemic to Kazakhstan.
