
'''Cloppenburg'''  (Low Saxon: ''Cloppenborg'', Saterland Frisian: ''Kloppenbuurich'') is a town in Lower Saxony, Germany, capital of Cloppenburg (district)|Cloppenburg District. It lies some  south-south-west of Oldenburg in the Weser-Ems region between Bremen (city)|Bremen and the Netherlands|Dutch border. The town is a centre for the largely agricultural region of southern Oldenburg (state)|Oldenburg.
