'''Mechatronics''' (or '''mechatronic engineering''') is a combination of mechanical engineering, electronic engineering, computer engineering, control engineering, and Systems engineering|systems-design engineering in order to design and manufacture some useful products. Mechatronics is an engineering system design that combines several areas of study and avoids splitting engineering into separate disciplines.
