'''Gonzo''' (aka '''Gonzo the Great''' or '''The Great Gonzo''') is a puppet character, one of Jim Henson's The Muppets|Muppets. He is green and has a faucet-shaped nose. In Muppets in Space he is revealed to be an alien.
