
'''Jerry Lee Rice''' (born October 13, 1962 in Starkville, Mississippi|Starkville, Mississippi) is an Americans|American retirement|retired American football|football player. He was a wide receiver in the NFL, and is widely considered the greatest to ever play this position. He is often thought of as one of the best players in the history of the game. Most of his success was with the San Francisco 49ers.
