
'''Do As Infinity''' is a Japanese j-pop|pop / rock music|rock band.  They had their first song on September 29, 1999, "Tangerine Dream". Their name is sometimes shortened to '''D.A.I.''' The band was named after the their guitarist and composer, Dai Nagao. The singer was  Van Tomiko. The group ended on September 29, 2005. They started again 3 years later.
