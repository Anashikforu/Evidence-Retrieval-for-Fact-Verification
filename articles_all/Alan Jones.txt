

'''Alan Stanley Jones''' Order of the British Empire|MBE (born 2 November 1946 in Melbourne, Australia) is an Australian former Formula One driver. He was the first driver to win a Formula One World Championship with the WilliamsF1|Williams team, becoming the 1980 Formula One season|1980 World Drivers' Champion.
