'''Koweta Mission Site''' is a National Register of Historic Places|NRHP site added in 1973.

The Koweta Mission was founded in 1843 when white missionaries arrived in Coweta, Oklahoma|Coweta to teach Native American children. Reverend Robert McGill Loughridge| Robert Loughridge and his wife, Olivia, bought an empty cabin for ten dollars and began teaching. He named the mission "Koweta".
