
'''Kalgoorlie''' is a city in Western Australia. It is 600 kilometres east of Perth, Western Australia|Perth. It is the largest urban centre in the Goldfield-Esperance region, with an population of 30,000. It was founded in the 1890s in response to the Australian gold rush|gold rush at the time. It has a hot semi-arid climate (BSh in the Koeppen climate classification).
