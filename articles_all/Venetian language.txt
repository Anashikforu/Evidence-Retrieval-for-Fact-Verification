
The '''Venetian language''' (in Venetian: '''') is a Romance language.

It was the language once spoken in the Republic of Venice.
Today
In the present day, it is spoken in the Italy|Italian regions of Veneto and Friuli-Venezia Giulia, in Slovenia and in Croatia. It is also spoken in Brazil (Rio Grande do Sul and Santa Catarina (state)|Santa Catarina states) and Mexico (town of Chipilo) by the descendants of Italian immigrants.
