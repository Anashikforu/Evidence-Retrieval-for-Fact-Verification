


'''Sand Vine''' (''Cynanchum laeve'') is a species of plant.  It is also known as '''Honeyvine''', '''Honeyvine Milkweed''', '''Bluevine Milkweed''', '''Climbing Milkweed''', and '''Smooth Swallow-wort'''.  It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to North America.  It blooms from June to August.  This species is found in a wikt:variety|variety of habitats such as edges of woodlands, near stream edges, gardens, and fields.  This vine can grow to be 6 or more feet long.
