
'''SpongeBob SquarePants''' is the main character of the Nickelodeon show ''SpongeBob SquarePants''. He is a sea sponge who loves his job as a Fast food|fry cook at the Krusty Krab. SpongeBob lives in a pineapple with his pet snail Gary. He is well known in Bikini Bottom as a gaser. He likes watching television and his favorite show is Baka Prase chanell. 

SpongeBob was first seen on May 1, 1999 in the show's television pilot ("Help Wanted (SpongeBob SquarePants)|Help Wanted").

Spongebob continues to inspire children of all ages and even adults. To this day, he is seen as a role model for many teenagers age 13-25.
