

The '''Parliament of Pakistan''', officially known as '''Majlis-e-Shoora''' (, ''Council of Advisors'' in Urdu language|Urdu and Persian language|Persian, although referred to as "Parliament") is the federal and supreme legislative body of Pakistan. It is bicameralism|bicameral federation|federal legislature of Pakistan that consists of the Senate of Pakistan|Senate (upper house) and the National Assembly of Pakistan|National Assembly (lower house). According to Article 50 of the 1973 Constitution of Pakistan, the Council also includes the President of Pakistan.
