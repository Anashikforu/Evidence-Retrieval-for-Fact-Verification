
The '''Anus language''' is a tribal language spoken in the Irian Jaya province of Indonesia. It is an Austronesian languages|Austronesian language.

2005|As of 2005, there are almost 70 speakers of the language all of whom are members of a tribal group living on an island in the Sarmi-Jayapura Bay in West Papua New Guinea|Papua.
