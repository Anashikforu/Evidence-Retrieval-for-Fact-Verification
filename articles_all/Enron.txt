
'''Enron Creditors Recovery Corporation''' (formerly '''Enron Corporation''', New York Stock Exchange|NYSE ticker symbol ENE) was an United States|American energy company based in Houston, Texas|Houston, Texas. Enron used to have around 22,000 people before it went bankrupt in late 2001. In 2001, it was revealed that people in Enron were involved in Enron scandal|accounting fraud. The company closed in 2007.
