'''Lasbela district''' is a coastal Districts of Pakistan|district of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province, Pakistan. It became a separate district in the Kalat District|Kalat division on 30 June 1954. The name is derived from the words ''Las'' which signifies a ''plain'', the greater part of the area being a flat plain, and ''Bela (Pakistan)|Bela'' which means "jungle" and is also the name of the principal town of the district. Uthal is the district headquarters. The district is divided into 9 tehsils and 21 Union Councils of Pakistan|union councils.
