



'''Oak Creek''' is a Township (United States)|township of Saunders County, Nebraska|Saunders County, in the U.S. state|state of Nebraska, in the United States.  949 people were living in the township of Oak Creek as of 2000, says the United States Census, 2000|2000 census.  The Valparaiso, Nebraska|village of Valparaiso can be found in Oak Creek.
