
'''Commodore''' is the common name for '''Commodore International Limited''', a U.S. computer and electronics manufacturing company. Headquarters were in West Chester, Pennsylvania. In the 1960s and 70s they made adding machines and calculators. This company was important in the development of the home computer|homeâ€“personal computer in the late 1970s and 1980s. Commodore developed the first color computer sold under $300, known as the Commodore_VIC-20|VIC-20 in 1981. The company sold about 22 million Commodore 64 machines, introduced in 1982. It is believed to be the biggest-selling computer in history. Commodore later released the Amiga computers in 1985. The company went bankrupt in 1994.
