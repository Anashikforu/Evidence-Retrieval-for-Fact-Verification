'''Alfonso Guilleard Soriano''' (born January 7, 1976) is a Dominican Republican professional Major League Baseball player. He currently is a free agent.  He has used to play with the Hiroshima Toyo Carp, New York Yankees, Texas Rangers (baseball)|Texas Rangers and Washington Nationals before the Cubs. He played as a second baseman for the Yankees and Rangers. He bats right-handed and has batted as a List of baseball jargon (L)#lead-off hitter|lead-off hitter for most of his career.
