'''L'Île-Rousse''' () is a commune in France|commune in the Haute-Corse department of Corsica, France. It has a permanent population of around 2,500. It was founded in 1758 by Pasquale Paoli to create a port that would not be in the hands of the Genoese like Calvi, Haute-Corse|Calvi.

 Famous people born in the city 
 Pascal Paoli
