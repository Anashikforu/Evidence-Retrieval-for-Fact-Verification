

'''''The Clangers''''' is a United Kingdom|British children's television series. The first episode was broadcast on the BBC on 16 November 1969. The series is about a family of Extraterrestrial life|aliens who live under the surface of the Moon in caves.  They looked like small, pink, knitting|knitted mouse|mice.  Other fictional character|characters include the Froglets and The Soup Dragon, a dragon that gives out soup. The family uses a United States|US flag as a Table (furniture)|table cloth.
