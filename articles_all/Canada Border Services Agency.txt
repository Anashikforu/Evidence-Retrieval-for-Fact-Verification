
The '''Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA)''' () is the Canada|Canadian government agency with a duty to act as border guards and customs services.

The agency was started on December 12, 2003 (the same day Paul Martin became Prime Minister of Canada), by an order-in-council joining Canada Customs with border and law enforcement agents from the Ministry (government department)|Department of Citizenship and Immigration Canada (CIC) and the Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA).
