
'''Zakynthos''' (or ''Zante'') is a Greece|Greek island. It is the third largest island in the Ionian Sea. It is in the south Ionian Sea, west of the Peloponnese. Zakynthos' total population amounts to 41,472 inhabitants. The largest town of Zakynthos is Zakynthos (city)|Zakynthos city. Zakynthos is mostly flat island, with an area of 410 square kilometres.To the north of the island, there are low mountains and hills. 

Sights and tourism

Zakynthos is a very Tourism|touristic island. Most tourists who visit the island come from Western Europe, mainly Italy and England.
