
'''''Littlest Pet Shop''''' is an video game that was made by Electronic Arts. It is part of the Littlest Pet Shop series. The video game was released on the PC, Wii, and Nintendo DS. The game was released on October 14, 2008 in North America.

History
In August 2007, Electronic Arts gained the rights to some Hasbro brands, like ''Monopoly'', ''Littlest Pet Shop'', and Nerf.  In February, EA Casual Entertainment announced that their EA Salt Lake Studio would develop and release ''Littlest Pet Shop'' and ''Nerf N-Strike''.Sinclair, Brandon.  
Versions
The Nintendo DS was 3 separate titles.  Each version had different pets.
