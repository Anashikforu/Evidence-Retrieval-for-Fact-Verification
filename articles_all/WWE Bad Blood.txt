

'''WWE Bad Blood''', (originally called ''Badd Blood'') was a professional wrestling Pay-Per-View that was produced by World Wrestling Entertainment|WWE that was produced in 1997, 2003 and 2004. It included only the WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brand for the 2003 and 2004 editions. The 1997 edition was an WWF In Your House|In Your House pay-per-view called "Badd Blood: In Your House", this event held the first ever Hell in a Cell|Hell in a Cell match, which was won by Shawn Michaels defeating The Undertaker, and also featured the debut of Glenn Jacobs|Kane.
