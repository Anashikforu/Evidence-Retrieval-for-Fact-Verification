

'''Sara Gilbert''' (born '''Sara Rebecca Abeles''' on January 29, 1975 in Santa Monica, California|Santa Monica, California) is an Americans|American actor|actress most famous for being Darlene Conner in ''Roseanne (TV series)|Roseanne''. She has guest-starred on many primetime programs such as ''The Simpsons'' and ''Will & Grace''. She was in the cast of ''The Big Bang Theory''. She is a vegetarian. She is coming out|openly lesbian and was in a relationship with Allison Adler from 2002 until their relationship ended in 2011.
