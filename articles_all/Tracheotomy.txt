'''Tracheotomy''' and '''tracheostomy''' is a surgery on the neck to open a direct path by cutting a hole in the vertebrate trachea|trachea (windpipe). They are performed by paramedics, veterinarians, emergency physicians, and surgeons. Both surgical and percutaneous techniques are now widely used.
