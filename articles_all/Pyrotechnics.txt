
'''Pyrotechnics''' is often thought to be Synonym|synonymous with the manufacture of fireworks, but it includes also items for military and industry|industrial uses. 

Items such as safety matches, oxygen candles, explosive bolts and fasteners and the airbag|automobile safety airbag all fall under pyrotechnics. Without pyrotechnics, modern aviation and spaceflight would be impracticable.

This is because pyrotechnic devices combine high reliability with very compact and efficient energy storage.

A pyrotechnic show is a pre-planned complex that includes ground and high-altitude fireworks. Sometimes such events are accompanied by the performance of specially trained artists of the original genre with pyrotechnic equipment.
