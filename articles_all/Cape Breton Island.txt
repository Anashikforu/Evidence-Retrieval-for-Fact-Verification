
'''Cape Breton Island''' (French language|French: ''île du Cap-Breton'' - formerly ''île Royale'', Scottish Gaelic: ''Eilean Cheap Breatuinn'', Míkmaq language|Míkmaq: ''Únamakika'', simply: ''Cape Breton'') is an island on the Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic coast of North America. It likely corresponds to the European word "Breton", referring to Brittany.
