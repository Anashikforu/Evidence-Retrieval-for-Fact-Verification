




'''Electric blue''' is a color that is a bright tone of cyan.

The first recorded use of ''electric blue'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1884. Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill, p.194; Color Sample of Electric Blue fabric 1890s: Page 97 Plate 37 Color Sample I7


 Meaning of electric blue 

 The tones of electric blue represent something or someone that is Energy|energetic like lightning.
