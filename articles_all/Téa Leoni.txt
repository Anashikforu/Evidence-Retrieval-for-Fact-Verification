
'''Elizabeth Téa Pantaleoni''' (born February 25, 1966), better known by her stage name '''Téa Leoni''', is an Americans|American actress. She has starred many movies. These include: ''Bad Boys (1995 movie)|Bad Boys'', ''Deep Impact (movie)|Deep Impact'', ''The Family Man'', ''Jurassic Park III'', ''Fun with Dick and Jane (2005 movie)|Fun with Dick and Jane'', ''Spanglish (movie)|Spanglish'', ''Ghost Town (movie)|Ghost Town'' and ''Tower Heist''.

Leoni separated from her husband, David Duchovny, in 2011.
