


A '''protest''' is when a lot of wikt:people|people come together to show others that they strongly like or are against an idea or event. For example, some people protest racism or war. 

There are many ways in which people can protest. Protesters can do things like write Letter (paper)|letters, Hunger strike|not eat food, sing Song|songs, or riot.
