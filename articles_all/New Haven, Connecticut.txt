

'''New Haven''' is the second largest city in Connecticut, after Bridgeport, Connecticut|Bridgeport, with a population of 129,779 at the 2010 United States Census."Profile of General Population and Housing Characteristics: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (DP-1): New Haven city, Connecticut". U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder. Retrieved September 26, 2012. "New Haven" may also refer to the wider Greater New Haven area, which has nearly 600,000 inhabitants in the immediate area.
The trial for the mutiny of the slave ship ''Amistad'' took place in New Haven.
