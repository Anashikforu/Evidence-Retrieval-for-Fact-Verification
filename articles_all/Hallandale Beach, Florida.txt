 
'''Hallandale Beach''' is a city in Broward County, Florida|Broward County, Florida, United States. The city is named after Luther Halland, a worker for Henry Flagler's Florida East Coast Railroad. The population was 37,282 at the United States Census 2000|2000 census. Sometimes the city is called the "southernmost Canada|Canadian city". This is because Hallandale Beach has been a popular vacation spot for years, and most of the tourism|tourists come from Quebec and the northeast United States. Many of these tourists eventually moved to the area to live there.
