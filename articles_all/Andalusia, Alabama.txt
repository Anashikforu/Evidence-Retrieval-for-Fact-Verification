
'''Andalusia''' is a city in Covington County, Alabama|Covington County, Alabama, United States. At the United States Census 2000|2000 census the population was 8,794. The 2007 U.S. Census estimated the city had a population of 8,705.  It is the home of the World Championship Domino Tournament.

Education
Public education is provided by Andalusia City School System. which consists of Andalusia Elementary School, Andalusia Middle School, and Andalusia High School.
