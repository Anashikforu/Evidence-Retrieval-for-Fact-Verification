'''Perry''' is an alcoholic beverage. It is made from fermentation|fermented pears. It is made similar to the way cider is made from Apple|apples.  Peary has been common for centuries in England. It is mostly found in the Counties of England|English counties  of Gloucestershire, Herefordshire, and Worcestershire. Tt is also made in parts of South Wales and the Normandy and Duchy of Anjou|Anjou areas of France. 

In more recent years, perry has also been referred to as "pear cider". Some groups (such as Campaign for Real Ale|CAMRA) do not accept this as a name for the drink. The National Association of Cider Makers says that the terms perry and pear cider are interchangeable.''Pear cider ruling gives perry a timely boost'', ''The Grocer'', 19-05-2007
