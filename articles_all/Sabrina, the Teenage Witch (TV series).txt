
'''''Sabrina the Teenage Witch''''' is an United States|American television sitcom. It is based on the ''Archie comics|Archie'' comic book series of Sabrina, the Teenage Witch|the same name. It is set in Massachusetts. It stars Melissa Joan Hart in the title role. Its first four seasons aired on American Broadcasting Company|ABC from September 27, 1996 to May 5, 2000; the final three seasons ran on The WB Television Network from September 22, 2000 to April 24, 2003.
