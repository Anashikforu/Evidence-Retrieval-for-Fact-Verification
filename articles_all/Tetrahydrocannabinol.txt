 THC in the body 
THC is very fatty (fat soluble) and binds to Adipose tissue|fat cells in our body and causes it to leave the body Metabolism|slowly, even if the effects of the drug or high has already ended.  Drug Testing 
Employers or people who work in a government agency, like the police or Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI, may test someone to see if they used any drugs recently. This is called a Cannabis drug testing|drug test. Police may do this if they think you have used marijuana before or while you are Driving under the influence|driving a car. THC lasts a long time in the body and can be found in body fluids, like urine or saliva, or hair and sweat. Since it lasts so long in the body, it can be a problem being sure if someone is high.
