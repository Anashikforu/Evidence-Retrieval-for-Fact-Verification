'''We Rock''' is a song off the soundtrack of Camp Rock. The song also featured on the Disney Channel Original Movie ''Camp Rock''. ''We Rock is also the first track on the ''Camp Rock Soundtrack''. The song was sung by Demi Lavoto and other cast members of the movie ''Camp Rock''. The song was released in May 2008. The song was also the on the pre-release version of the Soundtrack.
