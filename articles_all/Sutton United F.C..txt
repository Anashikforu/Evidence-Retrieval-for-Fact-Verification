'''Sutton United Football Club''' is a professional football club in London Borough of Sutton|Sutton, London. They play in League Two, the fourth division of English football. In 1970, they beat Coventry City F.C.|Coventry City, who were in English Premier League|Division One then, in the FA Cup, last time a non-league club beat a major league club.
