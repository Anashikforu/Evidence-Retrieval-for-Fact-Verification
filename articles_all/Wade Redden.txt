
'''Wade Redden''' (born June 12, 1977) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman (ice hockey)|defenceman. Right now, he plays in the New York Rangers organization for its American Hockey League (AHL) team, the Connecticut Whale. He joined the Rangers in 2008 after 11 seasons with the Ottawa Senators. He has played for Canada internationally seven times, winning two gold medals in the World Junior Hockey Championships|World Junior Championships and once in the World Cup of Hockey.
