
'''Bénonces''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department in the east of France. In 2009, 278 people lived in the commune. The inhabitants are the ''Bénonçards''. The most important monuments in the commune are the ''Chartreuse de Portes'', a 12th-century monastery listed as a ''monument historique'', and the Saint-Pierre church.
