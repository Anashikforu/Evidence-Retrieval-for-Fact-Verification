



'''''Dead Space''''' is a third-person shooter|third-person survival horror video game, made by Visceral Games for the PlayStation 3, Xbox 360 and Microsoft Windows. The player plays as an engineer named Isaac Clarke. He fights against an Extraterrestrial life|alien infestation which turns humans into monsters called "Necromorphs" on a mining ship in Outer space|space named the USG Ishimura.
Characters
'''Issac Clarke'''

He is the main character. He has a job as an engineer. The Concordance Extraction Corporation sent him to the USG Ishimura to repair it. 

'''Kendra Daniels'''

She is an engineer, too. She is good at network technology such as hacking. Her opinion often clashes with Hammond's.

'''Zach Hammond'''

He is Issac and Kendra's colleague. He is a black man and a senior guard.

'''Nicole Brennan'''

She is Issac's former lover. She is a member of the USG Ishimura's crew. She sent the video asking for help to Issac.
