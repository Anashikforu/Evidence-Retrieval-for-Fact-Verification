

The '''Kia Carnival''' is a car produced by Kia.

The first generation were launched in 1999 based on the Ford Windstar's platform. It were available with a 2.5 litre 6-cylinder petrol engine with 121&nbsp;kW/165&nbsp;hp and a 2.9 litre 4-cylinder turbocharger|turbo diesel engine with 93&nbsp;kW/126&nbsp;hp.

It gets a facelift in 2001, and were available until 2006 with a 2.5 litre 6-cylinder petrol engine with 110&nbsp;kW/150&nbsp;hp and a 2.9 litre 4-cylinder turbo diesel engine with 106&nbsp;kW/144&nbsp;hp.

In 2006, the second generation were launched based on the Hyundai Entourage's platform. It is available with 2.7 and 3.8 litre 6-cylinder petrol engines with 139&nbsp;kW/189&nbsp;hp and 187&nbsp;kW/254&nbsp;hp respectively, and a 2.9 litre 4-cylinder turbo diesel engine with 136&nbsp;kW/185&nbsp;hp.
