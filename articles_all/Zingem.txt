'''Zingem''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of East Flanders. On 1 January 2019, Kruishoutem and Zingem were merged into the new municipality of Kruisem.

In 2007, 6822 people lived there.

It is at 50° 54 North, 03° 39 East.
