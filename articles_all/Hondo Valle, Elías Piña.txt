
'''Hondo Valle''' (in English language|English, ''Deep Valley'') is a Dominican Republic|Dominican municipality in the Elías Piña Province|Elías Piña Province. It is in the northern ''Sierra de Neiba'' mountain range.

Population
The municipality had, in 2010, a total population of 7,194: 3,782 men and 3,412 women. The urban population was  of the total population.

History
Hondo Valle was elevated to the category of municipality by the law 916 of 12 August 1978. Before that date, it was a Municipality|municipal district of Comendador, Elías Piña|Comendador.

Geography
Hondo Valle is in a small valley on the Sierra de Neiba mountain range, at an elevation of 890 m. It is at  to the south of Comendador.

The limits of the municipality are the municipality of El Llano, Elías Piña|El Llano to the north, the municipality of Juan Santiago, Elías Piña|Juan Santiago to the east, the Independencia Province|Independencia province to the south and Haiti to the west.
