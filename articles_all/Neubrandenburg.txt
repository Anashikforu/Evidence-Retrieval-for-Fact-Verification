
'''Neubrandenburg''' (Low German language|Low German: ''Niegenbramborg'' or shortened: ''Bramborg'')  (''New Brandenburg an der Havel|Brandenburg'', ) is a city in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Germany. It is in the southeastern part of the state, at the shore of a lake called the ''Tollensesee''.

The city was founded in the year 1248.
