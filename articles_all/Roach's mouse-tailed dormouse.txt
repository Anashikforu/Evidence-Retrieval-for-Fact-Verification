'''Roach's mouse-tailed dormouse''' (''Myomimus roachi''), also known as the '''ground dormouse''' or '''mouse-tailed dormouse''', is a species of dormouse in the Gliridae family. It is found in Bulgaria, Greece, and Turkey.
