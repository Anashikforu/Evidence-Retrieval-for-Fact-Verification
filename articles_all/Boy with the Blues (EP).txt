


'''''Boy with the Blues''''' is an album by the rock and roll|rock band Oasis (band)|Oasis.  It was released in early July 2009 as an iTunes exclusive EP.  It contained two songs not previously available on general release and also a remix of "(Get Off Your) High Horse Lady" from their album ''Dig Out Your Soul''.

Track listing
# "Boy with the Blues"
# "I Believe in All"
# "(Get Off Your) High Horse Lady" - the May 29th Devendra Banhart Mix

The first two tracks were only available on the ''Dig Out Your Soul'' Deluxe Boxset Bonus Disc, while the third track had only been released as a listen only track on Oasisinet's Radio Supernova.
