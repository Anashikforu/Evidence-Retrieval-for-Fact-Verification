The '''Dome of the Rock''' (, transliteration: ''Masjid Qubbat As-Sakhrah'') is an Islamic mosque building in East Jerusalem.

It was built from 691 from 692 by Abd al-Malik|Caliph Abd al-Malik. It is built on the spot where the Jewish Temple is believed to have been, and where Muslims believe Muhammad went up to heaven. The rock on which the building stands is believed by Jews, Christians, and Muslims to be the holiest place on Earth.
