
'''Wheat flour''' is a type of flour made from wheat kernels. There are two main types of wheat flour: whole wheat flour and white flour. Whole wheat flour has the whole grain of wheat, making it more nutrition|nutritious. White flour only has the endosperm of the wheat grain, making it less nutritious. Wheat flour can be sifting|sifted to make it finer and smoother.
