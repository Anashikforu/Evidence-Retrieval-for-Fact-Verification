

An '''Impresario''' is somebody who organizes concerts, operas, ballets or Play (theatre)|plays.  The word comes from the Italian language|Italian word ''impresa,'' which means "an enterprise" or "undertaking".

Some very famous impresarios from the past include Rudolf Bing, Sergei Diaghilev, Richard D'Oyly Carte, Fortune Gallo, and Sol Hurok.
