

'''William Blaine "Bill" Richardson III''' (born November 15, 1947) is an Americans|American politician. He was the Governor of the United States U.S. state|state of New Mexico from 2003 until 2011. He was a candidate for President of the United States in the United States presidential election, 2008|2008 election. He was U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations and Energy Secretary in the Clinton administration. He is a United States Democratic Party|Democrat.
