'''Laura Ingraham''' (born 19 June 1964) is an Americans|American Conservatives in the United States|conservative Politics|political commentator, author and radio Presenter|host. Her nationally syndicated talk show ''The Laura Ingraham Show'' was broadcast for nine years on the Talk Radio Network. She has been known for her controversial statements against the left wing party.
