
The '''Iranian plateau''', also known as the '''Persian plateau''' is a geological formation in Southwest Asia, '''Southern Asia''' and the Caucasus region. As a historical region, it includes Parthia (satrapy)|Parthia, Medes|Media and eastern Fars|Persia, the heartlands of Greater Persia (mainly Iran, Afghanistan, and Pakistan west of the Indus River|River Indus).
