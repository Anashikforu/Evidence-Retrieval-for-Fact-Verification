

'''Niederurnen''' is a former municipality of the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Glarus (canton)|Glarus in the country of Switzerland. On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Niederurnen, Bilten, Filzbach, Mollis, Mühlehorn, Näfels, Oberurnen and Obstalden merged into the new municipality of Glarus Nord.
