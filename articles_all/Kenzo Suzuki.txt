
'''Kenzo Suzuki''' (born July 25, 1974) is a Japanese professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling for World Wrestling Entertainment|WWE and New Japan Pro Wrestling.  Suzuki is currently wrestling for All Japan Pro Wrestling and is part of the stable, Voodoo Murders. He is often managed to the ring by his real-life wife, Hiroko Suzuki.
