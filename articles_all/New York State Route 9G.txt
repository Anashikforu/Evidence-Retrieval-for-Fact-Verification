

'''New York State Route 9G''' (NY 9G) is a road in Dutchess County, New York, United States. It starts at U.S. Route 9 in New York|US 9 in Poughkeepsie, New York|Poughkeepsie and follows the Hudson River as it goes north. It crosses Route 9 just north of Rhinebeck, New York|Rhinebeck and ends in Hudson, New York|Hudson.
