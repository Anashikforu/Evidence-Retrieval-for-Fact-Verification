
'''Daleville''' is a city in Dale County, Alabama|Dale County of Alabama in the United States. At the United States Census 2000|2000 census, 4,653 people lived there. It is part of the Enterprise, Alabama|Enterprise–Ozark, Alabama|Ozark Enterprise-Ozark micropolitan area|Micropolitan Statistical Area.  The city's nickname is "Gateway to Fort Rucker", as this U.S. Army post is just north of town. Cairns Army Airfield is to the south of Daleville on the road to nearly Clayhatchee, Alabama|Clayhatchee.
