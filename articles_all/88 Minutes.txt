
'''''88 Minutes''''' is a 2007 United States|American crime movie|crime drama movie|drama thriller movie|thriller mystery movie. It is set in Seattle and stars Al Pacino. He gets a call on his cell phone by a deep-voiced person telling him he only has 88 minutes to live. Benjamin McKenzie is also in the movie.
