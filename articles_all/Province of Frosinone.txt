
The '''Province of Frosinone''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Lazio Regions of Italy|region of Central Italy. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Frosinone.

History
The province of Frosinone was created in December of 1926 with Territory|territories of the Lazio and Campania Regions of Italy|regions.

Geography

The province of Frosinone has an area of , the third largest province of the Lazio region.

The province is in the southeast of the Lazio region. It borders to the north with the Abruzzo region (Province of L'Aquila|L'Aquila province), to the northwest and west with the Metropolitan City of Rome Capital|Metropolitan City of Rome, to the east by the Molise region (Province of Isernia|Isernia province), to the southeast with the Campania region (Province of Caserta|Caserta), and to the south with the Province of Latina|Latina province.



The highest mountain in the province is ''Monte Meta'' or, simply, ''La Meta'' (; it is on the border with the Province of L'Aquila|L'Aquila province.
