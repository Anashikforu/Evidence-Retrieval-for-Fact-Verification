
'''Bryan Konietzko''' is the co-creator and executive producer of the animation|animated series ''Avatar: The Last Airbender''. He has also worked as a character designer for the animated series ''Family Guy''. He was a storyboard artist and art director for the Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon animated series ''Invader Zim''.
