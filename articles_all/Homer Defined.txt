'''"Homer Defined"''' is the seventh episode of ''The Simpsons'' third season, which first started on November 1st, 1991. The episode has the first wikt:appearance|appearance of "Milhouse Van Houten|Milhouse's mother" and the first time his last name, "Van Houten", is used.
