The '''Dodge Caravan''' is an American minivan manufactured by Chrysler|FCA US, LLC. and sold by Dodge. The Caravan was introduced around 1983, and '''Grand Caravan''' was introduced around 1987. The original Caravan was dropped after the 2007 model year and replaced by the '''Dodge Journey'''. Grand Caravan has been redesigned many times through the years. Chrysler announced that 2015 would be the last model year for the Grand Caravan, as part of the plan to refocus Dodge as a performance brand. However the Grand Caravan made a come for a later successor.
