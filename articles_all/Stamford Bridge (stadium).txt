
'''Stamford Bridge''' is the home of English Premier League|Premier League club Chelsea F.C.. It is located in Fulham in southwest London. 

It was built for Fulham F.C.|Fulham Football Club, who turned it down for money reasons, so Chelsea was created and decided to play at the ground. It currently holds a capacity of 41,631 seats. It is one of the oldest football stadiums in the world, being opened in 1877. It was renovated in 1998
