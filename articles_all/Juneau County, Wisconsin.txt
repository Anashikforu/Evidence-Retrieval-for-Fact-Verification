
'''Juneau County''' is a county (United States)|county found in the U.S. state|state of Wisconsin in the United States.  24,316 people were living in Juneau County as of 2000.  The county seat of Juneau County is Mauston, Wisconsin|Mauston.  The city Juneau, Wisconsin should not be confused with this county, because they are not related.

 Geography 
Juneau County has an area of 804 square miles, 37 of which is water, wikt:according|according to the United States Census Bureau.
