 

The '''Mayan languages''' ares a group of languages spoken in Central America and Mesoamerica.In linguistics, it is usual to use ''Mayan'' when talking about  the language 

Mayan languages are spoken by at least 6 million Maya people|native Maya. Most of them are in Guatemala, Mexico or Belize. In 1996, Guatemala formally recognized 21 Mayan languages by name, and Mexico recognises eight others.

The Mayan languages are some of the best documented and most studied in the Americas.  p.165. Modern Mayan languages come from Proto-Mayan, a language that was probably spoken at least 5000 years ago. It has been partly reconstructed.
