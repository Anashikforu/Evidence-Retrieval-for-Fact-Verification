



'''''Cypripedium reginae''''', also known as he '''Showy Lady's-slipper''', the '''Pink-and-white Lady's-slipper''', or the '''Queen's Lady's-slipper''', is a rare wikt:temperate|temperate Cypripedioideae|lady's-slipper Orchidaceae|orchid native to northern North America. It grows white flowers, with pink labella.
