



'''Wolfgang Paul''' (; born 25 January 1940 in Olsberg, Germany|Olsberg, Province of Westphalia) is a German former Association football|football player.

Captaining Borussia Dortmund to the European Cup Winners Cup in 1966, Paul got included in Helmut Schön's West German national football team for the 1966 FIFA World Cup. Despite this, the defender never played a match for West Germany national football team|West Germany and had to retire early because Wolfgang picked up an injury in the late 1960s.
