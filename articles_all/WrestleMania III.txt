

'''WrestleMania III''' was the third yearly WrestleMania professional wrestling pay-per-view event made by the World Wrestling Entertainment|World Wrestling Federation (WWF). The event was held on March 29, 1987 at the Pontiac Silverdome in Pontiac, Michigan.

The event is particularly notable for the record attendance of 93,173, the largest recorded attendance for a live indoor sporting event in North America.
