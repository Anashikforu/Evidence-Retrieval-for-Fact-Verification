

'''Mountain-mahogany''' is a family (biology)|family of 6 types of shrub. They are found in the United States and Mexico in very dry areas. They usually grow three to six metre|meters tall but can get as tall as 13 meters.  
Mountain-mahogany is an extremely important upland forage for New Mexico Mule Deer and to a lesser extent American Elk.
