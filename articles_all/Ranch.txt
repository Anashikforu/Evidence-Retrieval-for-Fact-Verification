

A '''ranch''' is a large farm for raising cows, horses, sheep or other livestock. The word ''ranch'' is from the Spanish language|Spanish word "rancho". It is used in American English|American and Canadian English.

A ranch may be on private or public land. The desert areas of the western United States have many ranches, because there is much land that is not very good for crops. There are also many in desert areas of Australia, where they are called Station (agriculture)|stations. Someone who takes care of the livestock may be called a stockman or cowboy.
