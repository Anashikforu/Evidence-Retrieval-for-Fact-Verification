'''Nankana Sahib District''' or '''Khairpur Bhattian''' () is a district in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Nankana Sahib is the capital.

Administration
Nankana Sahib District is administratively subdivided into four tehsils:

 Nankana Sahib Tehsil
 Safdarabad Tehsil
 Sangla Hill Tehsil
 Shahkot Tehsil
