

'''SummerSlam''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), which took place on August 17, 2008 at Bankers Life Fieldhouse|Conseco Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, Indiana. It was the 21st annual SummerSlam event and starred List of World Wrestling Entertainment employees|wrestlers from the WWE Raw|Raw, WWE SmackDown|SmackDown and Extreme Championship Wrestling (WWE)|ECW WWE Brand Extension|brands.
