
The '''2001 Formula One''' season was the 52nd edition of the championship. The List of Formula One World Drivers' Champions|Driver's Champion was Michael Schumacher. He dominated all the whole season, and won with a record margin of 58 points. Scuderia Ferrari|Ferrari won the List of Formula One World Constructors' Champions|Constructors' Championship.

After four years out of the sport, Renault F1|Renault returned as an engine supplier. Michelin returned as a Tire|tyre supplier. This was the first time Bridgestone had competition since Goodyear left the sport at the end of the  season.

2001 saw the first Formula One races for future champions Fernando Alonso and Kimi Räikkönen. It was the last year Jean Alesi and world champion Mika Häkkinen would race in Formula One.

At the end of the 2001, the Prost Grand Prix|Prost team closed down due to lack of money. The France|French company Renault purchased the Benetton Formula|Benetton team. Benetton was renamed as the Renault F1 team.
