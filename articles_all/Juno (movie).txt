


'''''Juno''''' is a 2007 United States|American teen movie|teen comedy-drama movie set in Minnesota. It was screenwriter|written by Diablo Cody and movie director|directed by Jason Reitman. It starred Elliot Page as Juno MacGuff, a teenage girl who becomes pregnancy|pregnant by her best friend (who is played by Michael Cera). The movie made $231 million around the world.

Social impact
In the movie, Juno MacGuff thinks about getting an abortion and goes to an abortion clinic, but chooses not to. Both people who think abortion should be legal and people who think it should not be legal thought that the movie supported their views.

Reception
''Juno'' got good reviews from most movie critics who saw it. 93% of the critics listed on RottenTomatoes.com gave the movie a good review. ''Juno'' won an Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay.
