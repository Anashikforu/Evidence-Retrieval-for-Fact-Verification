
'''Backlash (2009)''' was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by WWE|World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It was held on April 26, 2009 at the Dunkin' Donuts Center in Providence, Rhode Island|Providence, Rhode Island. It was the eleventh and last event with the "WWE Backlash|Backlash" name. Backlash would later be replaced by WWE Extreme Rules|Extreme Rules in 2010.
