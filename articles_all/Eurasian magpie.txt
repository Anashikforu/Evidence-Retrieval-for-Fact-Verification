

The '''Eurasian magpie''' or '''common magpie''' (''Pica pica'') is a bird that lives in Europe, much of Asia, and northwest Africa. It is one of several birds in the Corvidae|crow family named as magpies.

In Europe, "magpie" is used by English language|English speakers to call the European magpie; it is the only magpie in Europe outside the Iberian Peninsula.
