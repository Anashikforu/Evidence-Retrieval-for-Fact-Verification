'''Peter James Crouch''' (born 30 January 1981) is an English people|English Association football|footballer. He was born in Macclesfield and was raised in Singapore and Ealing, west London. At 6 feet, 7&nbsp;inches (201&nbsp;cm), he is one of the tallest people to ever play in the English Premier League|Premier League. Crouch played for Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool and England. He was the first player to score 10 goals for England in a single year. He is known for his famous robotic dance celebration, which he first performed after his goal against Hungary in May 2006.
