Biography
Okamoto was born on December 14, 1933. When he was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he won 1953 and 1955 Emperor's Cup at All Kwangaku which was consisted of his alma mater Kwansei Gakuin University players and graduates. After graduating from university, he played for Kashiwa Reysol|Hitachi.

On January 2, 1955, when Okamoto was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Myanmar national football team|Burma. He played 5 games for Japan in 1955.
