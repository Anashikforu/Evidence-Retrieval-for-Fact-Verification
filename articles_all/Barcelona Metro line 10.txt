'''Line 10''' ('''Zona Franca ZAL - Gorg''') is the name of one of the two branches of the Barcelona metro extension Barcelona Metro line 9|line 9, currently under construction and to be operated by Transports Metropolitans de Barcelona|TMB.



 Overview 
It links the Zona Franca with Badalona, through Barcelona proper, sharing a good deal of the area covered by Barcelona metro line 9|L9. L9/10 will be the deepest line in the network, and the longest line in Europe. Originally expected to be ready by 2008, ongoing problems with its construction are going to delay its completion until as late as 2013.
