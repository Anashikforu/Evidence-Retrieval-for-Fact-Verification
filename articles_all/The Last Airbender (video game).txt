

'''''The Last Airbender''''' is a video game for Wii and Nintendo DS. It is based on the The Last Airbender|movie of the same name. It was released on 29 June 2010 in USA. It was released in Europe on 6 August 2010. It will be released in Australia on September 2010. Like the previous Avatar: The Last Airbender|''Avatar'' games, it was developed by THQ Studio Australia and published by THQ.
