
'''Oladele Brendon Ayanbadejo''' (born September 6, 1976 in Chicago, Illinois|Chicago, Illinois) is an Americans|American American football|football Linebacker and Special teamer for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). He has played for 8 NFL teams as well as 3 Canadian Football League|CFL teams. He played college football at University of California, Los Angeles|UCLA. He won Super Bowl XLVII with the Ravens when they defeated the San Francisco 49ers 34-31.
