
'''Jason D. Harrow''' (born May 12, 1976 in Scarborough, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian rapper/songwriter and record producer better known by his stage name, '''Kardinal Offishall'''. 

Offishall has won many awards including 3 Juno Awards, 4 MuchMusic Video Awards, 3 SOCAN Awards and 1 Canadian Urban Music Awards|Canadian Urban Music Award.
