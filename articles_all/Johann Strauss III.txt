

'''Johann Strauss III''' (February 16, 1866 - January 9, 1939) was an Austrian composer and Conductor (music)|conductor. He was the son of Eduard Strauss, the nephew of Johann Strauss II, and grandson of Johann Strauss I. He wrote several Waltz (music)|waltzes, as well as an operetta, ''Katze und Maus'' (Cat and Mouse).
