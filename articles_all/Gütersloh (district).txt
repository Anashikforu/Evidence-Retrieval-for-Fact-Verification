

'''Gütersloh''' () is a Kreis (district) in the north-east of North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany.

 History 
''Kreis Gütersloh'' was made in 1973 when all of the districts in North Rhine-Westphalia, where changed. The old districts of Halle and Wiedenbrück were joined, and parts of the districts of Bielefeld, Paderborn (district)|Paderborn, Beckum and Warendorf were added to the new district.
