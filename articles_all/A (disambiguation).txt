'''A''' is the first letter of the alphabet. It may also refer to:

In English, it is one of the two article (grammar)|indefinite articles, the other being ''an''.
 In measurement, A is a symbol for area.
 In medicine, A is one of the human blood types.
 In music, A is a Note (music)|note.
 In the International System of Units, A is the symbol for the ampere (which is also shortened to ''amp'').
 In school, an A+ is the highest Grade (education)|grade you can get, with an A being the grade below.
 In transport, A is the international license plate code for Austria.
In the New York Stock Exchange, A is the symbol for Agilent Technologies.
