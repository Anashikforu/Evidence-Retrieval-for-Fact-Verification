 

'''Jango Fett''' is a Star Wars bounty hunter and the genetic template of the Clone troopers for the Clone Wars. He made his first appearance in Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones|Attack of the Clones (2002).

Portrayals
As with his clones and Boba Fett, he is portrayed by Temuera Morrison.
