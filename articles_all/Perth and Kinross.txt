'''Perth and Kinross''' (''Peairt agus Ceann Rois'' in Scottish Gaelic|Gaelic) is one of 32 unitary council areas in Scotland. It borders onto the Aberdeenshire (unitary authority area)|Aberdeenshire, Angus, City of Dundee, Fife, Clackmannanshire, Stirling (district)|Stirling, Argyll and Bute and Highland (council area)|Highland council areas. Perth, Scotland|Perth  is the administrative centre.  It corresponds broadly, but not exactly, with the former Counties of Scotland|counties of Perthshire and Kinross-shire.
