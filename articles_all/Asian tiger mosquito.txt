The '''Asian tiger mosquito''', or '''forest day mosquito''' is a kind of mosquito that is native to tropics|tropical and subtropical South-East Asia. It has black and white striped legs, and small black and white striped body. The Asian tiger mosquito is about 2 to 10&nbsp;mm long.Y.-M.
