

'''Barneveld''' () is a municipality in the middle of the Netherlands, in the province Gelderland. The main centre is the village of Barneveld. 

The municipality has an area of 176.74 km². 

 Towns and villages of the municipality



Other centres are: Essen (Barneveld)|Essen, Esveld, Garderbroek, Harselaar (dorp)|Harselaar, Kallenbroek, Moorst (partly), Wessel and Recreatiegebied Zeumeren.

 Geography 
The municipality neighbours Nijkerk, Putten (Gelderland)|Putten and Ermelo (Netherlands)|Ermelo to the north. Apeldoorn (gemeente)|Apeldoorn lies to the east. Municipalities Ede (municipality)|Ede, Renswoude, Scherpenzeel (Gelderland)|Scherpenzeel and Woudenberg are to the south. Municipalities Leusden and Amersfoort neighbour Barneveld to the west.
