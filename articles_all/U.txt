

'''U''' ('''u''') is the twenty-first (21) letter in the English language|English alphabet (it is also used as a short form for 'you').

 Meanings for U 
 On calendars, U is most times a abbreviation|short letter for Sunday, for use when S is being used for Saturday.
 In chemistry, U is the symbol for uranium.
 It is pronounced "you".
