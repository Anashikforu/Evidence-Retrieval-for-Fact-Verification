
The '''Visayas''' or '''Visayan Islands''' (Cebuano language|Cebuano and Waray-Waray|Wáray-Wáray: ''Kabisay-an'', Tagalog language|Tagalog: ''Kabisayaan'', Spanish language|Spanich: ''Bisayas''), is one of the three principal geographical divisions of  the Philippines; the other two divisions are Mindanao, to the south, and Luzon, to the north.

People living in this islands are called ''Visayans''.

Geography
The Visayas consists of several islands in central Philippines around the Visayan Sea, with the Philippine Sea to the east and the Sulu Sea to the west.

The main islands (and area) are:

 Cebu (island)|Cebu,

Regions and provinces
 of the Visayas.



The major islands, from west to east, are Panay Island|Panay, Negros Island|Negros, Cebu Island|Cebu, Bohol (island)|Bohol, Leyte Island|Leyte, and Samar Island|Samar.
Administratively, the Visayas is divided into 3 Regions of the Philippines|regions: Western Visayas, Central Visayas and Eastern Visayas. Each region is headed by a Regional Director.

The Visayas is composed of 16 Provinces of the Philippines|provinces, each headed by a governor.

=Western Visayas (Region VI)=
Western Visayas consists of the islands of Panay Island|Panay and the western half of Negros Island|Negros; it has a population, in 2010, of 7,102,438. The regional center is Iloilo City. Its provinces are:
 Aklan Province|Aklan
 Antique Province|Antique
 Capiz
 Guimaras
 Iloilo Province|Iloilo
 Negros Occidental

=Central Visayas (Region VII)=
Central Visayas includes the islands of Cebu Island|Cebu and Bohol (island)|Bohol and the eastern half of Negros Island|Negros; it has a population, in 2010, of 6,800,180. The regional center is Cebu City. Its provinces are:
 Bohol (province)|Bohol
 Cebu Province|Cebu
 Negros Oriental
 Siquijor

=Eastern Visayas (Region VIII)=
Eastern Visayas consists of the islands of Leyte (island)|Leyte and Samar (island)|Samar; it has a population, in 2010, of 4,101,322. The regional center is Tacloban. Its provinces are:
 Biliran
 Leyte Province|Leyte
 Southern Leyte
 Eastern Samar
 Northern Samar
 Samar Province|Samar
