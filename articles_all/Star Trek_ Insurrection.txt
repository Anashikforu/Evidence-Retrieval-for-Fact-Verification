


'''''Star Trek: Insurrection''''' is the ninth movie set in the Star Trek fictional universe|universe. It was made in 1998 by Paramount Pictures. Captain Jean-Luc Picard|Picard fights a Starfleet admiral who wants to move a race from their planet to use its natural resources.
