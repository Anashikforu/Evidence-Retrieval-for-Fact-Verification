
'''Kathy Bates''' (born Kathleen Doyle Bates, June 28, 1948) is an Americans|American actress and director. She was born in Memphis, Tennessee.

Bates has starred in many movies, most notably ''Misery (movie)|Misery'', ''Fried Green Tomatoes (movie)|Fried Green Tomatoes'', ''Titanic (1997 movie)|Titanic'' and ''Richard Jewell (movie)|Richard Jewell''.

Bates had ovarian cancer in 2003
Bates is also known for her multiple roles on ''American Horror Story''. She also played Jo Bennett in the American sitcom ''The Office (U.S. TV series)|The Office''.
