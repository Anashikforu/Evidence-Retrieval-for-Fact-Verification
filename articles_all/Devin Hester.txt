
'''Devin Hester''' (born November 4, 1982 in Riviera Beach, Florida) is an Americans|American American football|football return specialist for the Atlanta Falcons. Hester was drafted out of the University of Miami by the Chicago Bears with the 57th pick in the second round of the 2006 NFL Draft. Hester won an ESPY Award in 2007 for the Breakthrough Player of the Year.
