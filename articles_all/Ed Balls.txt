


'''Edward Michael "Ed" Balls''' (born 25 February 1967) is a British people|British politician.  He was the Shadow Minister|shadow Home Secretary in Ed Miliband's shadow cabinet. He was born in Norwich, Norfolk and moved to Nottinghamshire during his childhood. He is the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party and was theCo-operative Party Member of Parliament (MP) for the constituency of Morley and Outwood (UK Parliament constituency)|Morley and Outwood in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom.

He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom 2005 general election|2005 general election and held the post of Secretary of State for Children, Schools and Families from June 2007 to May 2010. He was a candidate to become leader of the Labour Party in September 2010 and lost to Ed Miliband. Ed Balls lost his seat in the United Kingdom general election, 2015|2015 general election
