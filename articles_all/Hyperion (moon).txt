
'''Hyperion''' is a Satellite (natural)|moon of Saturn (planet)|Saturn. It was discovered on September 16, 1848 by William Cranch Bond, George Phillips Bond and William Lassell. It is irregular in shape, and has a sponge-like appearance. It is the second largest irregular (i.e. not sphere|spherical) moon in the Solar System, the largest being Proteus (moon)|Proteus. Its rotation is chaotic, meaning its Axis (mathematics)|axis of rotation wobbles, unlike how the Earth and the Moon rotate, or any other moon in the Solar System.
