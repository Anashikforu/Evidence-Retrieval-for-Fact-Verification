A '''department store''' is a Shop|store that sells many different Product (consumer goods)|products. It does not sell the products of only one company, as some other stores do. In a department store a person can usually buy clothing, furniture, electronics, and sometimes paint, Household hardware|hardware, toiletries, cosmetics, photographic equipment, jewellery, toys, and Sports equipment. Department stores are often upmarket places. 

Some department stores sell products cheaper than other stores. They are then called discount department stores.
