'''Calcite''' is one of the most common minerals on the Earth's surface. It is a often part of sedimentary rocks, especially limestone. It is also the primary mineral in Metamorphic rock|metamorphic marble.

It is a chemical or biochemical calcium carbonate with the formula Calcium carbonate|CaCO3. It is a carbonate mineral. It can be a Vein (geology)|vein mineral in Deposition (geology)|deposits from hot springs, and in caverns as stalactites and stalactite|stalagmites.
