
'''Masashi Kishimoto''' (, ''Kishimoto Masashi'') (born 8 November 1974) is a Japanese manga artist. He is best known for creating the manga series ''Naruto (manga)|Naruto''. He has been drawing from a very early age. Osamu Kajisa (''Tattoo Hearts'') and Yuuichi Itakura (''Hand's'') served as his assistants. Kishimoto was born in Okayama Prefecture, Japan. His brother is Seishi Kishimoto.
