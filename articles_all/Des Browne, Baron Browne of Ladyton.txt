

'''Desmond "''Des''" Henry Browne, Baron Browne ''' (born March 22, 1952) is a Scotland|Scottish politician and was Secretary of State for Defence and Secretary of State for Scotland. He was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election, and was the Member of Parliament for the Kilmarnock and Loudoun (UK Parliament constituency)|Kilmarnock and Loudoun constituency in the United Kingdom until 2010, when he was created a Life Peer 

He is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
