
'''Paul Donald Wight''' (born February 8, 1971) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler and actor. He is currently signed to AEW. He is best known by his ring name, '''Paul Wright'''.

He is also best known for his career in World Championship Wrestling (WCW), where he wrestled as The Giant from June 1995 to December 1998. He is the first man in the history of wrestling to win the WWE Championship, WCW World Heavyweight Championship and the ECW World Heavyweight Championship|ECW World Championship, even though his ECW Title win was after the company became a WWE property.
