
'''Frank Oz''' (born '''Richard Frank Oznowicz''' on May 25, 1944) is an English people|English-Americans|American puppeteer, actor, and filmmaker. He is known for his work on ''The Muppets'', ''Star Wars'', and ''Sesame Street''. He also directed some popular films, such as ''Little Shop of Horrors (film)|Little Shop of Horrors'' and ''Dirty Rotten Scoundrels''. His most popular puppets are Yoda and Cookie Monster. He played Cookie Monster, Bert and Grover on Sesame Street and he did Miss Piggy, Fozzie Bear, Animal, and Sam the Eagle on the Muppets.
