'''Erzincan''' is the capital of Erzincan Province|Erzincan Provinces of Turkey|Province in the eastern Anatolian region of Turkey. 


On December 27, 1939 at 01:57 UTC, the city was hit by a Seismic magnitude scales|magnitude 7.8 earthquake, the most powerful to hit the country since the 1668 North Anatolia earthquake,

At 08:18 UTC on 13 March 1992, a magnitude 6.8 earthquake shook the city, killing 652 people and injuring more than 2,000. The city was badly damaged. 150 buildings collapsed and 8,000 buildings were damaged so badly, that they were later Demolition|demolished.
