

A '''punching bag''' is a tough bag which is supposed to be Punch (strike)|punched a lot.  It is used in exercise or Chronic stress|stress relief to improve one of three areas: Strength training|physical strength, aerobics|aerobic fitness or punching technique.

History
Punching bags have been used in martial arts for as long as there has been a written history of military training. Similar apparatus in Asian martial arts include the Okinawan ''makiwara'' and the Chinese ''mook jong'', which may have padded striking surfaces attached to them.
