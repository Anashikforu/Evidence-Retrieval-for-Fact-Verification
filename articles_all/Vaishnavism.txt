


'''Vaishnavism''' () is a tradition of Hinduism. Its followers worship Vishnu or his avatars, mainly Rama and Krishna as the highest, or original God. This worship in different perspectives or historical traditions addresses God under the names of Narayana, Krishna, VÄsudeva or more often "Vishnu", and their associated avatars.
