
'''Brent Michael Bowden''' (born May 21, 1987 in Huntsville, Alabama|Huntsville, Alabama) is an Americans|American american football|football Punter (American football)|Punter that is currently a free agent in the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Bowden was drafted out of the Virginia Tech University with the 172nd pick in the sixth round of the 2010 NFL Draft by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.
