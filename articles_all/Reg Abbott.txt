
'''Reginald Abbott''' (born February 4, 1930) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey player who played in the National Hockey League (NHL).

 Playing career 
Reg Abbott played only three games in the NHL in 1952–53 NHL season|1952–53 for the Montreal Canadiens. During those three games, he did not score any points nor did he earn any penalty minutes.

Abbott also played baseball as the second baseman for the provincial champion Rosedales in Manitoba.
