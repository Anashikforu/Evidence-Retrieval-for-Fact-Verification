
'''Rectory Road railway station''' is a railway station in Stoke Newington in the London Borough of Hackney, North London, United Kingdom.

It is on the Seven Sisters branch of the Lea Valley Lines, between Stoke Newington railway station|Stoke Newington and Hackney Downs railway station|Hackney Downs. It is in Zone 2 and has two platforms.

Trains run south to Liverpool Street station|Liverpool Street and north to Enfield Town railway station|Enfield Town and Cheshunt railway station|Cheshunt.
