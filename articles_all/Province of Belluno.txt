
The '''Province of Belluno''' (; German language|German: ''Provinz Belluno'') is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Veneto region of Italy. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Belluno.

Geography
The province of Belluno has an area of  high.

The province is rich in water, with the presence of the wide Piave river that flows through the province from north to south.

The southern part of the province is called ''Valbelluna'', the widest and most populous valley of the province.



The province of Belluno borders to the north with Austria (Tyrol (state)|Tyrol and Carinthia (state)|Carinthia), to the east with the Friuli-Venezia Giulia|Friuli Venezia Giulia region (Province of Udine|Udine and Province of Pordenone|Pordenone provinces), to the south with the Province of Treviso|Treviso and Province of Vicenza|Vicenza provinces) and to the west and northwest with the Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol region (Province of Trento|Trento and South Tyrol provinces).

= Language minorities =
In the province, there are two language Minority group|minorities, which are now recognized by law:
a Ladin language|Ladin (a Romance languages|Romance language) minority is present in the three communes of Colle Santa Lucia, Livinallongo del Col di Lana and Cortina d'Ampezzo.
a German language|German-speaking minority in the commune of Sappada, Farra d'Alpago and Tambre.
