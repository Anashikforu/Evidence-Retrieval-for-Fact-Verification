
'''Mekhi Thira Phifer''' (born December 29, 1974) is an Americans|American actor. He was born in Harlem|Harlem, New York. He played Greg Pratt on NBC's television series ''ER (TV series)|ER''. He won season four of Bravo's ''Celebrity Poker Showdown''. He had a recurring role in ''Curb Your Enthusiasm''.
