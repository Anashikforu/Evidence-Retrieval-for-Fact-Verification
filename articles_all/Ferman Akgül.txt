


'''Ferman Akgül'''  (born 25 December 1979 in Ankara) is a Turkey|Turkish songwriter  and the singer of the Rock music|rock band ''maNga (band)|maNga''. He writes both lyrics and music.

Ferman Akgül was born in Ankara and went to the Ankara Anatolian High School. He began playing classical guitar at the age of 9-10.
