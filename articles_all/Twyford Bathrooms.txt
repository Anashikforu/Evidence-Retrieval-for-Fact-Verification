
'''Twyford Bathrooms''' is a porcelain sanitary company founded in 1849, headquartered in Alsager, UK. Its core product is toilets however they also make bathroom fixtures and plumbing supplies. In 2010 it was announced that the company would have its headquarters based outside the UK and also some of its factories.
