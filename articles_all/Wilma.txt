'''Wilma''' is a female given name. It is a short form of the German name Wilhelmina (given name)|Wilhelmina.  The name means ''protector''.  The male version of the name is Will, short for William (name)|William.

People with this name include:
Wilma Mankiller, principal chief of the Cherokee Nation
Wilma Rudolph, American athlete
Wilma de Faria, Brazilian politician
