
'''''Philadelphia''''' is a 1993 United States|American drama movie starring Tom Hanks and Denzel Washington. It is about Andrew Beckett (Hanks), a gay lawyer who has AIDS. The people he works with find out and dismissal|fire him. He thinks this is unfair and hires another lawyer (Washington) who does not like gay people.
