

'''Virginia Wynette Pugh''' (usually known as '''Tammy Wynette''', May 5, 1942 â€“ April 6, 1998), was an Americans|American country music singer. She was born in Mississippi.

The best-known of her songs is "Stand by Your Man". She had 17 number-one hits during her career.

She died of pulmonary thrombosis.

Personal life
Wynette was married 5 times. Her third husband was George Jones, also a singer of country music.
