

'''''''''' (English language|English: ''Powerful and powerless: Tore Tønne, media and power play behind the scenes'') is a book written by Ivar Hippe, a Norwegian. The book is about the Tore Tønne case.

 Development 
When writing the book Ivar Hippe talked to over 60 people who knew Tore Tønne.

 Reviews 
Critics liked the book, with both ''Aftenposten'' and ''Dagbladet'' writing a very positive review about it.
