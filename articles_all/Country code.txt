'''Country codes''' are in mostly times created by few letters or numbers that represent geography|geographical codes (geocodes) of country|countries that use certain country code, it is in use in data processing and communications. There are few different systems created for this. The most known is ISO 3166-1. The term ''country code'' in many times refers to international dialing codes, the E.164 list of country calling codes|country calling codes.
