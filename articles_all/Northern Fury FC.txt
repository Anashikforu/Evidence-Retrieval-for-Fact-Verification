

'''Northern Fury F.C.''' is a association football|soccer club from Townsville, Queensland, Australia. The club was founded in 2008 and competed in the A-League as ''North Queensland Fury FC''. On 1 March 2011, the club was removed from the league due to financial instability. On 3 October 2012, the club was re-formed as Northern Fury FC after it was granted a license to play in the National Premier League Queensland|NPL Queensland.
