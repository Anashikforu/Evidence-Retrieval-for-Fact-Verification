
'''Taquería''' (sometimes misspelled as ''taquiria'') is a Spanish language|Spanish word meaning ''taco shop''. In some places, means a restaurant specializing in burritos, although tacos and other Mexico|Mexican dishes are often served as well. The first ''taquerías'' were people in stalls on the streets, but now many ''taquerías'' have moved into buildings and become restaurants.
