
'''Edward Shane Lechler''' (born August 7, 1976 in East Bernard, Texas|East Bernard, Texas) is an Americans|American American football|football punter for the Oakland Raiders of the National Football League (NFL). Lechler was drafted out of Texas A&M University by the Oakland Raiders with the 142nd pick in the fifth round of the 2000 NFL Draft.
