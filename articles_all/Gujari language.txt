

'''Gojri''', also known as 'Gujari' is an Indo-Aryan language spoken by the Gurjars, or Gujjars of Northern Areas|Northern Pakistan and the Republic of India. It is classified as a form or dialect of Rajasthani by the linguists.

The language is mainly spoken in the Punjab provinces of India|Hindustan and Pakistan and also in Jammu & Kashmir, it is most closely related to Marwari and Gujarati language|Gujarati.
