
'''WWE''' is an United States|American professional wrestling promotion based in Stamford, Connecticut. WWE personnel consists of professional wrestling|professional wrestlers, managers, Sports commentator|play-by-play and color commentators, ring announcers, interviewers, referees, trainers, producers, road agents, creative writers, and various other positions. Executives and board of directors|board members are also listed.

WWE contracts typically range from developmental contracts to multi-year deals. WWE describes most personnel as independent contractors. Personnel on the main roster appear on specific brands such as WWE Raw|Raw and WWE SmackDown|SmackDown Live while they can also appear on WWE programs|WWE's primary weekly television programming such as Main Event, WWE Superstars|Superstars, Total Divas as well as List of WWE pay-per-view events|pay-per-views, and at live events.

Personnel from the WWE NXT|NXT division appear on the television series of the same name, which airs online in the U.S. and on TV in select countries, and at live events. Personnel on the main roster may wrestle in NXT and vice versa.

If someone is inactive for any reason, due to injury, suspension, not appearing in more than 30 days, or otherwise, that information is noted. WWE refers to its performers as superstars.

Overview
As of 2021, WWE employs over 800 full-time employees. It also has many vendors and service providers working for it. 

=Brands=
Since the WWE's WWE brand extension|brand extension in 2016, performers work on one of two "main roster" brands – Raw (WWE brand)|Raw and SmackDown (WWE brand)|SmackDown.  

A third brand, NXT (WWE brand)|NXT, was created to be a developmental brand for the main roster. New people would go there to train to work for the main brands. In 2019 it was made into the third main roster brand. Some developmental performers work on NXT's United Kingdom-based brand NXT UK (WWE brand)|NXT UK.

Main roster performers work on ''WWE Raw|Monday Night Raw'' and ''WWE SmackDown|Friday Night SmackDown''. Developmental wrestlers work on ''WWE NXT|NXT 2.0'', ''WWE NXT Level Up|NXT: Level Up'' and  NXT UK,. At times, some main roster performers are seen on NXT.

Personnel in WWE's developmental system usualy train at the WWE Performance Center#United States|Performance Center in Orlando, Florida. There is also a WWE UK Performance Center|UK Performance Center in London, England.

=Championships=
Eaach brand has List of current champions in WWE|their own distinct championships. There are some titles which do not belong to either brand. Wrestlers from any brand can win these championships. They include the WWE 24/7 Championship and the WWE Women's Tag Team Championship.

McMahon family
The McMahon family are the main owners of WWE. In addition to their job for the company, they also appear on-air as Professional wrestling authority figures|authority figures and wrestlers on all of the brands.
