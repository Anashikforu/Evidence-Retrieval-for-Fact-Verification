'''Luba Goy''' (born 8 November 1945 in  Germany) is now a Canadians|Canadian comedienne and a Canada|Canadian television star.  She is of Ukraine|Ukrainian descent. She was probably best known as one of the stars of the Canadian comedy show ''Royal Canadian Air Farce''.
