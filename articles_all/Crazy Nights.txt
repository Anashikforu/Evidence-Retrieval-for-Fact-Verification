
'''''Crazy Nights''''' is a album|studio album by the United States|American hard rock/heavy metal music|heavy metal band Kiss (band)|Kiss. It was released on September 18, 1987. "Crazy Crazy Nights" was the first song released from the album. It reached #4 in the UK.
