

The '''1974 Atlantic hurricane season''' began on June 1, 1974 and ended on November 30, 1974. This season was about average in activity 11 storms, 7 of which got named. This season was one of the deadliest on record, most deaths were caused by Hurricane Fifi-Orlene|Hurricane Fifi. This season also had the most Subtropical storms (4).
