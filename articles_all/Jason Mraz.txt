
'''Jason Mraz''' (born June 23, 1977) is an Americans|American singer and songwriter. He has put out five albums, ''Waiting for My Rocket to Come'' (2002), ''Mr. A-Z'' (2005), '' We Sing. We Dance. We Steal Things'' (2008),  ''Love Is a Four Letter Word'' (2012), and "Yes!"(2014). Jason Mraz became internationally famous after the release of the single "I'm Yours" from the album ''We Sing. We Dance. We Steal Things''.
