'''Fatu Hiva''' is a tropical island in the Pacific Ocean. It is part of French Polynesia and the Marquesas Islands. 

There are about 600 people living on Fatu Hiva. It is 22&nbsp;km away from Motu Nao. The three main villages are Hana Vave, Omo'a and Uia.
