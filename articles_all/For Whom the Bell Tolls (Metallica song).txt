
'''For Whom the Bell Tolls''' is a 1985 Thrash metal song by rock band Metallica from their second album Ride the Lightning. The song's name comes from Ernest Hemingway's 1940 For Whom the Bell Tolls|novel of the same name.

In pop culture
The song was featured in the opening credits of the movie ''Zombieland''. 
It is played when the National Football League|NFL's Chicago Bears score a touchdown.
The song is used in the trailer for the video game Gaia: Honor and Vengeance.
