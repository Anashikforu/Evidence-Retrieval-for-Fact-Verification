
'''Valeri Mikhailovich Zelepukin''' (born September 17, 1968 in Voskresensk, Soviet Union|URS) is a Russians|Russian retired professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|left winger. He played a total of 11 seasons in the National Hockey League|NHL; 7 with the New Jersey Devils, 1 with the Edmonton Oilers, 2 with the Philadelphia Flyers and 1 with the Chicago Blackhawks. He moved back to Russia in 2002 and retired. He won the Stanley Cup with the New Jersey Devils in 1995.
