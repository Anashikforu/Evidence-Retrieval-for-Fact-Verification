'''''Futabasaurus''''' was a plesiosaur from the Upper Cretaceous. A complete skeleton of one has not been found. It is not known very well. 

''Futabasaurus'' is the first Elasmosauridae|elasmosaurid found in Japan. The type species is ''F. suzukii''.  Many of the bones of the holotype|type specimen show apparent scavenging or predation by sharks.
