'''Kim Delaney''' (born November 29, 1961) is an Americans|American movie and television actress. She played Jenny Gardner in the soap opera|soap ''All My Children''. She played a detective in the television series ''NYPD Blue''. She also acted in the television series ''Philly''. Delaney was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
