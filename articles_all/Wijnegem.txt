'''Wijnegem''' is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Antwerp (province)|Antwerp.

In 2007, 8890 people lived there.

It is at 51° 13 North, 04° 31 East.

Wijnegem is the home of the Wijnegem Shopping Center.
