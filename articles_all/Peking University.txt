
'''Peking University''' (PKU; Beijing University) is a prestigious university in Beijing, China. It was created in 1898. The university is also known as '''Beida''' and is associated with C9 League. Peking University ranked as the 22nd best university in the 2020 QS World University Rankings.
History
It was founded during the late Qing Dynasty in 1898. At that time, it was known as the Imperial University of Peking (京师大学堂). Its English name retains the use of "Beijing" which is widely used in other contexts as well.
