
'''Licheń Stary''' is a village. 1200 people live there. It is on the Lichen Lake in the Greater Poland Voivodeship, 15 km north of Konin, Poland. It is often referred to as simply Licheń. Legends trace the name to the ancient Slavic mythology|Slavic pagan deity Licho, whose sanctuary was allegedly located nearby.

It is the home of the famed Sanctuary of Mary (mother of Jesus)|Our Lady of Licheń hosting Our Lady of Sorrows, Queen of Poland.
