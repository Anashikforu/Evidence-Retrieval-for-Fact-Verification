A '''billionaire''' is a person who has a net worth of at least one billion (1,000,000,000) units of currency. This is usually used for major currencies with large values, like Pound sterling|Pound Sterlings £, United States Dollar|American dollars $, or Euro|Euros €.

Billionaires (in United States dollars)
In March 2021, there were 2,755 people in the world who were considered to be billionaires (in United States dollars).

Every year, the American Magazine|business magazine Forbes, and its website, releases a list of billionaires called ''The World's Billionaires''.
