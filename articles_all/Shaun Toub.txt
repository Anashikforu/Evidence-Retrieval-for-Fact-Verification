

'''Shaun Toub''' is an Iranian-United States|American movie and television actor. He is perhaps best known for his role as Farhad in the 2004 movie ''Crash (movie)|Crash'', as Rahim Khan in the movie ''The Kite Runner (movie)|The Kite Runner'', as Ho Yinsen|Yinsen in the movie ''Iron Man (movie)|Iron Man'' and as ''Iroh'' in the movie ''The Last Airbender''.
