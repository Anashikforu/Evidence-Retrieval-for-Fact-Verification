

'''Clarence Henry''' (b. March 27, 1926, d. February 28 1999) was an Americans|American heavyweight Boxing|boxer. Contender for Rocky Marciano's title. 

He was a leading boxer in the heavyweight division in the early to mid-1950s. Henry was 1948 ''Los Angeles Times'' Golden Gloves Heavyweight Champion. 

He was elected into the World Boxing Hall of Fame in 1998.
