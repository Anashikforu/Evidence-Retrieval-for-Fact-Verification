




The '''Bosque do Capão da Imbuia''' (''grove'') is a park in the city of Curitiba in Paraná (state)|Paraná, Brazil.

With a large size area of native forest, the grove (nature)|grove has many attractions for tourism|tourists.

The main attractions are:

 '''"Path of the Araucaria"''' - a path in the woods with Taxidermy|taxidermized animals;

 '''"Natural History Museum"''' - a museum that shows the typical fauna and flora of Brazil

The oldest tree in Curitiba is an ''Ocotea porosa'' that is almost a thousand years old. This tree is in  "'''Bosque do Capão da Imbuia'''".
