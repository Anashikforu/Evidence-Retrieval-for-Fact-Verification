A '''riksha''' or '''rickshaw''' is a kind of vehicle that has two wheels. They are usually pulled by a human but there are types that have been changed to be pulled by a bicycle or have their own motor, Rickshaws are popular vehicles in Bangladesh|Bangal Pradesh  (Bangladesh) and Japan.
