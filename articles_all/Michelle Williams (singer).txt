

'''Tenitra Michelle Williams''' (born July 23, 1979) is an Americans|American gospel music|gospel and rhythm and blues|R&B singer, songwriter, and actor|actress. She was a background singer for Monica (singer)|Monica, before rising to fame as one-third of the successful Grammy Award-winning musical group Destiny's Child, the world's best-selling female group of all time, selling over 100 million records worldwide. 

Williams played the character Shug Avery in the play ''The Color Purple''. She was a contestant in the 2010 series of British reality television show ''Strictly Come Dancing''.

In 2013, she made a return to inspirational music with the lead single, ''If We Had Your Eyes'' from her upcoming fourth studio album titled ''Journey to Freedom''.
