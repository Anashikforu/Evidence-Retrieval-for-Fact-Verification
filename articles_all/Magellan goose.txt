The '''Magellan goose''' (''Chloephaga picta picta'') or '''upland goose''' is a bird of ''Anatidae'' Family (biology)|family. It is most commonly found in the southern part of South America, Tierra del Fuego. The Upland goose is a subspecies of ''Chloephaga picta leucoptera''.
