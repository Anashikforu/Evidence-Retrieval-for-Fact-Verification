"'''18 Days'''" is the second single (music)|single off the 2008 album by Saving Abel called ''Saving Abel (2008 album)|Saving Abel''. The song is also on the previous self-titled album by Saving Abel. The song was released in late 2008 when it took major radio|airplay.

Charts
"18 Days" by Saving Abel reached 14 on the U.S. ''Billboard'' Hot Modern Rock Tracks. The single also peaked at #8 on the U.S. ''Billboard'' Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks.
