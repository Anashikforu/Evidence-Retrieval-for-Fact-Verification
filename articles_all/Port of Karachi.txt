The '''Port of Karachi''' () is Pakistan's largest and busiest harbor|seaport. It handles about 60% of the nation's cargo (25 million tons per year). The port is located between the towns of Kiamari and Saddar. It is close to the center of old Karachi, the main business district, and several industry|industrial areas. The geography|geographic position of Karachi places the port near major transportation|shipping routes such as the Strait of Hormuz. The administration of the port is carried out by the Karachi Port Trust which was created in the 19th century|nineteenth century.
