An '''airén''' is a  type of white grape used to make wine. The grape is native to Spain. It is one of the most grown types of grape worldwide.

The areas of Valdepeñas and La Mancha grow the most Airén. Ciudad Real, Toledo (province)|Toledo, Albacete and Cuenca (province)|Cuenca also grow large amounts. It can also be found significantly in Madrid and as far south as Montilla-Moriles.
