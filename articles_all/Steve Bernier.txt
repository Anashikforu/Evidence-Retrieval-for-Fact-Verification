
'''Steve Bernier''' (born March 31, 1985 in Quebec City, Quebec) is a former Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|right winger. He played for the New Jersey Devils, San Jose Sharks, Buffalo Sabres, Vancouver Canucks and Florida Panthers. The Sharks selected Bernier 16th overall in the 2003 NHL Entry Draft.
