Biography
Sakiya was born in Hiroshima on December 1, 1950. After graduating from Sanyo High School, he joined Nippon Steel Yawata SC|Yawata Steel (later ''Nippon Steel'') in 1969. He retired in 1981. He played 193 games and scored 48 goals in the league.

In September 1971, Sakiya was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for Football at the 1972 Summer Olympics|1972 Summer Olympics qualification. At this qualification, on September 27, he debuted against Philippines national football team|Philippines. He played 3 games for Japan until 1972.
