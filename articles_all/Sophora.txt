
'''''Sophora''''' is a genus of about 45 species of small trees and shrubs. It is in the subfamily ''Faboideae'' of the pea family (biology)|family ''Fabaceae''. Many students see Sophora as three genera: ''Sophora'', ''Calia'' (S. secundiflora) and ''Styphnolobium'' (Styphnolobium|S. japonica).
