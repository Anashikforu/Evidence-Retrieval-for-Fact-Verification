
'''Isaac Slade''' is an Americans|American singer-songwriter. He is a member of the rock and roll|rock band called The Fray. He was born in May 1981. He has released several albums with the band ''The Fray'' including ''How to Save a Life (album)|How to Save a Life'' and ''Live at the Electric Factory''. He records and performs more of a piano rock and acoustic. Singles include ''How to Save a Life'', ''Look After You'', and ''Cable Car''.
