
'''Dodoma''' is the capital (city)|capital city of Tanzania. It is the third biggest city in the country. The city is also the capital of the Dodoma Region|Dodoma region. The city has a population of 410,956 people. The name Dodoma means "It has sunk".

History
During Germany|German colony|colonial rule in East Africa, the town of Dodoma was :wikt:found|founded at the same time as the creation of the Central Line (Tanzania)|Tanzanian central railway. The United Kingdom|British took over the city after World War I. Dodoma became an Administration|administrative center for the region until the independence of Tanzania in 1964. In 1973, plans were made to move the capital from Dar es Salaam to Dodoma. Tanzania's National Assembly moved there the next year. Many government offices are still in Dar es Salaam.
