
'''British Rail Class 415''' (or '''4EPB''') was a suburban 750V Direct current|DC third rail electric multiple unit commissioned by the Southern Region of British Railways. Built between 1951 and 1957, it became the most numerous class on the region after the withdrawal of the British Rail Class 405|4Subs. The final trains were withdrawn in the 1990s, replaced by British Rail Class 455|Class 455, British Rail Class 456|456, British Rail Class 465|465 and British Rail Class 466|466
