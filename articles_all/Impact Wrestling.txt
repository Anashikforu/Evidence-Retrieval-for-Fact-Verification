'''Impact Wrestling''' is a professional wrestling company founded in 2002 shortly after World Championship Wrestling closed down. They are the main rivals for World Wrestling Entertainment.  They made a video game called TNA Impact! (video game)|TNA iMPACT! and a sequel to it called TNA Impact! 2011 after it's weekly program which was of the same name before it was renamed Impact Wrestling.

Jeff Jarrett left the company in December 2013 and announced that he was starting his own professional wrestling promotion called Global Force Wrestling.

On November 19, 2014, it was announced that TNA would be ending their relationship with Spike (TV network)|Spike, and that they would instead partner with Discovery Communications to distribute their shows beginning in January 2015.
