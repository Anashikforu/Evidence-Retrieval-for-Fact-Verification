


'''Seo Ju-hyun''' (born June 28, 1991 in Seoul) is a Koreans|South Korean singer and actress. She is known by her Stage name '''Seohyun'''. She was a member of the girl group Girls' Generation but later become a solo artist. She sings K-pop, Contemporary R&B|R&B and Soul music|soul. She is also a dancer and actress.
