
'''Leighton Baines''' (born 11 December 1984) is a retired English people|English Association football|footballer. He was known for his time at Everton F.C.|Everton and the England national football team.
 
Baines started his career with Wigan Athletic F.C.|Wigan Athletic. He was a defender who plays on the left side. He wears the number 3 on his Everton shirt. He has played for England at England national under-21 football team|under-21 and senior levels. He has scored only one goal while playing for England. He scored four times while at Wigan.
