"'''Shiver'''" is a single released by rock and roll|rock band Coldplay. "Shiver" was released off the album ''Parachutes (album)|Parachutes'' in March 2000. A re-release was released a year later in February 2001. 

Format
The format of the single by Coldplay is a Compact disc|CD. It plays as an Extended Play (or EP).
