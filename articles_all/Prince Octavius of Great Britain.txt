'''Prince Octavius''' (23 February 1779 - 3 May 1783) was the eighth son of the British king George III and his queen, Charlotte of Mecklenburg-Strelitz|Charlotte. He died at the age of four. His father King George once said "There will be no heaven for me if Octavius is not there."
