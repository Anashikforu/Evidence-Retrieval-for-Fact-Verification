

'''Herman Li''' (, Hanyu Pinyin: ''Lǐ Kāng Mǐn'', born 3 October 1976) is the lead and rhythm guitarist, backing Singing|vocalist and record producer|producer in the power metal band DragonForce.

 Personal life
He was born in Hong Kong. His interests other than music are computer|computer technology and martial arts. He practices Jiu-Jitsu five days a week.
Herman Li has played in many bands, including the black metal band Demoniac.

Playing influences
Li likes Computer and video game|video game music and mimics sounds from popular retro games from the late 80s, early-90s arcade, and PC games. He has been known for making several Pac-Man-type noises in the song "Through the Fire and Flames" on ''Inhuman Rampage''.
