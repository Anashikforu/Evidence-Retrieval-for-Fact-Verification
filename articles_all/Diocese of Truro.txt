The '''Diocese of Truro''' is a Church of England diocese in the Province of Canterbury.
The area included in the diocese is that of the county of Cornwall including the Isles of Scilly. It was formed in 1876 from the western parts of the Diocese of Exeter and the first bishop was appointed in 1877.There are 225 parishes with a little more than 300 church buildings in the diocese.
