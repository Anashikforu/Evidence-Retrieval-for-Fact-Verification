
The '''United States Secretary of Housing and Urban Development''' is the head of the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development, concerned with ''urban housing matters.''  The Secretary is a member of the President of the United States|President's United States Cabinet|Cabinet. Marcia Fudge is currently serving as Secretary.

On January 13, 1966, Robert C. Weaver became the first African American Cabinet member by being appointed to the position.
