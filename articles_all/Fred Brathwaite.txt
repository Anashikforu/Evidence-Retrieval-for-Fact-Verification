
'''Fredrick Brathwaite''' (born November 24, 1972) was a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey goaltender that played for Adler Mannheim of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga. He previously played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Edmonton Oilers, Calgary Flames, St. Louis Blues (hockey)|St. Louis Blues, Columbus Blue Jackets and Atlanta Thrashers.  Brathwaite was named Deutsche Eishockey Liga MVP in 2009.
