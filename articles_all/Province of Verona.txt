
The '''Province of Verona''' () is a Provinces of Italy|province in the Veneto region of Italy. Its Capital city|capital is the city of Verona.

Geography
The province of Verona has an area of  high.

The main river in the province is the Adige; it flows through the capital of the province. To the west of the province is the Lake Garda (), the largest lake of Italy.

The climate in the city of Verona is Humid subtropical climate|Humid Subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification: '''Cfa''').



The province of Verona borders to the north with the Province of Trento|Trento (Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol region), to the east with the Province of Vicenza|Vicenza and Province of Padua|Padua provinces, to the southeast with the Province of Rovigo|Rovigo province, to the south and west with the Lombardy region (Province of Mantua|Mantua province) and to the northwest with the Province of Brescia|Brescia province (Lombardy region).
