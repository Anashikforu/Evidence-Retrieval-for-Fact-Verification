'''Patricia Deutsch Ross''' (born '''Elaine Patricia Deutsch'''; December 16, 1943 – July 26, 2017) was an Americans|American actor|actress. She was best known for appearing the television game show  ''Match Game'' in the 1970s.  She was also known for doing voice acting|voices in Animation|animated movies. Deutsch was born in Pittsburgh|Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Deutsch died in Los Angeles, California from cancer on July 26, 2017 at the age of 73.
