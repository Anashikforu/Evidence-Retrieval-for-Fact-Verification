
 

'''Prut''', or '''Pruth''', () is a 953 Kilometre|km long river in Eastern Europe. It flows to the southeast to join the Danube river near Reni, Ukraine|Reni.  This is east of Galaţi. 

Towns
These towns can be found along the Prut River, from source to mouth: Delatyn, Kolomyia, Sniatyn, Chernivtsi, Novoselytsia, Darabani, Ungheni, Cahul.
