

'''''The High End of Low''''' is the seventh studio album by United States|American rock music|rock band, Marilyn Manson (band)|Marilyn Manson. It was released on May 20, 2009. The producers of the album were Sean Beavan, Marilyn Manson (musician)|Marilyn Manson, Chris Vrenna and Jeordie White. Some critics said that they praised the album for showing a more human Manson after following his divorce, and is a return to the sound of the well-received Mechanical Animals.
