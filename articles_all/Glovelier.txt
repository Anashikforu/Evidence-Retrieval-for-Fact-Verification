

'''Glovelier''' is a former municipality of the district of Delémont (district)|Delémont in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland. On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Glovelier, Bassecourt, Courfaivre, Soulce and Undervelier merged into the new municipality of Haute-Sorne.
