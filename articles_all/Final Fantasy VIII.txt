'''''Final Fantasy VIII''''' is a fantasy role-playing video game. It was released for the PlayStation (console)|PlayStation in 1999 and Microsoft Windows|Windows computers in 2000. It was made by Square Co.|Square (now Square Enix). It is the eight title of the ''Final Fantasy'' series. It removed magic point-based spell-casting. It was the first title to consistently use realistically proportioned characters. The game became available on PlayStation Network as a PSone Classics title in 2009.
