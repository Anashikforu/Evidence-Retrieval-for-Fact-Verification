


'''William Henry Macy''' (born March 13, 1950 is an Emmy Award-winning and Academy Award-nominated Americans|American Actor. He was in the movie ''Fargo (movie)|Fargo''. He is also a teacher and theatre director|director in theater|theatre, movie and television. He is now known for his award-winning role as Frank Gallagher in the television series ''Shameless (U.S. TV series)|Shameless''.

Personal Life
Macy and actress Felicity Huffman dated on-and-off for 15 years and married on September 6, 1997. They have three children, two daughters, Sophia Grace (born December 1, 2000) and Georgia Grace (born March 14, 2002), and a son James Robert (born February 23, 2003).
