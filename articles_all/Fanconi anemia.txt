
'''Fanconi anemia''' (FA) is a rare genetics|genetic disorder. It is a kind of anemia. People suffering from the disease produce a smaller amount of red blood cell|red and white blood cells. It is named after Guido Fanconi who first described the disease, in 1929. It should not be confused with Fanconi syndrome. Fanconi syndrome is a disorder affecting the kidneys.
