
'''Jessica Lange''' (born April 20, 1949) is an Americans|American television, movie and theater|stage actress. She was born in Cloquet, Minnesota. Her most notable performances happened in the movies ''King Kong (1976 movie)|King Kong'' (her first movie role), ''The Postman Always Rings Twice (1981 movie)|The Postman Always Rings Twice'', ''Tootsie'', ''Music Box (movie)|Music Box'', ''Rob Roy (movie)|Rob Roy'', and ''Frances'' (a movie about Frances Farmer). She starred in many other movies. During her career, she has gotten several awards.
