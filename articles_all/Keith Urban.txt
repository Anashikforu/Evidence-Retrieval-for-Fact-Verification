
'''Keith Lionel Urban''' (born 26 October 1967 in Whangarei, New Zealand) is a New Zealand-born Australians|Australian-Americans|American Grammy Award winning country music singer and songwriter judge in ''American Idol''. 

Personal life
Urban is married to actress Nicole Kidman. They met at G'Day LA, a Hollywood event honoring Australians. The couple have two daughters: Sunday Rose Kidman Urban (born 7 July 2008) and Faith Margaret Kidman Urban (born 28 December 2010).
