'''McDiarmid Park''' is the home stadium of St. Johnstone F.C.|St. Johnstone Football Club from Perth, Perth and Kinross|Perth, Scotland.  It has an all-seated capacity of 10,673. The record attendance is 10,525, which occurred on May 23, 1999, when Tayside rivals Dundee F.C.|Dundee were the visitors for the last game of the season.
