'''''The Twist''''' was a popular dance in the early 1960s. It was made famous by a song of the same title, written by Hank Ballard. Ballard's version of the song was not a hit, but a cover version by Chubby Checker became an international hit, and sparked off a wikt:craze|craze for the dance.

Checker followed with ''Let's Twist Again'', which was another huge hit.Chubby Checker ''Let's Twist Again''.
