


'''''Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country''''' was the sixth movie set in the Star Trek fictional universe|universe. It was the last movie to be about the crew from ''Star Trek: The Original Series''. It was made in 1991 by Paramount Pictures. The Klingons turn to the United Federation of Planets for help when their world is in danger. The crew of the Enterprise|USS Enterprise must help keep the peace.
