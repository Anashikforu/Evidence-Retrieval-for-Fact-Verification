'''Hatfield Peverel''' is a village and Civil parishes in England|civil parish in Braintree (district)|Braintree, Essex, England. In 2001 there were 4,384 people living in Hatfield Peverel.

 Transport 

= Road =
The B1137 and B1019 roads pass through the village.

The A12 road bypasses the village.

= Rail =
Hatfield Peverel has a station.
