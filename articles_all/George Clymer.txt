= Pennsylvania Representative =
As a Pennsylvania representative, Clymer was, along with only five others, a signer of both the United States Declaration of Independence|Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution.

= Continental Congress =
'''George Clymer''' attended the Continental Congress and served in political office until the end of his life.
