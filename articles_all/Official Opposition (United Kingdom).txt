'''Her Majesty's Loyal Opposition''', or the '''Official Opposition''' in the United Kingdom is led by Leader of the Opposition (United Kingdom)|The Leader of the Opposition.  This is usually the political party with the second largest number of seats in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons, as the largest party will usually form the government. Since May, United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010, the Official Opposition has been the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
