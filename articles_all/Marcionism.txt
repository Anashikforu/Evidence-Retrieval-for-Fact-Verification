'''Marcionism''' is a type of Christianity which started around 144 C.E.(According to Tertullian in ''Adversus Marcionnem'', it started 115 years and 6 months after the Crucifixion of Jesus) It was started by Marcion of Sinope in Rome. Marcionn believed in Jesus Christ, but did not accept the Bible or Yahweh. He was a dualism|dualist, and believed that the god who wrote the Old Testament was different than the god who wrote the New Testament. 

Traditionally members of the church are required to wear pointed-head garments to show reverence to the god of the Old Testament.
