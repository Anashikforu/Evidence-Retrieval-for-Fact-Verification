


'''Harald zur Hausen''' (born 11 March 1936 in Gelsenkirchen) is a Germany|German Virology|virologist and professor emeritus. He won the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine in 2008 for his discoveries on the role of papilloma viruses which he shared with Luc Montagnier and Françoise Barré-Sinoussi.
