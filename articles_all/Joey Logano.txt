
'''Joseph Thomas Logano''' (born May 27, 1990) is an Americans|American racecar driver who drives the No. 22 Ford Mustang in the NASCAR Cup Series for Team Penske. Logano is the 2018 NASCAR Cup Series champion. He also won the 2012 Pocono 400 at Pocono Raceway from the pole, ending a 31 race losing streak for pole winners.
