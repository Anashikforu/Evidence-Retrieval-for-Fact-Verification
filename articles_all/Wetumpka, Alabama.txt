
'''Wetumpka''' is a city in Elmore County, Alabama|Elmore County, Alabama, United States. According to 2000 US census, there are 5,726 people living there. The city is the county seat of Elmore County, Alabama|Elmore County. It is the third fastest growing county in the state. Together with Montgomery, Alabama|Montgomery, Prattville, Alabama|Prattville, Millbrook, Alabama|Millbrook, and Tallassee, Alabama|Tallassee the city is considered part of the Montgomery Metropolitan Area, or River Region. There are approximately 346,578 people living in the region.
