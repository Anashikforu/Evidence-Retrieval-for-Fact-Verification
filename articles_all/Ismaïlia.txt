
'''Ismailia''' () is a city in Egypt, on the Suez Canal. Ismailia is the Capital (political)|capital of the Ismailia Governorate|Governorate with the same name. The city has a population of approximately 750,000. It is approximately halfway between Port Said to the north and Suez to the south. The Canal widens at this point to include Lake Timsah, one of the Bitter Lakes.
