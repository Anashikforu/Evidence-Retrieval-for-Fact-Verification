= Typing errors =
Some spelling errors are because people's typing is not perfect, such as
 letters are doubled or tripled, such as "careeer" and "betweeen"
 letters are singled, such as "betwen"
 keys are transposed, so "because" becomes "becuase".

= Foreign words =
A misspelling in English might be a legitimate spelling in another language; for example, the word "address" is spelled in German language|German as "adress" (one letter "d"). "immigrate" can be written "inmigrate" by speakers of Spanish.
