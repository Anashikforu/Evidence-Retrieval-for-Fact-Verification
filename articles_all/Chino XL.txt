'''Derek Keith Barbosa''' (born April 8, 1974 in The Bronx, New York) is an Americans|American rapper and actor better known by his stage name, '''Chino XL'''. As an actor he has made guest appearances on the television shows Reno 911! and CSI: Miami, as well as movies with Kate Hudson, Luke Wilson, and Rob Reiner.
