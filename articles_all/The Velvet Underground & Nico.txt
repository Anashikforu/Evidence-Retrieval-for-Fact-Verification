

'''''The Velvet Underground & Nico''''' is the first album by American Rock music|rock band The Velvet Underground. It is said to be one of the greatest albums ever made. ''Rolling Stone'' ranked it the 13th best album of all time. The album was produced by famous artist Andy Warhol. Some of the songs talked about drugs, prostitution and sex. They were very controversial back then that radio stations won't play any of their songs.
