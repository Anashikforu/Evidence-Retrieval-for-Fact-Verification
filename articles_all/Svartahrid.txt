
'''Svartahrid''' is a black metal band from Norway. The band was formed in Skien, Telemark in 1994.

 Members 
Istar - guitarist and singer
Forn - drummer
NidGrim - bassist

 Studioalbums 
 Forthcoming Storm (1999)
 As The Sunrise Flickers (2000)
 Sadness And Wrath (2007)
 Malicious Pride (2008)
