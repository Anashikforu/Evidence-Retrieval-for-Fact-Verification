
'''''Destroy All Humans!''''' is a video game developed by Pandemic Studios and published by THQ. It was released for the Xbox and PlayStation 2 game consoles. The game is set in 1950s U.S. and parodies the lifestyles, pop culture, and politics of this time period. The player controls Cryptosporidium (Destroy All Humans!)|Cryptosporidium 137, a member of the Furon race of aliens, who has come to Earth to harvest DNA from humans to continue the cloning process of his species. Destroy All Humans has gameplay similar to the Grand Theft Auto (series)|''Grand Theft Auto'' series.

Sequels
A sequel called ''Destroy All Humans! 2'' was released in October 17, 2006, and also ''Destroy All Humans! Big Willy Unleashed'' has released on February 25, 2008. Exclusive on Wii|Nintendo Wii.
