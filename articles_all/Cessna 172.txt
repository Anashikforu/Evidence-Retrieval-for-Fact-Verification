
The '''Cessna 172''' is one of the best-selling single engine airplanes in the earth|world. Over 43,000 of them have been made. It is made by Cessna|Cessna Aircraft Company.  It can carry 4 people including the pilot.  From 1956 until 1967 it had a Continental six-cylinder piston engine. Beginning in 1968, the 172 was powered by a Lycoming four-cylinder piston engine.
