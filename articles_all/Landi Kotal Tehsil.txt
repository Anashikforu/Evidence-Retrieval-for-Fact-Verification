'''Landi Kotal''' or '''Landikotal''' ( Landi Kotal is often visited by tourists and can be reached by train or road.

Landi Kotal is the main shopping centre for both the Shinwari and Afridi tribes.

 History 
In 1897, during British Raj|British rule, the Afridis attacked Landi Kotal and other posts in the Khyber Pass. The British counter-attacked with a force of 34,500 men under William Lockhart (Indian Army officer)|Sir William Lockhart, defeating the Afridis.
In 1925 work began on a railway line linking Jamrud to Landi Kotal.
