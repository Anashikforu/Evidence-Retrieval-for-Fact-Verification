
'''Eugene "Mean Gene" Okerlund''' (December 19, 1942 &ndash; January 2, 2019) was an Americans|American Professional wrestling|pro wrestling commentator and announcer. He was most known for his time with both then-World Wrestling Federation (WWF), now World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), and World Championship Wrestling (WCW). He was the host of WWE Vintage Collection.

Okerlund died on January 2, 2019 at a hospital in Sarasota, Florida of complications from a fall at the age of 76.
