
'''Edelweiss''' is a well-known Europe mountain flower.

 Description 
Leaf|Leaves and flowers are covered with white hairs and look wikt:woolly|woolly. Each Edelweiss bloom has five to six small yellow flower heads surrounded by leaflets in a star shape. The flowers are in bloom between July and September.

 Range 
The Edelweiss prefers Rock (geology)|rocky limestone places. It has been used traditionally in folk medicine. The hair on the flower protect the Edelweiss from cold.
