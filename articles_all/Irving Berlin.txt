Selected works
Berlin's first popular success was "Alexander's Ragtime Band."

In 1938, Kate Smith made "God Bless America" a popular hit.

In 1942, Berlin won the Academy Award for the best original song in the movie "Holiday Inn". The song was "White Christmas (song)|White Christmas.". Bing Crosby recorded the song. This became "the best-selling record of all time."Harris, Roy J. 
Broadway musicals written by Berlin included "Annie Get Your Gun" and "Call me Madam."
