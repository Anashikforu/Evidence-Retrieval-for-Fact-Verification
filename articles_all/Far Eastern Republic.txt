

'''Far Eastern Republic''' (;  romanization of Russian|romanised: ''Dalnevostochnaya Respublika, DVR''), sometimes called the '''Chita Republic''', was a nominally independent state established at Blagoveshchensk, covering the former Russian Far East and Siberia east of Lake Baikal on April 6, 1920.
