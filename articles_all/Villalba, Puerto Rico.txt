
'''Villalba'''  (vee-YAHL-bah) is a municipality of Puerto Rico. It is in the central region, northeast of Juana Díaz, Puerto Rico|Juana Díaz; south of Orocovis, Puerto Rico|Orocovis; and west of Coamo, Puerto Rico|Coamo. Villalba is spread over 6 wards and Villalba Pueblo (the downtown area and the administrative center of the city).
