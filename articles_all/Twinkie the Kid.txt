
'''Twinkie the Kid''' is the mascot for Twinkies, a cream-filled snack cake.  He is a  trademark of Interstate Bakeries Corporation and has appeared on Twinkie packaging, in Advertising|commercials, and as collectible related merchandise.

Description
Twinkie the Kid is an Anthropomorphism|anthropomorphized Twinkie, which means he is a Twinkie that has been given human-like features.  He looks like a Wrangler (profession)|wrangler and wears boots, gloves, a kerchief, and a ten-gallon hat.
