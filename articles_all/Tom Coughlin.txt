
'''Thomas Richard Coughlin''' (born August 31, 1946 in Waterloo, New York|Waterloo, New York) is the head coach for the New York Giants, the 2007 National Football League (NFL) Super Bowl Champions. He was the coach at Boston College before moving to the NFL.
