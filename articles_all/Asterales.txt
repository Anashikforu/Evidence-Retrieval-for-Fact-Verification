

The '''Asterales''' are an order of flowering plants which include the composite family Asteraceae (sunflowers, Daisy|daisies, thistles, etc.) and its related families.

Argophyllaceae
Asteraceae - Daisy|Daisies

Goodeniaceae (incl. Brunoniaceae)
Menyanthaceae
Pentaphragmaceae
Phellinaceae
Rousseaceae (incl. Carpodetaceae)
Stylidiaceae (also Donatiaceae)
