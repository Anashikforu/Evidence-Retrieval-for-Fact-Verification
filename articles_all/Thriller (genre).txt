
A '''thriller''' is a genre found in literature, movies and television program|television. It uses wikt:suspense|suspense, where the audience is made to wonder what is going to happen next. Thrillers often have a horror movie|horror or crime theme. Some examples of thriller movies are ''Psycho (1960 movie)|Psycho'', ''Silence of the Lambs'', ''Vertigo (movie)|Vertigo'', ''Rear Window'', ''Argo (movie)|Argo'' and ''The Birds (movie)|The Birds''.
