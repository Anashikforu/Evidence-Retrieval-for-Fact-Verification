
'''19521 Chaos''' is a Kuiper belt|Kuiper belt object. Chaos was found in 1998 by the Deep Ecliptic Survey, with Kitt Peak National Observatory|Kitt Peak's 4&nbsp;m telescope. It is big at about 560&nbsp;km, and bright at H=4.755. It is named after Chaos (mythology)|the primeval state of existence in Greek mythology, from which the first gods appeared.

 Trivia 
A jazz-fusion/progressive-rock band from Connecticut got their name from this minor planet.
