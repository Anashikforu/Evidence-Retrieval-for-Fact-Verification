

'''''On the Road''''' is a novel by American writer Jack Kerouac, written in April 1951, and published in 1957. Most of it is Autobiography|autobiographical and was written based on the road trips of Kerouac and his friends across mid-century United States|America and is often considered a defining work of the postwar Beat Generation.

When the book was originally released, the ''New York Times'' hailed it as "the most beautifully executed, the clearest and most important utterance" of Kerouac's generation. Millstien, Gillbert.
