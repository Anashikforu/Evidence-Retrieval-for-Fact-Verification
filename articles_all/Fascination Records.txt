
'''Fascination Records''' is a Polydor record label|sub-label that launched in spring 2006 in music|2006. The record label is managed by former Top of the Pops (magazine)|TOTP Magazine editor Peter Loraine.

As part of Polydor Records UK, the label will concentrate on a small number of Pop music|pop acts and will complement the re-activated Fiction rock imprint.
