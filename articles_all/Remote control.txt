

A '''remote control''' is a electronic component|device used to issue commands (controlling it) from a distance to televisions, DVD players, doorbells  and so on. 

A '''remote controller''' does not have a wire and can be held by hand. It has buttons for volume, changing television Television channel|channels and more.

Infrared or radio signals are used to communicate with the devices. Battery|Batteries make remote controls and controllers work. Mostly AAA (battery)|AAA, AA (battery)|AA or coin battery size batteries are used.
