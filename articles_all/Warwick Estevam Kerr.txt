'''Warwick Estevam Kerr''' (born September 9, 1922, Santana do Parnaíba, São Paulo (state)|São Paulo, Brazil- 15 September 2018) is a Brazilian engineer, geneticist, entomologist, and professor. He made many discoveries in the genetics and sex determination of bees. He is also responsible for the spread of africanized bees across the world.
