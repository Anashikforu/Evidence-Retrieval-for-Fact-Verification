
'''Richard Roeper''' (born October 17, 1959 in Chicago, Illinois) is an Americans|American movie critic, television personality, journalist, and historian. He starred on the American television program "At the Movies (U.S. TV series|At the Movies" with Roger Ebert from 2000 to 2008 as Gene Siskel's successor. Roeper also writes about movies for an American newspaper, the ''Chicago Sun-Times''.
