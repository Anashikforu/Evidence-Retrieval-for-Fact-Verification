
'''''Ilium/Olympos''''' is a science fiction series that was written by Dan Simmons. The events in the books are set in motion by beings who have taken on the roles of the List of Greek mythological characters|Greek gods. The first book in the series, ''Illium (book)|Illium'' received the Locus Award for best book in 2004.

 Books 
There are two books in the series:
 ''Ilium (book)|Ilium'' (2003) - Locus Award 2004
 ''Olympos (book)|Olympos'' (2005)
