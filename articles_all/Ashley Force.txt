

'''Ashley Force''' (born November 29, 1982) is a funny car drag racing|drag racer. She is the daughter of 14-time National Hot Rod Association|NHRA Funny Car division champion John Force and his wife Laurie Force.

Television
Ashley Force is featured with her family on A&E Network|A&E's reality show ''Driving Force''.
