
'''Dame Anne Begg''', Order of the British Empire|DBE (born December 6, 1955) is a Scotland|Scottish politician and the  Member of Parliament for the Aberdeen South (UK Parliament constituency)|Aberdeen South wikt:constituency|constituency in the United Kingdom. She was wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election.

Begg is the first permanent wheelchair user in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons.

She is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
