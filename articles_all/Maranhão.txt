
'''Maranhão''' is one of the states of Brazil|states of Brazil. It is in the northeastern region of the country. To the north is the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. Maranhão shares borders with Piauí, Tocantins (state)|Tocantins and Pará. In the 19th century, the state used plantation Slavery|slaves to produce cotton for Europe.

Major city|cities in the state besides the state capital (city)|capital São Luís, Maranhão|São Luís are Imperatriz, Timon, Maranhão|Timon, and Caxias, Maranhão|Caxias. The Centro de Lançamento de Alcântara|Alcantara spaceport is in the north of the state, not far from São Luís.
