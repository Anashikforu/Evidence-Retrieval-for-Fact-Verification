'''Charles, Prince of Wales''' Order of the Garter|KG Order of the Thistle|KT Order of the Bath|GCB Order of Merit|OM Privy Council of the United Kingdom|PC (Charles Philip Arthur George; born 14 November 1948), is next inline to the British throne..
Moreover, Charles is next in line for the British Throne, direct successor and heir of Queen Elizabeth II. 
Early life
Prince Charles was born at Buckingham Palace in London. He is the first son of Elizabeth II and Prince Phillip Duke of Edinburgh. He is grand son of George VI of the United Kingdom and Queen Elizabeth The Queen Mother. He was baptised at Buckingham Palace. 

Charles went to the University of Cambridge and became the first British royal heir to earn a college degree.
Personal life
He was married to his first wife, Diana, Princess of Wales, from 1981 until they divorced in 1996. They had two sons, Prince William, Duke of Cambridge|Prince William and Prince Harry of Wales|Prince Harry. Charles has been married to his second wife, Camilla, Duchess of Cornwall, since 2005.

On 25 March 2020, Charles tested positive for COVID-19.
