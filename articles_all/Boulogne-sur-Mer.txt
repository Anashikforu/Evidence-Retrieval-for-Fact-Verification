
'''Boulogne-sur-Mer''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is in Nord-Pas-de-Calais in the Pas-de-Calais Departments of France|department in the north of France.  Boulogne-sur-Mer set its record high temperature (35.4°C or 95.7°Fahrenheit|F) in July 2015. The record had been 34.8°C in Aug. 11, 2003.
