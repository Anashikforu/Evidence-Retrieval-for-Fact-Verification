

'''''Ace Attorney''''', known as  in Japan, is a video game video game series|series made by Capcom, with games released on the Game Boy Advance, Nintendo DS, and Nintendo 3DS. The series is a visual novel style courtroom drama where a rookie defense attorney must get their falsely accused client a not guilty verdict and find the true culprit.

Games

The first ''Ace Attorney'' game was released in Japan in 2001. The game, ''Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney'' was made for the Game Boy Advance. In 2005, the game was released outside of Japan for the first time. This was with a Nintendo DS version of the game. Right now, the series has six main games and five spin-off games.
