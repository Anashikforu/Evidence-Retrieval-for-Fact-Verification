A '''morgue''' is a place where dead human bodies are kept for a short time soon after the person died. Sometimes, the body is examined by autopsy. Most often, the body is delivered to a Noredirect|mortuary and bury|buried in a grave or cremation|cremated. Morgues are found in hospitals, usually on the lowest floor or basement.
