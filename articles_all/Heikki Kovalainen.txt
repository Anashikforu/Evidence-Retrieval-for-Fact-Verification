

'''Heikki Johannes Kovalainen''' (born October 19, 1981 in Suomussalmi, Finland) is a Finland|Finnish Formula One racing driver who spent the 2008 and 2009 seasons with British-based team Vodafone McLaren Mercedes. In 2010, he joined the new Lotus Racing|Lotus team.
