

'''The Hives''' are a Swedish people|Swedish rock band that started in 1993 but rose to fame in the early 2000s as a leading group of the Garage rock#Revival|garage rock revival, playing garage punk. Their breakthrough came with the release of the "best-of" compilation ''Your New Favourite Band'', featuring their most commercially successful song "Hate to Say I Told You So".
