
'''Dippoldiswalde''' is a town in the Free State of Saxony, Germany. It was the capital city of the Weißeritzkreis Rural District|Weißeritzkreis district. Today, Dippoldiswalde is a city in Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge Rural District. It is  south of Dresden.

The town is on the Weisseritz railway, a narrow gauge railway powered by steam locomotives.
