'''Essen''' is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Antwerp (province)|Antwerp. The municipality includes the towns of Essen Centrum, Heikant, Horendonk, Wildert, Statie en Hoek. On January 1, 2007 Essen had a total population of 17,143. The total area is 47.48&nbsp;km² which gives a population density of 358 inhabitants per km².

The town is near the border with the Netherlands. Its train station is the last one in Belgium, the next station is Roosendaal, North Brabant|Noord-Brabant, Netherlands.
