
'''Russell William Wallace, Jr.''' (born August 14, 1956 in Fenton, Missouri|Fenton, Missoui) is a Retirement|retired Americans|American race car driver. Wallace won the 1989 NASCAR#Sprint cup|NASCAR Cup Series.

He is known for driving the number 2 Miller Brewing Company|Miller Lite/Miller Genuine Draft car for Team Penske in NASCAR. He raced from 1980 to 2005. He won 55 races from 1986 to 2004. Wallace won many races on short tracks such as Bristol Motor Speedway and Richmond Raceway.

In 2006, he became a broadcaster for ESPN and ABC (American TV network)|ABC. He covered many auto racing events, such as the Indianapolis 500.
