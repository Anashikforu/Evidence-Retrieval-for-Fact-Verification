'''Ba Đình Square''' (Vietnamese language|Vietnamese: Quảng Trường Ba Đình) is a place in the Vietnamese capital city|capital, Hà Nội. It is where President of Vietnam|Vietnamese President Hồ Chí Minh Vietnamese Declaration of Independence|declared the independence of the Democratic Republic of Việt Nam from France|French imperialist rule on 2 September 1945, the same day that World War II ended.  This is now where Hồ Chí Minh Mausoleum|his mausoleum is.
