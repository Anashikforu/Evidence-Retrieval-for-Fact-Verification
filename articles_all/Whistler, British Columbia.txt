
'''Whistler''' is a village in the British Columbian province of Canada. The population as of 2006 is 9,248 and the mayor of Whistler is Nancy Wilhelm-Morden. It is located on the British Columbia Highway 99|Sea to Sky Highway (Highway 99), which goes from the Peace Arch|Peace Arch Border Crossing in Surrey, British Columbia to the British Columbia Highway 97|Cariboo Highway (Highway 97) 10 kilometres north of Cache Creek, British Columbia. Over two million people visit Whistler annually, and it is usually for alpine skiing and mountain biking at the Whistler-Blackcomb ski resort. Whistler was the host Mountain Resort of the 2010 Winter Olympics|Vancouver 2010 Winter Olympics and 2010 Winter Paralympics|Paralympic Games.
