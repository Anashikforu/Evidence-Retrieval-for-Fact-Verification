


'''''SimCity 2000''''' is a city building computer game released in 1994. It is the sequel to ''SimCity''. It had a lot of new things such as more power plants, hills and mountains, subways, pipe|water pipes, prisons, schools, library|libraries, museums, and hospitals. There were also roads, highways, and other buildings.

The game also had different scenarios.
