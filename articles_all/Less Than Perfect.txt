
'''''Less Than Perfect''''' was an United States|American television programme|television series. It was a sitcom that began airing on American Broadcasting Company|ABC on October 1, 2002 and ended on June 6, 2006. The series is about a young female office employee (played by Sara Rue) and her co-workers  The series was directed by Skip Collector and Ted Wass, among others.
