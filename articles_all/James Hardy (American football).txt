
'''James W. Hardy, III''' (December 24, 1985 &ndash; June 7, 2017) was an Americans|American American football|football wide receiver for the Baltimore Ravens. Hardy also played for the Buffalo Bills from 2008 to 2010. Hardy was drafted out of Indiana University by the Buffalo Bills with the 41st pick in the second round of the 2008 NFL Draft.

On June 7, 2017, a body was found in the Maumee River in Fort Wayne, Indiana. The cause of his death has yet to be determined. He was 31 years old.
