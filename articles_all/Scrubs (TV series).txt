

'''''Scrubs''''' is an United States|American sitcom. It is about Medical doctor|doctors and nurses in a hospital, and the title ''Scrubs'' comes from the name of the clothes they wear to keep themselves from getting dirty.

The series, which ran for nine seasons, was well known for its creative flashbacks and fantasies.
Season One
The first season introduces John Michael "J.D." Dorian and his best friend Christopher Turk in their first year out of medical school as interns at Sacred Heart Hospital.
