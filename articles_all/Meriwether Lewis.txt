
'''Meriwether Lewis''' (August 18, 1774 &ndash; October 11, 1809) was an Americans|American explorer, soldier, and public administrator. He was best known for his role as the leader of the Lewis and Clark Expedition with William Clark. Their mission was to explore the territory of the Louisiana Purchase, establish trade and sovereignty over the natives near the Missouri River, and claim the Pacific Northwest and Oregon Country for the United States before European nations. They also collected Scientific revolution|scientific data, and information on indigenous nations.Fritz, Harry W. (2004).
