
'''Lake Tahoe''' or '''Tahoe''' is a fresh water lake in the Sierra Nevada (US)|Sierra Nevada. It is on the borders of the states of California and Nevada, in the United States of America.

Lake Tahoe is one of the deepest (1645 feet/501 m), largest (192 sq. mi./497&nbsp;kmÂ²), and highest elevation (6229 feet/1898 m) lakes in the United States. Of the lakes in the United States, only Oregon's Crater Lake is deeper at 1930 feet (588 m).

Some visitors experience altitude sickness, because there is less oxygen in the air at higher altitudes. To fight this, experts recommend avoiding heavy exercise or drinking Alcoholic drink|alcohol until the body adjusts to the lower oxygen level.
