'''Alicia Silverstone''' (born October 4, 1976) is an Americans|American actor|actress and activist. She was born in San Francisco, California to an English people|English father and Scottish people|Scottish mother. Silverstone's movies include ''Clueless (movie)|Clueless'', ''Batman and Robin'' and ''Scooby-Doo 2: Monsters Unleashed''.

 Personal life 
Alicia was married to musician, radio show host and actor Christopher Jarecki on June 11, 2005. They have one child together, a son named Bear Blu Jarecki (born May 5, 2011). They divorced in 2018.
