

'''Daniel Alberto Passarella''' (born 25 May 1953) is an Argentine former professional Association football|footballer who played as a centre back, and former manager of the Argentina national football team|Argentina and Uruguay national football team|Uruguay national football teams. He was Captain (association football)|captain of the 1978 FIFA World Cup squads#Group 1|Argentina team that won the 1978 FIFA World Cup|1978 World Cup. He was president of the Club Atlético River Plate|River Plate sports club for four years after winning the elections by a very close margin in December 2009.
