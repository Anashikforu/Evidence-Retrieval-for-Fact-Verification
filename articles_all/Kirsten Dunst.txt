'''Kirsten Caroline Dunst''' (born April 30, 1982) is a Germany|German-United States|American actor|actress, famous for her roles in ''Interview with the Vampire: The Vampire Chronicles|Interview with the Vampire'', ''The Virgin Suicides (movie)|The Virgin Suicides'', ''Marie Antoinette (2006 movie)|Marie Antoinette'', and ''Bring It On'', as well as Mary Jane-Watson in the Spider-Man movie series|''Spider-Man'' movie series. She was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for being the main heroine of many films. In 2022, she was nominated for an Academy Award for her role as Rose Gordon in ''The Power of the Dog (movie)|The Power of the Dog''.
