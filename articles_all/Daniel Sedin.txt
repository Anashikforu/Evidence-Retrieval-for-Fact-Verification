
'''Daniel Sedin''' (born September 26, 1980) is a Swedish professional ice hockey winger (ice hockey)|winger with the Vancouver Canucks of the National Hockey League (NHL), and serves as an alternate captain for the Canucks during home (sports)|home games. His identical twin brother Henrik Sedin|Henrik also plays for the Canucks, and is the team captain. Born and raised in Örnsköldsvik, Sweden, they have played together throughout their careers; the pair are known for their effectiveness playing off one another.
