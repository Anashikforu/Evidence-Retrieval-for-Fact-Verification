'''Magnoliopsida''' or '''dicotyledons''' is the name of  a Class (biology)|class of plants in taxonomy. They are also called dicots. It is the biggest group of flowering plants. Seedlings in this group have two seed leaves. Monocotyledon|Monocots have one seed leaf.
