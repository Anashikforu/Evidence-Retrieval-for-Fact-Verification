The '''1916 Summer Olympics ''', officially known as the '''Games of the VI Olympiad''', were not held.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics), 
The games were planned for Berlin in Germany.

In 1912, workers began building the sports facilities for the games. The ''Deutsches Stadion'' ("German Stadium") began in 1912.
The games were cancelled because of World War I.Zarnowski, C. Frank.
