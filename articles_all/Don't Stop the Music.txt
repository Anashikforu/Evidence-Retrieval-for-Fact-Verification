'''Don't Stop the Music''' (also known as '''Please Don't Stop the Music''') is a song reocrded by Barbados|Barbadian recording artist Rihanna. It was released September 7, 2007 from her third studio album ''Good Girl Gone Bad''. ''Don'y Stop the Music'' was written by Tawanna Dabney and Michael Jackson with producers Stargate (production team)|StarGate. The song also samples "Wanna Be Startin' Somethin'" by Michael Jackson.
