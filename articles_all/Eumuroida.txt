
'''Eumuroida''' are a group of all Muroidea|muroid rodents, or the true muroids. It was made in 2004. The group is not in the standard taxonomic Scientific classification|classification, but it is between superfamily and family (biology)|family. The Eumuroida is the clade including all organisms which evolved from the most recent ancestor of the Calomyscidae, Nesomyidae, Cricetidae, and Muridae.
