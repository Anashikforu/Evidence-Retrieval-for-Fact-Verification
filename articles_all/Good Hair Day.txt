'''Good Hair Day''' (also called '''ghd''') is an United Kingdom|English hair styling
 Ownership 
Good Hair Day is owned by Jemella Group. The company was started in 2001 by Martin Penny, Gary Douglas and Robert Powls.

Popularity
The brand has received endorsements from celebrity|celebrities, such as Madonna (entertainer)|Madonna, Gwyneth Paltrow, and Victoria Beckham. Because of this, Martin Penny won the North Region Consumer Products Entrepreneur of the Year in 2005 for the product,
