
'''''Spin''''' was a music magazine that competed with the leading magazine ''Rolling Stone''. Bob Guccione, Jr. created the magazine in 1985 and sold it to Miller Publishing in 1997. In February 2006, Miller Publishing sold the magazine for less than US$5 million to a San Francisco, California|San Francisco-based company called the McEvoy Group LLC.Raine, George (March 1, 2006).
