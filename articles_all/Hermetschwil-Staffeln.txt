

'''Hermetschwil-Staffeln''' was a municipality of the district of Bremgarten (district)|Bremgarten in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Aargau in Switzerland. On 1 January 2014 the former municipality of Hermetschwil-Staffeln merged into the municipality of Bremgarten, Switzerland|Bremgarten.
