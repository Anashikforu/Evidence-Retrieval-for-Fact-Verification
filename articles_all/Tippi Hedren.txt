
'''Tippi Hedren''' (born Nathalie Kay Hedren, January 19, 1930) is an United States|American actress. Her paternal grandparents were Sweden|Swedish immigrants. Her maternal ancestry is Germany|German and Norway|Norwegian. She is best known for her performances in two movies movie director|directed by Alfred Hitchcock: ''The Birds (movie)|The Birds'' and ''Marnie''. She is the mother of actress Melanie Griffith and has acted in movies with her daughter.
