'''Raven-Symoné Christina Pearman''' (born December 10, 1985) is an Americans|American actor|actress and singer. She was born in Atlanta, Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. She moved to Ossining (village), New York|Ossining, New York at age three. She played the role of "Olivia" in the last three seasons of ''The Cosby Show'' (1984). 

Pearman is most well known as the person Raven in the TV show ''That's So Raven'' and other parts in ''The Cheetah Girls'' and ''The Cheetah Girls 2''. Pearman goes by the name '''Raven''' for most of her acting.

In August 2013 Raven-Symoné commented on legalizing same-sex marriage in the United States|gay marriage, "I was excited to hear today that more states legalized gay marriage. I, however am not currently getting married, but it is great to know I can now, should I wish to". She was dating AzMarie Livingston since 2012, and in October 2015, she announced that she had broken up with her.
