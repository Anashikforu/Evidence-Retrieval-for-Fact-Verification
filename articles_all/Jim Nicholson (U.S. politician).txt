
'''Robert James "Jim" Nicholson''' (born February 4, 1938

Political career
Nicholson has never held elected office, but has long been active in the Republican Party. In January 1986, he was elected committeeman from Colorado for the Republican National Committee (RNC). In 1993, he was elected Vice-Chairman of the RNC, and was the "surprise pick"
Between 2001 and his appointment to the position of Secretary of Veterans Affairs, he served as United States Ambassadors to the Holy See|United States Ambassador to the Holy See (the Vatican).

Business career
Before becoming active in civilian government service, he worked as a lawyer in Denver, Colorado, specializing in real estate, municipal finance and zoning law. In 1978 he founded Nicholson Enterprises, Inc., a developer of planned residential communities, and in 1987 he bought Renaissance Homes, a custom-house builder.  He now works in the Washington, D.C. office of Brownstein Hyatt Farber & Schreck LLP.

Military service
He is a 1961 graduate of the United States Military Academy at West Point, New York and served eight years in active duty.  He was a paratrooper and Army Rangers|Ranger-qualified Army officer.  He fought in the Vietnam War, where he earned the Bronze Star Medal|Bronze Star, Combat Infantryman Badge, the Meritorious Service Medal (United States)|Meritorious Service Medal, Republic of Vietnam Cross of Gallantry and two Air Medals.

After thirty years of working in the Army, he retired in 1991 with the rank of Colonel (United States)|Colonel.

Personal life
Nicholson was born on a farm near Struble, Iowa.  Nicholson has wikt:described|described his childhood as "growing up dirt poor in a tenant house without plumbing and sometimes without food".
Nicholson has a Master of Public Policy|Master's degree in Public Policy from Columbia University.  He received a law degree from the University of Denver in 1972. In May 2005, he was given the Distinguished Graduate of the U.S. Military Academy Award.

Nicholson is married to the former Suzanne Marie Ferrell of Highland Falls, New York, who is an artist. They are the parents of three adult children.
