'''Al-Shams''' () was a militia force formed in Bangladesh#History|East Pakistan in early 1971 by the Pakistan Army.

Al-Shams is an Arabic language|Arabic word meaning 'The Sun'. Al Shams was Pakistani armed groups formed by the Pakistan Army to fight out and resist Mukti Bahini and support the army in its campaigns in the former East Pakistan provincial region.
