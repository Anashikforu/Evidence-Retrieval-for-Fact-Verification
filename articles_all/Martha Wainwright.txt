
'''Martha Wainwright''' (born May 8, 1976) is a Canada|Canadian-United States|American folk music|folk-Rock music|rock singer. She is the daughter of singers Loudon Wainwright III and Kate McGarrigle. Her brother Rufus Wainwright is also a well known singer.
