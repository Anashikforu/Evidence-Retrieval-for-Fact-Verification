


'''''F-Zero''''' is a racing video game|racing video game first made for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System|SNES, where Captain Falcon made his debut. It involves racing around a Race track|track at high speeds. Other games in the ''F-Zero'' series are ''F-Zero X'' for Nintendo 64, ''F-Zero: Maximum Velocity'' for Game Boy Advance. ''F-Zero GX'' for GameCube|Nintendo GameCube and ''F-Zero: GP Legend'' for Game Boy Advance.
