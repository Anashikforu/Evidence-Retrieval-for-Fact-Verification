
'''Helen Elizabeth Hunt''' (born June 15, 1963) is an Americans|American actor|actress. She has won the Emmy Award|Emmy, Golden Globe and Academy Awards|Academy Awards. She is best known for her role in the television sitcom ''Mad About You''.

 Awards 
Hunt has been given many awards in her career. In 1998 she became one of only two actresses (the other is Helen Mirren) to win a Golden Globe Award, an Academy Awards|Academy Award and an Emmy Award in the same year. She is the only actress to win four consecutive Emmys and to win four Blockbuster Entertainment Awards.
