
'''Adams County''' is a County (United States)|county in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. There were 18,643 people living in Adams County as of the year 2000. Its county seat is Friendship, Wisconsin|Friendship. The United States Census Bureau|U.S. Census Bureau says that the county has an area of 689&nbsp;square miles.
