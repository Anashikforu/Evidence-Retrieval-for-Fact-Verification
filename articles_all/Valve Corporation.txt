

'''Valve Corporation''' is an American video game developer and digital distribution|distributor company in Bellevue, Washington. It was founded in 1996 by Gabe Newell and Mike Harrington, two Microsoft employees in the past. The company is widely known for Steam (software)|Steam and its critically acclaimed first-person shooter series, ''Half-Life (Series)|Half-Life'', with the first being released in 1998. Also, the company develops the Steamoperating system|OS.
