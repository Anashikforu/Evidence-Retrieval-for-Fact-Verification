
'''''Cumhuriyet''''' (English: ''The Republic'') is a Turkey|Turkish nationwide daily newspaper based in Istanbul. It was founded on May 7, 1924 by Yunus Nadi Abalıoğlu.

Cumhuriyet newspaper includes columnists that are generally considered to share Social democracy|social democratic, secularism|secularist and republican ideas with its readers. Cumhuriyet is sold about 60,000 everyday.
