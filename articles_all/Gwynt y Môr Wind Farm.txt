'''Gwynt y MÃ´r''' (Welsh language|Welsh: meaning ''sea wind'') is a 576 MW offshore wind farm off the coast of North Wales. Permission for the project was given on 3 December 2008, and building began in 2012. The wind farm will begin working in 2014. It will be Wales' largest wind farm.

The wind farm is near the existing North Hoyle offshore wind farm and the Rhyl Flats offshore wind farm. All of these projects are owned by RWE npower.
