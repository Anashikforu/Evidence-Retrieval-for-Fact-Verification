

'''Nathan Jonas "Joey" Jordison''' (April 26, 1975 &ndash; July 26, 2021) was an Americans|American musician. He was the drummer for the band Slipknot. In Slipknot, he was known as '''#1'''. He is also the guitarist for Murderdolls.

Jordison died on July 26, 2021 at the age of 46.
