
'''Benjamin Richard Grubbs''' (born March 10, 1984 in Eclectic, Alabama|Eclectic, Alabama) is an Americans|American American football|football offensive guard who currently plays for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Grubbs was drafted out of Auburn University by the Baltimore Ravens with the 29th pick in the first round of the 2007 NFL Draft.
