A '''jar''' is a type of container, mostly used to store food. Jars are usually cylinder|cylindrical, and are usually made of glass or clay.

Glass jars are able to be heated in microwave ovens.

Types of jars
 Mason jar, a jar used to preservation|preserve serval foods (such as fruit, jam, vegetables, ...)
 Kilner jar, another type of jar for storing foods
 Mayonnaise jar, a mason jar especially made for mayonnaise
 Canopic jar, used in ancient Egyptian burial
 Leyden jar, a simple capacitor
 Killing jar
