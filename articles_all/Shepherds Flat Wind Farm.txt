

The '''Shepherds Flat Wind Farm''' is an 845 Watt#Megawatt|megawatt (MW) wind farm in the eastern part of U.S. state of Oregon, near Arlington, Oregon|Arlington, in both Gilliam County, Oregon|Gilliam and Morrow County, Oregon|Morrow counties. Groundbreaking came in 2009, and it officially opened in September 2012.
