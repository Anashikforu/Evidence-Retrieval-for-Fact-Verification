

'''Bornem''' is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Antwerp (province)|Antwerp. It includes the towns of Hingene, Mariekerke, Weert and Bornem proper. There are also the hamlet (place)|hamlets of Branst, Buitenland, Eikevliet and Wintam.

In 2007, 20235 people lived there.

It is at 51° 05 North, 04° 14 East.
