


'''''Asclepias speciosa''''' (also known as the '''showy milkweed''' or '''Greek milkweed''') is a species of '''milkweed''' plant. It wikt:belong|belongs in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to western North America.  It blooms from May to July.  This species is found in savannas, prairies, meadows, and fields.
