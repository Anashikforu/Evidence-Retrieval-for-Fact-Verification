
'''Cabarrus County''' is a county in the U.S. state of North Carolina. In 2000, 131,063 people lived there.  Its county seat is Concord, North Carolina|Concord.

 History 
The county was made in 1792 from Mecklenburg County, North Carolina|Mecklenburg County.

 Government 
Cabarrus County is part of the local Centralina Council of Governments.

 Schools 
The Cabarrus County Schools runs all the public schools in the county except for parts of Kannapolis, North Carolina|Kannapolis, which has Kannapolis City Schools|its own school district.

The county also has Barber-Scotia College, the Cabarrus College of Health Sciences (a four-year college), and a branch of Rowan-Cabarrus Community College.  University of North Carolina at Charlotte|UNC Charlotte is in Mecklenburg County, North Carolina|Mecklenburg County but is close Cabarrus and is easy to get to by driving on Interstate 85.

 Lowe's Motor Speedway 
The county is home to Lowe's Motor Speedway, which has three NASCAR events every year (the NASCAR All-Star Challenge, the Coca-Cola 600, and the Bank of America 500).
