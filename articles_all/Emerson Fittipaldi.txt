
'''Emerson Fittipaldi''' (born December 12, 1946 in São Paulo (city)|São Paulo, Brazil) is a highly successful Brazilian automobile racing driver. He has won championships in both Formula One, and Champcars|CART. Fittipaldi won the Indianapolis 500 twice.

Early life
Emerson Fittipaldi was born in São Paulo, Brazil. He is the younger son of prominent Italian-Brazilian motorsports journalist and radio commentator Wilson Fittipaldi Sr and his wife Józefa "Juzy" Wojciechowska, an immigrant from Saint Petersburg, Russia and of Poles|Polish and Russians|Russian descent.
