

'''''Amor Gitano''''' (English language|English: "Gypsy Love") is the theme song for the telenovela ''Zorro: La Espada y la Rosa'', performed in Spanish language|Spanish by Alejandro Fernández and Beyoncé Knowles|Beyoncé. The single (music)|single was sold mainly in markets with a strong Latin America|Latin demographic, such as Mexico, where it reached number thirty-eight on its official singles chart and number five on the Digital Sales Chart in late April 2007.
