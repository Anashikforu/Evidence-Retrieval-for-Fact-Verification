
'''Bogno''' is a former municipality of the district of Lugano (district)|Lugano in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 14 April 2013 the former municipalities of Bogno, Cadro, Carona, Switzerland|Carona, Certara, Cimadera, Valcolla and Sonvico merged into the city of Lugano.
