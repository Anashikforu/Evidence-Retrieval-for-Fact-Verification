


The '''rock pigeon''' (otherwise known as a '''rock dove''' or '''common pigeon''') (''Columba livia'') is a member of the bird family Dove|Columbidae, commonly referred to as simply pigeons. They can be seen scrounging around dumpsters, picking up French fries dropped near the door to a fast food restaurant, and begging from picnickers in the park. They have long necks.
