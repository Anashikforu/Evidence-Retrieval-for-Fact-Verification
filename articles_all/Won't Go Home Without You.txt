'''"Won't Go Home Without You"''' is the third single (music)|single off the album by Pop music|pop-Rock and roll|rock band Maroon 5 called ''It Won't Be Soon Before Long''. The song is also the 5th track (music)|track on the album. The music video for the song has to do with Adam Levine (lead singer) trying to get one more chance for his ex-girlfriend to come back to him, but she was already taken by another man.

 Chart Rankings 
"Won't Go Home Without You" peaked #1 in Israel, #16 in Canada's Hot 100, and #3 on the United States|U.S. Billboard charts|''Billboard'' Adult Top 40.
