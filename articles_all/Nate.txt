

'''Nate''' is a South Korean web portal. It was created by SK communications Inc.|SK communications. It joined with NETSGO and then with Lycos Korea in 2002. In 2003, Nate took over the online community service Cyworld. In 2009, Nate combined with Empas and became one of the most famous Korean web portals.

By 2006 users Nate had 19 million users. This dropped to 18 million by 2008. In 2009, it had 7 million users from People's Republic of China|China and 450,000 from Vietnam.
