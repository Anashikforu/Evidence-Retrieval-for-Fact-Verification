
'''James Neville Mason''' (15 May 1909 - 27 July 1984) was an English people|English actor. He reached celebrity|stardom in both United Kingdom|British and United States|American movies. He was born in Huddersfield, West Riding of Yorkshire. He died in Lausanne, Switzerland from a myocardial infarction|heart attack at the age of 75. He played Humbert Humbert in Lolita (1962 movie)|Lolita (1962).
