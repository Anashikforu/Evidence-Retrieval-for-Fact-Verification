

'''Christopher Allen Lloyd''' (born October 22, 1938) is an United States|American actor. He has appeared in various theatre productions, films, stage, television and video game work since 1961 and is most famous for the movies ''Back to the Future'' and ''The Addams Family (1991 movie)|The Addams Family'', the television programme|television programs ''Taxi (TV series)|Taxi'' and ''Cyberchase'', and ''Piranha 3D''.
