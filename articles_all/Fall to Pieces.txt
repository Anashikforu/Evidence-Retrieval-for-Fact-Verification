"'''Fall to Pieces'''" is a song by Canadians|Canadian singer Avril Lavigne, and was the fifth and last single from her second album, Under My Skin; it was the fourth single in the United States. The song was produced and written by Avril Lavigne and Raine Maida. It was Lavigne's least liked song in the U.S., but was very well liked in Canada. There is no music video for this single, because the shoot for the video was cancelled.

Charts
"Fall to Pieces" was not put out in the United Kingdom, but it made it all the way to number seven on the Canadian Nielsen Broadcast Data Systems|BDS Airplay Chart, her third successful single in Canada after "Nobody's Home (Avril Lavigne song)|Nobody's Home" and "Don't Tell Me (Avril Lavigne song)|Don't Tell Me". It made it all the way to number sixteen in South Africa. Lavigne did not advertise the single, and because of this it failed to chart on U.S. Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100; it made it all the way to number six on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart, and at number fifty-five on the ''Billboard'' Pop 100; However without video the single played a lot in radios all over US, Canada and Latin America .
