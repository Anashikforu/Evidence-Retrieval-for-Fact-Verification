
'''Opglabbeek''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of Limburg (Belgium)|Limburg. On 1 January 2019, Meeuwen-Gruitrode and Opglabbeek were merged into the new municipality of Oudsbergen.

In 2007, 9718 people lived there.

It is at 51° 02 North, 05° 35 East.
