'''Loralai''' is a Districts of Pakistan|district in the centre of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province of Pakistan. Loralai district was created on October, 1903. Loralai town is the district headquarters. Loralai was further partitioned in 1992 when Musakhel District|Musakhel and Barkhan District|Barkhan were given the status of separate administrative districts.
