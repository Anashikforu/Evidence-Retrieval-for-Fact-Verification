

The '''Fagales''' are an order (biology)|order of flowering plants, including some of the best known trees. The order name is derived from genus ''Fagus'', Beeches. They belong among the rosid group of dicotyledons. The families currently included are as follows:

 Betulaceae - Birch family
 Casuarinaceae - She-oak family
 Fagaceae - Beech family
 Juglandaceae - Walnut family
 Myricaceae - Bayberry family
 Nothofagus|Nothofagaceae - Southern beech family
 Rhoipteleaceae - Rhoiptelea family
 Ticodendron|Ticodendraceae - Ticodendron family
