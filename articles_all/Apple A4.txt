

The '''Apple A4''' is a microprocessor designed by Apple Inc.|Apple and manufactured by Samsung then in the iPhone|iPhone 4 smartphone. It was also used in the iPod Touch (4th generation).

 History 
Apple announced the A4 on January 27, 2010, during their "Latest Creation" event.

On June 7, 2010, Steve Jobs publicly confirmed that the iPhone 4 would contain the A4 Processor, which is underclocked from 1.0 GHz to 800 MHz..
