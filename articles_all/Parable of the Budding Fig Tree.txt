

The '''Parable of the Budding Fig Tree''' is a parables of Jesus|parable of Jesus.  It is written in the New Testament in Gospel of Matthew|Matthew 24:32-35, Gospel of Mark|Mark 13:28-31 and Gospel of Luke|Luke 21:29-33.  The parable compares the Kingdom of God with a budding fig tree, the opposite of the parable of the barren fig tree|barren fig tree.
