
The '''president of Brazil''' () or simply the ''President of the Republic'', is the head of state and head of government of Brazil.

Requirements
The Constitution of Brazil|national constitution says that a president be a native-born citizen of Brazil, at least 35 years of age, a resident of Brazil, a registered voter, and a member of a List of political parties in Brazil|political party.

Terms
The president of Brazil can only be president for a term of four years, This two-term limit, however, is not for life—a former president who has served for two consecutive terms may run for the presidency again after at least one term has ended.
