
'''Susan Margaret Collins''' (born December 7, 1952) is an American politician who is the senior United States Senate|U.S. Senator from Maine since 1997. in the Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party. Collins was elected to the Senate in 1996. The Senator was born December 7, 1952.

On February 13, 2021, Collins was one of seven Republican senators to vote Donald Trump guilty of sedition|starting an insurrection at the 2021 storming of the United States Capitol|U.S. Capitol attack in his Second impeachment of Donald Trump|second impeachment trial.
