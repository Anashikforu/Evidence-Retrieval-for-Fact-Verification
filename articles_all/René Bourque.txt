

'''René Gary Wayne Bourque''' (born December 10, 1981 in Lac La Biche, Alberta|Lac La Biche, Alberta) is a former Canadians|Canadian ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|left winger. He played in the NHL for the Chicago Blackhawks, Calgary Flames, Montreal Canadiens, and Columbus Blue Jackets.

 Awards 
He was named to the AJHL All-Rookie Team in the 1999–00 AJHL season.
He won the Dudley "Red" Garrett Memorial Award in the 2004–05 AHL season.
He was named to the AHL All-Rookie Team in the 2004–05 AHL season.
