The '''MiG-17 Fresco''' is a fighter aircraft made by Mikoyan, a Russia|Russian company. It first flew in 1950, and was supposed to replace the MiG-15 in the Korean War, but it did not arrive fast enough. It was used by the Vietnam|North Vietnamese in the Vietnam War. It carried 3 guns on it, and later versions carried missiles. Today, it is used as a trainer in other air force|air forces.
