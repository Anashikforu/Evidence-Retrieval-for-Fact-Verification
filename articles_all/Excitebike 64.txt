'''''Excitebike 64''''' is a video game published by Nintendo and developed by Left Field Productions. It was released in North America on April 30, 2000 for the Nintendo 64. It is the series' first 3D computer graphics|3D game. ''Excitebike 64'' is the sequel to the acclaimed Nintendo Entertainment System|NES game ''Excitebike'' and is the second title in the series. It was later succeeded by the Wii game ''Excite Truck''.
