
Image:RECHERCHE CLINIQUE HNS.jpg|thumb|220px|A vet artificially inseminates a horse '''Artificial insemination''' is when sperm is put into the cervix of a female to make her pregnant without sexual intercourse. It is often used for breeding livestock. It can also be used in human women insemination. It is the process of breeding without sexual inter course. It is when a males sperm is extracted from the body and inserted the cervix and which in theory will make a woman pregnant.
