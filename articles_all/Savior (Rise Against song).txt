

'''Savior''' is a hit single from the United States|American punk rock band Rise Against's 2008 fifth studio album ''Appeal to Reason''. It was released as the album's third single on June 3, 2009, It became the band's second single to reach the Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, peaking at number 16.  It is also Rise Against's highest charting single on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles, peaking at number 2.

The song is downloadable, plus content for the Rock Band series along with "Prayer of the Refugee" and "Re-Education (Through Labor)". Also, in the trailer for the PC game The Sims 3: Ambitions the song plays in the background in Simlish.
