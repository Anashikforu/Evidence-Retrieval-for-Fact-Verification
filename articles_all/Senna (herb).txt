

'''Senna candolleana''' or Golden Senna associates with ''Lithrea'' and ''Porlieria chilensis'' in Central Chile Matorral or forms pure stands.,.Traditionally ''Senna'', a genus including 250 species, was classified as ''Cassia''. Near 200 species appear in South America,Schütz Rodrigues R et al. 2005. O gênero ''Senna'' (Leguminosae, Caesalpinioideae) no Rio Grande do Sul, Brasil Uses
This evergreen shrub, 5 to 6 feet tall (1.5-1.8 m), 6 feet wide (1.8 m); dark green leaves is suitable as ornamental.
