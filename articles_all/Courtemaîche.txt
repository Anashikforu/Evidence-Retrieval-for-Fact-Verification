


'''Courtemaîche''' is a former municipality of the district of Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland. On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Courtemaîche, Buix and Montignez merged into the new municipality of Basse-Allaine.
