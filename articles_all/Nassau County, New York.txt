
'''Nassau County''' is a suburban county in the New York Metropolitan area east of New York City and west of Suffolk County, in the United States|U.S. U.S. state|state of New York. In 2000, 1,334,544 people lived there. It and Suffolk County, New York|Suffolk County are together referred to as Long Island. Until 1899 Nassau County was part of Queens County. Along with Suffolk, it was named the safest county in the United States in 2005 by Forbes.

Hamlets in Nassau County include Manhasset, New York.
