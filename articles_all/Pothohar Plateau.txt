The '''Pothohar Plateau''' (also spelled '''Pothwar''', '''Potwar''' or '''Potohar''') () is a plateau in Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab, Pakistan. The area was the home of the Soanian|Soan Culture (a stone age culture), and there are many fossils, tools, coins, and remains of ancient archaeology|archaeological sites. The local people speak a dialect of Punjabi language|Punjabi, called Pahari-Potwari|Pothohari or 'Potwari'.
