'''Lollapalooza''' is a music festival. It was started in 1991 by Perry Farrell, lead singer of Jane's Addiction, as a wikt:farewell|farewell concert for his band. It happened every year until 1997, then took a break until 2003. There was no 2004 Lollapalooza.
