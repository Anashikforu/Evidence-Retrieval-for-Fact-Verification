

'''Hummus''' or '''houmous''' The dish then spread across The Levant and North Africa. It is high in nutrition|nutrients, for instance protein, iron, vitamin C and B6, and dietary fiber. Hummus is often eaten with pita. When hummus is served with bread the proteins of the hummus and of the bread go well together, creating a delicious taste. It has become popular in the Middle East and many other countries. It is considered a Healthy diet|health food.
