'''Saint-Paterne''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Pays de la Loire in the Sarthe Departments of France|department in the west of France. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of Saint-Paterne - Le Chevain.
