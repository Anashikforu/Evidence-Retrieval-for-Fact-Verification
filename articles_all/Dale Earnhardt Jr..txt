
'''Ralph Dale Earnhardt, Jr.''' (born October 10, 1974) is an Americans|American racecar driver. Earnhardt last drove the #88 Chevrolet|Chevrolet SS in the NASCAR#Sprint Cup|Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series for Hendrick Motorsports. Earnhardt, Jr. won the 2004 Daytona 500 with Dale Earnhardt, Inc.. Earnhardt is the son of the seven time Sprint cup champion Dale Earnhardt.
