'''Prosper Mérimée''' (September 28, 1803 – September 23, 1870) was a France|French writer. He was also a historian and an archaeologist. He is probably best known for writing the short story "Carmen". "Carmen" became the basis for the opera ''Carmen'' by Georges Bizet.
