'''Sint-Jans-Molenbeek''' (in Dutch) or '''Molenbeek-Saint-Jean''' (in French) is a municipality in the Belgium|Belgian Brussels-Capital Region.

In 2007, 81632 people lived there.

It is at 50° 51 North, 04° 19 East.
