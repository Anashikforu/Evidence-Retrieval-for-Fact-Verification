'''Danelectro''' is an United|American company that makes Musical instrument|musical instruments and accessories. Their main products are guitars, bass guitars, instrument amplifier|amplifiers and effects units. The company  was founded by Nathan Daniel in 1947 in Red Banks, New Jersey. Jimi Hendrix is among the many well-known guitarists to have used Danelectro. The company is headuartered in Camarillo, California
