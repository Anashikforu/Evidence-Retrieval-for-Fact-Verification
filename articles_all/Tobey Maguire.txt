 Personal life 
Maguire met Jennifer Meyer in 2003 while he was shooting the movie ''Seabiscuit (movie)|Seabiscuit''. They became engaged in April 2006. Their daughter Ruby Sweetheart Maguire was born on November 10, 2006. The couple divorced in 2020.

= Poker =
In 2004, Maguire took up tournament poker. Maguire can be seen on ESPN's coverage of the 2005, 2006, and 2007 World Series of Poker Main Event Championship.
