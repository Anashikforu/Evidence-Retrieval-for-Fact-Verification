'''James Keir Hardie''' (15 August 1856 â€“ 26 September 1915) was a Scottish people|Scottish socialist and labour leader, and was the first independent labour Member of Parliament (MPs) in the Parliament of the United Kingdom|UK Parliament, seven years before the start of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.

He was born in Newhouse, Lanarkshire. He worked as a coal mining|miner, but his bosses stopped him from working when he made a trade union|union.  He became a politician, and was wikt:elect|elected to (chosen for) parliament in 1892.  At first he was a Liberal Democrats|Liberal, but he was interested in making a working class party.  He started the Independent Labour Party in 1893, and what became the wikt:modern|modern Labour Party (UK)|Labour party in 1900. He died in Glasgow following a series of strokes.
