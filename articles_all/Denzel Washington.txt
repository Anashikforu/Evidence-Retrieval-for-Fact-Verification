
'''Denzel Washington''' (born December 28, 1954) is an Americans|American actor, Movie director|director, and movie producer|producer. He has won many awards during his acting career. In 2001 he became only the second African American man to win the Academy Award for "Best Actor".

Washington played Malcolm X in the 1992 movie ''Malcolm X''. He played Whip Whitaker in the 2012 film ''Flight''. His son, John David Washington|John David, is also an actor. He also made his film debut in the 1974 film ''Death Wish (1974 movie)|Death Wish''.
