
'''Buhl''' is a city in Idaho in the United States.  It is in Twin Falls County, Idaho|Twin Falls County.  The town is on the old Oregon Trail.

It is called the "Trout Capital of the World". The hatchery|hatcheries produce most of the rainbow trout eaten in the United States. Clear Springs Foods processes over 20 million pounds of rainbow trout each year.  This makes it the world's largest producer.
