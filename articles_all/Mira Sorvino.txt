
'''Mira Sorvino''' (born September 28, 1967) is an Americans|American actress. She received an Academy Award for "Best Supporting Actress" for her role in the 1995 movie ''Mighty Aphrodite''. She starred in many other Hollywood movies. More recently she has been starring in low budget or independent movies. 

 Personal life 
Sorvino and actor Christopher Backus were married June 11, 2004. The couple have four children. Their daughters are Mattea Angel (born 2004) and Holden Paul Terry Backus (born 2009).
