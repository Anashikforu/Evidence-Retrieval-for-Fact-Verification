
'''Michael Anthony Orlando Cassavitis ''' (born April 3 1944) known professionally as '''Tony Orlando''' is an United States|American singer. He is best known for being in the singing group, Tony Orlando and Dawn, also the name of a television show that was popular for variety entertainment. They were known for singing the songs, "Tie a Yellow Ribbon Round the Ole Oak Tree", "Knock Three Times", and "He Don't Love You (Like I Love You)".

Orlando is from Greece|Greek, Latino, and Puerto Rico|Puerto Rican ancestry|descent.
