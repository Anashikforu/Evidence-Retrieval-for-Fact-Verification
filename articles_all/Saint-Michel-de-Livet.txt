
'''Saint-Michel-de-Livet''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Basse-Normandie Regions of France|region in the Calvados (department)|Calvados Departments of France|department in the northwest of France. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of Livarot-Pays-d'Auge.
