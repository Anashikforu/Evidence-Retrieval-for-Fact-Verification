

'''''Just My Luck''''' is a 2006 United States|American romantic comedy movie starring Lindsay Lohan and Chris Pine. It is set in Manhattan, New York City. It was released in the US on May 12, 2006. Lohan has described the movie as "a little bit of an over-the-top comedy, but it's funny and it's cute and it's silly." It is rated PG-13 in the US, and PG in Canada, The United Kingdom, and Australia.

McFly (band)|McFly (Danny Jones, Tom Fletcher, Dougie Poynter and Harry Judd), play themselves in the movie.
