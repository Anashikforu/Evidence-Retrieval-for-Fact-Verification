'''Mother Mary Angelica of the Annunciation'''  ('''Rita Antoinette Rizzo'''; April 20, 1923 &ndash; March 27, 2016) was an Americans|American Roman Catholicism |Catholic nun. She created the Eternal Word Television Network (ETWN). It is a very popular television channel for Catholics.

Mother Angelica died on March 27, 2016, Easter Sunday, from complications of a stroke at her home in Hanceville, Alabama, aged 92.
