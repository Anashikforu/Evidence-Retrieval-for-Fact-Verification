



'''Filippa Desiree Amanda Cay Reinfeldt''' (née '''Holmberg''', born June 14, 1967 in Stockholm) is a Sweden|Swedish Moderate Party politician. She has been Municipal Commissioner () of Täby Municipality|Täby, Uppland, since 2002 and Mayor and Chairperson of the Täby Municipal Executive since 2005.
Filippa is married since 1992 to Moderate Party leader and Prime Minister of Sweden Fredrik Reinfeldt, with whom she has three children.
