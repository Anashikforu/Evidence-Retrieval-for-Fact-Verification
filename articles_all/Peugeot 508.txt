

The '''Peugeot 508''' is a car produced by Peugeot since 2011. It replaced both the smaller Peugeot 407 and the larger Peugeot 607.

 Engines 
 ''1.6 VTi'' 85 kW (115 hp) and 88 kW (120 hp)
 ''1.6 THP'' 110 kW (150 hp) and 115 kW (156 hp)
 ''1.6 HDi'' 82 kW (112 hp)
 ''2.0 HDi'' 103 kW (140 hp) and 120 kW (163 hp)
 ''2.2 HDi'' 150 kW (204 hp)
