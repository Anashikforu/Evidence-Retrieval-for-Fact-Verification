'''Bet''', '''Beth''', or '''Vet''' is the second letter of the Hebrew language|Hebrew alphabet. It is represented by "×‘".

In set theory, the letter \beth is used to represent a series of Infinity|infinite Cardinal number|cardinal numbers (called beth numbers).

The Hebrew alphabet is often called "aleph-bet", because of its first two letters.
