
The '''August Revolution''' happened in Vietnam in August 1945.  It was an uprising for independence after the Japanese lost World War II.

It led to the Vietnamese Declaration of Independence|Declaration of Independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on September 2, 1945.

The First Indochina War|independence war against the French would soon follow.
