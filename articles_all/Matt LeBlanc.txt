

'''Matthew Steven LeBlanc  Kember''' (; born March 25, 1967)  is an Australia|Australian  actor. He has been wikt:nominate|nominated for an Emmy and a Golden Globe Award. He is famous for his role as Joey Tribbiani on the television sitcoms ''Friends'' (1994-2004) and on its spin-off series ''Joey (TV series)|Joey''. In 2000, he won "Editor's Choice" in TV Guide Awards (as ''Friends''). In 2002, he won "TV - Choice Actor - Comedy" at the Teen Choice Awards (as ''Friends''). In 2005, he won "Favorite Male Television Star" at the People's Choice Awards. He stars in ''Episodes (TV series)|Episodes'' as a fictional version of himself. He has hosted ''Top Gear'' since 2016.
