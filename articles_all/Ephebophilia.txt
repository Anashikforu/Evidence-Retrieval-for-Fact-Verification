'''Ephebophilia''' is when an adult is mostly or only Sexual attraction|sexually attracted to post-Puberty|pubescent teenagers—usually those in the age range 15–19.

Specific words are used by scientists for chronophilias, which is when people are mostly or only sexually attracted to specific age groups. Along with ''ephebophilia'', another example is ''hebephilia'', which means that a person is mostly or only sexually attracted to younger Puberty|pubescent people.
