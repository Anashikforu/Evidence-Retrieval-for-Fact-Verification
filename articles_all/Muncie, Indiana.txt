
'''Muncie''' is a city in Indiana in the United States. It is known as "Middletown USA" because many sociology|sociological studies were conducted there because it was said to be the typical American city.

Muncie is in Delaware County, about  northeast of Indianapolis, Indiana|Indianapolis. The county was named after the Delaware Indians, a native American people, who had moved west from Ohio in the 1770's. They settled along the banks of the White River. One of the settlements was named Muncietown. In 1818 the Delaware signed a treaty with the US Government and moved out of the area to go further west.
