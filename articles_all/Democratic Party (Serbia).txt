
The '''Democratic Party''' () is a center-left political party in Serbia. It claims continuity of the Democratic Party (Yugoslavia)|historical Democratic Party.
The Democratic Party is a member of the Socialist International and Party of European Socialists. Both former President of Serbia Boris Tadić and former Prime Minister of Serbia|Prime Minister Mirko Cvetković are members of the Democratic Party.
