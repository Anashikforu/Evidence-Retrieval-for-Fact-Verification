'''Sirenomelia''', also known as '''Mermaid Syndrome''', is a very rare disorder in which the legs are stuck together, making them look like a mermaid's tail. This, however, is as wikt:rare|rare as conjoined twins, and only happens to one out of every 100,000 live births.{{
