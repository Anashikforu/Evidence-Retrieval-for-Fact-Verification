 
'''Michael Austin Cera''' (born June 7, 1988 in Brampton, Ontario|Brampton, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian actor who made his big screen debut in the 2000 movie Frequency (film)|Frequency as Gordy Jr., (Age 10) but first starred in the TV movie, Switching Goals as Taylor.

He was nominated for the Orange Rising Star Award in 2009.
