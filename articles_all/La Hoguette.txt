'''La Hoguette''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Basse-Normandie in the Calvados (department)|Calvados Departments of France|department in the northwest of France. The place name La Hoguette is derived from the Old Norse word ''haugr'' meaning a hill or mound.
