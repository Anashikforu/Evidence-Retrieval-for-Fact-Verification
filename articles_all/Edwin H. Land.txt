
'''Edwin H. Land''' (1909 - 1991) was an Americans|American physicist and inventor.

In 1932, he discovered a cheap and easy way to polarization|polarize light. In 1937 he set up the Polaroid Corporation to make scientific instruments and anti-glare sunglasses. In 1947 he developed the Polaroid camera. It could print photographs without needing photographic film|film.
