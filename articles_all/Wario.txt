

'''Wario''' is a fictional character|character from Nintendo's popular video game series ''Mario (Nintendo)|Mario''.  He has appeared in most of the ''Mario Party (series)|Mario Party'' and sports-related games, and has been the subject of a few Wario (series)|video games of his own.

Characteristics
Wario wears purple overalls with a yellow shirt, and green, pointed shoes. He has a purple W on his hat, similar to how Mario has an M on his. Wario is Mario's rival. He is greedy, and loves money and garlic. He is fat, but also very strong. He sometimes works together with Waluigi.
