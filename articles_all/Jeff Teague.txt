
'''Jeffrey "Jeff" Demarco Teague''' (born June 10, 1988 in Indianapolis, Indiana|Indianapolis, Indiana) is an Americans|American basketball player that currently plays for the Atlanta Hawks of the National Basketball Association|NBA. He went to Wake Forest University for college where he played for the Wake Forest Demon Deacons basketball team. He was drafted by the Atlanta Hawks with the 19th pick in the 2009 NBA Draft. Teague is probably best known for his known for his ability to drive to the basket and his shooting ability.
