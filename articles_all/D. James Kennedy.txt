
'''Dennis James Kennedy''' (or  '''D. James Kennedy''', November 3, 1930 - September 5, 2007) was an Americans|American Televangelism|televangelist and megachurch pastor. He started Coral Ridge Presbyterian Church in Fort Lauderdale, Florida and was the senior pastor there from 1960 until his death in 2007. He began Coral Ridge Ministries in 1974.
