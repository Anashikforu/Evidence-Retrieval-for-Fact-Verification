

'''Ericaceae''' is a family (biology)|family of flowering plants in the order (biology)|order Ericales. Plants of this family grow both in Northern Hemisphere and Southern Hemisphere. The common English language|English name of Ericaceae is the ''heath family''.


Many plants of Ericaceae are shrubs, but there are also trees and herbs.

There are about 120 
Many plants of Ericaceae are evergreen plants with small flowers in the shape of bells or tube. The color of flowers may be white, pink, red, Violet (color)|violet, rarely yellow. It may be interest plants for gardens.

Fruits of some species are food|edible.

Some plants of Ericaceae have a poison in all their parts.
The genus ''Epacris'' was treated in a separate family, Epacridaceae, but ''Epacris'' is now in this family.
