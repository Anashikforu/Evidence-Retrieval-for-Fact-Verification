
'''Bull Hill''', sometimes called '''Mount Taurus''', is a mountain in the U.S. state of New York. It is north of the village of Cold Spring, New York|Cold Spring on the Hudson River, in Putnam County, New York|Putnam County. It is part of the mountain range known as the Hudson Highlands which are part of the Appalachian Mountains.
Image:Breakneck Ridge panorama.jpg|thumb|200px|left|A view south from the summit of Bull Hill
An old quarry can be seen on its south ridge.

The mountain may have gotten its name from a Cattle|bull that farmers chased down it. The bull's fall gave the Breakneck Ridge its name.
