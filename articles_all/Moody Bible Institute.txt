

'''Moody Bible Institute''' ('''MBI''') is an Institution|institute in Chicago on the Near North Side, Chicago|Near North Side. In 1886, it was started by evangelist and businessman Dwight Lyman Moody. MBI has three major Religious ministry|ministries: education, broadcasting and publishing.
