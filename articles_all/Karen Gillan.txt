
'''Karen Sheila Gillan''' (born 28 November 1987) is a Scottish people|Scottish actress, Movie director|director, screenwriter and Model (person)|model. She is best known for playing the part of Amy Pond in the United Kingdom|British science fiction Television series|series, ''Doctor Who''.

Early life
Gillan was born on 28 November 1987 in Inverness, Scotland.

Her cousin, Caitlin Blackwood, played the part of the young Amy Pond in at least three episodes of ''Doctor Who''.
Career


= Modelling =

Before Gillan started acting on television, she worked in a London pub. She had had left Italia Conti for a television programme, but was hired by a modelling agency.
Gillan worked as a model in London Fashion Week, in 2007. She worked for Fashion design|designer Allegra Hicks' show.

= Acting =

Gillan began acting on television doing small parts, on several drama series. Her first larger role was as a part of the ensemble cast of the sketch comedy series ''The Kevin Bishop Show''. She worked for the series for two years. On the programme,  she played many different fictional character|characters. She also played celebrity|celebrities such as Katy Perry and Angelina Jolie. She was on Channel 4's 'Stacked'. She played the part of Ginny. Gillan also was on TV in a main part in a horror programme ''The Well''. It was broadcast as a series of short movies, on BBC Two. It was later shown on the internet.

Gillan was cast member|cast as Amy Pond on the British sci-fi series ''Doctor Who'' in May 2009. Her first episode as Amy Pond was shown on 3 April 2010.  Gillan had been in ''Doctor Who'' once before in the 2008 episode "The Fires of Pompeii" as a different character.

Gillan also acts in theatre. She first role was as Shirley in John Osborne's Play (theatre)|play ''Inadmissible Evidence'' at the Donmar Warehouse on October 16th 2011.

At the 2011 San Diego Comic-Con International|San Diego Comic-Con, Gillan said she will be on ''Doctor Who'' for its seventh series.

After leaving ''Doctor Who'', Gillan has appeared in 2013 horror film ''Oculus'' and 2014 Marvel superhero science fiction film ''Guardians of the Galaxy''. Both of films received positive reviews from critics, while ''Guardians of the Galaxy'' became a critical and commercial success, grossing over $774 million worldwide. Also in 2014, she was cast as the main character in ABC's sitcom ''Selfie''.

In May 2015, it was announced that Gillan was cast in the Adam McKay-directed drama film ''The Big Short'' alongside Brad Pitt, Christian Bale, Ryan Gosling, Steve Carell and Melissa Leo. Later that year, in September, Gillan was cast as one of the main characters, Annie, in the movie ''The Circle'' alongside Emma Watson, Tom Hanks and John Boyega. The film is directed and written by James Ponsoldt and is based on the novel written by Dave Eggers.
