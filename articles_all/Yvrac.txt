
'''Yvrac''' is a Communes of France|commune in the Regions of France|region Aquitaine in the Gironde Departments of France|department in southwest France.

In 2012 a large 18th century chateau near the commune was destroyed by mistake. Builders were supposed to remove another small building and then begin work on restoring the chateau. The owner, a Russian businessman, said he will have the house rebuilt.
