
'''Jacob Dowell''' (born March 4, 1985 in Eau Claire, Wisconsin|Eau Claire, Wisconsin) is an Americans|American professional ice hockey center that currently plays for the Dallas Stars of the National Hockey League|National Hockey League (NHL). He was selected by the Chicago Blackhawks with the 140th pick in the 2004 NHL Draft and played 4 seasons with them before being signed to the Dallas Stars on July 1, 2011.

Dowell also played for the Chicago Blackhawks American Hockey League|AHL farm team, the Rockford IceHogs.
