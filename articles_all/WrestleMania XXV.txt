

'''WrestleMania XXV''' (or The '''25th Anniversary of WrestleMania''' or '''WrestleMania 25'''), was the twenty-fifth yearly WrestleMania (PPV series)|WrestleMania professional wrestling Pay-per-view produced by World Wrestling Entertainment and took place on April 5, 2009 at Reliant Stadium in Houston, Texas. While WWE calls it the 25th Anniversary, it is only the 24th anniversary as WrestleMania|WrestleMania I cannot be an anniversary of itself.

Production
It was announced on January 30, 2009 that "Black Ice (album)|War Machine" and "Shoot to Thrill", both by AC/DC, would be the theme songs for WrestleMania.

=Background=


Since 1993, the winner of the yearly Royal Rumble#Match|Royal Rumble match at the event of WWE Royal Rumble|the PPV has won the opportunity to wrestle for a World Championship at WrestleMania.

On the February 16 edition of ''Raw'', Shawn Michaels challenged John Layfield|John "Bradshaw" Layfield to a match the following week for the right to face Undertaker at WrestleMania.

Money in the Bank (MITB) qualifying matches started on the February 23, 2009 edition of ''Raw'' with a triple threat match CM Punk versus John Morrison versus The Miz. CM Punk won the match and is the first of eight men to be added to the Money in the Bank ladder match. The following week on ''Raw'', another triple threat match between Rey Mysterio, Jr.|Rey Mysterio, Glen Jacobs|Kane and Mike Knox with the winner of this match qualifying for (winning his way into) the MITB ladder match. Kane won this match and as a result will be in the ladder match.
