
The '''Croatian Wikipedia''' is the Croatian language edition of Wikipedia. This edition was introduced in February 2003.

As of October 2015, it is the 41st largest edition of Wikipedia by number of articles.meta:List of Wikipedias|Wikimedia list of Wikipedias and their statistics.. Retrieved 25 October 2015.

, it has over 137,000 articles.:hr:Posebno:Statistika|Statistics at Croatian Wikipedia In September 2013 Croatia's Minister of Science, Education and Sports, Željko Jovanović, said students in Croatia should avoid using the Croatian Wikipedia. It has been accused of using forgery to promote the former Ustaše movement.
