

'''Alonzo Harding Mourning, Jr.''' (born February 8, 1970, in Chesapeake, Virginia) is a retired Americans|American professional basketball player who played most of his 16-year National Basketball Association|NBA career for the Miami Heat. He played college basketball at Georgetown University before playing in the NBA.
