
'''Losing Grip''' is a 2003 Single (music)|single by Avril Lavigne. It is the fourth single from her first studio album ''Let Go''.

On the ''Billboard (magazine)|Billboard'' charts, ''Losing Grip'' was able to peak at #17 on the Mainstream Top 40, #33 on the Adult Top 40, and at #64 on the Billboard Hot 100|Hot 100 charts.
