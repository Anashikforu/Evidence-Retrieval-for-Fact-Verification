'''Providence College''' is a private, Roman Catholic university in Providence, Rhode Island|Providence, Rhode Island. The university has about 4,585 students. It was founded in 1917. The current president of the university is Fr. Brian J. Shanley, O.P. . 

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Friars. They have 19 varsity teams and they compete in the Big East Conference.
