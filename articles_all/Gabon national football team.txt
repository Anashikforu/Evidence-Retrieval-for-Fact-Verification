
'''Gabon national football team''' is the national football (soccer)|football team of Gabon.

The team has never qualified for a FIFA World Cup|World Cup. Gabon has participated in 7 Africa Cup of Nations tournaments (1994 African Cup of Nations|1994, 1996 African Cup of Nations|1996, 2000 African Cup of Nations|2000, 2010 Africa Cup of Nations|2010, 2012 Africa Cup of Nations|2012, 2015 Africa Cup of Nations|2015 and 2017 Africa Cup of Nations|2017).

The team's home stadium is the Stade Omar Bongo in Libreville. The stadium can fit 41,000 people.
