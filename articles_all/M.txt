

'''M''' is the 13 (number)|13th letter#Letters of the alphabet|letter in the English language|English alphabet.

 Meanings for M 
 In calendars, M sometimes means Monday or means one of the months "March" or "May".
 In currency|money, M means million, such as $25M (25 (number)|twenty-five million dollars).
 In the International System of Units|SI, m is the symbol for the metre.
 In Roman numerals, M means 1000 (number)|one thousand.
 '''''M''''' is also the name of a 1930 Germany|German movie made by Fritz Lang and its 1951 remake.
