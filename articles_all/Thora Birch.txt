
'''Thora Birch''' (born March 11, 1982) is an Americans|American actor|actress. She was one of the most popular child actors of the 1990s in movies, such as ''Patriot Games'' (1992), ''Hocus Pocus (movie)|Hocus Pocus'' (1993), ''Clear and Present Danger'' (1994), ''Now and Then (1995 movie)|Now and Then'' (1995) and ''Alaska (movie)|Alaska'' (1996). Since the 1990s she has had more serious roles, with good acting in movies, such as ''American Beauty (movie)|American Beauty'' (1999) and ''Ghost World (movie)|Ghost World'' (2001).
