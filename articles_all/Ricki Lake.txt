'''Ricki Pamela Lake''' (born September 21, 1968) is an Americans|American actress, television producer|producer, and television host.  She wikt:host|hosted her own Ricki Lake (TV series)|talk show, named ''Ricki Lake'' from 1993 until 2004. Lake played Tracy Turnblad in the 1988 movie ''Hairspray (1988 movie)|Hairspray''. During the 1990s she starred in many movies. These included ''Serial Mom'' and ''Mrs. Winterbourne''. She recently hosted another talk show named ''The Ricki Lake Show'' which began in 2012.

Early life
Lake was born to secularism|secular, Jewish parents, Barry who is a pharmacist and Jill who is a housewife.
After her first talk show
Lake hosted ''Gameshow Marathon'' on CBS in 2006.

In September 2011, Lake competed in ''Dancing with the Stars'' with Derek Hough. She got third place. She lost to Rob Kardashian and J. R. Martinez, respectively.

Personal life

Lake married Rob Sussman on March 1994.
