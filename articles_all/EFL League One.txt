
'''English Football League One''' (commonly known as '''EFL League One''') is the third division of football in England. It was introduced in 2004, replacing the old Second Division.

 TV Coverage 
Sky Sports currently shows matches on their channel, and all highlights are available on the Sky Sports website. In the US, some important matches are shown on ESPN, along with EFL Championship and Football League Two|EFL League Two matches.
