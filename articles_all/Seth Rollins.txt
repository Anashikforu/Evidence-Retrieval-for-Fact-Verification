
'''Colby Lopez''' (born May 28, 1986) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He wrestles for the WWE currently on the WWE Raw|Raw brand. He competes under the ring name '''Seth Rollins'''. On March 29, 2015, he won the WWE Championship|WWE World Heavyweight Championship. Rollins is a former member of The Shield (professional wrestling)|The Shield and while he was with them, he was a WWE Tag Team Championship|WWE Tag Team Champion with Roman Reigns. Before being called up to the main roster, Rollins wrestled for the WWE's Farm team|developmental program ''WWE NXT|NXT'' and was the first NXT Championship|NXT Champion.

 Career 
Rollins has also wrestled for Ring of Honor (ROH), where he is a former ROH World Championship|ROH World Champion and a former two-time ROH World Tag Team Championship|ROH World Tag Team Champion with his Jimmy Jacobs. While on the independent circuit, he wrestled under the ring name '''Tyler Black'''. Aside from Ring of Honor, he has also competed in Scott County Wrestling, AAW: Professional Wrestling Redefined, Independent Wrestling Association Mid-South, the National Wrestling Alliance, Pro Wrestling Guerrilla, and Wrestling Society X.
