

'''Damian "Jr. Gong" Marley''' (born July 21, 1978 in Kingston, Jamaica) is a Jamaican singer. He is the son of Bob Marley. 

 Personal life 
Marley has released four studio albums. Marley has ten siblings: Ky-Mani Marley|Ky-Mani, Julian Marley|Julian, Stephanie, Karen, Rohan Marley|Rohan, Robbie, Stephen Marley (musician)|Stephen, Ziggy Marley|Ziggy, Cedella Marley|Cedella and Sharon Marley|Sharon.
