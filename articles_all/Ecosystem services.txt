'''Ecosystem services''' are processes by which the natural environment produces natural resource|resources useful to people, as to economic services. They include:

 Provision of clean water and air
 Flood control
 Pollination of agriculture|crops
 Mitigation of environmental natural hazard|hazards
 Pest and disease control
 Carbon sequestration
 Aesthetic, cultural and Ethics|ethical values associated with biodiversity.

To look at the way in which ecosystems provide Good (economics)|goods is popular now at Stanford University. The concept of ecosystem services is similar to that of natural capital.

The ''Millennium Ecosystem Assessment'' released in 2005 showed that 60% of ecosystem services are used in a way that destroys them.
