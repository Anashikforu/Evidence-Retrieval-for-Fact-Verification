
The '''Bernese Alps''' are a group of mountain ranges in the western part of the Alps, in Switzerland. Although the name notes that they are in the Bernese Oberland region of the canton of Bern, some of the Bernese Alps are found in the cantons of Vaud, Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg, Valais, Canton of Lucerne|Lucerne, Canton of Uri|Uri, and Nidwalden.
