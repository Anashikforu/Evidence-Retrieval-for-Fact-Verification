

'''''The Circus Surprise''''' is a children's picture book written by Ralph Fletcher with pictures drawn by Vladimir Vagin (illustrator)|Vladimir Vagin.  It was first published in 2001.

 Story 
Nick is taken to the circus as a surprise for his :wikt:birthday|birthday.  While at the circus he follows his nose looking for the cotton candy, and when he turns around his parents are gone.  A clown on :wikt:stilt|stilts comes to help him.  The clown puts him on his shoulders and they locate his parents.
