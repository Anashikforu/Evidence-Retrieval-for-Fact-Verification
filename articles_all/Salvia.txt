

'''''Salvia''''' is a genus in the mint family, Lamiaceae. It is one of three genus|genera commonly referred to as '''Sage'''. Sage generally means common sage (''Salvia officinalis''). This genus includes shrubs, herbs|herbaceous perennial plant|perennials, and annual plant|annuals. Different species of sage are grown as herbs and as ornamental plants. The ornamental species are commonly referred to by their scientific name ''Salvia''.
