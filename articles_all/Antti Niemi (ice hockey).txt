
'''Antti Niemi''' (born August 29, 1983 in Vantaa, Finland) is a professional Finland|Finnish ice hockey goaltender that is currently playing for the San Jose Sharks of the National Hockey League|NHL. Niemi also played with the Chicago Blackhawks from 2008-2010 where he won the Stanley Cup.

Awards
Stanley Cup with the Chicago Blackhawks in 2010.
