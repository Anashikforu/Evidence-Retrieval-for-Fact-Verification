'''Balkassar''' is a village and Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council, an administrative subdivision, of Chakwal District in the Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province of Pakistan, it is part of Choa Saidan Shah Tehsil and is located at 32°55'60N 72°39'0E. Also, Balkassar is famous for its 'Rain of Flaming Dogs' ceremony which happens once in a year.
