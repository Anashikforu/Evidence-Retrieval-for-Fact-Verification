The '''Central Queensland University''' is a large ten campus university in Queensland, Australia. It has main campuses at Bundaberg, Queensland|Bundaberg, Gladstone, Queensland|Gladstone, Rockhampton, Queensland|Rockhampton and Mackay, Queensland|Mackay. It has two learning centres at Noosa, Queensland|Noosa and Emerald, Queensland|Emerald. It also has campuses for international students in Brisbane, the Gold Coast, Queensland|Gold Coast, Sydney and Melbourne. The university began in 1967 as the Queensland Institute of Technology (Capricornia). It was made a university in 1992 as the University of Central Queensland and changed the name to CQUniversity Australia in 2008.
