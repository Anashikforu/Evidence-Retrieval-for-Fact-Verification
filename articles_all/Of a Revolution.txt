

'''Of a Revolution''', better known as '''O.A.R.''', is an United States|American rock band consisting of Marc Roberge (singer|vocals, guitar), Chris Culos (drum kit|drums, percussion), Richard On (guitar, backing vocals), Benj Gershman (bass guitar), Jerry DePizzo (saxophone, guitar, backing vocals). The band has a lot of fans in college and many of the band's songs relate to the band members' lives at The Ohio State University| Ohio State University (Columbus) and growing up in Rockville, Maryland.
