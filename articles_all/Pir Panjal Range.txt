The '''Pir Panjal Range''' (Kashmiri language|Kashmiri: .  Pir Panjal is the largest range of the Lesser Himalayas. Near the bank of the Sutlej River, it seperates itself from the Himalayas and forms a divide between the Beas River|Beas and Ravi River|Ravi rivers on one side and the Chenab on the other. The Galyat mountains are a part of this range. The region is connected to the Kashmir Valley|Valley of Kashmir by Mughal Road. It used to be the historical connection of Kashmir with India.
