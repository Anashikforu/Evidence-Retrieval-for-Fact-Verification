'''Tunceli''' is a province in the eastern part of Turkey. The province was named '''Dersim''' before 1936. The word '''Dersim''' means silver door in the Kurdish language. Its capital is also called Tunceli. 

Tunceli has one of the highest literacy rates in Turkey.
