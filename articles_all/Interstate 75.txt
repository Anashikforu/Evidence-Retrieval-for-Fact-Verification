'''Interstate 75''' (often shortened to '''75''') is an Interstate Highway System|Interstate Highway in the midwest and southeastern United States that runs north and south. It travels from State Road 826 (Florida)|State Road 826 (Palmetto Expressway) and State Road 924 (Florida)|State Road 924 (Gratigny Parkway) in Hialeah, Florida|Hialeah, Florida (just west of Miami, Florida|Miami) to Sault St. Marie, Michigan|Sault St. Marie, Michigan, at the Ontario, Canada, border. The route is  long.
