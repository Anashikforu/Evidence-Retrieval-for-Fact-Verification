
'''Two International Finance Centre''' (abbr. '''2 IFC''', branded as "'''ifc'''") is a skyscraper in Hong Kong.  It is 415 meters (1362&nbsp;feet) tall and has 88 floors.  It was built in 2003 and is one of the tallest buildings in the world
