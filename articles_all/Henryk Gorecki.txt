'''Henryk Mikolaj Gorecki''' (6 December 1933—12 November 2010) was a Poland|Polish composer. His most famous work was his ''Symphony No.&nbsp;3'', the ''Symphony of Sorrowful Songs'', written in 1992. More than one million recordings have been sold which makes it the most popular recording by a contemporary composer, getting to number 6 on the Britain|British album charts.

In 2010 Gorecki was given The Order of the White Eagle, Poland's highest honour.
