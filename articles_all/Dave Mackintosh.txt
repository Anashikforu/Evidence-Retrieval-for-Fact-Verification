


'''Dave "Compact Dynamo" Mackintosh''' is the drummer and backup vocalist for the band DragonForce. The band changed their musical genre|genre to 'Extreme Power Metal' after Dave started playing in the band. Dave does extreme metal drumming in DragonForce's music, such as blast beats.
