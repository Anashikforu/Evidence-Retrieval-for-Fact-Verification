

The  term '''French people''' (, the ''French'') refers to both an ethnic group in Western Europe and the Citizenship|citizens of France.  There are about 100 million French people in the world.

Ethnicity
French people are a group of people who have a common French France#Culture|culture, History of France|history and French language|lalnguage. French people can be found all over the world. The three countries, other than France, with the largest number of French people are the United States, Canada and Argentina. 

Citizenship
The term French people may also be used to describe a person who is a Citizenship|citizen of France. This is true no matter what their ethnicity. As long as they meet the requirements of French Law|laws to be a citizen of France, they are French people.
