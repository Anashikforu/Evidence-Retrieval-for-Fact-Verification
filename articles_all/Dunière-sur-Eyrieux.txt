
''' Dunière-sur-Eyrieux  ''' is a Communes of the Ardèche department|commune Ardèche in southern France.

The commune's name was changed from ''Dunières-sur-Eyrieux'' to ''Dunière-sur-Eyrieux'' (without "s") on 1st of January 2001.
