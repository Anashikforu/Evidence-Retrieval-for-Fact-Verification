

'''Dana Fuchs''' (born January 10, 1976) is an Americans|American singer, songwriter, actor|actress and voice artist|voice over talent.

The youngest of six musical children, Fuchs was born and raised in Wildwood, Florida. It is a small rural town. She is the daughter of Sandy and Don Fuchs.
Fuchs plays Sadie in the Julie Taymor movie ''Across the Universe (movie)|Across the Universe''. She also played the singing Janis Joplin in the Eric Nederlander production of Randall Myler's off Broadway musical ''Love, Janis''.
