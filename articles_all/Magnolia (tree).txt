


'''Magnolia''' is a genus of 210 species. The Flowering plant|Angiosperm taxonomy|taxonomical names ''Magnoliophyta'' (division (biology)|division) and ''Magnoliopsida'' (class (biology)|class) come from this genus. Charles Plumier gave a flowering tree from the island of Martinique the genus name ''Magnolia'', after France|French botany|botanist Pier Magnol.
Many magnolias that have pretty flowers are used as street trees.
