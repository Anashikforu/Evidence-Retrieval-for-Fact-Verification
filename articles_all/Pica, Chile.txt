'''Pica''' is a city in Chile, in the province of Tamarugal, Region of Tarapacá|Tarapacá. It is in an oasis in the middle of the Atacama Desert. Pica is famous for its small and unusually acidic lemons. It has an average temperature of 40°C. Pica has 6,178 inhabitants.
