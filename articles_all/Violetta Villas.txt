
'''Violetta Villas''' (born 10 June 1938  - 5 December 2011) was a Poles|Polish singer, actress, composer and songwriter. She was born in Verviers, Belgium. Her voice was a coloratura soprano which spans 4.7 octaves. In 1960s she was the star of revues in Paris, New York and Las Vegas.Witold Filler, Tygrysica z Magdalenki, 1993 She died in Lewin Klodzki, Poland.
