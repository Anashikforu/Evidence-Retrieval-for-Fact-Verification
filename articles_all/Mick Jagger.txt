Personal life
Jagger is known for his many high-profile relationships. He married Nicaraguan-born Bianca Jagger|Bianca De Macias on 12 May 1971. It was a Catholic ceremony in Saint-Tropez|Saint-Tropez, France. In late 1977, he began seeing model Jerry Hall. This was while still married to Bianca. Jagger and Hall lived together for many years. They were married on 21 November 1990, in a Hindu beach ceremony in Indonesia. The marriage ended in August 1999.
