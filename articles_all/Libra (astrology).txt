

'''Libra''' is an astrology|astrological sign,  related to the Libra (constellation)|constellation Libra, in the Southern Hemisphere near Scorpius and Virgo. In the tropical zodiac, the Sun meets Libra from September 23 to October 23, and in the Sidereal astrology|sidereal zodiac, it ends on November 15. Libra, (The Weighing scale|Scales of Balance), is the only symbol of the zodiac that is inanimate (it is not represented by an animal or person). The opposite sign to Libra is Aries (astrology)|Aries.

Symbol
Libra's symbol, like the scales, is the only symbol in the zodiac that is not represented by an animal or person.


 Mythology 
In mythology  a Libra is related to the Greek Goddess of Justice, Themis, the Greek mythology|Greek mythology version of Atalanta (meaning balanced), and Astraea (mythology)|Astraea (daughter of Themis), who went up to heaven and became the constellation of Virgo (astrology)|Virgo, and carried the scales of justice, which is the constellation Libra.

Libra is also related to the Greco-Roman goddess Aphrodite/Venus (mythology)|Venus and sometimes also the goddesses Eris (mythology)|Eris/Discordia & Harmonia (mythology)|Harmonia/Concordia (mythology)|Concordia,Dike, Themis, Hera/Juno (mythology)|Juno, Ishtar, Freyja, Persephone, Frigg and Forsetti the gods|god Xolotl.
