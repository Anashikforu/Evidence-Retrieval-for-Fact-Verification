'''Tuffé''' is a former Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Regions of France|region Pays de la Loire in the Sarthe Departments of France|department in the west of France. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of Tuffé-Val-de-la-Chéronne.
