
'''Baudouin I''' (; 7 September 1930 – 31 July 1993) was the King of the Belgium|Belgians from 1951 to 1993.Lyons, Richard D. 
The king was the oldest son of Leopold III of Belgium|King Leopold III (1901-1983) and his first wife, Princess Astrid of Sweden (1905-1935). 

On 15 December 1960, Baudouin was married in Brussels to Queen Fabiola of Belgium|Doña Fabiola de Mora y Aragón. The King and Queen had no children. All of the Queen's five pregnancies ended in miscarriage.
During Baudouin's reign the colony of Belgian Congo became independent.
