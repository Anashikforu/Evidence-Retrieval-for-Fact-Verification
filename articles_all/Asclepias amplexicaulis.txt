


'''''Asclepias amplexicaulis''''', also known as the '''clasping milkweed''', is a species of milkweed plant.  It is also known as '''blunt-leaved milkweed''' and '''sand milkweed'''.  It is in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to eastern North America.  The blooming period is in the spring.  This species is found in prairies and open woodlands, usually in sandy soil.  It is about 0.3 to 0.9 meters in height (1-3 feet).  Clasping Milkweed has poison that is wikt:dangerous|dangerous to humans and livestock.
