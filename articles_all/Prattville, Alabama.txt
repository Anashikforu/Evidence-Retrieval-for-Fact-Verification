
'''Prattville''' is a city in Autauga County, Alabama|Autauga and Elmore County, Alabama|Elmore counties in the U.S. state of Alabama. It is the county seat of Autauga County.  As of the 2010 United States Census|2010 Census, the population of the city is 33,960.  The city is northwest of Montgomery, Alabama|Montgomery.

Tornado
On February 17, 2008, Prattville was hit by an estimated EF3 tornado which destroyed more than 45 homes and businesses and damaged over 800.
