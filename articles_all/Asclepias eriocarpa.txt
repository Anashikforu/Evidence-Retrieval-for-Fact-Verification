


'''''Asclepias eriocarpa''''' (also known as the '''woollypod milkweed''') is a species of milkweed plant.  It is also known as '''Indian Milkweed''' and '''Kotolo'''.  It is in the subfamily Asclepiadoideae.  It is wikt:native|native to the western United States.  This species is found in dry, barren habitats.
