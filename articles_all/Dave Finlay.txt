'''David Finlay''' (born 20 October 1958) is a professional wrestling|professional wrestler and Road agent (professional wrestling)|road agent from Belfast, Northern Ireland. He is best known for his time in World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) under the ring name '''Finlay'''. He has held both the WCW World Television Championship and the WWE United States Championship.
