
'''Daniel''' "'''Dan'''" '''Tyminski''' (born 1967) is an Americans|American bluegrass musician. He is famous for being a member of Alison Krauss|Alison Krauss and Union Station. He has released two solo albums: ''Carry Me Across the Mountain'' (2000) and ''Wheels'' (2008).

Tyminski was born in Rutland, Vermont.
