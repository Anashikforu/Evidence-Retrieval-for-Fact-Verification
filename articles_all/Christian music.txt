'''Christian music''' is music about God, Jesus Christ and Christianity. Worship songs are found in the Jewish Psalms in the Bible.

 Styles 
Every kind of music genre and style is found in Christian music. This includes Country music|country, pop music|pop, Light music|easy listening, urban/hip hop, Rock music|rock, and hymns.

Artists include Bach, TobyMac, Phil Keaggy, Tenth Avenue North and Hillsong.
