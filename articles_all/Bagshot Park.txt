'''Bagshot Park''' (British national grid reference system|Grid reference: ) is a List of British Royal Residences|royal residence. It is eleven miles south of Windsor, Berkshire|Windsor. It is located in Bagshot, Surrey, and is the current home and official residence of Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex|Prince Edward, the Earl of Wessex and Sophie, Countess of Wessex. Bagshot Park is a few miles from Sunninghill Park, the former home of Prince Andrew, Duke of York.
