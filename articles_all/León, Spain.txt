'''León''' (''Llión'' in Leonese language) is a city and municipality in Province of Llión|León province in the northwest of Spain. About 135,000 people live there.

 History 
León was is the capital of the Kingdom of León, an ancient country.

 Language 
Leonese language is taught in sixteen schools.
