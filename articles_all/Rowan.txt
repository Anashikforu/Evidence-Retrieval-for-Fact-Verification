
A '''Rowan''' is a type of fruit-bearing plant in the family (biology)|family Rosaceae. They grow in the cooler areas of Europe and Asia. The fruit is a 4-8 millimetre|mm diameter Sphere|ball. It is bright Orange (color)|orange or red in most species, but pink, yellow or white in some Asian species. The fruit is soft and juicy.
