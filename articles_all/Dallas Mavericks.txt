

The '''Dallas Mavericks''' are an United States|American basketball team from Dallas, Texas|Dallas, Texas. They play in the National Basketball Association (NBA). They were founded in 1980. They play their home games at the American Airlines Center. Their owner is Mark Cuban. Though they have had good teams for most of the 2000s, they only made the NBA Finals once in 2006, where they lost to the Miami Heat 4-2. Dallas Mavericks players include Dirk Nowitzki, Jason Kidd, Jason Terry, Shawn Marion, Caron Butler, and Peja Stojakovic. They won their first Larry O'Brien Championship Trophy|NBA Championship in 2011 NBA Finals|2011 when they beat the Miami Heat in a 4-2 series in a rematch from 2006.
