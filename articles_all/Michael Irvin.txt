
'''Michael Jerome Irvin''' (born March 5, 1966) is a former American football player for the Dallas Cowboys. He is also a former broadcaster for ESPN|ESPN's ''NFL Countdown''. He is commonly known as one of the most successful wide receivers in the history of the National Football League.
