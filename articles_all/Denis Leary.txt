

'''Denis Colin Leary''' (born August 18, 1957 in Worcester, Massachusetts) is an Americans|American actor, comedian, producer, singer and writer. He is the star and co-creator of the television show ''Rescue Me (U.S. TV series)|Rescue Me''. His career as a comedian has been Controversy|controversial because he allegedly stole not only material but his entire act from the dead comedian Bill Hicks.
