


The South West Trains '''Class 458''' (or SR multiple unit numbering and classification|4JOP) electrical multiple units were built by Alstom at Washwood Heath between 1998 and 2000. These units are part of Alstom's Juniper (train)|''Juniper'' family of units, which also includes Classes British Rail Class 334|334 and British Rail Class 460|460.
