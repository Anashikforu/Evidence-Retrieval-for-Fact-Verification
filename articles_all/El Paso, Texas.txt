'''El Paso''' is a city in the U.S. state of Texas. It is in El Paso County, Texas|El Paso County and is the county seat (the city where the county is governed). It is at the western end of Texas, and is along Interstate 10. The name comes from El Paso de Norte, meaning The Passageway to the North, which was shortened to El Paso.

The large majority of the city's inhabitants are Hispanic.

El Paso has a desert climate.

On 3 August 2019, 22 people 2019 El Paso shooting|were killed in a mass shooting.
