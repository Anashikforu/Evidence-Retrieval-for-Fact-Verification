'''Hinterrhein''' is a district of the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Graubünden, Switzerland. It has an area of 617.67 km² and a population of 12,508 people (as of December 2004). 

Hinterrhein consists of five ''Kreise'' (sub-districts) and Thirty-nine|39 municipality|municipalities.
