"'''SOS'''" is a song by Barbadian people|Barbadian singer Rihanna. It is a dance-pop and Contemporary R&B|R&B song that samples the 1981 song "Tainted Love" by Soft Cell.  It was released on February 14, 2006 off of the album A Girl like Me (Rihanna album)|''A Girl like Me'' and went to number one on the Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100, her first song to do so. The song was originally written for Christina Milian.
