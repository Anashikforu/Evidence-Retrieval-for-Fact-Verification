
'''Pete Sampras''' (born August 12, 1971) is an Americans|American retirement|retired wikt:professional|professional tennis player. He has won the Wimbledon Championships seven times and has won 14 Grand Slam (tennis)|Grand Slams overall, which was a record until Roger Federer broke it in 2009. He is said to be one of the greatest tennis players of all time.

Personal and family life
Sampras' mother is an immigrant from Greece; his father is an American whose father was Greek and mother was Jewish. Sampras' older sister, Stella, is the women's tennis head coach at UCLA.

In the late 1990s, he dated actress Kimberly Williams-Paisley.

On September 30, 2000, Sampras married American actress and former Miss Teen USA, Bridgette Wilson. On November 21, 2002, their son, Christian Charles, was born. They live in Lake Sherwood, California.
