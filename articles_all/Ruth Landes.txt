

'''Ruth Landes''' (October 8, 1908, New York City – February 11, 1991, Ontario, Canada) was an USA|American cultural anthropologist best known for studies on Brazilian candomblé cults and her published study on the topic, ''City of Women'' (1947). Landes is recognized by some as a pioneer in the study of race and gender relations.
