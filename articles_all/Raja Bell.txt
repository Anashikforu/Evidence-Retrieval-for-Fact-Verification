
'''Raja Bell''' (born September 19, 1976 in Saint Croix, U.S. Virgin Islands|Saint Croix, U.S. Virgin Islands) is a basketball player who currently plays for the Utah Jazz in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He has also played for the Philadelphia 76ers, Dallas Mavericks, Utah Jazz, Phoenix Suns, and the Charlotte Bobcats. He began his NBA career with the San Antonio Spurs on August 2, 2000 but did not play a game with them. He is married to his wife, Cindy Green and they have three sons.
