
'''Altoids''' are a brand of breath Mint (candy)|mints and candy|candies that have been sold since the 1800s. There are many different flavors for each product.

 Flavors and Varieties 

= Mints =

Mint (candy)|Mints are sold in tin boxes, are round, and come in six flavors: Peppermint, Cinnamon, Wintergreen, Spearmint, and Arctic Strawberry

= Sours =

Sours are hard candy|candies that are sphere|round. They come in six flavors: Tangerine, Citrus, Raspberry, Apple, Mango, and Passion Fruit.

= Gum =

Altoid Chewing gum|gum is sugar-free, which means there is no sugar inside the gum. They come in six flavors: Peppermint, Spearmint, Cinnamon, Wintergreen, Cherry, and Apple.

= Breath Strips =

Breath Strips came in two flavors: Cinnamon and Peppermint. Since 2007, the product was discontinued, which means that the company is not selling them anymore.
