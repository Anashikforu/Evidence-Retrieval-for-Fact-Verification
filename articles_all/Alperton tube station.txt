'''Alperton''' is a London Underground station on the Uxbridge tube station|Uxbridge branch of the Piccadilly Line.  The station is between Sudbury Town tube station|Sudbury Town and Park Royal tube station|Park Royal. It is on Ealing Road (A4089 road) a short distance from the junction with Bridgewater Road (A4005 road|A4005) and is close to Alperton Bus Garage and the Paddington branch of the Grand Union Canal. The station is in Travelcard Zone 4.

Services
The typical off-peak service is a train in each direction every ten minutes. Half of northbound trains terminate at Rayners Lane tube station|Rayners Lane and the other half continue to the terminus of the line at Uxbridge.
