Biography
Sugamata was born in Utsunomiya, Tochigi|Utsunomiya on November 29, 1957. After graduating from Hosei University, he joined Japan Soccer League club Kashiwa Reysol|Hitachi in 1980. The club won the 2nd place in 1980 JSL Cup and 1982 Japan Soccer League. He retired in 1987. He played 131 games in the league. He was selected Best Eleven in 1980 and 1982).

On July 23, 1978, when Sugamata was a Hosei University student, he debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Singapore national football team|Singapore. After he joined Kashiwa Reysol|Hitachi, he played for Japan again in June 1980. In December, he was selected Japan for 1982 FIFA World Cup|1982 World Cup qualification. In 1982, he also played at Football at the 1982 Asian Games|1982 Asian Games. He played 23 games for Japan until 1984.
