
'''''Yippee''''' is a 2006 documentary movie|documentary movie created by Judaism|Jewish-United States|American Film director|director Paul Mazursky. The movie follows a Jewish pilgrimage to the town of Uman in the Ukraine on Rosh Hashanah, the Jewish New Year. The city is the burial site of the founder of Bratslav Hasidism, Nachman of Breslov|Rabbi Nachman.
