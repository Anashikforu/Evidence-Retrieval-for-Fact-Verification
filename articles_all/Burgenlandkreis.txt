
'''Burgenlandkreis''' is a district in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. It was formed by joining the old districts of Burgenlandkreis (former district)|Burgenlandkreis and Weißenfels (district)|Weißenfels as part of the boundary changes of 2007.

On 16 July 2007 the district parliament decided to change the name back to Burgenlandkreis from 1 August 2007.
