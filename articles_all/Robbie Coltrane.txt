

'''Anthony Robert McMillan''' Order of the British Empire|OBE (born 30 March 1950), known professionally as '''Robbie Coltrane''', is a Scottish people|Scottish actor, author and comedian. He is known both for his role as Dr. Eddie "Fitz" Fitzgerald in the British television series ''Cracker (UK TV series)|Cracker'' during the 1990s and as Rubeus Hagrid in the Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' movies.

 Life and career 
= Life =
Coltrane was born on 30 March 1950 in Rutherglen, South Lanarkshire.  He now lives in Killearn Stirlingshire.  He has a son named Spencer and a daughter named Alice.

= Career =
Coltrane began his acting career in his early twenties. He took the stage name Coltrane in tribute to jazz saxophonist John Coltrane In 2006, Robbie received an OBE for his services to drama.
