


'''''Ghoongat''''' (also spelled Ghunghat) () is an Urdu language|Urdu movie released in the summer of 1996. It starred Mohsin Khan, Andaleeb, Shaan, Resham and Arbaaz Khan (actor)|Arbaaz Khan. The movie marked Shaan's comeback into the Lahore industry and won the actor many plaudits for his performance as the villain. Ghoongat was movie director|directed by Syed Noor and movie producer|produced by Shezad Rafique.
