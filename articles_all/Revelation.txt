'''Revelation''' is when something is made clear or obvious that was hidden before. Most of the time, the term is used to speak about religion|religious revelations. That means that the revelation is done by communication with the divine. Revelation can be through a deity who may send a messenger. Messengers that are supernatural are often called angels (Angel literally means messenger). Human messengers tend to be called prophets.
