


'''''Star Trek V: The Final Frontier''''' was the fifth movie set in the Star Trek fictional universe|universe. It was made in 1989 by Paramount Pictures. It happens after the events of ''Star Trek IV: The Voyage Home|The Voyage Home''. Spock's Sibling|half-brother Sybok goes on a spiritual journey to find "God" and takes the Enterprise to the centre of the galaxy.
