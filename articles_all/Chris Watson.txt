
'''John Christian Watson''' (c. 9 April 1867 - 18 November 1941), known as '''Chris Waston''', was the third Prime Minister of Australia|Prime Minister of Australia. He was the first Prime Minister from the Australian Labor Party|Labor Party. He was Prime Minister for only four months.
