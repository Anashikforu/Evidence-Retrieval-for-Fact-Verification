
The '''Governor of Minnesota''' is the chief executive of the U.S. state of Minnesota, leading the state's executive branch. Thirty-nine different people have been governor of the state. State governors are elected to office by popular vote. The current governor of Minnesota is United States Democratic Party|Democratic Tim Walz.
