'''''Mickey Mouse Clubhouse''''' was a children's television program|television series on Playhouse Disney and Disney Channel. It was produced by Disney animation studio|Disney.
It showed Mickey Mouse and his playmates in their journeys in different places as they learned on the way.
They took help with 3, 4 or 5 items called Mouskatools given by a Mickey Head looking Toodles.
