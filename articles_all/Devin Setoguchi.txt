
'''Devin Setoguchi''' (born January 1, 1987 in Taber, Alberta (town)|Taber, Alberta) is an Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey right winger that currently plays for the Minnesota Wild. Setoguchi also played 4 seasons in the Western Hockey League|WHL; 3 for the Saskatoon Blades and 1 for the Prince George Cougars. Setoguchi is of Canadian-Japanese origins.
