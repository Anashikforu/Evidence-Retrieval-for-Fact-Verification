'''Kshatriya''' is a division of the caste system about Rajput Jatt Gurjar in Hinduism. A ''kshatriya'' is a warrior, ruler or noble. Rajputs are universally considered as kshatriyas .

There are 3 lineages of Kshatriya|Kshatriyas:-
 Suryavanshi (Solar Dynasty)
 Chandravanshi (Lunar Dynasty)
 Agnivanshi (Fire Dynasty)

However, there is one more lineage (not mentioned in any Puranas) - ‘’’Nagavanshi’’’ (snake-born), who claims descent from the Nagas.
