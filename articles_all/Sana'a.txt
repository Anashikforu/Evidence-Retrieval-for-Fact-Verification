

'''Sana'a''' or '''Sanˤā'''' (, romanized as Ṣanʻāʼ or Sanaa), is the capital city of Yemen and the center of Sana'a Governorate. It had a population of 1,747,627 (as of 2004). In the 2010s it became a battlefield in the Yemeni Civil War (2015–present)|Civil War and the government moved to Aden.
