

'''Karak District''' is a district in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. The city of Karak (Pakistan)|Karak is the capital. This district is the second smallest district in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa after Hangu District|Hangu. The area of this district is 3395&nbsp;km2.

Tehsils
The district of Karak is administratively subdivided into three tehsils.

 Banda Daud Shah Tehsil
 Karak Tehsil
 Takht-e-Nasrati Tehsil
