
'''Mark Appleyard''' (born November 11, 1982 in Oakville, Ontario|Oakville, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian professional Skateboarding|skateboarder. He is currently sponsored by Element Skateboards, Thunder, Autobahn, Globe Shoes, Volcom, CCS, Arnette, and Vivo. He was the Thrasher (magazine)|Thrasher Skater of the Year in 2003.
