
'''''Scratchy and Co''''' was a CITV television series. It was shown over the different times in the mid to late 1990s:
 May - December 1995
 May - August 1996
 January - April 1997
 January - April 1998

It replaced ''What's Up Doc? (TV series)|What's Up Doc?'' as the Saturday morning ITV show.

It starred Mark Speight as "Scratchy" and Elliot Henderson-Boyle as "Reg". The main gimmick of the series was Speight and Henderson-Boyle wearing "solid rubber wigs" and very unusual jackets. A Style|stylistic of this was similar to ''Max Headroom''.
