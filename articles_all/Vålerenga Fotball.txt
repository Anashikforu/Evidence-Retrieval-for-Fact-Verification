
'''Vålerenga Fotball''' is a Norway|Norwegian Football (soccer)|football club from Oslo. It was :wikt:found|founded in 1913. They are named after the neighbourhood of Vålerenga. They finished as runners-up in the 2010 Norwegian Premier League, the last time they won was in 2005. Vålerenga's home ground is Ullevaal Stadion, the national stadium for Norway national football team|Norway. Their current coach is Martin Andresen.
