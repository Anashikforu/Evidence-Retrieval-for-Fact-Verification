
'''Johan Cruyff''' (25 April 1947 - 24 March 2016) was a Dutch people|Dutch Association football|football player and manager. He played for Netherlands national football team|Netherlands national team.

Cruyff died of lung cancer. His son is football manager Jordi Cruyff. He is regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time.
