

'''Sinaloa''' is a northwestern Mexico|Mexican state. It is bordered by Sonora to the north, Chihuahua (state)|Chihuahua to the northeast, Durango to the east, and Nayarit to the south. To the west lies the Pacific Ocean and the Gulf of California, also known as the Sea of Cortez.
