'''Ávila Province''' () is a Provinces of Spain|province of central-western Spain, in the southern part of the Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community of Castile and León. It is bordered on the south by the provinces of Province of Toledo|Toledo and Province of Cáceres|Cáceres, on the west by Province of Salamanca|Salamanca, on the north by Province of Valladolid|Valladolid, and on the east by Province of Segovia|Segovia and Community of Madrid|Madrid. Ávila has a population of 165,138 (2002). Its capital is Ávila, Spain|Ávila.
