


'''Simon Cowell''' Order of the British Empire|MBE (born 19 April 1952) is a British television presenter and author who hosts the Animal Planet documentary series ''Wildlife SOS''. He is the co-founder of a Leatherhead, Surrey, based charity called Wildlife Aid.

In June 2005, Simon was awarded an Order of the British Empire|MBE for his services to wildlife.

Simon lives in Leatherhead, Surrey. He received a PhD for Biological sciences at Jesus College, Cambridge.
