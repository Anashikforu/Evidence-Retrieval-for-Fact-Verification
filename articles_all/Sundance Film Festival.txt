
The '''Sundance Film Festival''' is an American movie festival that takes place every year in Utah. It is one of the largest independent movie festivals in the United States. 

The festival is held in January in Park City, Utah|Park City, Salt Lake City, Ogden, Utah|Ogden, and at the Sundance Resort. It is an event for new work from Americans|American and international independent movie makers. The festival has competition for American and international Drama movie|drama and documentary movies, feature films and short films.

 2015 
The 2015 Sundance Film Festival took place January 22 to February 1, 2015.



 Success of festival 
Many notable independent movie makers first got noticed at Sundance, including Kevin Smith, Robert Rodriguez, Quentin Tarantino, Todd Field, David O. Russell, Steve James, Steven Soderbergh, Darren Aronofsky, James Wan, Edward Burns, and Jim Jarmusch. 

The festival has also given more attention and a larger audience to such movies as ''Saw (movie)|Saw'', ''Super Troopers'', ''The Blair Witch Project'', ''Spanking the Monkey'', ''Reservoir Dogs'', ''Primer (movie)|Primer'', ''Little Miss Sunshine'', ''Clerks'', ''Sex, Lies, and Videotape'', ''The Brothers McMullen'', ''Afternoon Delight'', ''Napoleon Dynamite'', and ''Whiplash (2014 movie)|Whiplash''.
