
'''Fabian Washington''' (born June 9, 1983 in Bradenton, Florida) is an Americans|American American football|football cornerback. He last played for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League|National Football League (NFL). Washington was drafted out of the University of Nebraska by the Baltimore Ravens with the 23rd pick in the first round of the 2005 NFL Draft.
