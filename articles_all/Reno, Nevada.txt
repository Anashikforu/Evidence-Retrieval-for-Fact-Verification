'''Reno''' is a city in the United States|American state of Nevada. It is the county seat of Washoe County, Nevada|Washoe County. As of 2000, there were 180,480 people living in Reno. Charles Fuller built the first log cabin in 1859 and was named Lakes Crossing in 1861.
