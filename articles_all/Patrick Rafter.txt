
'''Patrick''' "'''Pat'''" '''Rafter''' (born 28 December 1972) is a retirement|retired professional tennis player from Australia. He was once ranked number one in the world. Rafter won the U.S. Open (tennis)|US Open in 1997 and 1998. He also reached and lost the 2000 and 2001 The Championships, Wimbledon|Wimbledon finals to Pete Sampras and Goran Ivanišević respectively.
