The word '''being''' means a living person or animal. ‘Human being’ means the same as ’person’. man|Men, woman|women, and children are human beings.

Some people write stories or make movies about beings from other planets. Most religions talk about supernatural beings, for example spirits, angels, devils, deity|gods, or God.
