
'''Noel Ernest Edmonds''' (born 22 December 1948) is an English people|English television presenter. He is best known for hosting the television programmes ''Noel's House Party'' and ''Deal or No Deal (UK game show)|Deal or No Deal''. Edmonds is host of a game show called ''Are You Smarter Than A Ten Year Old'' (the British version of ''Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?''). 

He was a DJ on BBC Radio 1 throughout the 1970s. Edmonds also presented Swap Shop.

Personal life
Edmonds was married to Gillian Slater in 1971. The marriage ended in divorce after eleven years.

It was reported that Edmonds was involved with English teacher and former Miss England Pauline Bull. 

On July 23, 2009 Edmonds married his third wife, Liz Davies.  She was a make-up artist on the programme Deal or No Deal (UK game show)|Deal or No Deal.
