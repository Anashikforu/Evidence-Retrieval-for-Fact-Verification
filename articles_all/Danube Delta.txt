
The '''Danube river delta|Delta'''''Delta Dunării'' in Romanian language|Romanian, Дельта Дуная in Russian language|Russian, Дельта Дунаю in Ukrainian language|Ukrainian is the second largest river delta|delta in Europe, after the Volga Delta. It is one of the best preserved deltas on the continent.
