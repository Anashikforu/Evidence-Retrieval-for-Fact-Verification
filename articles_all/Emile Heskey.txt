

'''Emile William Ivanhoe Heskey''' (born 11 January 1978) is an England|English Association football|footballer. He currently plays for Bolton Wanderers in the Football League Championship. 

At the pinnacle of his career, he played for Leicester City F.C.|Leicester City, Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool and Birmingham City F.C.|Birmingham City. he later he played for Wigan Athletic F.C.|Wigan Athletic and Aston Villa F.C.|Aston Villa, then Newcastle United Jets F.C.|Newcastle Jets in the Australian A-League. For a while he was a regular member of the England national football team|England national team, striking up a good partnership with fellow forward Michael Owen, where he was often deployed as a 'Target Man' due to his immense strength, good first touch and passing skills.
