

During World War II, the Netherlands was taken by Germany by military force, and wikt:occupied|occupied it between 1940-1945. It started with the Battle of the Netherlands in 10 May 1940. On May 15, the Dutch government surrender (military)|surrendered and the Germans took over. The Dutch government went to London. The country was liberated on 5 May 1945, when the Germans surrendered to the Canada|Canadian forces.
