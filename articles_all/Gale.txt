A '''gale''' is a very strong wind. The Beaufort scale is a way to measure weather conditions. It is based on observations. The first level of the scale that uses the word gale is 7 beaufort (of a total of 13 steps). There are other definitions as well. The U.S. Government's National Weather Service defines a gale as 34 to 47 Knot (speed)|knots (63&nbsp;km/h to 87&nbsp;km/h or 39 miles per hour to 54 miles per hour) of sustained surface winds.
