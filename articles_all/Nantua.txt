Administration
Nantua is a Prefectures in France|subprefecture of the Ain department and the capital of the arrondissement of Nantua in the same department. It is also the capital of the canton of Canton of Nantua|Nantua, formed by 18 ''communes'' (21,477 inhabitants) (2014)

It is part of the Communes of France|intercommunality ''Haut Bugey''.
