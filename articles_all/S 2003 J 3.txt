'''''' is a Satellite (natural)|moon of Jupiter (planet)|Jupiter. It was found by a team of astronomers from the University of Hawaii led by Scott S. Sheppard in 2003.
 is about 2 kilometres in diameter, and orbits Jupiter at an average distance of 19,622,000&nbsp;km in 561.518 days, at an inclination of 146° to the ecliptic (146° to Jupiter's equator), with an orbital eccentricity of 0.2507.

It belongs to the Ananke group, retrograde non-Sphere|spherical moons which orbit Jupiter between 19,300,000 and 22,700,000&nbsp;km, at inclinations of about 150°.
