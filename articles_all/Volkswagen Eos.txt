
The '''Volkswagen Eos''' is a car produced by Volkswagen since 2006. It replaced the in 2001 discontinued Volkswagen Golf Cabriolet.

 ''3.2 FSI'' 184 kW (250 hp)
 ''3.6 FSI'' 191 kW (260 hp)
 ''2.0 TDI'' 103 kW (140 hp)
