'''Crop milk''' is a way of feeding from the Crop (anatomy)|crop of parent birds, by Regurgitation|regurgitating to young birds. They are found among all Columbidae|pigeons and doves where they are referred to as '''pigeon milk'''. Crop milk is also produced by flamingos and even some penguins.
