

'''Michael John Sweeney''' (born July 22, 1973 in Orange, California|Orange, California) is a retirement|retired first baseman and designated hitter in Major League Baseball. He played for the Kansas City Royals, Oakland Athletics, Seattle Mariners and the Philadelphia Phillies. Sweeny retired in 2011.
