'''Paul Rodgers''' (born 17 December 1949) is an England|English rock singer and songwriter.  He is best known for being a member of Free (band)|Free and Bad Company. He has recently toured and recorded with Brian May and Roger Taylor of Queen (band)|Queen in a group called Queen + Paul Rodgers.
