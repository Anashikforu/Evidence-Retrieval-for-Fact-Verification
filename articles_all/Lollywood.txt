'''Lollywood''' () is the name of Pakistani movie industry that is based in the city of Lahore. The name is a combination of the city's first letter with the name "Hollywood". The word "Lollywood" was first used in the summer of 1989.

Languages used in Lollywood movies
The movies made in Lollywood are usually in Urdu language|Urdu. Some are made in Punjabi language|Punjabi, which is the first language|main and official language of the Administrative units of Pakistan|province of Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab, where Lollywood is located. Some are also in English language|English.
