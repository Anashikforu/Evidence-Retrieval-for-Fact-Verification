 

'''Noah Andrew Ringer''' (born November 18, 1996) 
Life and career

Ringer is a young taekwondo practitioner who was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. After being signed for The Last Airbender movie, he joined the Creative Artists Agency.

Ringer attended the 2010 Nickelodeon Kids Choice Awards on March 27 with his ''Airbender'' cast mates Nicola Peltz, Dev Patel, and Jackson Rathbone to debut the new TV spot for the film. He watched Nicola Peltz and Jackson Rathbone perform "Slimebending".
