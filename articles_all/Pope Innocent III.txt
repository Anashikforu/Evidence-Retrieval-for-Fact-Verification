Early life 
Lotario di Conti was born at Anagni
During the short reigns of Pope Lucius III, Pope Urban III, Pope Gregory VIII, and Pope Clement III, he served in the church bureaucracy.

Cardinal
In 1190, Clement III raised him to the rank of Cardinal (Catholicism)|Cardinal.

Pope
Cardinal Segni was unanimously elected pope on January 8, 1198; and he took the name of Innocent III.

Pope Innocent was involved in Italian and European political disputes.

Pope Innocent promoted the theory that the spiritual power of the church was greater than than any king or emperor.

=Crusades=
Innocent III was responsible for starting the Fourth Crusade, the Albigensian Crusade, and the Fifth Crusade.
=Lateran Council=
In 1215, Innocent brought together the church's bishops and cardinals in a meeting called the Timeline of Christianity|Fourth Lateran Council.
