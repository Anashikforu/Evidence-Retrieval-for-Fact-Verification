'''Werner Naumann''' (16 June 1909 in Góra|Guhrau - 25 October 1982 in Lüdenscheid) was a Germany|German State Secretary in the Ministry of Public Enlightenment and Propaganda|Propaganda Ministry during the Third Reich. He was appointed head (minister) of the Propaganda Ministry by Adolf Hitler|Hitler in his political testament. This was because Joseph Goebbels|Goebbels was promoted to be the Chancellor of Germany.

He was a member of the NSDAP and the Sturmabteilung|SA. During the war he was also engaged in battle activities, first as an officer in the Luftwaffe, later on in the Waffen-SS and the Schutzstaffel|SS in general.

Naumann was in the Führerbunker during the last days of Hitler in April 1945.
