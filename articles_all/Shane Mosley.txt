He held the International Boxing Federation|IBF Lightweight Championship, World Boxing Council|WBC & World Boxing Association|WBA (Super) Welterweight Championships and WBC, WBA (Super) & The Ring (magazine)|''The Ring'' Light Middleweight Championships.
