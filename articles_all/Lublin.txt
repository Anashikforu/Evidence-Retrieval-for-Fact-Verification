'''Lublin''' - city in eastern Poland. It's in the Lublin Voivodeship. It has an area of 147.5 Kilometre|km2. About 354,000 people live in Lublin.

Characteristic for Lublin are: high schools (incl. Catholic University of Lublin, Maria Curie-Sk≈Çodowska University, Lublin University of Technology, Medical University of Lublin), Majdanek State Museum and trolleybuses.
