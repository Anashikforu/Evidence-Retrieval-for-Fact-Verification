
'''Ozark''' is a city in and the county seat of Dale County, Alabama|Dale County, Alabama, United States. In United States Census, 1980|1980 the population was 12,205, and in United States Census, 1990|1990 it was 13,030. As of the United States Census, 2000|2000 census, the population of the city is 15,119.
