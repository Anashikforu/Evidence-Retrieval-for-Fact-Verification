

'''Drew Galloway''' (born 6 June 1985, in Ayr, Scotland), is a Scottish professional wrestler, better know by his ring name '''Drew McIntyre'''. He is currently signed to World Wrestling Entertainment|WWE signed to the raw brand. During his previous run in the WWE, he won the WWE Intercontinental Championship one time and the WWE Tag Team Championship one time with Dolph ziggler. He has also won the NXT championship . McIntyre married former WWE diva Taryn Terrell (known by the ring name '''Tiffany''') in May 2011 but they divorced on 24 May 2011.

On 12 June 2014, McIntyre was released from his WWE contract.

In April 2017, he returned to World Wrestling Entertainment|WWE in their developmental territory WWE NXT|NXT. He is the current NXT Championship|NXT Champion. In 2020, he won the Royal Rumble.
