The '''1950 FIFA World Cup''' was a association football|football sporting event that was held in Brazil in 1950. 
This competition was special compared to other FIFA World Cup tournaments as the winner was decided by winning a mini-league instead of a knock-out game.  Uruguay won the trophy after finishing higher than Brazil, Sweden and Spain in the final group stage.
