

'''Hsinchu County''' (Chinese language|Chinese:新竹縣; Pe̍h-ōe-jī|POJ: Sin-tek-kōan) is a county in northwestern Taiwan. The population is mainly Hakka people|Hakka; there is a Taiwanese aboriginal minority in the southeastern part of the county.

 Management 
Hsinchu County controls one township-level city (縣轄市), three city-townships (鎮), and nine rural townships (鄉).
