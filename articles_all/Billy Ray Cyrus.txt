

'''Billy Ray Cyrus''' (born August 25, 1961)

He is best known for his popular single "Achy Breaky Heart" (1992).

From 2001 to 2004, Cyrus starred in the television series ''Doc (TV series)|Doc''. The series was about a country doctor who moved from Montana to New York City. He was in the Disney Channel series, ''Hannah Montana'', which he co-starred with his daughter Miley Cyrus.
