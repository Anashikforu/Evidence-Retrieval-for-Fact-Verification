'''"Wake Up Call"''' is the second single (music)|single off the second album|studio album by Maroon 5 called ''It Won't Be Soon Before Long''. The song is also the 4th track (music)|track on the album. The music video deals with the band committing crimes and Adam Levine|Levine acting as a murderer because of the murder of his girlfriends ex-boyfriend.

 Mark Ronson remix 
A remix version of the song was released in 2007 by Mark Ronson. The remix song was originally released on iTunes Store on November|November 13th, 2007. The song later appeared in the hit album|remix album by Maroon 5 called ''Call and Response: The Remix Album''.
