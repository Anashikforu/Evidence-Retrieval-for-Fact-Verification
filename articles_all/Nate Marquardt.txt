
'''Nathan Joel Marquardt''' (born April 20, 1979 in Lander, Wyoming|Lander, Wyoming) is an Americans|American mixed martial arts|mixed martial artist. He currently fights for the Ultimate Fighting Championship|UFC in the middleweight division and fights out of Denver, Colorado. Marquardt holds a second black belt in both Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Jujutsu. His current record is 32 wins, 10 losses and 2 draws.
