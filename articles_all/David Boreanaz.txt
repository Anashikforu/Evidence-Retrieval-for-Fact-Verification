

'''David Paul Boreanaz''' (born May 16, 1969) is an Americans|American actor. He is best known for his role on the television programme|television series ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer'' and ''Angel (TV series)|Angel''. On both of these shows, he played the fictional character|character Angel. From fall of 2005 to spring of 2017, he has played Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI agent Seeley Booth on ''Bones (TV series)|Bones''.

 Biography 
= Early life =
David Boreanaz was born on May 16, 1969 in Buffalo, New York. He grew up in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His father is a television Meteorology|weatherman and his mother is a travel agent.
Boreanaz went to high school in Malvern, Pennsylvania|Malvern, Pennsylvania. He went to college at Ithaca College in Ithaca, New York. After graduation|graduating, Boreanaz moved to Hollywood, California to become an actor.

= Career =
Boreanaz' first television acting job was a single episode on the hit American situation comedy|sitcom, ''Married... with Children''. He played Kelly Bundy's motorcycle|biker boyfriend. He was cast in the television series ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer'', after being seen by a movie producer|producer when Boreanaz was walking his dog. In the series, he played Angel, a vampire with a soul. The series became very successful and Boreanaz starred in a spin-off series, ''Angel''. This new series gave the character a chance to evolution|evolve. It was mainly about on Angel's battle for redemption. He appeared on ''Buffy'' from 1997 to 1999, at which point he began starring in ''Angel'', which ran until 2004. Totalling all of his appearances on ''Buffy'' & ''Angel'', including guest appearances, Boreanaz has been in more episodes of the 2 series than any other actor.

Boreanaz's only starring role in a major movie theater|theatrical movie was in 2001's Slasher movie|slasher horror movie, ''Valentine (movie)|Valentine''. This movie also starred Denise Richards and Katherine Heigl. In 2003, he appeared in the music video for singer Dido (singer)|Dido's "White Flag (song)|White Flag". He was also the voice actor|voice of Leon (aka Squall Leonhart) in the video game ''Kingdom Hearts''.

In 2005, Boreanaz began starring along with Emily Deschanel on the prime time television series, ''Bones (TV series)|Bones''. He most recently appeared in ''These Girls'', a Canada|Canadian movie in which he played a motorcycle|biker. The movie was first shown at the Toronto Film Festival and the Vancouver International Film Festival. It was later shown in a small number of movie theater|theaters in Canada in March 2006. His future roles include parts in the independent films ''Mr. Fix it'' and ''Suffering Man's Charity''. It is also wikt:rumor|rumored that Boreanaz will star in the upcoming movie ''Jurassic Park 4'' which will be released some time in 2008. In that same year, he also voiced Hal Jordan in the direct to video DC Comics animation|animated movie ''Justice League: The New Frontier''.

= Personal life =

Boreanaz lives in Los Angeles, California|Los Angeles, California. He was married to Ingrid Quinn from June 7, 1997 to October 1999. He married actress and model (person)|model Jaime Bergman on November 24, 2001. The couple has a son, Jaden Rayne Boreanaz, who was born on May 1, 2002. He and his wife are also good friends with Raven Symone. David attended her 21st birthday in December 2006.
