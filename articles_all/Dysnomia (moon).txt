
'''Dysnomia'''Formal designation: '''136199 Eris I Dysnomia'''; Provisional designation: '''S/2005 (2003 UB313) 1'''. is the only known Satellite (natural)|moon of the dwarf planet Eris (dwarf planet)|Eris. It was found on September 10, 2005 by Michael E. Brown and his team at the W. M. Keck Observatory.
