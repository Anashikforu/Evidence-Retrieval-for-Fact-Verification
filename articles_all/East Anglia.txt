'''East Anglia''' is a region of eastern England, named after one of the wikt:ancient|ancient Anglo-Saxons|Anglo-Saxon kingdoms, which was named after the homeland of the Angles, Angeln in northern Germany. The kingdom consisted of Norfolk and Suffolk ("North folk" and "South folk") but the region's borders are vague. 

Agriculture|Farming and gardening are very successful in this wikt:fertile|fertile country. The landscape has been heavily influenced by Netherlands|Dutch technology.
