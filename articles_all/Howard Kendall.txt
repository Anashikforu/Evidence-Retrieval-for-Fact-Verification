
'''Howard Kendall''' (22 May 1946 - 17 October 2015) was an English people|English association football|football player turned manager. He was born in Ryton, Tyne and Wear|Ryton, County Durham. He is most famous for managing Everton FC during one of their most successful periods. He was briefly :wikt:chairman|chairman of the club and has also been manager of Manchester City and Blackburn Rovers.
