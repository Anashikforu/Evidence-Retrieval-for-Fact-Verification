'''Danica Mae McKellar''' (born January 3, 1975) is an Americans|American actress and mathematician. She is best known for playing Winnie Cooper in ''The Wonder Years''. She also starred in several television movies.

She is also a ''New York Times'' bestselling author.

 Personal life 
She married composer Mike Verta on March 22, 2009, in La Jolla, California. The couple had dated since 2001.
