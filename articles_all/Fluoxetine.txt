'''Fluoxetine hydrochloride''' is an antidepressant medication. It is used as a drug against various illnesses, like major depressive disorder|depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder, Panic disorder, and other illnesses. Fluoxetine is a one of a group of antidepressants called selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs). It is commonly known as ''Prozac''.

There are newer drugs available to treat these conditions. Nevertheless, it remains very popular. Over 23.1 million prescriptions for fluoxetine were filled in the United States in 2006.
