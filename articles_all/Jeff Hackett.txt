
'''Jeff Hackett''' (born June 1, 1968 in London, Ontario|London, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired ice hockey goaltender who played for the Chicago Blackhawks, New York Islanders, San Jose Sharks, Montreal Canadiens, Boston Bruins and the Philadelphia Flyers of the National Hockey League (NHL).
