'''Chagai''' is the largest Districts of Pakistan|district of Pakistan and is on the northwest corner of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan, Pakistan. It forms a triangular internationalised border with Afghanistan and Iran.

Administration
Chagai is administratively subdivided into the following tehsils:

 Chagai Tehsil
 Dak Tehsil
 Nokundi Tehsil
 Saindak Tehsil
 Taftan Tehsil
