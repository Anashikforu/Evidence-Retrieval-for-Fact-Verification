




'''20''' is a year in the 1st century.  It was a leap year starting on Monday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Marcus Valerius Messalla Barbatus and Cotta.

 Events 
 Tiberias is built on the Sea of Galilee by Herod Antipas.
 Galba is a Roman Empire|Roman praetor.
 Marcus Valerius Messalla Barbatus becomes a Roman consul|consul.
 First year of ''Dihuang'' era of the Chinese Xin Dynasty.
 Philo defines philosophy as the maidservant of theology.

 Deaths 
 Gnaeus Calpurnius Piso, Roman Politician|statesman and governor of Syria (Roman province)|Syria (b. 44 BC) 
 Vipsania Agrippina, wife of Gaius Asinius Gallus and former wife of Tiberius (b. 36 BC)
