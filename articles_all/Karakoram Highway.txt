
The '''Karakoram Highway''' is a road that connects China and Pakistan. It runs through the Karakoram Range|Karakoram range. It is about 1300 km long, and runs from Kashgar in China, to Hasan Abdal Tehsil|Hasan Abdal in Pakistan. Because it runs through the mountains, it is also a popular tourist attraction.
