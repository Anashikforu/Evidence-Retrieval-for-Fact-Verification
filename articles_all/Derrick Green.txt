


'''Derrick Leon Green''' (born January 20, 1971) is an Americans|American singer. He is the lead singer for the Brazilian Heavy metal music|heavy metal band Sepultura. Allmusic said about the singer: "There was a time when the idea of Sepultura recording without Max Cavalera seemed unthinkable. But the incendiary Green turned out to be a fine replacement."
