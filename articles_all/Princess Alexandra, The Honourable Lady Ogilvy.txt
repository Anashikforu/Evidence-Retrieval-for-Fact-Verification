

'''Princess Alexandra, The Honourable Lady Ogilvy''' Order of the Garter|LG Royal Victorian Order|GCVO Canadian Forces Decoration|CD (Alexandra Helen Elizabeth Olga Christabel; born 25 December 1936), is a member of the British Royal Family, the youngest granddaughter of George V of the United Kingdom|King George V and Mary of Teck|Queen Mary. She had two children, Marina and James Ogilvy with Sir Angus Ogilvy. She is 56th in line for the Throne as of June 2021.
