
'''José Mário dos Santos Mourinho Félix''', Order of Infante D. Henrique|OIH, or simply '''José Mourinho''' (born January 26, 1963 in Setúbal) is a Portugal|Portuguese Association football|football manager and former player who is the head coach of Serie A club A.S. Roma|Roma.

Mourinho won four league titles in a row (two at F.C. Porto|Porto and two at Chelsea). He has also won the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA Cup with Porto. For two years in a row (2004 and 2005), Mourinho was named the world's best football coach by the International Federation of Football History and Statistics (IFFHS). After leaving Chelsea F.C., he was replaced by Avram Grant. He went to coach F.C. Internazionale Milano|Internazionale Milano and won the Serie A, Coppa Italia|Italian Super Cup and Champions League in a single season (2009/10).
