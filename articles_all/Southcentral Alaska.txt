'''Southcentral Alaska''' is a part of the U.S. state of Alaska. It is around the  Gulf of Alaska.  Most of the people of the state live in this part of the state.  Anchorage, Alaska|Anchorage, the largest city in Alaska, is here. 40% of the people in Alaska live in or near Anchorage.

The area includes Cook Inlet, the Matanuska-Susitna Valley, the Kenai Peninsula, Prince William Sound, and the Copper River (Alaska)|Copper River Valley.  Tourism, fishing, and making petroleum are the biggest businesses.

Other towns include Palmer, Alaska|Palmer, Wasilla, Alaska|Wasilla, Kenai, Alaska|Kenai/Soldotna, Alaska|Soldotna, Homer, Alaska|Homer, Seward, Alaska|Seward, Valdez, Alaska|Valdez, and Cordova, Alaska|Cordova.
