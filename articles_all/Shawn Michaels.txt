

'''Michael Shawn Hickenbottom''' (born May 31, 1966), better known by the ring name '''Shawn Michaels''', is an Americans|American television presenter, actor, and retired  professional wrestling|professional wrestler, he has been given the nickname of "The Heartbreak Kid" (sometimes referred to as HBK). He used to performs on the WWE Raw|Raw WWE Brand Extension|brand of World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). Michaels retired from wrestling after a loss to The Undertaker. He is a 2011 inductee in to the WWE Hall of Fame.

Michaels is one of the two wrestlers that performed on the very first episode of WWE Raw|''WWF Monday Night RAW'' that is still part of the World Wrestling Entertainment|company, the other being The Undertaker.
