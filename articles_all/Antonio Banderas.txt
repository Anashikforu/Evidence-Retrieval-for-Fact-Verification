
'''José Antonio Domínguez Banderas''' (born 10 August 1960), better known as '''Antonio Banderas''', is an Spanish people|Spanish movie actor, producer, and singer. He has acted in several important Hollywood movies including ''Ballistic: Ecks vs. Sever'', ''Assassins (movie)|Assassins'', ''Interview with the Vampire'', Mexico Trilogy|''Mariachi'' sequels, ''Philadelphia (movie)|Philadelphia'', ''The Mask of Zorro'', ''The Legend of Zorro'', and ''Puss in Boots'' in the Shrek movies.

Banderas and his wife Melanie Griffith said in June 2014 that they are divorcing. The divorce was finalized in 2015.
