
''' Christoph Ahlhaus ''' (born 28 August 1969) is a Germany|German politician. He is a member of the Christian Democratic Union (Germany)|German Christian Democratic Union. From 25 August 2010 to 7 March 2011 he was the First Mayor of Hamburg.

Ahlhaus was born on 28 August 1969. He is married and studied law at the universities of University of Heidelberg|Heidelberg, Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich|Munich and Humboldt University of Berlin|Berlin.

From 7 May 2008 to 24 August 2010 Ahlhaus was Government of Hamburg#Ministries|state minister of the Interior in Hamburg.
