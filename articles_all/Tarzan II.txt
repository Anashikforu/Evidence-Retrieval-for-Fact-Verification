


'''''Tarzan II''''' is a direct-to-video sequel|midquel to the 1999 The Walt Disney Company|Disney animated movie ''Tarzan (1999 movie)|Tarzan'', released on June 14, 2005. The movie tells the story of a young Tarzan's adventure to discover who he really is. It features two new songs from Phil Collins and takes place during the song "Son of Man" from the first movie. It is the last film in the chronology of Walt Disney's version of Tarzan.
