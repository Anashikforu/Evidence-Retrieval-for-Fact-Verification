


'''Ashley Margaret Anne Leggat''' (born September 26, 1986) is a Canadians|Canadian actress who is known for her roles as "Casey MacDonald" in the Canada|Canadian Television program|series ''Life with Derek''. She is close friends with her television co-star Michael Seater. She has four older brothers. She is also a supporter of anti-bullying legislation|anti-bullying along with Michael Seater.
