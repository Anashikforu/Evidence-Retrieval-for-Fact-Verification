
'''Sulfur dioxide''' (also '''sulphur dioxide''') is the chemical compound with the formula SO2.  It is a gas. It smells like burnt matches or Decomposition|rotten Egg (food)|eggs. It can also cause suffocation. Sulfur dioxide is produced by volcanoes and in various industrial processes. It is also used to protect wine from oxygen and bacteria. It can be produced by combustion|burning sulfur. It solution|dissolves in water to produce sulfurous acid. It can be oxidation|oxidized to trioxide, which is dissolved in sulfuric acid to make more sulfuric acid. It is used to make sulfites.
