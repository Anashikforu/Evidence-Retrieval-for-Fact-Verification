

The '''Royal Coat of Arms of the United Kingdom''' is the official coat of arms of the British monarch, currently Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom|Queen Elizabeth II. These arms are used by the Queen in her official capacity as monarch of the United Kingdom, and are officially known as her '''Arms of Dominion'''. Different versions are used by other members of the British Royal Family|Royal Family; and by the British Government in connection with the administration and government of the country. In Scotland, the Queen has a separate version of the Royal Arms, a variant of which is used by the Scotland Office.
