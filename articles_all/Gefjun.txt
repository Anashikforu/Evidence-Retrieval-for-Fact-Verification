'''Gefjun''' (or '''Gefjon''', '''Gefion''') is one of the Æsir in Norse mythology. This name could be from Old Norse ''geð fiá'' meaning "chaste"The article ''
It has also been suggested that she is the origin of Grendel's mother who appears in the epic poem ''Beowulf''.
