

'''Mahmut Celâl Bayar''' (16 May 1883 – 22 August 1986) was a Turkey|Turkish politician, politician|statesman and the List of Presidents of Turkey|third President of Turkey. At the time of his death, he was the longest lived former head of state, living over 103 years (he was surpassed in 2008 by Chau Sen Cocsal Chhum).
