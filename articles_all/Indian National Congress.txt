The party was founded in 1885.IH Qureshi, 'Indian Politics: A History'' 1962 It is the largest constituent party of the United Progressive Alliance|UPA, a coalition of Indian political parties that form the opposition against the ruling NDA coalition. The party's chairperson is Sonia Gandhi. It has registered its worst performance in an election in the 2014 Lok Sabha elections.
