Biography
Matsumoto was born in Saitama, Saitama|Saitama on August 13, 1934. After graduating from Saitama University, he played for his local club Urawa Club (:ja:浦和サッカークラブ|ja).

On December 28, 1958, he debuted for Japan national football team|Japan national team against Malaysia national football team|Malaya.

He later coach (sports)|coached at Urawa Minami High School. The manga ''Akakichi no Eleven'' was based on his team.

He died of heart disease in Saitama, Saitama|Saitama on September 2, 2019, aged 85.
