A '''fence''' is something built like a low wall, often made of metal or wood, to keep something in or to keep something out.  It differs from a wall, which has a Foundation (engineering)|foundation.  Fences are usually supported by fenceposts.  Fences can be large or small.  Many are used in gardens but some are used in different places.
