

'''Aeschlen bei Oberdiessbach''' (also known as '''Aeschlen''') is a former municipality of the district Konolfingen (district)|Konolfingen in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Canton of Bern|Bern in Switzerland. On 1 January 2010 the municipality of Aeschlen bei Oberdiessbach merged into the municipality of Oberdiessbach.
