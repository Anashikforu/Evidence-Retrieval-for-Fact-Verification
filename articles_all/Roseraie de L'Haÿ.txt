'''Roseraie du Val-de-Marne''' is a garden of roses. It was created in 1894 in L'Haÿ-les-Roses, Val-de-Marne, France. It is said to be the first garden created only for roses.

Roseraie du Val-de-Marne has 13,100 rose shrub|bushes of 3200 different kinds of roses. The garden has modern French and wikt:foreign|foreign roses on one side, the formal rose garden with a reflecting pool in the center, and the old garden roses and wikt:classic|classic roses on the other side.
