

The '''Football League Third Division''' was the 3rd division in England|English Association Football|football. Its first season was the 1920–21 Football League Third Division|1920–21. It would be brought back for the 1958–59 Football League Third Division|1958-59 season. It was the 3rd division until the 1991–92 Football League|1991-92 season. It became the 4th division in 1992, after the FA Premier League was created. It was replaced by the EFL League Two|Football League Two in 2004.
