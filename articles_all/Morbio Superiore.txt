
'''Morbio Superiore''' is a former municipality in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland in the hills above Chiasso. It is in the district of Mendrisio (district)|Mendrisio. On 25 October 2009 the former municipalities of Bruzella, Cabbio, Caneggio, Morbio Superiore, Muggio and Sagno merged into the new municipality of Breggia, Switzerland|Breggia.
