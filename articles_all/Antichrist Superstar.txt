

'''''Antichrist Superstar''''' is the second studio album by United States|American rock music|rock band, Marilyn Manson (band)|Marilyn Manson. It was released on October 8, 1996. The producers of the album were Trent Reznor, Dave Ogilvie, Marilyn Manson (musician)|Marilyn Manson and Sean Beavan. There were many protest from religious and civic groups such as the American Family Association because of the band's anti-Christian views.
