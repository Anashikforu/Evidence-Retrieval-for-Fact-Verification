
'''Hoja santa''' (''Piper auritum'') is a herb with a heart-shaped velvety leaf. The name ''hoja santa'' means "sacred leaf" in Spain|Spanish language. It is also known as '''yerba santa''', '''hierba santa''', '''Mexican pepperleaf''', '''root beer plant''', and '''sacred pepper'''.

 Description 

The difficult taste of Hoja santa is not easy to describe. It has been compared to eucalyptus, The flavor is stronger in the young plant stem|stems and veins.
