
'''Thomas Cullum Pyatt''' (born February 14, 1987) is a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey centre (ice hockey)|centre that played for the Ottawa Senators of the National Hockey League (NHL). He also played for the Montreal Canadiens and Tampa Bay Lightning. He has also played for the Montreal Canadiens from 2009 to 2011. He is the son of former NHL player Nelson Pyatt and brother of New York Rangers forward Taylor Pyatt.
