
'''Luise Rainer''' (January 12, 1910 &ndash; December 30, 2014) was a two-time Academy Awards|Academy Award-winning Germans|German-born Austrians|Austrian-Americans|American movie actress. She was the first person to win two Academy Awards in a row. The first was for her role as Anna Held in ''The Great Ziegfeld'', and the second was for her role as a China|Chinese farmer in ''The Good Earth (movie)|The Good Earth''.

Rainer was born in Duesseldorf and was Jewish.

Rainer died from pneumonia in her home in London, England, aged 104.
