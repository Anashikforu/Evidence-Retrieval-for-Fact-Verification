
'''STV''' is a free television channel in Scotland. It is part of the ITV (TV network)|ITV network. It is one of the five main channels in Northern and Central Scotland. It started in 2006, when the old Grampian Television (Northern Scotland) and Scottish Television (Central Scotland) were merged. STV has the channel 3 licence in these areas. In the Scottish Borders, ITV (TV channel)|ITV is shown. STV's director is Gordon MacMillan.
