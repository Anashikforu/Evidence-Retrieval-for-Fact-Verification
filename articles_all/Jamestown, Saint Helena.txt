 
'''Jamestown''' is a harbour and the capital (city)|capital of the United Kingdom|British island of Saint Helena. It was :wikt:found|founded in 1659 by the East India Company. It's also famous for being the place where the famous French emperor, Napoleon lived when he was exiled to St. Helena. The town has a population of about 1,000 people. 

The town's most known feature is Jacob's Ladder. It is a walkway up the mountains with 699 steps. The ladder was built in 1829 to connect Jamestown to the military base on Ladder Hill. The ladder is very popular with Tourism|tourists. It is beautifully lit at night. Each year, people come from all over the earth|world to take part in a timed run up the ladder.

Many buildings in the city are built out of volcano|volcanic Rock (geology)|rock from the local area. The town is has a church, and harbor|port for unloading items transport|delivered to the island.
