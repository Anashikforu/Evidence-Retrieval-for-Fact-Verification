Biography
Omi was born in Tokyo Metropolis on December 26, 1946. After graduating from Hosei University, he joined Kashiwa Reysol|Hitachi in 1970. In 1972, the club won Japan Soccer League and Emperor's Cup. In 1973, the club won the 2nd place at Japan Soccer League and Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1974. He played 49 games and scored 8 goals in the league.

On August 4, 1970, Omi debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Thailand national football team|Thailand. He also played at Football at the 1970 Asian Games|1970 Asian Games. He played 5 games and scored 1 goal for Japan in 1970.
