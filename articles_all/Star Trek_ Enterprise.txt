'''''Enterprise''''' (retitled '''''Star Trek: Enterprise''''' at the start of its third season) is a science fiction television program created by Brannon Braga and Rick Berman.  It is set in the fictional ''Star Trek'' universe created by Gene Roddenberry in the 1960s.  It aired from 2001 to 2005.  The series follows the adventures of humanity's first Warp drive|''Warp'' 5 starship, the Enterprise (NX-01)|''Enterprise'', ten years before the United Federation of Planets shown in previous ''Star Trek'' TV series|series.
