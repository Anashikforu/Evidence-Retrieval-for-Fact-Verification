'''Scott McLeod''' is a United Kingdom|British bassist who was a member of The Ya Ya's and briefly a member of Oasis (band)|Oasis in 1995 when Paul McGuigan (musician)|Paul McGuigan quit the band citing exhaustion after a long time touring.
Weeks later, McLeod contacted Oasis lead guitarist Noel Gallagher claiming he felt he had made the wrong decision. Gallagher replied: "I think you have too. Good luck signing on.".
