'''Wienerwald''' is a chain store|chain of Franchising|franchise restaurants. They are mainly in Germany and Austria. They specialize in chicken-based products. It was founded in 1955. It went Bankruptcy|bankrupt several times. Today the two chains in Austria and Germany are independent of each other.
