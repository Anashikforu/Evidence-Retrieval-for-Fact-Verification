
'''Geff Harrison''' (also known as '''Richie West''', August 24, 1947, Manchester, United Kingdom), is an English people|English singer-songwriter, mostly known for his bands King Ping Meh and Twenty Sixty Six And Then. He created the band Modern Talking along with Dieter Bohlen.

Controversy with Dieter Bohlen 

In January 20, 1986, According to the ruling of the court, Bohlen and Harrison created the group Modern Talking together.
