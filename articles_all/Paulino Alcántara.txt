


'''Paulino Alcántara Riestrá''' (7 October 1896 – 13 February 1964) was a Spanish Filipino Association football|football player and manager who played as a Forward (association football)|forward. He spent most of his playing career at FC Barcelona and represented Catalonia national football team|Catalonia, the Philippines national football team|Philippines and Spain national football team|Spain internationally.
