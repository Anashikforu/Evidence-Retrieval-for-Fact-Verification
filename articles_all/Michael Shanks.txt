

'''Michael Garrett Shanks''' (born December 15, 1970, Vancouver, British Columbia) is a Canadians|Canadian actor, writer and Television director|director. He became famous for his role as Dr. Daniel Jackson on the television series ''Stargate SG-1''. He also provided the voice of the character Thor for this show. He is a film and television actor.
