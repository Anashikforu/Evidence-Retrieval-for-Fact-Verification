
'''Jamshoro District''' () is a Districts of Pakistan|district of Sindh province, Pakistan. Jamshoro is the capital of Jamshoro District.

Administration
The district is administratively subdivided into the following tehsil|talukas:

 Kotri Tehsil
 Manjhand Tehsil
 Sehwan Sharif Tehsil
 Thana Bulla Khan Tehsil
