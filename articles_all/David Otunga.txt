
'''David Daniel Otunga, Sr.''' (born April 7, 1980) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler, lawyer, and actor. He is currently signed to the WWE. He is a two-time WWE Tag Team Championship|WWE Tag Team Champion; winning it once with John Cena and once with Curtis Axel|Michael McGillicutty. He was a member of WWE NXT|NXT season 1 and was a member of the professional wrestling stable ''Nexus (professional wrestling)|The Nexus''.

He is married to actress Jennifer Hudson.

Early life
Otunga is the son of a Kenyan father, Moses, and white American mother, Billie, both of whom were educators.
