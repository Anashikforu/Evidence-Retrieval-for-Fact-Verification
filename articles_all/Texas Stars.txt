The '''Texas Stars''' are an American professional ice hockey team that began playing in 2009. They currently play in the American Hockey League and are the farm team of the Dallas Stars of the National Hockey League|NHL. They play their home games in Cedar Park, Texas at the Cedar Park Center. Their ECHL affiliate team is the Idaho Steelheads and their Central Hockey League|CHL affiliate team is the Allen Americans.
