
'''Washington County''' is a county in the U.S. state of Florida. It was formed on July 21, 1821. As of 2000, 20,973 people lived there. Washington County's seat is Chipley, Florida|Chipley. The county is a dry county which means alcoholic drinks cannot be sold.
