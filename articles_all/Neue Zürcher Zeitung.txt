
The '''Neue Zürcher Zeitung''' (often shortened to '''NZZ''') is a Swiss daily newspaper. It is based in Zürich City|Zürich. It is one of the oldest newspapers that is still published today. It was first published (as ''Zürcher Zeitung'') on January 12, 1780. Politically, the paper holds liberalism|liberal values. Other newspapers which are still published, and have a similar age, are the Wiener Zeitung (published since 1703) and the Hildesheimer Allgemeine Zeitung (published since 1705).
