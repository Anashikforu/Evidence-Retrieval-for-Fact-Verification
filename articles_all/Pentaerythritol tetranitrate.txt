'''Pentaerythritol tetranitrate''' ('''PETN'''), also known as '''PENT''', '''PENTA''', '''TEN''', '''corpent''', '''penthrite''' (or — rarely and primarily in German — as '''nitropenta''') is one of the most powerful explosive material|high explosives known. It has  a relative effectiveness factor (R.E. factor) of 1.66.
