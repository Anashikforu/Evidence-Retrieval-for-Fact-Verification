
'''Ashley Cole''' (born 20 December 1980) is an England|English Association football|football player. He is notable for playing for England 77 times. 
He now plays for Los Angeles Galaxy|LA Galaxy as a defender in the left back position.

Cole married Cheryl Cole|Cheryl Tweedy in July 2006. She divorced him in September 2010.
