

A '''''commune''''' is the lowest administrative division in France. It represents all parts of a town or a village under the same name, for example Paris, Bordeaux or Rennes. As of January 1, 2008, there were 36,781 communes in France; 36,569 of them are in metropolitan France and 212 of them are in overseas France.  This is much more than in any other European Union|European country. Every commune in France Election|elects a mayor.
