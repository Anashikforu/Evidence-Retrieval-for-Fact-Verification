
'''Francisco''' is the closest non-Sphere|spherical moon of Uranus (planet)|Uranus. 

Francisco was found by Matthew J. Holman, et al. and Brett J. Gladman, et al. in 2003 from pictures taken in 2001 and given the designation '''S/2001&nbsp;U&nbsp;3'''.
Confirmed as '''Uranus XXII''', it was named after a lord in William Shakespeare's play ''The Tempest''.
