The '''University of Cincinnati''' is a public university in Cincinnati, Ohio. The university has about 41,357 students. It was founded in 1819. The current president of the university is Gregory H. Williams. 

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Bearcats. They have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the American Athletic Conference.
