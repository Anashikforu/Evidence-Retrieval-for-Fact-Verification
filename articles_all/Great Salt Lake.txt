
The '''Great Salt Lake'''

The lake is about , but because of its desert location, its size changes very often. The three major rivers that flow into it are the Jordan River (Utah)|Jordan, Weber River|Weber, and Bear River (Utah)|Bear rivers. Because of its similarity to the Dead Sea in Asia, it has been called "America's Dead Sea". However, the lake is a rich habitat for many species of birds, shrimp, and other animals.

The lakeshore borders Davis County|Davis, Tooele County|Tooele, Box Elder County|Box Elder, Salt Lake County|Salt Lake, and Weber County|Weber counties, all in the state of Utah.
