
'''Dawn Fraser'''  (born 4 September 1937) is an Australians|Australian champion swimming|swimmer. She is one of only two swimmers to win the same Olympic Games|Olympic event three different times. She was born in Balmain, New South Wales|Balmain, New South Wales.

 Politics 
In 1988, Fraser was Election|elected to the parliament of New South Wales. She served until 1991.
