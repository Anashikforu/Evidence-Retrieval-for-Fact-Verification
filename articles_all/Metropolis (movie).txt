'''''Metropolis''''' (1927) is a West Germany|West German silent movie|silent science fiction movie|science drama movie. It was screenwriter|written and Director (movie)|directed by Fritz Lang. It was made in Germany. It was the most expensive silent movie of the time.Richard Scheib (2003), The movie features special effects and set design. Lang took his inspiration for the sets from Manhattan. The movie opened in Germany in January 1927. It opened in the United States in March 1927. The American print is the only surviving copy.
