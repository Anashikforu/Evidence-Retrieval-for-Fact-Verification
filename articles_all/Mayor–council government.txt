'''The Mayor-Council government system,''' sometimes called the '''Mayor-Commission government system,''' is one of two variations of government most commonly used in modern Representative democracy|representative municipal governments in the United States.

Strong-mayor form
Strong-mayor is a form of mayor-council government. It is made up of an executive (government)|executive branch and a legislature|legislative branch. Both branches are election|elected by the people. The executive brance is usually a city mayor. The legislature is usually a city council.
