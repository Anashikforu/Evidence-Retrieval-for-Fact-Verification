The '''Conservative Party''' was a Brazilian political party of the Empire of Brazil|Imperial period, which was formed circa 1836 and was made extinct with the Proclamation of the Brazilian Republic|Proclamation of the Republic, in 1889. This party arose from the Restoration Party, which was a reunion of the old ''Caramurus'' and the dissident group of moderate liberals. The conservative party was also known as ''regressist'', being a counterpart to the Liberal Party (Empire of Brazil)|Liberal Party, whose leader was Diogo Antônio Feijó|Padre Feijó.
