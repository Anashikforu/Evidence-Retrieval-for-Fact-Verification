

The '''Trolleybus Museum at Sandtoft''' is a transport museum that has old trolleybuses. It is near to the village of Sandtoft, Lincolnshire|Sandtoft, near Belton, Lincolnshire|Belton on the Isle of Axholme in the England|English county of Lincolnshire.
