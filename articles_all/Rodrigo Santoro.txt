
'''Rodrigo Junqueira dos Reis Santoro''' (born August 22, 1975) is a Brazilian Actor. He started his career acting in different soap operas in his country. In the last few years, he has become known after his roles in the movies ''Love Actually'' and ''300 (film)|300''.

Santoro was born in Petrópolis, Rio de Janeiro. He is half of Italian descent.
