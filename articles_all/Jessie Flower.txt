
'''Jessie Flower''' (aka Michaela Jill Murphy, born August 18, 1994 in Indiana) is an Americans|American voice actress. She is most well known as the voice of Toph in the Nickelodeon animated television series ''Avatar: The Last Airbender''. She also voiced the character Meng in the season one episode "Avatar: The Last Airbender (season 1)#Episodes|The Fortuneteller" and Peggy in ''Random! Cartoons'' episode Tiffany. Jessie has been in several movies, including ''Meet the Robinsons'', ''Over the Hedge'', ''The Ant Bully'', ''Finding Nemo'', and ''Brother Bear 2''.
