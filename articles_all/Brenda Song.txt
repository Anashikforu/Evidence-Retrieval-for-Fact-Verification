
'''Brenda Song''' (born March 27, 1988) is an Americans|American actor|actress.  She is also a former child model (person)|model. Song was first famous for her appearances on the Disney Channel. She was a regular part on ''The Suite Life of Zack and Cody''. Her character was noted as one of the Disney Channel's longest continuous characters.

 Biography 
= Early years =
Brenda Song was born in Carmichael, California|Carmichael, California to a Hmong people|Hmong American father and a Thailand|Thai American mother. Brenda Song holds a black belt in Tae Kwon Do and was named an All-American Scholar in ninth grade.

= Career =
Brenda Song began acting and model (person)|modeling at age 6. She also played a very minor role in a skit on MADtv.

One of Song's more notable acting roles is her portrayal of London Tipton in the Disney Channel Original Series ''The Suite Life of Zack and Cody'' (along with Ashley Tisdale and twins Cole Sprouse|Cole and Dylan Sprouse). She later continued this role in the spin-off ''The Suite Life on Deck''. She also played a recurring role as Tia in the Disney Channel Original Series ''Phil of the Future''. Song had minor guest appearances on both ''That's So Raven'' and ''American Dragon: Jake Long''.

Song had her first starring role in 2006 in the movie ''Wendy Wu: Homecoming Warrior''.Albert Gutierrez (November 21, 2006). 
In 2010, Song was part of the main cast of ''The Social Network''.Russ Fischer (October 20, 2009). "
In 2012, Song starred in the short film ''First Kiss (2012 film)|First Kiss'' along with her ''Social Network'' costar Joseph Mazzello.

In 2017, Song was cast in Freeform (TV channel)|Freeform's movie, ''Angry Angel''.

In January 2019, Song was cast in a starring role in the Hulu television series ''Dollface'' alongside Kat Dennings.

= Personal =
She belongs to the Hmong people|Hmong tribe with their religion|sacred and history|historical beliefs. She has received a black belt in one martial arts and had used her skills in the Disney Channel movie, ''Wendy Wu:Homecoming Warrior''.

In 2017, Song began a relationship with actor Macaulay Culkin. They have a daughter together named Dakota, born in 2021.
