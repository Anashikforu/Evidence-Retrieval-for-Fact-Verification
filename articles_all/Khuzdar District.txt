'''Khuzdar''' () Districts of Pakistan|district is in the centre of Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province of Pakistan. Khuzdar is the capital of Khuzdar district.

Administration
The district is administratively subdivided into five tehsils, these are:

 Khuzdar Tehsil
 Karkh Tehsil
 Naal Tehsil
 Wadh Tehsil
 Zehri Tehsil
