
'''Walter Michael Poddubny''' (February 14, 1960 – March 21, 2009) was a Canadians|Canadian professional ice hockey Winger (ice hockey)|left winger and coach who played eleven seasons in the National Hockey League|National Hockey League (NHL) from 1981–82 NHL season|1981–1982 until 1991–92 NHL season|1991–1992.

He died of a heart attack at the age of 49, after collapsing at his sister's house in Thunder Bay.
