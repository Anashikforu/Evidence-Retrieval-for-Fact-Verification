
'''Kelly Michael Gregg''' (born November 1, 1976 in Wichita, Kansas) is an Americans|American American football|football nose tackle for the Baltimore Ravens. Gregg was drafted out of the University of Oklahoma by the Cincinnati Bengals with the 173rd overall pick in the sixth round of the 1999 NFL Draft. Gregg won Super Bowl XXXV while he was part of the Baltimore Ravens practice squad.
