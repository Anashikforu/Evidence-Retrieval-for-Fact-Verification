
'''Green County''' is a county (United States)|county that is found in the U.S. state|state of Wisconsin in the United States.  33,647 people live in Green County as of the 2000 census.  The county seat of Green County is Monroe, Wisconsin|Monroe.

Geography
Green County has an area of 585 square miles, says the United States Census Bureau.
