'''Saipan''' (International Phonetic Alphabet|IPA:  in English) is the largest island and Capital (city)|capital of the United States Northern Mariana Islands|Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands (CNMI), a chain of 15 tropical islands which are part of the Marianas archipelago in the western Pacific Ocean (15°10’51”N, 145°45’21”E) with a total area of 1 E8 m²|115.39 km² (44.55 sq mi). At the United States 2000 census|2000 census, there were more than 62,392 people.
Saipan is at latitude of 15.25° north and longitudeof 145.75° east, about 200&nbsp;km (120&nbsp;mi) north of Guam. Saipan is about 20&nbsp;km (12.5&nbsp;mi) long and 9&nbsp;km (5.5&nbsp;mi) wide. It is a popular tourist destination in the Pacific.
