'''Hakim Abu Mansur Muhammad Ibn Ahmad Daqiqi Balkhi''' (935/942-976/980) (Persian language|Persian: حكیم ابومنصور محمد بن احمد دقیقی بلخی) or '''Daqiqi''' was a Persian poet. He was a Zoroastrian. His slave killed him. He wrote the Goshtaspnama.
