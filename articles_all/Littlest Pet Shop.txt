'''''Littlest Pet Shop''''' are a line of toys sold by Hasbro. They are marketed for girls. The toys were first sold in 1992.

The toys
Each toy is a one inch statue of an animal. Most of these animals are commonly found in pet Shop|stores. For example, dog|puppies, cat|kittens, turtles and hamsters. They also sell other types of animal toys, for example horses, penguins and even skunks. There are many different types of each animal. The company has made more than 300 different versions of the animals. It changes the ones it sells every few months.

A Littlest Pet shop (also LPS) is a little toy. There are cats, dogs, birds, pigs, mice, and also snails. There are also playsets. Here are some playsets. Jungle playplace: comes with a steak, a tiger, playhouse, and a monkey.

Other media
The company has a website that can be used by people who buy the toys. This website lets the user create pets and play games. In 1995, an animation|animated television programme|television series was made based on the toys. In 2012, another show was made that aired on Sprout.

At E3 in July 2008, Electronic Arts said it was making a video game based on ''Littlest Pet Shop''. The game is  for the Nintendo DS, Wii, and the PC. It was released on October 14, 2008.
