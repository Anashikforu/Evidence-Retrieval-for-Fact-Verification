

'''Twenty-one''' is a number. It comes between twenty and twenty-two, and is an odd number. It is divisible by 1 (number)|1, Three|3, Seven|7, and 21.

Twenty-one is also a name of blackjack|a popular card game and is the age at which people in the United States can buy alcoholic drinks.

In Roman numerals, 21 is written as '''XXI'''.
