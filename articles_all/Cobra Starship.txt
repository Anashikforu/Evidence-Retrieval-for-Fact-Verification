
'''Cobra Starship''' was an United States|American alternative music|alternative dance music|dance band. The group started in 2005 in New York City. They are most famous for their songs "Snakes on a Plane (Bring It)" and "Good Girls Go Bad". They are currently signed to record label Fueled by Ramen.

Cobra Starship officially dissolved on November 10, 2015.

Band members

 Ryland Blackinton - guitarist
 Alex Suarez - bassist
