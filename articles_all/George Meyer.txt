
'''George Meyer''' is an Americans|American producer and screenwriter for the television programme|television series ''The Simpsons''. He is arguably the writer who has done the most to the series over the many years it has been made. In 2000, Meyer was called "the funniest man behind the funniest show on television|TV" by ''The New Yorker''.
