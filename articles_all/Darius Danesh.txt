
'''Darius Campbell''' (born 19 August 1980), also known professionally either as '''Darius Danesh''' or simply '''Darius''' is a Scotland|Scottish singer, artist and West End stage actor. His father is Iranian and his mother is Scottish.

He was an auditionee in television music talent show ''Popstars (UK)|Popstars'' in early 2001. He rose to fame on series 1 of ''Pop Idol''. Darius' first Single (music)|single was "Colourblind". It entered the UK singles chart at Chart-topper|number 1.  The album went platinum in 2004.

He was a contestant in series 1 of ''Popstar to Operastar in early 2010.

Campbell was married to Canadians|Canadian actress Natasha Henstridge. Campbell and Henstridge married in a secret ceremony in February 2011.
