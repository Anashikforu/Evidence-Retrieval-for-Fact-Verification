
'''Pecorino Sardo''' is a type of Pecorino cheese. It is made in the Italy|Italian state of Sardinia. It is made from sheep milk, mostly from the Sarda sheep|local breed. Since 1991, it has Protected designation of origin. It is aged for a couple of months, but can also age up to 6 months. It is not as well known outside of Italy as the Romano or Toscano cheeses. It can be used for grating like other cheeses.
