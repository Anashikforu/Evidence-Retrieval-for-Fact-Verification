

'''Ceará''' is one of the 26 States of Brazil|states of Brazil. It is in the northeastern part of the country, on the Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic coast. Ceará has many beaches, which brings a lot of tourism.

 Geography 
Ceará has an area of 148,016 square kilometre|km². It shares borders on the east by the states of Rio Grande do Norte and Paraíba, on the south by Pernambuco state, and on the west by Piauí. Its northern border is the Atlantic Ocean. Its capital is Fortaleza.
