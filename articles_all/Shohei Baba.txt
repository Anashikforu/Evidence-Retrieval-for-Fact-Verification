

'''Shohei Baba''' (''Baba Shōhei'', January 23, 1938 – January 31, 1999) was a Japanese professional wrestler. He was co-founder of All Japan Pro Wrestling. He was also known as '''Giant Baba'''. Baba, along with Antonio Inoki, became one of the most famous Japanese wrestlers of his time, with a popularity in Japan that was like Hulk Hogan's in the United States of America. He was also a List of NWA World Heavyweight Champions|3 time NWA World Heavyweight Championship|NWA Champion. 

Baba died on January 31, 1999 from cancer.
