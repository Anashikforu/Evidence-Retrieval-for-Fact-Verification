

'''Crown Prince Sado''' (), January 1735 - May 1762) was a Crown Prince of Joseon. He was the second son of King Yeongjo of Joseon|Yeongjo, and the father of King Jeongjo of Joseon.

After the death of his half brother Prince Hyojang, he became heir to the throne. Because of his mental illness and strange behaviour, his father had him locked in a rice chest and Starvation|starved to death.
