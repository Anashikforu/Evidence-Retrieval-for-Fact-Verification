
'''Jamar Shipman''' (born April 21, 1985 in Elizabeth, New Jersey|Elizabeth, New Jersey) is an American professional wrestler who currently wrestles for Ring of Honor (ROH) under the ring name, '''Jay Lethal'''. He is probably best known for wrestling for TNA Wrestling from 2005 to 2011.

Championships
Lethal has held many championships during his professional wrestling career:

'''American Championship Entertainment'''
ACE Tag Team Championship (one time) with Mo Sexton

'''American Wrestling Alliance'''
AWA Heavyweight Championship (one time)
AWA Lightweight Championship (one time)
AWA Tag Team Championship (one time) with Rob Vegas

'''Big Time Wrestling'''
BTW Heavyweight Championship (one time)

'''International High Powered Wrestling'''
IHPW Diamond Division Championship (one time)
IHPW United States Heavyweight Championship (one time)

'''Jersey All Pro Wrestling'''
JAPW Heavyweight Championship (two times)
JAPW Light Heavyweight Championship (one time)
JAPW Tag Team Championship (one time) with Azrieal
JAPW Television Championship (one time)
Jersey City Rumble in 2009

'''Jersey Championship Wrestling'''
JCW J-Cup Championship (one time)
JCW Light Heavyweight Championship (one time)
JCW Television Championship (one time)

'''Millennium Wrestling Federation'''
MWF Television Championship (one time)

'''National Wrestling Superstars'''
NWS/WSU King and Queen of the Ring Tournament in 2009 with AJ Lee|Miss April

'''Politically Incorrect Wrestling'''
PIW World Championship (one time)

'''Pro-Wrestling ELITE'''
PWE Interstate Championship (one time)

'''Pro Wrestling Illustrated'''
PWI ranked him #27 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the PWI 500 in 2008

'''Ring of Honor'''
ROH Pure Championship (one time)

'''Total Nonstop Action Wrestling'''
TNA World Tag Team Championship (one time) with Consequences Creed
TNA X Division Championship (six times)
TNA X Cup Tournaments|World X Cup in 2006 with Chris Sabin, Sonjay Dutt and Alex Shelley
X Division Wrestler of the Year in 2007

'''Unreal Championship Wrestling'''
UCW United States Heavyweight Championship (one time)
UCW World Heavyweight Championship (one time)

'''Wrestling Observer Newsletter awards'''
Worst Worked Match of the Year in 2006 (TNA Reverse Battle Royal at TNA Impact!)
