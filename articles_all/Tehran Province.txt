
'''Tehran Province''' (Persian language|Persian: استان تهران;) is one of the provinces of Iran|30 provinces of Iran.  It has an area of 18,909 square kilometers. It can be found to the north of the center plateau in Iran.  as of 2005|As of June 2005, this province contains 13 townships, 43 municipality|municipalities, and 1,358 villages.
