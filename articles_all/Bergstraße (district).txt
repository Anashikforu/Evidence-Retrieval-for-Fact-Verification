
'''Bergstraße''' (German language|German: ''Landkreis Bergstraße'', Hessian dialects|Hessian: ''Londgrääs Bergschdrooß'') is a Rural Districts of Germany|rural district in south Hesse, Germany.

 History 
The district was created in 1938. Two old districts, Bensheim and Heppenheim, joined and became Bergstraße.

 Geography 
The district is in the Odenwald mountains. Melibokus as the highest elevation at 517 meters. The ''Bergstraße Route|Bergstraße'' is a tourist route which from Darmstadt to Wiesloch. The northern part of this route goes through the district and gave it its name.
