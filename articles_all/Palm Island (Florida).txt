'''Palm Island''' is a Artificial island|man-made island in Biscayne Bay in the city of Miami Beach, Florida|Miami Beach, Florida, United States.  Palm Island lies just south of Hibiscus Island.  It is a residential area, the site of exclusive neighborhoods and multi-million dollar homes.  The island is accessible via the MacArthur Causeway. 

 Famous people who lived there 
 Al Capone
 Scott Storch
 Louis L. Gregory
