
'''Sarah Victoria "Torri" Higginson''' (born Burlington, Ontario|Burlington, Ontario, December 6, 1969) is a Canadians|Canadian actor|actress. She is best known for her roles in the ''TekWar'' movies and series, ''The English Patient (movie)|The English Patient'', ''Bliss (TV series)|Bliss'',  and ''Stargate Atlantis''. 

Career
Higginson took over the role of Elizabeth Weir (Stargate)|Dr. Elizabeth Weir from Jessica Steen in a guest spot on the season eight opener of ''Stargate SG-1''. She was then carried over as the leader of the Atlantis (Stargate)|Atlantis expedition on ''Stargate Atlantis'' and played that role until the end of the show's third season. She was reduced from a main cast member to a recurring role in the fourth season. She did not play the role for the show's fifth season.
