
'''General Drastamat Kanayan''' (May 31, 1884 – March 8, 1956) was an Armenian  politician, revolutionary, and General officer|general. He was the commander of the Armenian Legion of the Wehrmacht, the military|armed forces of Nazism|Nazi Germany and part of Armenian national liberation movement.

Drastamat Kanayan was born in Iğdır, Surmalu, Russian Empire (present-day Turkey) in 1884.
