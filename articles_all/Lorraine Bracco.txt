'''Lorraine Bracco''' (born October 2, 1954) is an Americans|American actress. She is best known for playing Karen Hill in the 1990 movie ''Goodfellas'' and Jennifer Melfi in the HBO television series|series ''The Sopranos''. She has also been in other movies, such as ''Medicine Man (movie)|Medicine Man'' and ''The Basketball Diaries''.
