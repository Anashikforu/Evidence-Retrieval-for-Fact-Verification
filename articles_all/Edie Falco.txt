'''Edie Falco''' (born July 5, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York City) is an Americans|American movie, television and stage actress. She plays Carmela in HBO's ''The Sopranos''. She starred in ''Nurse Jackie'' as the title character. She also acted in many movies.
