'''Suwanee''' is a city in the United States|American U.S. state|state of Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia. It is in Gwinnett County, Georgia|Gwinnett County. In 2005, about 12,500 people lived in Suwanee. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 25.6 km² (9.9 square mile|mi²).  25.4&nbsp;km² (9.8&nbsp;mi²) of it is land and 0.2&nbsp;km² (0.1&nbsp;mi²) of it (0.71%) is water.
