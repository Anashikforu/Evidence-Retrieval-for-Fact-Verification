
'''Paul Ivan Thompson''' (born November 2, 1906 in Calgary, Alberta – September 13, 1991) was a Canadians|Canadian ice hockey player. He was a Winger (ice hockey)|left wing. 

Thompson started playing in the National Hockey League (NHL) in 1926–27 NHL season|1926. He played for the New York Rangers and the Chicago Blackhawks|Chicago Black Hawks. He stopped playing in 1938–39 NHL season|1939. 

He was part of the All-Star team two times. He won the Stanley Cup three times. He won in 1928 with New York and in 1934 and 1938 with Chicago.

His brother was Tiny Thompson.
