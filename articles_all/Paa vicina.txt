'''''Paa vicina''''' is a species of frog in the Ranidae family. It is found in the India|Republic of India and Pakistan. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montanes, subtropical or tropical high-altitude grassland, rivers, and freshwater springs. It is threatened by habitat loss.
