
'''Bex''' () is a municipality in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Vaud, Switzerland. It is located in the Aigle (district)|district of Aigle. It has an area of 96.60 Kilometre|km², an altitude of 424 Meter|m, and a population of about 5709. It is a few miles south of its sister town municipality of Aigle.

 Geography 
Bex sits on the east side of the Rhône river in a big valley bordered on the east by the Bernese Alps and west by the Chablais Alps. Famous Swiss and France|French ski resorts are nearby. It is on Swiss National Route 9. Bex is connected to Les Diablerets by the mountain pass Col de la Croix (Vaud)|Col de la Croix
