


'''''Deceiver''''' is the fourth album by United States|American musician Chris Thile, which was released in 2004. The album uses thirty-nine different Musical instrument|instruments, which were all played by Thile alone. Some of the instruments played on the album include mandolin, piano, bass guitar, electric guitar, violin, and different types of percussion instruments. In 2005, the album was nominated a Grammy Award, for "Best Engineered Album, Non-Classical" to ''Deceiver'''s engineers, Thile and Gary Paczosa.
