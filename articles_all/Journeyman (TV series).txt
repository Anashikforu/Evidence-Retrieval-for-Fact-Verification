

'''''Journeyman''''' was an United States|American science-fiction television programme|television series on the NBC television network|network. It started in September 2007.

 Plot 

The series is about a reporter, Dan Vasser, who finds that he can time travel|travel through time with no warning. He can change things in his own past, so he has to be careful not to change too much.
