

'''Opel Agila''' was a car from  the Germany|German carmaker Opel from 2000 to 2015.

 Suzuki Wagon R+ and Splash 
The Suzuki Wagon R+ is a badge engineered version of the Agila. It was replaced by the Suzuki Splash|Splash in 2008 in Europe, however it is still available in countries such as Japan. You may still be able to buy a new Wagon R+ if you are a European, however they sold in small numbers.
