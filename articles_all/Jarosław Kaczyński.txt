

'''Jarosław Aleksander Kaczyński''' (born 18 June 1949) is a Poles|Polish politician. He was born in Warsaw.

As a child, he and his brother Lech Kaczyński starred in the 1962 Polish movie The Two Who Stole The Moon (''O dwóch takich, co ukradli księżyc''), based on a popular Polish children's story by Kornel Makuszyński.
He was Prime Minister of Poland|Prime Minister of Poland from July 2006
He ran for President in Polish presidential election, 2010|2010, but was defeated by Civic Platform candidate Bronisław Komorowski.
In October 2020, he became the Deputy Prime Minister of Poland.
