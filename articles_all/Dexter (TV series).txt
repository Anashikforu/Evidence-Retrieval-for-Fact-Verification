
'''''Dexter''''' is an United States|American television series that was shown on Showtime television network|network. It is based on the book ''Darkly Dreaming Dexter'' written by Jeff Lindsay. The show tells a story about a serial killer, Dexter Morgan, who works as a forensics analyst for the police in Miami. The main actor of the show is Michael C. Hall.
