
'''''Mister Rogers' Neighborhood''''', or just '''''Mister Rogers''''', is an United States|American children's Television program|television series that was created and hosted by Fred Rogers. ''Mister Rogers' Neighborhood'' was produced by Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania|Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA public broadcaster WQED (TV)|WQED  and Rogers' non-profit production company Family Communications, Inc.  (named Small World Enterprises until 1971).  It is the longest running series on PBS. The show is currently seen in reruns on many PBS stations. An animated spinoff called ''Daniel Tiger's Neighborhood'' started airing in 2012.
