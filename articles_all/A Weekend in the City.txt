'''''A Weekend in the City''''' is the second studio album by Bloc Party. It was released on 5 February 2007. The album was producer|produced by Jacknife Lee. The album cover is ''A Project'' by Rut Blees Luxemburg. This album deals more with original day working life than was on ''Silent Alarm''. The album has wikt:theme|themes from the 2005#July|7 July 2005 London bombings, immigration, illegal drugs|drug abuse, and criticism of teenager|youth and sex|casual sex. The album has gotten many high reviews by internet|web-critics and wikt:fan|fans.

The album came in at #56 in the United Kingdom end of year album music chart|chart. It sold about 266,500 copies in 2007. Over 1 million copies of the album have been sold worldwide.
