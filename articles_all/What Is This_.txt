
'''What Is This?''' was a rock music|rock band from Los Angeles, California. The band started in 1977 with two friends from Fairfax High School, Hillel Slovak and Jack Irons, who formed a band called Anthym. Flea (musician)|Flea joined the band as bass player, before leaving to go to college. Flea and another school friend, Anthony Kiedis, asked Slovak and Irons to play with them in a one off group they called Tony Flow and the Miraculously Majestic Masters of Mayhem.2011, Fitzpatrick, Rob, 'Red Hot Chili Peppers: The band that couldn't be stopped', The Guardian, 19 August 2011,
