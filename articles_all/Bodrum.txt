
 
'''Bodrum''' is a tourist resort in Turkey. It is in western Turkey along the Aegean Sea. Thousands of tourists come every year for sun and beach tourism. It has a hot-summer Mediterranean climate (''Csa'' in the Koeppen climate classification).
