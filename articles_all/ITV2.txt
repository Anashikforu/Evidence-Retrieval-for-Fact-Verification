
'''ITV2''' is a free television channel in the United Kingdom owned by ITV Digital Channels Ltd, a part of ITV plc. It started on 7 December 1998.

The channel shows many United States|American programmes such as ''Family Guy'', ''American Dad!'', and ''The Vampire Diaries''. It also has its own programmes that have been popular, such as ''Celebrity Juice'' and ''Love Island (2015 TV series)|Love Island''.
