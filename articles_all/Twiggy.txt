
'''Dame Leslie Lawson''' Order of the British Empire|DBE (born '''Leslie Hornby''' 19 September 1949, in London, England) is an English people|English Actor|actress, fashion model and singer, known professionally as '''Twiggy'''..

As an actress, she starred in ''The Boy Friend'', and had featured roles in ''The Blues Brothers'' and ''Young Charlie Chaplin''. She recently appeared as herself, on ''America's Next Top Model'', as one of the judges.
