
'''''Snakes on a Plane''''' (released in Japan as ''Snake Flight'' (スネーク・フライト)) is a 2006 United States|American action movie|action horror movie|horror crime movie|crime thriller movie. It was movie director|directed by David R. Ellis and it stars Samuel L. Jackson and released to movie theaters by Warner Bros. It is about many snakes on a Fixed-wing aircraft|plane that is traveling from Hawaii to Los Angeles, California.

Plot
The snakes attack people on the plane, and Samuel L. Jackson's fictional character|character, FBI agent Neville Flynn becomes angry at the snakes, so he fights them.

Reception
The movie became very popular before it was released because of the title and because of many people talked about it on the Internet.
