
The '''1928 Summer Olympics''', officially known as the '''Games of the IX Olympiad''', were held at Amsterdam in the Netherlands.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics),   
The games were thought to be a success. Half of the expense of the games was covered by the Dutch government.Zarnowski, C. Frank. 
 Participating nations 
A total of 46 nations were represented at the Amsterdam Games. Malta, Panama, and Southern Rhodesia|Rhodesia (now Zimbabwe) competed at the Olympic Games for the first time. Germany returned after having been banned in 1920 and 1924.
