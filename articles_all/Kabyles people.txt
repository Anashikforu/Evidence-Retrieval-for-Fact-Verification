
The '''Kabyles''' ('') are a Berber people|Berber people. Their traditional homeland is highlands of Kabylie (or Kabylia) in northeastern Algeria.

Their name comes from the name of the mountainous region in the north of Algeria where they live traditionally. Perhabs It means "tribes" (from the Arabic "qaba'il" which is the plural of "qabîlah" قبيلة tribe). They speak the Kabyle language|Kabyle a Berber language|Berber. Kabyles have been very active to fight for the official recognition of the Berber languages|Berber language in Algeria.
