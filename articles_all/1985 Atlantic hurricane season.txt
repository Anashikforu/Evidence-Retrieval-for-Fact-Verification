

The '''1985 Atlantic hurricane season''' officially began on June 1, 1985 and ended on November 30, 1985. However storms can form outside these dates for example, Tropical Depression Thirteen formed on December 7. As far as activity goes this was average. This season featured four powerful hurricanes, they include Hurricane Elena, Hurricane Gloria, Hurricane Juan (1985)|Hurricane Juan and Hurricane Kate (1985)|Hurricane Kate.
