'''High Island''' is an unincorporated area in Galveston County, Texas|Galveston County, Texas. It is in the extreme eastern part of the county on Bolivar Peninsula, less than one mile from Chambers County, Texas|Chambers county and less than two miles from Jefferson County, Texas|Jefferson county. As of 1990, 500 people resided in High Island.

In 2007, Hurricane Humberto (2007)|Hurricane Humberto made landfall near High Island.
