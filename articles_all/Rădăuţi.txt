
'''Rădăuţi''' is a Municipalities of Romania|municipality of Suceava County in Romania.  27,759 people live in Rădăuţi.

 Geography 
Rădăuţi can be found in Bukovina, between the Suceava River|Suceava and Suceviţa River|Suceviţa rivers.  It is 23 miles north of Suceava.
