An '''art exhibition''' is a place where works of art are shown to the public. The word exhibition comes from Latin language|Latin ''exhibere'', which means ''to show''. This can be in a museum or an exhibition hall. The exhibition can be temporary, or permanent (like a section of a museum). Some exhibitions show works of art of the same artist, others show works of art on a common wikt:theme|theme.
