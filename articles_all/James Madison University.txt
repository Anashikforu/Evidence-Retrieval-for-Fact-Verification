'''James Madison University''' is a public university in Harrisonburg, Virginia|Harrisonburg, Virginia. The university has about 18,971 students. It was founded in 1908. The current president of the university is Dr. Linwood H. Rose.

Sports
The athletics teams of the university are called the Dukes. They have 7 varsity teams and they compete in the Colonial Athletic Association.
