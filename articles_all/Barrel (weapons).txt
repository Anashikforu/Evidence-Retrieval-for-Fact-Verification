A '''barrel''' is a part of a gun. It is a long metal tube that the bullet or projectile goes through after it is fired. Guns can have many different sizes of barrels. Usually, longer barrels make it easier for the bullet to hit the bullseye (sports)|target more often. Barrels usually have spiral grooves on the inside called rifling. This makes the projectile more Stability|stable in flight and more accurate.
