
'''Tampere''' () is the third biggest city in Finland. It is the capital (city)|capital of the  Pirkanmaa area.
Neighbour municipalities are Kangasala, Lempäälä, Nokia, Finland|Nokia, Pirkkala, Vesilahti, Hämeenkyrö, Orivesi, Pälkäne and Ylöjärvi. 

The population of Tampere was 215,144 in 2011.  

History
Tampere was started in 1779. At 1800's, Tampere became to be a notable industrialized city and one of the biggest cities in Finland.
