
'''Neu-Ulm''' is a rural district in Swabia (Government Region)|Swabia in southwest Bavaria, Germany. Neighbor districts are: Günzburg (district), Unterallgäu and in Baden-Württemberg:  Biberach (district),  Alb-Donau|Alb-Donau-district and the Kreisfreie stadt|district-free city Ulm. Neu-Ulm is the capital (city)|capital of Neu-Ulm (district).
