The '''Americas''' (or '''America''') are lands in the Western Hemisphere that are also known as the New World. Comprising the continents of North America and South America, along with their associated Lists of islands of the Americas|islands, they cover 8.3% of the Earth's total surface area (28.4% of its land area).

Most people consider the countries in Central America to be part of North America instead of South America.

While the word ''American (word)|American'' is usually used to mean a person or a thing from the United States, the most populated country in the Americas, sometimes people and things from anywhere in the Americas are called "American".
