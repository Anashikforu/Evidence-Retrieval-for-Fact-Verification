'''Guacamole''' (called '''Guacamol''' in Central America and Cuba) is a Mexico|Mexican sauce|dip made of avocados, salt and sometimes tomatoes. Various herbs can be added too, such as chives. It is depicted as a green, creamy dip or garnish.  It is often served as a dip or garnish with Mexican foods such as tacos and tortilla chips.
