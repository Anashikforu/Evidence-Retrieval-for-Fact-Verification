

'''''6teen''''' was an United States|American-Canada|Canadian-France|French Animated series|animated television series produced by Nelvana, Fresh TV and Cartoon Network Studios.  The show was about six sixteen-year-old friends and followed them in their first part-time jobs and teenage lives. The series premiered airs at 4:00&nbsp;p.m. ET/PT times on November 7, 2004 on Cartoon Network in the United States.
