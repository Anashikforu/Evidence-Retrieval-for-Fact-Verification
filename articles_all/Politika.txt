
'''Politika''' (serbian language|sr. '''Политика''') is a daily newspaper that is very popular in Serbia. It is the longest-published newspaper in this country. It is over one hundred years old (established in 1904). This newspaper costs around  30 Dinars in Serbia. Its founder is Vladislav Ribnikar.
