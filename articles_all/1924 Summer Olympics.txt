


The '''1924 Summer Olympics''', officially known as the '''Games of the VIII Olympiad''', were held at Paris in France.Sports Reference.com (SR/Olympics), 
The games were thought to be successful, but costly. The events were well attended.Zarnowski, C. Frank. 
Participating nations
A total of 44 nations sent athletes to compete at the Paris games.Taking part in the games for the first time were Ecuador, Haiti, Republic of Ireland|Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, the Philippines, Poland and Uruguay.




































 



 



Germany was not invited to participate.Guttmann, Allen. (1992). ''The Olympics: A History of the Modern Games,'' p. 38.
