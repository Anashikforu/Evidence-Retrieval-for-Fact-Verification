'''Takht-e-Nasarati''' is an administrative subdivision (tehsil) of Karak District in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. The town of Takht-e-Nasrati is the headquarters of the tehsil.

Administration
The tehsil is administratively divided into 7 Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils - one of which makes up the tehsil capital. These are:

 Ahmad Abad
 Chowkara
 Jehangiri
 Mianki Banda
 Shanawa Godi Khel
 Siraj Khel
 Takht-E-Nasrati
 Shaheedan Banda
