
'''Maroon''' is a dark red color. 

The word ''maroon'' comes from the French language|French word ''marron'' which means "chestnut".

The first recorded use of ''maroon'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1789. Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 198; Colour Sample of Maroon: Page 37 Plate 7 Color Sample L7  

Meaning of maroon
 Vajrayana Buddhism|Buddhist monks wear robes that are colored various shades of maroon.
