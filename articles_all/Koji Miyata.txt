Biography
Miyata was born on January 15, 1923. After graduating from Waseda University, he joined Tanabe Mitsubishi Pharma SC|Tanabe Pharmaceutical in 1946.

In March 1951, Miyata was selected Japan national football team|Japan national team for Japan team first game after World War II, Football at the 1951 Asian Games|1951 Asian Games. At this competition, on March 7, he debuted against Iran national football team|Iran. He also played at Football at the 1954 Asian Games|1954 Asian Games. He played 6 games for Japan until 1954.

After retirement, Miyata became a manager for Tanabe Mitsubishi Pharma SC|Tanabe Pharmaceutical in 1961. The club joined Japanese Regional Leagues in 1968 and was promoted to new division Japan Soccer League Division 2 in 1972. In 1972 season, he led the club the 2nd place and promoted Division 1. However, in 1973 season, the club was the lowest place and he resigned end of 1973 season.
