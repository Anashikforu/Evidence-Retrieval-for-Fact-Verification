

'''Piers Stefan Pughe-Morgan''' (born 30 March 1965 in Guildford, Surrey, England) is a British people|British journalist and television personality best known as a co-presenter on the ITV Breakfast programme Good Morning Britain from 2014 to 2021. Morgan wrote for the ''News of the World'' from 1994 to 1995 and ''Daily Mirror'' from 1995 to 2004.
