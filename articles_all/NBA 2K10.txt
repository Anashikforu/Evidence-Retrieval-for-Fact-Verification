

'''''NBA 2K10''''' is a basketball video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It was released on October 6, 2009 on Xbox 360, PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, and PlayStation Portable|PSP and on October 12, 2009 on Microsoft Windows|PC. It was released on the Wii on November 9, 2009. The game is the first PSP and Wii release of the ''NBA 2K'' series, and the first on a Nintendo system since ''NBA 2K3'' for the Nintendo GameCube|GameCube in 2002.
