The '''Scottish Football Association Challenge Cup''' (usually known as the '''Scottish Cup''') is the national football (soccer)|football cup wikt:competition|competition of Scotland. The trophy is the oldest football national trophy in the world.

 History 
The Scottish Cup started in the 1873-74 season, and 16 teams played to try to win it.
