

'''Schinznach-Dorf''' is a former municipality of the district of Brugg (district)|Brugg in Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Aargau in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2014 the former municipalities of Schinznach-Dorf and Oberflachs merged into the new municipality of Schinznach.
