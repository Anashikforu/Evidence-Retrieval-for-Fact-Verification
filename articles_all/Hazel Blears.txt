'''Hazel Anne Blears''' (born May 14, 1956) is an England|English politician.   and is the Member of Parliament for the Salford and Eccles (UK Parliament constituency)|Salford and Eccles constituency. She was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom general election, 1997|1997 general election as member for the Salford (UK Parliament constituency)|Salford constituency.  

She held several government posts under Prime Minister of the United Kingdom|Prime Ministers Tony Blair and Gordon Brown, including Secretary of State for Communities and Local Government, up until the United Kingdom general election, 2010|2010 general election

She is a member of the Labour Party (UK)|Labour Party.
