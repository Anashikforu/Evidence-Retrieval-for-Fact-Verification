

'''William Anthony "Tony" Parker''' (born ) is a  retired France|French basketball player that played for the San Antonio Spurs of the NBA. He was drafted by them in 2001. 

Parker retired from the France men's national basketball team|French national team after the 2016 Summer Olympics|2016 Rio Olympics.

 Early Life 
Parker was born in Bruges, Belgium, and raised in France. His father, Tony Parker Sr., was also a professional basketball player.
