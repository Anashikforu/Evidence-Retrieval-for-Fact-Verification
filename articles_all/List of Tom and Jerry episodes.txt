
'''''Tom and Jerry''''' is an American animation|animated cartoon series. It is about two characters: a blue cat named Tom and a house mouse named Jerry.

Below is a list of the 164 ''Tom and Jerry'' animated shorts produced and released between 1940 and 2014. Of these, 162 are theatrical shorts, one is a made-for-TV short, and one is a 2-minute sketch shown as part of a telethon.

1975: ''The Tom and Jerry Show'' 48 cartoons
''The Tom & Jerry Show'' having 16 Episodes with 48 segments, each segment with 7-8 Minutes. The following 48 cartoons were produced by Hanna-Barbera Productions in association with MGM Television in 1975 for American Broadcasting Company|ABC based on the theatrical ''Tom and Jerry'' short series. This series marked the first time ever that Tom and Jerry appeared in animated installments produced exclusively for television. The show is currently owned by :en:Warner Bros.
