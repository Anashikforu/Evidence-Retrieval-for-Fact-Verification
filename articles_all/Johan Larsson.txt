

'''Johan Larsson''' (b. 8 December 1974) is a Sweden|Swedish guitarist. He was the first bassist of the Swedish melodic death metal band In Flames. Johan was also the first bassist of the Swedish power metal band HammerFall, but quit before the band recorded a studio album.
