
'''''The Color of Money''''' is a 1986 United States|American sports movie|sports drama movie about pool (cue sports)|pool players. It was released in 1986. The movie stars Paul Newman, Tom Cruise, and Mary Elizabeth Mastrantonio. Martin Scorsese was the director. It is the sequel to ''The Hustler (movie)|The Hustler''. Newman won the Academy Award for Best Actor for his performance, his first Oscar win after eight nominations, seven of them for Best Actor.
