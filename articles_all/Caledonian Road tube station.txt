'''Caledonian Road''' is a station on the Piccadilly Line of the London Underground, between King's Cross St. Pancras tube station|King's Cross St. Pancras and Holloway Road tube station|Holloway Road, and in Travelcard Zone 2. It was opened on 15 December 1906 by the Great Northern, Piccadilly and Brompton Railway.
