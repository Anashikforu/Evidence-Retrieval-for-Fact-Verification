'''Brná nad Labem''' (German language|German: ''Birnai'') is a part of city Ústí nad Labem, Czech republic. It is a small part of landscape park České středohoří on the right side of river Labe, and is close to Ústí nad Labem. Its area is about 4,23&nbsp;km².Ministry of Interior of the Czech Republic.
