
'''Prestonsburg''' is a city in the United States. It is the county seat of Floyd County, Kentucky|Floyd County in the U.S. state|state of Kentucky. The city is in the Eastern part of Kentucky. Prestonburg is on the river bank|bank of the Big Sandy River (Ohio River)|Big Sandy River. The area was fist settlement|settled in 1797 by Colonel John Preston, Solomon Stratton, Matthias Harman, and Andrew Hood. It is named after Colonel Preston. In 2010 United States Census|2010,  3,255 people lived in the city.
