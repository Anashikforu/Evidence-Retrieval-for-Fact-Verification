'''Span''' is the width of a human hand, from the tip of the thumb to the tip of the pinky finger. 
; 1 span := 9 inches
:= 0.2286 metre|m

In Slavic languages|Slavic language, the analogue of span is '''pyad''' (''peti, пядь''). It is the width from the tip of the thumb to the tip of the forefinger. 
; 1 pyad := 4 vershok
:= 7 inches
:= 0.1778 metre|m
