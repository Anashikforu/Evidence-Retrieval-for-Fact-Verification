Biography
Yoshimizu was born on August 21, 1946. After graduating from Keio University, he joined Japan Soccer League club JEF United Chiba|Furukawa Electric in 1969. He retired in 1972. He played 53 games and scored 6 goals in the league.

On July 31, 1970, Yoshimizu debuted for the Japan national football team|Japan national team against Hong Kong national football team|Hong Kong. He played 4 games and scored 1 goal for Japan in 1970.
