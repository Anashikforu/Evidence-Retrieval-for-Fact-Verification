'''Guerrero''' is a one of the 38 municipalities of Coahuila, in north-eastern Mexico. The municipal seat lies at Guerrero, Coahuila|Guerrero. The municipality covers an area of 3,219.7&nbsp;km² and is located on the U.S.-Mexico border|international border between Mexico and the USA, here formed by the Río Bravo del Norte (Rio Grande), adjacent to the U.S. state of Texas.
