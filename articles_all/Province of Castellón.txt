'''Province of Castellón''' () or '''Castelló''' (Valencian language|Valencian/Catalan language|Catalan) is a provinces of Spain|province in the northern part of the Valencia (autonomous community)|Valencian Community, Spain. It is bordered by the provinces of Valencia Province|Valencia, Teruel Province|Teruel, Tarragona Province|Tarragona, and the Mediterranean Sea.
