Year '''911''' ('''Roman numerals|CMXI''') was a common year starting on Tuesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar.

 Events 

= By place =
Africa
 Rebellion of the Kutama Berbers against the Fatimid Caliphate. The Kutama tribesmen have previously been the main supporters of the new Shi'ite regime.

 Europe 
 July 11 &ndash; The Treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte is signed between Charles the Simple, King of France and Rollo, leader of the Vikings, allowing the latter to settle in Neustria, the origin of the Normans.
 September &ndash; Following the death of Louis the Child, last Carolingian dynasty|Carolingian ruler of the East Franks, Charles the Simple of France, is elected List of rulers of Lorraine#Kings of Lotharingia|King of Lotharingia.
 The Fatimids begin the conquest of Sicily over their Aghlabid arch-rivals.

= By topic =
 Religion 
 April &ndash; Pope Anastasius III succeeds Pope Sergius III as the 120th pope.

 Deaths 
 April 14 &ndash; Pope Sergius III, pope of the Roman Catholic Church
