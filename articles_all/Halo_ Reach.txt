 

'''''Halo: Reach''''' is a first-person shooter video game exclusively for the Xbox 360. It is the prequel to the Halo (series)|''Halo'' series, and it was released on September 14, 2010.

 Gameplay 
= Firefight =
Firefight returned from Halo 3: ODST with the ability to play as Noble Six for all the maps with an equip-able voice.
