
'''Gloucestershire''' (pronounced ; ''GLOSS-ter-sher'') is a county in South West England. The county includes part of the Cotswolds|Cotswold Hills, part of the flat fertile valley of the River Severn, and the entire Forest of Dean.

The county town is Gloucester, and other principal towns include Cheltenham, Stroud, England|Stroud, Cirencester, and Tewkesbury.

Gloucestershire borders the Preserved counties of Wales|county of Gwent (county)|Gwent in Wales, and in England the counties of Herefordshire, Worcestershire, Warwickshire, Oxfordshire, Wiltshire, Somerset and Bristol.
