
'''Bay Saint Louis''' is a city in Hancock County, Mississippi|Hancock County, Mississippi. It is part of the Gulfport-Biloxi metropolitan area|Gulfport-Biloxi, Mississippi Metropolitan Statistical Area. As of the 2000 census, 8,209 people lived there. It is the county seat of Hancock County, Mississippi|Hancock County. Like several other upper Gulf Coast cities, Bay Saint Louis was devastated by Hurricane Katrina in August 2005.
