
The '''Hebrew Wikipedia''' () is the Hebrew language|Hebrew-language edition of Wikipedia. This edition was started in July 2003.

As of October 2015, it is the 39th largest edition of Wikipedia by number of articles.meta:List of Wikipedias|Wikimedia list of Wikipedias and their statistics.. Retrieved 25 October 2015.

By February 2012, it had over 130,000 articles, over 534,000 pages and over 33,500 files uploaded.:he:מיוחד:סטטיסטיקות
