'''Jamaica''' is a :wikt:neighborhood|neighborhood in the borough (New York City)|borough of Queens in New York City, New York (state)|New York, United States. It is the location of the county seat of Queens. Also located there is Jamaica Station. With over 200,000 daily passengers, the station is a rail hub for New York City Subway, the Long Island Rail Road, and the Airtrain to John F. Kennedy International Airport|JFK airport.
