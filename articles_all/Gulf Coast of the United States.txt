The '''Gulf Coast''' region of the United States is made up of the coasts of U.S. state|states which border the Gulf of Mexico. The states of Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Alabama, and Florida are known as the '''Gulf States'''. All Gulf States are in the Southern United States|Southern region of the United States.
