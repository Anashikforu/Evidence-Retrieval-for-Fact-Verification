
'''''Flipper (1995)''''', is an United States|American television series, also known as ''The New Adventures of Flipper''.  It is a remake of the Flipper (1964 TV series)|1964 ''Flipper'' television series.  The first two seasons appeared first in television syndication|syndication; seasons 3 and 4 appeared on the ION Television|PAX network.

The 1995 version brought back Bud Ricks as a scientist doing ocean|marine research in Florida.  The dolphin Flipper was one with whom Dr. Ricks was working.  Jessica Alba was in the show.
