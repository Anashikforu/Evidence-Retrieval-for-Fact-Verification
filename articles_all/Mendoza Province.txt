'''Mendoza''' is a Province of Argentina. It is in the region called Cuyo. The province borders San Juan Province, Argentina|San Juan in the north, San Luis Province|San Luis and La Pampa in the east, Neuquén Province|Neuquén in the south, and Chile to the west.

It is the major wine producer of Argentina and has many other crops. It has the highest mountain in the country, Aconcagua.

Mendoza has a population of 1,579,651 people (2001). Spanish language|Spanish is the main language but many people also speak English language|English, Italian language|Italian and Portuguese language|Portuguese.
