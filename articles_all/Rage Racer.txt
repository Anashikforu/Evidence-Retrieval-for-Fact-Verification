Gameplay
=Grand Prix=
In the Grand Prix mode, the player starts at Class 1. They choose a course and then a car. As the player wins races, they win prize money, or "credits", which are called '''e.g.''' in the game.
=Time Trial=
Time Trial is a mode where the player races the clock. They choose a car and course, and then are shown the record for the track. Tracks are also divided into "'''''sections'''''", parts of a track.Time Trial Mode: "not really a leisure" 
A player can save or load data with a memory card.
