



'''32''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a leap year starting on Tuesday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Ahenobarbus and Camillus'''.

 Events 
 Saint Peter traditionally becomes first pope
 Symbolic interpretation of the Old Testament by Philo

 Births 
 Ban Chao, Chinese general and cavalry commander (d. 102)
Otho, Roman emperor (d. 69)

 Deaths
