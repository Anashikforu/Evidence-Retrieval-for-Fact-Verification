Biography
Muraoka was born in Bunkyo, Tokyo|Bunkyo, Tokyo Metropolis|Tokyo on September 19, 1931. After graduating from University of Tsukuba|Tokyo University of Education, he played for Kyodai Club which was consisted of his alma mater Tokyo University of Education players and graduates. He retired in 1954.

In March 1954, when Muraoka was a University of Tsukuba|Tokyo University of Education student, he was selected in the Japan national football team|Japan national team for 1954 FIFA World Cup|1954 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on March 7, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea. He also played at Football at the 1954 Asian Games|1954 Asian Games. He retired from playing career after this tournament. He played 2 games for Japan in 1954.

After retirement, Muraoka became a journalist for Kyodo News and worked until 1991.

On March 13, 2017, Muraoka died of heart failure in Kodaira, Tokyo|Kodaira at the age of 85.
