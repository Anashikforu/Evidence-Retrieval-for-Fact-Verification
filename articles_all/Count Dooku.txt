
'''Count Dooku of Serenno''' (also known as '''Darth Tyranus''') is a fictional character|character from the ''Star Wars'' fictional universe|universe. Dooku is one of the major characters in ''Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones|Episode II: Attack of the Clones''. He was played by late Christopher Lee. 

He is the second known student of Palpatine|Darth Sidious. Dooku is also the leader of the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars.
