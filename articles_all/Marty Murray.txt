
'''Marty Murray''' (born February 16, 1975) is a former Canadian professional ice hockey Centre (ice hockey)|centre who played eight seasons in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Calgary Flames, Philadelphia Flyers, Carolina Hurricanes and Los Angeles Kings. 

Awards
Canadian Major Junior Second All-Star Team (1994)
WHL East First All-Star Team (1994, 1995)
Best Forward - World Junior Championships (1995)
World Junior Championships First All-Star Team (1995)
AHL Canadian All-Star team (1996)
AHL All-Star Rookie of the Game (1996)
