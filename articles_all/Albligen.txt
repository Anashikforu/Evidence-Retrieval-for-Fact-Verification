
'''Albligen''' (also known in French language|French as '''Albenon''') was a municipality of the administrative district Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Canton of Bern|Bern in Switzerland. On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Albligen and Wahlern merged into the new municipality of Schwarzenburg.
