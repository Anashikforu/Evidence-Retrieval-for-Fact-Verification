'''"Quid pro quo"''' is a List of Latin phrases (Q)|Latin phrase. It means roughly "this for that." In other words, the phrase describes the wikt:context|context when something is given in return for something else.Robertson, John G. (1991). 
The phrase means the same thing as the English language|English saying "a tit for a tat", or trading something for something else. It can also mean the same thing as "an eye for an eye".

Lawyers sometimes use this saying to mean "an even trade".

Quid pro quo means wikt:reciprocity|reciprocity
