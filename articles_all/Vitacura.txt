

'''Vitacura''' is a commune and city of Santiago Province, Chile. According to the Chilean census on 2002, Vitacura has an area of  and 81,499 inhabitants.

 History 
The name of this city is in honor to the cacique called ''Vitacura'' who was collaborator of the Spain|Spanish Conquistador|conqueror Pedro de Valdivia.
