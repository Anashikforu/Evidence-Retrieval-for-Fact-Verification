
'''Renton''' is a city in King County, Washington|King County, Washington, United States.  It is 13 miles (21&nbsp;km) southeast of Seattle, Washington. Renton is on the southeast shore of Lake Washington. At the 2000 census, 50,052 people lived there. As of April 1, 2008, there were about 78,780 people.
Based on Washington locations by per capita income|per capita income, Renton ranks 100th of 522 areas ranked in the state of Washington.
