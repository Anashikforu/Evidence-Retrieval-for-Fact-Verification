
'''Ralph Fiennes''' (born 22 December 1962 in Ipswich, Suffolk, England) is an English people|English actor. He won the Tony Award and was nominated for the Academy Awards|Academy Award for his work in Steven Spielberg's movie ''Schindler's List'', and the Genie Award. His parents were Jennifer Lash and Mark Fiennes. His brothers and sisters are Joseph Fiennes, Sophie Fiennes, Martha Fiennes, Magnus Fiennes and Jacob Fiennes.
