The '''British Rail Class 21''', originally known as the North British List of British Rail power classifications|Type 2 diesel-electric, was a type of  diesel locomotive built by the North British Locomotive Company in Glasgow for British Railways in 1958-1960.

Starting in 2007, the classification has been reused for new Vossloh G1206 acquired by Euro Cargo Rail/EWS.
