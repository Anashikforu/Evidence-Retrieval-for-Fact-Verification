Biography

In March 1951, when Kato was 31 years old, he was selected the Japan national football team|Japan national team for Japan team first game after World War II, Football at the 1951 Asian Games|1951 Asian Games. At this competition, on March 9, he debuted against Afghanistan national football team|Afghanistan.
