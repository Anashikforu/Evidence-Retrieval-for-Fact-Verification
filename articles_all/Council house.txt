The '''council house''' is a form of public housing found in Ireland and the United Kingdom, sometimes called '''social housing''' in modern times. Council houses were built and operated by local municipality|councils to help the local population. As of 2005, approximately 20% of the country's housing is owned by local councils or by housing associations.
