'''Mount Gongga''' is a  mountain in the Daxue Shan Mountain range|range, in Sichuan China. It is also known as '''Gongga Shan''' and '''Minya Konka'''.

Mount Gongga is thought of as one of the hardest and most dangerous mountains to climb.
