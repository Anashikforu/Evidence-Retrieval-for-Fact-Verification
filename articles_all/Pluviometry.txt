'''Pluviometry''' is a field of meteorology concerned with the measurement of rainfall. The word comes from the Latin word ''pluvius'' which means ''rain'', and the Greek language|Greek word ''metron (μέτρον)'', meaning ''measure''. The amount of rain can be measured in a rain gauge, or pluviometer.
