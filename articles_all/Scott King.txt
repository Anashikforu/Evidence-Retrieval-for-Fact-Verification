

'''Scott Glenndale Martin King''' (born June 25, 1967 in Thunder Bay, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender. He played in only two National Hockey League games for the Detroit Red Wings. He was drafted 10th (190th overall) in the 1986 NHL Entry Draft.

Named to '''Hockey East Second All-Star Team''': 1989
