'''WWE Unforgiven''' is a professional wrestling pay-per-view. It now happens every year in September. The event was put on by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). The event was WWE Brand Extension|exclusive to the WWE Raw|Raw brand from 2003 to 2006. The first event was an WWF In Your House|In Your House pay-per-view event. It took place on April 26, 1998. It featured the first Inferno match. The combatants were The Undertaker and his brother (kayfabe) Glen Jacobs|Kane. The event was succeeded by WWE Breaking Point.
