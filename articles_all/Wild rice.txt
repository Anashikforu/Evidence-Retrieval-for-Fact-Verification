

'''Wild rice''' (also called '''Canada rice''', '''Indian rice''', and '''water oats''') is four species of Poaceae|grasses forming the genus '''''Zizania''''', and the grain which can be harvested from them. The grain was history|historically gathered and eaten in both North America and China. While it is now something of a delicacy in North America, the grain is no longer eaten in China, where the plant's Plant stem|stem is used as a vegetable.
