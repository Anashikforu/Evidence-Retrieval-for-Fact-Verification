
'''Taryn Nicole Terrell''' (born December 20, 1984) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler, Referee (professional wrestling)|referee, actress, and Stuntman|stuntwoman. She is currently signed to Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). She is best known for her time with the WWE, where she competed under the ring name '''Tiffany'''.

On August 13, 2010, it was reported that she had been suspended by the WWE after she was involved in an incident which involved her real-life husband, Drew McIntyre.
