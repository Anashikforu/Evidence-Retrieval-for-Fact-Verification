
'''Glauchau''' (Thurginian-Upper Saxon dialect: ''Glauche'') is a town in Saxony, Germany. It is near the river Mulde, 7 miles north of Zwickau and 17 miles west of Chemnitz by Rail transport|rail. It is part of the Zwickau Rural District|Zwickau district.
