

'''''Caligula''''' is a 1979 Italy|Italian-United States|American history|historical movie. It was movie director|directed by Tinto Brass. It is about the life of Roman Emperor Caligula. It stars Malcolm McDowell as Caligula.

Cast
 Malcolm McDowell as Caligula
 Teresa Ann Savoy as Drusilla (sister of Caligula)|Drusilla
 Guido Mannari as Naevius Sutorius Macro|Macro
 John Gielgud as Nerva (son of Marcus Cocceius Nerva (consul 36 BC))
 Peter O'Toole as Tiberius
 Giancarlo Badessi as Claudius
 Bruno Brive as Tiberius Gemellus
 Paolo Bonacelli as Cassius Chaerea
 John Steiner as Longinus
 Helen Mirren as Caesonia
 Mirella D'Angelo as Livia
