
'''Kimberley Gail "Kym" Marsh'''  (formerly '''Ryder''', and '''Lomas''' born 13 June 1976
Marsh rose to fame when she and four other contestants won reality TV show ''Popstars'' and formed manufactured band|manufactured pop group|pop quintet Hear'Say. She is presently most recognisable from her role as Michelle Connor on the long-running ITV1 soap opera ''Coronation Street'' which she has played since 2006. Marsh was one of the contenders to represent the UK in the 2006 Eurovision Song Contest; she was third place and as such did not take part in the ESC.

Personal life
Marsh has two children, David Ryan (born April 1995) and Emily May (born November 1997). They are from a intimate relationship|relationship with Dave Cunliffe.
Marsh married former ''EastEnders'' actor Jack Ryder (actor)|Jack Ryder in St Albans, Hertfordshire, on 10 August 2002. On 20 March 2008 the couple said they would separate and may divorce. 

Marsh began dating ''Hollyoaks'' actor Jamie Lomas since July 2008.
Marsh and Lomas said Kym was three months pregnant on 26 October 2010.
