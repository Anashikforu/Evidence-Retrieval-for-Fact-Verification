




'''16''' is a year in the 1st century.  It was a leap year starting on Wednesday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul|Consulship of Taurus (astrology)|Taurus and Libo'''

 Events 
A Roman army of 90,000 men commanded by Germanicus gains a victory at Battle of the Weser River|Idistaviso.  They defeat the German war chief Arminius and capturing his wife Thusnelda.
Noricum|Noricans are defeated by Publius Silius, proconsul of Illyricum.
Ovid's "Epistulae ex Ponto" appears.

 Births 
September 16 – Drusilla (sister of Caligula)|Drusilla, daughter of Germanicus and Agrippina the Elder (d. 38)
September 16 – Samual Yare, druid of Brittany, assistant to the Roman Emperor 100-126
Agrippina the Younger, Roman Emperor|Roman Empress

 Deaths 
Possibly Scribonia, Second wife of Caesar Augustus and mother of Julia the Elder
