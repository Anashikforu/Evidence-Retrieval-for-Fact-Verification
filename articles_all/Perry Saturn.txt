
'''Perry Arthur Satullo''' (born October 25, 1966) is an Americans|American Professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is best known for wrestling with WWE|World Wrestling Federation (WWF) and Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) under the ring name, '''Perry Saturn'''.

In an interview, Saturn revealed that he had been shot with a .25 caliber handgun in the back of the neck while he was trying to save a woman who was being raped by two men. Many friends and fans believed that he was dead until he made a surprise appearance at a WWE event.
