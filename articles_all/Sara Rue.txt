
'''Sara Rue''' (born January 26, 1979 in New York City, New York) is an United States|American actress.  She played the roles of "Carmen Ferrara" on the drama ''Popular (TV series)|Popular'' and "Claudia Casey" on the sitcom ''Less Than Perfect''.
