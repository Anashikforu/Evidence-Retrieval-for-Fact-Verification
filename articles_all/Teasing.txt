'''Teasing''' is a word with different meanings. With humans, teasing usually means either ''playful'' and ''hurtful''. Teasing is often unkind, and is sometimes a type of harassment. It can have a Sexual intercourse|sexual contest as well, as in the sense of cause sexual arousal in someone, for example.
