
'''Rapides Parish''' () is a List of parishes in Louisiana|parish located in the U.S. state of Louisiana. As of the 2010 United States Census|2010 census, the population was 131,613. ''Rapides'' is the French spelling of "rapids" and is pronounced ra-PEEDS (I.P.A. ɹæ.pid͜z). 

The parish was created in 1807 after the United States bought this territory in the Louisiana Purchase.
