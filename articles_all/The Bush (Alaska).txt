'''The Bush''' is a term for the portions of Alaska that are not connected to North America's road network.

Places in the Bush include Bethel, Alaska|Bethel, Dillingham, King Salmon, Alaska|King Salmon, Nome, Alaska|Nome, Barrow, Alaska|Barrow, Katmai National Park, Kodiak Island, Kotzebue, and Unalaska, Alaska|Unalaska-Dutch Harbor.

Most parts of Alaska that are off the road system can only be reached by small airplane. Travel from place to place is usually by  snowmobile or snow machine, boat, or sometimes dog sled.
