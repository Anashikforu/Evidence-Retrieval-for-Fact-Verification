
'''Braşov''' ((); Hungarian language|Hungarian: ''Brassó'') is a county (judeţ) of Romania, in Transylvania.  The capital of Braşov County is Braşov.

 Geography 
Braşov County has an area of 5,363 km².

 Divisions 
Braşov County has 4 municipalities, 6 towns and 47 communes.
