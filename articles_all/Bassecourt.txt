

'''Bassecourt''' is a former municipality of the district of Delémont (district)|Delémont in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Jura (canton)|Jura in Switzerland. On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Bassecourt, Courfaivre, Glovelier, Soulce and Undervelier merged to form the new municipality of Haute-Sorne.
