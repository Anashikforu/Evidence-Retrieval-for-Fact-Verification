
'''Ivana Trump ''' (born '''Ivana Marie Zelníčková'''; (); February 20, 1949) is a Czech Republic|Czech businesswoman, former Olympic skiing|skiier and model (person)|model. She is best known for her marriage to Donald Trump, which ended in divorce. They had three children together, Donald Trump Jr.|Donald Trump Jr, Ivanka Trump, and Eric Trump.

In 2010, she was a contestant in the British version of ''Celebrity Big Brother (UK)|Celebrity Big Brother''.
