A '''cricket ball''' is a hard, solid ball used to play cricket. In men's cricket the ball must weigh between 5.5 and 5.75 ounces (137.5 and 143.8 g) and measure between  and 9 inches (220 and 225&nbsp;millimetres) in circumference.

A cricket ball is usually red, white or pink depending upon the game and has a seam over it. 

Red balls are used in day test matches
Pink balls are used in day/night test matches 
White balls are used in ODI and T-20s.
