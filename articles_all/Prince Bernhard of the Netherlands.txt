
'''Prince Bernhard of the Netherlands''' (29 June 1911 &ndash; 1 December 2004) was the prince consort of Queen Juliana of the Netherlands. He was the father of Beatrix of the Netherlands. He was born in Jena, Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach, German Empire. 

He died of lung cancer in Utrecht (city)|Utrecht, Netherlands at the age of 93.
