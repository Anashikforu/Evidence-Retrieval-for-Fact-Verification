


'''Andray Blatche''' (born August 22, 1986 in Syracuse, New York) is an Philippines|Filipino-United States|American basketball player that currently plays for the Washington Wizards of the National Basketball Association|NBA. On September 25, 2005, Blatche was shot in a carjacking in Alexandria, Virginia and recovered successfully. Blatche has played for the Washington Wizards for 5 seasons. Blatche became a Naturalized citizen|naturalized Filipino in 2014. He plays for the Philippine national basketball team.
