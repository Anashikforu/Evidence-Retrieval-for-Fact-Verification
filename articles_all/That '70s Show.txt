

'''''That '70s Show''''' is an United States|American television programme|television series. It is about the lives of a group of teenagers living in Point Place, Wisconsin from May 17, 1976 to December 31, 1979. The series first started August 23, 1998 and its final episode aired May 18, 2006.

The series continues to run in syndicated|syndication on FX (TV channel)|FX and The CW|the CW network in the United States and United Kingdom, CH (television system)|CH in Canada, as well as the Seven Network in Australia and Star World in Asia, including Malaysia.
