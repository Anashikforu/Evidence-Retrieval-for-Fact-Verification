The '''Billy Graham Evangelistic Association''' ('''BGEA''') is an organization founded by Rev. Billy Graham in 1950. The purpose of the ''BGEA'' is to tell the gospel of Jesus Christ to as many people as they can. They have done this through crusades, evangelism training, youth outreach, television broadcasts, and radio Radio programming|programs, just to name a few. The current President of BGEA is Graham's son Franklin Graham|Franklin.
