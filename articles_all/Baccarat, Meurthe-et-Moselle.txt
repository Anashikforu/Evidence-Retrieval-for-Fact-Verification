


'''Baccarat''' is a Communes of France|town in the Meurthe-et-Moselle Departments of France|area in northeastern France.

Crystal
Image:St-Remy Baccarat.jpg|thumb|left|260px|St. Remy church
In 1764, King Louis XV of France|Louis the fifteenth allowed to the Bishop of Metz to start a glass factory at Baccarat. The Baccarat (company)|Baccarat crystalworks are famous for their glass and crystal artwork, and the Musée du Cristal, owned by the company, is Lorraine's biggest private museum.

Sights
Image:Baccarat mairie.jpg|thumb|left|260px|Hotel de ville de Baccarat
The Church of Saint Rémy is well known for its artistic glass windows.
