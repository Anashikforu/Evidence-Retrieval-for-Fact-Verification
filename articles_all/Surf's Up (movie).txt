
'''''Surf's Up''''' is a 2007 United States|American computer-animation|animated movie made by Sony Pictures Animation. It stars Shia LaBeouf, Jeff Bridges, Zooey Deschanel, Jon Heder, Mario Cantone, James Woods, Rebecca Honig, Diedrich Bader, Rob Machado, Kelly Slater and Sal Masekela. The movie is a cartoon of surfing documentary|documentaries, like ''The Endless Summer'' and ''Riding Giants''. Composed by Randy Newman.
