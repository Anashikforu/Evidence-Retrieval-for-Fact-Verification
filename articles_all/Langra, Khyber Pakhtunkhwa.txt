
'''Langra''' is one of the 51 Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils of Abbottabad district in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan.

 Location 
Langra is at 34°4'0N 73°10'0E at a height of 871 metres (2860&nbsp;feet).
 Subdivisions 
The Union Council is subdivided into the following areas: Banda Sahib Khan, Lari and Langra.
