
'''DC Talk''' (also written as '''dc Talk''') is a well-known Christian Rock and roll|rock/rap band, most famous for its song ''Jesus Freak''. The band started in 1988, and was part of the rise in popularity of the contemporary Christian music genre during the 1990s. The band split up in 2000 to have solo careers.
