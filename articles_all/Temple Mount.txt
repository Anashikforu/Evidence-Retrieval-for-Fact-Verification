

The '''Temple Mount''' is a religious site in the Old City of Jerusalem|Old City of Jerusalem. It is a holy place in Judaism and Islam.

In Judaism it is the location of the two Jewish Temple|Jewish Temples, and is believed to be the place where Adam was born, where Adam built an altar for God, where Cain and Abel offer their sacrifices, and where Abraham offered Isaac as a sacrifice.).

In Islam, the Temple Mount is believed to be the place where Muhammad ascended to Heaven. It Arabic language|Arabic it is known as the '''Haram''' (, ''al-Ḥaram al-Šarīf''), which means "Noble Sanctuary".

It is also associated with Biblical prophets who are venerated in Judaism, Christianity and Islam.
