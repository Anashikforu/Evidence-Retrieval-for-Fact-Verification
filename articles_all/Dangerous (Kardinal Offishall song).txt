'''Dangerous''' is a 2008 Rhythm and blues|R&B song by Kardinal Offishall and featured R&B singer Akon. The song was released March 2008 on the radio and April 1, 2008 on iTunes. The song is from Kardinal Offishall's 2008 album, Not 4 Sale (Kardinal Offishall album)|Not 4 Sale. 

The song won Single of the Year at the 2009 Juno Awards and was certified 3x Platinum by the Canadian Recording Industry Association|CRIA.
