

'''Włocławek''' is a town in northern Poland in Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship with a population of 117,785 (June 2009).  It has a large Jewish community.  In 1939, when the Nazis invaded, the Jewish people of this town were the first to have to wear Yellow badge|yellow Stars of David.

Twin Areas
This town is twinned with the following places:
: Bamberg, Germany;
: Bedford, United Kingdom
