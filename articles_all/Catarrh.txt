'''Catarrh''' () is a thick fluid of mucus and Granulocyte|white blood cells caused by the swelling of the mucous membranes in the head because of an infection. It is a symptom usually from the common cold and coughs, but can also be found in people with infections of the adenoids, otitis media|middle ear, sinusitis|sinus or tonsilitis|tonsils.
