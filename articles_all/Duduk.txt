The '''Duduk''' is a traditional woodwind instrument of Armenians|Armenian origins.

 History 
The duduk is one of the oldest double Reed (instrument)|reed Instrument|instruments in the world which dates back over 3,000 years. Many different duduks can be found in Armenia and the Caucasus. The roots of Armenian duduk music go back to the times of the Armenian king Tigran the Great (95-55 BC). The instrument is seen in many Armenian manuscripts of the Middle Ages.
