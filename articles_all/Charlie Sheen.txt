On March 1, 2011, Sheen was living with two women, pornographic actress Bree Olson|Rachel Oberlin and model and graphic designer Natalie Kenly. He called them his "goddesses".

=Substance abuse and legal issues=
On May 20, 1998, Sheen overdosed while using cocaine. He was hospitalized. Sheen was already on probation in California for a previous drug offense. His probation was extended for an extra year. He entered a rehab clinic.

On December 25, 2009, Sheen was arrested for assaulting his wife, Brooke Mueller in Aspen, Colorado. He was released the same day from jail after posting an $8,500 bond (finance)|bond. Sheen was sentenced to 30 days in a drug rehab center, 30 days of probation, and 36 hours of anger management.

On October 26, 2010, the police removed Sheen from his suite at the Plaza Hotel after he reportedly caused $7,000 in damage.

Health
On November 16, 2015, it was revealed that Sheen would make a formal announcement through a television interview that he is officially HIV|HIV-positive. He said he was diagnosed four years earlier.
