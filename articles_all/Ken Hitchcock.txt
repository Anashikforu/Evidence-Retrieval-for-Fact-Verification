
'''Ken Hitchcock''' (born December 17, 1951 in Edmonton, Alberta|Edmonton, Alberta) is a Canadians|Canadian National Hockey League|NHL ice hockey coach. He coaches the Columbus Blue Jackets. He has also coached the Dallas Stars and the Philadelphia Flyers in the past. Ken has won one Stanley Cup with the Dallas Stars in the 1998-1999|99 NHL season.
