The {{nihongo foot|'''Empire of Japan'''|大日本帝国|Dai Nippon Teikoku (Japanese 大日本帝國) (Manchu Standard Chinese : 大日本帝国)  (Korean : 일본제국) (Russian : Японская Империя) was a historical Nation state|Nation State and great power during the period from the Meiji Restoration to the Japanese wikt:defeat|defeat in World War II. It ruled the Home Islands of Japan and many other areas. The Emperor of Japan|Emperors during this time, were Emperor Meiji (Mutsuhito),from 1868-1912 and Emperor Taishō (Yoshihito) from 1912-1926 and Emperor Shōwa (Hirohito) from 1926-1989 . It is considered to go from the year 1868 to 1945. Hirohito was still emperor of Japan after the empire dissolved in 1947 . Hirohito was emperor of Japan until his death in 1989 .

In those years, Japan changed greatly. It became one of the most powerful countries in the world. Japan built a powerful army and navy, and changed from an agriculture|agricultural to an industry|industrial economy. The Japanese began to invasion|invade and occupy other near countries like Korea under Japanese rule|Korea, Taiwan, and part of Second Sino-Japanese War|China to get resource (economics)|resources.

The Empire of Japan surrendered to the Allies of World War II|Allies on September 2, 1945, after a long war against the Allied nations of World War II ending with the atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki.  The Allies occupied the country and made many changes, including a Constitution of Japan|new constitution.  Allied occupation and reconstruction of the country continued well into the 1950s.
