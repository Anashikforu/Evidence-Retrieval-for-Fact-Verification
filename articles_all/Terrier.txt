A '''terrier''' is a dog of any one of many Dog breed|breeds of terrier types. They are named from the Latin word ''terra'' meaning Soil|earth. It refers to their love of digging after their prey (small burrowing mammals). They are descended from dogs bred to hunt and kill rodents. Terriers usually have 'wiry' (stiff hairs like wire) Hair|coats that need special care. Examples of terriers include Irish Terrier and the Cairn Terrier.
