

The '''Dominion of Pakistan''' was an independent federation|federal dominion in South Asia that was created in 1947 in the Partition of India|partition of British Raj|British India into two Sovereignty|sovereign dominions (the other being the Union of India).

The dominion included modern-day Pakistan (called West Pakistan) and Bangladesh (Bangladesh#History|East Pakistan). It was supposed to be a homeland for the Muslims of the Indian sub-continent.  It became the Pakistan|Islamic Republic of Pakistan in 1956. The eastern part of the country became independent as the Bangladesh|People's Republic of Bangladesh in 1971.
