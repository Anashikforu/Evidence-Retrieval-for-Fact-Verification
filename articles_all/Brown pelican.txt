The '''brown pelican''' (''Pelecanus occidentalis'') is a large bird that lives along the coasts of the Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic, Pacific Ocean, and Gulf of Mexico in North America|North and South America.

Brown pelicans are carnivores (meat-eaters) and hunt during the day (they are diurnal). They eat mostly fish and some amphibians and crustaceans.
