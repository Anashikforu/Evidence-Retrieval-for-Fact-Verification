
'''Gérard Paul Francis Houllier''' Order of the British Empire|OBE (3 September 1947 &ndash; 14 December 2020) was a France|French Association football|football manager and former player. He was the technical director of National Women's Soccer League|NSWL team OL Reign at the time of his death. Houllier is most known for his time at Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool, and is considered one of their best managers because of the trophies he won.

Houllier died on 14 December 2020 at a Paris hospital from heart surgery-related problems, aged 73.
