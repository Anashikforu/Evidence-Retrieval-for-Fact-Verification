The '''Honda S2000''' was a convertible|roadster created by the car company Honda. It was first on sale in April 1999. It was the successor (new model) of the S800. Production of the S2000 ended in 2009.

 Models 

'''AP1'''
The S2000 was introduced in 1999 as a 2000 model.
