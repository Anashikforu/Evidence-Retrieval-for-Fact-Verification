The '''British Rail Class 142''' is a class of ''Pacer (train)|Pacer'' multiple unit|diesel multiple units used in the United Kingdom. 96 units were built by BREL in Derby Carriage and Wagon Works|Derby between 1985 and 1987. They were a development of the earlier British Rail Class 141|Class 141 which were introduced in 1984.
