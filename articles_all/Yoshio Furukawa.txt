Biography
Furukawa was born in Osaka Prefecture on July 5, 1934. After graduating from Kansai University, he joined Dunlop Japan SC|Dunlop Japan in 1957.

In June 1956, when Furukawa was a Kansai University student, he was selected for the Japan national football team|Japan national team for the Football at the 1956 Summer Olympics|1956 Summer Olympics qualification. At this qualification, on June 3, he debuted against South Korea national football team|South Korea. In November, he played in the 1956 Summer Olympics in Melbourne. He also played in the Football at the 1958 Asian Games|1958 Asian Games. He played 19 games for Japan until 1962.
