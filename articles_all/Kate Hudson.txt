
'''Kate Garry Hudson''' (born April 19, 1979) is an Americans|American actress, movie director, screenwriter and producer. In 2001 she won Golden Globe for this role in ''Almost Famous'' and was nominated for Academy Awards|Oscar, but Marcia Gay Harden won.

 Biography 
Kate is the younger child of musician Bill Hudson (singer)|Bill Hudson and Oscar-winning actress Goldie Hawn who were divorced in 1980. She has one older brother, Oliver Hudson. She also has one half-brother and three half-sisters.

 Career 
Kate began a career as an actress in 1996.

In 2000 she played Penny Lane in ''Almost Famous'' and won a Golden Globe for this role. She was also nominated for an Academy Awards|Oscar for this role, but Marcia Gay Harden won.

She is also a movie director, screenwriter and producer.

 Personal life 
From 2000 to 2007 she was married to musician Chris Robinson (singer)|Chris Robinson. They had one child, Ryder Russell Robinson (born on January 4, 2004).
