


'''Münster''' (Münster dialect: ''Mönster'', Dutch language|Dutch: ''Munster'', Frisian language|Frisian: ''Múnster'', Old Saxon: ''Mimigernaford'', Latin: ''Monasterium'') is the historical capital of Westphalia and belongs now to the States of Germany|state of North Rhine-Westphalia.
It has about 270,000 inhabitants, an airport and a university.
Münster was very powerful in the Middle Ages and the Renaissance.
The Westphalian Peace was signed in Münster.
