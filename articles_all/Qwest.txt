
'''Qwest Communications International Inc.''' was an United States|American Telecommunication|telecom company. It served 14 north central and western states. Qwest merged with Baby Bell company '''US West Communications''' during 2000. US West had consisted of '''Pacific Northwest Bell''', '''Mountain Bell''' and '''Northwestern Bell''' before 1991, during which year the three telephone companies merged to become US West. Qwest Communications was founded around 1996.

On April 22, 2010, CenturyLink announced that it was going to buy Qwest for $22.4 billion.
