

 (born November 24, 1957) is a Japanese professional wrestler, best known as the original Tiger Mask. He has wrestled under his real name as well as the names '''Sammy Lee''', and masked '''Super Tiger''', '''Tiger King''', '''(Original) Tiger Mask''' and '''The Mask of Tiger'''. He is the only man to hold the WWF Junior Heavyweight Championship and the NWA World Junior Heavyweight Championship at the same time.
