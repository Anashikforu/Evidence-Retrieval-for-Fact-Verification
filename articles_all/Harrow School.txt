
'''Harrow School''', often called simply ''Harrow'', is a secondary school for boys in London Borough of Harrow|Harrow in London, England.The ''Guardian'' 
Harrow is an independent fee-paying school, of the type known in Britain as ''public schools''. The equivalent term in the US would probably be ''private schools'' (see discussion at public school). The Harrow Foundation also runs an independent Grammar School in Harrow, called the John Lyon School, and several international schools.Harrow Foundation 
In 2013 66% of pupils got 5 A-C GCSE grades including English and Maths. 14.1% of students go to Cambridge University|Cambridge or Oxford University|Oxford.
In 2013 a TV series called Harrow: a Very British School started.
Famous men who went to Harrow include Benedict Cumberbatch, eight prime ministers (seven UK and the first Prime Minister of India), Lord Byron, James Blunt, C. W. Alcock, and John Amery.
