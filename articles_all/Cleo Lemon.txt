

'''Cleotha Lemon, Jr.''' (born August 16, 1979 in Greenwood, Mississippi|Greenwood, Mississippi) is an Americans|American American football|football quarterback. He currently plays for the Jacksonville Jaguars of the National Football League (NFL). He started playing in the NFL for the Baltimore Ravens in 2002. Lemon played for the San Diego Chargers from 2003 to 2005. He then played for the Miami Dolphins until the 2007 season. He played college football at Arkansas State University|Arkansas State.
