
'''Aast, Pyrénées-Atlantiques''' is a Commune in France|commune in the Pyrénées-Atlantiques departments of France|department in southwestern France.

 Transport 

= Road =
The D311 and D64 roads pass through the commune.

 Geography 
Aast is on the border of the Pyrénées-Atlantiques department.
