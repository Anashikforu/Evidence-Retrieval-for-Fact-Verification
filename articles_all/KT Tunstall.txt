'''Katherine Victoria "Katie" Tunstall''', also known as '''KT Tunstall''' (born 23 June 1975), is a Scottish people|Scottish singer and songwriter. She was born in St Andrews, Fife. Her father is Irish people|Irish; her mother is half-Chinese people|Chinese, half-Scottish. She was adopted when she was 18 days old. She has had many hit songs, including, "Black Horse and the Cherry Tree"'.
