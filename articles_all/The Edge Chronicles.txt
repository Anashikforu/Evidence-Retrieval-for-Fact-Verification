


'''''The Edge Chronicles''''' are books of young-adult fiction|young-adult and fantasy literature|fantasy wrote by Paul Stewart (writer)|Paul Stewart and Chris Riddell. It contains three trilogies. All in the books is surealist. Characters, cities, places, monuments, animals, plants, transportation and more were invanted by the writers.
