
'''Béligneux''' is a Communes of France|commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes Regions of France|region in the Ain Departments of France|department in the east of France. In 2010, 2,905 people lived in the commune. The inhabitants are the ''Bélignards''. The most important monuments in the commune are a neo-gothic church from the 19th century and the Saint-André chapel from the 13th century. The commune is twinned with Ostfildern in Germany since 1978.
