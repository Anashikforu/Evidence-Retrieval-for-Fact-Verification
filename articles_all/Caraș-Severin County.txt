

'''Caraş-Severin''' (, Bulgarian language|Bulgarian: Караш-Северин, ''Karash-Severin'') is a county (judeţ) of Romania, in Historical regions of Romania|historical region Banat. The capital of Caraş-Severin County is Reşiţa.

 Demographics 
In 2002, about 332,000 people lived in Caraş-Severin County.



 Geography 
Caraş-Severin County has an area of 8,514 Square kilometre|km². It is the third biggest county in Romania, after Timiş County|Timiş and Suceava County|Suceava counties. The Danube enters Romania in Caraş-Severin County.

 Divisions 
Caraş-Severin County has 2 municipalities, 6 towns, and 69 communes.
