
'''Depression''' is a state of low Mood (psychology)|mood and not wanting to do anything. It can change what a person thinks, does, feels, and sense of well-being. It may lead to being sad, make it harder to think and a large change in wikt:appetite|appetite and time spent sleeping. People who have depression may feel no hope and, sometimes, thoughts of suicide. It can either be short term or long term. Depressed mood is a symptom of some mood disorders such as major depressive disorder or dysthymia;

 it is a normal wikt:reaction|reaction to life events, such as losing a loved one; and it is also caused by some wikt:physical|physical wikt:diseases|diseases and a side effect of some drugs and medical treatments.
