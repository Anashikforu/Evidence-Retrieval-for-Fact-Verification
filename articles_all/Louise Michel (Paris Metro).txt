'''Louise Michel''' is a rapid transit|station of the Paris Métro, serving Paris Métro Line 3|Line 3. It is located in the commune of Levallois-Perret northwest of the France|French capital (city)|capital, Paris.

History
Before 1946, this station was named ''Vallier'' after a mountain (2839m)in the Pyrenees near the Val d'Aran. 

The station was renamed on May 1, 1946 to ''Louise Michel'', in honour of the France|French anarchist and Paris Commune|communarde.
