
'''''Shakespeare in Love''''' is a 1998 United Kingdom|British-United States|American romance movie|romantic comedy drama|comedy-drama movie starring Gwyneth Paltrow, Ben Affleck and Judi Dench. It won 7 Academy Awards, including Best Picture of 1998. This movie is about the involvement of William Shakespeare in a serious love affair with Viola (played by Paltrow).
