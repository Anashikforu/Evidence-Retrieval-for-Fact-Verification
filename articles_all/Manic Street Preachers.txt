
'''Manic Street Preachers''' (often referred to as the '''Manics''') are an alternative rock band from Blackwood, Caerphilly|Blackwood, Wales, formed in 1986. They are James Dean Bradfield (vocals, guitars), Nicky Wire (bass, occasional vocals) and Sean Moore (musician)|Sean Moore (drums, backing vocals, occasional trumpet). The band is part of the Cardiff music scene. Their biggest success was during the 1990s.
