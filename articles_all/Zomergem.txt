'''Zomergem''' is a former municipality in the Belgium|Belgian province of East Flanders. On 1 January 2019, Lovendegem, Waarschoot and Zomergem were merged into the new municipality of Lievegem.

In 2007, 8,003 people lived there.

It is at 51° 07 North, 03° 33 East.
