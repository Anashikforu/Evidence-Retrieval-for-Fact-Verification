
'''Theresa Anne Villiers''' (born March 5, 1968) is an England|English politician. In July 2019, she became Secretary of State for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs for the Boris Johnson ministry. She stopped doing this job in 2020. She was first wikt:elect|elected in the United Kingdom 2005 general election|2005 general election. She is the Member of Parliament (MP) for Chipping Barnet (UK Parliament constituency)|Chipping Barnet and the Secretary of State for Northern Ireland . Villiers was the Minister of State for Transport from 2010 to 2012.
She was appointed as a Privy Council of the United Kingdom|Privy Counsellor on 9 June 2010. 

She is a member of the Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party.
