
'''Carolina Gynning''' (born 6 October 1978) is a Swedes|Swedish celebrity and Model (person)|model. She also is the winner of the 2004 season of reality television|reality television programme|television program Big Brother (TV series)|Big Brother. Gynning has also written the book, ''Ego Girl''. It is a biography of her early life. In 2008 it was reported that Gynning would write a continuation to ''Ego Girl'' called ''Ego Woman''.
