
'''Jaclyn Linetsky''' (January 8, 1986 – September 8, 2003) was a Canadians|Canadian teenager|teenage actress who has voiced many different people. She was best known for voicing Caillou in the animation|animated television show between 2000–2003. She later played as Megan in the television show ''15/love''. She died in a car crash on September 8, 2003 at the age of 17.
