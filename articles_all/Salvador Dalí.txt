

'''Salvador Domingo Felipe Jacinto Dalí i Domènech, 1st Marquess of Dalí of Púbol''' 

His most famous work was ''The Persistence of Memory'' (1931), which is now in MoMA, the Museum of Modern Art in New York. It is a dream-like landscape with a soft, wikt:melted|melted pocket-watch.
Dalí died of heart failure in Figueras. Salvador Dalí had a wife called Gala Dalí.
