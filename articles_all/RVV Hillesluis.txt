'''Rotterdamse Voetbalvereniging "Hillesluis"''' (translated:''Rotterdam's Football Society "Hillesluis"'') was a football (soccer)|football club in Rotterdam. It was located near the Feyenoord stadium. It was created in 1932 in a Rotterdam's town with the same name. It was disestablished in 2012.

This club has 21 teams from which:
4 senior teams
6 junior teams
7 student|pupil teams
1 lady's team
1 futsal team
2 disability|disabled teams
