 


 is a fighting game|fighting video game made by Namco Bandai for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 game consoles. It's the fifth installment in the ''Soul (series)|Soul'' series of video games. Like how ''Soulcalibur II'' had one exclusive character per system, this game has two ''Star Wars'' characters. The PlayStation 3 version has Darth Vader, while the Xbox 360 version has Yoda.
