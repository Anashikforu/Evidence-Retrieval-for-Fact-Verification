The '''shamisen''' or '''samisen''' (Japanese language|Japanese: 三味線, literal translation|literally "three taste strings"), also called '''sangen''' (literally "three strings") is a Japan|Japanese musical instrument with three strings, that is played with a big plectrum called a ''bachi''. The pronunciation in Japanese language|Japanese is usually "shamisen".
