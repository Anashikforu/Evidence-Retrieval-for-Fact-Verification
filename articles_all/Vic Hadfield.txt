
'''Victor Edward Hadfield''' (born October 4, 1940 in Oakville, Ontario|Oakville, Ontario) is a Canadians|Canadian retired professional ice hockey player. He is best known for playing with the New York Rangers where he is one of the most popular players in the history of the team. He also played with the Pittsburgh Penguins for the last three seasons of his career.
