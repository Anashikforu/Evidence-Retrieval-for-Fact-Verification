A '''salt mine''' is a Mining|mine where salt is taken from the earth. Very often, this is done in the mountains. It is different from a salt evaporation pond, where salt is taken from salt water. Getting salt from different layers of Rock (geology)|rock in a mountain used to be a very dangerous job. The Ancient Rome|Romans used to sentence prisoners to this kind of Labour (economics)|labour.

Usually the salt is mixed with rock that has chlorides and sulfates. 

Empty salt mines are used very often as a storage area, for example, for unused radioactive elements (from Nuclear power plant|nuclear power plants). Salt mines are empty if there is no salt left to take, or if taking the salt that is left would be too expensive.
