
A '''Chicano''' is a person who has a mixed Ancestor|ancestry and/or citizenship of the United States and usually Mexico.  Often, Chicano and Mexican-American are used to mean the same thing.  People from Central America|Central or South America and United States could also be called Chicano. '''Chicana''' is the female version of Chicano.  Chicanos often speak in Spanglish (language)|Spanglish.
