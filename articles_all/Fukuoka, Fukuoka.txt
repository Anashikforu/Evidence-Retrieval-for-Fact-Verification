 History
A short distance is there from Dazaifu and the Fukuoka area to Hakata-wan (博多湾). Hakata-wan was the landing site of the Mongol invasions of Japan in 1274 and 1281.

Fukuoka is known for its association football|football team, Avispa Fukuoka; and it is also known for its baseball team, the Fukuoka SoftBank Hawks. 

Geography
Fukuoka is on the northwest coast of Kyushu. It is about the same distance from Tokyo as it is from Shanghai or Seoul.Williams, Ingrid K. 
Fukuoka City is divided into 7 Wards of Japan|wards.
