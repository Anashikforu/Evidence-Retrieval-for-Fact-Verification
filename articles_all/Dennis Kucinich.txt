'''Dennis John Kucinich''' (born October 8, 1946) is a United States United States House of Representatives|Representative from Ohio. He was a hopeful for President of the United States in 2008. He is a United States Democratic Party|Democrat. Kucinich was raised in Cleveland, and was elected Mayor of the city in 1977. His tenure as mayor was a hard one, and he lost reelection to United States Republican Party|Republican challenger George Voinovich in 1979. Kucinich got back into politics in the 1990s, and became a member of the United States House of Representatives in the 1994 elections. A political progressive, he ran for president twice in 2004 and 2008.

In December 2020, Kucinich filled paperwork to run for Mayor of Cleveland in the 2021 election.
