'''Geddy Lee''' (born '''Gary Lee Weinrib''' on July 29, 1953 in Toronto, Ontario) is the Canadians|Canadian bass guitarist, singer, and synth player of the rock and roll|rock band Rush (band)|Rush. He is known for melodic basslines and high pitched singing. 

In the 1970s, Geddy Lee mainly used Rickenbacker 4001 basses. For the Grace Under Pressure album he used Steinberger basses. From the mid-80's to mid-90's he used Wal basses. Currently, he uses Fender Jazz basses.
