


'''Copper''' is the color of the metal copper.  It is a purplish-orange color.

The first recorded use of ''copper'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1594. Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 193; Color Sample of Copper: Page 31 Plate 4 Color Sample I11 


 Meaning of copper 

 Like the colors Gold (color)|gold and  Silver (color)|silver, copper represents wealth because the metal copper is Value|valuable, but less valuable than gold or silver.  The metal copper is of value mainly to use as electrical wiring because copper is the best conductor of electricity after silver.
