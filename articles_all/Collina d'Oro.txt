
'''Collina d'Oro''' (called Gold Hill in English) is a municipality of the district Lugano (district)|Lugano in the Cantons of Switzerland|canton of Ticino in Switzerland. It was made on 4 April 2004 from the merging of the villages of Agra, Switzerland|Agra, Gentilino, and Montagnola. On 1 April 2012, it incorporated the formerly independent municipality of Carabietta.
