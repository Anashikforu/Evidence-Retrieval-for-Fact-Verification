
'''Kofi Sarkodie-Mensah''' (born 14 March 1981) is a Ghanaian-Americans|American professional wrestling|professional wrestler. He is currently signed to World Wrestling Entertainment where he wrestles under the ring name '''Kofi Kingston'''. Kingston has won the wwe tag team championship on numerous occasions.
