
'''Matthew Raymond "Matt" Dillon''' (born February 18, 1964) is an Academy Award-nominated United States|American actor. He began acting in the late 1970s, gained fame as a teen idol during the 1980s, and made a successful career as an adult actor in the decades following, ending in an Academy Awards|Oscar nomination for his acting in the film ''Crash (2005 film)|Crash''.
