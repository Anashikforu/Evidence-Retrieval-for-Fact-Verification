

'''Bronze'''  is the color of the alloy bronze. 

The first recorded use of ''bronze'' as a color name in English language|English was in 1753.  Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 191; Color Sample of Bronze: Page 51 Plate 14 Color Sample L9

 Meaning of bronze 
 The color ''bronze'' means ''third place'' after Gold (color)|gold (''first place'') and Silver (color)|silver (''second place'') in awards such as the Olympic medals for the Olympic Games.
