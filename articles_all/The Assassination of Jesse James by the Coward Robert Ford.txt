

'''''The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford''''' is a 2007 in movies|2007 Western (genre)|Western movie. It was based on Ron Hansen (novelist)|Ron Hansen's 1983 The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford (novel)|novel of the same name. The movie is directed by Andrew Dominik, with Brad Pitt portraying Jesse James and Casey Affleck as Robert Ford (criminal)|Robert Ford. Filming took place in near Calgary, Alberta|Calgary, Edmonton, Alberta|Edmonton and Winnipeg, Manitoba|Winnipeg.
