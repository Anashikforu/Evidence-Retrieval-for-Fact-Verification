'''Harrow & Wealdstone station''' is a station on the Bakerloo line. The Bakerloo line is on London Underground. The station opened for underground trains in 1917. It was closed for underground services 1982 but reopened in 1984. It is in travelcard zone 5, so it is near the edge of London. The next stop southbound is Kenton station|Kenton.

 National Rail 
The station is served by London Overground, London Midland and Southern (train operating company)|Southern. On 8 October 1952, the station was the site of a train crash Harrow and Wealdstone rail crash|in which 112 people were killed.

Transport connections
Bus routes 140 (24 hour), 182, 186, 258, 340, H9, H10 and night bus route N18 serve various stops beside or near the station. All the buses that serve the station also serve Harrow on the Hill tube station|Harrow on the Hill station but not all as direct services.
