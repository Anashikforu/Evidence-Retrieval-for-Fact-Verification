
'''Faith Alexis Ford''' (born September 14, 1964, in Alexandria, Louisiana) is an Americans|American television and movie actor|actress. She is best known for her role as Corky Sherwood on ''Murphy Brown'' and as Hope in the television programme|television series, ''Hope & Faith''.
