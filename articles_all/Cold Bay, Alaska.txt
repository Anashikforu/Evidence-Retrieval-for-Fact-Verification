
'''Cold Bay''' is a town in Alaska, United States. The town name is '''Udaamagax''' in the Aleut language. As of the 2010 United States Census|2010 census, the population was 108.

Cold Bay is one of the main commerce|commercial centers of the Alaska Peninsula.  It is home to Cold Bay Airport.  The offices for Izembek National Wildlife Refuge are also in the town.
