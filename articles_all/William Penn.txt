
'''William Penn''' (14 October 1644-30 July 1718) was an England|English colony|colonial leader. He was very wealthy and owned slaves who he forced to work on his plantation. He was given what would become the United States|US U.S. state|state of Pennsylvania by King Charles II of England|Charles II as a debt to his father. Penn was a member of the Religious Society of Friends, who are known as Quakers.

Penn and his wife were made honorary United States citizens in 1984 by President (United States)|US President Ronald Reagan. He was friends with George Fox.
