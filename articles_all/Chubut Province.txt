
'''Chubut''' is a Provinces of Argentina|Province of Argentina.  It is in the region called Patagonia.

Some important cities are Comodoro Rivadavia, Trelew, Puerto Madryn, Rawson and Esquel.

The province borders Río Negro Province|Río Negro in the north, Santa Cruz Province|Santa Cruz in the south, and Chile to the west.
