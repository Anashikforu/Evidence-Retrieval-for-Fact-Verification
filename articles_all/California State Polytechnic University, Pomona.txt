The '''California State Polytechnic University, Pomona''' (also known as '''Cal Poly Pomona''') is a public university in Pomona, California, United States. It is one of 23 campuses in the California State University system. It was founded in 1938.
Cal Poly Pomona student-sportsperson|athletes play sports against other universities using the name ''Cal Poly Pomona Broncos''. The university is a member of the California Collegiate Athletic Association and the National Collegiate Athletic Association|NCAA. The official colors of the university and its athletic teams are green and Gold (color)|gold.
