
'''Buxaceae''' is a family (biology)|family of plants. There are five genus|genera, with about 70 species. The family has a cosmopolitan distribution. Many family members are grown as ornamental plants. The plants are Herbaceous plant|herbaceous and evergreen. Some are shrubs or small trees.
