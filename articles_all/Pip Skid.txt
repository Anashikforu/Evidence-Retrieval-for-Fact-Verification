
'''Patrick Skene''', known professionally as '''Pip Skid''' and '''Wicked Nut''', is a Canadians|Canadian rapper and writer, and a cornerstone of the successful Vancouver based independent hip hop label Peanuts & Corn.
As a co-founder of landmark Canadian hip hop group Farm Fresh, as well as a member of other famed crews such as Fermented Reptile, Hip Hop Weiners, Taking Care of Business (album)|Taking Care of Business, and Break Bread,
