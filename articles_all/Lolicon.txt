, is a slang word for the phrase "Lolita complex". Outside Japan, the word is less common. It denotes a genre of manga and anime in which young female Fictional character|characters are shown in a sexual way. The word is a reference to Vladimir Nabokov's book, ''Lolita''. In the book, a middle-aged man sexually desires a 12-year-old girl .Kinsella, 305.

Some people claim that lolicon adds to actual sexual abuse of children, while others claim that there is no facts that show this, or that facts show the opposite. Countries have tried to make lolicon that shows children naked or having sex illegal as a type of child pornography. 

One of the few that has made it actually illegal is the United Kingdom where the possession of lolicon pornography is illegal. These laws have been criticised heavily,
