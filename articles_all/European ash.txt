The '''European ash''' (''Fraxinus excelsior'') is a kind of ash tree. It is found throughout Europe and not just Scandinavia. It is a deciduous tree that grows to a height of 20â€“35 metres.

Uses
Ash wood is very easy to bend. It is therefore the material of choice to make Bow (weapon)|bows. Tennis rackets, and the long poles used for snooker are sometimes made from it. 

=Religion=
In Norse mythology, the tree Yggdrasil was a European ash. The tree linked all the nine worlds|world, Niflheim and Asgard, amongst others. Below the tree resides Hel as well as the Well of Urd.
