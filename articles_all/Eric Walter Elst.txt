'''Eric Walter Elst''' (30 November 1936 &ndash; 2 January 2022) was an astronomer from Belgium.  Elst has found nearly 3,500 asteroids, including many wikt:notable|notable asteroids.  An asteroid, 3936 Elst, was named after him. He was born in Mortsel, Belgium.

Elst died in 2 January 2022, aged 85.
