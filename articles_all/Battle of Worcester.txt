The '''Battle of Worcester''' was the final battle of the English Civil War. Oliver Cromwell and the Parliament of England|Parliamentarians defeated the Cavaliers (royalists)|Royalist, mainly Scotland|Scottish, forces of King Charles II of England|Charles II on September 3, 1651 at Worcester, England. The 16,000 Royalist forces were :wikt:defeat|defeated by the 28,000 strong "New Model Army" of Cromwell.
