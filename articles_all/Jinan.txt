'''Jinan''' (Chinese language|Chinese: 济南/濟南) is a city in eastern China and the capital of China's Shandong province. Jinan's most famous attraction is the Baotu Spring. Li Qingzhao(李清照, 1084 - 1151 A.D) was born in Jinan and is a famous poet in China.


 Political divisions 
Jinan Municipality is divided into 6 district and 3 counties.
