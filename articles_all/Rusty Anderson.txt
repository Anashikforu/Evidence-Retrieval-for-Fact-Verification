
'''Rusty Anderson''' (born January 20, 1959) is an Americans|American guitarist, singer and songwriter from La Habra, California|La Habra, California. He has played lead guitar for Paul McCartney since 2001. He has played for many famous musicians, for example, Elton John, The Bangles, Courtney Love and Belinda Carlisle. He also has a band called Rusty Anderson Afternoon whose first single "Effortless" hit Number One on American radio
