General officer|General '''Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono''' (born 9 September 1949), is an Indonesian retired military general and politician. He was the sixth President of Indonesia from 2004 to 2014. Yudhoyono is the first president to be elected directly by voters.  Yudhoyono won the presidency on 20 September 2004. He defeated President Megawati Sukarnoputri.
