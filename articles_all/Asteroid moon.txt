'''An asteroid moon''' is an asteroid that orbits or goes around another asteroid. Asteroid moons are quite rare. Only 2% of asteroids are thought to have moons. -->

To be an asteroid Satellite (natural)|moon, an asteroid has to be smaller than the object it goes around. If they are roughly the same size they are called a Binary asteroid|binary pair.
