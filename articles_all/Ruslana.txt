'''Ruslana Stepanivna Lyzhychko''' (May 24, 1973) is a popular Ukraine|Ukrainian pop music|pop singer, composer, producer, Conductor (music)|conductor and dancer. She was born in the city of Lviv in Ukraine. She won the Eurovision Song Contest in Istanbul in 2004.  She is also a former Member of Parliament serving as deputy in the Ukrainian parliament (Verkhovna Rada) for the Our Ukraine Party.

 Human rights 

In 2014, Ruslana became a leader in the Euromaidan|Maidan movement for human rights and democratic reform in Ukraine. 

Ruslana was also in the 2004 Orange Revolution.  She was UNICEF National Ambassador in Ukraine in 2005. In 2008 she spoke for Renewable energy|green energy.

 Awards 

In 2014 Ruslana received the International Women of Courage Award form the U.S. Department of State.
