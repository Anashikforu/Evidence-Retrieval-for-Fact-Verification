The''' Kingdom of Sophene''' (Armenian language|Armenian: Ծոփքի Թագավորութուն) was an ancient Armenian kingdom.Toumanoff, Cyril(1963) Studies in Christian Caucasian History, Georgetown University Press Found sometime around the 3rd century B.C. the kingdom stayed independent until 90's B.C. when Tigranes the great conquered these territories as part of his empire.

 Origin 
The first records show that it was part of Urartu, later it became a province of the Kingdom of Armenia|Armenian Kingdom around 600 BC.
