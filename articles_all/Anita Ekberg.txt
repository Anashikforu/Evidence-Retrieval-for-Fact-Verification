'''Kerstin Anita Marianne Ekberg''' (29 September 1931  11 January 2015) was a Swedes|Swedish actress. She was born in Malmö, Skåne. She was best known for her role in the movie ''La Dolce Vita'' (1960), which was directed by Federico Fellini. 

Ekberg died from a long-illness in Castelli Romani, Italy, aged 83.
