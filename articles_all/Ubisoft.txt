'''Ubisoft''' formerly as '''Ubi Soft''' is a company that makes video games. They are based in France|Canada. They are famous for making many popular games including the Rayman franchise video game|franchise and the Tom Clancy games such as ''Rainbow Six'' and ''Splinter Cell''.
