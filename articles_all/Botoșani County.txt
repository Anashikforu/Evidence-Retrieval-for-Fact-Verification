

'''Botoşani''' () is a county (judeţ) of Romania, in Moldavia.  The capital of Botoşani County is Botoşani.

 Geography 
Botoşani County has an area of 4,986 km².

 Divisions 
Botoşani County has 2 municipalities, 5 towns and 67 communes.
