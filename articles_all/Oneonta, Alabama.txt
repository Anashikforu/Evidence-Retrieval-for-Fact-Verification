
'''Oneonta''' is a city in Blount County, Alabama|Blount County, Alabama, United States. At the 2000 census the population was 5,576. The city is the county seat of Blount County, Alabama|Blount County. Oneonta is home to the Covered Bridge Festival.

History
Oneonta became the county seat in 1889 when it was moved from Blountsville.

During World War II, a small Prisoner of War|POW camp was outside of Oneonta.

City government
The city government is made up a Mayor and a five-person city council.
