
The '''Verein für Leibesübungen Bochum 1848''' ('''VfL Bochum''') is a football (soccer)|football and Sports club in Bochum, Germany.

Besides football, the VfL Bochum operates the following kinds of sport: Badminton, Basketball, Fencing, Ice hockey, Athletics (track and field)|athletics, Handball, Swimming, dance sport, Tennis, Table tennis and Volleyball.
