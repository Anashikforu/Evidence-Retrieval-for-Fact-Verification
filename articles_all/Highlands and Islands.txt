The '''Highlands and Islands''' of Scotland are broadly the Scottish Highlands plus Orkney, Shetland and the Hebrides.

This area consisted of parts of seven County|counties of Scotland: 

 Argyll
 Inverness-shire
 Ross and Cromarty
 Sutherland
 Caithness
 Orkney
 Shetland
