
'''Lucas Robin "Luke" Ridnour''' (born February 13, 1981 in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho|Coeur d'Alene, Idaho) is an Americans|American basketball player that currently plays for the Minnesota Timberwolves of the National Basketball Association|NBA. He went to University of Oregon for college where he played for the Oregon Ducks basketball team. Ridnour has also played with the Seattle SuperSonics from 2003-2007 and the Milwaukee Bucks from 2008-2010.
