

'''One Laptop Per Child''' is an organization founded at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. It is a non-profit organisation. It wants to make cheap laptops so that children in less rich countries without as much technology can also use a computer to learn. The laptop is called an XO.  It has an operating system called Sugar (operating system)|sugar based on GNU/Linux.  It also can run Microsoft Windows|Windows.
