


'''''Halo Wars''''' is a real-time strategy video game for the Xbox 360. It was developed by Ensemble Studios. The game was released by Microsoft. It was released in Japan and Australia on February&nbsp;26, 2009, in Europe on February&nbsp;27, and in North America on March&nbsp;3. It is based on the popular, award winning ''Halo (series)|Halo'' series of video games, and is set in the year 2531, 21 years before the events of ''Halo: Combat Evolved''. It's the first ''Halo'' game that is not a first person shooter.
